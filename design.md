# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury: warm, refined, editorial, and premium without looking flashy or promotional.

## Palette
- Background base: deep espresso brown for hero, footer, and dark editorial surfaces. Example Tailwind: `bg-stone-950`.
- Primary page background: soft parchment neutral. Example Tailwind: `bg-stone-100`.
- Card background: warm ivory. Example Tailwind: `bg-stone-50`.
- Accent metallic: muted champagne gold used sparingly for buttons, borders, stars, and micro-highlights. Example Tailwind: `bg-amber-200`, `text-amber-200`, `border-amber-200/60`.
- Primary text on light backgrounds: deep brown-charcoal. Example Tailwind: `text-stone-900`.
- Secondary text: softened taupe-brown. Example Tailwind: `text-stone-600`.
- Text on dark surfaces: warm ivory. Example Tailwind: `text-stone-100`.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: elegant serif with editorial scale. Use Playfair Display from Google Fonts.
- Body, UI labels, metadata: Inter.
- Product names should be uppercase with increased tracking. Example Tailwind: `uppercase tracking-[0.35em]`.

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous whitespace and airy section padding. Example Tailwind: `px-5 sm:px-8 lg:px-12`, `py-16 md:py-24`.
- Constrain content width for readability. Example Tailwind: `mx-auto max-w-7xl`.
- Use thin dividers instead of heavy borders. Example Tailwind: `border-stone-300/60`.
- Cards should feel elevated but restrained. Example Tailwind: `shadow-[0_18px_50px_rgba(28,25,23,0.08)]`.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, clean, slightly rounded, no loud gradients. Prefer dark solid buttons with light text, or subtle outlined buttons on light sections.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, then opaque dark surface on scroll.
- Product cards: minimal chrome, image-first, refined hover, no discount badges.
- Inputs: soft ivory fill with clear borders and readable dark text.

## Imagery
- Favor warm-lit, editorial jewelry photography with gold pieces on skin or rich neutral backgrounds.
- UGC/reels cards should still feel curated and premium.
- Avoid generic bright white studio visuals or anything that feels mass-market.

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text legible on every surface.
- Use animation subtly: fade, translate, shadow shifts.
- Maintain one cohesive elegant direction across every page.

## Do not
- Do not use neon, loud sale colors, or aggressive promotional treatments.
- Do not over-stack content on desktop into a narrow single-column mobile layout.
- Do not leave important text colors implicit on custom backgrounds.
