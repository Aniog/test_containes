# Design Guidelines

This document outlines the visual style and design principles for the project.

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter, system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif.
- **Headings**: Semi-bold to Bold (weights 600, 700).
- **Body**: Regular (weight 400).
- **Example Class**: `font-sans`

## Colors
- **Primary**: Indigo (e.g., `text-indigo-600`, `bg-indigo-600`)
- **Secondary**: Slate (e.g., `text-slate-600`, `bg-slate-50`)
- **Accent**: Emerald (e.g., `text-emerald-500`)
- **Background**: White (`bg-white`) or Light Gray (`bg-gray-50`)
- **Foreground**: Slate 900 (`text-slate-900`)

## Spacing & Layout
- **Containers**: Max width `max-w-7xl` with auto margins.
- **Padding**: Standard section padding `py-12` to `py-24`.
- **Gaps**: Use `gap-4`, `gap-8` for layout grids.

## Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners (`rounded-md` or `rounded-lg`), transition effects, clear focus states.
- **Cards**: Subtle shadows (`shadow-sm` or `shadow-md`), white background, rounded corners.

## Visual Effects
- **Shadows**: `shadow-sm` for discrete elements, `shadow-xl` for overlays.
- **Transitions**: `transition-all duration-200`
