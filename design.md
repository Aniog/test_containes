# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount.

## Typography
- **Headings / product names**: Cormorant Garamond (Google Font) — serif, elegant, uppercase with wide tracking for product names
- **Body / UI**: Inter (Google Font) — clean sans-serif
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, tracking-wider, font-medium

## Colors
- **Background**: #FBF9F6 (warm ivory)
- **Foreground / text**: #1A1A1A (soft black)
- **Muted text**: #7A7268 (warm taupe-gray)
- **Gold accent**: #C9A96E (warm muted gold)
- **Deep base**: #2C1810 (deep warm brown)
- **Border**: #E8E1D8 (warm taupe)
- **Card bg**: #FFFFFF
- **Light accent**: #F5F0EA (warm light beige)
- **Button gold**: #C9A96E with white text
- **Button outline**: border #C9A96E, text #C9A96E, hover fill

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card padding: p-6
- Gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E8E1D8]
- Rounded: rounded-sm or rounded-md on cards

## Effects
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
- Smooth transitions: transition-all duration-300
- Hover: slight lift (-translate-y-0.5) on cards

## Layout
- Max width: max-w-7xl mx-auto
- Mobile-first responsive
- Container padding: px-4 md:px-8 lg:px-12

## DOs
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable with good contrast
- Use gold accent sparingly for emphasis
- Soft, editorial imagery
- Thin, elegant borders

## DON'Ts
- No bright/flashy colors
- No heavy/dark backgrounds except hero overlay
- No cluttered layouts
- No loud patterns
