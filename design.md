# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Visual Identity

## Core Concept
"Quiet luxury, warm, editorial." A premium demi-fine jewelry brand that feels exclusive yet accessible.

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - Product names: `uppercase tracking-[0.2em] font-medium`
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - Clean, legible, modern.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Base**: `#FAF9F6` (Off-white / Paper) - `bg-[#FAF9F6]`
- **Text Primary**: `#1A1A1A` (Near black) - `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Text Muted**: `#6B6B6B` (Soft grey) - `text-[#6B6B6B]`
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#D4AF37` (Metallic Gold) - `text-[#D4AF37]` or `bg-[#D4AF37]`
- **Accent Soft**: `#F5EFE6` (Warm Sand) - `bg-[#F5EFE6]`
- **Border/Divider**: `#E5E5E5` (Thin hairline) - `border-[#E5E5E5]`

## Visual Elements
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace. `p-8`, `p-12`, `gap-16`.
- **Dividers**: Thin `h-[1px]` lines.
- **Imagery**: Large editorial shots with warm lighting.
- **Interactions**: Subtle hover transitions (opacity, scale 1.02), soft shadows on cards.
- **Buttons**: 
  - Solid: Dark background with light text.
  - Outlined: Thin border with elegant text.

## Accessibility
- Contrast ratios maintained for all text elements.
- Clear focus states.
- Semantic HTML tags.
