# Design System: ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style
Elegant, professional, industrial, and user-friendly. High contrast between dark backgrounds and golden accents. Precision-focused typography.

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Playfair Display', serif (Elegant, authoritative) - `font-serif`
- **Body**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Clean, readable) - `font-sans`

## Colors
- **Primary**: #1A1A1A (Deep charcoal/black) - `text-primary`, `bg-primary`
- **Accent**: #C5A059 (Gold/Bronze) - `text-accent`, `bg-accent`
- **Secondary**: #F5F5F7 (Light grey for backgrounds)
- **Text**: #1A1A1A (Primary text), #666666 (Secondary text)

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace for an "elegant" feel.
- Grid-based layouts for precision.
- Responsive breakpoints: `md` (768px), `lg` (1024px).

## Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners (`rounded-md`), solid accent foreground white, or outlined.
- **Cards**: Minimalistic with subtle shadows and border on hover.
- **Sections**: Clear separation with varying background colors (Primary vs Secondary).

## Icons
- Industrial and functional (Lucide React).
