# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Palette**:
  - Background: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory)
  - Surface: `#FFFFFF` (pure white)
  - Text primary: `#1A1A1A` (near black)
  - Text secondary: `#6B6560` (warm taupe)
  - Text muted: `#9B9590` (soft stone)
  - Accent (gold): `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod)
  - Accent hover: `#996F00`
  - Accent light: `#F5E6C8` (pale gold wash)
  - Border: `#E8E4DF` (warm light gray)
  - Dark section bg: `#1A1A1A` (for newsletter/footer)
  - Dark text on dark: `#FAF8F5`
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
  - Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern
  - Product names: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`)
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace. Section padding `py-20` to `py-28`.
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline `border-t border-[#E8E4DF]`
- **Shadows**: Soft, warm `shadow-sm`, `shadow-md` with warm tint
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary: solid gold bg, white text, `rounded-none` (sharp edges feel premium)
  - Secondary: outlined, gold border, transparent bg
  - Hover: subtle opacity shift or bg darkening
- **Transitions**: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`
- **Border radius**: Minimal — `rounded-sm` or `rounded-none` for premium feel
- **Images**: Warm-toned, editorial, gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography hierarchy clear
- Use serif for headings, sans-serif for body
- Product names always UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing
- Subtle hover animations (opacity, slight scale, underline)
- Maintain warm, cohesive color palette

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No discount-looking badges or loud CTAs
- No heavy shadows or rounded corners
- No generic e-commerce patterns (big red sale banners, etc.)
- No cluttered layouts
