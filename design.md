# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium.
- **Palette**:
  - **Background**: `#F9F7F2` (Soft Parchment)
  - **Foreground**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Obsidian)
  - **Accent**: `#C5A059` (Warm Gold Metallic)
  - **Muted**: `#E5E0D5` (Soft Stone)
- **Typography**:
  - **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif.
  - **Body/UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif.
  - **Product Names**: Uppercase, letter-spacing: 0.15em.
- **Components**:
  - **Buttons**: Square edges or subtle 2px rounding. Solid obsidian or outlined gold.
  - **Dividers**: 0.5px hairline (`border-stone-200`).
  - **Transitions**: Ease-in-out, 300ms.

## Tailwind Mapping
- `bg-parchment`: `#F9F7F2`
- `text-obsidian`: `#1A1A1A`
- `text-gold`: `#C5A059`
- `border-stone-200`: `rgba(229, 224, 213, 0.5)`
