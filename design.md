# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand Direction
Velmora uses a warm editorial luxury direction: soft porcelain backgrounds, espresso typography, refined champagne accents, thin dividers, and generous spacing. The site should feel premium, intimate, and calm.

## Color System
- Page background: `bg-stone-50`
- Elevated surfaces: `bg-white`, `bg-stone-100`
- Primary text: `text-stone-900`
- Secondary text: `text-stone-600`
- Subtle dividers: `border-stone-200`
- Dark sections / footer / overlays: `bg-neutral-950`, `text-stone-50`
- Accent highlights and premium CTA moments: `bg-amber-200`, `text-stone-900`, `border-amber-300`
- Accent text on light surfaces: `text-amber-700`

## Typography
- Headings, logo, product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body, navigation, UI, pricing: Inter
- Product names should be uppercase with wide built-in tracking such as `tracking-widest`

## Layout & Spacing
- Use large vertical rhythm: `py-16`, `py-20`, `lg:py-28`
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl`
- Horizontal padding: `px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-10`
- Keep whitespace generous and avoid crowded cards

## Components
- Buttons feel premium: either `bg-stone-900 text-stone-50` or `bg-amber-200 text-stone-900`
- Borders stay thin and elegant with `border border-stone-200`
- Cards use soft depth only: `shadow-sm`, `shadow-stone-200/40`
- Use rounded corners sparingly; prefer `rounded-full` for pills and `rounded-3xl` for large imagery or blocks

## Motion
- Hover transitions should be subtle: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`
- Use gentle scale or opacity changes only; avoid playful or bouncy motion

## Do
- Keep text contrast strong on every surface
- Let jewelry imagery feel warm, close, and editorial
- Use serif headlines with compact sans-serif supporting copy
- Preserve a premium, quiet tone in all UI labels

## Don't
- Do not use discount styling, loud gradients, neon accents, or oversized badges
- Do not use dense layouts or heavy shadows
- Do not use low-contrast gold text on light backgrounds
- Do not mix multiple visual directions on the same page
