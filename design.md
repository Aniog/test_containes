# ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style

*   **Vibe:** Elegant, authoritative, industrial yet modern, user-friendly, professional.
*   **Colors:**
    *   Primary: Deep Industrial Blue (`#1A365D`) - Gives a solid, reliable feel.
    *   Secondary: Steel Gray (`#718096`) - Neutral, metallic.
    *   Accent: Safety Orange / Amber (`#F6AD55` or `#ED8936`) - Adds energy, highlights calls to action, common in machinery.
    *   Background: Light Gray/White (`#F7FAFC` / `#FFFFFF`) - Clean, modern canvas.
    *   Text: Dark Slate (`#2D3748`) - High contrast readability.
*   **Typography:**
    *   Headings: Robust, clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter, correctly weighted). Focus on `font-bold` for impact.
    *   Body: Inter or system-ui, `text-gray-700` or `text-slate-800` for clear readability.
*   **Spacing:** Generous padding, structured layout to make complex machinery feel approachable and not overwhelming.
*   **Borders & Radius:** Slight rounding (`rounded-md` or `rounded-lg`) to soften the industrial edge and enhance the "user-friendly" feel. Subtle borders (`border-gray-200`) and shadows (`shadow-sm`, `shadow-md`) for depth.

## Do's
*   Use high contrast text on backgrounds.
*   Use the accent color sparingly for actual actions (buttons, key links).
*   Provide clear visual hierarchy with typography sizes.
*   Use cards for product listings.

## Don'ts
*   Don't use overly raw or sketchy industrial fonts (keep it professional).
*   Avoid cluttering pages with too much technical text without whitespace.
*   Don't overuse the accent orange; it should draw the eye, not overwhelm it.
