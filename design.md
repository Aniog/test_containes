# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, elevated and restrained. The storefront should feel like a boutique jewelry campaign rather than a discount marketplace.

## Palette
Use one consistent direction across the whole site:
- Base background: deep espresso `#1B1512`
- Elevated dark surface: warm plum espresso `#231C18`
- Light editorial surface: soft pearl `#F6F1EA`
- Warm accent: champagne gold `#C8A56A`
- Accent highlight: pale gold `#E7C998`
- Dark text on light surfaces: espresso ink `#2C211B`
- Light text on dark surfaces: ivory `#F7F1EA`
- Muted copy: taupe `#A89B8C`
- Dividers: translucent ivory on dark, translucent ink on light

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body, labels, navigation, UI: Inter
- Product names should be uppercase with generous letter spacing

## Spacing and layout
- Generous vertical spacing between sections
- Full-bleed hero with layered overlay
- Thin hairline borders and dividers
- Rounded corners should feel soft and premium, never bubbly
- Desktop layouts should feel editorial and spacious; mobile should remain compact but airy

## Components
- Buttons: premium, slightly tall, refined borders, soft hover lift
- Cards: light surfaces for products, dark surfaces for immersive brand sections
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid blurred dark background on scroll
- Drawer and overlays: dark glass treatment with clear text contrast

## Imagery
- Warm-lit jewelry photography
- Editorial framing, skin-close crops, soft neutral backgrounds, gold-forward styling
- Avoid loud props, bright sale colors, or overly saturated scenes

## Do
- Keep typography elegant and contrast strong
- Use restrained animation and soft shadows
- Make all body copy clearly readable on every surface

## Don’t
- Do not use flashy sale aesthetics
- Do not mix multiple accent colors
- Do not use low-contrast gold-on-beige text treatments
