# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand Direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury: warm, editorial, feminine, premium, and calm. The experience must feel elevated and never promotional, loud, or discount-driven.

## Palette
Use one consistent direction sitewide:
- Background base: deep espresso brown for hero, footer, and premium surfaces. Tailwind example: `bg-stone-950`.
- Soft canvas: warm ivory for primary page backgrounds. Tailwind example: `bg-stone-50`.
- Secondary surface: soft beige/champagne cards. Tailwind example: `bg-stone-100`.
- Accent metallic: muted gold for buttons, borders, badges, and highlights. Tailwind example: `bg-amber-200`, `text-amber-200`, `border-amber-200/60`.
- Primary text on light surfaces: rich espresso. Tailwind example: `text-stone-900`.
- Primary text on dark surfaces: warm ivory. Tailwind example: `text-stone-50`.
- Muted copy: warm gray. Tailwind example: `text-stone-500` or `text-stone-600`.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: elegant serif. Use Google Fonts `Cormorant Garamond`.
- Body and UI: clean sans-serif. Use Google Fonts `Inter`.
- Product names must be uppercase with wide letter-spacing. Tailwind example: `uppercase tracking-[0.28em]`.
- Hero and editorial headlines should feel spacious. Tailwind example: `font-serif text-5xl md:text-7xl leading-none`.

## Layout & Spacing
- Generous whitespace. Prefer roomy vertical padding such as `py-16 md:py-24`.
- Content width should feel premium and balanced. Tailwind example: `mx-auto w-full max-w-7xl px-5 md:px-8`.
- Use thin dividers only. Tailwind example: `border-stone-200` or `border-white/15`.
- Desktop layouts should remain editorial and multi-column; do not collapse everything into a mobile-like single column on large screens.

## Components
- Buttons: premium and restrained. Prefer pill or softly rounded buttons with subtle hover transitions. Tailwind example: `rounded-full px-6 py-3 transition duration-300`.
- Cards: clean surfaces, hairline borders, light shadows, visible readable text. Tailwind example: `border border-stone-200 bg-stone-50 shadow-[0_20px_60px_rgba(28,25,23,0.06)]`.
- Nav: transparent over hero, then solid dark with blur on scroll.
- Inputs: understated, elegant, thin border, generous padding. Never use low-contrast placeholder text.

## Imagery
- Images should feature warm-lit gold jewelry on skin, deep neutral backgrounds, or soft editorial still-life styling.
- Avoid overly bright, generic, or mass-market e-commerce visuals.
- Use large imagery with soft overlays where needed for text readability.

## Motion
- Keep animations subtle and smooth. Prefer opacity, slight translate, soft scale, and border-color transitions.
- Avoid bouncy or flashy motion.

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text always clearly readable.
- Maintain a calm, premium rhythm with consistent spacing.
- Use muted gold accents sparingly but confidently.

## Don't
- Do not use neon, saturated pinks, harsh blacks, or discount-red sale styling.
- Do not overcrowd sections.
- Do not use invisible or low-contrast text on cards, images, forms, or buttons.
