# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- Name: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- Industry: Industrial sheet metal folding machines
- Tone: Professional, precise, trustworthy, elegant

## Color Palette
- Primary: #1e293b (slate-800) - Deep professional navy
- Primary accent: #c9a227 (gold) - Industrial elegance
- Secondary: #475569 (slate-600) - Supporting text
- Background: #f8fafc (slate-50) - Clean light background
- Surface: #ffffff - Cards and sections
- Text primary: #0f172a (slate-900) - Headings
- Text secondary: #64748b (slate-500) - Body text
- Border: #e2e8f0 (slate-200) - Subtle dividers
- Hero overlay: rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.75) - Dark overlay for hero images

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-weight 700-800, tight letter-spacing
- Body: font-weight 400, line-height 1.6
- Small text: font-weight 500, uppercase for labels

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 (80px) on desktop, py-12 on mobile
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl (1280px)
- Card padding: p-6 to p-8
- Gap between elements: gap-6 to gap-8

## Components
- Buttons: Rounded-lg, px-6 py-3, gold accent on hover
- Cards: Rounded-xl, subtle shadow, white background
- Navigation: Fixed top, white background with subtle shadow on scroll
- Hero: Full-width with dark overlay, large heading, gold CTA

## Visual Style
- Clean lines and geometric shapes
- Subtle gradients (slate-50 to white)
- Gold accent lines for section dividers
- Professional photography of machinery
