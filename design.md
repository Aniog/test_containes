# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Colors
- **Primary (Cream/Neutral)**: `#FDFCF8` (bg-primary)
- **Secondary (Charcoal/Text)**: `#1A1A1A` (text-primary)
- **Accent (Gold/Metallic)**: `#C5A059` (brand-gold)
- **Muted (Soft Sand)**: `#F5F2EA` (bg-secondary)
- **Border**: `#E5E5E5` (border-light)

## Typography
- **Headings/Serif**: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif
- **Body/UI**: 'Inter', sans-serif

## UI Styles
- **Buttons**:
  - `solid`: `bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white hover:bg-opacity-90 transition-all px-8 py-3 text-sm uppercase tracking-widest`
  - `ghost`: `border border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A] hover:bg-[#1A1A1A] hover:text-white transition-all px-8 py-3 text-sm uppercase tracking-widest`
- **Inputs**: `border-b border-[#E5E5E5] focus:border-[#1A1A1A] outline-none py-2 transition-all`
- **Dividers**: `h-[1px] w-full bg-[#E5E5E5]`
- **Product Cards**: `group relative overflow-hidden bg-white`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace.
- Use thin hairline dividers.
- UPPERCASE for product names and CTA's with tracking-widest.
- Soft, editorial-style shadows.

## Don'ts
- No loud or bright colors.
- No heavy borders.
- No rounded corners on buttons (keep them sharp/classic).
- No discount banners (keep it subtle).
