# Visual Style Guide - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Colors
- **Primary (Gold Accent)**: `#D4AF37` (Metallic Gold)
- **Background (Editorial White)**: `#FDFCFB` (Warm white/cream)
- **Surface (Dark Base)**: `#1C1C1C` (Deep refined charcoal/black)
- **Text Primary**: `#1C1C1C`
- **Text Secondary**: `#6B6B6B`
- **Stone/Neutral**: `#F5F2F0`

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. Thin to Medium weights. 
  - Usage: `font-serif tracking-tight`
  - Product Names: `font-serif uppercase tracking-[0.2em]`
- **Body & UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif.
  - Usage: `font-sans`

## UI Elements
- **Dividers**: hairline width, low opacity. `border-stone-200/60`
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid dark background, white text, uppercase, tracked. `bg-stone-900 text-white px-8 py-3 text-sm tracking-widest uppercase`
  - Secondary/Outlined: Outlined dark or gold. `border border-stone-900 px-8 py-3 text-sm tracking-widest uppercase`
- **Inputs**: Minimal, bottom border or light outline.
- **Transitions**: Ease-in-out, 300ms–500ms for hover and page changes.

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace. `py-20` for sections, `gap-8` for grids.
