# Velmora Fine Jewelry Visual System

## Direction
Quiet luxury with a warm editorial feel. The storefront should feel like a premium demi-fine jewelry lookbook rather than a discount e-commerce template.

## Palette
Use one consistent palette that flatters gold jewelry:
- `velmora-ink` (`#211B18`): primary text and deep surfaces.
- `velmora-espresso` (`#342821`): refined dark panels and footer.
- `velmora-cream` (`#F7F1E8`): main background.
- `velmora-parchment` (`#EFE3D2`): soft section backgrounds.
- `velmora-champagne` (`#D6B47A`): metallic accent, buttons, focus rings.
- `velmora-brass` (`#9C713B`): darker accent for outlines and small UI.
- `velmora-rose` (`#C9A99A`): restrained warm highlight.
- `velmora-mist` (`#FBF8F3`): card and input surfaces.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, high contrast, generous line-height.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names: uppercase with wide letter spacing.

## Layout and spacing
- Generous whitespace, editorial section spacing, max-width containers on content-heavy sections.
- Full-bleed hero and large image tiles.
- Mobile-first layouts, then 2–4 column grids on tablet and desktop.
- Use thin hairline dividers with `border-velmora-ink/10` or `border-velmora-champagne/30`.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid champagne with ink text, or transparent outlined ink/champagne. Slight hover lift and soft shadow.
- Cards: warm mist surfaces, subtle shadows, thin borders, image-forward composition.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid cream on scroll with clear contrast.
- Cart drawer: cream/mist panel, readable dark foreground text, clear quantity controls.

## Do's
- Keep imagery warm, tactile, close-up, gold-focused.
- Use restrained transitions: opacity, transform, shadow.
- Ensure text contrast is always strong on image overlays and colored panels.

## Don'ts
- Do not use loud sale colors, generic blue CTAs, or discount badges.
- Do not overcrowd mobile layouts.
- Do not use arbitrary one-off colors outside this palette.
