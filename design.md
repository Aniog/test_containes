# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

## Color Palette
- Background: warm ivory (#F9F7F4) — hsl(40 20% 97%)
- Foreground: deep charcoal (#2C2420) — hsl(25 15% 15%)
- Primary (gold accent): warm gold (#A67C52) — hsl(35 45% 42%)
- Secondary: soft warm gray (#E8E4DF) — hsl(30 10% 92%)
- Muted: lighter warm gray (#E5E2DE) — hsl(30 8% 90%)
- Muted foreground: medium warm gray (#7A7269) — hsl(25 10% 45%)
- Border: subtle warm line (#E0DCD7) — hsl(30 10% 88%)
- Card: pure white (#FFFFFF)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weight 300-500
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif), weight 300-500
- Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond

## Spacing
- Section padding: px-6 md:px-12 lg:px-20 py-16 md:py-24
- Generous whitespace throughout

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-border/50
- Subtle, thin lines

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold background, white text, uppercase, tracking-widest
- Outline: gold border, gold text, hover fills with gold
- Padding: px-8 py-3, text-sm

## Shadows
- Soft, subtle shadows on hover
- No heavy drop shadows

## Transitions
- Smooth 300ms transitions on hover states
- Subtle scale and opacity changes

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use warm, inviting imagery
- Maintain consistent gold accent throughout
- Use uppercase for product names with wide letter-spacing

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No heavy borders or thick dividers
- No bright/neon accent colors
