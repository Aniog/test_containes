# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Restrained,
sophisticated, never loud or discount-looking. Generous whitespace, large
editorial imagery, thin hairline dividers, soft shadows, subtle motion.

## Color Palette
A deep refined base with warm metallic gold accents and soft cream neutrals.

- `espresso` (base dark): `#1C1714` — near-black warm brown, used for dark sections, hero overlays, footer.
- `ink` (text dark): `#2A2520` — warm near-black for body text on light surfaces.
- `cream` (page background): `#F7F3EC` — warm off-white, the primary page background.
- `sand` (secondary surface): `#EDE6DA` — slightly deeper warm neutral for cards/strips.
- `gold` (accent): `#B08A4F` — refined warm metallic gold for buttons, links, accents, dividers.
- `gold-soft` (hover/light gold): `#C9A86A` — lighter gold for hover states.
- `stone` (muted text): `#8A7F73` — warm gray for secondary/caption text.
- `hairline` (dividers): `#D9CFC0` — warm hairline border color.

Tailwind tokens: `espresso`, `ink`, `cream`, `sand`, `gold`, `gold-soft`, `stone`, `hairline`.

## Typography
- Headings & product names: **Cormorant Garamond** (serif), weights 400–600.
- Body & UI: **Inter** (sans-serif), weights 300–600.
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.18em]`).
- Section eyebrows: UPPERCASE, small, wide tracking, gold or stone color.
- Hero headline: large serif, tight leading.

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous vertical rhythm: section padding `py-20 md:py-28`.
- Max content width `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 md:px-10`.
- Hairline dividers: `border-hairline` / `border-t`.

## Buttons
- Primary (accent): solid `bg-gold text-cream`, hover `bg-gold-soft`, uppercase
  tracking-wide, `px-8 py-3.5`, subtle transition.
- Outlined: `border border-ink text-ink`, hover `bg-ink text-cream`.
- Pill variant buttons for selectors: `rounded-full border`.

## Imagery
- Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds.
- Large editorial hero (full-bleed).
- Product cards: 4:3 or 1:1, hover reveals second image.
- UGC reels: vertical 9:16 cards.

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names.
- Keep accent gold restrained — buttons, small details, eyebrows.
- Use hairline dividers between sections.
- Ensure strong contrast: dark text on cream, cream text on espresso.

## Don'ts
- No bright/saturated colors. No pure black or pure white.
- No heavy shadows; use soft, subtle shadows only.
- No rounded chunky buttons; keep refined.
- No generic e-commerce clutter; maintain editorial whitespace.
