# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry for women 25–45.

## Color Palette
- Background: warm ivory `#FAF7F2`
- Surface: white `#FFFFFF`
- Primary text: near-black `#1A1A1A`
- Secondary text: warm gray `#7A7570`
- Accent gold: refined muted gold `#B8944E`
- Dark background: deep warm `#1C1814`
- Border/divider: `#E8E2D9`
- Muted gold light: `#E8DCC8`
- Star/rating gold: `#D4A853`

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display, serif
- Body/UI: Inter, sans-serif
- Product names: UPPERCASE, tracking-wider (0.05em)

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Thin hairline dividers (border-t border-[#E8E2D9])
- 16px base padding on mobile, 24px+ on desktop

## Visual Style
- Soft shadows on cards (shadow-sm, shadow-md)
- Subtle hover transitions (transition-all duration-300)
- Hover: scale-[1.02] on product cards, opacity changes
- Buttons: solid accent gold or outlined with gold border
- Images: warm-toned, editorial style, dark/neutral backgrounds
- Thin, elegant borders

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use uppercase with tracking-wider for product names
- Use warm ivory backgrounds for section contrast
- Use hairline dividers between sections
- Keep UI minimal and uncluttered

## Don'ts
- No bright/flashy colors
- No heavy borders or thick lines
- No loud sale/discount badges
- No generic e-commerce patterns
