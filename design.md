# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand Identity
- Industrial precision meets elegant presentation
- Professional, trustworthy, modern
- User-friendly navigation with clear hierarchy

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Navy):** `navy-900` (#0f172a), `navy-800` (#1e293b), `navy-700` (#334155)
- **Accent (Brass/Gold):** `brass-500` (#b8860b), `brass-400` (#d4a017), `brass-300` (#e6be44)
- **Neutral:** `slate-50` (#f8fafc), `slate-100` (#f1f5f9), `slate-200` (#e2e8f0), `slate-600` (#475569), `slate-800` (#1e293b)
- **White:** #ffffff for cards and content areas
- **Background:** slate-50 for page, white for cards

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (headings + body)
- **Headings:** font-bold, tracking-tight
  - H1: text-5xl lg:text-6xl
  - H2: text-3xl lg:text-4xl
  - H3: text-xl lg:text-2xl
- **Body:** text-base, text-slate-600
- **Small/Caption:** text-sm, text-slate-500

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 lg:py-28
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 lg:p-8
- Component gaps: gap-6 or gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-2xl, shadow-sm, border border-slate-200/60
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Hover cards: shadow-md transition-shadow
- No harsh borders; prefer subtle dividers

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-brass-500 text-white hover:bg-brass-400 rounded-lg px-6 py-3 font-medium
- Secondary: border border-slate-300 text-slate-800 hover:bg-slate-100 rounded-lg px-6 py-3
- Ghost: text-brass-500 hover:text-brass-400 font-medium

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Keep text readable with max-w-2xl or max-w-3xl on paragraphs
- Use subtle gradients (from-navy-900 to-navy-800) for dark sections
- Use brass/gold accents sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Maintain clear visual hierarchy

## Don'ts
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors
- Don't crowd content; always leave breathing room
- Don't use pure black text; prefer slate-800 or slate-900
- Don't use small font sizes for important content
- Don't use harsh shadows or thick borders
