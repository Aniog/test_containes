# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
Industrial precision meets elegant design. ARTITECT MACHINERY manufactures premium double folding machines and sheet metal folding equipment.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Dark**: `#0f172a` (Deep navy - backgrounds, headers)
- **Primary Mid**: `#1e293b` (Slate 800 - card backgrounds)
- **Accent Gold**: `#c8a24d` (Warm gold - CTAs, highlights, borders)
- **Accent Gold Light**: `#dbb968` (Hover states)
- **Surface Light**: `#f1f5f9` (Light backgrounds)
- **Surface White**: `#ffffff` (Cards, sections)
- **Text Primary**: `#0f172a` (Main headings)
- **Text Secondary**: `#475569` (Body text)
- **Text Light**: `#f8fafc` (Text on dark backgrounds)
- **Steel Gray**: `#94a3b8` (Subtle elements, dividers)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, 700/800 weight, tight letter-spacing (-0.02em)
- **Body**: Inter, 400/500 weight
- **Labels/Badges**: Inter, 600 weight, uppercase tracking (0.08em)

## Layout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: 6rem vertical, 2rem horizontal
- Cards: Subtle shadow, 1rem border-radius
- Consistent 8px spacing grid

## Visual Style
- Clean, minimal, professional
- Dark hero sections with gold accents for contrast
- Light sections with strong typography
- Subtle animations on scroll (fade-in, slide-up)
- Industrial photography feel through stock images
- Sharp geometric shapes, no rounded corners on primary buttons
- Thin gold border accents on feature cards

## Do's
- Use strong contrast between text and backgrounds
- Keep whitespace generous
- Use gold accent sparingly for maximum impact
- Maintain consistent spacing rhythm

## Don'ts
- Avoid bright saturated colors
- Don't use playful or casual design elements
- Avoid excessive animations
- Don't use rounded pill-shaped buttons
