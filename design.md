# Design System - SSourcing China

## Visual Style
The visual style is professional, trustworthy, and international B2B. It uses a clean layout with plenty of white space, sharp edges for a corporate feel, and realistic imagery of logistics, factories, and quality control.

## Colors
- **Primary**: Deep Navy (#0F172A) - `bg-slate-900`, `text-slate-900`
- **Secondary**: Industrial Blue (#3B82F6) - `bg-blue-500`, `text-blue-500`
- **Accent**: Safety Orange (#F97316) - `bg-orange-500`, `text-orange-500` (Use sparingly for CTAs)
- **Background**: Soft Gray (#F8FAFC) - `bg-slate-50`
- **Text**: 
  - Main: (#1E293B) - `text-slate-800`
  - Muted: (#64748B) - `text-slate-500`

## Typography
- **Font Family**: 'Inter', sans-serif (via Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: Semi-bold to Bold, slate-900
- **Body**: Regular, slate-800, leading-relaxed

## Components Style
- **Buttons**: Rounded-md, transitioning from deep navy to slightly lighter blue or industrial blue. CTAs in orange.
- **Cards**: Minimal border (slate-200), subtle shadow on hover, white background.
- **Inputs**: slate-200 borders, focus: ring-blue-500.

## Spacing
- Standard Tailwind spacing (p-4, m-8, gap-6, etc.)
- Large section padding (py-16 or py-24) for a premium feel.

## Do's
- Use realistic, high-quality images of factories, shipping containers, and QC inspections.
- Keep text clear, concise, and professional.
- Use iconography to break up large blocks of text.

## Don'ts
- Avoid bright, neon colors.
- Avoid cluttered layouts.
- Avoid vague or exaggerated marketing claims.
