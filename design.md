# Velmora Fine Jewelry ŌĆö Design System

## Brand Direction
Quiet luxury. Warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- `velmora-obsidian`: #1A1614 ŌĆö Deep warm black (primary background, nav solid)
- `velmora-ivory`: #FAF7F2 ŌĆö Warm off-white (page background, cards)
- `velmora-champagne`: #C9A96E ŌĆö Warm gold accent (CTAs, highlights, borders)
- `velmora-blush`: #E8DDD4 ŌĆö Soft warm blush (secondary backgrounds, trust bar)
- `velmora-stone`: #8C7B6E ŌĆö Warm mid-tone (body text on light)
- `velmora-linen`: #F2EDE6 ŌĆö Warm linen (section alternates)

### Text Colors
- Headings on dark: `velmora-ivory` (#FAF7F2)
- Headings on light: `velmora-obsidian` (#1A1614)
- Body on light: `velmora-stone` (#8C7B6E)
- Accent text: `velmora-champagne` (#C9A96E)

## Typography

### Fonts
- **Serif (headings, product names, logo)**: Cormorant Garamond ŌĆö weights 300, 400, 500, 600
- **Sans-serif (body, UI, nav links)**: Manrope ŌĆö weights 300, 400, 500, 600

### Scale
- Hero headline: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-cormorant font-light tracking-wide`
- Section title: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-cormorant font-light`
- Product name: `text-xl font-cormorant uppercase tracking-widest`
- Nav links: `text-xs font-manrope uppercase tracking-widest`
- Body: `text-sm font-manrope font-light leading-relaxed`
- Price: `text-lg font-manrope font-medium`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 md:px-12`
- Card gap: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline divider: `border-t border-velmora-blush`
- Card border: `border border-velmora-blush/50`
- Accent border: `border border-velmora-champagne`

## Shadows
- Card hover: `shadow-lg shadow-velmora-obsidian/10`
- Drawer: `shadow-2xl shadow-velmora-obsidian/20`

## Buttons
- Primary (CTA): `bg-velmora-champagne text-velmora-obsidian px-8 py-3 text-xs font-manrope uppercase tracking-widest hover:bg-velmora-obsidian hover:text-velmora-ivory transition-all duration-300`
- Outlined: `border border-velmora-champagne text-velmora-champagne px-8 py-3 text-xs font-manrope uppercase tracking-widest hover:bg-velmora-champagne hover:text-velmora-obsidian transition-all duration-300`
- Ghost: `text-velmora-stone text-xs font-manrope uppercase tracking-widest hover:text-velmora-champagne transition-colors duration-200`

## Animations
- Hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`
- Image zoom: `hover:scale-105 transition-transform duration-500`
- Fade in: `opacity-0 animate-fadeIn`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Large editorial imagery (full-bleed or half-page)
- Thin hairline dividers between sections
- UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing for product names and nav
- Warm gold (#C9A96E) as the single accent color
- Serif for all display text, sans-serif for all functional text

## Don'ts
- No bright/saturated colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded corners on buttons (sharp edges feel more premium)
- No generic stock-photo vibes ŌĆö warm, editorial, intimate
- No more than 2 font families
