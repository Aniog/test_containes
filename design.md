# FIFA World Cup 2026 Design System

## Theme
Sports / Football / World Cup 2026 (USA, Canada, Mexico)
Dark, energetic, premium feel with bold typography and vivid accent colors.

## Color Palette
- Background primary: `#0a0e1a` (deep navy black)
- Background secondary: `#111827` (dark slate)
- Background card: `#1a2235` (dark blue-gray)
- Accent primary: `#e8b84b` (FIFA gold)
- Accent secondary: `#c0392b` (vibrant red)
- Accent tertiary: `#2563eb` (electric blue)
- Text primary: `#f1f5f9` (near white)
- Text secondary: `#94a3b8` (muted slate)
- Text muted: `#64748b`
- Border: `#1e293b`
- Success: `#22c55e`
- Danger: `#ef4444`

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold to font-extrabold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, leading-relaxed
- Labels/badges: font-semibold, uppercase, tracking-wider, text-xs

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 px-4` (mobile), `py-24 px-8` (desktop)
- Card padding: `p-4` (mobile), `p-6` (desktop)
- Gap between cards: `gap-4` (mobile), `gap-6` (desktop)

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-[#1e293b]`
- Card radius: `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`
- Card shadow: `shadow-lg shadow-black/40`
- Hover: `hover:border-[#e8b84b]/50 hover:shadow-[#e8b84b]/10`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-[#e8b84b] text-[#0a0e1a] font-bold rounded-lg px-6 py-3 hover:bg-yellow-400`
- Secondary: `border border-[#e8b84b] text-[#e8b84b] rounded-lg px-6 py-3 hover:bg-[#e8b84b]/10`
- Ghost: `text-[#94a3b8] hover:text-white`

## Navigation
- Sticky top nav, dark background with blur
- Logo left, links right
- Active link: gold underline or gold text

## Cards
- Dark background `bg-[#1a2235]`
- Gold accent border on hover
- Rounded corners `rounded-xl`
- Subtle shadow

## Badges / Tags
- Uppercase, small, rounded-full
- Live: `bg-red-600 text-white`
- Upcoming: `bg-blue-600 text-white`
- Finished: `bg-gray-600 text-white`
- Group stage: `bg-[#e8b84b]/20 text-[#e8b84b]`

## Do's
- Use gold (#e8b84b) as the primary accent for highlights, scores, and CTAs
- Use dark backgrounds for all cards and sections
- Use bold, large typography for scores and key stats
- Use flag emojis for team identification
- Maintain high contrast between text and backgrounds

## Don'ts
- Don't use light backgrounds (white/light gray) for main content areas
- Don't use low-contrast text on dark backgrounds
- Don't use more than 3 accent colors in one section
- Don't use small font sizes for scores or key data
