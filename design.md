# Visual Design Guidelines

## Colors
- **Primary:** Blue 600 (`#2563eb`)
- **Primary Hover:** Blue 700 (`#1d4ed8`)
- **Backgrounds:** White (`bg-white`), Slate 50 (`bg-slate-50`), Slate 900 (`bg-slate-900` for hero)
- **Text:** Slate 900 (`text-slate-900`) for headings, Slate 600 (`text-slate-600`) for body text
- **Accents:** Gradients matching `from-blue-400 to-cyan-300`

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter Sans-Serif (`font-sans`)
- **Headings:** Bold to Extrabold, tight tracking (`tracking-tight`)
- **Body Text:** Regular weight, relaxed line height (`leading-relaxed`)

## Layout & Components
- **Containers:** Max-width 6xl (`max-w-6xl`) centered, with standard padding.
- **Borders & Radii:** Rounded Large, XL, and 2XL for components to feel modern and soft. Slate 200/100 borders.
- **Shadows:** Soft shadows, specific colored shadows for primary buttons.
- **Interactions:** Transition-all over 150-200ms (`transition-all`).
