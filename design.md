# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Personality
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: warm off-white (#f7f5f2 / velvet-50). Cards: pure white.
- Text: deep warm brown-black (#2d241c / velvet-950 or #1b1f30 / midnight-900).
- Accent: warm gold (#d4941a / gold-500) for CTAs, links, highlights.
- Muted text: warm taupe (#a8977a / velvet-400 or #8690ab / midnight-300).
- Borders: soft beige (#d9d1c3 / velvet-200).
- Dark sections: deep midnight (#1b1f30 / midnight-900 or #10131f / midnight-950).
- Trust bar / hero overlay: near-black with warm undertone.

## Typography
- Headings / product names: Cormorant Garamond (serif), tracking-wider or tracking-widest.
- Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest).
- Body / UI: Inter (sans-serif), clean, lightweight.
- Sizes: serif headings at 3xl to 4xl on desktop, smaller on mobile. Body at text-sm.

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace. Section padding: py-16 lg:py-24.
- Product grids: gap-4 lg:gap-6.
- Max content width: max-w-7xl.

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers (hairline class: 1px, hsl(33 18% 86%)).
- Subtle borders on cards: border-velvet-100.
- No heavy box-shadows. Soft shadows only where needed (shadow-sm on scrolled nav).

## Buttons & CTA
- Primary: bg-gold-500 text-white, tracking-widest uppercase, hover:bg-gold-600.
- Secondary (outlined): border-midnight-900, text-midnight-900, uppercase.
- Quick-add on product hover: bg-white/90 backdrop-blur-sm, text-[10px].

## Interactive States
- Hover transitions: duration-300 or duration-400, ease-out.
- Product image hover: scale(1.05) over 600ms.
- Nav link hover: text-gold-600.
- Category tile: overlay goes from bg-black/20 to bg-black/40, label fades in from translate-y-4.

## Layout
- Mobile-first responsive. Breakpoints: lg (1024px) for desktop.
- Single-column on mobile, multi-column grid on desktop.
- Sticky nav: transparent over hero, bg-white/95 backdrop-blur-md on scroll.

## Do's
- Serif for emotional/editorial moments (headlines, product names, quotes).
- UPPERCASE + wide tracking for product names.
- Warm tones that complement gold jewelry.
- Clean, generous whitespace.
- Thin dividers.

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors.
- No heavy borders or harsh shadows.
- No discount-store aesthetic (avoid red sale tags, heavy badges).
- No generic sans-serif for headings.
- No text that blends into backgrounds.
