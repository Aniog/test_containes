# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry.
NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Colors
- Base: cream (#faf8f5) — warm off-white background
- Deep: noir (#0d0b0a) — nearly black, for text and primary buttons
- Accent: sand-500 (#d9a265) — warm gold, sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Neutrals: warmgray scale (50–950) — for dividers, secondary text, muted surfaces
- Surfaces: velvet-50 (#faf8f6), velvet-100 (#f5f0eb) — subtle warm card backgrounds

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) — elegant, editorial
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern
- Product names: uppercase, tracking-wider (0.15em), serif
- Body text: 15–16px, leading-relaxed

## Spacing
- Section vertical padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Generous whitespace, thin hairline dividers (border-warmgray-200)
- Cards: subtle shadow, rounded-none (no rounded corners for editorial feel)

## Components
- Buttons: uppercase, tracking-wider, 14px font, solid noir or outlined noir
- Product cards: hover reveals second image, quick add-to-cart overlay
- Dividers: hairline, warmgray-200

## Do's
- Use cream/noir/sand palette consistently
- Serif headings, sans body
- Uppercase product names with wide letter-spacing
- Generous whitespace
- Subtle hover transitions (300ms)

## Don'ts
- No rounded pill buttons (use subtle rounded or none)
- No bright colors outside the palette
- No generic e-commerce badges (SALE, discount percentages)
- No heavy box shadows
