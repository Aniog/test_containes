# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand
ARTITECT MACHINERY manufactures premium sheet metal folding machinery. The brand voice is elegant, precise, and trustworthy — reflecting German/Japanese engineering sensibilities with a warm, approachable feel.

## Colors
- **Primary (Slate/Charcoal)**: `slate-900` (#0f172a) — headers, primary backgrounds
- **Secondary (Warm Gold/Amber)**: `amber-500` (#f59e0b) — accents, CTAs, highlights
- **Surface**: White (#ffffff) for cards, light sections
- **Surface Alt**: `slate-50` (#f8fafc) — alternating sections
- **Text Primary**: `slate-900` (#0f172a)
- **Text Secondary**: `slate-600` (#475569)
- **Text Muted**: `slate-400` (#94a3b8)

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts) — clean, modern, highly readable
- Headings: font-semibold or font-bold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, leading-relaxed
- Sizes: hero h1 at text-5xl md:text-6xl, section h2 at text-3xl md:text-4xl

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between grid items: gap-8

## Visual Style
- Clean, minimal with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows on cards: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
- Rounded corners: rounded-xl for cards, rounded-lg for buttons
- Thin borders: border border-slate-200
- Amber/gold accent lines and hover states
- Use geometric patterns sparingly (thin lines, subtle)

## Do's
- Use ample whitespace for breathing room
- Pair bold headings with lighter body text
- Use amber/gold sparingly for emphasis
- Keep imagery clean and industrial
- Use consistent card layouts

## Don'ts
- Avoid cluttered layouts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No decorative fonts
- No busy backgrounds
