# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand Identity
ARTITECT MACHINERY manufactures precision sheet metal folding machines. The brand combines industrial engineering excellence with refined, elegant presentation.

## Typography
- **Display/Headings**: Playfair Display — elegant, sophisticated serif for brand statements and headings
- **Body**: Inter — clean, modern sans-serif for readability
- Headings use `font-display`, body uses `font-body`

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Deep Navy/Slate)**: brand-800 (#1e2a40) through brand-950 (#0a0f1a) — authority, trust
- **Accent (Burnished Copper)**: accent-500 (#d97a24) — warmth, precision, industrial heat
- **Background**: brand-50 (#f4f6f9) — soft, clean, airy
- **Surface/Cards**: white — contrast and clarity
- **Text**: brand-900 (#111a2c) — strong readability
- **Text Muted**: brand-400 (#6c80a2) — secondary information

## Visual Style
- Clean, generous whitespace — elegant breathing room
- Subtle borders and shadows for depth (never heavy)
- Rounded corners: `rounded-lg` (8px) for cards, `rounded-md` (6px) for buttons
- Icons: Lucide React with subtle accent colors
- Gradients: subtle dark-to-darker navy for hero, warm copper-to-amber for CTAs

## Do's
- Use generous padding on sections (py-20 or py-24)
- Let photography/product images dominate cards
- Keep text concise and impactful
- Use the copper accent sparingly for CTAs and key highlights
- Max-width container at 1280px (max-w-7xl)

## Don'ts
- Don't use pure black text — always brand-900 or brand-800
- Don't overcrowd sections — maintain visual breathing room
- Don't use bright/clashing colors
- Don't use heavy box shadows — prefer `shadow-sm` or `shadow-md`
- Don't use uppercase for body text
