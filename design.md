# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, editorial, warm, minimalist.
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif. (Regular 400, Medium 500)
  - Body/UI: 'Manrope', sans-serif. (Light 300, Regular 400, Semibold 600)
  - Product Names: Uppercase with letter-spacing 0.1em.

## Color Palette (Tailwind Classes)
- **Background (Primary)**: `bg-[#FAF9F6]` (Alabaster)
- **Text (Primary)**: `text-[#1A1A1A]` (Off-black)
- **Gold/Accent**: `text-[#B69750]` / `bg-[#B69750]`
- **Muted Background**: `bg-[#E5E1DA]` (Stone)
- **Border**: `border-[#E5E1DA]` / `border-[#1A1A1A]/10`

## Spacing & Components
- **Whitespace**: Generous (section padding py-20 or py-32).
- **Dividers**: Thin hairlines (border-[0.5px]).
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid black or gold, uppercase, sans-serif, letter-spacing-widest.
  - Secondary: Outlined, thin.
- **Transitions**: Ease-in-out, 400ms durations for soft feel.
- **Shadows**: Very soft or none (prefers borders and whitespace).
