# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

Velmora uses a quiet luxury editorial direction: warm ivory surfaces, deep espresso text, restrained antique gold accents, and generous negative space. The visual mood should feel premium and intimate, never loud or discount-oriented.

## Palette
- Porcelain: warm page background, `bg-velmora-porcelain`.
- Pearl: elevated card and input background, `bg-velmora-pearl`.
- Ink: primary readable text, `text-velmora-ink`.
- Espresso: refined dark sections, `bg-velmora-espresso` with `text-velmora-pearl`.
- Gold: restrained metallic accent for buttons, dividers, icons, and hover states, `bg-velmora-gold`, `text-velmora-gold`, `border-velmora-gold`.
- Sand and Mist: subtle borders and muted panels, `border-velmora-sand`, `bg-velmora-mist`.

## Typography
- Headings and brand marks use Cormorant Garamond via `font-serif`.
- Body and UI use Manrope via `font-sans`.
- Product names are uppercase with wide letter spacing using `uppercase tracking-[0.22em]` or the `.product-title` utility.

## Layout and Spacing
- Mobile-first with spacious sections: `py-16 md:py-24`, `px-5 sm:px-8 lg:px-12`.
- Use max width containers around `max-w-7xl mx-auto`.
- Hairline dividers use `border-velmora-sand` and `border-velmora-gold/30`.

## Components
- Primary buttons: solid gold with espresso text, pill-shaped, subtle hover lift.
- Secondary buttons: outlined ink or gold, transparent backgrounds.
- Cards: pearl background, fine border, soft shadow, image-forward.
- Dark editorial sections: espresso background with readable pearl text.

## Do's
- Use large editorial imagery and quiet overlays.
- Keep transitions subtle: opacity, transform, color.
- Keep all text high contrast against its background.

## Don'ts
- Do not use bright sale colors, loud gradients, heavy shadows, or generic blue links.
- Do not place gold text on light beige backgrounds unless large and legible.
- Do not crowd product cards or mobile layouts.
