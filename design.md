# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Mood
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: #FAF8F5 (warm ivory/paper)
- Text primary: #1A1A1A (almost black)
- Text muted: #6B645C (warm taupe)
- Accent / Gold: #C99A5B (warm antique gold)
- Accent light: #F0E6D5 (pale gold tint)
- Border / Hairline: #E5DCD0 (warm beige)
- Card bg: #FFFFFF (white)
- Footer bg: #1A1A1A (dark)
- Footer text: #E5DCD0 (warm beige)
- Badge/bg: #F5F0E8 (light warm)

## Typography
- Headings / Product names: Cormorant Garamond, serif. UPPERCASE with tracking-widest (0.15em) for product names.
- Body / UI / Nav: Inter, sans-serif. font-light (300) for body, font-medium (500) for nav.
- Hero headline: 4xl-6xl, uppercase, tracking-wide, font-light
- Section titles: 2xl-3xl, uppercase, tracking-wider

## Spacing & Layout
- Page max-width: 1280px, centered
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Generous whitespace
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E5DCD0] (0.5px or 1px)
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm with warm undertones
- Hover transitions: all 0.3s ease

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C99A5B] text-white, hover:bg-[#B8894A], rounded-none, uppercase tracking-widest, px-8 py-3
- Outline: border border-[#C99A5B] text-[#C99A5B], hover:bg-[#C99A5B] hover:text-white
- Transitions: all 0.3s ease

## Image Style
- Editorial photography, warm lighting, dark/neutral backgrounds
- Gold jewelry close-ups on warm skin tones or dark velvet
- Aspect ratios: 1:1 for product cards, 3:4 for hero/full bleed, 9:16 for UGC reels

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use thin, elegant dividers
- Use uppercase + wide letter-spacing for product names
- Use warm gold accent sparingly — let jewelry images be the hero

## Don'ts
- No bright colors, no gradients, no heavy shadows
- No discount-banner styling
- No tight spacing or crowded layouts
- No heavy borders or harsh lines
