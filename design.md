# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Mood & Concept
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry - not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
The color scheme is designed to complement and flatter gold jewelry, focusing on warm neutrals with a deep editorial base and a subtle, sophisticated accent color.

- **Backgrounds:** `#FAFAFA` (Very light warm gray, clean but not harsh white)
- **Secondary Backgrounds:** `#F3EFE9` (Warm beige/alabaster for sections)
- **Text (Primary):** `#1A1817` (Deep charcoal/espresso, almost black for high contrast)
- **Text (Secondary):** `#706C68` (Warm mid-gray for descriptions, metadata)
- **Accent (Buttons/Highlights):** `#8C6239` (Warm amber/bronze, evoking gold without being shiny yellow)
- **Accent Hover:** `#6B4A2B` (Darker bronze)
- **Borders/Lines:** `#E6E1DC` (Subtle warm gray for hairline dividers)

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** Cormorant Garamond (or similar elegant serif). Elegant, editorial feel. Used often in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).
- **Body & UI Elements:** Inter (Clean sans-serif). Used for descriptions, navigation, small metadata.

## Spacing & Structure
- Generous whitespace (`py-24`, `gap-12`)
- Large editorial imagery, full-bleed where appropriate.
- Thin hairline dividers between sections or accordion items.
- Restrained accent color usage.

## Component Styles
- **Buttons:** Solid accent (`bg-amber-700 text-white`) or outlined accent (`border-amber-700 text-amber-700`). Premium feel, no rounded pills, strictly subtle rounded corners (`rounded-sm` or `rounded-none`).
- **Shadows:** Soft, subtle (`shadow-sm` or `shadow-md` softly spread).
- **Hover effects:** Smooth opacity fades or slight upward translations (`transition-all duration-300`).
