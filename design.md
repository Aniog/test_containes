## Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

### Brand Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — not loud, not discount-looking.

### Color Palette
- Base: #1A1512 (warm near-black) — primary background, footer, deep elements
- Cream: #FBF7F2 (warm off-white) — page background
- Sand: #E8DFD5 (warm mid-beige) — secondary backgrounds, cards
- Taupe: #B8A99A (warm darker beige) — subtle text, accents
- Gold: #C49A3C (warm metallic) — accent, buttons, highlights
- Gold-dark: #A07828 — hover states
- White: #FFFDFA — text on dark, cards
- Muted: #8B8076 — body text, secondary
- Text: #2C2621 — primary text
- Border: #D9D0C5 — hairline dividers

### Typography
- Headings / Product Names: Playfair Display (serif), UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing
- Body / UI: Inter (sans-serif)
- Product names: text-xs tracking-extra-wide uppercase
- Section titles: font-serif text-section / text-hero
- Buttons: font-sans text-sm tracking-widest uppercase

### Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 md:px-10

### Visual Rules
- Thin hairline dividers (border-velmora-border)
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- Soft shadows only where needed
- No loud badges, no sale stickers, no discount language
- Product images: warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Buttons: solid gold or outlined, never pill-shaped unless it's a variant selector
