# Velmora Fine Jewelry Visual Design

## Direction
Quiet luxury with a warm editorial feel. The storefront should feel like a premium demi-fine jewelry lookbook, not a discount marketplace.

## Palette
- `velmora-ink` (`#171411`): deep espresso-black base for hero overlays, footer, strong text, and luxury contrast.
- `velmora-cream` (`#F7F1E8`): warm ivory page background.
- `velmora-parchment` (`#EFE4D3`): soft editorial panels and product cards.
- `velmora-champagne` (`#C8A46A`): restrained metallic accent for CTAs, highlights, stars, and hairlines.
- `velmora-bronze` (`#8A623A`): warm hover/accent depth.
- `velmora-mist` (`#D8CDBE`): subtle dividers and borders.
- `velmora-rose` (`#B78973`): gentle secondary warmth for badges and small accents.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names are uppercase with wide letter spacing.

## Layout & Spacing
- Use generous whitespace, wide editorial sections, and thin hairline dividers.
- Product cards should be airy, with soft shadows and quiet hover motion.
- Desktop layouts can be multi-column; mobile should stack naturally with comfortable spacing.

## Components
- Buttons: premium champagne solid buttons with ink text, or hairline outlined buttons with refined hover states.
- Cards: parchment/cream surfaces, explicit ink text, subtle borders using mist.
- Navigation: transparent over the hero, solid cream after scroll or on interior pages.
- Imagery: warm gold jewelry close-ups, model-worn editorial crops, dark/neutral backgrounds.

## Do
- Keep colors restrained and consistent.
- Ensure every foreground/background pairing has strong contrast.
- Use smooth, subtle transitions.

## Don't
- Do not use loud sale colors, heavy gradients, neon tones, or generic marketplace styling.
- Do not use unreadable muted text or low-contrast overlays.
- Do not add arbitrary hex colors directly in JSX classes; use named Tailwind colors.
