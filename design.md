# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury for demi-fine gold jewelry: warm, editorial, premium-but-accessible. The site should feel like an intimate jewelry atelier rather than a discount storefront.

## Palette
- Porcelain: soft warm ivory background (`bg-porcelain`, `#F8F3EA`).
- Pearl: elevated card surface (`bg-pearl`, `#FFFCF6`).
- Espresso: primary text and deep editorial sections (`text-espresso`, `bg-espresso`, `#231A15`).
- Mocha: secondary body text (`text-mocha`, `#6D5C50`).
- Champagne: restrained metallic accent (`bg-champagne`, `text-champagne`, `#B8894A`).
- Champagne dark: hover and strong accent (`#8C6534`).
- Mist: hairline border (`border-mist`, `#E7DCCB`).
- Blush: warm newsletter/accent panel (`bg-blush`, `#EFE1D2`).

## Typography
- Headings and logo: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, high contrast, generous line height.
- Product names: Cormorant Garamond, uppercase, wide letter-spacing.
- Body/UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.

## Components
- Buttons: refined rectangular/pill shapes, solid champagne on light backgrounds, porcelain on espresso backgrounds. Use subtle hover lift and color deepening.
- Cards: warm pearl surfaces, thin mist borders, soft shadows only on hover.
- Dividers: one-pixel mist hairlines, generous spacing.
- Imagery: large editorial crops, warm gold jewelry, neutral/dark backgrounds; use rounded top corners only where card framing needs softness.

## Do
- Keep whitespace generous and typography calm.
- Use strong contrast: espresso text on light surfaces, porcelain text on espresso surfaces.
- Keep accents restrained and consistent.
- Use subtle transitions for hover, cart drawer, and accordions.

## Don't
- Do not use bright sale colors, loud badges, neon, heavy gradients, or crowded layouts.
- Do not make product cards look generic or discount-driven.
- Do not use low-contrast muted text on pale backgrounds.
