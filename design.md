# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial Metal Folding Machinery
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly — premium industrial feel with clean, approachable design

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Navy `#0F1B2D` — authority, precision, trust
- **Primary Accent**: Steel Blue `#2E7DBF` — technology, reliability
- **Secondary Accent**: Warm Gold `#C8963E` — premium quality, craftsmanship
- **Background Light**: Off-White `#F7F8FA` — clean, spacious
- **Background White**: Pure White `#FFFFFF`
- **Text Primary**: Dark Charcoal `#1A1A2E`
- **Text Secondary**: Slate Gray `#5A6275`
- **Border/Divider**: Light Gray `#E2E5EB`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, font-weight 700-800, tracking tight
- **Body**: Inter, font-weight 400, line-height 1.6
- **Accent/Label**: Inter, font-weight 500-600, uppercase tracking wide for labels

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows on cards: `shadow-sm` to `shadow-md`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-lg` to `rounded-xl`
- Hover effects: smooth transitions, subtle scale or shadow increase
- Background sections alternate between white and off-white
- Hero uses dark background with overlay for dramatic effect

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid primary (navy bg, white text), outline secondary (border navy, navy text), gold accent CTA
- **Cards**: White bg, subtle border, hover shadow elevation
- **Navigation**: Sticky top, transparent on hero, white bg on scroll
- **Icons**: Lucide React icons, consistent sizing

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace for an elegant feel
- Keep typography hierarchy clear
- Use gold accents sparingly for premium highlights
- Ensure all text is highly readable against backgrounds
- Use smooth transitions and hover states

## Don'ts
- Don't use overly bright or saturated colors
- Don't crowd sections with too much content
- Don't use light text on light backgrounds
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors prominently
- Don't use decorative fonts — keep it professional
