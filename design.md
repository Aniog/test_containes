# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Colors
- Background: warm-white (#F7F4F0) — soft off-white with warmth
- Text: warm-black (#1C1816) — deep brown-black, softer than pure black
- Accent / Gold: gold (#B8863F) — warm metallic gold
- Gold light: #D4B886 — for hover states, highlights
- Gold pale: #EDE0CC — for subtle backgrounds, borders
- Gold dark: #8B6930 — for hover on gold buttons
- Secondary text / borders: taupe (#9C8B7E), taupe-light (#C4B8AE), taupe-pale (#E5DDD4)
- Dark sections: warm-dark (#2A2522) — for footer, dark hero overlays

## Typography
- Headings / Product Names: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- Body / UI / Navigation: Inter (sans) — clean, modern
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- Navigation links: uppercase, small, tracked (tracking-wider)

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout (py-16 to py-24 for sections)
- Max-width containers (max-w-7xl, mx-auto) for desktop
- Thin hairline dividers between sections (border-t border-taupe-pale)
- Large, editorial imagery — full-bleed hero, product images

## Buttons
- Primary: gold background, white text, uppercase, tracked, 8px horizontal padding, 12px vertical
- Outline: gold border, gold text, same sizing
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- No rounded corners (sharp look = editorial)

## Navigation
- Sticky, transparent over hero, solid warm-white on scroll
- Serif logo "VELMORA" in uppercase
- Center nav links: uppercase sans, tracked
- Right: search + cart icons

## Cards & Products
- Large product images with subtle shadow on hover
- Product name in serif uppercase with wide letter-spacing
- Price in sans-serif, gold accent color
- Quick "Add to Cart" overlay on hover

## Dividers
- Thin hairline rule: 1px border-top in taupe-pale (#E5DDD4)

## Animations
- fade-in: 0.6s ease-out
- slide-up: 0.6s ease-out (for scroll-revealed content)
- slide-down: 0.3s ease-out (for dropdowns/drawers)
- Subtle hover transitions: 300ms
