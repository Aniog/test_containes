# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — restrained, elegant, never loud or discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Base (Deep Charcoal)**: #1A1816 — primary text, dark backgrounds
- **Cream (Warm Off-White)**: #F8F5F1 — main background, cards
- **Soft Taupe**: #C4B5A5 — subtle accents, dividers
- **Gold Accent**: #C5A46E — primary CTA, hover states, metallic feel
- **Dark Gold**: #8B7355 — hover on gold elements
- **White**: #FFFFFF — overlays, buttons on dark
- **Muted Text**: #6B665F — secondary text
- **Light Border**: #E8E4DE — hairline dividers

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Playfair Display (serif), elegant, refined
- **Body & UI**: Inter (sans-serif), clean and readable
- **Product Names**: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.08em

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace
- Large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers (1px)
- Subtle hover transitions (150-200ms)
- Soft shadows on cards

## Buttons
- Solid gold accent: bg-[#C5A46E] text-white
- Outlined: border-[#C5A46E] text-[#C5A46E]
- Premium feel, rounded-sm, smooth transitions

## Components
- Cards: cream bg, soft shadow, gold hover lift
- Accordions: clean, minimal
- Filters: subtle pills, active state in gold
- Cart drawer: slides from right, cream bg
