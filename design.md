# Design Guidelines

This project follows the Strikingly AI generators hub visual system.

## Typography
- Headings and buttons use Brandon Grotesque when available, with Josefin Sans as the web-safe fallback.
- Body text uses Open Sans at a 14px base size with 1.5 line-height.
- Headings and buttons are uppercase and bold.

## Colors
- Brand purple: `#8159BB` for accents, badges, focus states, and line icons.
- AI gradient: `#7671FF` to `#CB0C9F`, used only for primary CTA fills and the second line of the hero H1.
- Body text: `#636972`; headings: `#4B5056`; hero heading: `#2E2E2F`.
- Card borders: `#C6C9CD`; dividers: `#E2E4E7`; default background: `#FFFFFF`.

## Components
- Buttons are 36px high by default, 44px for large CTAs, with 4px radius and white text on filled styles.
- Cards use white backgrounds, 1px borders, 3px radius, and 20px padding.
- Tags are ghost-style purple outlines with uppercase text.

## Do / Don't
- Do keep all important text explicit and high contrast.
- Do keep the page static-source friendly so all generator links appear in view-source.
- Do not use fake ratings, fake counts, placeholder `href="#"` links, or embedded builder forms.
