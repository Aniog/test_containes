# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora Fine Jewelry uses a quiet-luxury editorial look with warm light, rich contrast, and generous whitespace. The experience should feel premium, calm, and feminine without looking flashy or discount-driven.

## Color palette
- Background base: deep espresso brown for hero, header states, footer, and dramatic sections. Tailwind direction: `bg-[var(--color-espresso)]`
- Surface: soft ivory and warm pearl for cards and page backgrounds. Tailwind direction: `bg-[var(--color-ivory)]`, `bg-[var(--color-pearl)]`
- Accent metallic: champagne gold for buttons, borders, icons, and focus moments. Tailwind direction: `bg-[var(--color-gold)]`, `border-[var(--color-gold)]`, `text-[var(--color-gold-deep)]`
- Foreground: warm charcoal for body text on light surfaces and ivory for dark surfaces. Tailwind direction: `text-[var(--color-ink)]`, `text-[var(--color-ivory)]`
- Muted text: soft taupe-gray with strong enough contrast for supporting labels. Tailwind direction: `text-[var(--color-muted)]`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant and editorial. Product names should be uppercase with wide tracking.
- Body, navigation, pricing, filters, and inputs: Inter.
- Use generous heading sizes and restrained body sizing.

## Spacing and layout
- Use large vertical rhythm between homepage sections.
- Keep content within a max width around `max-w-7xl`.
- Product cards and forms should have comfortable padding, never cramped.
- Use thin hairline dividers and rounded corners sparingly.

## Components
- Buttons: premium and restrained, either solid champagne gold with dark text or transparent with thin gold/ivory borders.
- Cards: soft surfaces, subtle shadows, refined borders, clear readable text.
- Header: transparent over the hero, solid espresso after scroll, with subtle backdrop blur.
- Filters and drawers: clean ivory surfaces with clear section dividers.

## Motion
- Use subtle transitions only: opacity, translate, shadow, and color.
- Avoid bouncy animations or flashy effects.

## Do
- Keep text contrast strong and explicit on every surface.
- Use editorial image crops and lots of breathing room.
- Make mobile layouts elegant and easy to browse.

## Don’t
- Do not use bright sale colors, loud gradients, or discount-store styling.
- Do not use tiny text or low-contrast metallic text on light backgrounds.
- Do not overcrowd product cards or navigation.
