# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF9F7` (warm ivory/cream)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (pure white for cards)
- Text Primary: `#1A1A1A` (near black)
- Text Secondary: `#6B6560` (warm taupe)
- Text Muted: `#9B9590` (soft warm gray)
- Accent (Gold): `#C9A96E` (warm metallic gold)
- Accent Hover: `#B8944D` (deeper gold)
- Border: `#E8E4DF` (warm light border)
- Border Light: `#F0EDE8` (very subtle divider)
- Dark Section: `#1A1A1A` (near black for footer/CTA blocks)
- Dark Text: `#FAF9F7` (warm ivory on dark)

## Typography
- Headings / Product Names: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
  - Hero: text-5xl md:text-7xl lg:text-8xl, font-light (300)
  - Section titles: text-3xl md:text-4xl, font-normal (400)
  - Product names: UPPERCASE, tracking-widest, text-sm
- Body / UI: Inter (sans-serif)
  - Body: text-base, font-light (300) or font-normal (400)
  - Small text: text-xs or text-sm, tracking-wide
  - Buttons: text-sm, font-medium (500), tracking-wide

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-32
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8 lg:px-12
- Card gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Generous whitespace between sections

## Components
- Buttons: 
  - Primary: bg-[#1A1A1A] text-[#FAF9F7] hover:bg-[#C9A96E] transition-colors duration-300
  - Outlined: border border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A] hover:bg-[#1A1A1A] hover:text-[#FAF9F7]
  - Accent: bg-[#C9A96E] text-[#1A1A1A] hover:bg-[#B8944D]
- Cards: bg-white, subtle shadow on hover, thin border
- Dividers: border-t border-[#E8E4DF] or hairline border-t border-[#F0EDE8]

## Animations
- Hover transitions: transition-all duration-300 ease-out
- Image hover: scale-105 with overflow-hidden container
- Fade in: opacity-0 to opacity-100 with translate-y-4 to translate-y-0
- Cart drawer: slide-in from right

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable with strong contrast
- Use serif for headings, sans-serif for body
- Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing
- Subtle, refined hover states
- Editorial, large imagery

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No heavy shadows or rounded corners (keep subtle)
- No bright primary colors
