# Visual Style Guide for ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Theme
Elegant but user-friendly. The design should convey professionalism, reliability, and precision, suitable for an industrial machinery company, while remaining accessible and easy to navigate for users.

## Colors
- **Primary**: Slate/Navy Blue (e.g., `bg-slate-900`, `text-slate-900`) - for a strong, industrial, yet elegant presence.
- **Secondary**: Cool Gray (e.g., `bg-slate-100`, `text-slate-600`) - for backgrounds and secondary text.
- **Accent**: Steel Blue or Subtle Metallic (e.g., `text-blue-600`, `bg-blue-600`) - for buttons, links, and highlights.
- **Background**: White (`bg-white`) and off-white (`bg-slate-50`) - for clean, open spaces.

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter, sans-serif (Clean, modern, highly legible).
- **Headings**:
  - `h1`: Bold, prominent, dark slate (`text-slate-900`, `font-bold`, `tracking-tight`).
  - `h2`: Semi-bold, dark slate (`text-slate-800`, `font-semibold`).
  - `h3`: Medium, dark slate (`text-slate-800`, `font-medium`).
- **Body Text**:
  - Base: Regular, cool gray (`text-slate-600`, `font-normal`, `leading-relaxed`).

## Spacing & Layout
- **Container**: Generous padding and margins to create an elegant, uncluttered feel (`py-16`, `px-4`, `gap-8`).
- **Layout**: Clean grid structures for displaying products, centered content for readability.
- **Borders & Shadows**: Subtle borders and light shadows to give depth without being overpowering (`shadow-sm`, `border-slate-200`, `rounded-lg`).

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid background, white text, subtle hover effect (`bg-blue-600`, `text-white`, `hover:bg-blue-700`, `rounded-md`, `px-6`, `py-2`, `transition-colors`).
  - Secondary: Outline or light background, primary text (`bg-slate-100`, `text-slate-900`, `hover:bg-slate-200`).
- **Cards (Products)**:
  - Clean white background, subtle shadow, rounded corners, clear hierarchy of image, title, and description.

## Do's and Don'ts
- **Do**: Use ample whitespace to make the site feel premium.
- **Do**: Use high-quality, relevant stock images of machinery and industrial settings.
- **Do**: Keep the color palette restrained and professional.
- **Don't**: Use overly bright or neon colors.
- **Don't**: Clutter pages with too much text; use clear, concise copy.
- **Don't**: Use complex, hard-to-read fonts.
