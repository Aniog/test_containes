# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, refined, feminine, premium-but-accessible.
The storefront should feel like a fashion editorial rather than a mass-market storefront.

## Visual direction
Commit to one elegant direction sitewide:
- Base surfaces: deep espresso and soft ivory
- Accent: muted antique gold
- Supporting neutrals: warm taupe, stone, mist
- Use high contrast text on every surface
- Avoid bright promotional colors, loud gradients, and discount-store styling

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, serif
- Body, navigation, UI labels: Inter, sans-serif
- Product names should use uppercase with generous tracking
- Long-form copy should feel airy with comfortable line height

## Tailwind style tokens
- Page background: `bg-stone-50`
- Deep dark sections: `bg-stone-950`
- Primary text on light: `text-stone-900`
- Secondary text on light: `text-stone-600`
- Primary text on dark: `text-stone-50`
- Secondary text on dark: `text-stone-300`
- Accent surfaces: `bg-amber-200/80`
- Accent text/details: `text-amber-300`, `border-amber-200/40`
- Hairlines: `border-stone-200` on light, `border-white/10` on dark
- Card surfaces: `bg-white`, `bg-stone-900/70`
- Shadows: `shadow-[0_18px_50px_rgba(28,25,23,0.08)]`

## Spacing and layout
- Use generous vertical rhythm: `py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-28`
- Constrain content with `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Use wide gaps in editorial sections: `gap-8 md:gap-12 lg:gap-16`
- Product cards should breathe with consistent padding and image ratios

## Components
- Buttons: premium, restrained, slightly rounded, no loud fills
- Inputs: thin borders, warm backgrounds, clear readable text
- Dividers: thin and subtle
- Navigation: sticky, transparent over hero, solid after scroll
- Hover states: subtle lift, opacity fade, soft image zoom
- Cart drawer: elevated dark panel with clear light text and warm accent CTA

## Imagery
- Use warm-lit jewelry imagery
- Favor close-up portraits, editorial still life, ear stacks, and necklace details
- Use dark or neutral backdrops that flatter gold tones
- Avoid busy backgrounds and generic studio ecommerce styling

## Do
- Keep layouts calm and spacious
- Use serif headlines to create a luxury tone
- Keep badges and labels understated
- Ensure every visible text element has strong contrast
- Make mobile feel premium, not cramped

## Don’t
- Don’t use countdowns, sale stickers, or aggressive promo UI
- Don’t use neon, pure black-on-gold, or harsh gradients
- Don’t overcrowd product cards or navigation
- Don’t mix multiple visual directions
