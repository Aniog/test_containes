# Cake Shop Design System

## Brand Identity
Warm, artisanal, and inviting. The brand feels like a cozy neighborhood bakery with a premium touch.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Rose):** `#c2185b` — deep rose pink for CTAs, accents, highlights. Tailwind: `bg-rose-700`
- **Primary Light:** `#f48fb1` — soft pink for hover states. Tailwind: `bg-rose-300`
- **Cream / Background:** `#fdf6f0` — warm off-white page background. Tailwind: `bg-[#fdf6f0]`
- **Card Background:** `#ffffff` — pure white for cards. Tailwind: `bg-white`
- **Dark Text:** `#2d1b0e` — rich dark brown for headings. Tailwind: `text-[#2d1b0e]`
- **Body Text:** `#5c3d2e` — warm brown for body copy. Tailwind: `text-[#5c3d2e]`
- **Muted Text:** `#9e7b6b` — lighter brown for captions, labels. Tailwind: `text-[#9e7b6b]`
- **Accent Gold:** `#f9a825` — warm gold for stars, badges. Tailwind: `text-[#f9a825]`
- **Border:** `#f0ddd4` — soft warm border. Tailwind: `border-[#f0ddd4]`

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body). Load both from Google Fonts.
- **H1:** `font-playfair text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold text-[#2d1b0e]`
- **H2:** `font-playfair text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-[#2d1b0e]`
- **H3:** `font-playfair text-xl font-semibold text-[#2d1b0e]`
- **Body:** `font-inter text-base text-[#5c3d2e] leading-relaxed`
- **Caption/Label:** `font-inter text-sm text-[#9e7b6b]`
- **Button Text:** `font-inter text-sm font-semibold uppercase tracking-wide`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6 md:gap-8`
- Max content width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-[#f0ddd4] rounded-2xl`
- Card shadow: `shadow-md hover:shadow-xl transition-shadow`
- Button border radius: `rounded-full`

## Buttons
- **Primary CTA:** `bg-[#c2185b] text-white px-8 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold uppercase tracking-wide hover:bg-[#ad1457] transition-colors`
- **Secondary/Outline:** `border-2 border-[#c2185b] text-[#c2185b] px-8 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold uppercase tracking-wide hover:bg-[#c2185b] hover:text-white transition-colors`

## Do's
- Use warm, inviting photography with soft lighting
- Keep headings in Playfair Display for elegance
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Highlight prices and CTAs with rose pink
- Use gold stars for ratings

## Don'ts
- Don't use cold blues or grays as primary colors
- Don't use harsh black (#000) — use dark brown instead
- Don't crowd cards — keep padding generous
- Don't use all-caps for body text
