# Design System — Fried Chicken Around the World

## Concept
Warm, appetizing, and globally curious. The site celebrates the universal love of fried chicken with a rich, food-magazine aesthetic. Think deep golden yellows, warm reds, and earthy browns against clean cream/white backgrounds.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Golden Yellow):** `#F5A623` — used for accents, highlights, CTAs. Tailwind: `text-amber-500`, `bg-amber-500`
- **Deep Red:** `#C0392B` — used for headings, bold accents. Tailwind: `text-red-700`, `bg-red-700`
- **Warm Brown:** `#7B4F2E` — used for secondary text, borders. Tailwind: `text-amber-900`
- **Cream Background:** `#FDF6EC` — page background. Tailwind: `bg-amber-50`
- **Dark Text:** `#1C1C1C` — body text. Tailwind: `text-gray-900`
- **Muted Text:** `#6B6B6B` — secondary/caption text. Tailwind: `text-gray-500`
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` — card backgrounds, nav. Tailwind: `bg-white`

## Typography
- **Font:** "Playfair Display" for headings (serif, editorial feel), "Inter" for body text
- **H1:** `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold font-serif text-gray-900`
- **H2:** `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold font-serif text-gray-900`
- **H3:** `text-xl font-semibold font-serif text-gray-800`
- **Body:** `text-base text-gray-700 leading-relaxed`
- **Caption/Label:** `text-sm text-gray-500 uppercase tracking-widest`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl shadow-md hover:shadow-xl transition-shadow`
- Subtle border: `border border-amber-100`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-amber-500 hover:bg-amber-600 text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-full transition-colors`
- Secondary: `border-2 border-amber-500 text-amber-600 hover:bg-amber-50 font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-full transition-colors`

## Do's
- Use warm, food-photography-inspired imagery
- Keep card layouts clean with generous whitespace
- Use serif fonts for headings to give an editorial, magazine feel
- Use flag emojis to add personality to country cards
- Highlight dish names in amber/golden color

## Don'ts
- Don't use cold blues or grays as primary colors
- Don't use tight, cramped layouts
- Don't use all-caps for body text
- Don't use low-contrast text on colored backgrounds
