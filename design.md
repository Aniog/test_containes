# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Colors
- `bg-cream`: #FAF8F5 (warm off-white, main background)
- `bg-white`: #FFFFFF (card backgrounds)
- `text-primary`: #0D0D0D (headings, dark)
- `text-body`: #2A2A2A (body text)
- `text-muted`: #8A7F75 (secondary text)
- `accent-gold`: #C9A96E (primary accent — warm, refined)
- `accent-gold-hover`: #B8944F (darker gold for hover states)
- `border-light`: #E5DDD4 (soft warm gray dividers)
- `border-medium`: #D4C9BC (medium borders)
- `bg-dark`: #1A1410 (deep warm dark for hero overlays)
- `taupe`: #6B5E50 (secondary accent)
- `star-gold`: #D4A853 (star rating)

## Typography
- **Headings** (h1-h3, product names): Cormorant Garamond, serif
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing wide (tracking-widest)
- **Body/UI**: Inter, sans-serif
- **Nav logo**: Cormorant Garamond, uppercase

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card padding: p-6
- Grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E5DDD4] (1px)
- Border radius: rounded-sm on cards, rounded-none on buttons

## Shadows
- Soft shadows: shadow-sm on cards, shadow-md on hover
- Elevation: shadow-[0_2px_20px_rgba(0,0,0,0.06)]

## Buttons
- Primary: solid accent-gold background, text-white, hover:accent-gold-hover
- Outline: border accent-gold, text-accent-gold, hover:bg-accent-gold hover:text-white
- Text: uppercase, tracking-widest, text-sm font-medium

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions: transition-all duration-300
- Image hover: scale-[1.02] with slow ease
- Fade in on scroll: opacity-0 to opacity-100 transition

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep gold accent restrained (one accent element per section)
- Use large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers between sections
- Warm, soft lighting feel in images
- Product names in UPPERCASE with wide tracking

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright, flashy colors
- Don't use heavy borders or thick lines
- Don't overcrowd elements
- Don't use discount/vibrant sale aesthetics
- Don't use multiple accent colors
