# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- **Primary (Deep Base):** `#1A1A1A` (Rich Charcoal Black) - `bg-[#1A1A1A]`, `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Secondary (Warm Neutral):** `#FAF9F6` (Alabaster White) - `bg-[#FAF9F6]`, `text-[#FAF9F6]`
- **Accent (Gold):** `#B88E2F` (Muted Gold) - `text-[#B88E2F]`, `bg-[#B88E2F]`
- **Muted:** `#717171` (Editorial Grey) - `text-[#717171]`
- **Border:** `#E5E5E5` (Thin Hairline) - `border-[#E5E5E5]`

## Typography
- **Headings:** `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. `font-serif`.
- **Product Names:** `Cormorant Garamond`, serif, UPPERCASE, tracking-widest. `font-serif uppercase tracking-[0.2em]`.
- **Body & UI:** `Inter` or `Manrope`, sans-serif. `font-sans`.

## Design Elements
- **Borders:** Thin hairline dividers (`0.5px` or `1px`).
- **Whitespace:** Generous, editorial spacing.
- **Imagery:** Large, warm-lit, high-quality jewelry photography.
- **Animations:** Subtle fades and transforms on scroll/hover.
- **Buttons:** Solid deep base with light text, or outlined for secondary actions.

## Do's & Don'ts
### Do
- Use wide letter-spacing for uppercase serif text.
- Maintain high contrast between text and background.
- Use thin dividers to separate editorial sections.
- Ensure all buttons have explicit hover states.

### Don't
- Use generic blue links.
- Overcrowd the layout; keep it "quiet".
- Use neon colors or harsh gradients.
- Use rounded corners that are too large (keep them subtle or sharp).
