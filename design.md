# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial metal folding machinery
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly — premium industrial feel with approachable clarity

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep navy blue `#0F1B2D` — authority, precision, trust
- **Primary Accent**: Steel blue `#2E6BA6` — modern, professional
- **Secondary Accent**: Warm gold `#C8963E` — premium quality, craftsmanship
- **Background**: Off-white `#F7F8FA` — clean, spacious
- **Card/Surface**: White `#FFFFFF` — clarity
- **Dark Surface**: Charcoal `#1A2332` — sophistication
- **Text Primary**: Dark navy `#0F1B2D`
- **Text Secondary**: Slate gray `#5A6577`
- **Text on Dark**: White `#FFFFFF` / Light gray `#C8D1DC`
- **Border**: Light gray `#E2E6ED`
- **Success/CTA**: Steel blue `#2E6BA6`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, font-weight 700-800, tracking tight
- **Body**: Inter, font-weight 400-500, line-height 1.6-1.8
- **Accent/Label**: Inter, font-weight 600, uppercase tracking-wide for small labels

### Font Sizes (Tailwind)
- Hero heading: `text-4xl md:text-6xl lg:text-7xl`
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl lg:text-5xl`
- Subheading: `text-xl md:text-2xl`
- Body: `text-base md:text-lg`
- Small/Label: `text-sm`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Element gaps: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-gray-200` or `border border-[#E2E6ED]`
- Card shadow: `shadow-sm` default, `shadow-lg` on hover
- Rounded corners: `rounded-lg` for cards, `rounded-full` for buttons/badges
- Subtle hover lift: `hover:shadow-xl hover:-translate-y-1 transition-all duration-300`

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle gradients for hero sections (navy to dark blue)
- Gold accents for premium touches (borders, icons, highlights)
- Industrial imagery with clean overlays
- Card-based product showcases with hover effects
- Smooth scroll behavior
- Professional iconography (Lucide icons)

## Do's
- Use navy + gold color combination for premium feel
- Keep layouts clean with ample whitespace
- Use subtle animations and transitions
- Ensure high contrast for readability
- Use consistent spacing and alignment
- Make CTAs prominent with steel blue or gold

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much text
- Don't use low-contrast text on dark backgrounds
- Don't use more than 2-3 font sizes per section
- Don't use heavy drop shadows — keep them subtle
- Don't use playful or casual design elements
