# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** `font-family: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif;`
  - Elegant, high-end editorial feel.
  - Product names in `UPPERCASE` with `letter-spacing: 0.15em;`.
- **Body & UI:** `font-family: 'Manrope', sans-serif;`
  - Clean, modern, highly readable.

## Colors
- **Background:** `#F9F8F6` (Soft warm neutral)
- **Foreground/Primary:** `#1A1A1A` (Almost black black for luxury contrast)
- **Accent (Gold):** `#C0A468` (Refined metallic gold)
- **Muted/Borders:** `#E5E7EB` (Subtle hairlines)

## Components
- **Buttons:**
  - Solid Primary: High contrast, sharp corners.
  - Outlined: Thin hairline border, elegant hover.
- **Dividers:** Thin hairlines (`opacity: 0.4`).
- **Cards:** No border, soft shadows, large imagery.

## Animations
- **Transitions:** Slow, smooth (500ms) luxury transitions.
- **Hover:** Subtle scale or opacity changes.
