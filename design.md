# MicroCosmos Design System

## Theme
A dark, immersive, science-inspired design that evokes the mystery and beauty of the microscopic world.

## Colors
- **Background**: Deep dark navy/black (`bg-cosmos-dark` → #0a0e1a)
- **Surface**: Slightly lighter dark (`bg-cosmos-surface` → #111827)
- **Card**: Dark card background (`bg-cosmos-card` → #1a2035)
- **Primary accent**: Vibrant teal/cyan (`text-cosmos-accent` → #06d6a0)
- **Secondary accent**: Soft purple/violet (`text-cosmos-violet` → #8b5cf6)
- **Tertiary accent**: Warm amber (`text-cosmos-amber` → #f59e0b)
- **Text primary**: White (`text-white`)
- **Text secondary**: Light gray (`text-gray-300`)
- **Text muted**: Medium gray (`text-gray-400`)

## Typography
- Font: "Inter" for body, "Space Grotesk" for headings
- Headings: Bold (700-800), large sizes, white
- Body: Regular (400), gray-300
- Captions/labels: Small, uppercase, tracking-wider, accent color

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Grid gap: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `border border-white/10 rounded-2xl`
- Hover glow: `hover:border-cosmos-accent/30`
- Subtle shadow: `shadow-lg shadow-black/20`

## Visual Style
- Dark immersive backgrounds
- Glassmorphism-inspired cards with subtle borders
- Gradient overlays on hero sections
- Rounded corners (rounded-xl, rounded-2xl)
- Smooth transitions on hover

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Use the stock image system extensively for microscopic imagery
- Use gradient text for key headings
- Keep text readable with high contrast against dark backgrounds

## Don'ts
- No light backgrounds
- No harsh borders
- No small, cramped layouts
- No low-contrast text
