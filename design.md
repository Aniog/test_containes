# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury with a warm editorial point of view. The site should feel premium, calm, intimate, and crafted, never promotional or generic marketplace. Use generous whitespace, fine hairline borders, softened shadows, and restrained motion.

## Palette
Use a deep refined base with warm ivory and antique-gold accents to flatter gold jewelry.

- Midnight espresso: `velmora-espresso` (`#15100D`) for primary text and dark surfaces.
- Charcoal cacao: `velmora-cacao` (`#2B211C`) for elevated dark panels.
- Warm ivory: `velmora-ivory` (`#F7F0E6`) for page backgrounds.
- Porcelain: `velmora-porcelain` (`#FFFBF5`) for cards and light surfaces.
- Sand: `velmora-sand` (`#E7D7C3`) for dividers and muted blocks.
- Champagne gold: `velmora-gold` (`#B8894A`) for CTAs, accents, stars, and active states.
- Deep bronze: `velmora-bronze` (`#7A5731`) for hover states and secondary accent text.
- Rose clay: `velmora-clay` (`#B68472`) for soft editorial highlights.

Always pair dark backgrounds with ivory/porcelain text. Pair light backgrounds with espresso/cacao text. Important text must have explicit readable color.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Playfair Display, elegant serif, high contrast.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names: uppercase, wide tracking, calm serif styling.

## Layout & Spacing
- Mobile-first, airy sections with `py-16` to `py-24`, expanding to `lg:py-28` on desktop.
- Use max-width containers (`max-w-7xl`) and generous horizontal padding.
- Desktop should use multi-column editorial layouts; mobile should stack cleanly.
- Use thin dividers (`border-velmora-sand/70`) and subtle separation instead of heavy outlines.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, rounded-full, either solid gold with espresso text or outlined ivory/gold. Smooth hover transitions.
- Cards: porcelain or transparent editorial surfaces with hairline borders and soft shadows only on hover.
- Product imagery: warm gold jewelry on model, dark neutral, or editorial neutral backgrounds. Use stock image data attributes instead of hardcoded URLs.
- Cart drawer: porcelain surface, clear espresso text, soft overlay, accessible close/remove controls.

## Motion
Use subtle transitions: opacity, translate, scale, color, shadow. Avoid bouncy or flashy motion.

## Do
- Keep text contrast strong.
- Keep accents restrained and consistent.
- Let imagery and typography create luxury.
- Use refined empty space.

## Don't
- Do not use loud sale colors, discount badges, neon tones, or overly busy gradients.
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-beige text.
- Do not hardcode stock image URLs.
