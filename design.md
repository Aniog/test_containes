# Mexico Food — Design System

## Visual Identity
Warm, vibrant, and appetizing. Inspired by the rich colors of Mexican culture: terracotta, chili red, golden yellow, and deep forest green.

## Colors
- **Primary (Chili Red):** `#C0392B` → Tailwind custom: `red-700` or `bg-[#C0392B]`
- **Accent (Warm Amber):** `#E67E22` → `orange-500`
- **Highlight (Golden):** `#F39C12` → `yellow-500`
- **Green (Avocado):** `#27AE60` → `green-600`
- **Background (Cream):** `#FDF6EC` → `bg-[#FDF6EC]`
- **Surface (White):** `#FFFFFF`
- **Text Dark:** `#1C1C1C` → `text-gray-900`
- **Text Muted:** `#6B7280` → `text-gray-500`

## Typography
- **Headings:** "Playfair Display" — serif, bold, elegant
- **Body:** "Inter" — clean, readable sans-serif
- **Hero title:** text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold
- **Section title:** text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold
- **Card title:** text-xl font-semibold
- **Body text:** text-base text-gray-600

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-6`
- Card gap: `gap-6` or `gap-8`
- Container max width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl shadow-md`
- Images: `rounded-xl object-cover`
- Hover: `hover:shadow-xl transition-shadow duration-300`

## Do's
- Use warm background tones (cream, off-white) for sections
- Alternate section backgrounds for visual rhythm
- Use bold serif headings for section titles
- Keep text clearly readable — dark text on light backgrounds

## Don'ts
- No dark text on dark backgrounds
- No pure white text on light sections
- No overly complex layouts — keep it clean and spacious
