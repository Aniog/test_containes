# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking
- Target: women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase

## Color Palette
- `bg-primary`: #F8F5F0 (warm cream/ivory background)
- `bg-card`: #FFFFFF (pure white cards)
- `bg-accent`: #F5EDE4 (soft warm beige for accent sections)
- `text-primary`: #1C1C1C (near-black for body text)
- `text-secondary`: #6B655E (warm gray for secondary text)
- `text-muted`: #9C958C (muted gray for subtle text)
- `accent-gold`: #B8860B (dark goldenrod for CTAs and accents)
- `accent-gold-light`: #D4A843 (lighter gold for hover)
- `border-light`: #E8E2D8 (thin hairline borders)
- `border-medium`: #D4CCC0 (medium borders)

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif
  - Product names in UPPERCASE with tracking (letter-spacing)
- **Body & UI**: 'Inter', sans-serif
- **Font Weights**: headings 400-600, body 300-400, buttons 500

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-32
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E8E2D8]
- Card borders: border border-[#E8E2D8] rounded-sm
- No heavy borders

## Shadows
- Subtle: shadow-sm (soft, barely visible)
- Elevated: shadow-md for hover states

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#B8860B] text-white, hover:bg-[#D4A843]
- Outline: border border-[#B8860B] text-[#B8860B] hover:bg-[#B8860B] hover:text-white
- Both: rounded-none, px-8 py-3, uppercase tracking-widest text-sm font-medium

## Icons
- Lucide React icons, stroke-width 1.5, warm tone

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers
- Restrained use of gold accent color
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- Serif for all headings and product names
- UPPERCASE product names with wide tracking

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or heavy borders
- No discount/clearance visual language
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No harsh shadows
- No text that blends into background
