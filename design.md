# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury with a warm editorial tone. The storefront should feel like a premium demi-fine jewelry journal: refined, spacious, tactile, and never promotional or loud.

## Palette
- Espresso base: `velmora-espresso` (`#241914`) for deep navigation, overlays, footer, and strong text.
- Ink: `velmora-ink` (`#3A2B24`) for primary readable body text on light surfaces.
- Ivory: `velmora-ivory` (`#F8F2EA`) for page backgrounds and calm sections.
- Porcelain: `velmora-porcelain` (`#FFFDF8`) for cards, drawers, and elevated surfaces.
- Champagne: `velmora-champagne` (`#E8D8C5`) for soft panels and dividers.
- Antique gold: `velmora-gold` (`#B8894D`) for premium CTA buttons, stars, and accent details.
- Deep gold: `velmora-deepgold` (`#8B6333`) for hover states and readable accent text.
- Rose clay: `velmora-clay` (`#C9A08A`) for secondary editorial accents.

## Typography
- Headings, logo, and large editorial captions: Playfair Display, elegant serif.
- Body, navigation, UI, forms, and product metadata: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names: uppercase, wide letter spacing, serif, restrained sizes.

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first, generous whitespace, and clear touch targets.
- Use full-bleed editorial imagery on hero/UGC, but keep product grids calm and ordered.
- Prefer thin hairline dividers (`border-velmora-champagne/70`) over heavy borders.
- Cards should use soft shadows and subtle hover lifts, never thick borders.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid antique-gold with espresso text, or outlined espresso/gold. Smooth hover transitions.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, then solid porcelain/ivory with readable ink text on scroll.
- Drawers and modals: porcelain surfaces, explicit ink text, gentle backdrop.
- Forms: ivory/porcelain inputs with visible borders and strong focus rings.

## Do's
- Keep all text high-contrast and explicitly colored.
- Use warm imagery queries referencing nearby section copy.
- Use subtle transitions: opacity, transform, and color changes.
- Keep accents restrained; gold should guide attention, not dominate.

## Don'ts
- Do not use discount-style red badges or loud sale treatments.
- Do not use generic blue/purple ecommerce styling.
- Do not crowd mobile screens.
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-beige text.
