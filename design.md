# SSourcing China Design System

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- **Headings**: Semi-bold to Bold, slate-900 or blue-900 (`text-slate-900`)
- **Body**: Regular, slate-600 or slate-700 (`text-slate-600`)

## Colors
- **Primary**: Deep Blue (#1a365d) - `bg-primary`, `text-primary`
- **Secondary**: Light Blue/Slate (#f8fafc) - `bg-secondary`, `text-secondary`
- **Accent**: Trust/Action Red (#e53e3e) or Gold/Orange (#f6ad55) - `bg-accent`, `text-accent`
- **Neutral**: Slate Grays (slate-50 to slate-950)

## Spacing & Layout
- **Containers**: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- **Section Padding**: `py-16 sm:py-24`
- **Gap**: `gap-8` for grids, `gap-4` for lists

## Visual Style
- **Borders**: Thin, light gray (`border-slate-200`)
- **Shadows**: Subtle, natural (`shadow-sm`)
- **Cards**: Grounded white background with clean borders
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid deep blue, rounded-md, md:text-lg
  - Outline: Bordered deep blue
- **Icons**: Lucide React icons in primary color or secondary accents.

## Do's
- Use high-quality, professional B2B imagery (factory, shipping, office).
- Keep contrast high for readability.
- Maintain consistent white space.
- Use clear, professional language.

## Don'ts
- Avoid overly bright or neon colors.
- Don't use generic, low-quality stock photos with watermarks.
- Avoid clutter in the UI.
