# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Colors
- background: warm off-white #FDF8F3 (page background, cards)
- foreground: deep espresso #1C1814 (headings, primary text)
- muted-bg: soft champagne #F5EFE6 (subtle sections, hover states)
- muted-fg: warm gray #8A8278 (secondary text, captions)
- accent: burnished gold #C9A96E (CTAs, accents, highlights)
- accent-hover: #B8944F (darker gold for hover)
- border: #E8DFD3 (thin hairline dividers)
- card: #FFFFFF (product cards)
- hero-overlay: rgba(28, 24, 20, 0.35)

## Typography
- Font headings: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif — elegant, refined
- Font body: 'Inter', sans-serif — clean, modern
- Product names: UPPERCASE with tracking-wide (letter-spacing)
- Hero headline: font-heading, text-5xl/text-6xl, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Section titles: font-heading, text-3xl/text-4xl
- Body: font-body, text-sm/text-base

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Max width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Product grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Thin hairline borders: border border-[#E8DFD3]
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow
- Dividers: hr with border-t border-[#E8DFD3]

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C9A96E] text-white, hover:bg-[#B8944F], rounded-none, px-8 py-3
- Outline: border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E] hover:bg-[#C9A96E] hover:text-white, rounded-none
- Transitions: duration-300 ease-in-out

## Images
- Editorial style, warm lighting, gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Product cards: aspect-[4/5] for editorial feel
- Hero: full-bleed, large format
- UGC reels: aspect-[9/16] vertical

## Hover Effects
- Scale: hover:scale-[1.02] for product cards
- Opacity: hover:opacity-90 for buttons
- Underline: hover:underline decoration-[#C9A96E] decoration-1 for links
- Smooth transitions on all interactive elements
