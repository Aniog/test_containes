# Football Website Design System

## Brand Identity
A modern, energetic football website with a dark, stadium-inspired aesthetic.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Green**: `#16a34a` (grass green) — `text-green-600`, `bg-green-600`
- **Dark Background**: `#0f172a` (deep navy-black) — `bg-slate-900`
- **Card Background**: `#1e293b` — `bg-slate-800`
- **Surface**: `#334155` — `bg-slate-700`
- **Accent Yellow**: `#facc15` — `text-yellow-400`
- **Text Primary**: `#f8fafc` — `text-slate-50`
- **Text Secondary**: `#94a3b8` — `text-slate-400`
- **Border**: `#334155` — `border-slate-700`

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Hero Title**: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-black tracking-tight`
- **Section Title**: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- **Card Title**: `text-lg font-semibold`
- **Body**: `text-base text-slate-300`
- **Caption/Meta**: `text-sm text-slate-400`

## Spacing
- **Section padding**: `py-16 md:py-24`
- **Container**: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- **Card padding**: `p-6`
- **Gap between cards**: `gap-6`

## Components

### Cards
- Background: `bg-slate-800`
- Border: `border border-slate-700`
- Rounded: `rounded-2xl`
- Hover: `hover:border-green-500 transition-colors`

### Buttons
- Primary: `bg-green-600 hover:bg-green-500 text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-xl`
- Secondary: `bg-slate-700 hover:bg-slate-600 text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-xl`
- Ghost: `text-green-400 hover:text-green-300 font-medium`

### Badges
- Live: `bg-red-600 text-white text-xs font-bold px-2 py-1 rounded-full`
- Win: `bg-green-600 text-white text-xs font-bold px-2 py-1 rounded`
- Draw: `bg-yellow-500 text-black text-xs font-bold px-2 py-1 rounded`
- Loss: `bg-red-600 text-white text-xs font-bold px-2 py-1 rounded`

### Navigation
- Background: `bg-slate-900/95 backdrop-blur-sm`
- Border bottom: `border-b border-slate-800`
- Logo: `text-green-400 font-black text-2xl`

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds throughout for a premium stadium feel
- Use green as the primary accent color (grass/pitch reference)
- Use bold, large typography for scores and headlines
- Add subtle hover effects on interactive elements
- Use grid layouts for match cards and news articles

## Don'ts
- Don't use white backgrounds — keep the dark theme consistent
- Don't use light text on light backgrounds
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors per section
- Don't use small font sizes for scores or key stats
