# Velmora Fine Jewelry ‚Äî Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FDFBF7` (warm ivory) ‚Äî `ivory`
- **Foreground / Text**: `#1A1A1A` (near-black) ‚Äî `charcoal`
- **Accent**: `#8B6914` (burnished gold) ‚Äî `gold`
- **Accent Hover**: `#6B5010` (deep gold) ‚Äî `gold-dark`
- **Muted**: `#F5F0E8` (warm cream) ‚Äî `cream`
- **Muted Foreground**: `#6B6356` (warm gray) ‚Äî `stone`
- **Border**: `#E8E0D4` (warm sand) ‚Äî `sand`
- **Card Background**: `#FFFFFF` ‚Äî `white`

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif ‚Äî elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif ‚Äî clean, modern
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.12em
- **Heading sizes**: Hero: text-5xl md:text-7xl, Section: text-3xl md:text-4xl

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Cards: rounded-none or rounded-sm (sharp, editorial)
- Thin hairline dividers: border-sand (1px)

## Components
- **Buttons**: bg-gold text-white px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm font-medium hover:bg-gold-dark transition-colors
- **Outlined buttons**: border border-gold text-gold hover:bg-gold hover:text-white
- **Cards**: no border-radius, subtle shadow-sm on hover
- **Links**: text-stone hover:text-gold transition-colors

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers
- Subtle hover transitions (transition-all duration-300)
- Soft shadows (shadow-sm)
- Restrained accent color usage

## Don'ts
- No rounded corners on product cards
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/sale badges
- No generic e-commerce patterns
