# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry for women 25–45. No loud discount cues, no generic marketplace tropes. Confidence through restraint.

## Color palette
- Base: deep refined brown/black `#1c1917` (stone-900)
- Surface: warm off-white `#faf9f7` (warm white)
- Paper: soft cream `#f2f0eb`
- Muted: warm gray `#a8a29e`
- Accent: antique gold `#bfa15f` — used sparingly for CTAs, highlights, stars
- Accent dark: `#9a7e44`
- Text primary: `#1c1917`
- Text secondary: `#78716c`
- Hairline divider: `rgba(28,25,23,0.12)`

## Typography
- Headings / product names: Cormorant Garamond (serif), elegant, uppercase product names with wide tracking.
- Body / UI: Inter (sans-serif), clean and readable.

## Components
- Buttons: solid accent `#bfa15f` with dark text, or outlined dark. Premium feel, subtle hover lift and shadow.
- Cards: minimal, no heavy borders, soft hover shadow, image-forward.
- Dividers: 1px hairline `border-stone-200`.
- Inputs: subtle bottom border or full outlined, rounded-sm.

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace. Sections separated by 80–120px on desktop, 56–80px mobile.
- Container max-w-7xl, px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8.

## Motion
- Subtle transitions 200–300ms ease-out.
- Fade/slide for cart drawer and mobile menu.
- Hover scale on images 1.03–1.05.

## Do's
- Use warm gold imagery on dark/neutral backgrounds.
- Keep text contrast high.
- Use uppercase wide-tracking product names.
- Reserve accent color for CTAs and small highlights.

## Don'ts
- No bright red sale badges.
- No harsh shadows or neon colors.
- No discount-style countdowns.
