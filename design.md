# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand Identity
Premium industrial machinery brand. Elegant, confident, and trustworthy.
The design conveys precision engineering with warmth and approachability.

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (sans-serif)
- Headings: font-bold, tight tracking
- Body: font-normal, comfortable line-height (1.6)
- Sizes: responsive scale from mobile to desktop
- Example classes: text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold tracking-tight

## Color Palette
- Primary (Deep Navy): #0f172a (slate-900) — headers, footer, primary backgrounds
- Secondary (Steel Blue): #1e293b (slate-800) — cards, secondary backgrounds
- Accent (Warm Amber): #f59e0b (amber-500) — CTAs, highlights, links
- Accent Hover: #d97706 (amber-600)
- Surface (Light Gray): #f8fafc (slate-50) — page backgrounds
- Muted (Warm Gray): #64748b (slate-500) — secondary text
- Border: #e2e8f0 (slate-200) — dividers, card borders

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Visual Style
- Clean, generous white space
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md (never harsh)
- Rounded corners: rounded-xl for cards, rounded-lg for buttons
- Smooth transitions: transition-all duration-300
- Dividers: thin, subtle borders

## Do's
- Use deep navy for hero and footer sections
- Amber accents for CTAs and key interactive elements
- White cards on light gray backgrounds for product showcases
- Clean grid layouts with clear hierarchy
- Large, bold headings with ample breathing room

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No harsh shadows or sharp corners
- No crowded layouts
- No text on low-contrast backgrounds
- No excessive animations
