# SSourcing China - Design System

## Visual Style
Clean, professional B2B website for a China-based sourcing agent. Trustworthy, international feel.

## Colors
- Primary: deep navy blue `#0f2b46` (brand, headers, footer)
- Primary light: `#1a3a5c` (hover states)
- Accent: warm gold `#c8963e` (CTAs, highlights)
- Accent hover: `#b8862e`
- Background: white `#ffffff`
- Background light: `#f5f7fa` (section alternation)
- Text primary: `#1a1a2e` (headings, body)
- Text secondary: `#4a5568` (subtitles, descriptions)
- Text muted: `#718096` (labels, captions)
- Border: `#e2e8f0`
- Success: `#38a169`
- Error: `#e53e3e`

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: 600-700 weight
- Body: 400 weight
- Hero headline: 48-56px desktop, 32-36px mobile
- Section titles: 32-36px desktop, 24-28px mobile
- Body text: 16px base, 18px for lead paragraphs

## Spacing
- Section padding: 80-100px vertical desktop, 48-64px mobile
- Container max-width: 1200px
- Grid gaps: 24-32px
- Card padding: 24-32px

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-lg (8px), solid fill, uppercase tracking-wide for CTAs
- Cards: white background, subtle shadow, rounded-xl (12px), hover lift effect
- Forms: clean inputs with border, focus ring in accent color
- Navigation: sticky header, transparent on hero, solid on scroll

## Layout
- Full-width sections with centered content containers
- Alternating white/light backgrounds for section separation
- 3-column grids for services/products, 2-column for case studies
- Mobile: single column stacking

## Do's
- Use plenty of white space
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use icons for service features
- Show trust indicators (years, clients, certifications)
- Use realistic factory/QC imagery

## Don'ts
- No exaggerated claims or hype language
- No cluttered layouts
- No bright/neon colors
- No overly decorative elements
- No stock photos that look fake
