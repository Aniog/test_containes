# Design System - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Brand Identity
Velmora is a high-end DTC demi-fine jewelry brand. The visual identity is "quiet luxury" - refined, warm, and editorial.

## Colors
- **Background**: `#f9f7f4` (Warm Chalk) - `bg-[#f9f7f4]`
- **Foreground**: `#1a1a1a` (Deep Carbon) - `text-[#1a1a1a]`
- **Accent**: `#c5a059` (Refined Gold) - `bg-[#c5a059]` / `text-[#c5a059]`
- **Muted**: `#7a756d` (Taupe) - `text-[#7a756d]`
- **Border**: `#e5e1da` (Soft Stone) - `border-[#e5e1da]`

## Typography
- **Headings (Serif)**: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif. Used for headlines and product names.
  - Tailwind: `font-serif`
- **Body/UI (Sans)**: 'Manrope', sans-serif. Used for body text, UI elements, and navigation.
  - Tailwind: `font-sans`
- **Product Names**: Uppercase with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Accent color, white text, uppercase, tracked.
  - Secondary: Outlined, thin border, uppercase.
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline dividers (`border-t-[0.5px]`).
- **Cards**: Minimal, no borders, soft shadows on hover.
- **Imagery**: Large editorial images, warm lighting.

## Do's and Don'ts
### Do's
- Use generous whitespace.
- Maintain high contrast for accessibility.
- Use uppercase for product titles.
- Use thin dividers for a premium feel.

### Don'ts
- Don't use vibrant or neon colors.
- Don't use rounded corners excessively (keep them sharp or very subtle).
- Don't use loud, "salesy" badges.
