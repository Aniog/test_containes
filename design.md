# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial Machinery / Metal Folding Equipment
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly — professional industrial feel with clean, modern aesthetics

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Steel Blue `#1B3A5C` — conveys trust, strength, industrial authority
- **Primary Light**: `#2A5A8C` — hover states, accents
- **Accent**: Warm Gold `#C8963E` — premium feel, CTAs, highlights
- **Accent Light**: `#DDB05C` — hover states for accent
- **Neutral Dark**: `#1A1A2E` — headings, strong text
- **Neutral Mid**: `#4A5568` — body text
- **Neutral Light**: `#F7F8FA` — section backgrounds
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` — cards, clean surfaces
- **Border**: `#E2E8F0` — subtle borders

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: font-weight 700-800, tracking tight, color Neutral Dark
- **Body**: font-weight 400, color Neutral Mid
- **Accent Text**: font-weight 600, color Primary or Accent

## Spacing & Layout
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows on cards: `shadow-lg` or custom soft shadows
- Rounded corners: `rounded-lg` to `rounded-xl`
- Professional imagery of machinery and industrial settings
- Gold accent lines/borders for premium feel
- Smooth hover transitions on interactive elements

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid primary (steel blue), solid accent (gold), outline variants
- **Cards**: White background, subtle border, soft shadow, hover lift effect
- **Navigation**: Clean top bar with logo, links, CTA button
- **Sections**: Alternating white and light gray backgrounds

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace for an elegant feel
- Keep typography clean and hierarchical
- Use gold accents sparingly for premium touch
- Ensure all text is highly readable against backgrounds
- Use professional industrial imagery

## Don'ts
- Don't use overly bright or neon colors
- Don't clutter layouts with too many elements
- Don't use thin/light font weights for important text
- Don't use low-contrast text combinations
- Don't use playful or casual design elements
