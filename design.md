# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium but accessible. The experience should feel like a modern fashion editorial rather than a discount marketplace.

## Color direction
Use one consistent palette:
- Background ivory: `bg-velmora-ivory`
- Soft panel: `bg-velmora-latte`
- Deep espresso text/base: `text-velmora-espresso`
- Muted body text: `text-velmora-mocha`
- Gold accent: `bg-velmora-gold`, `text-velmora-gold`, `border-velmora-gold`
- Dark hero surfaces: `bg-velmora-ink`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Playfair Display
- Body and UI: Inter
- Product names should use uppercase with wide letter spacing.

## Spacing and layout
- Generous vertical spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Max width container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Use thin dividers: `border border-white/10` or `border-velmora-line`

## Components
- Buttons: refined, slightly rounded, no pill-heavy discount styling
- Cards: soft shadows, restrained hover lift, visible readable text at all times
- Inputs: warm light surfaces with strong contrast
- Header: transparent over hero, solid after scroll

## Do
- Keep imagery large and editorial
- Use soft transitions and subtle hover motion
- Preserve whitespace and clear content hierarchy
- Keep text contrast strong on every surface

## Don't
- Do not use neon colors or bright sales-red accents
- Do not use cramped grids or loud badges
- Do not use invisible or low-contrast text
- Do not make it feel like a discount marketplace
