# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Quiet luxury with warm editorial restraint. The site should feel premium, calm, feminine, and modern. Avoid loud sale aesthetics, bright promotional colors, chunky UI, or crowded layouts.

## Color system
Use one cohesive direction across the entire storefront:
- Background base: deep espresso-plum
- Surface: warm ivory and soft champagne
- Accent: antique gold
- Secondary neutral: muted taupe
- Text on dark backgrounds: warm ivory
- Text on light backgrounds: deep espresso
- Dividers: thin hairline lines in softened gold or low-contrast taupe

Recommended Tailwind-friendly named colors:
- `velvet`: dark brand base
- `ivory`: primary light background
- `champagne`: secondary light surface
- `gold`: accent
- `taupe`: soft neutral

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body and UI: Inter
- Product names should appear in uppercase with generous letter spacing.
- Use large elegant serif headlines with restrained line heights.

## Layout and spacing
- Generous whitespace.
- Large editorial image blocks.
- Max content width around `max-w-7xl`.
- Use roomy section padding such as `py-16`, `py-20`, `py-24`.
- Use hairline borders and subtle shadows instead of heavy outlines.

## Buttons and interactions
- Primary buttons: solid dark or gold-accented with refined hover transitions.
- Secondary buttons: transparent or ivory with thin border.
- Hover motion should be subtle: small translate, opacity, or shadow transitions.
- Avoid bouncy or playful interactions.

## Cards and surfaces
- Cards should use soft, premium surfaces with explicit readable text colors.
- Product cards should feel clean and image-led.
- Never rely on inherited text color on custom surfaces.

## Imagery
- Warm-lit close-up jewelry imagery.
- Editorial crops with dark or neutral backdrops.
- Gold jewelry should always feel luminous against the background.
- UGC/Reels strip can feel softer and more lifestyle-oriented.

## Do
- Use strong contrast and legible muted text.
- Keep hierarchy calm and premium.
- Use consistent gold accents sparingly.
- Maintain clean responsive spacing on mobile and desktop.

## Don't
- Do not use discount-store red, neon accents, or loud badges.
- Do not overcrowd sections.
- Do not use low-contrast text on ivory or dark backgrounds.
- Do not mix multiple visual directions.
