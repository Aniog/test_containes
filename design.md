# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Minimalist but sophisticated. Generous whitespace, thin hairlines, and sophisticated typography.

## Palette
- **Primary/Background:** `#FAF9F6` (Off-white / Alabaster) - Warm and soft, feels more premium than pure white.
- **Deep Contrast:** `#1A1A1A` (Rich Black) - Used for primary text, logos, and heavy accents.
- **Metallic Accent (Gold):** `#C5A059` (Soft Gold) - Subtle metallic for CTA buttons and highlights.
- **Muted Earth:** `#8D8273` (Stone) - Used for secondary text or subtle background shifts.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast for all text elements.

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. Elegant and classic.
  - Headings: `font-serif tracking-tight`
  - Product Names: `font-serif uppercase tracking-[0.2em] font-light`
- **Body & UI:** `Inter` or `Manrope`, sans-serif. Clean and functional.
  - Body: `font-sans text-sm md:text-base leading-relaxed text-zinc-900`
  - UI (Buttons, Links): `font-sans uppercase tracking-widest text-xs font-medium`

## UI Elements
- **Buttons:** 
  - Primary: Solid background (`bg-zinc-900 text-white`) or outlined (`border border-zinc-900 hover:bg-zinc-900 hover:text-white transition-all`).
  - Style: Sharp corners or very minimal radius (2px) to keep the "sharp" high-end feel.
- **Dividers:** Thin hairlines (`border-zinc-200` or `border-zinc-100`).
- **Imagery:** Large, high-quality editorial shots. Subtle hover zoom or fade transitions.
- **Transitions:** Smooth, ease-in-out durations (300ms-500ms).

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace between sections (`py-20 md:py-32`).
- Precise margins for a balanced, airy layout.
