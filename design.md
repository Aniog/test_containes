# SSourcing China - Design Guidelines

## Brand Colors
- Primary: Deep navy blue (#1B3A5C) - trustworthy, corporate, B2B
- Primary Light: (#2C5282)
- Accent: (#C44536) - warm red for CTAs and emphasis  
- Accent Hover: (#A3382B)
- Background: (#F8F9FA) - light gray
- Surface: (#FFFFFF) - white cards
- Text Primary: (#1A202C)
- Text Secondary: (#4A5568)
- Text Muted: (#718096)
- Border: (#E2E8F0)
- Success: (#38A169) - for trust badges
- Footer Background: (#0F2540)

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Font)
- H1: 48px, bold, deep navy
- H2: 36px, semibold, deep navy
- H3: 24px, semibold
- Body: 16px/1.6, text secondary
- Small: 14px, muted

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-24
- Container max: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap grid: gap-8

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-md, font-medium, transition
- Cards: bg-white rounded-lg shadow-sm border border-border p-6
- Inputs: rounded-md border border-border px-4 py-3
- Navigation: sticky top-0, bg-white/95 backdrop-blur-sm

## Visual Style
- Clean, professional, minimal
- Lots of whitespace
- Subtle shadows for depth
- Icons for service items
- High-quality imagery with data-strk-img tags
- Responsive, mobile-first
- No exaggerated claims - factual, practical tone

## Do's
- Use consistent spacing
- Keep CTAs prominent but professional
- Use cards to organize information
- Show social proof (logos, case studies, stats)
- Use professional B2B tone

## Don'ts
- No flashy animations
- No exaggerated marketing claims
- No cluttered layouts
- No informal/casual language
