# Velmora Fine Jewelry Visual System

Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial direction: warm ivory surfaces, deep espresso contrast, muted champagne metallic accents, generous whitespace, hairline borders, and soft shadows. The store should feel premium and intimate rather than promotional.

## Palette
- Page background: warm ivory / porcelain (`velmora-ivory`, example `bg-velmora-ivory`).
- Elevated surfaces: cream and warm stone (`velmora-cream`, `velmora-stone`).
- Primary text and dark surfaces: deep espresso (`velmora-espresso`).
- Secondary text: softened taupe (`velmora-taupe`).
- Accent: brushed champagne gold (`velmora-gold`) with darker antique gold (`velmora-antique`).
- Use strong contrast: espresso text on ivory/cream, ivory text on espresso. Do not use pale gold for body text.

## Typography
- Headings and editorial captions: Cormorant Garamond serif via `font-serif`.
- Body and UI: Manrope sans via `font-sans`.
- Product names: uppercase, wide letter spacing (`uppercase tracking-[0.22em]`) in serif or refined sans depending hierarchy.
- Headlines should be large, airy, and line-height tight. Body copy should remain highly readable.

## Layout & Spacing
- Mobile-first. Use single-column sections on mobile, refined multi-column layouts on tablet and desktop.
- Use generous vertical spacing (`py-16`, `md:py-24`) and max-width containers (`max-w-7xl`).
- Use thin hairline dividers (`border-velmora-line`) and restrained cards.
- Preserve editorial whitespace around product imagery and calls to action.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, measured, not loud. Primary is espresso or gold with clear foreground. Secondary is transparent with hairline border.
- Cards: soft cream surfaces, subtle shadows, image-led, smooth hover transitions.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, becomes cream/ivory with shadow and hairline border after scroll.
- Cart drawer and controls must use explicit foreground colors for readability.

## Do's
- Use warm gold jewelry imagery against neutral, editorial, or deep backgrounds.
- Keep animations subtle: small fades, image scale, opacity changes.
- Use concise luxury copy and understated details.

## Don'ts
- No discount-style colors, neon colors, heavy badges, or cluttered promotional blocks.
- No low-contrast text on gold or image backgrounds.
- No generic e-commerce blue links or default browser button styling.
