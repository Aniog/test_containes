# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora Fine Jewelry uses a quiet-luxury editorial style. The experience should feel warm, refined, premium, and modern without looking flashy, discount-driven, or overly minimal to the point of feeling cold.

## Palette
Use one consistent palette sitewide.

- Background base: warm ivory — `bg-velmora-ivory`
- Surface: soft champagne — `bg-velmora-champagne`
- Primary text / dark surface: deep espresso — `text-velmora-espresso`, `bg-velmora-espresso`
- Secondary text: muted taupe — `text-velmora-taupe`
- Accent metallic: brushed gold — `bg-velmora-gold`, `text-velmora-gold`
- Hairline divider / borders: `border-velmora-line`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant and editorial.
- Body and UI: Inter.
- Product names should use uppercase styling with wider tracking, e.g. `uppercase tracking-[0.28em]`.
- Section labels may use uppercase microcopy with strong tracking.

## Spacing and layout
- Prefer generous vertical spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`, `gap-8 md:gap-12`.
- Main content container: `mx-auto w-full max-w-7xl px-5 sm:px-6 lg:px-10`.
- Use breathing room around cards and sections; avoid crowded layouts.

## Components
- Buttons should feel premium: rounded-full, subtle hover lift, strong contrast.
- Cards should use thin borders, soft shadows, and clear readable foreground colors.
- Navigation should be minimal and elegant with hairline separators.
- Use restrained motion only: `duration-300 ease-out`, subtle opacity/translate/scale changes.

## Imagery
- Favor warm-lit editorial close-ups of gold jewelry on skin or neutral studio surfaces.
- Backgrounds should never overpower the products.
- Category tiles and UGC cards should feel curated and refined.

## Do
- Use explicit readable text colors on every custom surface.
- Keep contrast accessible.
- Preserve a premium, premium-but-accessible tone.
- Let whitespace and typography do most of the visual work.

## Don't
- Do not use loud sale colors, bright gradients, or heavy shadows.
- Do not make the site feel generic marketplace or discount-shop.
- Do not stack everything into a single mobile-style column on desktop.
- Do not rely on low-contrast gold text on light backgrounds.
