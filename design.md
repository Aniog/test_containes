# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- Base (near-black): #1a1614 — primary text, deep backgrounds
- Surface (warm cream): #faf7f2 — page background
- Accent (burnished gold): #b8860b — buttons, links, CTAs
- Accent-hover: #9a7009 — hover state for accent
- Muted (warm taupe): #8b7d71 — secondary text, subtle UI
- Border (warm hairline): #e8e0d5 — dividers, card borders
- Card-bg: #ffffff — cards on cream surface
- Nav-bg: rgba(250, 247, 242, 0.92) — frosted glass nav

## Typography
- Headings (serif): Playfair Display — product names, section titles, hero
- Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.15em
- Body (sans): Inter — paragraphs, UI, buttons, navigation
- Sizing: generous, editorial scale

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Generous whitespace throughout

## Components
- Buttons: solid bg-accent text-white, or outlined border-accent text-accent. Rounded, letter-spacing: 0.05em
- Dividers: thin 1px hairline, color border
- Cards: white bg, subtle shadow, rounded-none or minimal radius
- Hover: subtle scale (1.02) + shadow reveal
- Transitions: duration-300 ease-out

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO use uppercase serif for product names with wide tracking
- DO keep generous whitespace
- DO use warm gold accent sparingly
- DON'T use bright/saturated colors
- DON'T use rounded-xl or large border-radius
- DON'T use box shadows that are too heavy
