# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury with a warm editorial sensibility. The store should feel premium, calm, and tactile, never loud or discount-oriented.

## Palette
- Deep base: `noir` / `#17120f` for headers, footer, overlays, and high-contrast editorial panels.
- Warm page surface: `ivory` / `#f8f1e6` for the main background.
- Card surface: `porcelain` / `#fffaf2` for cards, drawers, and form fields.
- Muted neutral: `taupe` / `#7c6a5d` for supporting copy.
- Hairline: `sand` / `#ded0bf` for dividers and borders.
- Metallic accent: `champagne` / `#c79b55` for CTAs, focus rings, icons, and refined highlights.
- Deep accent hover: `bronze` / `#9c7035` for button hover states.

## Typography
- Headings and product names use Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, high contrast, generous line-height.
- Body and UI use Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names are uppercase with wide letter spacing.

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first and spacious, with generous editorial whitespace.
- Desktop sections should use balanced grids instead of single-column mobile stacking.
- Use thin dividers, restrained borders, soft shadows, and large image areas.

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-full, refined padding, champagne or outlined noir/champagne, subtle hover transitions.
- Cards: porcelain surfaces, thin sand borders, soft shadows only on hover.
- Forms: porcelain fields with noir text, sand borders, champagne focus states.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid porcelain/noir text after scroll.

## Do's
- Keep contrast strong and text explicit on all surfaces.
- Use warm gold-friendly imagery and neutral/dark editorial backgrounds.
- Use subtle opacity, blur, and translate transitions.

## Don'ts
- Do not use bright sale colors, red discount badges, or generic ecommerce blue.
- Do not overcrowd product cards or hero copy.
- Do not use low-contrast taupe text for primary information.
