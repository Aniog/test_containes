# Design Style Guide for SSourcing China

## Typography
- Primary Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: Bold (600, 700, 800) with tight letter spacing
- Body: Regular (400) or Medium (500) for readability
- Base size: 16px

## Colors
- Primary Color: `#1E3A8A` (Deep Trust Blue) - Used for primary actions, buttons, and high-impact headings.
- Secondary Color: `#0EA5E9` (Vibrant Blue) - Used for accents, secondary buttons, and icons.
- Accent Color: `#F59E0B` (Action Amber) - Used for CTA buttons to stand out.
- Background Colors:
  - Main background: `#F8FAFC` (Very light blue-gray) or `#FFFFFF`
  - Section alternate background: `#F1F5F9` (Light gray) or `#0F172A` (Dark slate for contrast sections like footer or specific highlighted parts)
- Text Colors:
  - Primary text: `#1E293B` (Dark Slate)
  - Secondary text: `#475569` (Slate)
  - Light text (on dark backgrounds): `#F8FAFC`

## Borders & Radius
- Border Radius:
  - Buttons: `rounded-md` or `rounded-lg` (not fully pill-shaped, keeping it B2B professional)
  - Cards & Containers: `rounded-xl`
- Borders:
  - Subtle borders: `border-slate-200`
  - Hover states on cards: `hover:border-blue-200`

## Shadows
- Cards: `shadow-md` transitioning to `shadow-lg` on hover.
- Sticky Navigation: `shadow-sm`

## Spacing & Layout
- Use generous padding for sections: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container width: `container mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8 max-w-7xl`
- Element spacing: Consistent gaps (e.g., `gap-6` or `gap-8` for grid layouts)

## Visual Style Do's and Don'ts
- Do: Use clean lines, generous whitespace, and readable contrast.
- Do: Use high-quality, professional imagery showing actual factory settings, quality control processes, and shipping containers.
- Do: Use icons consistently (lucide-react) to break up text and illustrate services clearly.
- Don't: Use overly playful or casual typography.
- Don't: Clutter the layout with too much text or tight spacing.
- Don't: Use conflicting bright colors (focus on blues and grays, with one distinct accent for CTAs).
