# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial identity: warm, refined, intimate, and premium without feeling loud or discount-driven. The site should feel like a jewelry lookbook that can also convert quickly on mobile.

## Palette
Use one cohesive palette inspired by antique gold, soft ivory, and deep espresso.

- `velmora-ink` (`#1F1713`): primary text, deep navigation, high-contrast dark surfaces.
- `velmora-espresso` (`#2B211C`): elevated dark panels and footer.
- `velmora-cream` (`#F8F2EA`): main page background.
- `velmora-porcelain` (`#FFFDF8`): cards, forms, drawers.
- `velmora-champagne` (`#D8B978`): metallic accent, primary buttons, icons.
- `velmora-antique` (`#B4874F`): hover accent and secondary gold tone.
- `velmora-taupe` (`#8F7D70`): secondary text.
- `velmora-blush` (`#EFE1D8`): soft editorial blocks.
- `velmora-hairline` (`#E6D8C8`): thin borders and dividers.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: elegant serif, `Cormorant Garamond`.
- Body and UI: clean sans-serif, `Inter`.
- Product names must be uppercase with wide letter-spacing.
- Hero headlines should be generous, fluid, and editorial.

## Layout & spacing
- Mobile-first, generous whitespace, strong vertical rhythm.
- Desktop sections use wide max-width containers with editorial asymmetry.
- Prefer thin hairline dividers over heavy borders.
- Cards should feel calm: soft shadows, rounded corners, cream/porcelain surfaces.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid champagne on dark text or restrained outline buttons.
- Navigation: transparent over hero; solid porcelain/cream with hairline border after scroll.
- Product cards: warm image backgrounds, subtle hover transition, quick add reveal.
- Cart drawer: porcelain surface, explicit dark readable text, clear quantity controls.
- Forms: porcelain fields with hairline borders and high contrast labels/placeholders.

## Do
- Use warm gold accents sparingly.
- Keep all copy readable with explicit foreground colors.
- Use smooth, subtle transitions.
- Use editorial imagery and vertical UGC cards where appropriate.

## Don’t
- Do not use loud sale colors, neon accents, or generic marketplace styling.
- Do not use low-contrast muted text.
- Do not hardcode arbitrary colors in JSX; use Tailwind theme names.
