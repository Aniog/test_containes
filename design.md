# VELMORA Visual Identity

## Quiet Luxury | Warm | Editorial

### Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif). Elegant, sophisticated.
  - Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest-plus`).
- **Body & UI**: Manrope (Sans-serif). Clean, modern, readable.

### Color Palette
- **Background**: Soft Bone / Neutral (`#FDFDFB`)
- **Primary Text**: Deep Onyx (`#1A1A1A`)
- **Accent**: Warm Metallic Gold (`#C5A059`)
- **Muted**: Soft Gray-Beige (`#F5F5F0`)

### Visual Elements
- **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers (`border-black/5`).
- **Transitions**: Smooth, deliberate animations (`duration-500`, framer-motion).
- **Whitespace**: Generous, providing an airy and premium feel.
- **Imagery**: High-end editorial photography with warm lighting.

### Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Onyx or Gold with uppercase tracking.
  - Secondary: Outlined with subtle hover state.
- **Navigation**: Transparent over hero, transitions to solid on scroll.
- **Product Cards**: Minimalist, hover reveals secondary lifestyle image and quick-add button.
