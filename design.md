# SSourcing China Design Guidelines

## Visual Style
- Clean, professional, and trustworthy B2B aesthetic.
- Light, airy backgrounds with strong, high-contrast typography.
- Consistent use of professional blue and grey tones for reliability.

## Typography
- Main Font: Inter (Sans-serif)
- Headings: Semi-bold to Bold
- Body: 16px/1.6 line-height for readability

## Colors
- Primary: blue-700 (#1d4ed8) - Representing trust and authority
- Secondary: slate-600 (#475569) - For supporting text
- Accent: amber-500 (#f59e0b) - For CTAs (provides contrast without being aggressive)
- Background: slate-50 (#f8fafc)

## Components
- Cards: Minimal borders with subtle shadows
- Buttons: Rounded (md) with solid fills for primary actions
- Inputs: Clear borders with focus states

## Do's
- Use realistic stock images of factories, quality control, and shipping.
- Maintain plenty of whitespace.
- Clear, descriptive labels for all sections.
- Responsive layout for mobile and desktop.

## Don'ts
- Avoid using stock photos that look too staged or generic (e.g., handshake photos).
- No aggressive marketing claims or "get rich quick" vibes.
- Avoid cluttered layouts or too many competing CTAs.
