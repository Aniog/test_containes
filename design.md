# SSourcing China - Design System

## Visual Style
- Clean, professional B2B aesthetic
- Trustworthy, international feel
- Factory/QC/shipping imagery
- No exaggerated claims, practical tone

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-weight 600-700
- Body: font-weight 400-500
- Small text: font-weight 400

## Colors
- Primary: Blue (#1e40af) - trust, professionalism
- Primary dark: Dark blue (#1e3a8a)
- Primary light: Light blue (#3b82f6)
- Accent: Orange (#f97316) - CTAs, highlights
- Background: White (#ffffff)
- Background alt: Light gray (#f8fafc)
- Text primary: Dark gray (#0f172a)
- Text secondary: Medium gray (#475569)
- Text muted: Light gray (#94a3b8)
- Border: Light gray (#e2e8f0)
- Success: Green (#22c55e)
- Error: Red (#ef4444)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Component gaps: gap-4, gap-6, gap-8
- Card padding: p-6

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm
- Buttons: rounded-md
- Inputs: rounded-md border border-gray-300

## Layout
- Desktop: multi-column layouts
- Mobile: single column stacking
- Grid: grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3
