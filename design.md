# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **brand-cream** `#FAF7F2` — page background, light surfaces
- **brand-ivory** `#F5F0E8` — card backgrounds, subtle sections
- **brand-warm** `#EDE6DA` — hover states, dividers
- **brand-gold** `#B8860B` — primary accent, CTAs, highlights
- **brand-gold-light** `#D4A843` — hover accent
- **brand-gold-dark** `#8B6914` — pressed accent
- **brand-charcoal** `#1A1A1A` — primary text, dark backgrounds
- **brand-graphite** `#2D2D2D` — secondary dark
- **brand-muted** `#6B6B6B` — body text, descriptions
- **brand-muted-light** `#9A9A9A` — captions, placeholders

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names**: `font-serif` (Cormorant Garamond), weights 300–700
- **Body / UI**: `font-sans` (Inter), weights 300–600
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, `tracking-product` (0.2em)
- **Section headings**: `text-3xl md:text-5xl font-serif font-light`
- **Body text**: `text-sm md:text-base font-sans text-brand-muted`

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections use `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Cards: minimal border, subtle shadow `shadow-sm`, rounded `rounded-none` or `rounded-sm`

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: `border-brand-warm` or `border-brand-ivory`
- No heavy borders. Use `border-t` or `border-b` with 1px

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-brand-charcoal text-white hover:bg-brand-graphite` — solid, no border-radius or minimal
- Accent: `bg-brand-gold text-white hover:bg-brand-gold-light`
- Outlined: `border border-brand-charcoal text-brand-charcoal hover:bg-brand-charcoal hover:text-white`
- All buttons: `px-8 py-3 text-xs tracking-widest uppercase font-sans font-medium transition-all duration-300`

## Shadows & Effects
- Cards: `shadow-none` or very subtle `shadow-sm`
- Hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out`
- Image hover: slight scale `hover:scale-105` with overflow hidden

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep imagery large and editorial
- Use serif for emotional/brand text
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Subtle hover animations
- Warm, inviting tone

## Don'ts
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded corners on cards (keep sharp/editorial)
- No bright/neon colors
- No cluttered layouts
- No generic e-commerce feel
- No discount badges or sale stickers
