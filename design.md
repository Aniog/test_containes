# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Colors
- Background: `#FBF7F0` (warm cream/ivory)
- Secondary background: `#F5EFE6` (warm beige)
- Card/Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Text primary: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)
- Text muted: `#6B6358` (warm gray)
- Accent gold: `#C9A96E` (warm gold)
- Accent gold dark: `#B8944F` (darker gold)
- Border/Divider: `#E8DDD0` (warm taupe)
- Success: `#2D6A4F`
- Error: `#9B2226`

## Typography
- Headings / Product names: "Cormorant Garamond", serif — elegant, editorial
- Body / UI: "Inter", sans-serif — clean, modern
- Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking)
- Generous whitespace throughout

## Tailwind Extensions
- Named colors: `bg-cream`, `bg-warm`, `gold`, `gold-dark`, `warm-border`, `muted-text`
- Font families: `font-serif` for headings, `font-sans` for body

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card padding: p-6
- Whitespace between sections: generous

## Borders
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E8DDD0] (thin, refined)
- Subtle borders on cards: border border-[#E8DDD0]/40

## Shadows
- Soft shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md
- Hover: shadow-lg transition

## Buttons
- Primary: gold background (#C9A96E), white text, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Outline: transparent, gold border, gold text
- Hover: slight darkening of gold
- Pill-style: rounded-full for variant selectors

## Transitions
- Subtle hover transitions: 300ms ease
- Opacity/transform on cards
- Smooth scroll behavior
