# Design System

## Visual Style
Warm, artisan aesthetic inspired by aged cheese and rustic food photography.
Rich amber, golden, and cream tones with deep brown accents.

## Colors
- Primary background: warm cream `#FDF6E3`
- Accent gold: `#D4A017` (use as `text-amber-600` or `bg-amber-600`)
- Deep brown: `#3B1F0A` (use as `text-stone-900`)
- Muted text: `#6B5B45` (use as `text-stone-600`)
- Overlay dark: `rgba(0,0,0,0.45)` for hero overlays

## Typography
- Font: Playfair Display (serif) for headings — elegant, editorial
- Font: Inter (sans-serif) for body text
- Heading sizes: `text-5xl md:text-7xl` for hero titles
- Subheading: `text-xl md:text-2xl`
- Body: `text-base md:text-lg`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-24 px-6 md:px-12`
- Component gap: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Subtle card shadow: `shadow-lg`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-2xl`
- Dividers: `border-amber-200`

## Do's
- Use warm overlays on hero background images
- Use serif fonts for impactful headings
- Keep layouts centered and generous with whitespace

## Don'ts
- Don't use cold blue/grey tones
- Don't use tight spacing in hero sections
- Don't use all-caps for long headings
