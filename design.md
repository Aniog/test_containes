# SSourcing China Design System

## Brand Colors
- Primary: #1e40af (blue-800) - Trust, professionalism
- Primary Light: #3b82f6 (blue-500)
- Accent: #059669 (emerald-600) - Growth, quality
- Accent Light: #10b981 (emerald-500)
- Dark: #1e293b (slate-800)
- Gray: #64748b (slate-500)
- Light BG: #f8fafc (slate-50)
- White: #ffffff

## Typography
- Font: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- Heading weights: 700 (bold) for h1-h2, 600 (semibold) for h3-h4
- Body weight: 400 (regular)
- Body color: #334155 (slate-700)
- Headings: #0f172a (slate-900)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: border border-slate-200
- Card shadow: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
- Transition: transition-all duration-300

## Visual Style
- Clean, minimal, white backgrounds with subtle gray sections
- Gradient hero section with blue-to-slate gradient
- Cards with white bg, subtle borders, hover elevation
- Rounded corners: rounded-lg (cards), rounded-full (badges)
- Icons in primary or accent colors
- CTA buttons: solid blue-800 bg, white text, hover blue-700
- Outline buttons: border-slate-300, hover border-blue-800, hover text-blue-800
- Section headings centered, with a subtle underline accent bar
- Trust badges and stats with large numbers in primary color

## Do's
- Use consistent spacing between sections
- Use the primary blue for main CTAs
- Use emerald green for quality/trust indicators
- Use subtle dividers between sections
- Keep text readable with good contrast

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/flashy colors
- Don't use overly complex layouts
- Don't use exaggerated claims in text
- Don't overcrowd sections
