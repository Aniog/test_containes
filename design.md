# Design Guidelines - ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style: Elegant Industrial
The design should reflect precision, strength, and sophistication. It should be user-friendly with clear navigation and high-quality imagery of machinery.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Sans-serif)
- **Headings**: Semi-bold to Bold, Charcoal (#1a1a1a)
- **Body**: Regular, Slate Gray (#4a4a4a) for readability

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Charcoal (#1a1a1a) - `text-slate-900`, `bg-slate-900`
- **Secondary**: Steel Gray (#64748b) - `text-slate-500`
- **Accent**: Industrial Amber (#B45309) - `text-amber-700`, `bg-amber-700`
- **Background**: Off-White (#F8FAFC) - `bg-slate-50`
- **Surface**: White (#FFFFFF) - `bg-white`

## Components Style
- **Buttons**: Rounded-md, transitioning from Charcoal to Amber on hover.
- **Cards**: Subtle shadows (`shadow-sm`), rounded corners (`rounded-lg`), clean borders.
- **Layout**: Spacious with plenty of white space to emphasize high-end machinery.
- **Imagery**: High-resolution, professional shots of metal folders and folding machines.

## Do's
- Use high-contrast headings.
- Ensure all machinery terminology is prominent and clear.
- Use industrial icons (Lucide React).
- Implement responsive layouts (mobile-first).

## Don'ts
- Avoid cluttered layouts.
- Don't use neon or overly bright colors.
- Avoid generic stock photos if specific machine types are available.
