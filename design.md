# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry for women 25–45. No loud discount cues, no generic e-commerce clutter.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#F8F6F2` (warm cream)
- Surfaces: `#EFEBE4` (parchment), `#E8E3DB` (muted)
- Text: `#2A211C` (deep espresso), `#3E322A` (chocolate), `#7A6E64` (warm stone)
- Accent / CTA: `#BFA15F` (warm gold)
- Accent hover: `#9A7F4A`
- Borders / hairlines: `#D8D0C5`

## Typography
- Headings & product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif.
- Body & UI: Inter, clean sans-serif.
- Product names: uppercase, `tracking-widest`.
- Body size: 14–16px desktop, generous line-height (1.6–1.75).

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-32`
- Container: max-w-7xl, px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Generous whitespace; hairline dividers (`border-velmora-border`).

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold (`bg-velmora-accent`) or outlined (`border-velmora-espresso`); no radius; uppercase text; hover transitions.
- Inputs: no radius, thin border, focus ring in accent.
- Cards: minimal, no heavy shadows; soft hover lift.
- Accents: restrained — gold only for CTAs and small highlights.

## Imagery
- Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds.
- Stock slots use 1x1, 4x3, 3x4, 9:16 ratios; placeholder SVG for layout stability.
- Hero uses full-bleed background slot; category tiles use background slots.

## Do
- Maintain strong contrast; all text readable.
- Use consistent warm palette sitewide.
- Keep mobile layouts spacious and tap-friendly.

## Don't
- Use loud discount badges or neon colors.
- Clutter layouts with multiple competing CTAs.
- Use round corners on primary buttons.
