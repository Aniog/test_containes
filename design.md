# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity

### Mood
- **Quiet luxury, warm, editorial** — Premium demi-fine jewelry
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce
- Target: women 25-45, gifting and self-purchase, $30–$120 price point

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- `cream` — #FAF7F2 (warm off-white background)
- `charcoal` — #1A1A1A (primary text, dark backgrounds)
- `warm-black` — #0D0D0D (deepest backgrounds)

**Accent Colors:**
- `champagne` — #D4AF37 (gold accent, CTAs, highlights)
- `soft-gold` — #E8D5A3 (secondary gold, hover states)
- `rose-gold` — #B76E79 (subtle warmth, special accents)

**Neutral Colors:**
- `stone` — #8B8680 (secondary text, borders)
- `light-stone` — #E5E2DD (dividers, subtle backgrounds)
- `ivory` — #FFFEF9 (cards, elevated surfaces)

### Typography

**Headings & Product Names:**
- Font: **Cormorant Garamond** (Google Fonts)
- Weights: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)
- Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.15em

**Body & UI:**
- Font: **Manrope** (Google Fonts)
- Weights: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)
- Clean, readable, modern

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1400px
- Section padding: 80px (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Grid gap: 24px (desktop), 16px (mobile)

### Visual Elements
- Thin hairline dividers: 1px solid #E5E2DD
- Subtle hover transitions: 300ms ease
- Soft shadows: 0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- Buttons: solid or outlined accent, premium feel

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px

## Component Guidelines

### Buttons
- Primary: champagne background, charcoal text, solid
- Secondary: transparent with champagne border, champagne text
- Hover: soft-gold background transition

### Cards
- Ivory background (#FFFEF9)
- Subtle shadow on hover
- Image with second image reveal on hover

### Dividers
- Hairline style: 1px solid #E5E2DD
- Used between sections and within content

### Images
- Warm-lit, editorial style
- Gold jewelry on dark or neutral backgrounds
- Generous whitespace around products
