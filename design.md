# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory/cream)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (pure white for cards)
- Primary text: `#1A1714` (warm charcoal/black)
- Secondary text: `#6B6560` (warm taupe gray)
- Muted text: `#9B9590` (soft warm gray)
- Accent (gold): `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod — rich, not brassy)
- Accent hover: `#9A7209` (deeper gold)
- Accent light: `#F5EDDA` (pale gold wash)
- Border: `#E8E2D9` (warm light border)
- Divider: `#D4CFC7` (hairline warm gray)
- Error: `#C0392B`
- Success: `#27AE60`

## Typography
- Headings / Product names: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
  - Product names: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
  - Hero headlines: large, elegant, normal case
- Body / UI: Inter (sans-serif)
- Font weights: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card padding: p-6
- Gap between grid items: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#D4CFC7]
- Card borders: subtle border border-[#E8E2D9]
- Rounded corners: rounded-sm (2px) for premium feel, NOT rounded-lg

## Shadows
- Soft, subtle shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md on hover
- No heavy drop shadows

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold background, white text, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Secondary: outlined gold border, gold text, transparent bg
- Hover: slight darkening, smooth transition

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions: duration-300 ease-in-out
- Image hover: slight scale (scale-105)
- Fade-in on scroll: opacity transitions
- Cart drawer: slide-in from right

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable with strong contrast
- Use serif for headings, sans-serif for body
- Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing
- Warm, editorial imagery
- Restrained accent color usage

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No heavy shadows or rounded corners
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No bright/neon colors
- No cluttered layouts
