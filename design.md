# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background (cream):** `#FAF8F5` — warm off-white for page backgrounds
- **Surface (ivory):** `#F5F0EB` — slightly warmer for cards and sections
- **Text primary (espresso):** `#1A1412` — deep warm black for headings
- **Text secondary (walnut):** `#5C4A3E` — warm brown for body text
- **Text muted:** `#8C7B6F` — for captions, meta
- **Accent (burnished gold):** `#B8860B` — warm gold for CTAs, highlights
- **Accent hover:** `#9A7209` — darker gold on hover
- **Border (hairline):** `#E8E0D8` — subtle warm dividers
- **Dark surface:** `#1A1412` — for footer, dark sections

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names:** `'Cormorant Garamond', serif` — weights 300–600
- **Body / UI:** `'Inter', sans-serif` — weights 300–500
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em
- **Heading sizes:** Hero: text-5xl/6xl, Section: text-3xl/4xl, Card: text-lg/xl
- **Body:** text-sm to text-base, leading-relaxed

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections py-16 to py-24
- Container max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Cards: rounded-none or rounded-sm (sharp, editorial)
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t with border color `#E8E0D8`

## Components
- **Buttons:** Accent background with cream text, or outlined with accent border. Rounded-none (sharp). px-8 py-3. Uppercase tracking-widest text-xs font-medium.
- **Cards:** No border-radius, subtle shadow-sm on hover, transition-all duration-300
- **Hover effects:** opacity transitions, translateY(-2px), scale(1.02) for images
- **Accordions:** Minimal, hairline borders, serif titles

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep imagery large and editorial
- Use serif for emotional/brand text
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Subtle hover transitions (300ms)
- Uppercase + letter-spacing for product names

## Don'ts
- No rounded corners on cards/buttons (keep sharp/editorial)
- No heavy shadows
- No bright/saturated colors
- No generic e-commerce blue/green
- No small cramped layouts
- No discount-style badges or loud CTAs
