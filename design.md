# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **NOT**: Loud, discount-looking, generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette

### Base
- **Background**: cream (#FBF9F6) — warm off-white
- **Text**: velvet-900 (#1A1817) — deep charcoal, almost black
- **Surface**: cream-200 (#F5F1EA) — soft warm card backgrounds

### Accent
- **Gold**: gold-DEFAULT (#C8A55C) — warm muted gold, used for CTAs, highlights, sparkle
- **Gold hover**: gold-600 (#B08C40)

### Neutrals
- velvet-50 → 900 scale for text hierarchy, dividers, borders
- velvet-100 (#E8E5E0) for hairline dividers
- velvet-600 (#655C53) for secondary text

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Playfair Display (serif), medium weight, UPPERCASE with 0.15em letter-spacing
- **Body & UI**: Inter (sans-serif), 300–500 weight
- **Product names**: uppercase, wide spacing, serif
- **Prices**: sans-serif, medium weight
- **Nav links**: sans-serif, uppercase, small, wide spacing

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl

## Components
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary: bg-gold text-white, uppercase, wide tracking, subtle hover darkening
  - Outline: transparent with dark border, fills on hover
  - Ghost: text-only, gold on hover
- **Dividers**: hairline (1px), velvet-100 color
- **Cards**: soft shadows on hover, transition duration 500ms
- **Images**: editorial, warm-lit, gold jewelry on neutral/dark backgrounds

## Accessibility
- All text must have strong contrast against backgrounds
- Interactive elements must have visible focus states
- Minimum tap target 44px on mobile
