# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Colors
- Base background: `#0F0F0F` (near-black) — deep refined base makes gold glow
- Surface: `#1A1A1A` (dark charcoal) — cards, sections
- Surface elevated: `#222222` — hover states, elevated cards
- Border: `#2A2A2A` — hairline dividers
- Text primary: `#F5F0EB` (warm off-white) — main body text
- Text secondary: `#A09890` (warm gray) — secondary text, labels
- Accent / Gold: `#C9A96E` (warm brushed gold) — CTAs, accents, icons
- Accent hover: `#D4B97F` (lighter gold) — button hover
- Gold muted: `#8A7A5A` — subtle gold for borders
- Star rating: `#C9A96E`

## Typography
- Headings (h1-h4), product names, logo, decorative text: "Cormorant Garamond", serif
  - Product names in UPPERCASE with tracking-widest
- Body, UI, navigation, buttons: "Inter", sans-serif
- Testimonials, pull quotes: "Cormorant Garamond", serif italic

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#2A2A2A]
- Subtle card borders: border border-[#2A2A2A]
- Soft shadows on hover: shadow-lg shadow-black/20
- Rounded corners: rounded-sm for product images, rounded-none for editorial

## Buttons
- Primary (accent): bg-[#C9A96E] text-[#0F0F0F] font-medium uppercase tracking-widest text-xs px-8 py-4 hover:bg-[#D4B97F] transition-all
- Outline: border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E] hover:bg-[#C9A96E] hover:text-[#0F0F0F] transition-all

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions: transition-all duration-300
- Smooth scroll-reveal: opacity-0 translate-y-4 -> opacity-100 translate-y-0
- Nav background transition: bg-opacity transition on scroll
- Product card: image zoom on hover

## Images
- Editorial style, warm lighting
- Dark/neutral backgrounds to make gold jewelry pop
- 3:2 or 4:3 ratio for product shots
- 9:16 for UGC/reel-style content

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable with strong contrast
- Let the gold accent color be the only bright color
- Use thin lines and minimal borders
- Product names in uppercase with wide letter spacing
- Serif for elegance, sans-serif for clarity

## Don'ts
- No bright/bold colors beyond the gold accent
- No heavy borders or thick lines
- No mixed typography styles
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/voucher visual language
