# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury editorial storefront for demi-fine gold jewelry. The interface should feel warm, calm, premium, and accessible rather than promotional or loud.

## Palette
- Ink base: deep espresso-black for navigation, footer, and high-contrast panels. Example Tailwind: `bg-velmora-ink text-velmora-ivory`.
- Ivory surface: warm off-white page background. Example Tailwind: `bg-velmora-ivory text-velmora-ink`.
- Champagne: soft elevated card and section background. Example Tailwind: `bg-velmora-champagne`.
- Parchment: pale neutral panels and form surfaces. Example Tailwind: `bg-velmora-parchment`.
- Gold accent: restrained metallic CTA and highlights. Example Tailwind: `bg-velmora-gold text-velmora-ink`, `border-velmora-gold`.
- Umber: warm brown secondary text/accent. Example Tailwind: `text-velmora-umber`.

## Typography
- Headings and product names use Cormorant Garamond: elegant, high-contrast serif.
- Body, labels, UI, and navigation use Manrope: clean, modern sans-serif.
- Product names are uppercase with wide letter spacing: `uppercase tracking-[0.22em]`.

## Layout & Spacing
- Mobile-first with generous whitespace and clear breathing room.
- Desktop uses editorial two-column splits and spacious grids, never crowded.
- Use thin hairline borders: `border border-velmora-ink/10` or `border-velmora-gold/30`.

## Components
- Buttons: premium restrained pills or rectangles, solid gold for primary, ink outline for secondary.
- Cards: soft shadows, warm backgrounds, subtle hover lift, readable text at all times.
- Imagery: warm gold jewelry, model closeups, neutral/dark editorial styling. Use Strikingly stock image tags instead of hardcoded image URLs.
- Forms: warm neutral fields with explicit dark text and visible placeholder contrast.

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text readable.
- Use restrained animations and subtle transitions.
- Use editorial captions and refined dividers.

## Don’t
- Do not use bright sale colors, discount badges, neon accents, or generic marketplace styling.
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-white text.
- Do not hardcode external image URLs.
