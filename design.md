# Strikingly AI Generators Hub Design System

Use this page as a Strikingly marketing directory with a clean white base, restrained purple accents, and AI gradient only for primary CTAs and the second H1 line.

## Typography
- Headings and buttons: Brandon Grotesque when available; Josefin Sans 700 fallback; uppercase; line-height about 1.2.
- Body: Open Sans 400, 14px base, line-height 1.5.

## Colors
- Brand purple: `#8159BB` for accents, badges, icons, focus outlines, and ghost button borders.
- AI gradient: `linear-gradient(90deg, #7671FF, #CB0C9F)` for primary CTAs and H1 gradient text only.
- Body text: `#636972`; section headings: `#4B5056`; hero heading: `#2E2E2F`.
- Card border: `#C6C9CD`; subtle divider: `#E2E4E7`; light background: `#F4F6F8`; page background: `#FFFFFF`.

## Components
- Buttons: 36px standard height, 44px large height, 4px radius, 9px by 15px padding, uppercase. Filled/gradient buttons always use white text.
- Cards: white, 1px `#C6C9CD` border, 3px radius, 20px padding. Hover changes border to brand purple with a small shadow and slight vertical lift only.
- Tags: 11px uppercase heading font, 2px by 6px padding, 3px radius, brand-purple outline with no fill.

## Layout
- Max content width: 1200px centered.
- Use spacing in 10px increments, with 5px allowed for tight text pairs.
- Sections are separated by 40px vertical padding; hero uses 60-80px vertical padding.
- Responsive grids: 3 columns on desktop, 2 on tablet where relevant, 1 on mobile.

## Do
- Keep text contrast high and explicit.
- Use semantic HTML and visible keyboard focus states.
- Use inline SVG for decorative illustrations.

## Don’t
- Do not use fake social proof, ratings, or invented testimonials.
- Do not use the AI gradient on backgrounds or non-primary accents.
- Do not use placeholder links with `href="#"`.
