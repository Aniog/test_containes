# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry:** Industrial sheet metal folding machinery
- **Tone:** Elegant, professional, authoritative yet approachable

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Brand Blue):** Deep navy tones (brand-900: #102a43) for headers, hero backgrounds, and key UI elements
- **Secondary (Steel Gray):** Cool steel tones (steel-100 to steel-900) for cards, borders, and secondary surfaces
- **Accent (Gold):** Warm gold (#c9882c) for CTAs, highlights, and accent elements
- **Text Primary:** brand-900 (#102a43) on light backgrounds, white on dark backgrounds
- **Text Muted:** steel-500 (#5d7a96) for secondary text
- **Surface Light:** white or steel-50 (#f7f8fa)
- **Surface Dark:** brand-950 (#0a1929)

## Typography
- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings:** font-semibold or font-bold, tracking-tight
- **Body:** font-normal, leading-relaxed
- **Display titles:** font-extrabold, uppercase tracking-widest for brand name

## Layout
- **Max Width:** max-w-7xl (1280px) for content containers
- **Section Padding:** py-20 to py-28 for page sections
- **Card Radius:** rounded-xl or rounded-2xl
- **Card Shadows:** shadow-lg or shadow-xl for product cards

## Spacing
- **Section Gap:** space-y-16 to space-y-24
- **Grid Gap:** gap-6 to gap-8
- **Card Internal:** p-6 to p-8

## Components
- **Buttons:** Rounded-lg with accent gold for primary, outline for secondary
- **Cards:** White background with subtle shadow, hover elevation effect
- **Navigation:** Fixed/sticky top bar with brand colors, white links
- **Hero:** Full-width with background image, overlay gradient, centered text

## Do's
- Use consistent brand colors throughout
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use Inter font at all sizes
- Keep UI clean and professional
- Use Tailwind utility classes exclusively

## Don'ts
- Don't use decorative or script fonts
- Don't use excessive animations
- Don't mix too many accent colors
- Don't crowd elements together
