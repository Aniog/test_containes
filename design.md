# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Color Palette**:
  - **Primary (Background)**: `#FDFCFB` (Stone White) - `bg-[#FDFCFB]`
  - **Secondary (Accents)**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Charcoal) - `text-[#1A1A1A]`, `bg-[#1A1A1A]`
  - **Muted**: `#9D8C70` (Warm Gold/Taupe) - `text-[#9D8C70]`, `bg-[#9D8C70]`
  - **Border**: `#E5E5E5` (Soft Gray) - `border-[#E5E5E5]`
  - **Surface**: `#F9F8F6` (Cream) - `bg-[#F9F8F6]`

## Typography
- **Headings (Serif)**: "Cormorant Garamond", serif. Used for headlines and product names.
  - Tailwind class: `font-serif`
- **Body & UI (Sans)**: "Manrope", sans-serif. Used for body text, UI elements, and navigation.
  - Tailwind class: `font-sans`

## Components & Styles
- **Letter Spacing**: Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline dividers (`border-b border-[#E5E5E5]/50`).
- **Buttons**:
  - **Primary**: Solid Deep Charcoal (`bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white`).
  - **Secondary**: Outlined Deep Charcoal (`border border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A]`).
- **Transitions**: Soft transitions for hovers (`transition-all duration-300 ease-out`).
- **Shadows**: Very soft, subtle shadows (`shadow-sm`).

## Do's and Don'ts
- **Do**: Use generous whitespace.
- **Do**: Keep dividers thin and light.
- **Do**: Use high-quality editorial imagery.
- **Don't**: Use loud colors or heavy drop shadows.
- **Don't**: Use generic sans-serif fonts for main branding/product names.
