# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial sheet metal folding machines
- **Products**: Double folding machine, double folder, sheet metal folder, sheet metal folding machine, metal folder, metal folder machine, metal folding machine

## Visual Style: Elegant but User-Friendly

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a365d` (Deep Navy Blue) - Trust, professionalism, industrial strength
- **Secondary**: `#2d3748` (Charcoal Gray) - Modern, sleek
- **Accent**: `#c9a227` (Brass Gold) - Premium, elegant, machinery precision
- **Background Light**: `#f8f9fa` (Off-white)
- **Background Dark**: `#1a202c` (Dark slate)
- **Text Primary**: `#1a202c` (Near black)
- **Text Secondary**: `#4a5568` (Medium gray)
- **Success**: `#38a169` (Green)
- **Border**: `#e2e8f0` (Light gray)

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: "Playfair Display" (headings) - Elegant, sophisticated
- **Secondary Font**: "Source Sans 3" (body) - Clean, readable, user-friendly
- **Heading Sizes**: 
  - H1: 3.5rem (56px), font-weight: 700
  - H2: 2.5rem (40px), font-weight: 600
  - H3: 1.75rem (28px), font-weight: 600
  - H4: 1.25rem (20px), font-weight: 600
- **Body**: 1rem (16px), font-weight: 400, line-height: 1.6

### Layout & Spacing
- **Max Content Width**: 1280px
- **Section Padding**: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Container Padding**: 24px horizontal
- **Grid Gap**: 32px
- **Card Padding**: 32px

### Visual Effects
- **Shadows**: 
  - Card: `0 4px 20px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.08)`
  - Hover: `0 8px 30px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.12)`
  - Button: `0 4px 14px rgba(26, 54, 93, 0.3)`
- **Border Radius**: 
  - Buttons: 4px (slight rounding, industrial feel)
  - Cards: 8px
  - Images: 4px
- **Transitions**: All transitions 0.3s ease

### Components

#### Header/Navigation
- Fixed position, white background with subtle shadow on scroll
- Logo on left (brand name in Playfair Display, navy blue)
- Navigation links centered (Products, About, Contact)
- CTA button on right ("Get Quote" - gold accent)
- Mobile: Hamburger menu

#### Hero Section
- Full-width, dark background with subtle industrial pattern overlay
- Large headline with brand name emphasis
- Subheadline describing products
- Two CTA buttons: Primary (gold) and Secondary (outline white)
- Decorative machinery illustration or gradient overlay

#### Products Section
- Section title with decorative underline
- Product cards in 3-column grid (responsive to 2 and 1 column)
- Each card: Image placeholder, product name, brief description, "Learn More" link
- Subtle hover effect with shadow and slight lift

#### Features Section
- Alternating layout (image left/text right, then swapped)
- Icon + title + description for each feature
- Features: Precision Engineering, Durability, User-Friendly, Customization

#### About Section
- Company story with image
- Statistics (Years in business, Machines delivered, etc.)
- Clean, professional layout

#### Contact Section
- Contact form with fields: Name, Email, Phone, Company, Message
- Company contact information sidebar
- Map placeholder
- Gold accent on submit button

#### Footer
- Dark background (#1a202c)
- 4 columns: About, Products, Quick Links, Contact
- Social media icons
- Copyright notice

### Responsive Breakpoints
- **Desktop**: 1024px and above
- **Tablet**: 768px - 1023px
- **Mobile**: below 768px

### Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use generous white space for elegance
- DO: Keep text concise and scannable
- DO: Use high-quality imagery (placeholders for now)
- DO: Maintain consistent spacing throughout
- DON'T: Use too many colors (stick to palette)
- DON'T: Overcrowd content
- DON'T: Use flashy animations (keep professional)
- DON'T: Use casual language (keep professional tone)
