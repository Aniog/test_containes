# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#0F0F0F` (near-black) — deep, refined base that makes gold jewelry glow
- Surface: `#1A1A1A` (dark charcoal) — cards, panels, secondary backgrounds
- Surface Elevated: `#242424` — hover states, active elements
- Text Primary: `#F5F0EB` (warm cream) — main body text, readable on dark
- Text Secondary: `#A89F94` (warm taupe) — secondary text, muted labels
- Text Muted: `#6B6560` — captions, meta
- Accent Gold: `#C9A96E` (warm gold) — CTAs, accent lines, price highlights
- Accent Gold Light: `#E0C896` — hover states for gold elements
- Border: `#2A2A2A` — thin dividers, hairline rules
- Border Light: `#363636` — subtle borders
- White: `#FFFFFF` — pure white used sparingly

## Typography
- Headings & Product Names: `Playfair Display`, serif — elegant, editorial
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-lg or tracking-widest)
- Body & UI: `Inter`, sans-serif — clean, readable
- Scale: h1 (text-5xl/6xl), h2 (text-4xl), h3 (text-2xl), body (text-base), small (text-sm)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card padding: p-6
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#2A2A2A]
- Subtle borders on cards: border border-[#2A2A2A]
- No heavy borders

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C9A96E] text-[#0F0F0F] hover:bg-[#E0C896] — solid gold
- Outline: border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E] hover:bg-[#C9A96E] hover:text-[#0F0F0F]
- Pill shape: rounded-full
- Padding: px-8 py-3

## Shadows
- Subtle glow: shadow-lg shadow-black/20
- Card hover: shadow-xl shadow-black/30

## Transitions
- Default: transition-all duration-300 ease-out
- Hover: subtle opacity shifts, smooth color transitions

## DO's
- Use warm gold tones against the dark background
- Keep generous whitespace
- Thin, elegant dividers
- Serif for editorial feel
- Subtle hover animations
- Full-bleed editorial imagery

## DON'Ts
- No bright reds, blues, or neon
- No heavy borders
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount-store aesthetics
- No generic stock photos
- No loud or flashy animations
