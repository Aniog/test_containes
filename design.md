# SSourcing China — Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: SSourcing China
- **Tagline**: China Sourcing Agent for Global Buyers
- **Tone**: Professional, trustworthy, clear, practical. No exaggerated claims.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Navy `#0f2b46` — trust, authority, B2B professionalism
- **Primary Accent**: Steel Blue `#2563eb` — CTAs, links, interactive elements
- **Secondary**: Warm Gold `#d4a853` — premium feel, highlights, trust badges
- **Background**: White `#ffffff` — clean, spacious
- **Surface**: Light Gray `#f8fafc` — alternating sections
- **Text Primary**: Slate `#1e293b` — body text
- **Text Secondary**: Slate `#64748b` — captions, secondary info
- **Border**: Slate `#e2e8f0` — subtle dividers
- **Success**: Green `#16a34a` — trust indicators
- **Error**: Red `#dc2626` — form validation

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: font-weight 700-800, tracking tight
- **Body**: font-weight 400, line-height 1.6
- **H1**: text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl, font-extrabold
- **H2**: text-3xl md:text-4xl, font-bold
- **H3**: text-xl md:text-2xl, font-semibold
- **Body**: text-base md:text-lg
- **Small/Caption**: text-sm, text-slate-500

## Spacing & Layout
- **Section padding**: py-16 md:py-24
- **Container**: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- **Card padding**: p-6 md:p-8
- **Grid gaps**: gap-6 md:gap-8
- **Border radius**: rounded-lg for cards, rounded-xl for hero elements

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm to shadow-md on cards
- No heavy gradients; use flat colors with subtle depth
- Professional photography: factories, QC inspections, shipping containers, warehouses
- Icons: Lucide React, stroke-width 1.5-2, slate-600 or primary color
- Cards with white backgrounds, subtle borders, hover lift effect

## Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: bg-blue-600 hover:bg-blue-700 text-white, px-6 py-3, rounded-lg, font-semibold
- **Secondary**: border border-slate-300 text-slate-700 hover:bg-slate-50, px-6 py-3, rounded-lg
- **Ghost**: text-blue-600 hover:text-blue-700, no background

## Navigation
- Sticky top nav with white background and subtle shadow on scroll
- Logo left, nav links center/right, CTA button right
- Mobile: hamburger menu with slide-in drawer

## Do's
- Use realistic, professional imagery of factories, QC, shipping
- Keep copy concise and benefit-oriented
- Use data points and specific numbers where possible
- Maintain consistent spacing and alignment
- Use alternating white/light-gray section backgrounds

## Don'ts
- Don't use exaggerated claims ("#1 in China", "Guaranteed lowest prices")
- Don't use cartoon illustrations or clip art
- Don't use bright/saturated colors as backgrounds
- Don't crowd sections with too much text
- Don't use generic stock photos of handshakes
