# MicroCosmos Design System

## Concept
A dark, immersive, science-meets-wonder aesthetic. The site explores the microscopic world — cells, organisms, crystals, and natural patterns invisible to the naked eye. The visual language is deep, rich, and mysterious with pops of vivid bioluminescent color.

## Color Palette
- Background (deep space): `bg-[#050a0f]`
- Surface / card: `bg-[#0d1a24]`
- Surface elevated: `bg-[#112233]`
- Border subtle: `border-[#1e3a4a]`
- Accent teal (primary): `text-[#00d4c8]` / `bg-[#00d4c8]`
- Accent violet: `text-[#9b5de5]` / `bg-[#9b5de5]`
- Accent amber: `text-[#f4a261]`
- Text primary: `text-white`
- Text secondary: `text-slate-300`
- Text muted: `text-slate-500`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (loaded via Google Fonts in index.html)
- Hero heading: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-extrabold tracking-tight`
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- Card title: `text-xl font-semibold`
- Body: `text-base text-slate-300 leading-relaxed`
- Caption / label: `text-sm text-slate-500 uppercase tracking-widest`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Card gap: `gap-6`
- Stack gap: `gap-4`

## Borders & Radius
- Card radius: `rounded-2xl`
- Image radius: `rounded-xl`
- Badge radius: `rounded-full`
- Border: `border border-[#1e3a4a]`

## Shadows & Effects
- Card glow: `shadow-lg shadow-[#00d4c8]/10`
- Hover lift: `hover:-translate-y-1 transition-transform duration-300`
- Image overlay gradient: `bg-gradient-to-t from-[#050a0f] via-transparent to-transparent`

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds throughout; never white or light backgrounds
- Use vivid accent colors sparingly for highlights, badges, and CTAs
- Images should be large, full-bleed or grid-based with generous sizing
- Use subtle glowing borders on featured cards
- Maintain high contrast between text and background at all times

## Don'ts
- No light mode or white backgrounds
- No small, cramped image thumbnails — images should breathe
- No flat, colorless UI — always use the accent palette for interactive elements
- No low-contrast text (e.g., dark gray on dark background)
