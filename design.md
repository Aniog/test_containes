# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. High-end storefront feel with generous whitespace and large images.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Background)**: `#FDFBF7` (Soft Cream/Warm White) - Clean, editorial feel.
- **Secondary (Accents)**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Off-Black) - For high-contrast typography and buttons.
- **Muted (Dividers/Borders)**: `#E5E0D8` (Warm Stone) - Subtle hairline dividers.
- **Highlight (Gold)**: `#C5A059` (Soft Gold) - Reserved for specific accents or CTA buttons.
- **Text**: 
  - Main: `#2A2A2A`
  - Muted: `#6B6B6B`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif). Elegant, editorial style.
- **Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif), UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.15em.
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif). Clean, legible, modern.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**: Square edges or very slight (2px) radius. Solid black or outline black.
- **Dividers**: 1px solid stone hairlines.
- **Transitions**: Ease-in-out, 400ms duration for soft transitions.
- **Images**: Large, full-bleed or generous containers. Aspect ratios: 3:2, 4:5, 1:1.

## Components (Tailwind Classes)
- **Header**: `bg-transparent backdrop-blur-sm border-b transition-all`
- **Button (Primary)**: `bg-black text-white hover:bg-gray-800 transition-colors uppercase tracking-widest text-sm px-8 py-4`
- **Product Card**: `group relative overflow-hidden bg-white hover:shadow-lg transition-shadow`
- **Link (Footer)**: `text-sm text-gray-600 hover:text-black transition-colors`
