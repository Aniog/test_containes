# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Overview
Elegant industrial aesthetic for precision sheet metal machinery. Clean, trustworthy, and user-friendly.

## Typography
- Font family: Inter
- Headings: font-bold, tight tracking
- Body: font-normal, relaxed leading
- Small labels: uppercase tracking-wider

## Colors
- Primary background: white
- Secondary background: slate-50
- Dark sections: slate-900
- Primary text: slate-900
- Secondary text: slate-600
- Accent/interactive: slate-900 / slate-800
- Borders: slate-200

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 to py-20
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl
- Card padding: p-6
- Grid gaps: gap-6 to gap-8

## Components
- Cards: rounded-xl, border border-slate-200, shadow-sm
- Buttons: rounded-lg, bg-slate-900 text-white, hover:bg-slate-800
- Inputs: rounded-lg, border border-slate-300, focus:ring-slate-900
- Images: rounded-lg or rounded-xl depending on context

## Do's
- Use slate palette for consistency
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use subtle borders and shadows
- Maintain clear visual hierarchy

## Don'ts
- Avoid bright or neon colors
- Don't use heavy gradients
- Avoid cluttered layouts
- Don't use low-contrast text
