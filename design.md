# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury. Warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry aesthetic.
Deep obsidian base with warm ivory surfaces, gold metallic accents.

## Color Palette
- **Obsidian** `#1C1917` — primary dark background, text on light
- **Obsidian Light** `#292524` — secondary dark surface
- **Ivory** `#FAF7F2` — primary page background
- **Ivory Dark** `#F0EBE1` — card backgrounds, subtle sections
- **Parchment** `#E8DFD0` — borders, hairline dividers
- **Gold** `#C9A96E` — primary accent (CTAs, highlights, icons)
- **Gold Light** `#DFC08A` — hover states, shimmer
- **Gold Dark** `#A8854A` — pressed states
- **Warm Gray** `#8C8279` — secondary text
- **Warm Gray Light** `#B5AFA8` — muted/placeholder text

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: `font-serif` (Cormorant Garamond) — elegant, editorial
  - Hero H1: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-light tracking-wide`
  - Section titles: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-light`
  - Product names: `text-sm tracking-widest uppercase font-sans font-medium`
- **Body / UI**: `font-sans` (Manrope) — clean, modern
  - Body: `text-sm font-normal`
  - Labels: `text-xs tracking-wider uppercase`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Card gap: `gap-4 md:gap-6`

## Borders & Shadows
- Hairline dividers: `border-parchment` (1px)
- Card shadow: `shadow-sm` with `hover:shadow-md`
- No heavy borders — use whitespace and subtle shadows

## Buttons
- **Primary (Gold)**: `bg-gold text-obsidian hover:bg-gold-light` — solid, premium
- **Outlined**: `border border-gold text-gold hover:bg-gold hover:text-obsidian`
- **Ghost**: `text-obsidian hover:text-gold` — nav links
- Padding: `px-8 py-3` — generous
- Letter spacing: `tracking-widest` on button text
- Transition: `transition-all duration-300`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Large editorial imagery (full-bleed hero, big product images)
- Serif for all headings and product names
- Gold accent sparingly — it should feel precious
- Subtle hover transitions (opacity, scale, color)
- Thin hairline dividers between sections

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded corners on images (keep them square/rectangular)
- No generic e-commerce blue/green CTAs
- No crowded layouts — breathe
