# Strikingly Generators Hub Design System

## Visual direction
- Clean Strikingly product marketing page with a white page background and subtle gray dividers.
- Use the AI gradient only for primary CTA fills and the second line of the hero headline.
- Avoid decorative excess. The page should feel like a useful product directory, not a search trap.

## Typography
- Heading font: Brandon Grotesque, fallback Josefin Sans, then Poppins. Use uppercase and bold weight.
- Body font: Open Sans.
- Large heading rhythm: tight, geometric, compact.
- Body copy: clear, readable, neutral gray.

## Color tokens
- Brand purple: `#8159BB`
- AI gradient start: `#7671FF`
- AI gradient end: `#CB0C9F`
- Body text: `#636972`
- Section headings: `#4B5056`
- Hero heading dark: `#2E2E2F`
- Card border: `#C6C9CD`
- Divider: `#E2E4E7`
- Light background: `#F4F6F8`
- White background: `#FFFFFF`

## Components
- Buttons: 4px radius, uppercase heading font, 36px default height, 44px large height, white text on filled styles.
- Cards: white background, 1px border, 3px radius, 20px padding, subtle shadow on hover, purple border on hover.
- Tags: uppercase heading font, 11px, 2px by 6px padding, 3px radius, purple outline only.

## Spacing
- Use multiples of 10px for layout spacing, with 5px only for tight pairings.
- Max content width: 1200px.
- Section spacing: 40px between major sections.
- Hero padding: 60px to 80px vertical.

## Do
- Keep all text clearly readable with explicit foreground colors.
- Use semantic HTML and visible focus states.
- Keep desktop layouts multi-column where appropriate.

## Don’t
- Do not use black backgrounds or low-contrast text.
- Do not use gradient backgrounds for full sections.
- Do not use fake ratings, testimonials, or inflated metrics.
