# Visual Style Guide - SSourcing China

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: 'Inter', system-ui, sans-serif
- **Headings**: Semi-bold to Bold (font-weight: 600-700), clean sans-serif.
- **Body Text**: Regular (font-weight: 400), readable line height (1.6).

## Colors
- **Primary (Trust & Professionalism)**: Navy Blue (#1a365d) - `bg-slate-900`, `text-slate-900`
- **Secondary (Action & Energy)**: Royal Blue (#2563eb) - `bg-blue-600`, `text-blue-600`
- **Accent (Highlight)**: Amber (#d97706) - `bg-amber-600`, `text-amber-600` (Use sparingly for CTAs)
- **Backgrounds**: 
  - Main: White (#ffffff)
  - Section Alt: Light Gray (#f8fafc) - `bg-slate-50`
- **Text**:
  - Heading: Dark Slate (#0f172a) - `text-slate-900`
  - Body: Slate Gray (#475569) - `text-slate-600`

## Spacing & Layout
- Large section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container max-width: 1200px (`max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4`)
- Grid gaps: `gap-8`

## UI Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded-md, bold text, clear contrast.
- **Cards**: Subtle shadow (`shadow-sm`), white background, border (`border-slate-100`).
- **Icons**: Lucide icons in blue or slate.

## Images
- Professional, high-quality, realistic.
- Focus: Factory floors, quality control inspectors, cargo ships, business meetings.
- Overlays: Subtle dark overlays on hero backgrounds to ensure text readability.
