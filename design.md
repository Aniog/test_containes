# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial Machinery / Metal Folding Equipment
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly — premium industrial feel with approachable design

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Navy Dark** (#0B1426) - Main background, hero sections
- **Navy Medium** (#132040) - Card backgrounds, secondary sections
- **Navy Light** (#1A2D52) - Hover states, borders

### Accent Colors
- **Gold** (#C8A45C) - Primary accent, CTAs, highlights, icons
- **Gold Light** (#D4B76A) - Hover states for gold elements
- **Gold Muted** (#A68B4B) - Subtle accents

### Neutral Colors
- **White** (#FFFFFF) - Primary text on dark backgrounds
- **Gray Light** (#E2E8F0) - Secondary text, subtle borders
- **Gray Medium** (#94A3B8) - Muted text, placeholders
- **Gray Dark** (#475569) - Tertiary text

### Surface Colors
- **Card Dark** (#0F1B33) - Product cards, feature cards
- **Card Hover** (#162244) - Card hover states
- **Divider** (#1E3A5F) - Subtle dividers and lines

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: Font-weight 700-800, letter-spacing tight
- **Body**: Font-weight 400, line-height 1.6
- **CTA/Buttons**: Font-weight 600, uppercase, letter-spacing wide

### Scale
- Hero Title: text-5xl md:text-6xl lg:text-7xl
- Section Title: text-3xl md:text-4xl lg:text-5xl
- Card Title: text-xl md:text-2xl
- Body: text-base md:text-lg
- Small/Caption: text-sm

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28 lg:py-32
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Element gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Border radius: rounded-lg (cards), rounded-full (buttons, badges)
- Borders: border border-navy-light or border-gold/20
- Shadows: shadow-2xl with dark navy tones
- Card hover: subtle gold border glow

## Visual Style Rules
- Dark, premium aesthetic with navy backgrounds
- Gold accents for CTAs, icons, and key highlights
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle gradients from navy-dark to navy-medium
- Product images with dark overlays and gold accent borders
- Icons in gold color for feature highlights
- Smooth hover transitions (duration-300)

## Do's
- Use gold sparingly for emphasis and CTAs
- Maintain high contrast between text and backgrounds
- Use generous spacing for an elegant feel
- Keep navigation simple and intuitive
- Use subtle animations on hover states

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much content
- Don't use light backgrounds for main sections
- Don't use more than 2 font weights in a single component
- Don't use harsh shadows or heavy borders
