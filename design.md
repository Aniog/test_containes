# SSourcing China - Design System

## Brand Identity
A professional B2B sourcing agency website connecting global buyers with Chinese suppliers. Clean, trustworthy, international aesthetic.

## Colors
- Primary (Navy): #1a3a5c
- Primary Dark: #0d2137
- Accent (Warm Orange): #e8783b
- Accent Hover: #c9622f
- White: #ffffff
- Light Gray (bg): #f5f6f8
- Medium Gray (text secondary): #6b7280
- Dark Text: #1f2937
- Success Green: #059669
- Light Blue Tint: #e8f0f8

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, text-gray-700
- Small labels: text-sm, font-medium, uppercase tracking-wider

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-lg, font-semibold, transition-colors
- Cards: bg-white, rounded-xl, shadow-sm, border border-gray-100
- Section headings: text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold
- Badges: inline-flex, rounded-full, text-xs font-semibold

## Do's
- Use white backgrounds for cards against light gray page
- Use navy for headings and important text
- Use orange accent for CTAs and key actions
- Keep text left-aligned for readability
- Subtle shadows for depth (shadow-sm, shadow-md)

## Don'ts
- No bright colors besides orange accent
- No dark backgrounds for large text areas
- No centered body text
- No excessive animations
- No exaggerated marketing language
