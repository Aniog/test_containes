# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand
Industrial machinery manufacturer specializing in metal folding equipment.
Brand personality: elegant, precise, trustworthy, modern-industrial.

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts), weights 300, 400, 500, 600, 700
- Headings: font-semibold or font-bold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, leading-relaxed
- Sizes: hero headline text-5xl lg:text-7xl, section titles text-3xl lg:text-4xl

## Colors
- Primary: slate-900 (#0f172a) — deep industrial navy
- Accent: amber-500 (#f59e0b) — warm gold for CTAs and highlights
- Accent hover: amber-600
- Background: white / slate-50 for sections
- Surface: white with shadow for cards
- Text: slate-900 for headings, slate-600 for body
- Muted: slate-400

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 lg:py-28
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 lg:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 lg:gap-8

## Cards & Surfaces
- White background, rounded-xl, shadow-sm border border-slate-100
- Hover: shadow-md transition-shadow

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-amber-500 text-white hover:bg-amber-600 rounded-lg px-6 py-3 font-medium
- Secondary: border border-slate-300 text-slate-700 hover:border-slate-900 rounded-lg px-6 py-3

## Do's
- Use clean white backgrounds with subtle shadows
- Keep text high contrast against backgrounds
- Use amber accent sparingly for CTAs and key elements
- Maintain generous whitespace

## Don'ts
- Don't use dark backgrounds for text-heavy sections
- Don't overcrowd with too many elements
- Don't use low-contrast text
