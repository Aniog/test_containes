# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial sheet metal folding machines
- **Products**: Double folding machine, double folder, sheet metal folder, sheet metal folding machine, metal folder, metal folder machine, metal folding machine

## Visual Style: Elegant Industrial

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a1a2e` (Deep navy/charcoal - sophistication)
- **Secondary**: `#16213e` (Dark blue-gray - professionalism)
- **Accent**: `#e94560` (Coral red - industrial energy, CTAs)
- **Light Accent**: `#f5a623` (Golden amber - premium feel)
- **Neutral Light**: `#f8f9fa` (Off-white)
- **Neutral Mid**: `#6c757d` (Gray for secondary text)
- **Text Primary**: `#1a1a2e`
- **Text Light**: `#ffffff`
- **Text Muted**: `#adb5bd`

### Typography
- **Headings**: "Playfair Display" - elegant serif for sophistication
- **Body**: "DM Sans" - clean, modern, highly readable
- **Accent/Logo**: "Bebas Neue" - bold industrial feel

### Layout Principles
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Grid-based product displays
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Professional imagery with industrial aesthetic

### Design Elements
- Subtle box shadows for depth: `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- Rounded corners: 8px for cards, 4px for buttons
- Smooth transitions: 0.3s ease for hover effects
- Hero section with full-width imagery
- Product cards with hover lift effect

### Do's
- Use high-quality industrial imagery
- Maintain consistent spacing (section padding: 80px vertical)
- Use accent colors sparingly for CTAs
- Include trust indicators (certifications, experience)
- Clear navigation with product categories

### Don'ts
- Don't use too many colors (keep it cohesive)
- Don't clutter pages with excessive text
- Don't use casual fonts for this industrial brand
- Don't make it look like a generic e-commerce site
