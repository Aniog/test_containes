# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry with gold tones. Not loud, not discount, not generic.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#F7F4EF` warm off-white (paper-like)
- **Foreground / text**: `#2A2721` deep espresso
- **Muted text**: `#6F6A60` warm gray
- **Hairlines / borders**: `#D9D3C9`
- **Accent (CTA)**: `#9C6F38` antique gold
- **Accent hover**: `#7A562A` deeper gold
- **Accent foreground**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Surface card**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Dark overlay**: rgba(42, 39, 33, 0.55)
- **Hero text shadow**: rgba(42, 39, 33, 0.35)

## Typography
- **Headings / serif**: Cormorant Garamond (300, 400, 500, 600, 700)
- **Body / UI sans**: Inter (300, 400, 500, 600)
- Product names: uppercase, tracking-[0.2em], font-weight 500, font-serif
- Headings: lightweight serifs, large sizes, tight leading

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace. Section vertical padding: 80–120px desktop, 56–72px mobile.
- Container max-width: 1280px centered with px-6 md:px-10.
- Hairline dividers: 1px solid border.

## Buttons
- Primary: solid accent (`bg-accent text-white`), hover `bg-accent-hover`, no border-radius (square) or subtle 1px radius. Uppercase text, tracking-widest, py-3 px-8.
- Secondary / ghost: transparent with `border border-foreground/30`, hover `bg-foreground text-background`.
- Transition: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`.

## Imagery
- Warm gold jewelry on neutral/dark backgrounds.
- Editorial, large, full-bleed where appropriate.
- Use stock-image tagging system for dynamic assets.

## Elevation
- Soft shadows: `shadow-[0_8px_30px_rgba(42,39,33,0.08)]`
- Card hover lift: `-translate-y-1 shadow-lg`

## Do's
- Keep contrast high; all text readable.
- Use whitespace as a luxury signal.
- Keep animations subtle (fade/slide 300–500ms).

## Don'ts
- No neon colors, no heavy drop shadows, no loud discount badges.
- No black pure #000 backgrounds.
