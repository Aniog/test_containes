# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
**Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Elegant serif)
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Clean sans-serif)

## Color Palette
A cohesive, elegant palette that flatters gold jewelry.

- **Base Colors**:
  - Background: Soft cream/ivory (`#fdfbf7`)
  - Surface: White (`#ffffff`)
  - Foreground (Text): Very dark olive/brown-black (`#2d2c29`)
  - Subdued Text: Muted warm gray (`#7a7873`)

- **Accent Colors**:
  - Primary Accent (Buttons, Highlights): Warm tarnished gold / bronze (`#b49a5b`)
  - Primary Accent Hover: Darker bronze (`#947b41`)
  - Divider/Border: Faint warm gray (`#e8e6e1`)

## Styling Rules
- **Product Names**: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`).
- **Whitespace**: Generous padding and margins, creating an editorial feel.
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline dividers (`border-t border-border`).
- **Transitions**: Subtle hover effects (e.g. `transition-all duration-300`).
- **Shadows**: Soft, editorial shadows (`shadow-sm`, rarely harsh).
- **Buttons**: Premium feel. Solid accent for primary actions, outlined accent for secondary. No border radius (`rounded-none`).
