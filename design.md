# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Base (near-black): #1A1A1A - primary text, footer
- Surface (warm ivory): #F5F2ED - page background
- Surface Alt: #EBE7DF - card backgrounds, dividers
- Accent (warm gold): #C8A97E - buttons, links, highlights
- Accent Hover: #B8976A - button hover states
- Text Primary: #1A1A1A
- Text Secondary: #6B6560 - muted text, captions
- Text On Dark: #F5F2ED - text on dark backgrounds
- White: #FFFFFF - cards, elevated surfaces

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) - product names, section titles, hero
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) - UI, descriptions, buttons
- Product names: uppercase with wide letter-spacing (tracking-[0.15em])
- Section headings: 3xl-5xl serif with generous margins

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: section padding py-20 to py-28
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#EBE7DF]
- Card padding: p-6 to p-8

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C8A97E] text-white hover:bg-[#B8976A]
- Outline: border border-[#C8A97E] text-[#C8A97E] hover:bg-[#C8A97E] hover:text-white
- Rounded: minimal border-radius (rounded-sm or rounded)
- Uppercase tracking for main CTAs

## Shadows & Effects
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm or custom soft shadows
- Smooth transitions: transition-all duration-300
- Hover: subtle lift or color shift, never gaudy
