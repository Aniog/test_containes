# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: Velmora Fine Jewelry
- **Tagline**: Crafted to be Treasured
- **Target Audience**: Women 25-45, gifting and self-purchase
- **Price Range**: $30–$120 (premium-but-accessible)
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Background**: `#FAF9F7` (warm off-white/cream)
- **Background Dark**: `#1A1918` (warm charcoal for dark sections)
- **Text Primary**: `#1A1918` (warm charcoal)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6B6560` (warm gray)
- **Text Light**: `#FAF9F7` (for dark backgrounds)

### Accent Colors
- **Gold Accent**: `#C9A962` (warm gold)
- **Gold Hover**: `#B8954F` (darker gold)
- **Gold Light**: `#E8DCC4` (soft gold for backgrounds)

### UI Colors
- **Border**: `#E5E2DE` (warm light gray)
- **Border Dark**: `#3D3935` (warm dark gray)
- **Card Background**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Overlay**: `rgba(26, 25, 24, 0.5)`

## Typography

### Font Families
- **Headings/Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond` (serif) - elegant, editorial
- **Body/UI**: `Manrope` (sans-serif) - clean, modern, readable

### Font Sizes
- **Hero Title**: 56px / 3.5rem (desktop), 36px / 2.25rem (mobile)
- **Section Title**: 40px / 2.5rem (desktop), 28px / 1.75rem (mobile)
- **Product Name**: 18px / 1.125rem, uppercase, letter-spacing: 0.15em
- **Body**: 16px / 1rem
- **Small/Caption**: 14px / 0.875rem
- **Tiny**: 12px / 0.75rem

### Font Weights
- **Serif Headings**: 500, 600
- **Sans-serif Body**: 400, 500, 600

## Spacing System
- **Base unit**: 4px
- **Section padding**: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Container max-width**: 1280px
- **Grid gap**: 24px (desktop), 16px (mobile)
- **Card padding**: 24px

## Visual Effects

### Shadows
- **Card hover**: `0 20px 40px rgba(26, 25, 24, 0.08)`
- **Button hover**: `0 8px 24px rgba(201, 169, 98, 0.25)`
- **Modal/Drawer**: `0 25px 50px rgba(26, 25, 24, 0.15)`

### Borders
- **Hairline dividers**: 1px solid #E5E2DE
- **Button border**: 1.5px solid
- **Border radius**: 0 (sharp, editorial) or 4px (subtle)

### Animations
- **Transition duration**: 300ms ease
- **Hover transitions**: subtle transform (translateY), opacity changes
- **Page transitions**: fade-in on load

## Component Styles

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Gold background, dark text, solid
  - Default: bg-gold, text-charcoal
  - Hover: darker gold, subtle shadow
- **Secondary**: Outlined, transparent background
  - Default: border-gold, text-gold
  - Hover: gold background, dark text

### Cards
- White background
- Subtle shadow on hover
- Product image with second image on hover
- Quick "Add to Cart" appears on hover

### Form Inputs
- Clean border, no background
- Focus: gold border
- Placeholder: warm gray

### Dividers
- Hairline style: 1px solid with low opacity

## Layout Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: < 640px
- **Tablet**: 640px - 1024px
- **Desktop**: > 1024px

## Design Principles
1. Generous whitespace - let content breathe
2. Large editorial imagery - hero images should be impactful
3. Thin hairline dividers - subtle separation
4. Restrained accent color - gold used sparingly but confidently
5. Subtle hover transitions - nothing jarring
6. Clean, premium feel - avoid clutter
