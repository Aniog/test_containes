# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial look with a deep espresso-plum foundation, warm ivory surfaces, and restrained brushed-gold accents. The storefront should feel premium, calm, and intimate rather than promotional or loud.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant and airy
- Body/UI: Inter, clean and modern
- Product names should appear uppercase with generous tracking

## Tailwind tokens
- Background base: `bg-noir`
- Elevated dark surfaces: `bg-noir-soft`
- Warm light surfaces: `bg-ivory`
- Muted highlight surfaces: `bg-mist`
- Primary text on dark: `text-ivory`
- Primary text on light: `text-noir`
- Secondary text: `text-taupe`
- Accent: `bg-gold`, `text-gold`, `border-gold`
- Hairlines: `border-white/10` on dark, `border-noir/10` on light
- Heading font: `font-display`
- Body font: `font-sans`
- Signature letter spacing: `tracking-brand`
- Shadows: `shadow-soft`, `shadow-card`

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous vertical rhythm with roomy section padding
- Favor large editorial image blocks with clean text groupings
- Use thin dividers instead of heavy borders
- Keep cards elegant with soft corners and subtle shadows
- On desktop, preserve multi-column layouts with breathing room
- On mobile, stack content cleanly without making the design feel cramped

## Buttons and interactions
- Primary buttons use the gold accent with dark text and soft hover lift
- Secondary buttons use transparent or ivory surfaces with refined borders
- Hover states should be subtle: small translation, opacity shift, or shadow increase
- Avoid flashy animations, gradients, and discount-style badges

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text readable on every surface
- Let product imagery and whitespace carry the premium feel
- Use restrained accent color and serif headlines for emphasis
- Maintain consistency across homepage, collection pages, and PDP layouts

## Don't
- Do not use loud sale styling, bright promotional reds, or cluttered badges
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-cream text
- Do not overfill sections with copy or dense UI chrome
- Do not make buttons or cards feel generic marketplace-style
