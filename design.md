# ARTITECT MACHINERY Visual Style Guide

## Brand mood
Elegant, precise, and user-friendly. The site should feel like a premium industrial machinery partner: confident, clean, technical, and approachable.

## Color system
Use a deep graphite and steel palette with a controlled brass accent.
- Graphite surfaces: `machine-950`, `machine-900`, `machine-800`
- Light steel backgrounds: `steel-50`, `steel-100`, `steel-200`
- Body text: `machine-700` on light surfaces, `steel-100` on dark surfaces
- Muted text: `machine-500` on light surfaces, `steel-300` on dark surfaces
- Accent: `brass-500` for primary buttons, highlights, and small decorative details

## Typography
Use Inter from Google Fonts for all text. Headings should be bold, tightly spaced, and professional. Body text should be readable and friendly.
- Large hero headings: `text-4xl md:text-6xl font-semibold tracking-tight`
- Section headings: `text-3xl md:text-5xl font-semibold tracking-tight`
- Body text: `text-base md:text-lg leading-7`

## Layout and spacing
Use generous spacing, clear sections, and responsive grids.
- Page max width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 lg:px-8`
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Cards: rounded corners, subtle borders, soft shadows, and explicit foreground colors

## Components
- Buttons should be rounded, clear, and high contrast.
- Product cards should combine clean technical text with subtle image or icon support.
- Navigation should stay simple and readable.

## Do
- Keep contrast strong on every surface.
- Use industrial imagery sparingly and only where it supports the machinery story.
- Keep copy focused on folding machines, metal folders, precision, and easy operation.

## Don't
- Do not use low-contrast grey text on pale backgrounds.
- Do not overuse bright colors.
- Do not create crowded layouts on mobile.
