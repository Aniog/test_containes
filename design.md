# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- Industrial precision meets elegant simplicity
- Professional, trustworthy, and approachable
- Clean lines inspired by sheet metal fabrication

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Navy):** `navy-900` (#0f172a), `navy-800` (#1e293b), `navy-700` (#334155)
- **Accent (Amber/Gold):** `amber-500` (#f59e0b), `amber-400` (#fbbf24), `amber-600` (#d97706)
- **Neutral:** `slate-50` (#f8fafc), `slate-100` (#f1f5f9), `slate-200` (#e2e8f0), `slate-600` (#475569), `slate-800` (#1e293b)
- **White:** #ffffff for cards and content areas
- **Success:** emerald-500 for positive states

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (Google Fonts) — clean, modern, highly readable
- **Headings:** font-bold, tracking-tight
  - H1: text-5xl lg:text-6xl
  - H2: text-3xl lg:text-4xl
  - H3: text-xl lg:text-2xl
- **Body:** text-base, text-slate-600 on light backgrounds, text-slate-300 on dark
- **Small/Caption:** text-sm, text-slate-500

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 lg:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 lg:p-8
- Component gaps: gap-6 or gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-200
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Hover cards: shadow-lg transition-shadow duration-300
- Subtle dividers: border-slate-200

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-amber-500 hover:bg-amber-600 text-slate-900 font-semibold rounded-lg px-6 py-3
- Secondary: border-2 border-slate-800 text-slate-800 hover:bg-slate-800 hover:text-white rounded-lg px-6 py-3
- On dark: bg-amber-500 hover:bg-amber-400 text-slate-900

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Use subtle hover animations (transition-all duration-300)
- Keep text readable with proper contrast
- Use amber accents sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Use icons from Lucide React for visual clarity

## Don'ts
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors
- Don't use heavy drop shadows
- Don't crowd content — let it breathe
- Don't use pure black (#000) — use navy/slate dark tones instead
