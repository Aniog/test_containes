# Cake Shop Design System

## Brand Identity
Warm, artisan bakery feel. Elegant yet approachable. Soft pastels with rich accent tones.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Rose)**: `#e8637a` — CTAs, highlights, accents. Tailwind: `bg-rose-400`, `text-rose-400`
- **Primary Dark**: `#c94d63` — Hover states. Tailwind: `bg-rose-600`
- **Cream/Background**: `#fdf6ee` — Page background. Tailwind: `bg-[#fdf6ee]`
- **Warm White**: `#fffaf5` — Card backgrounds. Tailwind: `bg-[#fffaf5]`
- **Brown/Text**: `#3d2b1f` — Headings. Tailwind: `text-[#3d2b1f]`
- **Muted Brown**: `#7a5c4a` — Body text. Tailwind: `text-[#7a5c4a]`
- **Light Tan**: `#f0dcc8` — Borders, dividers. Tailwind: `border-[#f0dcc8]`
- **Gold Accent**: `#d4a853` — Stars, badges. Tailwind: `text-[#d4a853]`

## Typography
- **Font**: Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body)
- **Hero heading**: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold` Playfair Display
- **Section heading**: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold` Playfair Display
- **Card title**: `text-xl font-semibold`
- **Body**: `text-base` Inter, `text-[#7a5c4a]`
- **Small/caption**: `text-sm`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-6`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between cards: `gap-8`
- Max content width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-[#f0dcc8]`
- Card shadow: `shadow-md hover:shadow-xl`
- Border radius: `rounded-2xl` for cards, `rounded-full` for buttons/badges
- Transition: `transition-all duration-300`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-rose-400 hover:bg-rose-500 text-white font-semibold py-3 px-8 rounded-full`
- Secondary: `border-2 border-rose-400 text-rose-400 hover:bg-rose-50 font-semibold py-3 px-8 rounded-full`

## Do's
- Use warm, inviting photography
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use Playfair Display for all headings
- Keep CTAs prominent with rose color

## Don'ts
- No cold blues or grays as primary colors
- No harsh black backgrounds
- No tight, cramped layouts
