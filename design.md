# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF7F2` (warm cream)
- Card/Surface: `#FFFFFF` (white)
- Dark text: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)
- Muted text: `#6B6258` (warm gray)
- Accent Gold: `#C9A84C` (warm gold)
- Accent Gold light: `#E8D5A3` (pale gold)
- Border/Divider: `#E5D9C5` (warm beige)
- Deep Dark: `#1C1C1C` (used sparingly for dark sections)
- Light overlay: `#F5F0EB` (soft warm off-white)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif, 300-600 weight)
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif, 300-500 weight)
- Product names: UPPERCASE, tracking-widest, font-medium
- Logo: Cormorant Garamond, uppercase, tracking-wider

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card padding: p-4 md:p-6
- Gaps between elements: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E5D9C5] (1px)
- Subtle card borders: border border-[#E5D9C5]/40
- Rounded corners: rounded-sm (2px) or rounded (4px) — never large radii

## Buttons
- Primary: gold background (#C9A84C), dark text, uppercase, tracking-widest, text-xs
- Outline: border gold, transparent bg, gold text
- Hover: subtle darken on gold, or border fill on outline
- Transition: all 0.3s ease

## Hover Effects
- Images: scale(1.03) with 0.5s ease
- Links: opacity transition
- Product cards: subtle shadow elevation

## Shadows
- Card shadow: shadow-sm (subtle)
- Elevated: shadow-md
- Soft and warm, never harsh

## Product Grid
- 5 columns on desktop, 2 on mobile, 3 on tablet
- Product name uppercase, tracking-widest, text-xs
- Price in body font, text-sm
- Secondary image on hover with crossfade

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable with good contrast
- Thin, elegant dividers
- Warm gold accents sparingly
- Editorial, editorial, editorial

## Don'ts
- No large border radii (no rounded-2xl, no circles for images)
- No bright neons or cold grays
- No heavy shadows
- No loud call-to-action language
- No generic stock photos
- No cluttered layouts
