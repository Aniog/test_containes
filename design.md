# SSourcing China - Design System

## Visual Style

**Overall Aesthetic**: Clean, trustworthy, international B2B. Professional and practical with no exaggerated claims. Focus on clarity and credibility.

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: #1a365d (Deep navy blue) - Trust, professionalism
- Secondary: #2c5282 (Medium blue) - Supporting elements
- Accent: #3182ce (Bright blue) - CTAs and highlights
- Text Primary: #1a202c (Near black) - Main body text
- Text Secondary: #4a5568 (Dark gray) - Supporting text
- Background: #ffffff (White) - Main content areas
- Background Light: #f7fafc (Off-white) - Alternating sections
- Border: #e2e8f0 (Light gray) - Dividers and cards
- Success: #38a169 (Green) - Positive indicators

**Typography**:
- Font Family: Inter (system-ui fallback)
- Headings: 600-700 weight, tight tracking
- Body: 400-500 weight, 1.6 line-height
- Small text: 14px, secondary color

**Spacing**:
- Section padding: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Container max-width: 1280px
- Card gap: 24px
- Consistent 8px grid system

**Components**:
- Buttons: Rounded corners (6px), 48px height for primary CTAs
- Cards: White background, subtle shadow, 1px border
- Forms: Clean inputs with focus states, 48px height
- Navigation: Fixed header, clean links, mobile hamburger

**Imagery**:
- Professional factory, QC inspection, and shipping visuals
- Realistic photography style
- Consistent aspect ratios (16:9 for hero, 4:3 for cards)

**Do's**:
- Use ample white space for breathing room
- Maintain consistent alignment across sections
- Use icons sparingly and purposefully (Lucide icons)
- Ensure all text has sufficient contrast
- Keep CTAs prominent but not overwhelming

**Don'ts**:
- Avoid overly bright or saturated colors
- No exaggerated claims or hype language
- Don't overcrowd sections with too many elements
- Avoid decorative elements that don't serve a purpose
- No stock photo clichés or generic business imagery
