# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Colors
- `velmora-bg`: `#F5F0EB` — warm cream page background
- `velmora-bg-dark`: `#1A1512` — deep warm brown/black for dark sections
- `velmora-gold`: `#C9A96E` — warm metallic accent gold
- `velmora-gold-light`: `#E8D5B5` — soft gold for hover states
- `velmora-gold-dark`: `#B8924E` — darker gold for active states
- `velmora-text`: `#1A1512` — primary text on light
- `velmora-text-light`: `#F5F0EB` — primary text on dark
- `velmora-muted`: `#8B7E74` — secondary/muted text
- `velmora-border`: `#E5DCD4` — hairline borders, dividers
- `velmora-card`: `#FFFFFF` — card backgrounds
- `velmora-overlay`: `rgba(26, 21, 18, 0.6)` — overlay on dark bg

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond` — serif, elegant
- **Body / UI**: `Inter` — clean sans-serif
- Product names: UPPERCASE with `tracking-widest`
- Section headings: uppercase, letter-spacing, gold accent

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Card gap: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders
- Thin hairline dividers: `border-t border-[#E5DCD4]`
- Subtle shadows: `shadow-sm` or `shadow-md` for cards
- Soft hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300`

## Buttons
- Primary: gold background (`bg-velmora-gold`), dark text, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Outline: transparent with gold border, uppercase
- Hover: subtle darkening of gold
- Rounded: `rounded-none` for editorial feel, or `rounded-sm`

## Images
- Editorial style, warm lighting
- Gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Use data-strk-img attributes for stock image system
