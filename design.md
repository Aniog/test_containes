# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** cream-50 (#fefcf7) - warm off-white page background
- **Surface:** pearl-50 (#fafaf9) - card/testimonial backgrounds
- **Text primary:** midnight-900 (#4b3b32) - deep warm brown-black
- **Text secondary:** pearl-600 (#948674) - muted warm gray
- **Accent / CTA:** brand-600 (#b0762a) - warm gold/bronze
- **Dark sections:** midnight-950 (#1a1410) - very dark warm brown for footer, newsletter, UGC reel
- **Borders/divider:** pearl-200 (#e2dfd9) - thin hairline dividers
- **Stars:** brand-500 (#c98d34) - gold for ratings

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond (serif) — light weight (300), italic for emphasis
- **Product names:** Cormorant Garamond, uppercase, tracking-wider (0.12em)
- **Body / UI:** Inter (sans-serif) — weights 300, 400, 500
- **Section labels:** Inter, uppercase, tracking-widest, small, pearl-600
- **CTA buttons:** Inter, uppercase, tracking-wider, small/medium

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Max width: max-w-7xl mx-auto
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Grid gaps: 3-5 on mobile, 5-6 on desktop

## Components
- **Buttons:** `.btn-primary` (brand-600 bg, white text, uppercase) | `.btn-outline` (border midnight-900, transparent bg)
- **Dividers:** thin hairline borders using pearl-200
- **Cards:** clean, minimal borders, soft hover transitions
- **Product cards:** 3:4 aspect ratio images, centered name/price below, hover reveals second image + quick-add button
- **Navbar:** transparent over hero, solid cream-50/95 backdrop-blur on scroll

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep it editorial and clean
- Gold accents on warm neutral backgrounds
- Thin, elegant lines and borders
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300 to 500)
- Product names in uppercase with wide letter-spacing

## Don'ts
- No bright colors or flashy gradients
- No heavy shadows or thick borders
- No loud sale/discount banners
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- Don't overcrowd sections
