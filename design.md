# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Tagline:** Precision Engineered. Perfectly Formed.
- **Industry:** Industrial sheet metal folding machinery

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Deep Navy):** `#0D1B2A` — main backgrounds, navbar
- **Accent (Steel Blue):** `#1E6FA5` — CTAs, highlights, links
- **Accent Light:** `#3A9BD5` — hover states, gradients
- **Gold/Copper:** `#C8922A` — premium accent, badges, borders
- **Surface Light:** `#F4F6F9` — page background, card backgrounds
- **Surface White:** `#FFFFFF` — card surfaces
- **Text Dark:** `#0D1B2A` — headings on light backgrounds
- **Text Mid:** `#4A5568` — body text, descriptions
- **Text Light:** `#F4F6F9` — text on dark backgrounds

## Typography
- **Font Family:** "Montserrat" (headings) + "Inter" (body) — loaded via Google Fonts
- **H1:** `text-5xl font-bold tracking-tight` (Montserrat)
- **H2:** `text-3xl font-bold tracking-tight` (Montserrat)
- **H3:** `text-xl font-semibold` (Montserrat)
- **Body:** `text-base font-normal leading-relaxed` (Inter)
- **Small/Caption:** `text-sm text-gray-500`

## Spacing & Layout
- **Max content width:** `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6`
- **Section padding:** `py-20 px-6`
- **Card padding:** `p-8`
- **Border radius:** `rounded-2xl` for cards, `rounded-full` for pills/badges

## Component Styles

### Navbar
- Dark navy background `bg-[#0D1B2A]`
- Logo in white + gold accent
- Nav links in `text-gray-300 hover:text-white`
- CTA button: gold border + text, hover fills gold

### Buttons
- **Primary:** `bg-[#1E6FA5] text-white hover:bg-[#3A9BD5] rounded-lg px-6 py-3 font-semibold`
- **Outline Gold:** `border-2 border-[#C8922A] text-[#C8922A] hover:bg-[#C8922A] hover:text-white rounded-lg px-6 py-3 font-semibold`
- **Ghost:** `text-[#1E6FA5] hover:underline`

### Cards
- `bg-white rounded-2xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-xl transition-shadow`
- Subtle top border accent: `border-t-4 border-[#1E6FA5]`

### Hero
- Full-width dark navy background with subtle industrial texture overlay
- Large white heading, steel blue subheading accent
- Gold CTA button

### Section Dividers
- Thin gold line: `border-t border-[#C8922A] opacity-30`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace to convey premium quality
- Use steel blue for interactive elements
- Use gold sparingly for premium accents and highlights
- Keep imagery industrial and professional
- Use uppercase tracking for section labels: `text-xs uppercase tracking-widest text-[#C8922A]`

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/neon colors
- Don't crowd elements — maintain breathing room
- Don't use more than 2 font weights per section
- Don't use rounded corners larger than `rounded-2xl` on cards
