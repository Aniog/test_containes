# Design Guidelines - SSourcing China

## Visual Style
Clean, trustworthy, international B2B. A mix of professional corporate blue and industrial accents.

## Typography
- Main Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: Semibold/Bold, tracking-tight.
- Body: Regular, leading-relaxed for readability.

## Colors
- Primary: #1e3a8a (Slate Blue - Trust)
- Secondary: #f97316 (Orange - Action/CTA)
- Accent: #64748b (Slate - Professional)
- Background: #ffffff (White), #f8fafc (Off-white)
- Text: #0f172a (Dark Slate)

## Spacing & Layout
- Container: max-w-7xl
- Section Padding: py-16 to py-24
- Grid: 12-column system, standard gaps (6-8)

## Components
- Cards: subtle shadows, rounded-lg.
- Buttons: rounded-md, transition-all.
- Forms: clean inputs, clear labels.

## Do's
- Use realistic imagery of factories, QC, and logistics.
- Keep text clear and practical.
- Use Sonner for notifications.

## Don'ts
- No exaggerated claims.
- Avoid cluttered layouts.
- Don't use generic stock photos that look too staged.
