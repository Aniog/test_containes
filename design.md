# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF9F6` (warm ivory/cream)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (pure white)
- Text Primary: `#1A1A1A` (near black)
- Text Secondary: `#6B6B6B` (warm gray)
- Text Muted: `#9A9A9A`
- Accent Gold: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod - rich, not brassy)
- Accent Gold Light: `#D4A843` (lighter gold for hover)
- Accent Gold Dark: `#8B6914` (deep gold)
- Border: `#E8E4DF` (warm light border)
- Border Light: `#F0EDE8`
- Dark Section: `#1A1A1A` (near black for footer/hero overlays)
- Dark Text: `#FAF9F6` (ivory on dark)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, modern
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card padding: p-6
- Gap between elements: gap-4, gap-6, gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E8E4DF]
- Subtle borders on cards: border border-[#E8E4DF]
- Rounded corners: rounded-sm (2px) for premium feel

## Shadows
- Soft, subtle shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md
- Card hover: shadow-lg transition

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold background, white text, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Secondary: outlined gold border, gold text, transparent bg
- Hover: slight opacity change or darker gold

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions: transition-all duration-300
- Image zoom on hover: scale-105
- Fade in on scroll: opacity-0 to opacity-100

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use gold accents sparingly
- Maintain strong contrast for accessibility
- Use uppercase for product names with wide letter-spacing

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No heavy shadows or borders
- No bright/neon colors
