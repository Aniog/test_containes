# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: warm ivory `#FAF8F5` (tailwind: bg-stone-50)
- Surface: pure white `#FFFFFF` (tailwind: bg-white)
- Primary text: deep charcoal `#1C1917` (tailwind: text-stone-900)
- Secondary text: warm gray `#78716C` (tailwind: text-stone-500)
- Accent: warm gold `#B8860B` (tailwind: text-amber-700 / bg-amber-700)
- Accent hover: deeper gold `#996515` (tailwind: hover:bg-amber-800)
- Border: soft stone `#E7E5E4` (tailwind: border-stone-200)
- Hairline divider: `#D6D3D1` (tailwind: border-stone-300)
- Dark sections: rich espresso `#1C1917` (tailwind: bg-stone-900)
- Dark text on dark: warm ivory `#FAF8F5` (tailwind: text-stone-50)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable
- Font weights: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8

## Components
- Buttons: solid accent or outlined, rounded-none or rounded-sm (sharp/premium feel)
- Cards: minimal borders, soft shadows on hover
- Dividers: thin hairline (border-t border-stone-200)
- Inputs: minimal, bottom-border or outlined style

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep it minimal and editorial
- Use serif for headings, sans-serif for body
- Gold accents sparingly
- UPPERCASE product names with tracking-widest

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No rounded-full buttons (too casual)
- No heavy shadows
- No busy patterns
