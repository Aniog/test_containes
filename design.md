# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. A deep refined base of espresso brown paired with warm ivory, champagne gold metallic accents, and soft blush. Premium demi-fine jewelry; no loud discount styling.

## Palette
- Background: warm ivory `#F8F5F2`
- Surface: soft cream `#FFFFFF`
- Primary (base): rich espresso `#2C1E18`
- Primary text: espresso `#2C1E18`
- Secondary text: warm taupe `#6F5E56`
- Muted text: warm gray `#A89B95`
- Accent: antique gold `#B8956A`
- Accent hover: deep gold `#9C7B54`
- Hairline border: warm stone `#E6DDD6`
- Overlay: espresso 40% for image captions

## Typography
- Headings / logo / product names: `Cormorant Garamond` serif, 400-600.
- Body / UI: `Inter` or `Manrope` sans-serif, 400-500.
- Product names: uppercase, wide letter-spacing (0.12em-0.18em).

## Spacing & Layout
- Mobile-first; max content width `1280px` centered.
- Generous vertical section spacing: `py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-32`.
- Horizontal padding: `px-6 md:px-10 lg:px-16`.
- Hairline dividers: 1px `border-stone-200`.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid accent (`bg-accent text-white`) or outlined (`border-stone-800 text-stone-900`).
- Pills: rounded-full, thin border, uppercase tracking-widest text-xs.
- Cards: no border-radius on product cards, subtle hover lift.
- Inputs: minimal underline or soft bordered rectangle.

## Motion
- Subtle transitions: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`.
- Hover reveals: opacity/image swaps on product cards.
- Scroll nav: transparent to solid background with backdrop blur.
