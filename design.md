# SSourcing China - Design System

## Brand Identity
- Primary: Deep navy blue (#1a3a5c) - trust, professionalism, international trade
- Accent: Gold (#d4953a) - warmth, quality, premium service
- Background: White (#ffffff) and light gray (#f8fafc) for sections
- Text: Near-black (#0f172a) for headings, slate (#475569) for body text

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-tight
- H1: text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl
- H2: text-3xl md:text-4xl
- H3: text-xl md:text-2xl
- Body: text-base md:text-lg text-slate-600 leading-relaxed

## Spacing
- Sections: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card gaps: gap-8 for grids

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-brand-600 hover:bg-brand-700 text-white font-semibold rounded-lg px-6 py-3
- Secondary: border-2 border-brand-600 text-brand-600 hover:bg-brand-600 hover:text-white rounded-lg px-6 py-3
- CTA/Accent: bg-gold-500 hover:bg-gold-600 text-white font-semibold rounded-lg px-8 py-4

## Cards
- White bg, rounded-xl, shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-shadow
- Border: border border-gray-100

## Visual Style
- Clean, professional B2B look
- Consistent section padding and max-width
- Use white space generously
- Icons from Lucide React
- No exaggerated gradients or animations
- Subtle hover effects only
