# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- Brand: ARTITECT MACHINERY — Precision metal folding solutions
- Style: Elegant, professional, trustworthy, high-end industrial
- Audience: Industrial buyers, factory managers, metal fabrication professionals

## Colors
- Primary navy: `#1a1a2e` (deep, authoritative)
- Gold accent: `#c9a96e` (premium, quality)
- Dark gold: `#b8944f`
- Light gold: `#dfc79a`
- Light navy: `#2d2d4e`
- Slate: `#4a4a6a`
- Light slate: `#8a8aaa`
- Cream background: `#f8f6f1`
- Dark cream: `#ede8df`
- White: `#ffffff`
- Dark text: `#1a1a2e`

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) — elegant, refined
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern, readable
- Hero headings: Playfair Display, bold
- Section titles: Playfair Display, semi-bold
- Body text: Inter, regular

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Grid gaps: gap-8

## Component Styles
### Buttons
- Primary: bg-brand-gold text-white, hover:bg-brand-gold-dark, rounded-none, uppercase tracking-wider
- Secondary: border-2 border-brand-navy text-brand-navy, hover:bg-brand-navy hover:text-white

### Cards
- bg-white, shadow-sm, border border-brand-cream-dark
- Hover: shadow-md, subtle translateY

### Navigation
- Sticky header, white background with bottom border
- Navy text, gold hover accent
- Dropdown or simple inline nav

## Borders
- Thin borders: border border-[#e5e0d8]
- Card borders: border border-brand-cream-dark

## Effects
- Subtle box shadows for depth
- Smooth hover transitions (200-300ms)
- Fade-in animations on scroll sections
