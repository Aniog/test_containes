# SSourcing China Design System

## Brand direction
- Professional, reliable, international B2B presentation.
- Visual tone should feel practical and trustworthy rather than promotional.
- The design should suggest factory visits, quality control, export coordination, and buyer confidence.

## Color system
- Primary: deep navy for authority and trust. Example Tailwind classes: `bg-slate-950`, `text-slate-950`.
- Secondary: steel blue for accents and links. Example Tailwind classes: `bg-sky-700`, `text-sky-700`.
- Surface: clean white and soft slate backgrounds. Example Tailwind classes: `bg-white`, `bg-slate-50`, `bg-slate-100`.
- Success/status accents: muted emerald. Example Tailwind classes: `bg-emerald-50`, `text-emerald-700`.
- Borders: subtle neutral lines. Example Tailwind classes: `border-slate-200`, `border-slate-300`.

## Typography
- Use Inter from Google Fonts.
- Headlines: strong, compact, professional. Example classes: `text-4xl md:text-5xl font-semibold tracking-tight`.
- Section titles: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-semibold tracking-tight`.
- Body text: readable and neutral. Example classes: `text-base md:text-lg leading-7 text-slate-600`.
- Small labels and meta text: `text-sm font-medium uppercase tracking-[0.18em] text-sky-700`.

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous whitespace and clear section separation.
- Standard container: `mx-auto w-full max-w-7xl px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`.
- Section spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`.
- Card spacing: `rounded-3xl p-6 md:p-8`.
- Desktop layouts should use multi-column grids where content benefits from comparison.

## Components
- Buttons should be solid, simple, and businesslike.
  - Primary: `bg-sky-700 text-white hover:bg-sky-800`.
  - Secondary: `border border-slate-300 bg-white text-slate-900 hover:bg-slate-50`.
- Cards should use white backgrounds, subtle borders, and soft shadows.
- Badges should use small uppercase text with high contrast.
- Use explicit text colors inside every card and section.

## Images
- Use realistic visuals related to supplier verification, production follow-up, product inspection, shipping coordination, factories, and packaged export goods.
- Prefer wide aspect images in hero and case study areas, and balanced supporting images in service grids.
- Do not overuse imagery; content clarity remains the priority.

## Do
- Keep wording clear and practical.
- Show process, proof points, and operational details.
- Maintain strong readability on every background.
- Use consistent rounded corners and restrained shadows.

## Don’t
- Do not use exaggerated growth claims or flashy gradients.
- Do not use low-contrast text on light or dark surfaces.
- Do not crowd mobile layouts.
- Do not use casual startup-style marketing language.
