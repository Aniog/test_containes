# Design Guidelines - ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Brand Overview
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Products**: Double folding machine, double folder, sheet metal folder, sheet metal folding machine, metal folder, metal folder machine, metal folding machine
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a1a2e` (Deep navy/charcoal - professional, industrial)
- **Secondary**: `#16213e` (Darker blue for depth)
- **Accent**: `#e94560` (Elegant coral red - CTAs and highlights)
- **Light**: `#f8f9fa` (Off-white for backgrounds)
- **Text Primary**: `#1a1a2e` (Dark for readability)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6c757d` (Muted gray)
- **Border**: `#dee2e6` (Subtle borders)

## Typography
- **Headings**: "Playfair Display" - elegant serif for brand prestige
- **Body**: "Inter" - clean, readable sans-serif for user-friendliness
- **Font Sizes**:
  - H1: 3.5rem (hero), 2.5rem (sections)
  - H2: 2rem
  - H3: 1.5rem
  - Body: 1rem
  - Small: 0.875rem

## Layout & Spacing
- **Max Width**: 1200px container
- **Section Padding**: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Card Padding**: 24px
- **Grid Gap**: 32px
- **Border Radius**: 8px (cards), 4px (buttons)

## Visual Effects
- **Shadows**: Subtle box-shadow for cards: `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- **Hover Effects**: Smooth transitions (0.3s ease), slight lift on cards
- **Backgrounds**: Clean white/off-white with subtle gradients on hero

## Components

### Header
- Fixed navigation with white background
- Logo on left (Playfair Display font)
- Navigation links centered
- Contact CTA button on right

### Hero Section
- Full-width with subtle gradient background
- Large headline with brand name emphasis
- Subheadline describing products
- Two CTA buttons: primary (accent color) and secondary (outline)

### Products Section
- Section title with underline accent
- 3-column grid (responsive to 1 column on mobile)
- Product cards with:
  - Icon or placeholder image area
  - Product name (H3)
  - Brief description
  - "Learn More" link

### Features Section
- Alternating background (light gray)
- Feature items with icon, title, description
- 4-column grid on desktop

### Contact Section
- Dark background (primary color)
- Centered content
- Contact information and inquiry CTA

### Footer
- Dark background
- Brand info, quick links, contact info
- Copyright notice

## Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: < 640px (single column, stacked)
- **Tablet**: 640px - 1024px (2 columns)
- **Desktop**: > 1024px (full layout)

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use generous whitespace for elegance
- DO: Keep text concise and scannable
- DO: Use consistent accent color for CTAs
- DON'T: Use too many colors (keep to palette)
- DON'T: Make text too small or dense
- DON'T: Use flashy animations (keep professional)
