# Design Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, and premium demi-fine jewelry. The design avoids loud or generic "discount" e-commerce aesthetics in favor of a refined, high-end feel.

## Color Palette
- **Background (Primary)**: `bg-[#FDFCFB]` (Soft warm eggshell)
- **Foreground (Text)**: `text-[#1A1A1A]` (Deep charcoal)
- **Accent (Gold)**: `text-[#C5A059]` / `bg-[#C5A059]` (Refined muted gold)
- **Muted**: `text-[#71717A]` (Cool gray)
- **Border**: `border-[#E5E5E5]` (Thin light hairline)
- **Hero/Dark Sections**: `bg-[#1A1A1A]` with `text-[#FDFCFB]`

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Serif - Cormorant Garamond (`font-serif`)
  - Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`)
- **Body & UI**: Sans-serif - Inter (`font-sans`)

## Components Styles
- **Buttons**:
    - Primary: Solid deep charcoal (`bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white`) or muted gold.
    - Secondary: Outlined (`border border-[#1A1A1A]`)
    - Transitions: `transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out`
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace. Standard sections have `py-16` or `py-24`.
- **Dividers**: Thin `h-[1px]` or `border-t-[1px]` hairlines.
- **Shadows**: Very soft, subtle shadows (`shadow-sm`) or none.

## Do's
- Use uppercase for serif headings when appropriate.
- Leave plenty of breathing room around elements.
- Use thin hairlines instead of heavy borders.
- Center-aligned navigation links for balance.

## Don'ts
- No loud or saturated colors.
- No heavy drop shadows.
- No rounded corners on buttons (keep them sharp or very slightly rounded).
- No cluttered layouts.
