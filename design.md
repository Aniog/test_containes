# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design Identity

## Visual Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Generous whitespace, large editorial imagery, thin hairline dividers.

## Color Palette
- **Background (Primary)**: `#FDFCFB` (Soft warm white) - `bg-[#FDFCFB]`
- **Foreground (Text)**: `#1A1A1A` (Elegant charcoal/black) - `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#C5A059` (Warm metallic gold) - `bg-[#C5A059]`, `text-[#C5A059]`, `border-[#C5A059]`
- **Muted/Secondary**: `#F5F2F0` (Elegant greige) - `bg-[#F5F2F0]`
- **Border**: `#D1D5DB` (Light hairline dividers) - `border-gray-200` or `border-[#E5E5E5]`

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif). Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).
- **Body & UI**: `Inter` (Sans-serif).

## UI Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Accent (`bg-[#C5A059] text-white`) or Solid Dark (`bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white`).
  - Secondary: Outlined Accent (`border border-[#C5A059] text-[#C5A059] hover:bg-[#C5A059] hover:text-white transition-all`).
- **Cards**: Subtle hover transitions, soft shadows, clean hairline borders.
- **Dividers**: Thin `1px` lines.

## Guidelines
- Avoid loud colors or heavy gradients.
- Prioritize whitespace for a "breathable" luxury feel.
- Imagery should be warm-lit and editorial.
