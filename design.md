# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Typography
- Serif (Headings, Product Names, Logos): Cormorant Garamond
- Sans-serif (Body, UI, Metadata): Inter

## Color Palette
Base:
- Beige: #FAF9F5 (Background, cards)
- Stone: #E5E0D8 (Dividers, borders, image placeholders, inactive states)
- Charcoal: #2D2A26 (Primary text, dark backgrounds, footers)

Accents:
- Gold: #Cbb26A (Primary buttons, accents on hover)
- Dark Gold: #A68D47 (Secondary accents, prices, active states)
- Clay: #8B857D (Subtext, metallic text accents)

## Core Design Principles
- **Quiet Luxury:** Muted tones, expansive whitespace, no loud badges or bright red sales markers.
- **Editorial Feel:** Large edge-to-edge images, 3:4 aspect ratios on product cards.
- **Micro-interactions:** Slow fades on hover (e.g. 500ms opacity fades on images, 300ms color fades on links), delicate animations.
- **Clean Forms:** Inputs and buttons are minimalistic, borders are 1px and subtle.

## Do's
- Product names in serif, UPPERCASE, tracking-wide.
- Category names in sans-serif, UPPERCASE, tracking-widest.
- Lots of padding around text.

## Don'ts
- Saturated primary colors.
- Rounded playful borders (prefer sharp edges or tiny 2px/sm border radius).
- Drop shadows (unless extremely subtle).
