# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial Machinery - Metal Folding Equipment
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly. Professional industrial feel with refined aesthetics.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) - Clean, modern, highly legible
- **Headings**: Inter 700/800 weight, tracking tight
- **Body**: Inter 400 weight, leading relaxed
- **Accent/Label**: Inter 500 weight, uppercase tracking wide for labels

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Navy `#1B2A4A` - Trust, professionalism, authority
- **Primary Light**: `#2D4A7A` - Hover states, accents
- **Accent**: Warm Gold `#C8963E` - Premium feel, CTAs, highlights
- **Accent Light**: `#D4A854` - Hover states for gold elements
- **Background**: Off-White `#F8F7F4` - Warm, elegant base
- **Surface**: White `#FFFFFF` - Cards, elevated surfaces
- **Text Primary**: `#1B2A4A` - Same as primary for cohesion
- **Text Secondary**: `#5A6B82` - Muted descriptions
- **Text Muted**: `#8A96A8` - Subtle labels, placeholders
- **Border**: `#E2E0DB` - Warm gray borders
- **Success**: `#2D8A56` - Form success states
- **Error**: `#C44536` - Form error states

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Element gaps: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-[#E2E0DB]`
- Card shadow: `shadow-sm` or `shadow-md` on hover
- Button radius: `rounded-md` for primary, `rounded-lg` for cards
- Subtle transitions: `transition-all duration-300`

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle gold accents on navy backgrounds for premium feel
- Industrial imagery with warm, professional tone
- Cards with clean borders and subtle shadows
- Full-width hero sections with overlay text
- Grid-based product showcases

## Do's
- Use navy + gold color combination for premium industrial feel
- Keep layouts clean and spacious
- Use high-quality imagery of machinery
- Maintain consistent spacing and alignment
- Use subtle hover transitions on interactive elements
- Ensure all text is clearly readable against backgrounds

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much content
- Don't use thin/light fonts on dark backgrounds
- Don't mix too many font weights in one section
- Don't use harsh shadows or heavy borders
- Don't leave text with low contrast against its background
