# Design Strategy for SSourcing China

## Visual Style
SSourcing China is a professional B2B sourcing agency. The design should reflect trust, transparency, and industrial expertise.

### Typography
- Primary Font: 'Inter', sans-serif (Clean, professional, and universal).
- Headings: Bold, high contrast (text-slate-900).
- Body: Readable, balanced line height (text-slate-600).

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Navy Blue (`#1e293b`, `bg-slate-900`) - Represents stability and professionalism.
- **Secondary/Action**: Industrial Gold/Orange (`#f59e0b`, `bg-amber-500`) - Used for CTAs (Call to Actions) to stand out against navy.
- **Neutral**: Off-White (`#f8fafc`, `bg-slate-50`) and Pure White (`#ffffff`) for clean backgrounds.
- **Accent**: Slate Gray (`#64748b`, `text-slate-500`) for secondary text.

### Spacing & Borders
- Generous white space to maintain a clean, high-end feel.
- Small border radius (`rounded-md` or `rounded-lg`) for a modern yet professional look.
- Subtle shadows (`shadow-sm`, `shadow-md`) to define cards and sections.

### Components
- **Navbar**: Sticky, transparent-to-solid transition, clean navigation links.
- **Hero**: High-impact background image of a modern port or factory, clear headline, and prominent "Free Sourcing Quote" CTA.
- **Service Cards**: Icon-led, brief description, "Learn More" link.
- **Process Timeline**: Visual step-by-step guide.
- **Trust Bar**: Logos of verified certifications or shipping lines.
- **Footer**: Detailed site map, contact info, and social links.

## Do's and Don'ts
### Do's
- Use realistic, high-quality industrial and logistics imagery.
- Use explicit foreground colors (text-slate-900 on white).
- Keep CTAs consistent (Amber/Gold).
- Implement responsive design for mobile (md: and lg: prefixes).

### Don'ts
- Use overly bright or neon colors.
- Use messy or complex fonts.
- Overcrowd sections with too much text.
- Use low-contrast text (e.g., light gray on white).
