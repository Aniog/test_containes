# SSourcing China - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
Professional B2B sourcing agent website. Clean, trustworthy, international.

## Typography
- Font Family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, leading-relaxed
- Sizes: h1 (text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl), h2 (text-3xl md:text-4xl), h3 (text-xl md:text-2xl), body (text-base md:text-lg)

## Colors
- Primary: Navy Blue #1a365d (slate-900 variant) — headers, primary buttons, footer
- Primary Light: #2a4a7f — hover states
- Accent: Amber/Gold #d69e2e — CTAs, highlights, trust badges
- Accent Light: #f6e05e — subtle highlights
- Background: White #ffffff — main bg
- Surface: Gray-50 #f9fafb — section alternates
- Text Primary: #111827 (gray-900)
- Text Secondary: #4b5563 (gray-600)
- Text Muted: #9ca3af (gray-400)
- Success: #059669 (emerald-600)
- Border: #e5e7eb (gray-200)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Inputs: rounded-lg border-gray-300

## Do's
- Use navy blue for primary actions and headings
- Use amber/gold for CTA buttons
- Keep ample white space
- Use professional, factory/manufacturing themed stock images
- Use clear section hierarchy with consistent spacing

## Don'ts
- No neon or overly bright colors
- No exaggerated marketing claims
- No cluttered layouts
- No dark backgrounds for main content areas
- No cursive or decorative fonts
