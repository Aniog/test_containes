# Visual Style Guide - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Typography
- Headings: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif). Elegant, editorial feel. Use for product names, section titles, and hero headlines.
- Body/UI: `Inter` (Sans-serif). Clean, readable.
- Product Names: Uppercase with `tracking-[0.2em]`.

## Colors
- Primary: `#B4975A` (Warm Gold). Used for CTA buttons, accents, and highlights.
- Secondary/Background: `#F5F2ED` (Soft Neutral Paper). Used for sections, cards, and backgrounds to flatters gold.
- Text: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Charcoal) for readability.
- Border: `#E5E7EB` (Light Gray) for thin hairline dividers.

## UI Components
- Buttons: Solid Primary with white text for main CTAs. Outlined for secondary actions.
- Dividers: Thin 1px hairlines.
- Spacing: Generous whitespace to maintain a high-end feel.
- Shadows: Very soft or none to keep a flat, editorial look.

## Imagery
- Large, high-quality editorial images.
- Warm lighting, close-ups of jewelry.
- Ratio: 3x2 or 4x3 for products; 16x9 for hero; 9x16 for UGC reels.

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use uppercase for product titles.
- DO: Keep dividers subtle.
- DON'T: Use harsh shadows or vibrant neon colors.
- DON'T: Overcrowd the layout.
