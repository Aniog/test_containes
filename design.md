# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- Industrial, precision-engineered aesthetic
- Elegant yet approachable
- Conveys strength, reliability, and craftsmanship

## Color Palette
- **Primary Dark (Navy):** #0A1628 — Headlines, hero backgrounds, footer
- **Primary Medium (Navy Light):** #132240 — Card backgrounds, secondary surfaces
- **Accent (Gold):** #C8A44E — CTA buttons, highlights, icons, accent lines
- **Accent Hover (Gold Light):** #D4B86A — Hover states for gold elements
- **Accent Dark (Gold Dark):** #A88A3A — Active/pressed states
- **Steel:** #2C3E50 — Body text on light backgrounds
- **Steel Light:** #3D566E — Secondary body text
- **Gray Light:** #F4F6F8 — Light section backgrounds
- **Gray Medium:** #E0E4EA — Borders, dividers
- **Gray Dark:** #6B7A8D — Muted text, captions
- **White:** #FFFFFF — Light backgrounds, text on dark

## Typography
- Font Family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-weight 700-800, tracking tight
- Body: font-weight 400, leading-relaxed
- Accent/labels: font-weight 600, uppercase tracking-widest (small caps style)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28 lg:py-32
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Component margin: space-y-6 to space-y-12

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl with subtle shadow-lg
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Subtle gold accent lines: h-[2px] or h-[3px] bg-brand-gold
- Cards on dark bg: border border-white/10

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Dark navy hero/CTA sections with gold accents
- Light gray alternating sections for visual rhythm
- Subtle gradients on hero backgrounds
- Professional photography style for machinery images
