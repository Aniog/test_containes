# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif), elegant, uppercase with wide letter spacing.
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif), clean, legible.

## Color Palette
- **Base (Background)**: `#FAF9F6` (Warm off-white, editorial feel)
- **Primary Text**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep almost-black, provides strong contrast)
- **Secondary Text (Muted)**: `#5C5C5C` (Soft gray for descriptions)
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#D4AF37` (Rich, warm metallic gold)
- **Accent Hover**: `#B5952F` (Darker gold for interaction states)
- **Borders/Dividers**: `#E5E5E5` (Thin, subtle haircells)

## Styling
- Generous whitespace
- Thin hairline dividers
- Soft, subtle drop shadows: `shadow-sm` or custom `shadow-[0_4px_20px_-4px_rgba(0,0,0,0.05)]`
- Buttons: Solid accent or outlined with accent text
- Transitions: Subtle 300ms ease-in-out
