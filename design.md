# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Brand Overview
- **Brand Name**: Velmora Fine Jewelry
- **Type**: Direct-to-consumer (B2C) demi-fine jewelry e-commerce
- **Target Audience**: Women 25-45, gifting and self-purchase
- **Price Range**: $30–$120 (premium-but-accessible)

## Visual Identity

### Mood
- Quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- `velmora-cream`: #FAF7F2 (warm off-white background)
- `velmora-charcoal`: #1A1A1A (primary text, dark backgrounds)
- `velmora-gold`: #C9A962 (accent color - warm gold)
- `velmora-gold-light`: #E8DCC4 (subtle gold accents)

**Secondary Colors:**
- `velmora-sand`: #F0EBE0 (secondary backgrounds, cards)
- `velmora-muted`: #8A8A8A (secondary text)
- `velmora-border`: #E5E0D8 (borders, dividers)

**Semantic Colors:**
- `success`: #4A7C59 (muted green)
- `error`: #A65D57 (muted red)

### Typography
**Headings & Product Names:**
- Font Family: "Cormorant Garamond", serif
- Weight: 500-600
- Letter-spacing: 0.05em (wide for uppercase product names)
- Product names: UPPERCASE

**Body & UI:**
- Font Family: "Inter", sans-serif
- Weight: 400-500
- Line-height: 1.6

**Font Sizes:**
- Hero headline: 48px-64px (desktop), 32px-40px (mobile)
- Section titles: 32px-40px
- Product names: 14px-16px
- Body text: 14px-16px
- Small/caption: 12px

### Spacing System
- Base unit: 4px
- Section padding: 80px (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Card padding: 24px
- Component gaps: 16px-24px

### Visual Effects
- **Shadows**: Soft, subtle (0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.06))
- **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers (1px solid #E5E0D8)
- **Hover transitions**: 0.3s ease, subtle transforms
- **Buttons**: Solid or outlined accent, premium feel

## Component Guidelines

### Buttons
- Primary: Gold background (#C9A962), dark text, subtle shadow
- Secondary: Outlined with gold border, transparent background
- Hover: Slight scale (1.02) and shadow increase

### Cards
- Background: #F0EBE0 or white
- Border-radius: 4px (subtle)
- Shadow: Soft, on hover
- Image aspect ratio: 4:3 for products

### Forms
- Input: Clean borders, subtle background
- Focus: Gold accent border
- Placeholder: Muted text color

### Dividers
- Hairline style: 1px solid #E5E0D8
- Used between sections and within content

## Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use generous whitespace
- DO: Use large editorial imagery
- DO: Keep accents restrained
- DO: Use subtle hover transitions
- DON'T: Use loud colors or gradients
- DON'T: Use discount-style callouts
- DON'T: Use generic e-commerce layouts
- DON'T: Overcrowd content
