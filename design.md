# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury in a modern editorial setting. The storefront balances deep espresso tones with warm parchment surfaces so gold jewelry feels luminous without the interface becoming flashy or discount-driven.

## Tailwind theme tokens
Use named tokens from `tailwind.config.js` instead of raw hex values in class strings.

- `velmora-ink`: #161312
- `velmora-espresso`: #221B19
- `velmora-parchment`: #F5F0E8
- `velmora-porcelain`: #FBF8F3
- `velmora-taupe`: #A79284
- `velmora-truffle`: #6B5A52
- `velmora-gold`: #B8955D
- `velmora-champagne`: #E3D2B0
- `velmora-line`: #DED3C4
- `velmora-line-dark`: #3D312D

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `font-display` using Cormorant Garamond
- Body and UI: `font-sans` using Inter
- Product names are uppercase with `tracking-product`
- Large editorial headlines use generous leading and tighter max widths

## Layout and spacing
- Keep sections airy with generous vertical rhythm
- Prefer `max-w-7xl` containers with balanced horizontal padding
- Use thin dividers and subtle shadows for depth
- Mobile-first layouts should stack cleanly, while desktop uses multi-column editorial compositions

## Components
- Primary CTA: warm gold fill with dark text
- Secondary CTA: transparent or parchment background with subtle border
- Cards: rounded corners, soft shadows, light surfaces on light sections, deep surfaces on dark sections
- Navigation: transparent over the home hero, solid parchment with blur once scrolled
- Filters and drawers: clean panels with clear labels, soft dividers, readable muted copy

## Imagery
- Use warm-lit editorial jewelry photography
- Backgrounds and product images should emphasize gold tones, skin texture, and refined styling
- Avoid bright studio white, loud gradients, neon accents, or busy collage treatments

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text fully readable
- Use restrained gold accents for key moments only
- Let whitespace and typography communicate luxury
- Maintain subtle hover transitions and soft motion

## Don't
- Do not use discount badges, loud sale colors, or flashy gradients
- Do not mix multiple accent colors
- Do not crowd cards or sections with dense UI chrome
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-beige text
