# SSourcing China - Design System

## Brand Identity
A professional B2B sourcing agent connecting global buyers with verified Chinese suppliers.
Tone: trustworthy, professional, international, practical, clean.

## Colors
- Primary: #1e3a5f (deep navy blue — trust, stability)
- Primary Light: #2c5282
- Primary Dark: #0f2440
- Accent: #dc2626 (red — for CTAs, draws attention)
- Surface: #ffffff (white cards/sections)
- Background: #f8fafc (light gray page background)
- Text Primary: #0f172a (near-black)
- Text Secondary: #475569 (slate gray)
- Text Muted: #94a3b8 (light slate)
- Border: #e2e8f0 (light border)

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts), weights 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800
- Headings: font-semibold or font-bold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, text-secondary
- Headline (hero): text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl font-bold
- Section headings: text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-primary
- Card titles: text-xl font-semibold

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-lg, font-semibold, px-6 py-3
  - Primary: bg-accent (red-600) text-white hover:bg-red-700
  - Secondary: bg-primary text-white hover:bg-primary-light
  - Outline: border-2 border-primary text-primary hover:bg-primary hover:text-white
- Cards: bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-border
- Sections: alternating bg-white and bg-background

## Do's
- Use clear section hierarchy with ample whitespace
- Keep CTAs prominent and action-oriented
- Use icons from lucide-react for visual support
- Maintain consistent spacing throughout
- Use stock images with factory/QC/shipping context

## Don'ts
- No exaggerated claims or hype language
- No dark backgrounds for text-heavy sections
- No overly bright or playful colors
- No excessive animations
