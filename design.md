# Design System - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood & Tone
- Quiet luxury, warm, editorial.
- Minimalist but premium.
- Soft shadows, generous whitespace, thin hairline dividers.

## Colors
- **Background**: `#FDFCFB` (Pearl White) - `bg-background`
- **Foreground**: `#1C1C1C` (Coal Black) - `text-foreground`
- **Primary/Accent**: `#C5A059` (Warm Gold) - `text-primary` / `bg-primary`
- **Secondary**: `#F5F2F0` (Stone) - `bg-secondary`
- **Muted**: `#707070` (Driftwood) - `text-muted-foreground`
- **Border**: `#E5E5E5` (Mist) - `border-border`

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. `font-serif`
- **Body/UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif. `font-sans`
- **Product Names**: uppercase, wide tracking (`tracking-[0.2em]`).

## Components Style
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Gold (`#C5A059`), white text, uppercase, tracked.
  - Secondary: Outlined Coal (`#1C1C1C`), uppercase, tracked.
- **Inputs**: Minimalist, bottom border only or very thin border.
- **Cards**: No visible border except maybe on hover, soft shadows, large imagery.

## Grid & Spacing
- Mobile: 16px margins.
- Desktop: 48px-64px margins.
- Generous vertical padding between sections (80px-120px).
