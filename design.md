# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background (primary):** cream-100 `#FAF7F2` — warm off-white
- **Surface:** cream-50 `#FFFDF9` — lighter cream for cards
- **Muted surface:** cream-200 `#F5F0E8` — subtle sections
- **Border:** cream-300 `#EDE5D8` / cream-400 `#E2D8C8` — hairline dividers
- **Text primary:** slate-850 `#1A1D23` — near-black
- **Text muted:** slate-850/60 — secondary text
- **Accent/CTA:** gold-600 `#8B6914` — warm deep gold
- **Accent hover:** gold-700 `#6B5010` — darker gold
- **Accent light:** gold-400 `#C9A55C` — lighter gold for highlights
- **Footer/hero:** slate-850 `#1A1D23` — dark backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond, Georgia, serif (font-serif)
- **Body/UI:** Inter, system-ui, sans-serif (font-sans)
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, tracking-ultra-wide (0.25em)
- **Nav links:** UPPERCASE, tracking-ultra-wide, text-[11px]
- **Section labels:** UPPERCASE, tracking-mega-wide (0.35em), text-[10px]

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card gaps: gap-4 md:gap-6

## Components
- **Buttons:** `.btn-primary` (solid gold), `.btn-outline` (gold border)
- **Dividers:** `.divider-hairline` — 1px cream-400/60 border
- **Inputs:** `.input-field` — cream-50 bg, cream-400 border

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Keep transitions subtle (duration-200 to duration-300)
- Use soft shadows (hover:shadow-lg)
- Maintain consistent uppercase letter-spacing for labels

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright colors or neon accents
- Don't use rounded corners on product cards (keep sharp/editorial)
- Don't use heavy borders or box shadows
- Don't mix font families beyond serif + sans-serif pair
