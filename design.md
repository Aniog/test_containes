# SSourcing China Visual Design

Style direction: clean, trustworthy, international B2B sourcing consultancy with practical factory, quality control, logistics, and supplier verification cues.

Typography:
- Use Inter as the primary font for all UI and marketing copy.
- Use large, confident headings with tight tracking and clear hierarchy.
- Body text should be practical, easy to scan, and medium contrast on light surfaces.

Colors:
- Primary navy: use Tailwind slate/blue utility classes such as `text-slate-950`, `bg-slate-950`, `bg-blue-700`, and `text-blue-700`.
- Trust accents: use `bg-cyan-50`, `text-cyan-700`, `border-cyan-200`, and `bg-amber-400` sparingly for calls to action and highlights.
- Backgrounds: use `bg-white`, `bg-slate-50`, and subtle gradients like `from-slate-950 to-blue-950`.
- Text must always have explicit readable foreground classes, especially on cards, forms, badges, and dark sections.

Layout and spacing:
- Use generous section padding: `py-16`, `py-20`, and `lg:py-24`.
- Keep content centered in `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8` containers.
- Use responsive grids: single column on mobile, two or three columns on desktop.

Components:
- Cards should use `rounded-2xl`, clear borders, soft shadows, and white or slate backgrounds.
- CTA buttons should be high contrast with clear hover states.
- Forms should feel enterprise-ready with readable labels, large inputs, and explicit success/error states.

Do:
- Use realistic factory, inspection, warehouse, and shipping visuals via stock image data attributes.
- Keep claims credible and specific.
- Use clear labels and practical B2B wording.

Don't:
- Do not use exaggerated claims like “guaranteed best price.”
- Do not use low-contrast text on images or colored backgrounds.
- Do not overcrowd mobile screens.
