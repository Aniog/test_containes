# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry for the modern woman.
NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Base BG: #FAF7F2 (warm ivory/cream) — main page background
- Surface: #FFFFFF — cards, modals, dropdowns
- Text Primary: #1A1410 (deep espresso) — body text, headings
- Text Secondary: #6B6258 (warm taupe) — secondary text, metadata
- Accent Gold: #C9A96E (warm gold) — CTAs, accents, hover states
- Accent Gold Light: #E8D5A3 (soft gold) — subtle highlights, borders
- Border: #E5DDD4 (warm light beige) — dividers, borders
- Muted: #F0EBE3 (warm off-white) — muted backgrounds

## Typography
- Headings: 'Playfair Display', serif — for hero titles, section headings, product names
- Body: 'Inter', sans-serif — for body text, UI elements, navigation
- Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing tracking-widest
- Hero headline: Playfair Display, 700 weight, large size
- Section headings: Playfair Display, 500 weight

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card padding: p-6
- Grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E5DDD4]
- Subtle borders on cards: border border-[#E5DDD4]
- No heavy or thick borders

## Shadows
- Soft, subtle: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
- No harsh or large shadows

## Buttons
- Primary: solid accent gold (#C9A96E) bg, white text, rounded-none
- Outline: border accent gold, text accent gold, transparent bg
- Hover: subtle darkening of the gold
- Padding: px-8 py-3

## Images
- Editorial style, warm lighting
- Gold jewelry on dark or neutral backgrounds
- Full-bleed hero imagery
- Product images with consistent aspect ratios

## Transitions
- Subtle: duration-300 ease-in-out on hovers
- Smooth opacity fades for image swaps
- Gentle scale transforms on hover (scale-105)

## Cart / UI
- Clean, minimal cart drawer with slide-in animation
- Toast notifications for add-to-cart
- Thin scrollbar styling

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable with high contrast
- Use gold accent sparingly for impact
- Maintain consistent editorial feel

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't use heavy borders or shadows
- Don't overcrowd sections
- Don't use loud sale/discount styling
