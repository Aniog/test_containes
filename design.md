# MicroCosmos Design System

## Theme
Dark, immersive, scientific-wonder aesthetic. Deep navy/black backgrounds evoking the depth of microscopic worlds, with vibrant teal and cyan accents that suggest bioluminescence and life.

## Colors
- Background primary: `#050d1a` (deep dark navy) — `bg-[#050d1a]`
- Background secondary: `#0a1628` — `bg-[#0a1628]`
- Background card: `#0d1f3c` — `bg-[#0d1f3c]`
- Accent primary (teal): `#00d4c8` — `text-[#00d4c8]`, `border-[#00d4c8]`
- Accent secondary (cyan-blue): `#38bdf8` — `text-sky-400`
- Accent glow (green): `#4ade80` — `text-green-400`
- Text primary: `#f0f9ff` — `text-slate-50`
- Text secondary: `#94a3b8` — `text-slate-400`
- Text muted: `#475569` — `text-slate-600`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (loaded via Google Fonts)
- Hero heading: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-black tracking-tight`
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- Card title: `text-lg font-semibold`
- Body: `text-base font-normal leading-relaxed`
- Caption/label: `text-sm font-medium tracking-widest uppercase`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-4` or `p-6`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-4` or `gap-6`

## Borders & Radius
- Cards: `rounded-2xl`
- Buttons: `rounded-full`
- Image containers: `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`
- Subtle borders: `border border-slate-800` or `border border-[#00d4c8]/20`

## Shadows & Effects
- Card hover: `hover:shadow-lg hover:shadow-[#00d4c8]/10`
- Glow text: use `drop-shadow` or gradient text for headings
- Gradient overlays on images: `bg-gradient-to-t from-[#050d1a] to-transparent`

## Images
- Use the strk-img system for all images
- Hero: 16x9, 1600px wide
- Gallery grid: 4x3 or 1x1, 600px wide
- Feature cards: 3x2, 800px wide
- Portrait/specimen cards: 3x4, 400px wide

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds throughout — never white or light gray
- Use teal/cyan accents for interactive elements and highlights
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Overlay gradient on images to blend with dark background
- Use uppercase tracking-widest for section labels/eyebrows

## Don'ts
- No white or light backgrounds
- No bright red or orange accents
- No small unreadable text on dark backgrounds
- No flat, unadorned image grids — always add hover effects
