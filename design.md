# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Audience**: Women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase, $30–$120 price point.

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Section padding: 4rem vertical minimum
- Generous whitespace between sections (5rem+)
- Grid gaps: 1.5rem mobile, 2rem desktop

## Components
- Buttons: Solid charcoal (primary), outlined charcoal (secondary), gold accent
- Cards: Clean, no border, soft shadow on hover
- Dividers: 1px hairline, cream-300/40
- Nav: Transparent on hero, solid cream-100 on scroll

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep transitions subtle (300ms ease)
- Maintain strong contrast for readability
- Use uppercase + wide letter-spacing for product names

## Don'ts
- No bright or saturated colors
- No heavy borders or boxy layouts
- No discount/sale badges in loud colors
- No generic e-commerce patterns
