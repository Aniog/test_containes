# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand Direction
Velmora Fine Jewelry should feel like quiet luxury: editorial, warm, refined, and premium without being intimidating. The experience should feel boutique and elevated, never discount-driven or loud.

## Palette
Use one confident direction sitewide:
- Background: deep espresso `#221a17`
- Secondary surface: warm mocha `#332725`
- Card / panel: soft ivory `#f6f0e8`
- Muted panel: champagne mist `#ece1d4`
- Accent: brushed gold `#b89259`
- Accent deep: antique gold `#8d6b3d`
- Foreground on dark surfaces: `#f8f2ea`
- Foreground on light surfaces: `#241b18`
- Muted text: `#78685f`
- Divider: `#d7c5b3`

## Typography
- Headings, logo, product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body, navigation, buttons, UI: Inter
- Product names should be uppercase with wide letter-spacing.

## Tailwind Translation
- App shell: `bg-velmora-espresso text-velmora-cream`
- Light cards: `bg-velmora-ivory text-velmora-ink`
- Accent buttons: `bg-velmora-gold text-velmora-ink hover:bg-velmora-gold-deep hover:text-velmora-cream`
- Hairlines: `border border-velmora-line/70`
- Serif headlines: `font-display tracking-[0.08em]`
- Product labels: `font-display uppercase tracking-[0.28em]`

## Layout & Spacing
- Use generous vertical spacing: `py-16 md:py-24` for main sections.
- Keep max width around `max-w-7xl` with roomy gutters `px-5 md:px-8 lg:px-10`.
- Favor asymmetry and editorial compositions on desktop, stacked cleanly on mobile.

## Components
- Buttons should feel tailored: rounded-full, thin borders, subtle shadows, gentle transform on hover.
- Cards should use soft shadows and clearly readable text against the surface.
- Product cards should prioritize imagery, clean product names, price, and minimal supporting copy.
- Filters, pills, and selectors should be refined with clear active states using gold or ivory contrast.

## Imagery
- Prefer warm, close-up gold jewelry imagery on skin, velvet, stone, or soft neutral backgrounds.
- Use editorial crops and avoid busy or over-saturated scenes.
- UGC/reel cards should feel candid but still polished.

## Do
- Keep contrast high and readable on every surface.
- Use thin dividers and subtle motion.
- Let whitespace carry the luxury feel.

## Don’t
- Don’t use bright sales colors, loud gradients, or aggressive badges.
- Don’t overcrowd the layout.
- Don’t rely on low-contrast beige-on-beige text.
