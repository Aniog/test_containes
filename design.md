# Velmora Fine Jewelry Visual Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury with warm editorial restraint. The experience should feel like a premium demi-fine jewelry magazine spread: generous whitespace, large soft imagery, precise typography, thin dividers, and polished interactions. Avoid loud sale language, generic marketplace styling, heavy gradients, and overly saturated colors.

## Color Palette
Use a single warm neutral palette that flatters gold jewelry:

- Ink: `#171412` for primary text and deep surfaces.
- Espresso: `#2A211B` for dark section backgrounds and nav states.
- Porcelain: `#F8F3EC` for the main page background.
- Pearl: `#FFFDF8` for cards, drawer, and input surfaces.
- Sand: `#E8DCCB` for quiet panels and borders.
- Champagne: `#C9A45C` as the primary metallic accent for buttons, icons, stars, and focus states.
- Antique Gold: `#8A642C` for hover states and secondary accents.
- Stone: `#6F665D` for readable muted copy.

Always pair dark backgrounds with Porcelain/Pearl text and light backgrounds with Ink/Espresso text. Accent text must remain legible and should not be used for long paragraphs.

## Typography
- Headings and brand moments: `Cormorant Garamond`, elegant serif, high contrast, refined tracking where appropriate.
- Body and UI: `Manrope`, clean sans-serif.
- Product names: uppercase, wide tracking, serif, restrained size.
- Use editorial line heights and avoid cramped text blocks.

## Layout and Spacing
- Mobile-first with comfortable single-column stacking.
- Desktop layouts should use editorial two-column/split sections and multi-column product grids.
- Use generous vertical rhythm: large section padding, open gutters, and centered max-width containers.
- Hairline dividers should use Sand/Espresso at low opacity.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid Champagne with Ink text or outlined Champagne on dark surfaces. Subtle hover lift and color shift to Antique Gold.
- Product cards: Pearl surface, soft shadow, thin Sand border, second-image hover reveal, quick add overlay.
- Cart drawer: Pearl surface, clear Ink text, dark overlay, precise quantity controls.
- Inputs/selects: Pearl or transparent surfaces, Sand borders, Ink text, visible focus ring in Champagne.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid Pearl/Espresso on scroll with readable foreground.

## Imagery
Use Strikingly stock-image tags for warm gold jewelry imagery. Queries should reference nearby static text IDs, never hardcoded external URLs. Imagery should feel warm-lit, close-up, model-worn, editorial, and premium.

## Motion
Use subtle transitions: opacity, transform, border color, and shadow. Avoid bouncy or playful animations. Keep interactions fast and refined.

## Do's
- Use thin uppercase labels and small editorial captions.
- Keep product and price text explicitly readable.
- Maintain strong contrast in all cards, drawers, badges, and overlays.
- Use restrained gold accents consistently.

## Don'ts
- Do not use discount-style red sale banners or loud promotional patterns.
- Do not mix unrelated color palettes.
- Do not leave important text colors inherited on custom backgrounds.
- Do not crowd mobile layouts or force desktop-style grids on small screens.
