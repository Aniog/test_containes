# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Colors
- **Background:** #FAF7F2 (warm cream/ivory)
- **Primary text:** #1C1C1C (near-black, warm)
- **Secondary text:** #8B8378 (warm medium gray)
- **Accent/Gold:** #B8913A (refined gold)
- **Light gold:** #E8D5A3 (pale gold for highlights)
- **Border/Divider:** #E2DCD3 (warm light gray)
- **Card:** #FFFFFF (white)
- **Dark section bg:** #2C2824 (deep warm charcoal)
- **Error:** #C94A4A
- **Success:** #4A7C59

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names:** 'Playfair Display', serif — uppercase, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest or tracking-wide)
- **Body / UI:** 'Inter', sans-serif — clean, readable
- **Product prices:** Playfair Display italic or Inter medium

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace everywhere (py-16, py-24 for sections)
- Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E2DCD3]

## Components
- **Buttons:** Tall (py-3 px-8), uppercase, tracking-widest, text-sm. Solid gold (#B8913A) bg or outlined with gold border. Hover: subtle darkening.
- **Links:** uppercase, text-xs, tracking-widest, hover:text-[#B8913A] transition
- **Product cards:** Clean white bg, subtle shadow on hover, smooth transition. Names in uppercase tracking-wider.
- **Section dividers:** Thin 1px border, color #E2DCD3

## Do's
- Use warm cream backgrounds (#FAF7F2) as the main page color
- Gold accents (#B8913A) for CTAs, hover states, and key highlights
- Large editorial imagery with generous padding
- Thin, refined borders and dividers
- Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing
- Serif (Playfair Display) for all headings and product names
- Sans-serif (Inter) for body text, prices, UI elements

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/discount-looking colors
- Don't use loud patterns or gradients
- Don't overcrowd — maintain generous whitespace
- Don't use generic e-commerce stock photos
- Don't use black (#000) — use warm near-black (#1C1C1C)
