# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. The visual tone should feel refined, intimate, and giftable without looking flashy, trendy, or discount-driven.

## Palette
Use one consistent dark editorial direction that flatters gold jewelry:
- Background: deep espresso `#1a1411`
- Surface: warm charcoal `#241c18`
- Elevated surface: cocoa brown `#302621`
- Soft neutral panel: champagne beige `#efe4d7`
- Border: translucent gold-brown `rgba(214, 183, 131, 0.24)`
- Primary text on dark: ivory `#f7f1ea`
- Secondary text on dark: warm taupe `#cbbcae`
- Primary text on light: espresso `#241914`
- Accent: muted gold `#d6b783`
- Accent strong: antique gold `#b89252`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `Cormorant Garamond` with elegant high-contrast serif styling.
- Body and UI: `Inter`.
- Product names should be uppercase with generous tracking.

## Spacing and layout
- Prefer large vertical spacing between sections.
- Use wide desktop gutters and comfortable mobile padding.
- Cards should breathe with generous image ratio and subtle spacing.
- Use thin hairline borders instead of heavy outlines.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, lightly rounded, solid muted-gold or transparent outlined styles.
- Navigation: transparent over homepage hero, solid dark after scroll.
- Product cards: editorial imagery, subtle hover lift, quick action on hover.
- Inputs: soft neutral fills or dark translucent fills with visible borders and strong text contrast.
- Accordions and drawers: clean dividers, understated motion, no heavy shadows.

## Motion
- Use subtle transitions only: fade, translateY by a few pixels, soft opacity shifts.
- Avoid bouncy or playful motion.

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text explicit on every surface.
- Use restrained gold accents to highlight premium details.
- Preserve generous whitespace and editorial balance.

## Don't
- Do not use bright promo colors, sale-style red badges, or loud gradients.
- Do not overcrowd sections or collapse desktop layouts into mobile-style single columns.
- Do not use generic marketplace styling or chunky UI controls.
