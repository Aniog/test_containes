# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Overview
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial machinery manufacturing (sheet metal folding machines)
- **Tone**: Elegant, professional, trustworthy, yet user-friendly

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a1a2e` (Deep navy/charcoal - sophistication)
- **Secondary**: `#16213e` (Dark blue - industrial reliability)
- **Accent**: `#e94560` (Coral red - action/CTA buttons)
- **Accent Secondary**: `#0f3460` (Medium blue - depth)
- **Light**: `#f8f9fa` (Off-white - backgrounds)
- **Text Primary**: `#1a1a2e` (Dark for readability)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6c757d` (Muted gray)
- **Text Light**: `#ffffff` (White for dark backgrounds)
- **Border**: `#e9ecef` (Light gray)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: "Outfit" (Google Fonts) - Modern, geometric, professional
- **Headings**: Outfit, weights 600-700
- **Body**: Outfit, weight 400
- **Font Sizes**:
  - Hero Title: 56px (desktop), 36px (mobile)
  - Section Titles: 42px (desktop), 28px (mobile)
  - Subheadings: 24px
  - Body: 16px
  - Small: 14px

## Layout & Spacing
- **Max Content Width**: 1200px
- **Section Padding**: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Component Spacing**: 24px between elements
- **Border Radius**: 8px (cards), 4px (buttons), 50% (icons)

## Visual Style
- Clean, minimalist design with ample white space
- Subtle shadows for depth: `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- Smooth transitions: 0.3s ease
- Professional imagery with industrial aesthetic
- Grid-based layouts for products

## Components

### Header
- Fixed position, white background with subtle shadow
- Logo on left, navigation center, CTA button right
- Mobile: hamburger menu

### Hero Section
- Full-width with dark gradient overlay
- Large headline with brand name emphasis
- Subheadline describing products
- Two CTA buttons: primary (accent) and secondary (outline)
- Background: industrial machinery image or gradient

### Products Section
- Section title with accent underline
- 3-column grid (desktop), 1-column (mobile)
- Product cards with:
  - Image placeholder (industrial style)
  - Product name
  - Brief description
  - "Learn More" link

### Features/Benefits Section
- Alternating layout (image + text)
- Key selling points with icons
- 3-4 main benefits

### About Section
- Company story with image
- Brief history and mission

### Contact Section
- Contact form with fields: name, email, phone, message
- Company contact information
- Map or location info

### Footer
- Dark background
- Logo, quick links, contact info
- Copyright notice

## Animations
- Fade-in on scroll for sections
- Hover effects on buttons and cards (scale, shadow)
- Smooth scroll for navigation

## Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use clean, professional imagery
- DO: Keep plenty of white space
- DO: Use accent color sparingly for CTAs
- DON'T: Use too many colors (keep it minimal)
- DON'T: Clutter the design with too many elements
- DON'T: Use informal language
