# MicroCosmos Design System

## Theme
A dark, immersive, science-inspired design that evokes the mystery and beauty of the microscopic world. Deep dark backgrounds with vibrant accent colors reminiscent of fluorescence microscopy.

## Colors
- **Background**: `cosmos-dark` (#0a0e1a) - Deep space-like dark
- **Surface**: `cosmos-surface` (#131a2e) - Slightly lighter card backgrounds
- **Border**: `cosmos-border` (#1e2a45) - Subtle borders
- **Primary**: `cosmos-primary` (#00d4aa) - Vibrant teal/cyan for accents
- **Secondary**: `cosmos-secondary` (#7c3aed) - Purple for secondary accents
- **Accent**: `cosmos-accent` (#f472b6) - Pink for highlights
- **Text Primary**: `cosmos-text` (#e8edf5) - Light text on dark backgrounds
- **Text Muted**: `cosmos-muted` (#8b9dc3) - Muted/secondary text

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts), weights 300-800
- Headings: font-bold to font-extrabold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, leading-relaxed
- Hero title: text-5xl md:text-7xl
- Section titles: text-3xl md:text-5xl
- Subtitles: text-lg md:text-xl
- Body text: text-base

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-32
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: border border-cosmos-border rounded-2xl
- Hover glow: shadow-lg shadow-cosmos-primary/10
- Image containers: rounded-xl overflow-hidden

## Visual Style
- Do: Use gradient overlays on images, subtle glow effects, smooth transitions
- Do: Use opacity and backdrop-blur for glass-morphism effects
- Do: Keep text highly readable with sufficient contrast
- Don't: Use flat/plain backgrounds without depth
- Don't: Use light mode colors
- Don't: Overcrowd sections - maintain breathing room
