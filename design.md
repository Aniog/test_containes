# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial direction: deep espresso grounding, warm ivory surfaces, champagne-gold accents, generous whitespace, hairline borders, and soft warm imagery. The site should feel premium and calm, never promotional or loud.

## Color palette
- `velmora-espresso` (`#231713`): primary text, dark editorial sections, premium buttons.
- `velmora-ink` (`#38261f`): secondary dark text and solid navigation.
- `velmora-ivory` (`#fbf7ef`): main page background.
- `velmora-porcelain` (`#f5eadc`): soft card and panel backgrounds.
- `velmora-linen` (`#ead9c4`): dividers, soft borders, muted surfaces.
- `velmora-champagne` (`#c89f5d`): refined metallic accent for CTAs, stars, and highlights.
- `velmora-bronze` (`#8b6338`): hover/accent text.
- `velmora-mist` (`#8d7b70`): muted readable copy.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, generous line-height.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names must be uppercase with wide letter spacing (`tracking-[0.22em]` equivalent via component classes or Tailwind tracking utilities).

## Layout and surfaces
- Use generous vertical rhythm, mobile-first stacking, and desktop multi-column layouts.
- Use thin hairline dividers (`border-velmora-linen`) and restrained shadows.
- Cards should use warm ivory/porcelain surfaces with clear dark text.
- Dark editorial panels must explicitly use readable ivory text.

## Buttons and interactions
- Primary buttons: champagne or espresso fills with strong contrast, uppercase label, wide tracking.
- Secondary buttons: transparent/outlined with espresso or champagne borders.
- Hover states should be subtle: slight lift, accent color shift, or image scale only.
- Avoid discount language, bright sale colors, and heavy ecommerce badges.

## Do's
- Use warm gold-friendly image prompts through the Strikingly image attributes.
- Keep captions editorial and refined.
- Use spacious product grids with quick actions revealed on hover/focus.

## Don'ts
- Do not use loud colors, large sale banners, generic marketplace styling, or low-contrast muted text.
- Do not hardcode image URLs; use Strikingly image attributes for stock imagery.
