# Design System for Artitect Machinery

## Typography
- Primary Font: 'Inter', sans-serif (Clean, modern, highly readable). Ensure weights from 300 to 800 are loaded.
- Headings: Bold and Semi-bold. E.g., `font-bold tracking-tight text-slate-900`.
- Body Text: Regular or Medium. E.g., `text-slate-600 leading-relaxed`.

## Colors
- Primary Brand Color: Slate/Steel aesthetic for machinery. `slate-900` for main headings, `slate-800` for text, `slate-100` for light backgrounds.
- Accent Color: Elegant metallic blue or deep teal. Let's use Tailwind's `blue-900` or `indigo-900` as accent to convey professionalism and trust `text-blue-900`, `bg-blue-900`.
- Background Colors: White `bg-white`, Off-white `bg-slate-50`, Dark `bg-slate-900` for contrast sections.
- Borders/Dividers: `border-slate-200`.

## Layout & Spacing
- Container: standard `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`.
- Section Spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`.
- Grid: Standard 12-column grid or flexible CSS grid for items `grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-3 gap-8`.

## Components
- Buttons: Solid color `bg-slate-900 hover:bg-slate-800 text-white rounded-md px-6 py-3`.
- Cards: Subtle shadows, rounded corners, clean borders `bg-white border border-slate-200 rounded-lg shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow duration-200`.
- Images: Rounded corners `rounded-lg object-cover`.

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do use ample whitespace to create an elegant, uncluttered feel.
- Do use high contrast for readability (e.g. slate-900 on white).
- Don't use overly vibrant or flashy colors; stick to professional slate, white, and metallic/blue accents.
- Don't clutter sections; give machinery products room to breathe.
