# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.
- **Audience**: Women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase, premium-but-accessible ($30–$120).

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF7F2` (warm off-white / cream)
- **Surface / Card**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white)
- **Text Primary**: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6B6460` (warm gray-brown)
- **Text Muted**: `#9C9490` (light warm gray)
- **Accent**: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod — warm gold)
- **Accent Hover**: `#9A7209` (deeper gold)
- **Accent Light**: `#F5ECD7` (pale gold wash)
- **Border**: `#E8E2DA` (warm light border)
- **Divider**: `#D4CCC2` (warm mid border)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weight 400–600
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weight 300–500
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, serif
- **Buttons**: Uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em, sans-serif, weight 500

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace. Sections separated by 80–120px vertical padding.
- Max content width: 1280px, centered.
- Hairline dividers (1px, warm border color).
- Cards: subtle shadow on hover only, rounded corners (4px).

## Buttons
- **Primary**: Solid accent gold background, white text, uppercase, tracking-wide.
- **Secondary**: Outlined (1px accent border), accent text, transparent bg.
- Hover: slight darkening, smooth 200ms transition.

## Images
- Large editorial imagery. Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds.
- Product cards: square aspect, hover reveals second image.
- Hero: full-bleed, warm-lit close-up.

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth. 200–300ms transitions.
- Hover: image swap, shadow lift, color shifts.
- No bouncy or playful animations — restrained and elegant.

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use warm gold accent sparingly
- Ensure strong text contrast on all backgrounds

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/saturated colors
- Don't use playful or bouncy animations
- Don't crowd sections — let content breathe
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (badges, countdowns, aggressive CTAs)
