# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Brand
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry that flatters gold and reads as high-end — never loud, never discount.

## Color Palette
- `ink`        `#1A1310` — primary deep base, near-black with warm undertone (text, dark sections, footer)
- `cream`      `#F6F0E6` — primary surface, warm off-white (page background, light sections)
- `champagne`  `#C8A66B` — primary accent, warm metallic gold (CTAs, key icons, dividers, hairlines, links)
- `bronze`     `#8A6A3D` — secondary accent, deeper gold (hover, secondary buttons)
- `rose`       `#E8D6C3` — soft warm neutral (cards, alt sections, subtle backgrounds)
- `pearl`      `#FBF7F0` — softest warm white (image placeholders, modal surfaces)
- `mute`       `#6B5A4A` — muted text on light, soft brown-grey
- `muteOnDark` `#A99B8A` — muted text on dark

## Typography
- Headings & product names: `Cormorant Garamond` (Google Fonts) — elegant serif. Weight 300/400/500. Italic for accents.
- Body & UI: `Inter` (Google Fonts) — clean sans. Weights 400/500/600.
- Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.18em, weight 500, serif.
- Headlines: serif, tight tracking, light weight.
- Buttons & nav: sans, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.14em, weight 500.

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: `max-w-[1440px]`.
- Generous vertical rhythm: section padding `py-20 md:py-28 lg:py-32`.
- Hairline dividers: 1px `champagne/30` or `ink/10` lines, full-bleed.
- Card radii: `rounded-sm` (2px) for a refined look, `rounded-none` for editorial cards.
- Shadows: very soft, low-opacity (`shadow-[0_1px_2px_rgba(26,19,16,0.04),0_8px_24px_rgba(26,19,16,0.04)]`).

## Buttons
- **Primary (accent):** solid `ink` background, `cream` text, uppercase, tracking 0.18em, px-8 py-3.5. Hover: bg `#000` + tiny translate-y -1px.
- **Gold accent:** solid `champagne` background, `ink` text. Used sparingly for the one hero CTA and newsletter submit.
- **Outline:** 1px `ink` border, transparent bg, `ink` text. Hover: fill `ink` + `cream` text.
- All buttons: rounded-sm, no shadow, smooth 200ms ease.

## Imagery
- Hero & editorial: dark, warm, low-key lighting; close-up of gold on skin.
- Product cards: square (1:1) or 4:5; warm gradient placeholders.
- Category tiles: 4:5, with text overlay revealed on hover.
- UGC reel: 9:16 vertical cards.

## Motion
- Subtle. 200–400ms `ease-out`. No bounces, no flashy parallax.
- Hover: image zoom (1.04), button lift (-1px), underline grow.
- Nav: transparent at top → solid cream + thin hairline at 80px scroll.

## Do's
- Use `champagne` for ALL metallic lines, dots, hairlines, hover underlines.
- Keep product names uppercase + wide tracking.
- Always set readable foreground (cream-on-ink, ink-on-cream).

## Don'ts
- No pure black (`#000`) on text — use `ink` `#1A1310` for warmth.
- No gradients on body backgrounds.
- No emoji, no discount badges, no "SALE!" red — quiet luxury only.
- No drop-shadow glows around images; soft shadow only.
