# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (pure white)
- Text primary: `#1A1A1A` (near black)
- Text secondary: `#6B6560` (warm taupe)
- Text muted: `#9B9590` (soft stone)
- Accent: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod - gold jewelry tone)
- Accent hover: `#996F0A` (deeper gold)
- Accent light: `#F5E6C8` (pale gold wash)
- Border: `#E8E4DF` (warm light border)
- Border thin: `#D4CFC9` (hairline divider)
- Dark section bg: `#1A1A1A` (for newsletter/hero overlays)
- Dark text: `#FAF8F5` (on dark backgrounds)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Generous whitespace throughout

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#D4CFC9]
- Subtle borders: border border-[#E8E4DF]
- No heavy borders, keep it light and airy

## Shadows
- Soft shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md
- Card hover: shadow-lg transition-shadow duration-300
- No harsh or large shadows

## Buttons
- Primary: solid accent bg, white text, rounded-none (sharp edges for luxury feel)
- Secondary: outlined accent border, accent text, transparent bg
- Hover: slight opacity change or bg shift
- Full-width on mobile, auto on desktop for CTAs

## Animations
- Hover transitions: duration-300 ease-in-out
- Image zoom on hover: scale-105
- Fade in: opacity-0 to opacity-100
- Smooth scroll behavior

## Images
- Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Editorial style, large imagery
- Aspect ratios: 3x4 for product cards, 16x9 for hero, 9x16 for UGC
