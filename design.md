# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium-but-accessible demi-fine jewelry. The storefront should feel serene, tactile, and confident rather than promotional or loud.

## Palette
Use one consistent warm neutral palette that flatters gold jewelry:

- `velmora-ink` `#1B1714` — primary text and dark editorial surfaces.
- `velmora-espresso` `#2A211B` — navigation, footer, dark cards.
- `velmora-parchment` `#F6F0E8` — main page background.
- `velmora-ivory` `#FFFCF7` — elevated card surfaces.
- `velmora-champagne` `#C99A5B` — primary accent buttons and highlights.
- `velmora-gold` `#B98943` — hover/accent line color.
- `velmora-taupe` `#8E7B6A` — secondary body copy.
- `velmora-blush` `#E7D5C6` — soft panels and newsletter surfaces.
- `velmora-line` `#DED1C3` — hairline dividers and borders.

Always pair dark surfaces with ivory text and light surfaces with ink text. Secondary copy should remain readable with `velmora-taupe` only on light backgrounds.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, refined line height.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names: uppercase with wide letter spacing.
- Logo: serif uppercase with generous tracking.

## Layout & Spacing
- Mobile-first with generous whitespace.
- Desktop grids should feel editorial and airy, not stacked mobile layouts.
- Use thin hairline dividers, soft shadows, and restrained transitions.
- Prefer rounded `rounded-sm`, `rounded-xl`, or `rounded-2xl` depending on surface scale.

## Components
- Primary button: champagne background, ink text, subtle hover to gold.
- Secondary button: transparent/ivory with hairline border and ink text.
- Cards: ivory or transparent surfaces, soft shadow only on hover.
- Images: warm jewelry/model imagery via Strikingly stock image tags; no hardcoded external image URLs.

## Do
- Use spacious sections, editorial imagery, clear contrast, and subtle motion.
- Keep CTA language premium and direct.
- Make cart interactions feel polished and immediate.

## Don't
- Do not use sale/discount visual language except the newsletter offer.
- Do not use loud colors, heavy drop shadows, cluttered badges, or generic marketplace styling.
- Do not place low-contrast taupe text on dark backgrounds.
