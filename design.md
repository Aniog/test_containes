# SSourcing China - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- Professional B2B sourcing agent website
- Clean, trustworthy, international feel
- No exaggerated claims - practical and clear

## Colors
- **Primary**: Deep navy blue (#1e40af) - trust, professionalism, reliability
- **Primary Dark**: (#1e3a8a) - headers, strong CTAs
- **Accent**: Amber (#f59e0b) - highlights, CTAs, attention points
- **Neutral Background**: White (#ffffff) and slate-50 (#f8fafc)
- **Text**: Slate-800 (#1e293b) for body, slate-900 (#0f172a) for headings
- **Muted Text**: Slate-500 (#64748b) for secondary text
- **Borders**: Slate-200 (#e2e8f0)

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-weight 700-800, tracking tight
- Body: font-weight 400, line-height 1.6
- Small text: font-weight 500, tracking normal

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between grid items: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Component Styles
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary: bg-primary-500 text-white hover:bg-primary-600 rounded-lg px-6 py-3 font-semibold
  - Secondary: border-2 border-primary-500 text-primary-500 hover:bg-primary-50 rounded-lg px-6 py-3 font-semibold
  - Accent CTA: bg-accent-500 text-white hover:bg-accent-600 rounded-lg px-8 py-4 font-bold text-lg
- **Cards**: bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-neutral-200 hover:shadow-md transition
- **Section Headers**: text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-neutral-900, with accent underline

## Visual Style
- Clean white backgrounds with subtle gray sections for contrast
- Professional stock images of factories, QC inspections, shipping containers
- Icons from Lucide React for service features
- Subtle shadows and borders, no heavy gradients
- Rounded corners (rounded-lg to rounded-xl)

## Do's
- Use plenty of white space
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use data and specific numbers where possible
- Include trust indicators (certifications, years, client count)
- Make CTAs prominent and clear

## Don'ts
- Don't use dark mode (B2B professional site)
- Don't use flashy animations or transitions
- Don't use generic stock photo placeholders without context
- Don't use exaggerated claims ("best in the world", "guaranteed")
- Don't use bright or neon colors
