# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- `bg-cream`: #FAF7F2 (soft warm cream — main page background)
- `bg-white`: #FFFFFF (cards, modals, elevated surfaces)
- `text-primary`: #1A1A1A (deep charcoal — body text)
- `text-muted`: #6B6570 (muted warm gray — secondary text)
- `accent-gold`: #C9A96E (warm gold — CTAs, accents, hover states)
- `accent-gold-light`: #E8D5A3 (light gold — subtle highlights)
- `accent-gold-dark`: #A8894A (deep gold — active/visited)
- `border-light`: #E8E4DE (thin hairline divider)
- `border-medium`: #D4CFC8 (medium border)
- `bg-dark`: #1A1A1A (hero overlays, dark sections)
- `text-on-dark`: #F5F0EB (warm light text on dark bg)

## Typography
- Headings / Product Names: `Cormorant Garamond` (serif, elegant)
- Body / UI: `Inter` (clean sans-serif)
- Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest or tracking-wide)
- Generous whitespace, large sizes for editorial feel

## Spacing & Layout
- max-w-7xl for content containers
- Generous padding: px-6 md:px-12 lg:px-16
- Section spacing: py-16 md:py-24
- Thin hairline borders: border-t border-[#E8E4DE]

## Components

### Buttons
- Primary: bg-accent-gold text-white, hover:bg-accent-gold-dark, px-8 py-3 rounded-none
- Outline: border border-accent-gold text-accent-gold, hover:bg-accent-gold hover:text-white
- No rounded corners (sharp = premium editorial)

### Cards
- No border, subtle shadow on hover
- Smooth transitions on hover (scale, opacity)

### Dividers
- Thin hr with border-[#E8E4DE] (height 0.5px or 1px)

### Transitions
- All interactive elements: transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out

### Shadows
- Cards: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
- Nav: shadow-sm when solid

### CTAs
- Accent gold (#C9A96E) for primary buttons, links, accents
- Gold hover states

## Stock Images
- Use warm-lit gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Editorial style, close-up shots
- 9:16 for UGC reel cards, 3:2 or 4:3 for product grid, 16:9 for hero
