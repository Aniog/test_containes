# Visual Identity - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking. Generous whitespace, thin hairline dividers.

## Colors
- **Primary Background**: `#FBF9F6` (Soft Parchment)
- **Primary Text**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Charcoal)
- **Accent Gold**: `#C5A059` (Warm Muted Gold)
- **Muted Accents**: `#EADEDA` (Dusty Rose/Nude)
- **Divider**: `#E5E5E5` (Light Grey)

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. Used for page titles, section headers, and product names (Uppercase).
- **Body & UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif. Used for descriptions, navigation, and functional elements.
- **Product Names**: Uppercase, letter-spacing: `0.15em`.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**: Solid accent or outlined charcoal. High padding, minimal rounding (2px or 0px).
- **Transitions**: 0.3s ease-in-out for hover effects.
- **Images**: Large editorial crops, warm lighting.
- **Cards**: Subtle shadows, no borders, clean typography.
