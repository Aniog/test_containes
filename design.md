# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
Elegant, premium industrial machinery brand. Professional yet approachable.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a1f2e` (deep navy) - headers, primary buttons, dark backgrounds
- **Primary Light**: `#2a3448` (steel blue) - secondary elements, card backgrounds
- **Accent**: `#c9a44b` (warm gold) - CTAs, highlights, links
- **Accent Light**: `#e8d5a3` (light gold) - hover states, subtle accents
- **Surface**: `#f7f8fa` (off-white) - page backgrounds
- **White**: `#ffffff` (pure white) - cards, content areas
- **Text Primary**: `#1a1f2e` (navy)
- **Text Secondary**: `#5a6478` (slate gray)
- **Text Muted**: `#8b94a6` (light slate)
- **Border**: `#e2e6ed` (light border)

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-semibold to font-bold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, text-base leading-relaxed
- Sizes: text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl for hero, text-2xl md:text-3xl for section titles

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between elements: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow, border border-gray-100 rounded-xl
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Sections: clean, no heavy borders

## Dos
- Use ample white space
- Use gold accent sparingly for CTAs
- Keep text high contrast against backgrounds
- Use subtle transitions on interactive elements

## Don'ts
- Don't use overly bright or saturated colors
- Don't use dark text on dark backgrounds
- Don't use gold text on white backgrounds (low contrast)
- Don't use heavy shadows or borders
