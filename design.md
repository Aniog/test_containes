# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background (cream):** `#FAF7F2` — warm off-white for page backgrounds
- **Foreground (charcoal):** `#1A1A1A` — near-black for body text
- **Accent (deep gold):** `#8B6914` — rich warm gold for CTAs, links, highlights
- **Accent light:** `#C9A84C` — lighter gold for hover states, decorative elements
- **Muted:** `#6B6560` — warm gray for secondary text, captions
- **Border:** `#E8E2DA` — warm light border/divider color
- **Surface:** `#FFFFFF` — white cards, modals
- **Dark surface:** `#1A1A1A` — dark sections (footer, newsletter)
- **Dark muted:** `#A39E99` — muted text on dark backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names:** `Cormorant Garamond`, serif — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI:** `Inter`, sans-serif — clean, modern, readable
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.12em
- **Heading sizes:** Hero: text-5xl/6xl, Section: text-3xl/4xl, Card: text-xl
- **Body:** text-base (16px), leading-relaxed

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections py-20 lg:py-28
- Container max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 lg:px-8
- Cards: rounded-none or rounded-sm (sharp, editorial)
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-border (1px)

## Components
- **Buttons:** Solid accent bg with white text, or outlined with accent border. Uppercase, tracking-wider, text-sm, py-3 px-8. Hover: slight opacity or darker shade.
- **Cards:** No border-radius, subtle shadow-sm on hover, transition-all duration-300
- **Images:** object-cover, aspect ratios maintained
- **Hover transitions:** duration-300, ease-in-out, subtle scale or opacity shifts

## Do's
- Use generous padding and margin
- Keep text hierarchy clear (serif headings, sans body)
- Use gold accent sparingly — CTAs, active states, decorative lines
- Maintain warm, cohesive tone throughout

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded-lg or pill shapes (keep angular/editorial)
- No cluttered layouts — whitespace is a feature
- No generic e-commerce blue/green CTAs
