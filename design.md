# Design System - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- **Primary (Sand)**: `#F9F7F2` (Background)
- **Accent (Deep Gold)**: `#A68A56` (Buttons, Accents)
- **Text (Charcoal)**: `#1C1C1C` (Headings)
- **Secondary Text**: `#555555` (Body)
- **Border**: `#E5E2DA` (Hairline dividers)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - Style: Elegant, high contrast.
  - Class: `font-serif`
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - Style: Clean, readable.
  - Class: `font-sans`
- **Product Names**: `font-serif uppercase tracking-[0.2em]`

## Components Style
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid deep gold (`bg-[#A68A56]`), white text, hover soft fade.
  - Secondary: Outlined deep gold, wide letter spacing.
- **Dividers**: Thin hairlines (`border-b border-[#E5E2DA]`).
- **Cards**: Minimal, no borders, soft lift on hover or simple image swap.
- **Images**: Large, warm lighting, editorial crops.
