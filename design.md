# Design System — Bloom & Grow Flower Shop

## Brand Identity
A warm, natural, and inviting aesthetic that evokes the freshness of living plants and the joy of bringing nature indoors.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Green**: `#3d7a4f` — main brand color, buttons, accents (`text-green-700`, `bg-green-700`)
- **Light Green**: `#e8f5e9` — soft backgrounds, cards (`bg-green-50`)
- **Warm Cream**: `#fdf8f0` — page background (`bg-[#fdf8f0]`)
- **Earthy Brown**: `#5c3d2e` — headings, rich text (`text-[#5c3d2e]`)
- **Soft Peach**: `#f9ede3` — section alternates, highlights
- **White**: `#ffffff` — card backgrounds, clean surfaces
- **Muted Text**: `#6b7280` — secondary text (`text-gray-500`)

## Typography
- **Font Family**: "Playfair Display" (headings) + "Inter" (body) — loaded via Google Fonts
- **Headings**: `font-serif font-bold` with `text-[#5c3d2e]`
- **Body**: `font-sans text-gray-700`
- **Hero Title**: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-serif font-bold`
- **Section Title**: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-serif font-bold`
- **Card Title**: `text-xl font-serif font-semibold`
- **Small Label**: `text-sm font-medium uppercase tracking-wider`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-6 md:gap-8`
- Max content width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl shadow-md hover:shadow-xl transition-shadow`
- Buttons: `rounded-full`
- Images: `rounded-xl object-cover`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-green-700 text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-full font-medium hover:bg-green-800 transition-colors`
- Outline: `border-2 border-green-700 text-green-700 px-6 py-3 rounded-full font-medium hover:bg-green-50 transition-colors`

## Do's
- Use warm, earthy tones throughout
- Use serif fonts for headings to feel premium and natural
- Use generous whitespace to feel airy and fresh
- Use rounded corners on all cards and buttons
- Ensure all text is clearly readable against its background

## Don'ts
- Don't use harsh dark backgrounds (no pure black sections)
- Don't use cold blue or grey as primary colors
- Don't use tight spacing — plants need room to breathe
- Don't use sharp corners on interactive elements
