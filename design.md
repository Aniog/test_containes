# MicroCosmos Design System

## Concept
A dark, immersive, science-meets-wonder aesthetic. The site explores the microscopic world — cells, organisms, crystals, and natural patterns invisible to the naked eye. The visual language is deep, rich, and mysterious with pops of vivid bioluminescent color.

## Color Palette
- Background (deep space black): `bg-[#050810]`
- Surface (dark navy): `bg-[#0a0f1e]`
- Card surface: `bg-[#0d1428]`
- Accent teal/cyan: `text-cyan-400`, `border-cyan-400`, `bg-cyan-400`
- Accent violet: `text-violet-400`, `bg-violet-500`
- Accent emerald: `text-emerald-400`
- Accent amber: `text-amber-400`
- Muted text: `text-slate-400`
- Body text: `text-slate-200`
- Headings: `text-white`

## Typography
- Font: "Space Grotesk" (headings) + "Inter" (body) from Google Fonts
- Hero heading: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold tracking-tight`
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- Card title: `text-lg font-semibold`
- Body: `text-base text-slate-300 leading-relaxed`
- Caption/label: `text-xs uppercase tracking-widest text-slate-500`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-slate-700/50`
- Glow effect: `shadow-[0_0_30px_rgba(34,211,238,0.15)]`
- Hover glow: `hover:shadow-[0_0_40px_rgba(34,211,238,0.3)]`
- Rounded cards: `rounded-2xl`
- Rounded images: `rounded-xl`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Card gap: `gap-6`
- Stack gap: `gap-4`

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds with vivid accent colors for contrast
- Use large, full-bleed hero images with overlay gradients
- Use grid layouts with varied card sizes (masonry-style)
- Use subtle glowing borders on hover
- Use uppercase tracking-widest labels for section categories
- Ensure all text is clearly readable against dark backgrounds

## Don'ts
- No light backgrounds
- No flat, colorless designs
- No small or cramped images
- No low-contrast text on dark surfaces
