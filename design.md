# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
- Quiet luxury, warm, editorial.
- Premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Colors
- **Base**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Off-Black)
- **Background**: `#F9F7F2` (Warm Vanilla/Alabaster)
- **Accent**: `#C4A484` (Soft Gold Metallic)
- **Accent Dark**: `#8B735B` (Muted Bronze)
- **Text**: `#2D2D2D` (Refined Grey)
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - Style: Light / Regular / Italic
  - Application: Branding, section titles, product names (UPPERCASE with wide tracking)
- **Body / UI**: Inter (Sans-Serif)
  - Style: Regular / Medium
  - Application: Descriptions, interface buttons, labels

## Visual Elements
- **Borders**: Hairline thin (`0.5px` or `1px`) in `#E5E5E5`.
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace to feel premium.
- **Animations**: Soft fades, subtle scale on hover.
- **Shadows**: Very soft, subtle elevation for cards (`shadow-sm`).

## Buttons
- **Primary**: Solid Base color with White text.
- **Secondary**: Outlined Base color with Base text.
- **CTA**: Soft Gold background with White text for focal points.

## Do's
- Use large editorial imagery.
- Maintain consistent letter-spacing for uppercase text.
- Keep everything aligned to a clean grid.

## Don'ts
- No loud or bright colors (e.g. bright red or neons).
- No cluttered layouts.
- No heavy drop shadows.
