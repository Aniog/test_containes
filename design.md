# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry for women 25-45.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory) — main page background
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white) — cards, overlays
- **Foreground**: `#1A1A1A` (near black) — primary text
- **Muted**: `#6B6560` (warm taupe) — secondary text
- **Muted-light**: `#E8E2DB` (soft warm gray) — borders, dividers
- **Accent**: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod) — CTAs, highlights, links
- **Accent-hover**: `#9A7209` — hover state for accent
- **Accent-light**: `#F5ECD7` (pale gold wash) — subtle backgrounds
- **Star**: `#D4A843` — rating stars

## Typography
- **Headings / Brand**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card gaps: gap-6 to gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border border-[#E8E2DB]
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md on hover

## Buttons
- Primary: solid accent bg, white text, rounded-none (sharp edges for luxury feel)
- Secondary: outlined accent border, accent text, transparent bg
- Hover: slight opacity or bg shift

## Animations
- Subtle transitions: transition-all duration-300
- Hover scale on product images: hover:scale-105
- Fade-in on scroll sections

## Image Style
- Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Editorial, lifestyle photography feel
- 9:16 ratio for UGC/reels cards
- 4:5 or 3:4 for product cards
