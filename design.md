# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Minimalist but sophisticated.

## Color Palette
- **Background (Base):** `#FDFCFB` (Warm Cream) - `bg-[#FDFCFB]`
- **Foreground (Primary Text):** `#1A1A1A` (Deep Charcoal) - `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Accent (Gold):** `#C5A059` (Soft Warm Gold) - `text-[#C5A059]` or `bg-[#C5A059]`
- **Muted (Secondary):** `#717171` (Cool Gray) - `text-[#717171]`
- **Surface (Card/Section):** `#F4F1EE` (Soft Sand) - `bg-[#F4F1EE]`

## Typography
- **Headings/Product Names:** `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif).
  - Use `font-serif` and `tracking-[0.2em] uppercase` for product names.
- **Body/UI:** `Inter` (Sans-serif).
  - Use `font-sans` for descriptions, buttons, and navigation.

## Elements
- **Borders:** Thin hairline dividers (`border-[#1A1A1A]/10`).
- **Buttons:** Solid charcoal with white text for primary (`bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white`), or outlined charcoal for secondary.
- **Spacing:** Generous whitespace between sections (`py-24`).
- **Transitions:** Smooth 300ms ease-in-out.
- **Shadows:** Very soft, barely visible (`shadow-[0_4px_20px_-4px_rgba(0,0,0,0.05)]`).

## Do's and Don'ts
### Do:
- Use large, high-quality editorial images.
- Keep text aligned and balanced.
- Use uppercase for headings when appropriate.
- Ensure high contrast for readability.

### Don't:
- Use bright, neon, or discount-style colors.
- Overcrowd the layout.
- Use generic sans-serif for headings.
- Use heavy shadows or thick borders.
