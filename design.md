# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury. Warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **obsidian** `#1a1714` — primary text, dark backgrounds, nav
- **obsidian-light** `#2a2520` — secondary dark surfaces
- **ivory** `#f5f0e8` — primary background, light surfaces
- **ivory-dark** `#ede6d6` — card backgrounds, subtle dividers
- **gold** `#c9a96e` — primary accent, CTAs, highlights
- **gold-light** `#dfc08a` — hover states, subtle accents
- **gold-dark** `#a8854a` — pressed states, deep accents
- **taupe** `#8c7b6b` — secondary text, muted labels
- **taupe-light** `#b5a898` — placeholder text, dividers
- **blush** `#e8d5c4` — soft accent backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: `font-serif` = Cormorant Garamond (light 300, regular 400, medium 500)
- **Body / UI**: `font-sans` = Manrope (300, 400, 500, 600, 700)
- Product names: UPPERCASE, `tracking-widest` (0.25em)
- Section titles: `font-serif font-light`, large scale
- Nav links: `font-sans text-xs tracking-widest uppercase`

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections use `py-20 md:py-28`
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Hairline dividers: `border-t border-taupe/20`
- Cards: soft shadow `shadow-sm hover:shadow-md`

## Buttons
- **Primary**: `btn-primary` — gold fill, obsidian text, uppercase, wide tracking
- **Outline**: `btn-outline` — gold border, gold text, fills on hover
- **Dark**: `btn-dark` — obsidian fill, ivory text

## Do's
- Use Cormorant Garamond for all headings and product names
- Keep backgrounds warm (ivory, not pure white)
- Use gold sparingly as a true accent
- Generous padding and whitespace
- Subtle transitions (300–400ms ease)
- Thin hairline borders, not thick borders

## Don'ts
- No pure white (#ffffff) backgrounds — use ivory
- No bright/neon colors
- No thick borders or heavy shadows
- No generic e-commerce blue/green CTAs
- No tight spacing or cramped layouts
