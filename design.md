# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
*   **Mood:** Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium-but-accessible.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Base:** Deep warm charcoal/espresso (`text-stone-900`, `bg-stone-50`), soft bone/creme for backgrounds.
    *   **Accent:** Warm muted gold/brass (`bg-amber-700`, `text-amber-700`, bordering elements).
    *   **Neutrals:** `stone-100`, `stone-200`, `stone-300`, `stone-400` for dividers and borders.
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headings:** Elegant serif (Playfair Display).
    *   **Body & UI:** Clean sans-serif (Inter).
    *   **Product Names:** UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`).

## Tailwind Configuration & Global Styles
*   **Borders & Dividers:** Thin hairline dividers (`border-stone-200`).
*   **Shadows:** Soft, subtle shadows (`shadow-sm`, `hover:shadow-md`).
*   **Animations:** Smooth hover transitions (`transition-all duration-300`).
*   **Spacing:** Generous whitespace between sections (`py-16`, `py-24`).

## Component Guidelines
*   **Buttons:**
    *   Primary: Accent color background, white text (`bg-amber-700 text-white hover:bg-amber-800`).
    *   Secondary: Outline or text-only (`border border-amber-700 text-amber-700 hover:bg-amber-50`).
*   **Cards:** Minimal styling, mostly rely on high-quality imagery and whitespace.

## Do's and Don'ts
*   **Do:** Use high contrast for text readability.
*   **Do:** Ensure generous padding on mobile.
*   **Don't:** Emphasize discount badges or standard e-commerce visual noise.
*   **Don't:** Overuse the accent color. Keep it for interactive or key visual elements.
