# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-weight 700-800, tight letter-spacing
- Body text: font-weight 400-500, normal letter-spacing
- Small text: font-weight 400, slightly increased letter-spacing

## Colors
- Primary: slate-900 (dark navy/charcoal for text and headers)
- Secondary: blue-600 (accent color for CTAs and highlights)
- Background: white, slate-50 (light sections)
- Text: slate-900 (primary), slate-600 (secondary), slate-400 (muted)
- Borders: slate-200
- Hover states: blue-700

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6
- Component gaps: gap-6, gap-8, gap-12
- Card padding: p-6, p-8

## Visual Style
- Clean, industrial elegance
- Sharp corners with subtle rounded-lg on cards
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md on hover
- Professional photography for machinery
- Grid layouts for products (2-col mobile, 3-col desktop)
- Smooth transitions on interactive elements

## Do's
- Use high contrast for readability
- Keep navigation simple and sticky
- Use consistent button styles
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use stock images of industrial machinery

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No overly decorative fonts
- No cluttered layouts
- No low-contrast text
- No excessive animations
