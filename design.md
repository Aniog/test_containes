# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory) — main page background
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white) — cards, overlays
- **Base/Text Primary**: `#1A1A1A` (near-black) — headings, body text
- **Text Secondary**: `#6B6560` (warm taupe) — subtitles, muted text
- **Text Muted**: `#9B9590` (soft warm gray) — placeholders, hints
- **Accent/Gold**: `#B8956A` (warm metallic gold) — CTAs, links, highlights
- **Accent Hover**: `#A07D52` (deeper gold) — hover states
- **Border**: `#E8E2DB` (warm light taupe) — dividers, card borders
- **Border Light**: `#F0EBE5` (very light warm) — subtle separators
- **Star Rating**: `#C9A96E` (gold star)
- **Success**: `#4A7C59` (muted sage green)
- **Error**: `#B54545` (muted burgundy)

## Typography
- **Headings / Brand**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
  - Logo: 28px, weight 400, letter-spacing 0.15em
  - H1: 48-64px, weight 300, letter-spacing 0.02em
  - H2: 32-40px, weight 400, letter-spacing 0.02em
  - H3: 20-24px, weight 400, letter-spacing 0.04em
- **Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond, UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.12em
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern
  - Body: 15px, weight 400, line-height 1.6
  - Small: 13px, weight 400
  - Buttons: 13px, weight 500, letter-spacing 0.08em, UPPERCASE

## Spacing
- Section padding: 80-120px vertical on desktop, 48-64px on mobile
- Card gap: 24px
- Component gap: 16px
- Tight gap: 8px

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: 1px solid `#E8E2DB`
- Card borders: 1px solid `#F0EBE5`
- Border radius: 2px (minimal, refined)

## Shadows
- Card hover: `0 4px 20px rgba(26, 26, 26, 0.06)`
- Dropdown/overlay: `0 8px 40px rgba(26, 26, 26, 0.12)`

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold background, white text, 2px border-radius
- Secondary: transparent background, gold border, gold text
- Full-width on product pages
- Hover: slight darken, subtle scale (1.01)

## Animations
- Hover transitions: 300ms ease
- Fade in: 400ms ease-out
- Cart drawer slide: 300ms cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- Image zoom on hover: scale(1.03) over 500ms

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text hierarchy clear
- Use serif for emotional/brand moments, sans-serif for functional UI
- Product names always UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing
- Thin hairline dividers between sections
- Warm, editorial imagery

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy shadows or rounded corners > 4px
- No loud gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No generic e-commerce patterns (countdown timers, "only 3 left!")
- No emoji or playful icons
