# SSourcing China — Design System

## Brand Identity
- Company: SSourcing China
- Tagline: China Sourcing Agent for Global Buyers
- Tone: Professional, trustworthy, international, B2B, practical

## Color Palette
- Primary Blue: `#1A3C6E` (deep navy — trust, authority) → Tailwind: `blue-900` or custom `brand-blue`
- Accent Red: `#C0392B` (China red — identity, energy) → Tailwind: custom `brand-red`
- Accent Gold: `#D4A017` (quality, premium) → Tailwind: custom `brand-gold`
- Background Light: `#F8F9FB` → Tailwind: `gray-50`
- Surface White: `#FFFFFF`
- Text Primary: `#1A1A2E` → Tailwind: `gray-900`
- Text Secondary: `#4A5568` → Tailwind: `gray-600`
- Text Muted: `#718096` → Tailwind: `gray-500`
- Border: `#E2E8F0` → Tailwind: `gray-200`
- Section Alt BG: `#EEF2F7` → Tailwind: custom `brand-bg-alt`

## Typography
- Font Family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-tight
  - H1: text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl
  - H2: text-3xl md:text-4xl
  - H3: text-xl md:text-2xl
  - H4: text-lg md:text-xl
- Body: text-base (16px), leading-relaxed
- Small/Caption: text-sm, text-gray-500

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: border border-gray-200
- Card shadow: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow
- Rounded corners: rounded-xl (cards), rounded-lg (buttons), rounded-full (badges/pills)

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-brand-red text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-lg font-semibold hover:bg-red-700 transition
- Secondary: border-2 border-brand-blue text-brand-blue px-6 py-3 rounded-lg font-semibold hover:bg-brand-blue hover:text-white transition
- Ghost: text-brand-blue underline hover:no-underline

## Navigation
- Sticky top nav, white background, shadow on scroll
- Logo left, nav links center/right, CTA button far right
- Mobile: hamburger menu

## Section Patterns
- Alternating white / brand-bg-alt backgrounds
- Section titles: centered with colored underline accent
- Cards: white bg, border, subtle shadow, hover lift

## Do's
- Use navy blue for headings and trust elements
- Use China red sparingly for CTAs and accents
- Use gold for highlights and quality indicators
- Keep layouts clean with generous whitespace
- Use real-looking factory/QC/shipping imagery via stock system

## Don'ts
- No dark mode (B2B professional site)
- No neon colors or gradients that look cheap
- No exaggerated claims in copy
- No cluttered layouts
