# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium.
- **Palette**:
  - Luxury Black: `#1A1A1A` (Tailwind `luxury-black`)
  - Gold Accents: `#D4AF37` (Tailwind `gold-600`)
  - Warm Neutral: `#FAF9F6`
  - Border/Divider: `#F3F4F6`
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: Serif (Inter/Cormorant Garamond/Playfair Display)
  - UI/Body: Sans-serif (Inter) - Uppercase for product names with wide tracking.
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, elegant hairline dividers.
- **Buttons**: solid or outlined luxury-black, sharp or slightly rounded (2px).

## Components
- **Buttons**: `.bg-luxury-black .text-white .tracking-[0.3em]`
- **Cards**: Minimalist, high-quality images, subtle hover scales.
- **Dividers**: `.border-gray-100` or `.border-gold-100`
