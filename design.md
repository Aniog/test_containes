# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

## Colors
- **Background**: white (`#ffffff`) for main content, dark-50 (`#f5f5f1`) for alternate sections
- **Text**: dark-900 (`#12110d`) for headings, dark-500 (`#6a6554`) for body, dark-300 (`#a39f92`) for muted
- **Accent**: gold-500 (`#d49a2f`) for CTAs, gold-400 (`#e7b149`) for hover states, gold-100 (`#f9edd3`) for badges
- **Dark sections**: dark-900 (`#12110d`) backgrounds with cream-50 (`#fefcf8`) text
- **Borders/ Dividers**: dark-100 (`#e0ded8`) thin hairline borders
- **Gold jewelry flattering**: dark backgrounds make gold pop; cream/gold accents warm the palette

## Typography
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif), regular weight 400-600, uppercase tracking for product names
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif), 300-500 weight
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE with `tracking-widest` class
- **UI labels**: uppercase with `tracking-widest`, small text size

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 lg:py-24`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 lg:px-8`
- Generous whitespace between sections

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: `border-t border-dark-100` or `w-12 h-px bg-gold-400`
- No rounded corners on buttons (square = premium)
- No heavy borders

## Buttons
- Primary (dark): `bg-dark-900 text-cream-50 hover:bg-dark-800`
- Accent (gold): `bg-gold-500 text-dark-900 hover:bg-gold-400`
- Outline: `border border-dark-900 text-dark-900 bg-transparent hover:bg-dark-900 hover:text-cream-50`
- All uppercase, tracking-widest, small text, 12px/14px

## Effects
- Subtle hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300`
- Image zoom on hover: `group-hover:scale-105` with `duration-700`
- Fade-in animations: `animate-fade-in`, `animate-slide-up`
- Soft shadows on scroll: `shadow-sm` for sticky nav

## Product Cards
- Aspect ratio 4:5 for product images
- Star ratings with gold fill
- Product name in uppercase with wide tracking
- Price in serif or medium weight

## DO's
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use warm gold tones sparingly as accent
- Use thin dividers between sections
- Maintain editorial/editorial feel
- Gold jewelry on dark or neutral backgrounds

## DON'Ts
- No bright saturated colors
- No heavy shadows or borders
- No rounded corners on buttons and cards
- No loud sale/ discount callouts
- No overcrowded layouts
