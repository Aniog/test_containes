# Visual Style - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Elegant Serif (`Cormorant Garamond`, `Playfair Display`)
  - Class: `font-serif` or `font-heading`
  - Style: Uppercase with wide letter-spacing for product names (`letter-spacing-wide uppercase`)
- **Body & UI**: Clean Sans-Serif (`Inter`, `Manrope`)
  - Class: `font-sans`

## Colors
- **Background**: Soft Warm White (`hsl(30, 20%, 98%)`)
- **Foreground**: Deep Charcoal/Brown (`hsl(20, 10%, 12%)`)
- **Primary/Accent**: Deep Gold/Bronze (`hsl(35, 30%, 15%)`)
- **Muted**: Soft Grey-Beige (`hsl(35, 10%, 45%)`)
- **Borders**: Thin Hairline (`hsl(35, 15%, 88%)` or `rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.08)`)

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid deep primary color, minimal height, elegant padding.
  - Secondary/Outline: Hairline border, transparent or soft background.
- **Transitions**: Subtle, smooth animations (`transition-luxury`)
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline dividers
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, editorial layout

## Imagery
- Large editorial shots
- Warm-lit close-ups
- 9:16 vertical cards for UGC sections
- Hover reveals for second product images
