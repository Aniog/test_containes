# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background (cream):** `#FAF8F5` — warm off-white for page backgrounds
- **Surface (warm white):** `#FFFFFF` — cards, modals
- **Primary text (deep espresso):** `#1C1917` — headings, body
- **Secondary text (warm gray):** `#78716C` — captions, muted text
- **Accent (burnished gold):** `#B8860B` — CTAs, highlights, hover states
- **Accent hover (deep gold):** `#996F09` — button hover
- **Accent light (champagne):** `#F5ECD7` — subtle accent backgrounds
- **Border (hairline):** `#E7E5E4` — dividers, card borders
- **Dark surface:** `#1C1917` — footer, dark sections

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names:** `Cormorant Garamond`, serif — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI:** `Inter`, sans-serif — clean, modern
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.1em–0.15em
- **Heading sizes:** Hero: text-5xl/6xl, Section: text-3xl/4xl, Card: text-lg/xl

## Tailwind Classes (common patterns)
- Headings: `font-serif text-stone-900 tracking-wide`
- Body: `font-sans text-stone-600`
- Accent button: `bg-gold text-white hover:bg-gold-dark transition-colors duration-300`
- Outlined button: `border border-gold text-gold hover:bg-gold hover:text-white transition-colors duration-300`
- Cards: `bg-white border border-stone-200 hover:shadow-md transition-shadow duration-300`
- Hairline divider: `border-t border-stone-200`
- Section spacing: `py-16 md:py-24 px-4 md:px-8 lg:px-16`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections (py-16 to py-24)
- Large editorial imagery with warm tones
- Thin hairline dividers between sections
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- Soft shadows on hover (shadow-md)
- Buttons feel premium — solid gold or outlined with gold border
- Product names always uppercase with wide letter-spacing

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded-full buttons (use rounded-sm or rounded-none for premium feel)
- No cluttered layouts — always breathe
- No generic e-commerce blue/green CTAs
- No dark mode (this is a warm, light brand)
