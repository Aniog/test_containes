# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design Guide

## Typography
- **Headings (Logo, Product Names, Main Titles):** Cormorant Garamond (serif). Elegant, classic. Use UPPERCASE and wide letter-spacing for product names and small headers.
- **Body & UI (Text, Buttons, Navigation):** Inter (sans-serif). Clean, highly readable, modern.

## Colors
- **Base (Background):** Soft neutral/warm white `bg-[#FAFAF8]` or classic stark bright `#FFFFFF`. Let's use a very soft cream `bg-[#FDFCFB]` for the main background to flatter gold.
- **Text (Foreground):** Deep refined gray-black `text-[#2D2B2A]`.
- **Accent/Brand:** Warm gold/bronze metallic accent `bg-[#C6A87C]` / `text-[#C6A87C]` for buttons and key highlights.
- **Muted/Secondary:** Soft taupe/beige `#EBE7E0` for borders, dividers, secondary section backgrounds.

## Layout & Space
- Generous whitespace (heavy use of `py-16`, `py-24`, `gap-8`, `gap-12`).
- Thin hairline dividers (`border-t border-[#EBE7E0]`).
- Soft UI: subtle hover transitions, minimal soft shadows only where necessary (like sticky nav or dropdown).
- Buttons: Solid or outlined accent. Rectangular with 0 or very slight rounding (e.g. `rounded-sm`). Feel premium and structured.

## Images
- Large editorial shots.
- Warm lighting, dark or neutral backgrounds to make gold pop.
- Ratios: `3x4` or `4x5` for products to mimic fashion editorial style. UGC uses `9x16`.
