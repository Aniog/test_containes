# Design Guidelines - SSourcing China

SSourcing China is a professional B2B sourcing agency. The design should convey trust, efficiency, and international standards.

## Visual Style
- **Clean & Trustworthy**: Plenty of white space, clear typography, and a structured layout.
- **Professional B2B**: Avoid over-the-top animations or flashy colors. Use solid, professional tones.
- **International**: Modern look that appeals to global buyers (Europe, North America, Oceania).

## Colors
- **Primary (Navy Blue)**: `#1e3a8a` (Tailwind: `blue-900`) - Represents trust and stability.
- **Secondary (Slate/Silver)**: `#64748b` (Tailwind: `slate-500`) - For secondary text and borders.
- **Accent (Orange/Gold)**: `#f59e0b` (Tailwind: `amber-500`) - For CTAs and highlighting key points.
- **Background**: `#ffffff` (White) for main areas, `#f8fafc` (Tailwind: `slate-50`) for section backgrounds.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, bold, dark navy colors.
- **Body**: Inter, regular, medium gray for readability.
- **Font Stack**: `Inter, system-ui, sans-serif`.

## Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded (default `md`), solid primary colors for main CTA, outlined for secondary.
- **Cards**: Subtle borders or very soft shadows (`shadow-sm`).
- **Icons**: Lucide icons in primary or accent colors.
- **Images**: Realistic B2B/Industrial photography. Avoid generic "handshake" stock if possible.

## Spacing
- **Section Padding**: `py-16` or `py-20` for desktop, `py-10` for mobile.
- **Container**: Max-width `7xl` centered with `px-4`.

## Do's
- Use high-quality realistic industrial/office/shipping images.
- Keep the language clear and practical.
- Use explicit labels for form fields.
- Ensure all text is high contrast.

## Don'ts
- Don't use neon or overly bright colors (except for the accent Orange).
- Don't clutter the pages with too much text in one block.
- Don't use low-resolution images.
