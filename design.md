# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand Identity
Premium industrial machinery manufacturer specializing in sheet metal folding solutions. 
The brand conveys precision engineering, reliability, and sophistication.

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) — elegant, authoritative
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern, readable
- Font weights: Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500), Semibold (600), Bold (700)

## Color Palette
- Primary Dark: #0a0e17 (near-black with blue undertone)
- Primary Surface: #111827 (dark charcoal)
- Card Surface: #1a1f2e (dark navy)
- Accent Gold: #c8a45a (warm brass/gold)
- Accent Gold Light: #dfc07a
- Text Primary: #f1f5f9 (near-white)
- Text Secondary: #94a3b8 (slate gray)
- Text Muted: #64748b (muted slate)
- Border Subtle: rgba(255,255,255,0.06)
- Border Medium: rgba(255,255,255,0.1)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between grid items: gap-8

## Visual Style
- Dark, sophisticated background with subtle gradients
- Gold accent lines and highlights for elegance
- Card-based layouts with subtle borders and hover effects
- Clean iconography using Lucide icons
- Smooth transitions and hover states
- Generous whitespace for readability

## Do's
- Use Playfair Display for headings, Inter for body text
- Use gold accent color sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Maintain high contrast — light text on dark backgrounds
- Keep cards with subtle borders and hover lift effects
- Use responsive grids that adapt from 1 to 3 columns

## Don'ts
- Do not use bright, saturated colors
- Do not use heavy drop shadows or harsh borders
- Do not overcrowd sections — maintain breathing room
- Do not use pure black (#000) — use the near-black palette instead
