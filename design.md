# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial Metal Folding Machinery
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly — sophisticated industrial design with clean, approachable UI

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) — clean, modern, highly legible
- **Headings**: Inter 700-800 weight, tracking tight
- **Body**: Inter 400 weight, leading relaxed
- **Accent/Label**: Inter 500 weight, uppercase tracking-wide for labels and tags

## Color Palette
- **Primary Dark**: `#1a1a2e` — deep navy-charcoal (backgrounds, hero)
- **Primary Mid**: `#16213e` — dark blue (secondary backgrounds)
- **Accent Gold**: `#c9a84c` — warm metallic gold (CTAs, highlights, accents)
- **Accent Gold Hover**: `#d4b85c` — lighter gold for hover states
- **Steel Gray**: `#4a5568` — mid gray for secondary text
- **Light Gray**: `#e2e8f0` — borders, dividers
- **Off-White**: `#f7f8fa` — light section backgrounds
- **White**: `#ffffff` — card backgrounds, text on dark
- **Dark Text**: `#1a202c` — primary text on light backgrounds

## Tailwind Color Mapping
- Use `bg-[#1a1a2e]` for dark sections
- Use `bg-[#16213e]` for secondary dark sections
- Use `text-[#c9a84c]` and `bg-[#c9a84c]` for gold accents
- Use `bg-[#f7f8fa]` for light alternating sections
- Use `text-[#1a202c]` for dark text on light backgrounds

## Spacing & Layout
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows on cards: `shadow-lg` or `shadow-2xl`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-lg` to `rounded-xl`
- Gold accent borders and underlines for emphasis
- Metallic/industrial imagery with dark overlays
- Smooth transitions: `transition-all duration-300`

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: Gold background (`bg-[#c9a84c]`), dark text, hover lighter gold
- Secondary CTA: Transparent with gold border, gold text
- Rounded: `rounded-lg`
- Padding: `px-6 py-3` to `px-8 py-4`

## Cards
- White or dark background depending on section
- Subtle border or shadow
- Gold accent on hover (border-top or underline)
- Clean iconography

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace for elegance
- Use gold accents sparingly for sophistication
- Ensure high contrast between text and backgrounds
- Use smooth hover transitions
- Keep layouts clean and uncluttered

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much content
- Don't use low-contrast text (e.g., light gray on white)
- Don't use more than 2-3 colors per section
- Don't use decorative fonts — stick with Inter
