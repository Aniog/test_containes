# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury: warm, editorial, refined, and premium without feeling flashy or inaccessible. The mood is intimate and polished, with generous whitespace, thin dividers, soft shadows, and elegant typography.

## Palette
Use one consistent neutral-luxury palette that flatters gold jewelry.

- `velmora-mist` — warm ivory page background
- `velmora-ivory` — light editorial surface
- `velmora-pearl` — soft elevated cards
- `velmora-sand` — subtle secondary fills and chips
- `velmora-line` — hairline borders and dividers
- `velmora-bronze` — premium accent for CTAs and highlights
- `velmora-bronze-dark` — hover / pressed accent
- `velmora-ink` — primary body text
- `velmora-espresso` — deep header, footer, and hero overlays

## Typography
- Headings, brand wordmark, product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body copy, navigation, UI, prices: Inter
- Product names should be uppercase with generous letter spacing.

## Layout and spacing
- Favor wide section padding and breathing room.
- Use editorial proportions: large imagery, balanced text blocks, and asymmetry where it feels intentional.
- On mobile, keep spacing airy but compact enough to feel premium and easy to browse.

## UI details
- Buttons should feel polished: solid bronze for primary CTAs, outlined ivory or line-based buttons for secondary actions.
- Borders should be thin and understated.
- Use soft shadows only on elevated cards and the cart drawer.
- Hover transitions should be subtle and smooth, never bouncy.

## Do
- Use warm neutral surfaces and high-contrast text.
- Keep product imagery large and elegant.
- Maintain a premium editorial rhythm with serif headlines and refined spacing.
- Use consistent accent color treatment for CTAs, stars, and subtle highlights.

## Don't
- Do not use discount-style badges, bright sale colors, or loud gradients.
- Do not use heavy borders, oversized shadows, or crowded layouts.
- Do not mix multiple accent colors.
- Do not allow low-contrast text on images or soft backgrounds.
