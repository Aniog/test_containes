# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand Identity
- Brand Name: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- Industry: Industrial machinery / sheet metal folding equipment
- Style: Elegant but user-friendly — premium industrial feel with approachable UI

## Color Palette
- Primary: Deep navy blue (#1a2744) — conveys precision, trust, industrial strength
- Primary Light: (#2a3f6a) — hover states, secondary elements
- Accent: Warm gold (#c9953c) — premium accents, CTAs, highlights
- Accent Light: (#e0b05e) — hover states for gold elements
- Background: Off-white (#f8f7f4) — warm, elegant base
- Surface: White (#ffffff) — cards, modals
- Text Primary: (#1a1a2e) — headings, body
- Text Secondary: (#5a5a7a) — muted text
- Border: (#e5e0d8) — subtle dividers

## Typography
- Headings: 'Playfair Display', serif — elegant, premium feel
- Body: 'Inter', sans-serif — clean, readable, modern
- Font sizes follow Tailwind default scale

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card gap: gap-8

## Component Styles

### Navigation
- Fixed top, transparent initially, bg-white/95 backdrop-blur on scroll
- Logo: Playfair Display, bold
- Links: Inter medium, uppercase tracking-wide, small text
- Gold underline hover effect

### Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#c9953c] text-white, rounded-none, hover:bg-[#e0b05e]
- Secondary: border-2 border-[#1a2744] text-[#1a2744], hover:bg-[#1a2744] hover:text-white
- All buttons: uppercase tracking-wider, font-medium, transition-all

### Cards
- White background, subtle shadow
- Clean borders, generous padding
- Hover: slight lift with shadow increase

### Hero
- Full-screen dark overlay on background image
- Large Playfair Display heading in white
- Gold accent line separator
- CTA buttons

### Product Grid
- 3-column on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 on mobile
- Product cards with image, title, short desc, specs badges
- Hover: subtle lift effect

### Sections
- Alternating background: off-white and white
- Section titles: Playfair Display, centered, with gold accent underline

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep industrial feel with clean lines
- Gold accents sparingly for premium touches
- Responsive typography

## Don'ts
- Don't use overly bright/flashy colors
- Don't clutter with too many competing elements
- Don't use playful/casual fonts
