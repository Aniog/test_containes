# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Style Guide

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: warm off-white/ivory (#FAF7F2) — editorial warmth
- Surface: pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Text primary: deep warm charcoal (#2C2420)
- Text secondary: muted warm brown (#7A7066)
- Text muted: lighter warm brown (#A59A8F)
- Accent gold: burnished gold (#C9A96E) — buttons, highlights, accents
- Accent gold hover: deeper gold (#B08D4E)
- Divider/light: warm beige (#E8E1D5)
- Border light: very light warm beige (#F0EBE3)
- Overlay dark: semi-transparent dark (#1A1513CC)
- Star/rating: gold (#D4A853)

## Typography
- Headings (h1-h4, product names): "Playfair Display", Georgia, serif
- Body text, UI, nav, buttons: "Inter", -apple-system, sans-serif
- Product names: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest or 0.15em)
- Price: Playfair Display italic
- Section labels/accents: uppercase, small, tracked

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px, centered
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card gap: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Visual Elements
- Thin hairline dividers (border-t border-[#E8E1D5])
- Subtle hover transitions (transition-all duration-300)
- Soft shadows on cards (shadow-sm, hover:shadow-md)
- Buttons: solid accent gold with warm white text, or outlined accent gold
  - Primary button: bg-[#C9A96E] text-white hover:bg-[#B08D4E]
  - Outline button: border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E] hover:bg-[#C9A96E] hover:text-white
- Images: large, editorial, warm-toned
- Borders: thin, warm beige

## Do's
- Use warm off-white backgrounds
- Generous padding and whitespace
- Serif for elegance, sans-serif for clarity
- Uppercase product names with letter-spacing
- Gold accent sparingly — let it pop
- Subtle animations (fade in, slide up)

## Don'ts
- Don't use pure black (#000) — always use dark warm charcoal
- Don't use bright/cold colors
- Don't overcrowd — let the jewelry breathe
- Don't use loud sale/badge styling
- Don't use harsh borders
