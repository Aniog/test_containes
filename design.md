# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual direction
Quiet luxury with a dark editorial base, warm champagne gold accents, soft ivory surfaces, and generous breathing room. The brand should feel premium, calm, feminine, and elevated.

## Color palette
- `obsidian` — deep refined background for hero, footer, overlays. Example: `bg-obsidian text-ivory`
- `ink` — primary readable text on light surfaces. Example: `text-ink`
- `ivory` — soft page background and cards. Example: `bg-ivory text-ink`
- `sand` — secondary neutral surface. Example: `bg-sand`
- `champagne` — premium accent for buttons, rules, and badges. Example: `bg-champagne text-obsidian`
- `bronze` — darker metallic hover/accent state. Example: `text-bronze border-bronze`
- `mist` — muted borders and separators. Example: `border-mist`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `Cormorant Garamond`, elegant serif, used with balanced line height.
- Body and UI: `Inter`, clean sans-serif.
- Product names should appear in uppercase with strong letter spacing. Example: `uppercase tracking-[0.28em]`.

## Spacing and layout
- Use wide section padding: `px-5 sm:px-8 lg:px-12 xl:px-16`
- Constrain content with `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- Use large vertical rhythm: `py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-28`
- Prefer grid gaps of `gap-6`, `gap-8`, or `gap-10`

## Borders and surfaces
- Favor hairline borders: `border border-mist/70`
- Cards should use soft shadows only: `shadow-[0_20px_60px_rgba(18,18,18,0.08)]`
- Rounded corners should stay refined: `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl`, not overly playful

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: `bg-champagne text-obsidian hover:bg-bronze hover:text-ivory`
- Secondary CTA: `border border-ink/20 text-ink hover:border-bronze hover:text-bronze`
- Buttons should feel substantial and premium with `uppercase tracking-[0.24em] text-[0.72rem]`

## Imagery
- Warm-lit gold jewelry, close crops, editorial portraits, dark neutral backdrops
- Avoid busy, bright, discount-looking, or overly commercial images

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text always explicit on each surface
- Use generous whitespace and restrained motion
- Use thin dividers and small editorial details

## Don't
- Don't use bright promotional reds, neon accents, or sale-banner styling
- Don't crowd cards with too many badges
- Don't mix multiple visual directions
