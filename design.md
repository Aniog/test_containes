# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Base (deep warm charcoal):** `#1C1917` (stone-900) — primary background for hero/dark sections
- **Cream (warm off-white):** `#FAF7F2` — page background, light sections
- **Gold Accent:** `#C9A96E` — primary accent, CTAs, highlights
- **Gold Hover:** `#B8944F` — darker gold for hover states
- **Text Primary:** `#1C1917` — headings, body on light
- **Text Secondary:** `#78716C` (stone-500) — muted text, descriptions
- **Text on Dark:** `#FAF7F2` — text on dark backgrounds
- **Divider:** `#E7E0D6` — hairline dividers, borders
- **Card Background:** `#FFFFFF` — product cards on cream

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond, serif — elegant, editorial
- **Body/UI:** Inter, sans-serif — clean, modern
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond
- **Section headings:** Cormorant Garamond, italic for subheadings

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace — sections separated by 80-120px on desktop
- Max content width: 1280px
- Product grid: 4 columns desktop, 2 mobile
- Hairline dividers (1px, warm gray)

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold (#C9A96E) background, cream text, rounded-none, uppercase, letter-spacing
- Secondary: outlined with gold border, gold text, transparent bg
- Hover: darken gold, smooth transition 200ms

## Images
- Warm-lit, editorial style
- Product images on neutral/dark backgrounds
- Hover reveals second image
- Large hero imagery, full-bleed

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth transitions (200-300ms ease)
- No bouncy or playful animations
- Soft fade-ins on scroll
- Gentle hover lifts on cards

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use gold accent sparingly
- Ensure strong contrast for accessibility
- Use thin hairline dividers

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or saturated colors
- Don't use rounded buttons (keep sharp/minimal rounding)
- Don't crowd content
- Don't use playful or bouncy animations
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (badges, aggressive sales text)
