# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Style
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.
- **Direction**: Warm neutral/editorial scheme with deep refined base + warm metallic accents.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), elegant, refined
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif), clean, modern
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-wide/tracking-ultra-wide)
- **Labels/buttons**: UPPERCASE, extra-small (text-xs), wide letter-spacing, font-semibold

## Colors
- `brand-cream` (#FAF7F2): Main background — warm, soft cream
- `brand-warm` (#F5EDE3): Section alternate background
- `brand-sand` (#E8DDD0): Borders, dividers, subtle accents
- `brand-gold` (#C9A96E): Primary accent — buttons, highlights, stars
- `brand-gold-light` (#D4BA8A): Hover states, secondary gold
- `brand-gold-dark` (#A8864E): Active/pressed gold states
- `brand-charcoal` (#2C2C2C): Primary text
- `brand-espresso` (#1A1612): Dark sections (footer, trust bar, newsletter)
- `brand-muted` (#8A8279): Secondary text
- `brand-muted-light` (#A69E94): Tertiary text, placeholders

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 7xl (1280px)
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Hairline dividers: h-px bg-brand-gold or bg-brand-sand

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-brand-gold text-white, hover:bg-brand-gold-dark
- Secondary: border border-brand-gold text-brand-gold, hover:bg-brand-gold hover:text-white
- All buttons: text-xs tracking-wide uppercase font-semibold

## Images
- Product cards: aspect-[3/4], hover reveals second image
- Category tiles: aspect-[4/5]
- Hero: full-bleed, aspect-[16/9] or full-screen
- UGC: aspect-[9/16] vertical cards

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions (300-500ms)
- Soft shadows, no heavy drop shadows
- Scale on hover for images (scale-105)
- Fade/slide for overlays and drawers

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Keep accent color (gold) restrained — not everywhere
- Ensure strong contrast for readability

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or saturated colors
- Don't use heavy borders or thick dividers
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (starbursts, aggressive CTAs)
- Don't mix too many font styles
