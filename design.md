# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand Summary
ARTITECT MACHINERY builds premium sheet-metal folding machines: double folding machines, double folders, sheet metal folders, sheet metal folding machines, metal folders, metal folder machines, and metal folding machines. The site must feel elegant, industrial, and user-friendly.

## Visual Style
- Clean, confident, engineering-led aesthetic.
- Lots of white/negative space, generous section padding.
- Strong typographic hierarchy with a single accent color.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#ffffff` (white)
- Surface/Card: `#f8f9fa` (light gray)
- Primary accent: `#c69c3f` (warm brass/gold — industrial luxury)
- Primary accent hover: `#b08a35`
- Text primary: `#1a1a1a` (near black)
- Text secondary: `#6b7280` (cool gray)
- Border/Divider: `#e5e7eb`

## Typography
- Headings: Inter, 700–800 weight, tight line-height.
- Body: Inter, 400–500 weight, comfortable line-height.
- Hero headline: large, uppercase tracking for the brand name.

## Buttons
- Primary: gold background (`#c69c3f`), white text, rounded-md, medium padding.
- Secondary/Outline: transparent with dark border, dark text.
- Hover: slight lift shadow and color shift.

## Spacing
- Section vertical padding: `py-20` to `py-28`.
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl`, centered.
- Card/grid gaps: `gap-6` to `gap-8`.

## Components
- Navigation: fixed top, white background, subtle shadow, logo left, links right, mobile hamburger.
- Hero: full-width, headline + subheadline + CTA buttons, optional industrial background image.
- Product cards: image, title, short description, specs list, CTA.
- Feature list: icon + title + description.
- Contact section: simple form (name, email, message) on light surface.
- Footer: dark background, white text, company info + links.

## Do's
- Use Tailwind utility classes for everything.
- Ensure all text has strong contrast.
- Keep buttons and links clearly interactive.
- Use Lucide icons for features and UI cues.

## Don'ts
- Don't use neon or overly bright colors.
- Don't crowd sections; keep whitespace generous.
- Don't use low-contrast text on light surfaces.
