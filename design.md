# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial machinery manufacturing (sheet metal folding machines)
- **Target Audience**: Factory owners, metal fabrication companies, industrial buyers

## Visual Style
Elegant but user-friendly - clean, professional, industrial yet approachable

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: #1a1a2e (Deep Navy) - Trust, professionalism
- **Secondary**: #16213e (Dark Blue) - Depth, sophistication
- **Accent**: #e94560 (Industrial Red) - Energy, action, machinery
- **Light**: #f8f9fa (Off-white) - Cleanliness, clarity
- **Text Primary**: #1a1a2e (Deep Navy)
- **Text Secondary**: #6c757d (Muted Gray)
- **Background**: #ffffff (White) and #f8f9fa (Light Gray)

## Typography
- **Headings**: "DM Sans" - Modern, geometric, professional
- **Body**: "Inter" - Clean, readable, user-friendly
- **Font Sizes**:
  - H1: 56px (hero), H2: 42px (sections), H3: 24px (cards)
  - Body: 16px, Small: 14px

## Layout & Spacing
- **Max Width**: 1280px centered
- **Section Padding**: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Card Padding**: 24px
- **Grid Gap**: 32px

## Visual Elements
- Subtle shadows: 0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- Border radius: 12px (cards), 8px (buttons)
- Smooth transitions: 0.3s ease

## Components

### Header
- Fixed navigation with white background
- Logo on left, nav links center, CTA button right
- Subtle shadow on scroll

### Hero Section
- Full-width with dark gradient overlay
- Bold headline with accent color highlight
- Subheadline with product keywords
- Two CTA buttons: Primary (red) and Secondary (outline)
- Industrial machinery background image

### Products Section
- Section title with accent underline
- 3-column grid of product cards
- Each card: image, title, brief description, "Learn More" link
- Hover effect: lift and shadow

### Features Section
- Alternating layout (icon + text)
- 4 key selling points
- Icons in accent color

### About Section
- Two-column: image + text
- Company story and expertise

### Contact Section
- Dark background
- Contact form with fields
- Company info sidebar

### Footer
- Dark background
- 4 columns: About, Products, Support, Contact
- Social links
- Copyright
