# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood & Direction
Quiet luxury, editorial, warm. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Not loud or discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- Base: Creamy warm neutrals (ivory, creme, sand)
- Deep tones: Deep espresso (#1A1817), warm charcoal (#2C2A29)
- Gold accents: Warm champagne gold (#C9A96E), light gold (#DBBF8A), dark gold (#A68A4A)
- Rose accent: Muted rose (#B76E5E) — used sparingly
- Borders: Subtle warm beige (#E8E3DC)

## Typography
- Headings & Product Names: Playfair Display (serif), UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing
- Body & UI: Inter (sans-serif), clean readable sizes
- Product names: tracking-wider (0.12em), uppercase
- Section titles: Playfair Display, 2xl–4xl sizes, normal case or uppercase
- Prices: Inter medium weight

## Layout
- Generous whitespace, max-w-7xl centered containers
- Thin hairline dividers (border-velmora-border)
- Full-bleed hero sections
- Cards with subtle hover transitions and soft shadows
- Mobile-first responsive, single column on mobile, grid on desktop

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold bg, white text, uppercase, wide tracking
- Outline: gold border, gold text, fills gold on hover
- Both have hover shadow-lift effect

## Do's
- Use velmora color tokens
- Serif for headings/product names, sans for body
- UPPERCASE product names with letter-spacing
- Generous padding, breathing room
- Subtle transitions on interactive elements
- Hairline dividers

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy black borders
- No default blue links
- No generic e-commerce look
- No discount/badge/sale aesthetics
- No dark backgrounds for main content
