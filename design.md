# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. The site should feel elevated and intimate rather than loud or promotional.

## Palette
Use one consistent editorial direction built around deep espresso neutrals and warm champagne-metal accents.

- Background base: `bg-stone-950`
- Elevated dark surfaces: `bg-stone-900`
- Warm ivory sections: `bg-stone-100`
- Card surface: `bg-stone-50` or `bg-stone-900`
- Primary text on dark: `text-stone-50`
- Primary text on light: `text-stone-950`
- Secondary text on dark: `text-stone-300`
- Secondary text on light: `text-stone-600`
- Hairline borders: `border-stone-200/15` on dark, `border-stone-300` on light
- Accent metallic tone: `text-amber-200`, `bg-amber-200`, `border-amber-200`
- Accent button dark text: `text-stone-950`

## Typography
- Heading font: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif
- Body/UI font: `Inter`, sans-serif
- Product names and premium labels: uppercase with tracking classes such as `tracking-[0.28em]` or `tracking-[0.22em]`
- Editorial headlines: large serif with relaxed leading and medium weight

## Layout and spacing
- Generous whitespace, especially in hero, story, and newsletter sections
- Desktop containers should feel airy: `max-w-7xl`, `px-6`, `lg:px-10`
- Use thin dividers and subtle shadows rather than heavy borders
- Cards should have soft radii: `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl`

## Components
- Buttons: premium, slightly tall, refined transitions, no harsh colors
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid dark on scroll
- Product cards: editorial imagery, soft hover lift, secondary image reveal, quick add button
- Forms: understated with strong contrast, readable placeholders
- Cart drawer: dark luxurious shell with ivory product rows and strong readable text

## Motion
- Subtle transitions only: `duration-300` or `duration-500`
- Use opacity, translate, and soft shadow changes
- Avoid bouncy or discount-style animations

## Do
- Maintain strong readability on every surface
- Keep imagery warm and elevated
- Use restrained accent color and clean UI chrome
- Prioritize mobile polish while preserving rich desktop layout

## Don’t
- Do not use bright promotional reds or discount badges
- Do not crowd sections or collapse desktop into a narrow single-column feel
- Do not mix multiple visual directions
- Do not leave text color implicit on custom backgrounds
