# SSourcing China - Design System & Visual Guidelines

## Brand Overview
SSourcing China is a professional China-based sourcing agent helping overseas B2B buyers find reliable suppliers, verify factories, inspect quality, manage production, and coordinate shipping. The brand must convey trust, professionalism, clarity, and international competence.

## Core Design Principles
- Clean and minimal
- Trustworthy and professional (B2B)
- International and modern
- Practical, no hype or exaggerated claims
- High readability and accessibility

## Color Palette
Primary (Trust & Professionalism):
- Primary Navy: #0F172A (slate-900)
- Primary Blue: #1E40AF (blue-800)
- Accent Blue: #2563EB (blue-600)

Neutrals:
- Dark Text: #0F172A
- Body Text: #334155 (slate-700)
- Muted Text: #64748B (slate-500)
- Light Background: #F8FAFC (slate-50)
- Card/Section Background: #FFFFFF
- Border: #E2E8F0 (slate-200)

Accent / CTAs:
- CTA Green: #15803D (green-700) - for primary actions
- CTA Hover: #166534 (green-800)
- Warning/Highlight: #B45309 (amber-700) - used sparingly

## Typography
- Font Family: Inter (system-ui fallback)
- Headings: 600-700 weight, tight tracking
- Body: 400-500 weight, 1.6 line-height
- Base size: 16px

Heading Scale:
- H1: 42px / 48px (desktop), 32px (mobile)
- H2: 30px / 36px
- H3: 22px / 26px
- H4: 18px

## Spacing & Layout
- Consistent 8px grid
- Max content width: 1280px (container)
- Section padding: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Card padding: 32px
- Gaps: 24px, 32px, 48px

## Components

### Buttons
- Primary CTA: bg-green-700 text-white px-8 py-3 rounded-lg font-medium hover:bg-green-800 transition
- Secondary: border border-slate-300 text-slate-700 px-6 py-2.5 rounded-lg hover:bg-slate-50
- Ghost: text-slate-600 hover:text-slate-900

### Cards
- White background, subtle border (slate-200), rounded-xl
- Shadow: subtle 0 1px 3px rgba(15,23,42,0.06)
- Hover lift for interactive cards

### Navigation
- Clean top nav with logo left, links center, CTA right
- Mobile: hamburger menu
- Active state: underline or bold navy

### Forms
- Clean inputs with slate-200 borders
- Focus: blue-600 ring
- Labels above fields
- Submit button full-width on mobile

## Imagery
- Realistic factory, QC inspection, shipping container, warehouse visuals
- Use data-strk-img system for stock images
- Professional, documentary-style photography
- Avoid stock clichés; prefer authentic industrial scenes

## Tone & Copy
- Professional, clear, practical
- No exaggerated claims ("best", "guaranteed", "100%")
- Focus on process, reliability, and risk reduction
- Use specific numbers and real-sounding case details

## Responsive
- Mobile-first
- Breakpoints: 640px, 768px, 1024px, 1280px
- Navigation collapses below 768px

## Page Structure (Consistent)
1. Top Navigation (sticky)
2. Main Content
3. Footer with links and contact

## Trust Elements
- Client logos (text or simple marks)
- Certifications / years in business
- "No upfront payment until supplier confirmed" style messaging
- Clear process steps

## Inquiry Form
- Appears on Home, Contact, and as floating/secondary CTA
- Fields: Name, Company, Email, Phone (optional), Product Category, Target Quantity, Timeline, Message
- Success state with thank you message
- Form submission simulated (console + UI success)

## SEO
- Title: China Sourcing Agent | Supplier Verification, QC & Shipping | SSourcing China
- Meta description focused on qualified sourcing inquiries
