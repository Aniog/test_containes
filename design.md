# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guide

## Brand Identity
- Brand: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- Industry: Industrial machinery (metal folding equipment)
- Style: Elegant, sophisticated, user-friendly, professional

## Color Palette
- Primary (Dark): `#1a2332` - Deep navy/charcoal for headers, footers, primary buttons
- Primary Light: `#2a3a52` - Lighter navy for hover states
- Accent (Gold): `#c9a84c` - Warm metallic gold for accents, highlights, CTAs
- Accent Light: `#dfc06a` - Lighter gold for hover states
- Background: `#f5f6fa` - Clean light gray-white
- Background Alt: `#eef0f5` - Slightly darker gray for alternating sections
- Card Background: `#ffffff` - White for cards
- Text Primary: `#1a2332` - Dark navy for headings
- Text Secondary: `#4a5568` - Slate for body text
- Text Muted: `#8b95a5` - Muted gray for subtle text
- Border: `#e2e6ef` - Light border color
- Success: `#2d8a4e` - Green for positive indicators
- White: `#ffffff`

## Typography
- Headings: Inter (weights 600, 700, 800)
- Body: Inter (weights 300, 400, 500)
- Scale: h1 ~3rem, h2 ~2.25rem, h3 ~1.5rem, body ~1rem, small ~0.875rem

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Content max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto

## Component Styles

### Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#c9a84c] text-white, hover:bg-[#dfc06a], rounded-sm, font-medium
- Secondary: bg-[#1a2332] text-white, hover:bg-[#2a3a52], rounded-sm, font-medium
- Outline: border border-[#c9a84c] text-[#c9a84c], hover:bg-[#c9a84c] hover:text-white, rounded-sm

### Cards
- White background, subtle shadow (shadow-sm), hover:shadow-md
- Border: border border-[#e2e6ef]
- Rounded-sm
- Transition on hover

### Navigation
- Sticky top, transparent to solid on scroll
- White background after scroll with shadow

### Hero
- Full-width, dark background (#1a2332) with geometric pattern overlay
- Large heading in white, subtitle in light gray, gold accent CTA

## Do's
- Use ample whitespace
- Use consistent gold accents
- Keep card designs clean and minimal
- Use high-quality product imagery
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Professional, confident tone

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright, flashy colors
- Don't overcrowd sections
- Don't use heavy textures
- Don't use informal/casual language
- Don't mix different border radius styles
