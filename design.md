# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity

### Mood
- Quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry aesthetic
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Charcoal: `#1A1A1A` (main background, text)
- Warm Cream: `#FAF7F2` (light background sections)
- Rich Black: `#0D0D0D` (dark sections)

**Accent Colors:**
- Warm Gold: `#C9A962` (primary accent, CTAs, highlights)
- Soft Gold: `#E8DCC4` (secondary accent, borders)
- Muted Bronze: `#8B7355` (subtle accents)

**Neutral Colors:**
- Warm Gray: `#6B6560` (secondary text)
- Light Taupe: `#E5E0D8` (dividers, subtle backgrounds)

### Typography
**Headings & Product Names:**
- Font: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- Weight: 500, 600
- Letter-spacing: 0.05em to 0.15em for uppercase
- Product names: UPPERCASE with 0.1em letter-spacing

**Body & UI:**
- Font: Manrope (sans-serif)
- Weight: 400, 500, 600
- Clean, readable, modern

### Spacing System
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: 80px-120px desktop, 40px-60px mobile
- Component spacing: 24px-48px
- Grid gaps: 24px-32px

### Visual Elements
- Thin hairline dividers: 1px solid #E5E0D8
- Subtle shadows: `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`
- Soft hover transitions: 300ms ease
- Premium button styles (solid gold, outlined gold)

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px

## Component Guidelines

### Buttons
- Primary: Gold background (#C9A962), dark text, rounded-sm
- Secondary: Transparent with gold border, gold text
- Hover: Slight brightness increase, subtle scale

### Cards
- Clean white/cream backgrounds
- Subtle shadow on hover
- Image zoom on hover for products

### Dividers
- Thin, warm-toned hairline dividers
- Used between sections and content blocks

### Images
- Editorial, warm-lit photography style
- Gold jewelry on dark or neutral backgrounds
- Generous whitespace around images
