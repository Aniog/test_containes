# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Design Guide

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Primary background**: `velmora-off-white` (#FAF8F5) — warm, soft cream
- **Secondary background**: `velmora-cream` (#F5F0EB) — slightly warmer for cards/sections
- **Accent background**: `velmora-charcoal` (#2C2C2C) — deep charcoal for hero overlays, footer
- **Gold accent**: `velmora-gold` (#C9A96E) — warm metallic gold for buttons, highlights, CTAs
- **Gold hover**: `velmora-gold-dark` (#A8854A) — darker gold for hover states
- **Text primary**: `velmora-charcoal` (#2C2C2C) — deep charcoal for body text
- **Text secondary**: `velmora-muted` (#8A8278) — warm muted for descriptions, labels
- **Text light**: `velmora-muted-light` (#B0A89E) — light muted for subtle labels
- **Divider**: `velmora-sand` (#D4C5B5) — warm sand for hairline dividers
- **White**: `velmora-white` (#FFFFFF) — pure white for contrast areas

## Typography
- **Headings & product names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weights 400-600
- **Body & UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weights 300-500
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em (tracking-product)
- **Nav links**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.08em (tracking-nav)
- **Hero headline**: serif, ~48-64px, weight 400-500
- **Section headings**: serif, ~32-40px, weight 500
- **Body text**: sans, ~14-16px, weight 400, line-height 1.6

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: 80-120px vertical on desktop, 48-64px on mobile
- Card padding: 24-32px
- Grid gaps: 24-32px

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers (1px, velmora-sand color)
- No heavy borders or box shadows
- Subtle soft shadows on cards: `shadow-sm` or custom `0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`
- Buttons: thin 1px border or solid accent fill

## Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: solid velmora-gold background, velmora-white text, rounded-none or slight rounded, padding 12-16px vertical, 32-48px horizontal, font-sans uppercase tracking-nav
- **Secondary**: transparent bg, velmora-charcoal text, 1px velmora-sand border
- **Hover**: velmora-gold-dark for primary, slight bg tint for secondary
- **Pill buttons** (variant selector): small rounded-full, border, selected = velmora-gold bg

## Images
- Large editorial imagery, warm-lit
- Product cards: square or 4:3 ratio
- Hero: full-bleed, 16:9 or wider
- UGC reel: 9:16 vertical cards
- Hover on product cards: reveal second image with smooth crossfade

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth transitions (200-300ms ease)
- Fade-in on scroll for sections
- Hover: gentle opacity/scale transitions
- Cart drawer: slide-in from right
- No jarring or flashy animations

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use warm gold as the single accent color
- Ensure strong text contrast on all backgrounds
- Use hairline dividers between sections

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/saturated colors
- Don't use heavy shadows or thick borders
- Don't use generic e-commerce styling
- Don't mix too many font weights
- Don't use lowercase product names — always uppercase with wide tracking
