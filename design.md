# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Restrained, never loud or discount-looking.

## Color Palette (committed direction: warm espresso + metallic gold + cream)
- Base background: `cream` `#f7f3ee` (warm editorial neutral)
- Deep base / dark sections: `espresso-800` `#1c1714` (warm near-black)
- Text on cream: `ink` `#2a2420`
- Text on espresso: `cream` `#f7f3ee`
- Accent (metallic gold): `gold` `#b08d57`, hover `gold-dark` `#8a6d3f`, soft `gold-soft` `#d9c4a3`
- Hairline dividers: `espresso-200/40`

## Typography
- Headings & product names: **Cormorant Garamond** (serif), weight 500
- Body & UI: **Inter** (sans), weight 400/500
- Product names: UPPERCASE, `tracking-widest2` (0.25em)
- Eyebrow labels: UPPERCASE, `text-xs`, `tracking-widest2`, gold or espresso

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: `max-w-editorial` (1400px), `.container-editorial`
- Generous whitespace; section padding `py-20 md:py-28`
- Thin hairline dividers between sections

## Components
- Buttons: solid espresso (`btn-accent`) or outlined (`btn-outline`); uppercase, wide tracking, subtle hover
- Cards: soft shadow on hover, image swap on hover, quick add-to-cart reveal
- Pills (variant selector): rounded-full, border, active = espresso bg

## Do's
- Use serif for headlines and product names
- Keep accent gold restrained — small touches, not large fills
- Ensure strong contrast (cream/espresso pairs)

## Don'ts
- No bright/discount reds, no generic e-commerce blues
- No loud gradients
- No low-contrast text on cream or espresso
