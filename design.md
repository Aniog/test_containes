# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Primary background**: `#F5F0EB` (velmora-cream) — warm off-white, the main page background
- **Secondary background**: `#E8DDD3` (velmora-warm) — slightly warmer, used for cards, sections
- **Accent surface**: `#D4C5B5` (velmora-sand) — borders, dividers, subtle backgrounds
- **Primary accent**: `#C9A96E` (velmora-gold) — warm metallic gold, CTAs, highlights, links
- **Gold hover**: `#D4B87A` (velmora-gold-light) — lighter gold for hover states
- **Gold pressed**: `#A88B4A` (velmora-gold-dark) — darker gold for active states
- **Primary text**: `#2C2C2C` (velmora-charcoal) — main body text, headings
- **Secondary text**: `#8A8278` (velmora-muted) — descriptions, captions, secondary info
- **Light muted**: `#A69E94` (velmora-muted-light) — placeholder text, disabled states
- **Dark surface**: `#1A1A1A` (velmora-ink) — hero overlays, footer background

## Typography
- **Headings & product names**: `Cormorant Garamond` (serif), weights 400-600
- **Body & UI**: `Inter` (sans-serif), weights 400-500
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE with `tracking-product` (0.15em) or `tracking-wide-product` (0.2em)
- **Section headings**: serif, large, generous spacing
- **Body text**: sans-serif, 14-16px, relaxed line-height

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections separated by 80-120px vertical padding
- Hairline dividers: 1px borders in velmora-sand color
- Cards: rounded corners (rounded-sm or rounded), subtle shadows
- Max content width: ~1200px, centered
- Mobile: stacked layouts, reduced padding (40-60px vertical)

## Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: solid velmora-gold background, white text, rounded, hover to gold-light
- **Secondary**: outlined with velmora-gold border, gold text, hover fills gold
- **Pill buttons** (variant selector): small, rounded-full, velmora-warm bg, active state gold border
- All buttons: smooth 200ms transition, no harsh shadows

## Images
- Editorial, warm-lit, gold jewelry on neutral/dark backgrounds
- Product cards: square or 4:3 ratio, hover reveals second image
- Hero: full-bleed, 16:9 or wider
- UGC: 9:16 vertical, Instagram Reels style

## Interactions
- Subtle hover transitions (200-300ms ease)
- Soft shadows, no hard edges
- Cart drawer slides in from right
- Nav: transparent over hero, solid cream on scroll
- Product card: image crossfade on hover, quick-add button appears

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep accent color restrained — gold for CTAs and highlights only
- Ensure strong contrast: charcoal text on cream, white text on ink/gold
- Use thin hairline dividers between sections

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/saturated colors
- Don't use generic e-commerce blue/purple
- Don't crowd elements — always leave breathing room
- Don't use sans-serif for headings
- Don't use heavy shadows or thick borders
- Don't use lowercase for product names — always uppercase with wide tracking
