# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

## Color Palette
- Background (light): `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory)
- Background (dark): `#1C1917` (rich charcoal)
- Text primary: `#1A1A1A` (deep charcoal)
- Text secondary: `#78716C` (warm stone)
- Text on dark: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory)
- Accent (gold): `#C9A96E` (warm metallic gold)
- Accent hover: `#B8944F` (deeper gold)
- Border: `#E7E5E4` (soft stone)
- Border dark: `#44403C` (warm dark stone)
- Card background: `#FFFFFF` (pure white)
- Muted: `#A8A29E` (stone)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif)
  - Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
  - Hero headlines: large, elegant serif
- Body/UI: Inter (clean sans-serif)
  - Body text: normal weight, comfortable line-height
  - UI elements: medium weight

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8

## Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-stone-200

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold background, white text, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Secondary: outlined with gold border, transparent background
- Hover: subtle opacity change or deeper gold

## Shadows
- Soft, subtle shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md
- Card hover: shadow-lg transition

## Transitions
- Smooth, subtle: transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out
- Hover effects: scale, opacity, translate

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use gold accents sparingly
- Maintain strong contrast for accessibility
- Use uppercase for product names with wide letter-spacing

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount-looking elements (no red sale badges, no strikethrough prices)
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No heavy shadows or borders
- No cluttered layouts
