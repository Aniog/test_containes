# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand
Quiet luxury demi-fine jewelry. Premium-but-accessible ($30–$120). Warm, editorial, refined.

## Color Palette
- **Base**: velvet-50 (#faf8f5) — warm off-white cream for backgrounds
- **Base Alt**: velvet-100 (#f3efe8) — slightly darker cream for alternating sections
- **Body Text**: velvet-900 (#3d3228) — deep warm brown-black
- **Muted Text**: velvet-600 (#9b8262) — warm taupe for secondary text
- **Accent**: warm-600 (#cd883c) — burnished gold for CTAs and highlights
- **Accent Hover**: warm-700 (#ab6b32) — deeper gold on hover
- **Dark Base**: velvet-950 (#241d17) — deep espresso for footer, dark sections
- **Border / Divider**: velvet-200 (#e6dfd2) — thin hairline rules

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names / Logo**: Cormorant Garamond (serif, elegant, editorial)
  - Product names: UPPERCASE, tracking-widest (0.25em), font-medium
  - Section headings: text-4xl md:text-5xl, normal case or small caps feel
- **Body / UI / Buttons**: Manrope (sans-serif, clean, modern)
  - Body: text-sm md:text-base, leading-relaxed, velvet-900
  - Small labels: text-xs tracking-widest uppercase

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: sections use py-20 md:py-28
- Card grids: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Content max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 md:px-8

## Components
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary: bg-warm-600 hover:bg-warm-700 text-white, tracking-widest uppercase text-xs px-10 py-3.5, rounded-none (sharp, architectural feel)
  - Secondary: border-2 border-warm-600 text-warm-600 hover:bg-warm-600 hover:text-white, same sizing
  - Ghost: text-velvet-600 hover:text-velvet-900, underline-offset-4
- **Cards**: bg-white, subtle shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition, no rounded corners or very subtle (rounded-sm)
- **Dividers**: 1px hairline, velvet-200
- **Inputs**: border-velvet-200 focus:border-warm-500 focus:ring-warm-500/20, rounded-none

## Imagery
- Warm-lit gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Editorial, close-up, model-worn
- Dark, moody backgrounds for hero sections
- Light/cream backgrounds for product shots

## Do's & Don'ts
- DO use generous whitespace and breathing room
- DO use hairline dividers for separation (not thick borders)
- DO use subtle hover transitions (200–300ms ease)
- DO maintain strong contrast for text (velvet-900 on cream backgrounds)
- DON'T use rounded buttons or cards (sharp/precision feel)
- DON'T use bright/saturated colors beyond the warm gold accent
- DON'T use drop shadows heavier than shadow-sm
- DON'T use emoji or casual language
