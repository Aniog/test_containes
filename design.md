# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- Direction: Deep warm base with gold accents — refined, confident, not loud.

## Color Palette
- Base background: velvet-950 (#1A1715) — deep warm near-black
- Surface: velvet-900 (#3D2F15) — warm dark brown
- Surface raised: velvet-800 (#5C4720) — mid brown
- Primary accent: gold-500 (#C4A265) / gold-600 (#B89347)
- Text primary: warm-50 (#FAF8F6) — off-white cream
- Text secondary: warm-400 (#B8AD9D) — muted warm taupe
- Text muted: warm-600 (#6E6860)
- Text on light: velvet-950 — for light surface backgrounds
- Dividers: warm-800 at 30% opacity
- Buttons: gold-500/600 as solid or outlined

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) — product names uppercase with wide letter-spacing
- Body: Inter (sans-serif)
- Sizes: Large editorial headings, generous line-height

## Do's
- Use dark warm backgrounds (velvet-950/900) as primary surfaces
- Use gold accent sparingly for CTAs, links, highlights
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use hairline dividers
- Subtle hover transitions (300ms ease)
- Soft shadows in warm tones

## Don'ts
- No bright white backgrounds
- No loud colors or harsh contrasts
- No discount badges or sale callouts
- No generic e-commerce patterns
