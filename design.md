# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Base background: #F5F2ED (warm ivory/cream)
- Card/surface: #FFFFFF
- Text primary: #1A1A1A (near-black)
- Text secondary: #6B6258 (warm taupe)
- Text muted: #9C9286 (lighter taupe)
- Accent / CTAs: #B8864E (warm gold/metallic)
- Accent hover: #A0763E (deeper gold)
- Border / divider: #E5DED5 (warm light beige)
- Hero/tag background: #2C2418 (deep espresso)
- Newsletter bg: #3C2E22 (warm dark brown)
- Star rating: #D4A853 (amber gold)

## Typography
- Headings: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif (light 300, regular 400, medium 500, semibold 600, bold 700)
- Body UI: 'Inter', sans-serif (light 300, regular 400, medium 500, semibold 600)
- Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.08em-0.12em, Cormorant Garamond semibold

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-32
- Page max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 md:px-8
- Card gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Whitespace: generous, breathe between sections

## Borders
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E5DED5] or border-b
- Thin 1px rule
- No heavy borders anywhere

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#B8864E] text-white, hover:bg-[#A0763E], px-8 py-3, uppercase tracking-widest text-xs font-medium
- Outline: border border-[#B8864E] text-[#B8864E] hover:bg-[#B8864E] hover:text-white, px-8 py-3, uppercase tracking-widest text-xs font-medium
- None: no heavy shadows, no rounded-2xl, just subtle

## Cards & Products
- Product card: bg-white, subtle shadow-sm, border border-[#E5DED5]/50
- No heavy borders, no sharp corners
- Image aspect: 4:5 for product images
- Hover: subtle scale-102, image swap

## Animations
- Smooth transitions: duration-300 ease-out
- Hover: translate-y-(-2px), subtle shadow increase
- Page load: fade-in-up
- Cart drawer: slide from right

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use hairline thin dividers
- Keep it editorial and clean
- Use uppercase + wide letter-spacing for product names
- Gold accent sparingly but confidently

## Don'ts
- No bright red, no bright blue, no neon
- No heavy borders
- No discount-store look
- No crowded layouts
- No rounded corners on buttons (just slight, e.g. rounded-sm)
