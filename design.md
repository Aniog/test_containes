# Design System — Camera Site

## Visual Style
Dark, cinematic aesthetic inspired by professional photography. Clean, minimal layout with generous whitespace.

## Colors
- Background (primary): `bg-zinc-950` (#09090b)
- Background (secondary): `bg-zinc-900` (#18181b)
- Background (card): `bg-zinc-800` (#27272a)
- Accent: `bg-amber-400` / `text-amber-400` (#fbbf24) — used for highlights, CTAs, icons
- Text primary: `text-white`
- Text muted: `text-zinc-400`
- Border: `border-zinc-700`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: `font-bold` or `font-semibold`, tight tracking (`tracking-tight`)
- Hero headline: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold`
- Section title: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- Body: `text-base text-zinc-400`
- Labels/caps: `text-xs uppercase tracking-widest text-amber-400`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-6`
- Card padding: `p-6` or `p-8`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl border border-zinc-700`
- Buttons: `rounded-full`
- Subtle shadow on cards: `shadow-lg`

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: `bg-amber-400 text-zinc-950 font-semibold rounded-full px-8 py-3 hover:bg-amber-300`
- Secondary: `border border-zinc-600 text-white rounded-full px-8 py-3 hover:border-amber-400`

## Do's
- Use amber accent sparingly for emphasis
- Keep sections well-separated with dark backgrounds
- Use stock images for hero and gallery
- Maintain high contrast: white/amber text on dark backgrounds

## Don'ts
- No light backgrounds
- No colorful gradients
- No cluttered layouts
