# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry for women 25–45. Premium-but-accessible ($30–$120).

## Color Palette
- **Cream** (#FAF7F2): Primary background, warm off-white
- **Sand** (#F0EBE3): Secondary background, cards
- **Stone** (#E6DFD3): Dividers, subtle borders
- **Taupe** (#B8A99A): Secondary text, muted UI
- **Bronze** (#A68A6B): Accent text, hover states
- **Gold** (#C9A96E): Primary accent, buttons, CTAs
- **Gold Deep** (#B8944E): Button hover, active states
- **Charcoal** (#2C2C2C): Body text
- **Ink** (#1A1A1A): Headings, primary text
- **Rose** (#D4A9A5): Subtle accent, testimonial stars

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weight 400–600
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weight 300–600
- Product names: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- Section headings: serif, 3xl–5xl, text-velmora-ink

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: section padding py-20 md:py-28
- Card gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Content max-width: 1440px

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold (btn-primary) or outlined gold (btn-outline)
- Dividers: hairline gradient (stone → transparent)
- Cards: bg-white/60 backdrop-blur, subtle shadow, rounded-none
- Hover: subtle scale (1.02), soft shadow, smooth 300ms transitions

## Do's
- Use velmora color tokens exclusively
- Keep backgrounds warm and light
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use uppercase + wide tracking for product names

## Don'ts
- No dark backgrounds (except footer)
- No bright/saturated colors
- No rounded buttons (keep them crisp)
- No discount badges or sale language
- No generic e-commerce patterns
