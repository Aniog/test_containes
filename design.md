# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory/cream)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (pure white for cards)
- Text primary: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)
- Text secondary: `#6B6560` (warm taupe)
- Text muted: `#9B9590` (soft warm gray)
- Accent / CTA: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod — warm gold)
- Accent hover: `#9A7209` (deeper gold)
- Accent light: `#F5E6C8` (pale gold wash)
- Border / divider: `#E8E2DA` (warm light taupe)
- Dark section bg: `#1A1A1A` (near-black for newsletter/footer)
- Dark text: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory on dark)

## Typography
- Headings / Product names: Cormorant Garamond (serif), elegant, uppercase with wide letter-spacing
- Body / UI: Inter (sans-serif), clean, readable
- Product names: UPPERCASE, tracking-widest (letter-spacing: 0.15em)
- Hero headline: Cormorant Garamond, italic or regular, large (text-5xl to text-7xl)

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: py-16 to py-24 for sections
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E8E2DA]
- Card shadows: shadow-sm, hover:shadow-md with transition

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: bg-[#B8860B] text-white hover:bg-[#9A7209] rounded-none (sharp edges for luxury feel)
- Secondary/Outlined: border border-[#B8860B] text-[#B8860B] hover:bg-[#B8860B] hover:text-white
- Full-width on product pages, medium on nav

## Components
- Cards: white bg, subtle shadow, hover lift effect
- Accordions: thin border, smooth expand/collapse
- Pills/Tags: rounded-full, small text, warm border
- Inputs: border border-[#E8E2DA], focus:border-[#B8860B], no rounded corners or minimal

## Animations
- Hover transitions: transition-all duration-300 ease-out
- Image hover: scale-105 on overflow-hidden containers
- Fade-in on scroll (optional)
- Cart drawer: slide-in from right

## Responsive
- Mobile-first approach
- Grid: 1 col mobile, 2 col tablet, 3-4 col desktop
- Nav: hamburger on mobile, full links on desktop
- Hero: full-bleed on all sizes
