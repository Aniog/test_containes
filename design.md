# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
ARTITECT MACHINERY is a premium industrial machinery brand specializing in metal folding machines. The design should convey precision, power, and elegance — combining industrial strength with refined sophistication.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Navy Dark**: `#0B1426` — Main background, hero sections
- **Navy Medium**: `#132042` — Card backgrounds, secondary sections
- **Navy Light**: `#1C2D5A` — Hover states, accents

### Accent Colors
- **Gold**: `#C8A45C` — Primary accent, CTAs, highlights, borders
- **Gold Light**: `#D4B876` — Hover states on gold elements
- **Gold Dark**: `#A88940` — Active states on gold elements

### Neutral Colors
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` — Primary text on dark backgrounds
- **Gray Light**: `#94A3B8` — Secondary text, captions
- **Gray Medium**: `#64748B` — Muted text, placeholders
- **Slate**: `#E2E8F0` — Light section backgrounds
- **Off-White**: `#F8FAFC` — Alternate light backgrounds

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: Font-weight 700-800, letter-spacing -0.02em
- **Body**: Font-weight 400, line-height 1.6
- **Captions/Labels**: Font-weight 500, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.05em

### Scale
- Hero Title: text-5xl md:text-7xl
- Section Title: text-3xl md:text-5xl
- Subsection Title: text-xl md:text-2xl
- Body: text-base md:text-lg
- Caption: text-sm

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-6
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Element gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Card borders: border border-navy-light/30 or border-gold/20
- Subtle shadows: shadow-lg shadow-black/20
- Gold accent lines: h-1 w-16 bg-gold (section dividers)
- Border radius: rounded-lg for cards, rounded-full for buttons

## Visual Style
- Dark, premium aesthetic with navy backgrounds
- Gold accents for elegance and luxury feel
- Clean geometric layouts reflecting precision engineering
- Generous whitespace for readability
- Subtle gradients and overlays for depth
- Industrial imagery with dark overlays

## Do's
- Use gold sparingly for maximum impact
- Maintain high contrast between text and backgrounds
- Use clean, geometric layouts
- Keep navigation simple and intuitive
- Use consistent spacing throughout

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much text
- Don't use more than 2 font weights in a single component
- Don't use low-contrast text on dark backgrounds
- Don't mix warm and cool tones outside the defined palette
