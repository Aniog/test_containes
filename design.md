# Velmora Fine Jewelry Visual System

Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial direction: soft ivory surfaces, deep espresso text, warm champagne metallic accents, and generous whitespace. The mood should feel premium, calm, feminine, and direct-to-consumer without discount styling.

## Color palette
- `velmora-ivory` (`#F8F3EA`): primary page background, warm and flattering to gold jewelry.
- `velmora-cream` (`#FFFBF4`): cards, inputs, drawer surfaces.
- `velmora-linen` (`#E9DDCB`): dividers, soft panels, borders.
- `velmora-espresso` (`#241914`): main text and dark surfaces.
- `velmora-mocha` (`#5D463A`): secondary text.
- `velmora-champagne` (`#C8A46D`): primary accent for buttons, icons, stars, premium highlights.
- `velmora-bronze` (`#8A633E`): hover/pressed accent state.
- `velmora-rose` (`#D9B8A6`): restrained warm secondary accent.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Playfair Display, elegant serif.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names should be uppercase with wide letter spacing (`uppercase tracking-[0.22em]` or a semantic utility).
- Use large editorial headings with refined line-height and avoid overly bold weights.

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first, with generous section padding (`py-16 md:py-24`) and centered content width (`max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 md:px-8`).
- Use thin hairline dividers in `velmora-linen`.
- Prefer asymmetric editorial layouts on desktop and simple stacked layouts on mobile.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, understated, either solid champagne with espresso text or transparent outlined espresso/champagne. Rounded-full, letter-spaced small caps, smooth hover transitions.
- Cards: warm cream or transparent, soft shadow only on hover, thin borders, never heavy boxes.
- Product imagery: warm gold jewelry on neutral or deep editorial backgrounds. Use Strikingly stock image tags, not hardcoded image URLs.
- Cart drawer: cream surface with espresso text, clear readable line items, premium accent checkout button.

## Do's
- Keep contrast strong and all text explicitly readable.
- Use whitespace, hairlines, and subtle animation to create luxury.
- Keep accent color restrained and consistent.

## Don'ts
- Do not use loud sale badges, neon colors, heavy discount language, or generic marketplace styling.
- Do not use low-contrast beige text on beige backgrounds.
- Do not mix multiple accent directions or arbitrary one-off colors.
