# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry aesthetic.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory) - main page background
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white) - cards, overlays
- **Primary Text**: `#1A1A1A` (near black) - headings, body
- **Secondary Text**: `#6B6560` (warm taupe) - muted text, captions
- **Accent**: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod) - CTAs, links, highlights
- **Accent Hover**: `#996F0A` (darker gold)
- **Border**: `#E8E2DA` (warm light beige) - dividers, borders
- **Dark**: `#2C2825` (deep warm brown) - footer, dark sections
- **Star**: `#D4A853` (gold) - ratings

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable
- **Product Names**: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- **Font weights**: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 to py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl
- Card gaps: gap-6 to gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border border-[#E8E2DA]
- Subtle rounded corners: rounded-sm (2px) for cards, rounded-full for pills

## Shadows
- Soft, warm shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md
- Hover elevation: shadow-lg with transition

## Buttons
- Primary: solid accent background, white text, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Secondary: outlined accent border, accent text, transparent bg
- Full-width on product pages
- Hover: slight darkening, subtle scale

## Animations
- Smooth transitions: transition-all duration-300
- Hover: slight scale (scale-[1.02]), shadow increase
- Fade-in on scroll (optional)
- Cart drawer: slide-in from right

## Image Style
- Warm-lit gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Editorial, lifestyle photography feel
- 9:16 vertical for UGC/reels
- 4:3 or 1:1 for product cards
