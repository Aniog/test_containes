# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif), elegant, refined
- Body: Inter (sans-serif), clean, legible
- Product names: UPPERCASE, tracking-wide (letter-spacing: 0.12em)
- Nav logo: Cormorant Garamond, uppercase, tracking-widest

## Color Palette
- Base background: #faf8f6 (velvet-50) — warm off-white
- Dark text: #1c1c20 (ink-800) — near-black
- Accent / CTAs: #c98a2e (gold-500) — warm gold
- Card/panel backgrounds: #ffffff
- Dividers: #e0d8cc (velvet-200) — thin hairline
- Hero overlay: #1a1510 (velvet-950) with 50% opacity
- Footer: #2c241b (velvet-900) — deep warm brown
- Muted text: #6b6b75 (ink-400)

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold-500 background, white text, px-8 py-3, rounded-none, hover:bg-gold-600 transition
- Outline: border-2 border-ink-800, text-ink-800, bg-transparent, hover:bg-ink-800 hover:text-white
- Pill variant selector: border border-velvet-300, px-5 py-2, rounded-full, selected state = ink-800 bg

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Product grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Generous whitespace between sections

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-velvet-200
- Cards: no border, subtle shadow, hover:shadow-md transition
- Images: full-bleed, object-cover

## Transitions & Animation
- All interactive: transition-all duration-300
- Nav background: transition-colors duration-300
- Product card hover: translate-y-[-2px], shadow increase
- Cart drawer: slide from right, duration-300

## Imagery
- Editorial style, warm lighting, gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Product images: 1:1 ratio for grid, 4:3 for detail page
- Hero: full-bleed 16:9 or 3:2
- UGC: 9:16 vertical cards
- Category tiles: 3:4 ratio, elegant
