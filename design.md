# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial sheet metal folding machines
- **Tone**: Elegant, professional, trustworthy, yet user-friendly

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: #1a1a2e (Deep Navy) - conveys professionalism and reliability
- **Secondary**: #16213e (Dark Blue) - adds depth and sophistication
- **Accent**: #e94560 (Coral Red) - for CTAs and highlights, adds energy
- **Light Background**: #f8f9fa (Off-white) - clean, modern feel
- **Text Primary**: #1a1a2e (Deep Navy)
- **Text Secondary**: #6c757d (Muted Gray)
- **White**: #ffffff

## Typography
- **Headings**: "Playfair Display" - elegant serif for brand sophistication
- **Body**: "Inter" - clean, readable sans-serif for user-friendliness
- **Font Sizes**:
  - Hero Title: 56px (desktop), 36px (mobile)
  - Section Titles: 42px (desktop), 28px (mobile)
  - Subheadings: 24px
  - Body: 16px
  - Small: 14px

## Layout & Spacing
- **Max Content Width**: 1200px
- **Section Padding**: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Grid Gap**: 32px
- **Card Padding**: 24px

## Visual Style
- Clean, minimalist design with generous white space
- Subtle shadows for depth: `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- Rounded corners: 8px for cards, 4px for buttons
- Smooth transitions: 0.3s ease for all interactive elements

## Components

### Header/Navigation
- Fixed position, white background with subtle shadow on scroll
- Logo on left, navigation links center, CTA button right
- Mobile: hamburger menu

### Hero Section
- Full-width with subtle gradient overlay
- Large headline with brand name emphasis
- Subheadline describing the products
- Two CTA buttons: "View Products" (primary), "Contact Us" (secondary)
- Background: subtle industrial pattern or gradient

### Products Section
- Section title with accent underline
- Grid of product cards (3 columns desktop, 1 mobile)
- Each card: image placeholder, product name, brief description, "Learn More" link

### Features Section
- Alternating layout (image + text)
- Key benefits: Precision, Durability, Efficiency, Support
- Icon accents for each feature

### About Section
- Company story with elegant typography
- Stats/metrics (years experience, machines sold, etc.)

### Contact Section
- Clean form layout
- Fields: Name, Email, Phone, Message
- Submit button with accent color

### Footer
- Dark background (#1a1a2e)
- Logo, quick links, contact info
- Copyright notice

## Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use generous white space, clean typography, subtle animations
- DON'T: Use too many colors, cluttered layouts, aggressive animations
- DO: Make CTAs prominent but not overwhelming
- DON'T: Use bright/neon colors that clash with industrial theme
