# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Base background: `velvet-50` (#FAF8F5) — warm cream
- Deep base: `velvet-950` (#1C1A18) — near-black, warm
- Primary accent: `gold` (#C4A265) — warm gold
- Light gold: `gold-light` (#E8D5B7)
- Mid tones: `velvet-700` (#7A6E60), `velvet-800` (#5C4F45)
- Neutral: `velvet-100` (#F5F2ED), `velvet-200` (#EBE5DA)

## Typography
- Headings & product names: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- Product names: UPPERCASE with tracking-widest
- Body & UI: Inter (sans-serif)
- Button text: UPPERCASE, tracking-wider, Inter, font-medium

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace, section padding: py-20 to py-24
- Card gaps: 6-8 (tailwind)
- Content max-width: max-w-7xl centered

## Visual Elements
- Thin hairline dividers (border-velvet-950/10)
- Subtle hover transitions (300ms)
- Soft shadows: shadow-sm or custom
- Button styles: solid dark (btn-primary) or outlined (btn-outline)
- Image treatment: warm, editorial

## Do's
- Use serif for headings and product names
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use warm neutral backgrounds
- Subtle gold accents sparingly
- Uppercase product names with wide letter-spacing
- Mobile-first responsive design

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or neon
- No discount badges or sale tags
- No generic blue links
- No heavy borders
- No crowded layouts
- No emoji
