# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry for women 25–45. No loud discounts, no generic big-box feel.

## Color Palette
Commit to one warm-cream editorial direction:

- **Background (page):** `#f6f3ef` — warm cream
- **Foreground (body text):** `#2c211b` — espresso / dark brown
- **Muted / secondary text:** `#7a6b60` — warm taupe
- **Accent / CTA:** `#c9a86c` — warm gold
- **Accent hover:** `#b89658` — deeper gold
- **Surface / card:** `#ffffff` or `#faf8f5`
- **Hairline dividers:** `#e0d8cf`, `#d6cdc2`
- **Dark sections (hero, newsletter, footer):** `#2c211b` background with `#f6f3ef` text

## Typography
- **Headings & product names:** Cormorant Garamond, serif. Product names are UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).
- **Body & UI:** Inter, sans-serif.
- Sizes are generous; headings feel editorial, body is clean and readable.

## Components
- Buttons: solid accent (`bg-velmora-gold text-velmora-espresso`) or outlined (`border-espresso text-espresso hover:bg-espresso hover:text-cream`). Premium, rounded-none or subtle rounded.
- Cards: white/cream surfaces, very soft shadow, thin hairline borders.
- Dividers: 1px hairline `border-stone-200`.
- Hover transitions: 300ms ease, image cross-fade, slight lift.

## Imagery
Warm gold jewelry on dark or neutral backgrounds. Large editorial crops. Use the stock-image tagging system with nearby text references.

## Accessibility
Strong contrast on all text. Accent gold on espresso passes contrast; cream on espresso passes.
