# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount, not generic.

## Color Palette
- Background: #FAF8F5 (warm ivory/paper)
- Text primary: #1A1A18 (near-black with warmth)
- Text secondary: #6B655E (warm taupe)
- Accent / Gold: #C9A96E (aged gold — buttons, links, dividers)
- Accent muted: #E8DCC8 (warm sand — newsletter block, highlights)
- Border / Hairline: #E5DDD4 (warm beige)
- Card background: #FFFFFF
- Star / Rating: #C9A96E (same gold)
- Trust bar background: #F3EFE8 (warm very light sand)
- Footer background: #1A1A18 (dark near-black)
- Footer text: #D4CEC4 (warm light taupe)
- Error: #C94A4A

## Typography
- Headings: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif — elegant, light/regular weight
- Body / UI: 'Inter', sans-serif — clean, light weight
- Product names: Inter, uppercase, letter-spacing: 0.12em, 11px
- Price: Cormorant Garamond, italic, 18px

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container max: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Product grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Card padding: p-6

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline borders: border-b border-[#E5DDD4] (thin warm beige)
- Thin horizontal rules for section separation
- No heavy borders or harsh lines

## Shadows
- Soft hover shadows: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow
- Product cards: subtle shadow on hover only

## Buttons
- Primary: solid bg-[#C9A96E] text-white, hover:bg-[#B8944E], transition, uppercase tracking-widest text-xs
- Outline: border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E], hover:bg-[#F5F0E8]

## Animations
- Subtle fade-in on scroll (opacity-0 to opacity-100)
- Hover: scale-[1.02] on product images, smooth opacity transitions
- Nav: background opacity transition on scroll

## Image Style
- Warm, editorial, gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- High contrast, warm lighting
- 9:16 for UGC reel-style, 1:1 for product, 3:2 for hero
