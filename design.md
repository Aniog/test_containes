# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF7F2` (warm cream/ivory) — editorial base
- Text primary: `#1A1A1A` (almost black)
- Text muted: `#6B6B6B` (warm gray)
- Accent gold: `#C9A96E` (warm metallic gold)
- Accent gold hover: `#B8954A` (darker gold)
- Border/divider: `#E8E0D4` (warm beige)
- Card bg: `#FFFFFF`
- Light gold tint: `#F5F0E8` (for newsletter blocks, highlights)
- Star/gold elements: `#D4A84B`

## Typography
- Headings & product names: 'Playfair Display', serif
- Product names: uppercase with `tracking-[0.15em]`
- Body & UI: 'Inter', sans-serif
- Font weights: 300 light, 400 regular, 500 medium, 600 semibold, 700 bold

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Thin hairline dividers (`border-t border-[#E8E0D4]`)
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24

## Visual Elements
- Buttons: solid accent gold (`bg-[#C9A96E]`) with hover state, or outlined with gold border
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- Soft shadows on cards (`shadow-sm hover:shadow-md`)
- Thin borders, refined look
- Hover reveals second image on product cards

## DO's
- Use warm gold accents sparingly for CTAs and key highlights
- Large editorial imagery with generous whitespace
- Product names in uppercase with wide letter-spacing
- Elegant serif for hero headlines and section titles
- Consistent border/divider color throughout

## DON'Ts
- No bright reds, blues, or discount-store colors
- No loud patterns or busy backgrounds
- No generic stock photo feel
- No cluttered layouts
- No low-contrast text
