# MicroCosmos Design System

## Theme
- A dark, scientific, immersive theme focusing on showcasing vibrant microscopic photography.
- **Backgrounds:** Deep dark blues and blacks (e.g., `bg-slate-950`).
- **Foregrounds/Text:** Light grays and whites (e.g., `text-slate-200`, `text-white`).
- **Accents:** Neon cyans, greens, or purples to represent fluorescence in microscopy (e.g., `text-cyan-400`).

## Typography
- Fonts: `Inter` or standard sans-serif.
- Headings: Bold, clean, slightly tracked out (`tracking-tight`).

## Layout
- Use generous padding and margins (`py-20`, `gap-8`) to let the images breathe.
- Image heavy: grid layouts and full-width background spans.

## Components
- **Hero:** Full height `min-h-[80vh]` with a dark overlay over a microscopic background image.
- **Gallery Grid:** Responsive grids (`grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3` or `lg:grid-cols-4`).
- **Cards:** Simple, borderless, just image and caption. Dark slightly transparent backgrounds for text overlays.
