# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background (primary)**: `#FAF8F5` — warm ivory/cream
- **Background (secondary)**: `#F3F0EB` — warm beige for sections
- **Text (primary)**: `#1A1A1A` — near-black charcoal
- **Text (secondary)**: `#6B6560` — warm taupe gray
- **Text (muted)**: `#9B9590` — soft warm gray
- **Accent (gold)**: `#B8956A` — warm metallic gold
- **Accent (gold hover)**: `#A07D55` — deeper gold
- **Accent (dark)**: `#2C2420` — rich warm dark brown
- **Border**: `#E8E4DF` — soft warm border
- **White**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Star rating**: `#C9A96E` — gold stars

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`)
- Headings: `font-light` or `font-normal` for elegance

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 md:px-8`

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold or outlined, rounded-none or slightly rounded
- Cards: minimal borders, soft shadows on hover
- Dividers: thin hairline (`border-t border-[#E8E4DF]`)
- Accordions: clean, minimal with chevron icons

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300`
- Soft shadows on hover: `hover:shadow-lg`
- Image zoom on hover: `hover:scale-105`
- Fade-in for overlays

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use gold accents sparingly
- Maintain strong contrast for accessibility
- Use serif for headings, sans-serif for body

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No heavy shadows or borders
- No bright/neon colors
