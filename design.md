# 庆巷家居用品有限公司 — Design System

## Brand Identity
Warm, cozy, trustworthy home goods brand. Evokes feelings of family, comfort, and quality craftsmanship.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Warm Amber**: `#C17F4A` — main brand color, buttons, accents (`text-amber-700`, `bg-amber-700`)
- **Primary Light**: `#F5E6D3` — soft warm tint for section backgrounds (`bg-amber-50`)
- **Background Cream**: `#FDF8F3` — page background, warm white
- **Text Dark**: `#3D2B1F` — headings, primary text (`text-stone-800`)
- **Text Medium**: `#6B4F3A` — body text, secondary (`text-stone-600`)
- **Text Light**: `#9C7B65` — captions, muted text (`text-stone-400`)
- **Accent Sage**: `#8FAF8A` — subtle green accent for freshness (`text-green-700`)
- **Border Warm**: `#E8D5C0` — card borders, dividers (`border-amber-200`)
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` — card backgrounds, overlays

## Typography
- **Font Family**: "Noto Serif SC" for headings (elegant Chinese serif), "Noto Sans SC" for body
- **H1**: `text-4xl md:text-6xl font-bold` — hero titles
- **H2**: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold` — section titles
- **H3**: `text-xl font-semibold` — card titles
- **Body**: `text-base leading-relaxed` — paragraph text
- **Caption**: `text-sm text-stone-500` — small labels

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container: `max-w-6xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Card gap: `gap-6 md:gap-8`
- Component inner padding: `p-6 md:p-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-amber-100 rounded-2xl`
- Card shadow: `shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-shadow`
- Button radius: `rounded-full`
- Image radius: `rounded-xl`

## Buttons
- **Primary**: `bg-amber-700 text-white px-8 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold hover:bg-amber-800 transition-colors`
- **Secondary**: `border-2 border-amber-700 text-amber-700 px-8 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold hover:bg-amber-50 transition-colors`
- **Ghost**: `text-amber-700 underline hover:text-amber-800`

## Do's
- Use warm, earthy tones throughout
- Maintain generous whitespace for a premium feel
- Use rounded corners on all cards and images
- Pair every section with relevant imagery
- Use Chinese serif fonts for headings to feel authentic and elegant

## Don'ts
- No cold blues or harsh grays as primary colors
- No sharp corners on cards
- No cluttered layouts — keep it airy
- No dark backgrounds except footer
