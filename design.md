# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium but accessible.
- **Audience**: Women 25-45, gifting and self-purchase.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Cream/Neutral)**: `#FDFCF8` (Page Background)
- **Secondary (Charcoal/Text)**: `#1A1A1A` (Primary Text, Buttons)
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#C5A059` (Soft Gold accents, hover states)
- **Muted (Beige)**: `#F5F2EA` (Secondary backgrounds, cards)
- **Border**: `#E5E2D9` (Hairline dividers)

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond`, Serif. Elegant and editorial.
- **Body/UI**: `Inter`, Sans-serif. Clean and readable.
- **Product Names**: Uppercase, wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal with White text.
  - Secondary: Outlined Charcoal.
  - Transitions: Soft ease-in-out for hovers and reveals.
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline borders (`border-b border-[#E5E2D9]`).
- **Whitespace**: Generous padding and margins for an airy, premium feel.

## Tailwind Classes
- Heading Serif: `font-serif` (mapped to Cormorant Garamond)
- UI Sans: `font-sans` (mapped to Inter)
- Product Name: `uppercase tracking-[0.2em] font-serif`
- Section Padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container Max Width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
