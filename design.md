# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial direction: warm ivory surfaces, deep espresso text, antique-gold accents, soft blush neutrals, thin hairline borders, and large warm jewelry imagery. The site should feel premium, calm, and intimate, never loud or discount-driven.

## Color palette

Use named Tailwind colors only. Do not use arbitrary hex values in JSX class strings.

- `velmora-ivory` (`#F8F3EA`): primary page background.
- `velmora-porcelain` (`#FFFDF8`): cards, inputs, elevated panels.
- `velmora-mink` (`#D8C7B3`): warm neutral section surfaces and subtle borders.
- `velmora-truffle` (`#7A604E`): muted editorial body copy and secondary text.
- `velmora-espresso` (`#211814`): primary text and dark luxury surfaces.
- `velmora-champagne` (`#E7D5B5`): soft highlight surfaces.
- `velmora-gold` (`#B4894B`): primary accent, buttons, stars, active states.
- `velmora-olive` (`#2C3128`): refined dark green alternative surface.
- `velmora-blush` (`#EFE2DA`): newsletter and soft editorial blocks.

## Typography

- Headings and product names use Cormorant Garamond: elegant, spacious, high-contrast serif.
- UI, navigation, body copy, forms, and prices use Manrope: clean, readable sans-serif.
- Product names are uppercase with wide letter spacing.
- Headlines should be large with generous line-height and whitespace.

## Layout and spacing

- Mobile-first, with generous padding and stacked sections on small screens.
- Desktop layouts use editorial grids, large imagery, and asymmetry where useful.
- Use thin hairline dividers with warm neutral borders.
- Keep product cards clean: image first, understated metadata, restrained hover states.

## Components

- Buttons: premium solid gold or dark outline styles, rounded-full, medium tracking, smooth hover transitions.
- Cards: porcelain or ivory surfaces, soft shadow only on hover, no heavy boxes.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, turns porcelain/ivory with hairline border on scroll.
- Cart drawer: porcelain surface with espresso text and clear controls.
- Forms: porcelain inputs, warm borders, focus ring in antique gold.

## Do

- Use warm editorial jewelry imagery and model closeups.
- Keep text contrast strong: espresso on light surfaces, ivory on dark surfaces.
- Use small uppercase labels and serif section headings.
- Use subtle transitions and restrained motion.

## Don't

- Do not use bright sale colors, discount badges, or loud gradients.
- Do not use generic blue ecommerce styling.
- Do not crowd product grids on mobile.
- Do not rely on inherited text colors for cards, drawers, or forms.
