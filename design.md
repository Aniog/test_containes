# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium.
- **Color Palette**:
  - **Primary (Background)**: `#FAF9F6` (Off-white/Stone) - A warm, airy base.
  - **Secondary (Accents)**: `#1A1A1A` (Rich Black) - For text and primary buttons.
  - **Highlight (Warm Metal)**: `#D4AF37` (Soft Gold) - Subtle accents, not overpowering.
  - **Muted**: `#71717A` (Stone Grey) - For secondary text and borders.
- **Typography**:
  - **Headings/Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif). Elegant and timeless.
  - **Body/UI**: `Inter` (Sans-serif). Clean and modern for legibility.
  - **Styling**: Product names in UPPERCASE with `tracking-widest`.
- **UI Elements**:
  - **Dividers**: Thin hairline borders (`border-stone-200`).
  - **Transitions**: Smooth, subtle fades and scale effects.
  - **Shadows**: Soft, minimal elevations (`shadow-sm`).
  - **Buttons**:
    - Primary: Solid Rich Black, white text, minimal padding.
    - Secondary: Outlined Rich Black, serif text.

## Tailwind Classes Mapping
- Background: `bg-[#FAF9F6]`
- Text Primary: `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- Text Muted: `text-stone-500`
- Border: `border-stone-200`
- Heading Font: `font-serif` (Cormorant Garamond)
- Body Font: `font-sans` (Inter)
