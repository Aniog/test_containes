# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual Identity
**Brand:** Velmora Fine Jewelry
**Vibe:** Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, sophisticated, demi-fine.
**Target:** Women 25-45, gifting & self-purchase, premium-but-accessible ($30-$120).

## Color Palette
Primary strategy: Deep refined neutral base with warm metallic gold accents.
- **Backgrounds:** `bg-[#FDFBF7]` (Warm alabaster for main views to let gold pop), `bg-[#1A1A1A]` (Deep charcoal/black for stark editorial sections or footer).
- **Text:** `text-[#2A2A2A]` (Soft black/dark charcoal for main text), `text-[#FDFBF7]` (On dark backgrounds).
- **Accents:** `text-[#C4A052]` / `bg-[#C4A052]`, `border-[#C4A052]` (Warm muted gold).
- **Secondary Accents:** `text-[#8C8C8C]` (Muted grey for secondary text/meta).

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** Cormorant Garamond (Serif). Elegant, editorial. `font-serif`.
  - Use `uppercase tracking-widest` for product names and small headers.
- **Body & UI:** Inter (Sans-serif). Clean, legible. `font-sans`.

## Components & Spacing
- **Generous Whitespace:** Use high padding values (e.g., `py-16`, `py-24`, `gap-8`, `gap-12`).
- **Dividers:** Thin hairline dividers `divide-y divide-[#E5E5E5]` or `border-[#E5E5E5]`.
- **Buttons:** 
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal `bg-[#2A2A2A] text-white hover:bg-[#1A1A1A]` or Solid Gold `bg-[#C4A052] text-white hover:bg-[#B39045]`. Must feel premium (no border radius `rounded-none` or very slight `rounded-sm`).
  - Secondary: Outlined `border border-[#2A2A2A] text-[#2A2A2A] hover:bg-[#F9F9F9]`.
- **Shadows & Transitions:** Very subtle, soft shadows for floating elements (if any), smooth `transition-all duration-300` for hovers. No bouncy animations.

## Imagery
- Large, editorial. Warm-lit.
- Image ratios: Hero 16:9 or full viewport, Product cards 3:4 (portrait), UGC 9:16.
