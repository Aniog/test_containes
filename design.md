# Design System - StrkFlow

## Visual Style
A modern, clean, and professional SaaS aesthetic. Primarily using whites, slates, and a vibrant indigo primary color.

## Typography
- Font Family: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- Headings: Bold (font-bold) with tight tracking (tracking-tight)
- Body: Normal (font-normal) with relaxed leading (leading-relaxed)

## Colors (Tailwind Classes)
- Primary: Indigo-600 (bg-indigo-600, text-indigo-600)
- Background: Slate-50 (bg-slate-50)
- Foreground: Slate-900 (text-slate-900)
- Muted: Slate-500 (text-slate-500)
- Borders: Slate-200 (border-slate-200)

## Spacing
- Sections: py-20 or py-32
- Containers: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8

## Components
- Buttons: Rounded-md, font-medium, transition-colors
- Cards: bg-white, rounded-xl, shadow-sm, border border-slate-200, p-6
- Inputs: border-slate-300, rounded-md, focus:ring-indigo-500

## Do's
- Use plenty of white space.
- Use Lucide icons for visual interest.
- Ensure high contrast for readability.

## Don'ts
- Avoid cluttered layouts.
- Avoid low-contrast text (e.g., light gray on light gray).
- Do not use arbitrary hex codes; use Tailwind slate and indigo scales.
