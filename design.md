# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry for women 25–45. The site should feel like a glossy lookbook, not a discount marketplace.

## Color palette
- **Background** — `#F9F7F4` (warm off-white)
- **Surface** — `#FFFFFF` (pure white cards)
- **Surface-muted** — `#F2EEE9` (soft beige panels)
- **Foreground** — `#231F1B` (deep espresso brown)
- **Muted** — `#6B5E54` (warm gray-brown)
- **Border** — `#DDD6CC` (hairline dividers)
- **Accent** — `#A67C52` (warm bronze/gold)
- **Accent hover** — `#8B663F`
- **Star / highlight** — `#C8A46E` (soft gold)

## Typography
- **Headings & product names** — `Cormorant Garamond`, serif, uppercase product names with `letter-spacing: 0.14em`.
- **Body & UI** — `Inter`, sans-serif.
- Product names are uppercase, small, tracked wide.

## Components
- Buttons: solid accent with white text, or white/transparent with dark border. Rounded `sm`. Subtle hover lift.
- Cards: white surface, thin border, soft shadow, image-first.
- Dividers: 1px `border` color.
- Inputs: minimal, rounded, border color.

## Imagery
- Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds.
- Editorial crops: ears, neck, hands, model close-ups.
- Use stock-image helper tags with `[titleId] [descId]` references.

## Layout principles
- Mobile-first.
- Generous whitespace (`py-16` to `py-24`).
- Max container `max-w-7xl`.
- 12-column grid feel, 1–2–3 product grids.
- Sticky nav with transparency transition.
