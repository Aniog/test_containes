# MicroCosmos Design Style

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) — elegant, scientific feel
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern readability
- Hero title: font-size 4xl–6xl with tight letter-spacing
- Section titles: 3xl with decorative underline accent

## Colors
- Primary background: deep navy #0a0a1a
- Card/section background: dark teal #0d2137
- Surface cards: semi-transparent dark with border
- Accent cyan: #00e5ff (fluorescent microscopy feel)
- Accent green: #39ff14
- Accent magenta: #c742f5
- Text primary: #e2e8f0 (light gray-white)
- Text secondary: #94a3b8 (muted slate)

## Visual Style
- Dark, immersive theme evoking microscopy and deep space
- Generous use of images with rounded corners and subtle shadows
- Card-based grid layouts with hover scale + glow effects
- Gradient overlays on hero and section backgrounds
- Thin borders with low opacity for separation
- Do: use deep dark backgrounds with bright accent pops
- Do: use plenty of images, large and small
- Don't: use harsh white backgrounds
- Don't: let text blend into dark backgrounds — always ensure readable contrast

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card gaps: gap-6 or gap-8 in grids
- Image rounding: rounded-xl or rounded-2xl
