# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Design System

## Brand Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Cream (#FBF8F4): Primary background, warm white
- Charcoal (#1C1917): Primary text, deep base
- Gold (#B49450): Accent, buttons, CTAs
- Gold Dark (#8B6914): Accent hover states
- Taupe (#8C7E72): Secondary text, muted UI
- Sand (#E7DFD3): Dividers, subtle backgrounds
- Rose (#A67B6B): Subtle accent, warmth

## Typography
- Headings & Product Names: Playfair Display (serif), uppercase product names with wide letter-spacing
- Body & UI: Inter (sans-serif), clean, modern
- Hierarchy: Generous size steps, ample whitespace

## Visual Rules
- Generous whitespace, large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers (`border-t border-sand`)
- Restrained accent color (gold used sparingly for CTAs and highlights)
- Subtle hover transitions (opacity, scale, shadow)
- Soft shadows when appropriate (cards)
- Buttons: solid gold (primary) or outlined charcoal (secondary), uppercase, wide tracking
- Product cards: hover reveals second image, quick add-to-cart overlay
- Mobile-first, fully responsive

## Do's
- Use cream backgrounds with charcoal text
- Use gold ONLY for accents, CTAs, highlights
- Use uppercase + wide letter-spacing for product names
- Maintain generous padding/margins

## Don'ts
- No bright colors, neon, or discount-red badges
- No heavy borders or harsh shadows
- No crowded layouts
- No generic blue links
