# Skateboarding Site — Design System

## Visual Identity
Raw, urban, high-energy. Street culture meets bold graphic design. Dark backgrounds with electric accent colors. No softness — sharp edges, strong contrast, attitude.

## Colors
- **Background (primary):** `#0a0a0a` — near-black → `bg-[#0a0a0a]`
- **Background (surface):** `#141414` — dark card surface → `bg-[#141414]`
- **Background (elevated):** `#1e1e1e` — slightly lighter surface → `bg-[#1e1e1e]`
- **Accent Yellow:** `#f5e642` — neon yellow, primary CTA and highlights → `text-skate-yellow`, `bg-skate-yellow`
- **Accent Orange:** `#ff6b35` — secondary accent, energy → `text-skate-orange`, `bg-skate-orange`
- **Text Primary:** `#ffffff` — white → `text-white`
- **Text Secondary:** `#a0a0a0` — muted gray → `text-[#a0a0a0]`
- **Border:** `#2a2a2a` — subtle dividers → `border-[#2a2a2a]`

## Typography
- **Display / Headings:** "Bebas Neue" — all-caps, condensed, bold. Use for section titles, hero text.
  - Hero: `text-7xl md:text-9xl font-display tracking-wide`
  - Section titles: `text-5xl md:text-6xl font-display tracking-wide`
  - Card titles: `text-2xl font-display tracking-wide`
- **Body / UI:** "Inter" — clean, readable. Use for paragraphs, labels, nav.
  - Body: `text-base font-sans leading-relaxed`
  - Small/label: `text-sm font-sans uppercase tracking-widest`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28 px-6 md:px-12`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Radius
- Cards: `rounded-none` — sharp corners for urban feel
- Buttons: `rounded-none`
- Accent lines: `border-l-4 border-skate-yellow`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-skate-yellow text-black font-sans font-bold uppercase tracking-widest px-8 py-4 rounded-none hover:bg-white transition-colors`
- Secondary/outline: `border-2 border-white text-white font-sans font-bold uppercase tracking-widest px-8 py-4 rounded-none hover:bg-white hover:text-black transition-colors`

## Images
- Use full-bleed images with dark overlays for hero/backgrounds
- Aspect ratios: hero 16x9, cards 3x2 or 4x3

## Do's
- Use Bebas Neue for all headings
- Keep backgrounds dark; use accent colors sparingly for maximum impact
- Use uppercase text for labels and buttons
- Strong contrast between text and background at all times

## Don'ts
- No rounded corners on cards or buttons
- No light backgrounds (except for intentional contrast sections)
- No pastel or muted accent colors
- No small or thin typography for headings
