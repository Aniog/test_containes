# Strikingly Generators Hub Design System

Clean Strikingly marketing style with white surfaces, crisp gray typography, subtle borders, and restrained purple accents. Headings use Brandon Grotesque with Josefin Sans fallback, uppercase, 700 weight, and 1.2 line-height. Body text uses Open Sans at 14px and 1.5 line-height.

Brand tokens: purple #8159BB, AI gradient #7671FF to #CB0C9F only for primary CTAs and the second hero headline line, body #636972, headings #4B5056, hero heading #2E2E2F, border #C6C9CD, divider #E2E4E7, light background #F4F6F8, white #FFFFFF.

Cards are white with 1px #C6C9CD borders, 3px radius, 20px padding, and a subtle purple-border lift on hover/focus. Buttons are uppercase heading-font controls with 4px radius, 36px or 44px height, and white text on any filled gradient. Use 20px block spacing, 40px major section spacing, 60-80px hero padding, and max content width 1200px.

Use inline SVG only for illustrations. Avoid fake social proof, bare link lists, low-contrast text, and hardcoded external image URLs.
