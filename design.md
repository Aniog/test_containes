# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Base**: Cream (#FAF8F5) — warm off-white background
- **Dark**: Espresso (#1C1917) — deep brown-black for text and accent blocks
- **Accent**: Gold (#C4A265) — warm metallic gold for highlights, CTAs, and decorative elements
- **Light Gold**: Gold Light (#F0E8D8) — for subtle warm accents
- **Dark Gold**: Gold Dark (#A88A4D) — for hover states
- **Muted**: Muted (#8B8378) — warm gray for secondary text
- **Surface**: Surface (#F5F2ED) — slightly darker than cream for cards and sections
- **Border**: Border (#E8E3DA) — delicate warm border

## Typography
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern, readable
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE with 0.15em letter-spacing in Playfair Display
- **Section labels**: 0.3em letter-spacing, uppercase, gold color, Inter font
- **Nav links**: 0.12em letter-spacing, uppercase, Inter font

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: 4rem top/bottom on mobile, 6rem on desktop
- Hairline dividers: 1px border-t border-border
- Gold accent line: 12px width, 1px height, gold/40

## Components
- **Buttons**: Clean, uppercase, 0.12em letter-spacing. Solid espresso (hover: gold), outline (hover: filled espresso), gold (hover: dark gold)
- **Cards**: Subtle hover lift/animation, rounded-sm corners
- **Shadows**: Soft, minimal — only where needed for depth
- **Transitions**: 400ms duration, smooth easing

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Hairline dividers for separation
- Warm, dark imagery backgrounds
- Gold accent sparingly for emphasis
- Product cards with hover states

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy shadows
- No discount badges or sale styling
- No busy patterns
- No pure black — use espresso instead
