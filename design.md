# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking.

## Colors
- **Cream** (#FBF8F4) — page background, soft neutral base
- **Espresso** (#1C1917) — primary text, buttons, dark accents
- **Taupe** (#7A7169) — secondary text, muted elements
- **Gold** (#C7A55B) — accent color, CTAs, highlights
- **Gold Hover** (#B8944A) — hover state for gold elements
- **Gold Pale** (#F5ECD7) — subtle gold backgrounds
- **Stone Warm** (#E8E2D9) — dividers, borders, subtle accents
- **Stone Lighter** (#F2EDE6) — card backgrounds, subtle surface variation

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weight 400-600, uppercase for product names with wide letter-spacing (0.22em)
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weight 300-500
- **Accent headings**: Cormorant Garamond italic for editorial moments

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 to py-28 on desktop, py-16 on mobile
- Card gaps: 6-8 on grid layouts

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers (1px, stone-warm color)
- No heavy borders or box-shadows by default
- Subtle hover shadows: 0 20px 40px -12px rgba(28, 25, 23, 0.08)

## Buttons
- Primary: espresso bg, cream text, transitions to gold on hover
- Outline: transparent bg, espresso border/text
- Gold accent: gold bg, espresso text
- All uppercase, wide letter-spacing, generous padding

## Imagery
- Large editorial hero images
- Product images on cream/neutral backgrounds
- Warm lighting, gold jewelry focus
