# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Colors
- **cream:** #FAF7F2 — Page background
- **sand:** #F0EBE3 — Dividers, subtle backgrounds
- **gold:** #C8A96E — Primary accent, buttons, highlights
- **gold-light:** #DCC69A — Hover states, lighter accents
- **gold-dark:** #B8944D — Active states, darker accents
- **charcoal:** #2C2C2C — Body text
- **ink:** #1A1A1A — Headings, strong text
- **stone:** #8C8C8C — Secondary text, captions
- **warmwhite:** #FDFAF5 — Card backgrounds
- **blush:** #F5EDE4 — Alternate soft backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond (serif) — Product names, section titles, hero
- **Body/UI:** Inter (sans-serif) — Body text, buttons, nav, forms
- Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing 0.15em–0.25em

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: section padding py-20 md:py-28
- Card spacing: gap-8
- Content max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8

## Buttons
- **Primary:** bg-velmora-gold text-white, hover:bg-velmora-gold-dark
- **Outline:** border-velmora-gold text-velmora-gold, hover:bg-velmora-gold
- No rounded-full pills — use subtle rounded (rounded-sm or none)

## DO
- Use thin hairline dividers (border-velmora-sand)
- Generous use of whitespace
- Subtle hover transitions (300ms)
- Soft shadows (shadow-sm, shadow-md)
- Warm neutral backgrounds

## DON'T
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy borders or strong shadows
- No discount badges or sale tags
- No rounded-full buttons
- No generic blue links
