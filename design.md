# SSourcing China - Design Guidelines

## Brand Overview
SSourcing China is a professional China-based sourcing agent helping overseas buyers find reliable suppliers, verify factories, inspect quality, follow production, and coordinate shipping. The brand is clean, trustworthy, international, and B2B-focused.

## Visual Style
- **Style**: Clean, professional, trustworthy, international B2B
- **Tone**: Professional, clear, practical — no exaggerated claims
- **Imagery**: Realistic factory, QC inspection, shipping, and supply chain visuals

## Color Palette
Primary (Trust & Professionalism):
- Primary Navy: #0F172A (slate-900)
- Primary Blue: #1E3A8A (blue-800)
- Accent Blue: #2563EB (blue-600)

Secondary (Clean & Neutral):
- Slate 50: #F8FAFC (backgrounds)
- Slate 100: #F1F5F9 (cards, sections)
- Slate 200: #E2E8F0 (borders)
- Slate 500: #64748B (secondary text)
- Slate 700: #334155 (body text)
- White: #FFFFFF

Accent (CTAs & Highlights):
- CTA Green: #15803D (green-700) — for primary actions
- CTA Green Hover: #166534
- Warning/Highlight: #B45309 (amber-700) — used sparingly

## Typography
- Font Family: Inter (system-ui fallback)
- Headings: 600-700 weight, tight tracking
- Body: 400-500 weight, 1.6 line-height for readability
- Sizes:
  - H1: 2.5rem - 3rem (40-48px)
  - H2: 1.875rem - 2.25rem (30-36px)
  - H3: 1.25rem - 1.5rem (20-24px)
  - Body: 1rem (16px)
  - Small: 0.875rem (14px)

## Spacing & Layout
- Container max-width: 1280px (mx-auto px-6)
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-20
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Consistent 8px grid system
- Generous whitespace for professional feel

## Components
- Buttons: Rounded (6px), clear hierarchy
  - Primary: bg-green-700 text-white hover:bg-green-800
  - Secondary: border border-slate-300 text-slate-700 hover:bg-slate-50
- Cards: bg-white border border-slate-200 shadow-sm rounded-lg
- Forms: Clean inputs with focus states, proper labels
- Navigation: Clean, sticky on scroll, professional

## Imagery Guidelines
- Use realistic industrial/factory/QC/shipping visuals
- Prefer data-strk-img for dynamic stock images
- Maintain professional, non-staged appearance
- Good lighting, authentic environments

## Responsive
- Mobile-first approach
- Breakpoints: sm (640px), md (768px), lg (1024px), xl (1280px)
- Touch-friendly interactive elements (min 44px)

## Do's
- Use plenty of white space
- Keep copy concise and practical
- Use real numbers and specific details in case studies
- Ensure high contrast for accessibility
- Use icons sparingly and meaningfully (lucide-react)

## Don'ts
- No exaggerated claims ("#1", "guaranteed", "100%")
- No overly bright or playful colors
- No stock-photo clichés (smiling handshakes, generic globes)
- No dense walls of text
- No decorative elements that distract from content
