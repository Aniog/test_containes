# Design System - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Color Palette**:
  - Background (Base): Cream/Beige (`#FDFCF9`) - Tailwind: `bg-[#FDFCF9]`
  - Foreground (Text): Charcoal/Deep Olive (`#1C1C1C`) - Tailwind: `text-[#1C1C1C]`
  - Accent (Metallic): Muted Gold (`#C5A059`) - Tailwind: `text-[#C5A059]` / `bg-[#C5A059]`
  - Neutral: Stone (`#E8E4DF`) - Tailwind: `bg-[#E8E4DF]`
- **Typography**:
  - Headings/Product Names: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif)
  - Body/UI: `Manrope` (Sans-serif)
- **Visual Elements**:
  - Generous whitespace.
  - Thin hairline dividers (0.5px or `border-stone-200`).
  - Large editorial imagery.
  - Buttons: Solid charcoal with white text or outlined stone with charcoal text.
  - Transitions: Soft, subtle 300ms ease.

## Do's and Don'ts
### Do
- Use uppercase and wide tracking (`tracking-[0.2em]`) for product titles.
- Use serif for storytelling and editorial sections.
- Keep the layout airy and balanced.
- Use high-quality imagery that feels warm and lit.

### Don't
- Use vibrant or neon colors.
- Overcrowd sections with too many products.
- Use heavy, blocky borders.
- Use generic sans-serif for headlines.
