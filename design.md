# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand Direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury with a warm editorial point of view. The brand is premium and giftable, but still approachable for everyday self-purchase. Every section should feel airy, refined, and intentionally restrained.

## Typography
- Display / headings: Cormorant Garamond (`font-display`) for elegance and fashion-editorial tone.
- Body / UI: Inter (`font-sans`) for clarity and modern contrast.
- Product names and labels: uppercase with wide tracking using `tracking-product` or `tracking-brand`.

## Color Palette
Use these named theme tokens consistently:
- `velmora-ink`: deep espresso for main text and dark surfaces.
- `velmora-noir`: richer dark tone for hero overlays and premium backgrounds.
- `velmora-pearl`: soft editorial page background.
- `velmora-ivory`: warm surface color for cards and panels.
- `velmora-champagne`: warm neutral tint for image frames and accent surfaces.
- `velmora-line`: soft divider / hairline border color.
- `velmora-taupe`: muted supporting copy.
- `velmora-gold`: primary metallic accent for buttons and highlights.
- `velmora-gold-soft`: lighter accent for subtle emphasis.
