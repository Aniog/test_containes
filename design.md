# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry
- NOT loud, NOT discount, NOT generic e-commerce

## Color Palette

### Base
- `bg`: #FAF7F2 — warm ivory (main page background)
- `bg-card`: #FFFFFF — pure white (cards, modals)
- `bg-elevated`: #F5EFE7 — cream (subtle section alternate)
- `bg-dark`: #1C1917 — warm near-black (footer, hero overlays)
- `bg-accent`: #B08D57 — muted warm gold (buttons, accents)
- `bg-accent-hover`: #9A7A48 — darker gold hover

### Text
- `text`: #1C1917 — primary text (warm near-black)
- `text-secondary`: #78716C — secondary text (stone-600)
- `text-muted`: #A8A29E — muted text (stone-400)
- `text-inverse`: #FAF7F2 — text on dark backgrounds
- `text-accent`: #B08D57 — gold accent text

### Borders
- `border`: #E7E0D8 — default border (warm stone-200)
- `border-light`: #F0EBE4 — subtle border
- `border-accent`: #B08D57 — accent border (gold)

## Typography
- Headings: `font-serif` → Cormorant Garamond (400, 500, 600)
- Body/UI: `font-sans` → Inter (300, 400, 500, 600)
- Product names: uppercase, tracking-[0.15em], font-serif
- Logo: uppercase, tracking-[0.25em], font-serif, font-medium

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: max-w-7xl (1280px)
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card padding: p-4 md:p-6
- Generous whitespace throughout

## Components
- Buttons: uppercase tracking-wider text-sm, solid bg-accent or outlined border-accent
- Cards: rounded-lg shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow, bg-card
- Dividers: border-t border-border (thin hairline)
- Accents: gold (bg-accent/text-accent) used sparingly

## Shadows
- Card default: shadow-sm
- Card hover: shadow-md
- Elevated: shadow-lg

## Transitions
- Hover: transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out
- Button hover: bg-accent-hover, slight scale
- Image hover: subtle zoom (scale-105)

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep contrast high (dark text on light bg)
- Use warm tones that flatter gold jewelry
- Keep the mood editorial and premium

## Don'ts
- No neon or bright colors
- No heavy borders or shadows
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/sale badges (premium positioning)
