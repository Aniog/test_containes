# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Design System

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm editorial aesthetic. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry for women 25–45.
NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: #FAF7F2 (warm ivory/cream)
- Surface/Cards: #FFFFFF
- Accent/Gold: #C9A96E (warm gold metallic)
- Accent Hover: #B89450
- Text Primary: #1C1C1C (near black)
- Text Secondary: #6B6258 (warm gray)
- Nav/Footer Background: #1C1C1C
- Trust Bar Background: #1C1C1C
- Border/Divider: #E5DED3 (warm taupe)
- Muted Background: #F5F0EA

## Typography
- Headings (serif): Cormorant Garamond — used for hero, section titles, product names, brand logo
- Body (sans-serif): Manrope — used for body text, UI, navigation, buttons
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- Scale: 12 / 14 / 16 / 18 / 20 / 24 / 32 / 40 / 56 / 72

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E5DED3]

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C9A96E] text-white, hover:bg-[#B89450], uppercase, tracking-widest, text-sm, px-8 py-3
- Outline: border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E], hover:bg-[#C9A96E] hover:text-white

## Navigation
- Transparent on hero, solid (#1C1C1C) on scroll
- Logo: Cormorant Garamond, uppercase, tracking-widest
- Links: Manrope, uppercase, text-xs tracking-widest

## Cards
- Product cards: bg-white, subtle shadow, overflow-hidden
- Category tiles: image with overlay label on hover

## Effects
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- Soft shadows (shadow-sm, shadow-md)
- Smooth scroll
