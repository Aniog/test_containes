# SSourcing China Design System

## Brand direction
SSourcing China should feel clean, trustworthy, international, and practical for overseas B2B buyers. The design should communicate factory access, quality control, logistics coordination, and transparent process management without exaggerated claims.

## Color palette
Use a restrained business palette with strong contrast:
- Primary navy: deep slate/navy surfaces and text, e.g. `bg-slate-950`, `text-slate-950`, `border-slate-200`.
- Trust blue: calls to action and key highlights, e.g. `bg-blue-700`, `text-blue-700`, `hover:bg-blue-800`.
- Fresh green: operational/QC proof points, e.g. `bg-emerald-600`, `text-emerald-700`, `bg-emerald-50`.
- Warm amber: subtle risk/problem accents, e.g. `bg-amber-50`, `text-amber-700`.
- Neutral backgrounds: `bg-white`, `bg-slate-50`, `bg-slate-100`.

## Typography
Use Inter from Google Fonts. Headings should be confident and concise with tight tracking. Body copy should be readable, practical, and direct.
- Hero heading: `text-4xl md:text-6xl font-semibold tracking-tight`.
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-semibold tracking-tight`.
- Body: `text-base md:text-lg text-slate-600 leading-7`.
- Small labels: `text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-[0.18em] text-blue-700`.

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`.
- Keep content in centered containers: `mx-auto max-w-7xl px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`.
- Desktop layouts should use balanced grids (`lg:grid-cols-2`, `lg:grid-cols-3`, `lg:grid-cols-4`). Mobile stacks naturally.
- Use sticky top navigation with clear contrast and simple page anchors.

## Cards and surfaces
- Cards: `rounded-3xl border border-slate-200 bg-white p-6 shadow-sm`.
- Dark cards: `rounded-3xl bg-slate-950 text-white` with explicit light text.
- Callout panels: `rounded-3xl bg-blue-700 text-white` or `bg-slate-950 text-white`.
- Avoid low-contrast text. Every custom surface must set explicit readable text colors.

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: `rounded-full bg-blue-700 px-6 py-3 font-semibold text-white shadow-sm hover:bg-blue-800`.
- Secondary CTA: `rounded-full border border-slate-300 bg-white px-6 py-3 font-semibold text-slate-900 hover:bg-slate-50`.
- Dark surface secondary: `border-white/30 text-white hover:bg-white/10`.

## Visuals
Use realistic factory, inspection, production, warehouse, and shipping visuals through the Strikingly image tags. Prefer images inside rounded cards with subtle overlays. Do not use decorative images where they do not support the buyer journey.

## Do's
- Use plain, practical B2B language.
- Emphasize verification, clear communication, production follow-up, QC, and shipping coordination.
- Keep forms readable and conversion-oriented.
- Make all navigation and CTAs obvious.

## Don'ts
- Do not use exaggerated claims such as “guaranteed best price” or “risk-free”.
- Do not use hardcoded external image URLs.
- Do not use low contrast gray text on gray backgrounds.
- Do not crowd desktop pages with mobile-only stacking layouts.
