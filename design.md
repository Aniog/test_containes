# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Tagline:** Precision Engineering for Sheet Metal Excellence
- **Industry:** Industrial machinery / sheet metal folding equipment
- **Tone:** Professional, confident, elegant, precise

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Brand):** `#b8860b` — Dark goldenrod. Represents quality metalwork and industrial prestige.
- **Primary Dark:** `#8a6508` — Hover/active states.
- **Background Light:** `#f8f9fb` — Main page background, clean and airy.
- **Background Dark:** `#0f172a` — Hero, footer, and dark sections for contrast.
- **Text Primary:** `#1e293b` — Headings and body on light backgrounds.
- **Text Secondary:** `#64748b` — Captions, descriptions.
- **Text Light:** `#f1f5f9` — Text on dark backgrounds.
- **Accent Border:** `#e2e8f0` — Card borders, dividers.
- **White:** `#ffffff` — Cards, button text.

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (Google Fonts), weights 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800.
- **Headings:** Bold, tight letter-spacing (`tracking-tight`), dark on light / light on dark.
- **Body:** Regular weight, comfortable line-height (`leading-relaxed`), secondary color.
- **Labels / Small Caps:** Uppercase, small size, medium weight, wide letter-spacing.

## Spacing & Layout
- **Section Padding:** `py-20` to `py-28` (80–112px vertical) for breathing room.
- **Container:** Max-width `1280px`, centered with `mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`.
- **Card Style:** White background, subtle shadow (`shadow-sm`), rounded corners (`rounded-xl`), bordered (`border border-slate-200`).
- **Button Primary:** Goldenrod background, white text, rounded-lg, medium padding. Hover: darken background.
- **Button Outline:** Transparent background, goldenrod border and text. Hover: fill with goldenrod, white text.

## Visual Style
- Clean, modern industrial aesthetic.
- High-quality machinery photography with stock image system.
- Generous whitespace to convey quality and confidence.
- Subtle transitions and hover effects for interactivity.
- Responsive: full-width mobile, contained desktop.

## Do's and Don'ts
- **Do** use high-contrast text on all backgrounds.
- **Do** keep buttons large and easy to click.
- **Do** use the goldenrod accent sparingly for emphasis.
- **Don't** use cluttered layouts or excessive decorative elements.
- **Don't** use pure black for text or backgrounds.
