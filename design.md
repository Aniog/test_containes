# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry.

## Colors
- Base (near-black): #1B1A1A
- Background (warm cream): #FAF8F5
- Surface (white): #FFFFFF
- Gold accent: #C8A96E
- Gold accent hover: #B8934F
- Gold light: #F5EFE6
- Text primary: #1B1A1A
- Text secondary: #6B6560
- Text muted: #9C958D
- Divider (hairline): #E8E3DC
- Success: #4A7C59
- Error: #C53030

## Typography
- Headings & product names: Playfair Display (serif), uppercase with wide letter-spacing for product names
- Body & UI: Inter (sans-serif, 400/500/600 weights)
- Nav logo: Playfair Display, 500 weight, letter-spacing 0.15em

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-[#E8E3DC] border-t
- Subtle hover transitions: transition-all duration-300

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C8A96E] text-white hover:bg-[#B8934F] (solid gold)
- Outline: border border-[#C8A96E] text-[#C8A96E] hover:bg-[#C8A96E] hover:text-white
- Text links: text-[#C8A96E] hover:text-[#B8934F] underline-offset-4

## Shadows
- Card: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
- Soft, restrained — never heavy

## Image Treatment
- Warm-lit, editorial gold jewelry imagery
- 3:4 or 4:5 ratios for product cards
- 16:9 for hero

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use gold accent sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Maintain strong contrast for accessibility
- Thin hairline dividers between sections

## Don'ts
- No loud colors, no neon, no heavy shadows
- No discount/badge-heavy look
- No generic e-commerce styling
- No dark text on dark backgrounds
