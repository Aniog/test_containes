# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Overview
ARTITECT MACHINERY specializes in premium sheet metal folding equipment. The brand represents precision engineering, reliability, and industrial excellence.

## Visual Style
- **Overall aesthetic**: Elegant industrial — refined, professional, trustworthy
- **Tone**: Sophisticated yet approachable, expert but not intimidating

## Color Palette
Primary:
- Deep Navy: #0A1628 (headers, navigation, accents)
- Charcoal: #1C2833 (body text, secondary elements)
- Warm Gold/Brass: #B8860B (accent highlights, CTAs, premium feel)

Neutrals:
- Off-White: #F8F9FA (backgrounds)
- Light Gray: #E8ECF0 (cards, sections)
- Medium Gray: #6B7280 (secondary text)
- White: #FFFFFF (cards, overlays)

## Typography
- **Primary font**: Inter (clean, modern, highly legible)
- **Headings**: 600-700 weight, tight tracking for authority
- **Body**: 400-500 weight, generous line-height for readability
- **Scale**: Clear hierarchy (H1 3.5rem, H2 2.25rem, H3 1.5rem, body 1rem)

## Layout Principles
- Generous whitespace (section padding 5rem+ on desktop)
- Max content width: 1280px with responsive padding
- Cards use subtle shadows and clean borders
- Consistent 8px grid system
- Mobile-first responsive breakpoints

## Component Guidelines
- Buttons: Rounded (6px), clear hierarchy (primary gold, secondary navy outline)
- Cards: White/off-white background, subtle border or shadow
- Navigation: Sticky, clean, with active states
- Forms: Clean inputs with focus states matching brand colors
- Icons: Lucide icons, consistent sizing (stroke width 2)

## Do's
- Use ample padding and breathing room
- Maintain high contrast for accessibility
- Use professional product photography style
- Keep CTAs prominent but not aggressive

## Don'ts
- Avoid overly bright or saturated colors
- Don't use decorative elements that feel cheap or cluttered
- Avoid cramped layouts
- Don't use all-caps for body text
