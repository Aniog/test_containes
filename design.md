# Design System — Nature Landing Page

## Visual Style
Clean, organic, and immersive. The design draws from nature's palette — deep forest greens, warm earthy tones, and soft sky blues. Typography is elegant and readable, with generous whitespace to let imagery breathe.

## Colors
- Primary green: `#2d6a4f` (Tailwind custom: `green-forest`)
- Light green accent: `#52b788` (Tailwind custom: `green-leaf`)
- Warm earth: `#a07850` (Tailwind custom: `earth`)
- Sky blue: `#90c2e7` (Tailwind custom: `sky-nature`)
- Off-white background: `#f8f5f0` (Tailwind custom: `cream`)
- Dark text: `#1a2e1a`
- Muted text: `#5a7a5a`

## Typography
- Font family: "Playfair Display" (headings), "Inter" (body)
- Heading sizes: `text-5xl` / `text-4xl` / `text-2xl`
- Body: `text-base` or `text-lg`, `leading-relaxed`
- Heading weight: `font-bold` or `font-semibold`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-6` or `py-24 px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl shadow-md`
- Images: `rounded-xl overflow-hidden`
- Buttons: `rounded-full`

## Do's
- Use full-bleed hero images with overlay text
- Use rounded corners on all cards and images
- Keep text on dark overlays white and clearly readable
- Use generous padding and whitespace

## Don'ts
- Don't use harsh neon or overly saturated colors
- Don't crowd elements — let the imagery breathe
- Don't use small font sizes for body text
