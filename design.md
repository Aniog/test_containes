# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Style**: Generous whitespace, large editorial imagery, thin hairline dividers, restrained accent color.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: Warm ivory (#FAF8F5) - hsl(40, 20%, 98%)
- **Foreground/Text**: Deep charcoal (#2C2420) - hsl(20, 15%, 15%)
- **Primary**: Same as foreground for buttons, links
- **Accent**: Warm gold (#C4944A) - hsl(38, 65%, 55%)
- **Secondary**: Soft warm gray (#EDE8E0) - hsl(35, 30%, 92%)
- **Muted**: Lighter warm gray (#E5DFD6) - hsl(35, 15%, 90%)
- **Muted Foreground**: Medium warm gray (#7A7068) - hsl(25, 10%, 45%)
- **Border**: Soft warm border (#D8D0C6) - hsl(35, 15%, 85%)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - weights 300, 400, 500, 600
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif) - weights 300, 400, 500, 600
- **Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond, UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em

## Spacing
- Section padding: px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-12 xl:px-20
- Generous vertical spacing between sections: py-16 lg:py-24

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid primary or outlined, uppercase, tracking-widest, text-sm
- **Cards**: Clean, minimal borders, subtle hover transitions
- **Dividers**: Hairline thin borders (border-t border-border/50)

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use warm gold accent sparingly
- Ensure strong contrast for accessibility
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or discount-looking elements
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No heavy shadows or gradients
- No cluttered layouts
