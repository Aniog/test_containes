# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design Guide

## Visual Identity
Velmora represents quiet luxury, selling demi-fine gold jewelry to women aged 25-45. The design should convey a premium, elegant, and warm editorial feel.

## Colors
We will use a warm, neutral palette that complements gold imagery, with deep, refined accents.
- **Background**: Soft ivory/cream (`bg-stone-50`, `#FAFAF9`)
- **Text (Primary)**: Deep charcoal/almost black (`text-stone-900`, `#1C1917`)
- **Text (Secondary)**: Muted warm gray (`text-stone-500`, `#78716C`)
- **Accent (Buttons, key highlights)**: Deep rich olive or muted gold tone (`bg-stone-800`, text-on-accent `#FAFAF9`)
- **Borders/Dividers**: Hairline subtle borders (`border-stone-200`, `#E7E5E4`)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif). Used for logo, page titles, product names, section headers. Elegant and refined.
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif). Clean and highly legible for navigation, descriptions, buttons, prices.
- **Product Names**: Uppercase, wide letter-spacing (`uppercase tracking-wider`).

## Layout & Components
- **Whitespace**: Generous padding and margins (`py-16`, `py-24`, `gap-8`) to maintain an editorial, uncluttered feel.
- **Images**: Large full-bleed backgrounds where appropriate, delicate framing for product cards, consistent aspect ratios.
- **Buttons**: Premium feel. Either solid deep accent `bg-stone-900 text-stone-50 hover:bg-stone-800` or delicate outlines `border border-stone-800 text-stone-900 hover:bg-stone-50`. Rectangular or very slightly rounded (`rounded-sm`).
- **Dividers**: Thin, subtle, and restrained. `border-t border-stone-200`.
- **Shadows**: Soft, diffuse shadows only when necessary for depth (e.g., dropdowns, sticky header). `shadow-sm` or `shadow-md` for structural elements, avoid heavy drop shadows in content.

## Do's and Don'ts
- **DO**: Maintain high contrast between text and background.
- **DO**: Use uppercase & tracking for small subheadings or labels.
- **DON'T**: Use bright stark white (`#FFFFFF`) as the main background universally if it feels too cold; prefer off-whites or warm grays.
- **DON'T**: Overcrowd product grids on mobile. Keep it breathable (1 or 2 columns max).
- **DON'T**: Use thick borders or heavy shadows. Keep everything delicate.
