# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora Fine Jewelry uses a quiet-luxury editorial aesthetic. The site should feel warm, refined, and premium without looking flashy, discount-led, or overly trend-driven.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body, navigation, UI labels, inputs, and buttons: Inter
- Product names should be uppercase with generous tracking.

## Color palette
Use these named colors consistently through Tailwind theme tokens:
- `ivory`: soft warm background for the overall page
- `sand`: soft panel background and secondary surfaces
- `ink`: primary text and dark hero overlays
- `mocha`: secondary text and borders
- `gold`: premium accent for buttons, highlights, rating stars, and micro accents
- `champagne`: soft highlight surface

## Visual language
- Large editorial imagery with warm lighting
- Generous whitespace and spacious section padding
- Thin hairline dividers and soft shadows
- Subtle transitions on cards, buttons, and navigation
- Rounded corners should be restrained and elegant, not playful

## Component guidance
- Buttons: use solid `gold` with `ink` text or transparent/outlined surfaces with `ivory`/`ink` contrast
- Cards: use ivory or sand surfaces with visible readable text; never rely on inherited foreground colors
- Navigation: transparent over hero, then solid ivory with a subtle border when scrolled
- Trust bar: thin height, uppercase microcopy, letter-spaced
- Product tiles: editorial image-first layout with small typography and premium spacing

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text readable on every background
- Use serif headlines for emotional storytelling and product names
- Keep interactions subtle and smooth
- Favor symmetrical layouts and clean spacing rhythms

## Don't
- Do not use loud sale colors, bright gradients, or discount-store styling
- Do not overcrowd mobile layouts
- Do not use generic blue link styling
- Do not use low-contrast text on beige or gold surfaces
