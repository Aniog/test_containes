# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Base background**: `#F7F4F0` (warm ivory)
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white)
- **Primary text**: `#1A1714` (deep espresso)
- **Secondary text**: `#6E665F` (warm taupe)
- **Muted text**: `#9E9690` (soft gray-brown)
- **Accent**: `#B88A64` (warm gold/bronze)
- **Accent hover**: `#A07652` (deeper bronze)
- **Hairline border**: `#E2DDD8` (warm light gray)
- **Dark section bg**: `#1A1714` (deep espresso)
- **Dark section text**: `#F7F4F0` (warm ivory)

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names**: Cormorant Garamond, serif. Product names: uppercase, letter-spacing wide (`tracking-[0.2em]`)
- **Body / UI**: Inter, sans-serif
- **Hero headline**: `text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl`, font-weight 300, leading tight
- **Section titles**: `text-2xl md:text-3xl`, font-weight 400, tracking wide
- **Body**: `text-sm md:text-base`, font-weight 400, leading-relaxed
- **UI labels / buttons**: `text-xs md:text-sm`, font-weight 500, tracking-wide

## Spacing
- Section vertical padding: `py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-32`
- Content max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Card gap: `gap-4 md:gap-6`
- Hairline dividers: `border-b border-[#E2DDD8]` or `h-px bg-[#E2DDD8]`

## Buttons
- Primary: solid accent `#B88A64`, white text, no border-radius (or `rounded-sm`), uppercase tracking-wide, hover `#A07652`
- Secondary / outline: transparent bg, `1px solid #1A1714`, dark text, hover fill dark
- Ghost: no border, text only with arrow icon

## Shadows
- Card hover: `shadow-lg shadow-stone-900/5`
- Subtle elevation: `shadow-md shadow-stone-900/5`

## Transitions
- Default: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`
- Image hover scale: `scale-105` over `500ms`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use large editorial imagery
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Keep contrast strong for accessibility
- Use uppercase wide-tracked product names

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No discount-style badges (red, starbursts)
- No generic ecommerce clutter
