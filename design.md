# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand
- Name: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- Positioning: Precision-engineered sheet metal folding solutions. Elegant industrial craftsmanship meets modern usability.

## Visual Style
- Elegant yet approachable: refined typography, generous whitespace, premium materials feel.
- Color Palette:
  - Primary Dark: #0F172A (slate-900) — deep, sophisticated
  - Charcoal: #1E2937 (slate-800)
  - Accent Gold: #BFA46F (warm metallic)
  - Accent Teal: #0F766E (refined industrial)
  - Light Background: #F8FAFC (slate-50)
  - Card/Section BG: #FFFFFF
  - Text Primary: #0F172A
  - Text Secondary: #475569 (slate-600)
  - Muted: #94A3B8 (slate-400)
- Typography: Inter (system). Strong hierarchy with 600-700 weights for headings.
- Buttons: Solid dark or gold accent. Subtle hover lift. Clear focus states.
- Cards: Clean white with subtle shadow, elegant borders. Hover: gentle lift + border accent.
- Spacing: Generous padding (py-16/20, px-6/8). Consistent 4/8/12/16 rhythm.
- Radius: 8px for cards, 6px for buttons/inputs.
- Shadows: Subtle (shadow-sm, shadow-md) — never heavy.

## Layout Principles
- Centered max-width container (max-w-7xl) with responsive padding.
- Clear visual sections with subtle dividers or background shifts.
- Mobile-first: stacked layouts, large touch targets, collapsible nav.
- User-friendly: obvious CTAs, descriptive microcopy, no jargon overload.

## Sections (Single Page)
1. Sticky Header (logo + nav + CTA)
2. Hero (bold headline, sub, dual CTAs, elegant hero visual)
3. About Us (craft & precision story)
4. Our Machines (product grid — covers all product keywords elegantly)
5. Engineering Excellence (key features/benefits)
6. Why ARTITECT (trust & support)
7. Request a Quote (contact form)
8. Footer

## Interactions
- Smooth scroll to sections.
- Mobile hamburger menu.
- Product cards with "Request Quote" that can scroll to form and pre-select machine.
- Form: client-side validation + success state.
- Subtle scroll-triggered reveals (optional, keep light).

## Images
- Use data-strk-bg and data-strk-img for professional industrial photography.
- Hero: powerful machinery in elegant workshop.
- Products: close-ups of folding machines, clean metal bends, operator interaction.
- Keep alt text descriptive and benefit-oriented.

## Accessibility & Polish
- Semantic HTML, proper heading hierarchy.
- High contrast text.
- Keyboard focus visible.
- All interactive elements have clear labels.
- Loading states and form feedback are calm and clear.
