# Design Guidelines: ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style: Elegant Industrial

The brand "ARTITECT MACHINERY" represents precision, strength, and high-end engineering. The design should feel robust yet sophisticated.

### Colors
- **Charcoal Black**: `#121212` (Main text, primary background for dark sections)
- **Titanium White**: `#FFFFFF` (Main background)
- **Industrial Steel**: `#E2E8F0` (Borders, accent backgrounds)
- **Precision Blue**: `#1B4D89` (Primary accent, CTAs, highlights)
- **Muted Gold**: `#B59410` (Secondary accent for luxury/premium feel)

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - Headings: Bold (700-800), high letter spacing moderately tight.
  - Body: Regular (400), generous line height (1.6).

### Spacing & Layout
- Large whitespace to emphasize "Elegant" feel.
- Standardized padding: `px-4 md:px-8 lg:px-16`.
- Border radius: `rounded-none` or `rounded-sm` for a sharp, industrial look.

### UI Components
- **Buttons**: Flat with border or solid deep blue. Hover states should be subtle (e.g., slight opacity shift).
- **Cards**: Minimalist with thin borders (`border-slate-200`) and subtle shadows.
- **Navigation**: Clean, sticky header with backdrop-blur.

### Imagery
- High-contrast photography of machinery.
- Detail shots showing precision folding.
- Backgrounds often use subtle industrial textures or clean white space.
