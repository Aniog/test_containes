# SSourcing China - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Tone**: Professional, trustworthy, international B2B
- **Style**: Clean, modern, corporate with subtle Chinese sourcing industry context
- **Audience**: Overseas buyers, procurement managers, business owners

## Color Palette
- **Primary Blue**: #1e40af (trust, professionalism)
- **Primary Dark**: #1e3a8a (headers, navigation)
- **Accent Blue**: #3b82f6 (buttons, links, highlights)
- **Success Green**: #10b981 (positive actions, checkmarks)
- **Warning Amber**: #f59e0b (alerts, important notes)
- **Neutral Gray**: #6b7280 (secondary text)
- **Light Gray**: #f3f4f6 (backgrounds, cards)
- **White**: #ffffff (cards, sections)
- **Dark Text**: #111827 (primary text)
- **Border Gray**: #e5e7eb (borders, dividers)

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, modern, highly readable
- **Headings**: font-semibold to font-bold, tight tracking
- **Body**: font-normal, leading-relaxed for readability
- **Small Text**: text-sm to text-xs for captions and labels

## Spacing System
- **Section Padding**: py-16 to py-24 (desktop), py-12 (mobile)
- **Container Max Width**: max-w-7xl (1280px)
- **Grid Gap**: gap-6 to gap-8
- **Card Padding**: p-6 to p-8

## Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded-lg, clear hover states, primary/secondary variants
- **Cards**: White background, subtle shadow, rounded-xl, border border-gray-100
- **Forms**: Clean inputs with focus rings, clear labels, validation states
- **Navigation**: Sticky header, clear active states, mobile hamburger menu

## Visual Elements
- **Icons**: Lucide React icons, consistent sizing (w-5 h-5 to w-6 h-6)
- **Images**: Professional factory, QC, shipping visuals via stock image system
- **Backgrounds**: Subtle gradients, light gray sections for contrast
- **Borders**: Subtle gray borders, no heavy outlines

## Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: < 768px (single column, stacked layouts)
- **Tablet**: 768px - 1024px (2 columns where appropriate)
- **Desktop**: > 1024px (full multi-column layouts)

## Do's and Don'ts
- **DO**: Use ample white space, clear hierarchy, professional imagery
- **DO**: Keep text concise and scannable with bullet points
- **DO**: Use consistent iconography and spacing
- **DON'T**: Use exaggerated claims or hype language
- **DON'T**: Overcrowd sections with too much content
- **DON'T**: Use low-contrast text or hard-to-read combinations
- **DON'T**: Mix multiple font families
