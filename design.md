# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury. Warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. Never loud, never discount-looking.

## Palette
- Background: warm cream (#faf8f5 → velvet-50)
- Surface: white (#ffffff)
- Foreground: deep espresso (#2d241c → velvet-950)
- Muted text: warm taupe (#846650 → velvet-700)
- Accent: rich gold (#b37d15 → gold-600) — used sparingly for CTAs, highlights
- Accent hover: deeper gold (#8f5b15 → gold-700)
- Border: warm stone (#e6ddd0 → velvet-200)
- Subtle bg: warm ivory (#f3efe8 → velvet-100)

## Typography
- Headings / Product Names: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weight 600
  - Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.1em
  - Hero headlines: large, italic available for emphasis
- Body / UI: Inter (sans-serif), weights 300-700
  - Body text: 400-500
  - UI labels: 500, uppercase with letter-spacing

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card gaps: gap-6 to gap-8
- Page max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8

## Components
- Buttons: accent-filled (gold bg, white text) or outline (transparent, dark border). Uppercase, wide letter-spacing, smooth hover transitions.
- Dividers: thin hairline (1px, velvet-200)
- Cards: white surface, subtle shadow on hover, smooth transitions
- Inputs: clean bordered, focus ring in accent

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep gold accent restrained — CTA buttons only
- Use serif for emotion/headlines, sans for information
- Keep imagery warm and editorial
- Thin, elegant borders

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or aggressive gradients
- No discount badges, sale flags, urgency timers
- No heavy box shadows
- No pure black text (use velvet-950)
- No generic e-commerce tropes
