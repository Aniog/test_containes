# Visual Style Guide - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Brand Identity
Velmora is a high-end, direct-to-consumer demi-fine jewelry brand. The visual identity is "Quiet Luxury" - refined, editorial, and elegant.

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif). Elegant, editorial feel. 
  - Product names in UI: `uppercase tracking-[0.2em] font-medium`
- **Body & UI**: `Inter` or `Manrope` (Sans-serif). Clean, highly legible.
  - Base body: `text-[#1A1A1A] font-light leading-relaxed`

## Color Palette
- **Primary Base**: `#FDFCF8` (Soft Cream/Off-white) - `bg-[#FDFCF8]`
- **Deep Accent/Text**: `#1A1A1A` (Dark Charcoal) - `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Brand Accent (Gold)**: `#C5A059` (Soft Gold/Champagne) - `bg-[#C5A059]` or `text-[#C5A059]`
- **Neutral/Secondary**: `#9A968F` (Warm Stone) - `text-[#9A968F]`
- **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers - `border-[#1A1A1A]/10`

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal (`bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white hover:bg-[#C5A059] transition-all`)
  - Secondary: Outlined (`border border-[#1A1A1A] hover:bg-[#1A1A1A] hover:text-white transition-all`)
- **Cards**: Minimal, generous whitespace, large imagery.
- **Transitions**: Subtle fades and soft hovers (`duration-300 ease-out`).

## DO's & DON'Ts
- **DO**: Use generous whitespace.
- **DO**: Use large, warm-lit editorial photography.
- **DO**: Keep UI elements thin and refined.
- **DON'T**: Use loud, saturated colors.
- **DON'T**: Use heavy shadows or rounded corners (keep it sharp or subtly rounded).
- **DON'T**: Use generic, clunky icons.
