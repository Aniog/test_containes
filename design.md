# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial machinery manufacturing (sheet metal folding machines)
- **Tone**: Elegant, professional, trustworthy, yet user-friendly

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a1a2e` (Deep navy/charcoal - sophistication)
- **Secondary**: `#16213e` (Dark blue - trust)
- **Accent**: `#e94560` (Coral red - action/CTA)
- **Accent Secondary**: `#0f3460` (Medium blue - depth)
- **Light**: `#f8f9fa` (Off-white - clean backgrounds)
- **Text Primary**: `#1a1a2e` (Dark for readability)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6c757d` (Muted gray)
- **Text Light**: `#ffffff` (White for dark backgrounds)
- **Border**: `#e9ecef` (Subtle borders)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: "Outfit" (Google Fonts) - Modern, geometric, professional
- **Headings**: Outfit, weights 600-700
- **Body**: Outfit, weight 400
- **Font Sizes**:
  - Hero Title: 56px (desktop), 36px (mobile)
  - Section Title: 42px (desktop), 28px (mobile)
  - Subtitle: 20px
  - Body: 16px
  - Small: 14px

## Layout Principles
- **Max Width**: 1280px centered
- **Spacing Scale**: 8px base (8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96)
- **Border Radius**: 8px (cards), 4px (buttons), 50% (circles)
- **Shadows**: Subtle, layered shadows for depth

## Visual Style
- Clean, minimalist design with ample white space
- Professional imagery with industrial aesthetic
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Smooth transitions and hover effects
- Card-based layouts for products
- Subtle gradient overlays on hero sections

## Components

### Header/Navigation
- Fixed position, white background with subtle shadow
- Logo on left, navigation links center, CTA button right
- Mobile: Hamburger menu

### Hero Section
- Full-width with dark gradient overlay
- Bold headline with brand name emphasis
- Subheadline describing value proposition
- Primary CTA button (coral accent)
- Secondary CTA for contact

### Products Section
- Grid layout (3 columns desktop, 2 tablet, 1 mobile)
- Product cards with:
  - Image placeholder area
  - Product name
  - Brief description
  - "Learn More" link
- Hover effect: subtle lift and shadow

### Features/Benefits Section
- Icon-based layout
- 3-4 key benefits with icons
- Clean, scannable format

### About Section
- Split layout (text + image placeholder)
- Company story and expertise

### Contact/CTA Section
- Dark background
- Clear call-to-action
- Contact information

### Footer
- Dark background
- Logo, navigation links, contact info
- Copyright

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use ample white space
- DO: Keep text concise and scannable
- DO: Use consistent spacing
- DO: Ensure mobile responsiveness
- DON'T: Use too many colors (stick to palette)
- DON'T: Overcrowd content
- DON'T: Use generic stock photos (use placeholders)
- DON'T: Make it feel cold/impersonal (add warmth through design)
