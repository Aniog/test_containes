# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Color Palette
- Dark background: `#1A1512` (very dark brown-black)
- Warm cream/ivory: `#F5F0EB`
- Gold accent: `#C9A96E`
- Dark text: `#2C2420`
- Light text: `#F5F0EB`
- Muted text: `#8C8279`
- Border: `#E5DDD5`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- **Headings (serif)**: Cormorant Garamond — for all headings, product names, logo
- **Body (sans-serif)**: Inter — for body text, UI, buttons
- Product names: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace (py-16 to py-24 for sections)
- Max-width: 1280px container
- Thin hairline dividers (border-t border-[#E5DDD5])
- Cards: clean, minimal, soft shadows (shadow-sm)

## Buttons
- Primary: gold accent bg (`#C9A96E`), dark text, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Outline: transparent bg, gold border, gold text
- Hover: subtle darkening/slight lift

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions (200ms ease)
- Soft shadow on hover
- Fade-in on scroll (optional)
- Smooth cart drawer slide

## Visual Style
- Editorial, quiet luxury feel
- Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Large product imagery
- Refined, not flashy
- Thin borders, minimal ornamentation
- Stars: gold color
