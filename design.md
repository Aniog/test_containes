# Strikingly AI Generators Hub Visual System

Use a clean Strikingly-style marketing page with generous spacing, white surfaces, light gray dividers, and restrained purple accents.

## Typography
- Headings and buttons use Brandon Grotesque when available, with Josefin Sans as the free fallback. Use uppercase, weight 700, and tight geometric letterforms.
- Body text uses Open Sans at 14px with 1.5 line-height.

## Colors
- Brand purple: `#8159BB` for accents, badges, line icons, borders, and ghost button text.
- AI gradient: `linear-gradient(90deg, #7671FF 0%, #CB0C9F 100%)` only for primary CTAs and the second H1 line.
- Body text: `#636972`; section headings: `#4B5056`; hero dark heading: `#2E2E2F`.
- Card border: `#C6C9CD`; dividers: `#E2E4E7`; light section background: `#F4F6F8`; default background: white.

## Components
- Cards are white with a 1px card border, 3px radius, and 20px padding. Hover uses a purple border and subtle shadow only.
- Buttons are uppercase, 4px radius, 36px high by default and 44px for hero/closing CTAs. Filled buttons always use white text.
- Category tags are ghost style: purple text, purple 1px border, no fill.

## Spacing
- Use 20px between related blocks, 40px between major sections, and 60-80px in the hero.
- Keep content centered in a 1200px max-width container with 20px side padding.

## Do / Don't
- Do keep text contrast high and explicit on cards, buttons, and badges.
- Do use inline SVG line art for illustration.
- Do not use gradient backgrounds outside primary CTAs and the H1 gradient line.
- Do not use fake testimonials, ratings, or placeholder `href="#"` links.
