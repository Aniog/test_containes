# Design System — GameVault

## Brand Identity
A dark-themed gaming hub with vibrant accent colors. The aesthetic is modern, bold, and immersive — inspired by gaming UIs.

## Color Palette
- Background primary: `#0f0f13` (near-black) — `bg-[#0f0f13]`
- Background secondary: `#1a1a24` (dark card) — `bg-[#1a1a24]`
- Background tertiary: `#22222f` (elevated card) — `bg-[#22222f]`
- Accent primary: `#7c3aed` (violet) — `bg-violet-600`, `text-violet-400`
- Accent secondary: `#06b6d4` (cyan) — `text-cyan-400`, `bg-cyan-500`
- Accent danger/sale: `#ef4444` (red) — `text-red-400`, `bg-red-500`
- Accent success: `#22c55e` (green) — `text-green-400`
- Text primary: `#f1f5f9` — `text-slate-100`
- Text secondary: `#94a3b8` — `text-slate-400`
- Text muted: `#64748b` — `text-slate-500`
- Border: `#2d2d3d` — `border-[#2d2d3d]`

## Platform Colors
- Steam: `#1b2838` bg, `#66c0f4` accent
- Epic: `#2d2d2d` bg, `#ffffff` accent
- Nintendo: `#e4000f` accent
- PlayStation: `#003087` bg, `#00439c` accent
- Xbox: `#107c10` accent

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: `font-bold` or `font-extrabold`, `tracking-tight`
- Body: `font-normal`, `leading-relaxed`
- Labels/badges: `font-semibold`, `text-xs` or `text-sm`, `uppercase tracking-wider`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 px-4` or `py-12 px-6`
- Card padding: `p-4` or `p-6`
- Gap between cards: `gap-4` or `gap-6`

## Borders & Radius
- Cards: `rounded-xl` with `border border-[#2d2d3d]`
- Buttons: `rounded-lg`
- Badges: `rounded-full`
- Images: `rounded-lg` or `rounded-xl`

## Shadows
- Cards: `shadow-lg shadow-black/40`
- Hover: `hover:shadow-violet-500/20`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-violet-600 hover:bg-violet-700 text-white font-semibold rounded-lg px-5 py-2.5`
- Secondary: `bg-[#22222f] hover:bg-[#2d2d3d] text-slate-100 border border-[#2d2d3d] rounded-lg px-5 py-2.5`
- Danger/Sale: `bg-red-500 hover:bg-red-600 text-white font-semibold rounded-lg`

## Cards
- Background: `bg-[#1a1a24]` or `bg-[#22222f]`
- Border: `border border-[#2d2d3d]`
- Hover: `hover:border-violet-500/50 transition-all duration-200`
- Radius: `rounded-xl`

## Navigation
- Background: `bg-[#0f0f13]/95 backdrop-blur-md`
- Border bottom: `border-b border-[#2d2d3d]`
- Active link: `text-violet-400`
- Inactive link: `text-slate-400 hover:text-slate-100`

## Badges / Tags
- Platform badge: colored pill with platform color
- Sale badge: `bg-red-500 text-white text-xs font-bold px-2 py-0.5 rounded-full`
- Category badge: `bg-violet-600/20 text-violet-400 text-xs font-semibold px-2.5 py-1 rounded-full`

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds throughout for immersive gaming feel
- Use violet as the primary brand accent
- Use cyan for secondary highlights and interactive elements
- Use red for sale/discount callouts
- Ensure all text has strong contrast against dark backgrounds
- Use subtle gradients on hero sections: `from-violet-900/30 to-cyan-900/20`

## Don'ts
- Never use white backgrounds
- Never use light gray text on dark backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Don't use more than 3 accent colors in one section
- Don't use serif fonts
