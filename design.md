# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Ink / Primary BG**: `#1B1917` — deep warm charcoal (main dark background)
- **Parchment**: `#FAF7F2` — warm ivory (light background, cards)
- **Sand**: `#F0EBE3` — soft warm neutral (alternate sections)
- **Gold Accent**: `#C6A962` — muted antique gold (CTAs, accents, highlights)
- **Gold Hover**: `#B89A4F` — deeper gold for hover states
- **Warm Gray**: `#8A8279` — secondary text on light backgrounds
- **Slate**: `#4A4540` — body text on light backgrounds
- **Cream**: `#E8E0D4` — borders, dividers

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
  - Product names: uppercase, letter-spacing: 0.15em
  - Section titles: 400–500 weight, large sizes
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
  - 400 weight body, 500 weight for buttons/labels
- **Accent / Prices**: Cormorant Garamond 500 weight

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace — sections padded 80px+ vertically on desktop, 48px on mobile
- Content max-width: 1280px
- Card gaps: 24px desktop, 16px mobile

## Buttons
- Primary: solid Gold background, Ink text, uppercase, letter-spacing, no border-radius (or 2px)
- Secondary: outlined Gold border, transparent bg, Gold text
- Hover: darken gold by 10%, subtle lift shadow
- Transition: 200ms ease

## Cards / Surfaces
- Product cards: Parchment bg, subtle shadow on hover, no harsh borders
- Hairline dividers: 1px Cream borders
- Rounded corners: 0–4px (minimal)

## Images
- Warm, editorial style
- Dark/neutral backgrounds for product shots
- Lifestyle images with warm golden lighting

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Maintain warm, muted color palette
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Subtle hover transitions (200–300ms)

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or saturated colors
- Don't use heavy shadows or gradients
- Don't use rounded corners larger than 4px
- Don't mix too many font weights
- Don't use discount/sale styling
