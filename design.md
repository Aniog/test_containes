# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background (light)**: `#FAF8F5` - warm cream/off-white
- **Background (dark)**: `#1A1714` - deep warm charcoal
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` - pure white for cards
- **Text primary**: `#1A1714` - deep charcoal
- **Text secondary**: `#6B6560` - warm muted brown-gray
- **Text on dark**: `#FAF8F5` - warm cream
- **Accent (gold)**: `#C9A96E` - warm metallic gold
- **Accent hover**: `#B8944F` - deeper gold
- **Border**: `#E8E4DF` - soft warm gray
- **Border light**: `#F0EDE8` - very light warm gray
- **Star rating**: `#C9A96E` - gold
- **Success**: `#4A7C59` - muted green
- **Error**: `#8B3A3A` - muted red

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- **Font weights**: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8

## Components
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary: solid gold background, dark text, rounded-none (sharp edges for luxury feel)
  - Secondary: outlined with gold border, transparent bg
  - Hover: subtle opacity change or slight scale
- **Cards**: white bg, subtle shadow on hover, thin border
- **Dividers**: hairline (border-t border-[#E8E4DF])
- **Inputs**: minimal border, warm gray, focus ring in gold

## Animations
- Subtle fade-in on scroll
- Smooth hover transitions (duration-300 ease-out)
- Cart drawer slide-in from right
- Image hover: slight scale (scale-105)

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use gold accent sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Maintain consistent warm color palette
- Use uppercase with wide letter-spacing for product names

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No discount badges or sale-looking elements
- No rounded corners on primary buttons (sharp = luxury)
- No heavy shadows or gradients
- No loud animations or transitions
