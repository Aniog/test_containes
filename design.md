# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guide

## Brand Overview
ARTITECT MACHINERY is an industrial machinery company specializing in sheet metal folding machines. The brand identity balances precision engineering with elegant sophistication.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif) - for elegance and premium feel
  - Hero title: font-display, text-5xl to text-7xl, font-bold, tracking-tight
  - Section headings: font-display, text-3xl to text-4xl, font-semibold
  - Sub-headings: font-display, text-xl to text-2xl
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) - for readability and modern feel
  - Body text: font-body, text-base to text-lg, text-brand-700
  - Small/text: font-body, text-sm, text-brand-500

## Colors
- **Primary (Deep Navy)**: #1a365d (primary DEFAULT) - main brand color for headers, navigation
  - Light: #2b4f8e (primary-light) - hover states, links
  - Dark: #0f1f3d (primary-dark) - footer backgrounds
- **Accent (Gold)**: #c9a84c (accent DEFAULT) - CTAs, highlights, decorative elements
  - Light: #dfc06e (accent-light) - hover states
  - Dark: #a88830 (accent-dark) - active states
- **Neutral Scale** (brand):
  - 50: #f0f4f8 - page background
  - 100: #d9e2ec - card backgrounds, sections
  - 200: #bcccdc - borders
  - 500: #627d98 - secondary text
  - 700: #334e68 - body text
  - 800: #243b53 - headings
  - 900: #102a43 - darkest text

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between elements: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-lg, shadow-sm, border border-brand-100
- Cards hover: shadow-md, border-brand-200
- Buttons: rounded-md
- Input fields: rounded-md, border-brand-200

## Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: bg-accent hover:bg-accent-light text-white font-medium py-3 px-8 rounded-md
- **Secondary CTA**: bg-white/10 hover:bg-white/20 text-white border border-white/30 backdrop-blur-sm
- **Outline**: border-2 border-primary text-primary hover:bg-primary hover:text-white

## Visual Style DOs
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Professional dark navy headers with subtle gold accents
- High-quality product imagery
- Smooth hover transitions (transition-all duration-300)
- Use brand-50 for alternate section backgrounds
- Gold accent color for important CTAs and highlights
- Playfair Display for impactful headings
- Subtle shadows for depth

## Visual Style DON'Ts
- No cluttered layouts
- No bright/neon colors
- No multiple accent colors (stick to gold)
- No overly large/bloated typography
- No harsh borders or backgrounds
