# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand DNA
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#f8f6f3` (warm off-white/ivory) — sitewide page background
- Surface / Card: `#ffffff` — cards, sheets, dropdowns
- Text primary: `#1a1412` (deep espresso) — headings, body
- Text secondary: `#6b6561` (warm gray) — meta, captions
- Accent / Gold: `#c9a96e` (antique gold) — CTAs, accents, hover states
- Accent hover: `#b8944f` (darker gold)
- Border / Hairline: `#e5ddd6` (warm beige) — dividers, thin borders
- Light accent tint: `#f5f0ea` — newsletter block, subtle highlights
- Star / positive: `#c9a96e` (gold stars)
- White: `#ffffff` — text on dark, badge text

## Typography
- Heading font (serif): 'Cormorant Garamond', serif — all headings, product names, big quotes
- Body font (sans): 'Inter', sans-serif — body copy, UI, buttons
- Logo: 'Cormorant Garamond' serif, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Product names: uppercase, tracking-[0.15em] (wide letter-spacing)
- Section headings: uppercase, tracking-[0.08em]

## Spacing & Layout
- Page max-width: 1440px, centered
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Whitespace: generous, breathe
- Grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Card padding: p-6

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#e5ddd6] (thin, warm beige)
- Card border: border border-[#e5ddd6]/60
- No heavy borders or thick lines

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#c9a96e] text-white, hover:bg-[#b8944f], rounded-none, uppercase, tracking-widest, text-sm, px-8 py-4
- Outline: border border-[#c9a96e] text-[#c9a96e] hover:bg-[#c9a96e] hover:text-white, rounded-none, uppercase, tracking-widest, text-sm

## Hover & Transitions
- All interactive: transition-all duration-300 ease-out
- Images: hover:scale-[1.02] subtle zoom
- Cards: hover:shadow-lg hover:shadow-black/5
- Links: underline decoration-[#c9a96e] decoration-1 underline-offset-4 hover:decoration-2

## Shadows
- Soft: shadow-sm shadow-black/5
- Card hover: shadow-lg shadow-black/8

## Product Cards
- Image: aspect-[3/4], object-cover
- Name: uppercase, tracking-[0.15em], text-xs md:text-sm
- Price: text-[#c9a96e], font-medium

## Iconography
- Lucide React icons, stroke-width 1.5
- Color: text-[#1a1412] or text-[#6b6561] for secondary
