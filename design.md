# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette

### Base
- `ivory`: `#FAF7F2` — page background, warm off-white
- `ivory-dark`: `#F2EDE4` — subtle section backgrounds, card hover
- `charcoal`: `#1A1714` — primary text, nav, headings
- `charcoal-light`: `#2E2A26` — secondary dark surfaces

### Accent
- `gold`: `#C9A96E` — primary accent, CTAs, borders, icons
- `gold-light`: `#E8D5B0` — hover states, subtle highlights
- `gold-dark`: `#A8854A` — pressed states, deep accents

### Neutral
- `stone`: `#8C8279` — secondary text, captions, muted labels
- `stone-light`: `#C4BAB2` — dividers, hairlines, borders
- `stone-pale`: `#EDE8E2` — card backgrounds, input fills

## Typography

### Fonts
- Headings / Product names: `Cormorant Garamond` (serif) — elegant, editorial
- Body / UI: `Inter` (sans-serif) — clean, readable

### Scale
- Display: `text-5xl` to `text-7xl`, `font-light`, `tracking-wide`, Cormorant
- H1: `text-4xl`, `font-light`, Cormorant
- H2: `text-3xl`, `font-light`, Cormorant
- H3: `text-xl`, `font-light`, Cormorant
- Product name: `text-sm` to `text-base`, `uppercase`, `tracking-widest`, Cormorant
- Body: `text-sm`, Inter, `text-stone`
- Caption: `text-xs`, Inter, `text-stone`
- Button: `text-xs`, `uppercase`, `tracking-widest`, Inter

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20` to `py-28`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 lg:px-12`
- Card gap: `gap-6` to `gap-8`
- Generous whitespace is a design feature

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: `border-stone-light` (`1px solid #C4BAB2`)
- Card borders: `border border-stone-pale`
- Accent borders: `border-gold`

## Shadows
- Subtle card shadow: `shadow-sm` with warm tint
- Hover elevation: `shadow-md`
- No harsh drop shadows

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-charcoal text-ivory` — solid dark, uppercase, wide tracking
- Accent: `bg-gold text-charcoal` — warm gold fill
- Outlined: `border border-charcoal text-charcoal` — ghost style
- All buttons: `text-xs uppercase tracking-widest px-8 py-3 transition-all duration-300`

## Animations
- Hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`
- Image zoom on hover: `scale-105`
- Fade-in: `opacity-0 → opacity-100`
- No bouncy or playful animations — everything is smooth and restrained

## Do's
- Use Cormorant Garamond for all headings and product names
- Use UPPERCASE + wide letter-spacing for product names and labels
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Use warm ivory backgrounds, not pure white
- Use hairline dividers (1px) not thick borders
- Keep the gold accent restrained — use it for CTAs and key accents only

## Don'ts
- No pure white (#FFFFFF) backgrounds — use ivory (#FAF7F2)
- No pure black — use charcoal (#1A1714)
- No bright/saturated colors
- No rounded pill buttons (use sharp or very slightly rounded: `rounded-none` or `rounded-sm`)
- No generic e-commerce blue links
- No heavy drop shadows
- No Comic Sans, Roboto, or generic fonts
