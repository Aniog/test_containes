# ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style

*   **Colors**:
    *   Primary: Deep, industrial blue/navy (`#1E3A8A` / `blue-900`) conveying trust and engineering precision.
    *   Secondary: Metallic silver/gray (`#9CA3AF` / `gray-400`) representing the machinery.
    *   Accent: High-visibility orange (`#F97316` / `orange-500`) for call-to-action buttons.
    *   Background: Clean white (`#FFFFFF`) or very light off-white (`#F8FAFC` / `slate-50`) to keep the feel elegant.
    *   Text: Dark gray (`#1F2937` / `gray-800`) for readability, with lighter grays for secondary text.
*   **Typography**:
    *   Sans-serif fonts: Clean, modern, legibile (e.g., system font stack, or a web font like Inter or Roboto). Clean lines to emulate machinery.
*   **Layout**:
    *   Spacious and structured. Grid layouts for products to show precision.
    *   Card-based designs for product categories, with subtle borders.
*   **Component Styling**:
    *   Buttons: Slightly rounded corners, solid colors with a subtle hover effect (e.g., slight darkening or shadow).
    *   Navigation: Sticky top navbar.
    *   Footer: Dark background with lighter text, structured into columns.
*   **Do's**:
    *   Use generous whitespace to create an elegant feel despite the heavy machinery subject.
    *   Ensure high contrast for text over backgrounds.
*   **Don'ts**:
    *   Clutter the pages with too much text or cramped elements.
    *   Use overly bright or neon colors as the main background.
