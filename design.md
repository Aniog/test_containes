# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Specification

## Visual Style
- **Theme**: Industrial elegance meets modern minimalism
- **Feel**: Professional, trustworthy, powerful yet approachable

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Navy (#1a1f2e) - authority and professionalism
- **Secondary**: Steel Gray (#3d4f5f) - industrial authenticity
- **Accent**: Warm Gold (#c9a961) - premium quality and precision
- **Background Light**: Off-White (#f8f9fa) - clean and approachable
- **Background Dark**: Charcoal (#0d1117) - dramatic sections
- **Text Primary**: Dark (#1a1f2e)
- **Text Secondary**: Muted Gray (#6b7280)
- **Text Light**: White (#ffffff)

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, modern, highly readable
- **Headings**: Bold/Semibold, tracking-tight
- **Body**: Regular weight, relaxed line-height

## Design Principles
- Generous whitespace for elegance
- Subtle shadows and borders for depth
- Smooth transitions and hover states
- High contrast for accessibility
- Mobile-responsive layouts

## Component Patterns
- Rounded corners (rounded-xl, rounded-2xl)
- Soft shadows (shadow-lg, shadow-xl)
- Gradient backgrounds for hero sections
- Card-based layouts for products
- Clean navigation with clear CTAs
