# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial Machinery / Metal Folding Equipment
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly — premium industrial feel with approachable design

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) — clean, modern, professional
- **Headings**: Inter 700-800 weight, tracking tight
- **Body**: Inter 400 weight, leading relaxed
- **Accent/Label**: Inter 500-600 weight, uppercase tracking wide for labels

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Navy `#0F172A` — authority, trust, industrial strength
- **Primary Accent**: Steel Blue `#3B82F6` — modern, technical, trustworthy
- **Secondary Accent**: Amber/Gold `#D97706` — premium, warmth, craftsmanship
- **Background**: Off-White `#F8FAFC` — clean, spacious, elegant
- **Surface**: White `#FFFFFF` — cards, panels
- **Text Primary**: Slate 900 `#0F172A`
- **Text Secondary**: Slate 500 `#64748B`
- **Border**: Slate 200 `#E2E8F0`
- **Success**: Emerald `#059669`
- **Dark Section BG**: Slate 900 `#0F172A` with white text

## Spacing & Layout
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6 md:gap-8`
- Generous whitespace — let the content breathe

## Visual Style
- Clean lines and geometric shapes reflecting precision engineering
- Subtle shadows: `shadow-sm` to `shadow-lg` — never harsh
- Rounded corners: `rounded-lg` to `rounded-2xl` — approachable but not playful
- Borders: thin, subtle — `border border-slate-200`
- Hover effects: smooth transitions, slight lifts (`hover:shadow-md hover:-translate-y-0.5`)
- Dark sections for contrast and visual drama
- Full-width hero with background imagery

## Component Patterns
- **Buttons**: Solid primary (navy/blue), outline secondary, amber CTA
- **Cards**: White surface, subtle border, hover lift effect
- **Navigation**: Clean, minimal, sticky header with transparent-to-solid scroll
- **Sections**: Alternating light/dark for visual rhythm
- **Icons**: Lucide React — line-style, consistent sizing

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography clean and hierarchical
- Use amber/gold sparingly for emphasis and CTAs
- Maintain consistent spacing rhythm
- Use subtle transitions and hover effects
- Ensure high contrast for readability

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much content
- Don't use playful or casual design elements
- Don't use heavy drop shadows
- Don't mix too many font weights
- Don't use low-contrast text on any background
