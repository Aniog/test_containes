# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- `cream`: #FAF7F2 — main page background, warm ivory
- `parchment`: #F0EAE0 — section alternates, card backgrounds
- `charcoal`: #1C1917 — primary text, dark backgrounds
- `stone`: #6B6560 — secondary/muted text
- `gold`: #B8935A — primary accent (CTAs, highlights, borders)
- `gold-light`: #D4AF7A — hover states, lighter gold
- `gold-pale`: #F0E4CC — very light gold tint for backgrounds
- `white`: #FFFFFF

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- Product names: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`)
- Section headings: sentence case or title case, generous line-height

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: sections use `py-20` to `py-32`
- Cards: `p-6` to `p-8`
- Consistent `gap-6` to `gap-8` in grids

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: `border-gold/20` or `border-stone/20`
- Card borders: `border border-gold/15`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-none` for editorial feel, `rounded-sm` for buttons

## Shadows
- Soft, warm: `shadow-[0_4px_24px_rgba(184,147,90,0.08)]`
- Hover: `shadow-[0_8px_32px_rgba(184,147,90,0.15)]`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-charcoal text-cream hover:bg-gold` — solid, premium
- Outlined: `border border-charcoal text-charcoal hover:bg-charcoal hover:text-cream`
- Accent: `bg-gold text-white hover:bg-gold-light`
- All buttons: `tracking-widest text-xs uppercase font-medium px-8 py-3`

## Animations
- Transitions: `transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out`
- Hover image scale: `hover:scale-105`
- Fade-in on scroll: subtle opacity transitions

## Do's
- Use Cormorant Garamond for all headings and product names
- Keep backgrounds warm (cream, parchment) — never cold white or grey
- Use gold sparingly as an accent, not everywhere
- Large editorial imagery with generous whitespace
- Thin hairline borders for structure

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded pill buttons (keep them rectangular/slightly rounded)
- No cluttered layouts
- No cold blue/grey tones
