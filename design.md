# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#F7F5F1` — warm off-white / cream
- **Foreground**: `#1A1816` — deep warm charcoal (almost black)
- **Muted**: `#9B958D` — warm stone gray
- **Accent**: `#A67B5B` — warm bronze / antique gold
- **Accent hover**: `#8C6649` — deeper bronze
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` — pure white for cards
- **Surface muted**: `#EDEAE4` — warm light gray for alternating sections
- **Border**: `#D9D5CE` — warm hairline divider
- **Success**: `#5A7D5A` — sage green
- **Star**: `#C8A45C` — gold star rating

## Typography
- **Headings / Serif**: `Cormorant Garamond`, weight 300–600
- **Body / UI**: `Inter`, weight 300–600
- Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing `0.12em`

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace. Section padding: `80px–120px` desktop, `48px–64px` mobile
- Hairline dividers: `1px solid #D9D5CE`

## Shadows
- Card hover: `0 8px 32px rgba(26, 24, 22, 0.08)`
- Subtle: `0 2px 8px rgba(26, 24, 22, 0.04)`

## Buttons
- Primary: solid accent `#A67B5B`, white text, no radius (square)
- Secondary: transparent with `1px solid #1A1816`, dark text, no radius
- Hover: slight darken, transition `0.25s ease`

## Transitions
- Hover image swap: `0.4s ease`
- Button: `0.25s ease`
- Link underline: `0.3s ease`
