# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial direction: warm ivory surfaces, refined espresso contrast, and restrained antique-gold accents that flatter demi-fine jewelry without feeling loud or discount-driven.

## Palette
- `velmora-ivory` (`#F8F3EA`): primary page background. Use with `text-velmora-espresso`.
- `velmora-cream` (`#FFFDF8`): card and form surfaces. Use with `text-velmora-espresso`.
- `velmora-champagne` (`#E7D6BD`): soft panels, trust strips, secondary sections. Use with `text-velmora-espresso`.
- `velmora-gold` (`#B8894B`): primary accent, CTA background, star color, fine details. Use with `text-velmora-cream` for solid buttons.
- `velmora-amber` (`#D4A866`): hover accent and subtle highlights. Use sparingly.
- `velmora-espresso` (`#211713`): main text, dark editorial backgrounds, footer. Use with `text-velmora-ivory`.
- `velmora-mocha` (`#5A4438`): muted body copy and borders on light backgrounds.
- `velmora-sage` (`#8B8A74`): restrained secondary accent for badges and small UI labels.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, high contrast, generous line height.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names: uppercase with wide letter spacing (`uppercase tracking-[0.22em]`) and serif styling.

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first with spacious vertical rhythm.
- Use generous section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`.
- Desktop content max width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 sm:px-8 lg:px-10`.
- Thin hairline dividers: `border-velmora-gold/20` or `border-velmora-espresso/10`.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, restrained, no harsh shadows. Primary buttons use `bg-velmora-gold text-velmora-cream hover:bg-velmora-espresso`; secondary buttons use hairline borders and transparent backgrounds.
- Cards: soft cream surfaces with thin borders, subtle shadow on hover only.
- Nav: transparent over hero, solid ivory/cream with subtle border after scroll.
- Forms: cream or ivory backgrounds, espresso text, mocha placeholder text, gold focus ring.

## Imagery
- Use warm editorial jewelry imagery with the Strikingly stock image attributes.
- Prefer close-up gold jewelry on model, neutral fabric, gift boxes, ear and neck detail shots.
- Keep overlays dark enough for strong text contrast.

## Do's
- Keep whitespace generous.
- Use accent color intentionally and consistently.
- Make all text explicitly readable against its surface.
- Use slow, subtle hover transitions.

## Don'ts
- Do not use loud sale colors, discount badges, neon accents, or generic marketplace styling.
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-beige text.
- Do not overcrowd mobile layouts.
