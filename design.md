# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
ARTITECT MACHINERY produces premium sheet metal folding machines. The brand voice is confident, precise, and refined — blending industrial strength with sophisticated elegance.

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif, elegant)
- Body: Inter (sans-serif, clean, readable)
- Font weights: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold)

## Colors
- Primary Dark:    #1a2332  (deep navy/charcoal — headers, footer, nav)
- Primary Accent:  #c8963e  (warm gold — CTAs, highlights, links)
- Light BG:        #f8f7f4  (warm off-white — page background)
- Surface:         #ffffff  (white — cards, sections)
- Text Primary:    #2d2d2d  (dark charcoal)
- Text Secondary:  #6b7280  (medium gray — subtext)
- Steel Blue:      #5b7b9a  (subtle accents, icons)

## Spacing & Layout
- Sections: generous vertical padding (py-20 to py-32)
- Container: max-w-7xl centered
- Cards: rounded-lg with subtle shadow (shadow-md)

## Do's
- Use ample white space for breathing room
- Keep navigation clean and minimal
- Use gold accent sparingly for emphasis
- Maintain strong contrast between text and backgrounds
- Use consistent border-radius (rounded-lg)

## Don'ts
- Don't overcrowd sections
- Don't use bright, harsh colors
- Don't use tiny fonts (body min 16px)
- Don't use dark backgrounds for body text areas
