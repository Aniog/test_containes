# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Quiet luxury with a warm editorial finish. The storefront should feel intimate, polished, and premium without looking flashy, trend-chasing, or discount-driven.

## Core palette
Use these named colors consistently throughout the experience.

- `velmora-ivory` — `#F8F4EF` — primary page background
- `velmora-rose` — `#E7DDD1` — soft panels and secondary surfaces
- `velmora-sand` — `#D8C6B2` — borders, dividers, subtle fills
- `velmora-gold` — `#B99267` — accent color for buttons and highlights
- `velmora-gold-deep` — `#8F6A45` — hover and pressed accent tone
- `velmora-mocha` — `#6D5A4D` — muted text
- `velmora-noir` — `#27201D` — dark section surfaces
- `velmora-ink` — `#1E1A1A` — primary foreground text

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `Cormorant Garamond`, elegant serif, refined scale, generous line-height
- Body and UI: `Inter`, clean sans-serif
- Product names: uppercase with wide letter-spacing

## Layout and spacing
- Generous whitespace on desktop and mobile
- Large editorial image blocks with rounded corners only where appropriate
- Thin hairline dividers using `velmora-sand`
- Cards should feel airy, never crowded
- Prefer max width containers with strong vertical rhythm

## Components
- Buttons: premium, understated; use solid `velmora-gold` for primary CTAs and outlined ivory/noir treatments for secondary actions
- Surfaces: soft shadows, subtle borders, and layered translucent headers where useful
- Ratings, badges, and utility text: restrained and readable
- Cart drawer: warm ivory surface with elegant product summary rows

## Motion
- Use subtle transitions only: opacity, transform, shadow
- Hover states should feel smooth and soft, not springy or playful

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text legible on every surface
- Use warm gold accents sparingly
- Let imagery and typography carry the premium feel
- Maintain a consistent editorial tone across all pages

## Don’t
- Do not use neon, overly saturated accent colors, or heavy gradients
- Do not use discount-style badges or loud sale treatments
- Do not overcrowd cards, grids, or navigation
- Do not rely on low-contrast text on warm neutral backgrounds
