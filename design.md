# SSourcing China - Design System

## Brand Identity
Professional B2B sourcing agent website. Clean, trustworthy, international.

## Colors
- **Primary (Navy):** `navy-900` (#0f2a4a), `navy-800` (#1a3a5c), `navy-700` (#1e4d7b)
- **Accent (Orange):** `accent-500` (#e86c2b), `accent-600` (#d45a1a) — used for CTAs
- **Neutral:** `slate-50` (#f8fafc), `slate-100` (#f1f5f9), `slate-200` (#e2e8f0), `slate-600` (#475569), `slate-800` (#1e293b), `slate-900` (#0f172a)
- **Success:** `emerald-600` (#059669)
- **Background:** White `#ffffff` for content, `slate-50` for alternating sections

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: `font-bold`, sizes from `text-4xl` (h1) to `text-xl` (h3)
- Body: `text-base` (16px), `text-slate-600` on light backgrounds
- Small: `text-sm`, `text-slate-500`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Components
- Buttons: Rounded `rounded-lg`, primary uses `bg-accent-500 hover:bg-accent-600 text-white`, secondary uses `border-2 border-navy-800 text-navy-800 hover:bg-navy-800 hover:text-white`
- Cards: `bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-200 p-6`
- Sections alternate between `bg-white` and `bg-slate-50`

## Do's
- Use plenty of whitespace
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use icons (Lucide) to support text
- Use stock images for factory/QC/shipping visuals
- Maintain consistent section rhythm

## Don'ts
- No dark mode (B2B audience prefers light)
- No exaggerated claims or flashy animations
- No cluttered layouts
- No low-contrast text
