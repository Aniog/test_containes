# Design System: ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style
The brand "ARTITECT MACHINERY" combines technical engineering precision with architectural elegance. The design should feel premium, reliable, and sophisticated.

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Playfair Display', serif. Elegant and authoritative.
- **Body/UI**: 'Inter', sans-serif. Modern, highly readable, and professional.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Deep Charcoal)**: `#1A1A1A` - Base for dark sections and text.
- **Secondary (Burnished Gold)**: `#C5A059` - Use for accents, buttons, and highlighting quality.
- **Accent (Industrial Steel)**: `#4A5568` - For borders and secondary text.
- **Background (Soft Ivory)**: `#FAFAFA` - Main background for content sections.
- **Foreground (White)**: `#FFFFFF` - For card backgrounds and contrast.

## UI Principles
- **Generous Whitespace**: High-end industrial brands use space to convey quality.
- **Clean Lines**: Sharp borders (1px) with subtle shadows or no shadows at all.
- **High-Quality Imagery**: Focus on close-ups of machinery details and industrial installations.
- **Responsive Layouts**: Multi-column for desktop, single stacking for mobile.

## Tailwind Configuration (Customizations)
Define these in `src/index.css`:
- `--primary`: 0 0% 10% (#1A1A1A)
- `--secondary`: 40 48% 56% (#C5A059)
- `--accent`: 210 10% 35% (#4A5568)
- `--background`: 0 0% 98% (#FAFAFA)
