# VELMORA Visual Identity

## Overview
VELMORA is a high-end DTC jewelry brand conveying "quiet luxury". The design is warm, editorial, and premium.

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: `font-serif` (Cormorant Garamond). Product names use uppercase with tracking-widest (`tracking-[0.4em]`).
- **Body & UI**: `font-sans` (Inter). Clean and highly legible.

## Palette
- **Primary/Background**: `#FFFFFF` (White)
- **Secondary/Text**: `#242424` (Deep Stone/Charcoal)
- **Accent**: `#C5A059` (Warm Gold / Metallic accent)
- **Muted**: `#A8A29E` (Stone 400)
- **Surface**: `#F5F5F4` (Stone 100)

## UI Elements
- **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers (`border-stone-100`).
- **Buttons**:
  - `btn-premium`: Solid or outlined with high letter-spacing and uppercase text.
- **Imagery**: Large editorial shots with soft lighting. 4:3 and 4:5 ratios preferred for products.
- **Whitespace**: Generous padding and margins between sections.

## Transitions
- Soft scale and opacity transitions on hover.
- Fade-in animations for page loads.
