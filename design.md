# Visual Style Guide - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Concept
"Quiet luxury, warm, editorial." A premium but accessible feel focusing on 18K gold-plated jewelry.

## Colors
- **Background (Cream)**: \`#FAF9F6\` (Tailwind: \`bg-[#FAF9F6]\`)
- **Foreground (Deep Charcoal)**: \`#1A1A1A\` (Tailwind: \`text-[#1A1A1A]\`)
- **Accent (Warm Gold)**: \`#C5A059\` (Tailwind: \`text-[#C5A059]\` or \`bg-[#C5A059]\`)
- **Muted (Soft Taupe)**: \`#E5E0D8\` (Tailwind: \`bg-[#E5E0D8]\`)
- **Border (Hairline)**: \`rgba(26, 26, 26, 0.1)\` (Tailwind: \`border-[#1A1A1A]/10\`)

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Serif (Cormorant Garamond).
  - Product names: Uppercase, tracking (letter-spacing) 0.25em.
- **Body / UI**: Sans-serif (Inter).
  - Clean, readable, slightly compressed line-height for a tidy look.

## Layout & Components
- **Whitespace**: Generous, providing an "editorial" breathing space.
- **Imagery**: Large, high-quality close-ups with warm lighting.
- **Dividers**: 1px hairline dividers for subtle separation.
- **Transitions**: Soft fade and scale effects on hover.
- **Shadows**: Very soft, minimal depth.

## Do's
- Use uppercase for navigational elements and product titles.
- Keep UI elements minimal to let imagery shine.
- Use warm, model-led imagery.

## Don'ts
- Use neon or harsh primary colors.
- Overcrowd sections with too much text.
- Use rounded buttons with huge radiuses; prefer square or slightly rounded (2-4px).
