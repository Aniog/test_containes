# 没接墨世界杯 Design System

## Theme
Dark, dramatic football/soccer tournament aesthetic. Deep navy and black backgrounds with vibrant gold/amber accents. Energetic and modern.

## Colors
- Background primary: `bg-gray-950` (#030712)
- Background secondary: `bg-gray-900` (#111827)
- Background card: `bg-gray-800` (#1f2937)
- Accent gold: `text-amber-400` / `bg-amber-400` (#fbbf24)
- Accent gold dark: `text-amber-500` (#f59e0b)
- Accent green: `text-emerald-400` (#34d399)
- Accent red: `text-red-400` (#f87171)
- Text primary: `text-white`
- Text secondary: `text-gray-300`
- Text muted: `text-gray-500`
- Border: `border-gray-700`
- Border accent: `border-amber-400`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-tight
- Hero title: text-5xl md:text-7xl font-black
- Section title: text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold
- Card title: text-xl font-semibold
- Body: text-base text-gray-300
- Small/muted: text-sm text-gray-500

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Card padding: p-6
- Gap between cards: gap-6

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: border border-gray-700
- Card hover: hover:border-amber-400 transition
- Card shadow: shadow-lg
- Accent border: border-l-4 border-amber-400

## Components
- Navbar: sticky top-0, bg-gray-950/90 backdrop-blur, border-b border-gray-800
- Hero: full-width dark gradient with gold text accents
- Cards: rounded-xl bg-gray-800 border border-gray-700
- Badges: rounded-full px-3 py-1 text-xs font-semibold
- Buttons primary: bg-amber-400 text-gray-950 font-bold hover:bg-amber-300
- Buttons secondary: border border-amber-400 text-amber-400 hover:bg-amber-400/10
- Tables: bg-gray-800 with alternating row bg-gray-900/50

## Do's
- Use gold (amber-400) as the primary accent color
- Keep backgrounds very dark for drama
- Use uppercase tracking-widest for labels and badges
- Add subtle gradients on hero sections
- Use rounded-xl for cards

## Don'ts
- No light backgrounds
- No low-contrast text on dark backgrounds
- No pastel colors
- No serif fonts
