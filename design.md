# Cafe Website Design Guide

## Visual Style
A warm, inviting, and modern cafe aesthetic that balances rustic charm with contemporary design.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Brown**: `bg-amber-900` (#78350f) - Rich coffee brown for headers and accents
- **Secondary Brown**: `bg-amber-800` (#92400e) - Medium brown for buttons and highlights
- **Light Brown**: `bg-amber-100` (#fef3c7) - Cream background for sections
- **Warm White**: `bg-amber-50` (#fffbeb) - Main background color
- **Text Dark**: `text-amber-950` (#451a03) - Primary text color
- **Text Medium**: `text-amber-800` (#92400e) - Secondary text color
- **Text Light**: `text-amber-600` (#d97706) - Accent text color
- **Accent Gold**: `text-yellow-600` (#ca8a04) - Special highlights

## Typography
- **Headings**: Large, bold fonts with `font-bold` and `text-4xl` to `text-6xl`
- **Subheadings**: Medium weight with `font-semibold` and `text-xl` to `text-2xl`
- **Body Text**: Regular weight with `font-normal` and `text-base` to `text-lg`
- **Accent Text**: Italic styling with `italic` for special emphasis

## Spacing & Layout
- **Sections**: Large padding with `py-16` to `py-20` for desktop, `py-12` for mobile
- **Containers**: Max width with `max-w-6xl` and centered with `mx-auto`
- **Cards**: Rounded corners with `rounded-lg` and subtle shadows with `shadow-md`
- **Gaps**: Consistent spacing with `gap-8` for grids, `gap-4` for smaller elements

## Visual Elements
- **Borders**: Subtle borders with `border-amber-200`
- **Shadows**: Warm shadows with `shadow-amber-100/50`
- **Hover Effects**: Gentle transitions with `hover:bg-amber-800` and `transition-colors`
- **Images**: Rounded with `rounded-lg` and proper aspect ratios

## Responsive Design
- **Mobile First**: Use `md:` and `lg:` prefixes for larger screens
- **Grid**: Single column on mobile, 2-3 columns on desktop
- **Text**: Smaller sizes on mobile, larger on desktop
- **Padding**: Reduced padding on mobile with `px-4` vs `px-8` on desktop

## Do's
- Use warm, coffee-inspired colors throughout
- Maintain consistent spacing and typography
- Include plenty of white space for readability
- Use high-quality images with proper alt text
- Ensure good contrast for accessibility

## Don'ts
- Don't use cold colors (blues, grays) as primary colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much content
- Don't use harsh shadows or borders
- Don't forget mobile responsiveness
- Don't use more than 3-4 font sizes per section
