# Food Health Care — Design System

## Brand Identity
Clean, fresh, and trustworthy. The visual language communicates wellness, nature, and vitality through a green-forward palette with warm accents.

## Colors
- **Primary green:** `#2d7a4f` (deep forest green) — `text-[#2d7a4f]`, `bg-[#2d7a4f]`
- **Light green:** `#e8f5ee` — `bg-[#e8f5ee]`
- **Accent orange:** `#f97316` — `text-orange-500`, `bg-orange-500`
- **Warm yellow:** `#fbbf24` — `text-amber-400`
- **Background white:** `#ffffff` — `bg-white`
- **Section alt bg:** `#f9fafb` — `bg-gray-50`
- **Text primary:** `#111827` — `text-gray-900`
- **Text secondary:** `#6b7280` — `text-gray-500`
- **Text muted:** `#9ca3af` — `text-gray-400`
- **Border:** `#e5e7eb` — `border-gray-200`

## Typography
- **Font family:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Hero heading:** `text-5xl md:text-6xl font-extrabold text-gray-900 leading-tight`
- **Section heading:** `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-gray-900`
- **Card heading:** `text-xl font-semibold text-gray-900`
- **Body text:** `text-base text-gray-600 leading-relaxed`
- **Small/caption:** `text-sm text-gray-500`
- **Nav links:** `text-sm font-medium text-gray-700 hover:text-[#2d7a4f]`

## Spacing
- Section vertical padding: `py-20`
- Container max width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6` or `p-8`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-gray-200 rounded-2xl`
- Card shadow: `shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow`
- Button radius: `rounded-full`
- Image radius: `rounded-2xl`

## Buttons
- **Primary:** `bg-[#2d7a4f] text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold hover:bg-[#245f3e] transition-colors`
- **Secondary/outline:** `border-2 border-[#2d7a4f] text-[#2d7a4f] px-6 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold hover:bg-[#e8f5ee] transition-colors`

## Layout
- Single-page site with sticky navigation
- Sections: Hero, Features, Nutrition Tips, Healthy Recipes, Testimonials, Newsletter CTA, Footer
- Desktop: multi-column grids (2–4 cols)
- Mobile: single column stacking

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Use rounded corners on all cards and images
- Keep text on light backgrounds always dark (`text-gray-900` or `text-gray-700`)
- Use green accents for icons and highlights
- Use subtle background alternation (`bg-white` / `bg-gray-50`) between sections

## Don'ts
- Don't use dark backgrounds for body text sections
- Don't use low-contrast text (e.g. gray text on green background without checking contrast)
- Don't use sharp corners on cards
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors per section
