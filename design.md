# Design Guidelines for ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Typography
- Main Font: 'Inter', sans-serif (crisp, clean, user-friendly).
- Headings: Bold, clean, using slate-900 (dark).
- Body Text: slate-600 or slate-700.

## Colors
- Primary: Deep Slate (slate-900) - represents metal, industry, but in an elegant way.
- Accent: Crisp Blue (blue-600) or subtle metallic grays.
- Backgrounds: White (bg-white), light slate (bg-slate-50) for section contrasts.

## Layout & Spacing
- Container center alignment with paddings.
- Generous whitespace to feel "elegant" and not cluttered.
- Rounded corners for a friendlier feel (rounded-lg or rounded-xl).
- Soft shadows (shadow-md or shadow-lg) on cards.

## Components
- Buttons: Dark slate or blue background, white text, fully rounded or md-rounded.
- Product Cards: Image on top, title, description, and "Learn More" button.
- Hero: Large background text, strong clear typography.
