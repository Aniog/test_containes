# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial metal folding machinery
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly — premium industrial feel with approachable clarity

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) — clean, modern, highly legible
- **Headings**: Inter, font-weight 700-800, tracking tight
- **Body**: Inter, font-weight 400, tracking normal
- **Accent/Label**: Inter, font-weight 500-600, uppercase tracking-wide for labels and tags

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Navy `#0F1B2D` — authority, precision, trust
- **Primary Light**: `#1A2D47` — hover states, secondary backgrounds
- **Accent**: Warm Gold `#C8973E` — premium feel, CTAs, highlights
- **Accent Light**: `#D4A94F` — hover states for gold elements
- **Background**: Off-White `#F7F8FA` — clean, spacious feel
- **Surface**: White `#FFFFFF` — cards, content areas
- **Text Primary**: `#0F1B2D` — on light backgrounds
- **Text Secondary**: `#5A6577` — descriptions, supporting text
- **Text Muted**: `#8A94A6` — placeholders, subtle labels
- **Border**: `#E2E6ED` — subtle dividers
- **Success**: `#2D8A56` — confirmations
- **Error**: `#C84040` — errors, warnings

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Content max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Element gaps: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows: `shadow-sm` to `shadow-md` for cards
- Rounded corners: `rounded-lg` to `rounded-xl` for cards and buttons
- Gold accent lines and borders for premium feel
- Dark navy sections alternating with light sections for visual rhythm
- High-quality product imagery with subtle hover effects
- Icons from Lucide React for consistency

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid gold primary buttons with navy text, navy outline secondary buttons
- **Cards**: White surface with subtle border, hover lift effect
- **Navigation**: Transparent on hero, solid navy on scroll
- **Section Headers**: Small uppercase gold label + large navy heading + subtle description

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace to convey elegance
- Keep typography hierarchy clear and consistent
- Use gold sparingly as an accent — not as a dominant color
- Ensure all text is highly readable against its background
- Use smooth transitions for hover states (transition-all duration-300)
- Make CTAs prominent and easy to find

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much content
- Don't use light text on light backgrounds
- Don't use more than 2 font families
- Don't use heavy drop shadows — keep them subtle
- Don't use gold for large background areas
