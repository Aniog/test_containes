# SSourcing China - Design System

## Visual Style
Clean, professional B2B website for a China-based sourcing agent. The design should convey trust, reliability, and international business competence.

## Colors
- Primary: Deep navy blue `#1a2744` - used for headers, primary buttons, trust elements
- Primary light: `#2d4a7a` - hover states, secondary elements
- Accent: Gold/amber `#d4a843` - CTAs, highlights, trust badges
- Accent hover: `#c49a35`
- Background: White `#ffffff` and light gray `#f5f7fa`
- Text primary: `#1a1a2e` - near black for headings
- Text secondary: `#4a5568` - gray for body text
- Text muted: `#718096` - for captions, labels
- Border: `#e2e8f0` - subtle dividers
- Success: `#38a169` - for positive indicators
- Error: `#e53e3e` - for form errors

## Typography
- Font family: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- Headings: 600-700 weight, tight letter-spacing
- Body: 400 weight, 1.6 line-height
- Small text: 14px, muted color
- Hero headline: 48-56px, 700 weight
- Section headings: 32-36px, 600 weight
- Subsection headings: 24px, 600 weight

## Layout
- Max content width: 1200px
- Section padding: 80px vertical on desktop, 48px on mobile
- Grid gaps: 24-32px
- Card padding: 24-32px
- Border radius: 8px for cards, 6px for buttons, 4px for inputs

## Components
- Buttons: Solid primary with hover state, outline secondary
- Cards: White background, subtle shadow, rounded corners
- Forms: Clean inputs with focus states, clear labels
- Navigation: Sticky header with logo left, links center, CTA right
- Footer: Dark background, multi-column layout

## Spacing Scale
- xs: 4px
- sm: 8px
- md: 16px
- lg: 24px
- xl: 32px
- 2xl: 48px
- 3xl: 64px
- 4xl: 80px

## Do's
- Use plenty of white space
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use icons to support content
- Show real factory/QC imagery
- Maintain consistent alignment
- Use subtle shadows for depth

## Don'ts
- No flashy animations or gradients
- No exaggerated claims or hype language
- No cluttered layouts
- No bright/neon colors
- No decorative elements that distract from content
- No stock photos that look obviously fake
