# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Velmora is a quiet-luxury DTC demi-fine jewelry brand. The visual language is warm, editorial, and refined — never loud, never discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#F9F6F1` (warm cream) — main page background
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` — cards, modals, drawers
- Primary Text: `#1C1A18` (warm black) — headings, body
- Secondary Text: `#6B645C` (warm gray) — labels, captions
- Accent Gold: `#C9A96E` — CTAs, accent elements, hover states
- Accent Gold Hover: `#B8944F` — darker gold for hover
- Dark Background: `#2C2824` — footer, dark sections
- Border: `#E5DFD6` (warm beige) — dividers, hairline rules
- Muted: `#8B8378` — secondary text, placeholder
- Light Gold: `#F5F0E8` — subtle gold tinted surfaces

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (Google Font) — serif, elegant, for hero titles, section headings, product names
- Body: Inter (Google Font) — clean sans-serif for UI text, prices, descriptions
- Product names: UPPERCASE with tracking-wide (letter-spacing)
- Weight: headings 300-500, body 400, UI 500

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Card padding: p-6

## Borders & Shadows
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E5DFD6]
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
- Card hover: subtle lift with shadow transition

## Buttons
- Primary: filled gold (#C9A96E) background, white text, uppercase, tracking-wider, px-8 py-3
- Outline: border gold (#C9A96E), text gold, transparent bg, hover: bg gold light
- Text button: no border, gold text, underline on hover

## Transitions
- All interactive: transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out
- Hover: opacity, shadow, transform (subtle translate-y)

## Don'ts
- No bright neon colors
- No heavy shadows
- No cluttered layouts
- No generic stock photos
- No loud patterns
- No discount-sale styling
