# Visual Style Guide - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, and premium demi-fine jewelry. High-end DTC aesthetic.

## Color Palette
- **Primary/Background:** `#FAF9F6` (Ecru/Off-white) - `bg-primary`
- **Secondary/Background:** `#F5F2ED` (Warm Sand) - `bg-secondary`
- **Foreground/Text:** `#1A1A1A` (Soft Black) - `text-foreground`
- **Muted/Text:** `#666666` (Stone Gray) - `text-muted-foreground`
- **Accent/Link:** `#A68B5B` (Champagne Gold) - `text-accent` / `bg-accent`
- **Border:** `#E5E2DD` (Warm Grey) - `border-border`

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** 'Cormorant Garamond', Serif
  - Tailwind: `font-serif`
  - Style: Elegant, uppercase for product names with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).
- **Body & UI:** 'Inter', Sans-serif
  - Tailwind: `font-sans`
  - Style: Clean, readable.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons:**
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal (`bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white`) or Accent Gold.
  - Secondary: Outlined/Ghost with thin borders.
- **Spacing:** Generous whitespace, editorial layout.
- **Dividers:** Thin hairline dividers (`border-t-[0.5px]`).
- **Cards:** Subtle hover transitions, soft shadows or simple borders.

## Do's
- Use large, high-quality editorial imagery.
- Keep margins wide and breathable.
- Stick to the warm, neutral palette.
- Use uppercase and letter-spacing for a premium feel on titles.

## Don'ts
- No loud or vibrant colors (except subtle gold accents).
- No heavy shadows or rounded corners (prefer sharp or very slightly rounded).
- No crowded layouts.
- No generic e-commerce icons; keep them thin and minimal.
