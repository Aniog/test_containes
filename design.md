# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand
- Name: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- Personality: Precision engineering, industrial elegance, trustworthy, user-friendly
- Products: Sheet metal folding machines, double folders, metal folders

## Color Palette
- Primary (accent): `#bfa15f` — warm gold/brass, suggests metalwork quality
- Primary dark: `#8c7340` — deeper brass for hover states
- Background light: `#f7f7f5` — warm off-white, clean industrial feel
- Background dark: `#1a1a1a` — near black for contrast sections
- Surface: `#ffffff` — pure white for cards and forms
- Text primary: `#1f1f1f` — near black for headings
- Text secondary: `#5a5a5a` — medium gray for body copy
- Text muted: `#8a8a8a` — light gray for captions
- Border: `#e5e5e0` — subtle warm gray for dividers

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (Google Fonts), system-ui fallback
- Headings: 700–800 weight, tight line-height (1.1–1.2)
- Body: 400–500 weight, line-height 1.6
- Hero display: large uppercase tracking-wide for brand name

## Spacing & Layout
- Max container width: 1280px (`max-w-7xl`)
- Section vertical padding: `py-20` desktop, `py-14` mobile
- Grid gaps: `gap-8` standard, `gap-6` on mobile
- Card border radius: `rounded-lg` (8px)
- Button border radius: `rounded` (4px) for industrial feel

## Components
- Buttons:
  - Primary: gold background, near-black text, hover darkens to `#8c7340`
  - Secondary/outline: transparent with dark border, dark text, hover fills light
- Cards: white background, subtle border, light shadow on hover
- Navigation: sticky top, white/transparent with blur, dark text
- Hero: full-width background image with dark overlay, centered content

## Elevation
- Card shadow: `shadow-sm` default, `shadow-md` on hover
- Navigation: subtle backdrop blur and bottom border

## Imagery
- Industrial machinery photography, sheet metal fabrication, precision engineering
- Use stock image tagging system with machinery and metalwork queries
