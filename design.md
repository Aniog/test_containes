# ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Brand Name
* ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Style
* Elegant but user-friendly
* Professional, industrial, precision-focused
* Clean spacing, modern typography

## Typography
* Global / Base Font: 'Inter', sans-serif
* Headings: Bold, clean, using Inter or similar sans-serif.

## Colors
* Primary: Slate/Navy Blue or Deep Steel Blue (e.g. `bg-slate-900`, `text-slate-900`) representing machinery and precision.
* Secondary: Accent color conveying action and industrial output, maybe a vibrant orange or subtle gold (e.g. `bg-orange-500`, `text-orange-500`).
* Background: Clean white (`bg-white`) and light gray (`bg-gray-50`) for sections to contrast with the deep blue elements.
* Text: Dark gray (`text-gray-800`) for paragraphs for readability, lighter gray (`text-gray-500`) for muted text.

## Layout and Spacing
* Container: Standard max-width container (`max-w-7xl mx-auto xl:px-8`) with responsive padding.
* Margins/Padding: Ample breathing room (`py-16` or `py-20`) for sections.
* Border radius: Slight rounding (`rounded-lg` or `rounded-xl`) to look friendly, but not too rounded to maintain the industrial edge.
* Shadows: Subtle drop shadows for cards (`shadow-sm` or `shadow`).

## Visual Elements
* Images: High-quality photos of modern industrial machinery, specifically folding and metal working. Use `data-strk-bg` and `data-strk-img`.
* Icons: Lucide icons with solid or outline style, in primary or secondary colors.
* Buttons: Solid primary color with white text, or outline with primary color text. Slight hover effects (e.g., `hover:bg-slate-800`).

## Components
* Navigation: Fixed or sticky top nav with logo on left, links and CTA on right.
* Hero Section: Background image of a pristine factory or machinery close-up, large elegant typography, clear CTA.
* Feature/Benefits: Grid layout with icons.
* Products: Grid layout highlighting double folding machines and sheet metal folders. Card style.
* Footer: Simple, dark background (`bg-slate-900`) with white/light gray text, links, and contact info.
