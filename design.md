# Strikingly Generators Hub Design Guide

Visual style follows the Strikingly generator hub brief exactly.

## Typography
- Headings and buttons use Brandon Grotesque when available, with Josefin Sans as the primary free fallback and Poppins/geometric sans as secondary fallbacks.
- Body copy uses Open Sans at a 14px base size with 1.5 line-height.
- Headings are uppercase, weight 700, and use tight but readable letter spacing.

## Colors
- Brand purple: #8159BB for accents, badges, outlines, and focus rings.
- AI gradient: linear-gradient(90deg, #7671FF, #CB0C9F), reserved for primary CTAs and the second H1 line only.
- Heading text: #4B5056 for section headings and #2E2E2F for the first H1 line.
- Body text: #636972.
- Card border: #C6C9CD.
- Divider: #E2E4E7.
- Light background: #F4F6F8.
- White: #FFFFFF.

## Components
- Cards are white with 1px #C6C9CD borders, 3px radius, and 20px padding.
- Card hover uses a subtle lift shadow and brand-purple border, with no scale or rotation.
- Primary buttons use the AI gradient with white text.
- Ghost buttons use transparent backgrounds, brand-purple text, and a 1px brand-purple border.
- Tags are uppercase ghost badges with 11px heading font, 2px by 6px padding, and 3px radius.

## Layout
- Max content width is 1200px, centered.
- Spacing uses 10px multiples, with 5px allowed for tight pairs.
- Desktop layouts use multi-column grids; mobile stacks to one column at 375px without horizontal overflow.

## Do / Don't
- Do keep text contrast strong on every surface.
- Do keep illustrations inline SVG and decorative.
- Do keep the AI gradient restrained.
- Don't use fake social proof, ratings, or testimonial imagery.
- Don't embed builder forms on the hub page.
