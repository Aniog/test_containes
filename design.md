# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury. Warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.
Deep obsidian base with warm ivory surfaces and gold metallic accents.

## Color Palette
- **obsidian** `#1a1714` — primary dark background, nav, footer
- **obsidian-light** `#2a2520` — secondary dark surfaces
- **ivory** `#f5f0e8` — primary light background, page base
- **ivory-dark** `#ede6d6` — cards, subtle section backgrounds
- **gold** `#c9a96e` — primary accent: CTAs, borders, highlights
- **gold-light** `#dfc08a` — hover states, shimmer
- **gold-dark** `#a8854a` — pressed states
- **taupe** `#8c7b6b` — body text on light, secondary text
- **taupe-light** `#b5a898` — placeholder, muted text
- **blush** `#e8d5c4` — subtle warm tint for section backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond` serif — weights 300, 400, 500, 600
- **Body / UI**: `Manrope` sans-serif — weights 300, 400, 500, 600, 700
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond 500
- **Section labels**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.25em, Manrope 500, small size
- **Hero headline**: Cormorant Garamond 300–400, very large, italic optional

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace — sections have py-20 to py-32 on desktop
- Max content width: max-w-7xl centered
- Hairline dividers: 1px border in gold/20 opacity
- Grid: 2-col mobile, 3-col tablet, 4-col desktop for product grids

## Buttons
- **Primary (CTA)**: bg-gold text-obsidian, px-8 py-3, uppercase tracking-widest, Manrope 500, no border-radius or very subtle (rounded-none or rounded-sm)
- **Secondary (outlined)**: border border-gold text-gold, same sizing
- **Ghost**: text-obsidian underline-offset-4 hover:underline
- Hover: bg-gold-dark or opacity shift, transition-all duration-300

## Cards
- Product cards: bg-ivory-dark or white, no heavy shadow — subtle shadow-sm
- Hover: image scale 1.03, shadow-md, transition-all duration-400
- Thin gold border on hover optional

## Dividers
- `border-t border-gold/20` — hairline gold tint

## Do's
- Large editorial imagery, full-bleed hero
- Lots of breathing room between elements
- Serif for all display text, sans for all functional text
- Warm tones throughout — no cold blues or grays
- Subtle transitions (300–400ms ease)

## Don'ts
- No bright colors, no neon, no heavy drop shadows
- No rounded pill buttons (keep rectangular/slightly rounded)
- No generic stock-photo vibes — editorial framing
- No cluttered layouts — less is more
