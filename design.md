# SSourcing China - Design System

## Brand
- Name: SSourcing China
- Tagline: China Sourcing Agent for Global Buyers
- Industry: B2B Sourcing / Manufacturing / Supply Chain

## Colors
- Primary: #0F4C81 (deep navy blue - trust, professionalism)
- Primary Hover: #0D3D68
- Secondary: #F59E0B (amber/gold - action, energy)
- Secondary Hover: #D97706
- Accent: #10B981 (emerald green - success, quality)
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Surface: #F8FAFC (slate-50)
- Surface Dark: #1E293B (slate-800)
- Text Primary: #1E293B (slate-800)
- Text Secondary: #64748B (slate-500)
- Text Light: #94A3B8 (slate-400)
- Border: #E2E8F0 (slate-200)

## Typography
- Headings: Inter, 700-800 weight
- Body: Inter, 400-500 weight
- H1: 48px/56px, letter-spacing -0.02em
- H2: 36px/44px, letter-spacing -0.01em
- H3: 24px/32px
- Body: 16px/24px
- Small: 14px/20px

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 (80px) desktop, py-12 (48px) mobile
- Content max-width: 1280px
- Container padding: px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8 xl:px-12
- Card padding: p-6 to p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 to gap-8

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-md, px-6 py-3, font-semibold
  - Primary: bg-primary text-white hover:bg-primary-hover
  - Secondary: bg-secondary text-white hover:bg-secondary-hover
  - Outline: border-2 border-primary text-primary hover:bg-primary hover:text-white
- Cards: rounded-lg, bg-white, shadow-sm, border border-slate-100
  - Hover: shadow-md, border-primary/20
- Icons: Lucide icons, 24px default, primary color for feature icons

## Layout
- Navbar: fixed, bg-white/95 backdrop-blur, border-b border-slate-200
- Hero: full-width, gradient or image background, centered content
- Sections: alternating white/surface backgrounds
- Footer: bg-surface-dark, text-white

## Do's
- Use clean, professional imagery of factories, shipping, quality control
- Keep copy concise and action-oriented
- Use data/statistics where relevant
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use clear hierarchy with headings and subheadings

## Don'ts
- Don't use playful or casual fonts
- Don't use overly bright or neon colors
- Don't clutter with too many competing CTAs
- Don't use generic stock photo people smiling at cameras
