Visual style for ARTITECT MACHINERY

Brand direction
- Elegant industrial presentation with a user-friendly buying experience.
- Tone should feel precise, premium, reliable, and approachable.
- Layout should balance strong machinery aesthetics with clear readability and generous spacing.

Typography
- Use Inter from index.html.
- Headlines: bold, tight tracking, large scale. Example Tailwind: text-4xl md:text-6xl font-semibold tracking-[-0.03em]
- Section headings: text-3xl md:text-4xl font-semibold tracking-[-0.02em]
- Body text: text-base md:text-lg leading-7 text-slate-600
- Labels and overlines: uppercase, text-xs tracking-[0.28em] font-semibold text-amber-700

Color system
- Background: warm off-white and slate surfaces. Examples: bg-stone-50, bg-white, bg-slate-900
- Primary text: slate neutrals. Examples: text-slate-950, text-slate-800, text-slate-600
- Accent: restrained brass/amber for premium emphasis. Examples: text-amber-700, bg-amber-500, border-amber-400/40
- Support surfaces: subtle slate tint and soft gradients. Examples: bg-slate-100, bg-slate-900/95, from-stone-100 via-white to-slate-100

Spacing and layout
- Use wide desktop containers: max-w-7xl.
- Section spacing should feel generous. Examples: py-20 md:py-28.
- Cards should use rounded-3xl with balanced padding. Example: rounded-3xl p-6 md:p-8.
- Keep desktop layouts multi-column where appropriate; stack only on smaller screens.

Borders and effects
- Prefer soft borders over heavy shadows. Examples: border border-slate-200, shadow-[0_24px_80px_-40px_rgba(15,23,42,0.35)] only on hero or featured cards.
- Use subtle backdrop or blur only if readability stays strong.

Buttons
- Primary: dark slate background with light text. Example: bg-slate-950 text-white hover:bg-slate-800.
- Secondary: white or transparent background with clear border. Example: border border-slate-300 text-slate-900 hover:bg-slate-100.
- Buttons should have rounded-full, px-6 py-3, font-medium.

Imagery
- Use imagery sparingly to support product trust, not to clutter.
- Favor clean workshop or fabricated metal visuals that feel premium and precise.
- Text over imagery must always have strong contrast.

Do
- Keep all text clearly readable against every surface.
- Use consistent slate and amber accents throughout.
- Emphasize trust, manufacturing precision, and product clarity.
- Keep sections calm, structured, and easy to scan.

Do not
- Do not use neon or oversaturated colors.
- Do not use cramped card layouts or tiny text.
- Do not rely on low-contrast muted text for key information.
- Do not make desktop layouts look like narrow mobile stacks.
