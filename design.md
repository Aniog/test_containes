# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Brand
- High-end DTC demi-fine gold jewelry
- Target: women 25-45, gifting and self-purchase
- Price point: $30-$120
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial

## Colors
- **Background / Light**: `#FAF7F2` (warm ivory) — main page background
- **Surface / Cards**: `#FFFFFF` — card, drawer, modal backgrounds
- **Text Primary**: `#1A1A1A` (near-black) — headings, body
- **Text Muted**: `#7A6B5D` (warm taupe) — secondary text, captions
- **Accent / Gold**: `#C9A96E` (burnished gold) — CTAs, accents, dividers
- **Accent Hover**: `#B8954E` (darker gold) — button hover
- **Border / Hairline**: `#E8E0D6` (warm beige) — dividers, borders
- **Footer BG**: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)
- **Footer Text**: `#C9A96E` (gold) — logo, links
- **Footer Muted**: `#9C8C7E` (muted taupe) — helper text
- **Success**: `#2D6A4F` — notifications

## Typography
- **Headings, Logo, Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond, serif
- **Body, UI, Buttons**: Inter, sans-serif
- Product names: UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.15em]`
- Logo: UPPERCASE, tracking-widest

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl` (1280px)
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Generous whitespace between sections
- All cards: rounded-sm (subtle)
- Grid gap: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders
- Thin hairline: `border border-[#E8E0D6]`
- Dividers: `h-px bg-[#E8E0D6]`

## Shadows
- Card hover: `shadow-lg shadow-black/5`
- Subtle card: `shadow-sm shadow-black/5`

## Buttons
- **Primary**: solid `bg-[#C9A96E]` text-white, hover `bg-[#B8954E]`
- **Outline**: `border border-[#C9A96E]` text-[#C9A96E], hover bg-[#C9A96E] hover:text-white
- All buttons: `transition-all duration-300`

## Animations
- Hover transitions: `duration-300`
- Fade in on scroll: `opacity-0 translate-y-4` → `opacity-100 translate-y-0`
- Image hover: scale 1.03 on product cards
- Cart drawer: slide from right

## Do's
- Use warm ivory backgrounds with generous whitespace
- Gold accents sparingly — CTAs, dividers, selected states
- Serif for elegance, sans-serif for readability
- Product images on dark/neutral warm backgrounds
- Thin gold dividers between sections

## Don'ts
- No bright colors, no discount banners, no loud patterns
- No bold black borders — use warm beige hairlines
- No generic e-commerce stock UI feel
- No cluttered layouts
