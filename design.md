# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry.
Target: Women 25–45, gifting + self-purchase, $30–$120 price point.

## Color Palette
- Base: cream (#FAF8F5), warm-white (#F5F2ED), rose (#F0EBE6)
- Text: espresso (#1A1817) for primary, taupe (#8B7E74) for secondary
- Accent: gold (#B8860B), gold-light (#D4A843), gold-dark (#9A6F0A), gold-pale (#F5E6C8)
- Borders: border (#E8E2D9)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — 300–500 weight, letter-spacing: 0.04em
- Product names: Cormorant Garamond — UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.20em, weight: 500
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) — 300–500 weight, letter-spacing: normal

## Spacing
- Section padding: px-6 md:px-12 lg:px-16 xl:px-24
- Generous whitespace, large imagery, thin hairline dividers

## Components
- Buttons: solid (btn-primary, btn-gold) or outlined (btn-outline), uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em
- Cards: minimal shadow, hover scale transition
- Dividers: 1px, border color
- Forms: clean underlined inputs, minimal borders

## Do
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Use uppercase with wide letter-spacing for product names
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use gold as accent only, never as primary background
- Use warm neutrals for backgrounds

## Don't
- No loud colors, no discount/sale badges
- No generic e-commerce tropes
- No heavy shadows or borders
- No dark backgrounds for text-heavy areas
- No sans-serif for product names or headings
