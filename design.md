# MicroCosmos Design Guidelines

A visual guide for the MicroCosmos website, focusing on the hidden beauty of the microscopic world.

## Typography
- **Heading Font:** 'Inter', sans-serif (Bold, tracking-tight)
- **Body Font:** 'Inter', sans-serif (Light to Regular)

## Colors
- **Primary:** Deep cosmic blue (#0a192f) - `bg-[#0a192f]`
- **Secondary:** Vibrant cyan (#64ffda) - `text-[#64ffda]`
- **Accent:** Soft purple (#bd93f9) - `text-[#bd93f9]`
- **Background:** Dark slate (#112240) - `bg-[#112240]`
- **Text:** Slate white (#e6f1ff) - `text-[#e6f1ff]`

## Spacing & Layout
- Standard section padding: `py-20 px-6 md:px-12 lg:px-24`
- Grid layouts for image-heavy sections.

## Visual Styles
- Use semi-transparent cards with glassmorphism effects: `bg-white/5 backdrop-blur-md`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-xl`
- Subtle borders: `border border-white/10`

## Do's
- Use high-quality, high-contrast microscopic imagery.
- Keep the UI minimal to let the images shine.
- Use smooth transitions and hover effects.

## Don'ts
- Avoid cluttered layouts.
- Don't use heavy shadows; stick to subtle glows or borders.
- No harsh pure black (#000000) or pure white (#ffffff).
