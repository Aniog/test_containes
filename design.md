# Design System — AB橄榄球CD

## Brand Colors
- Primary: Deep forest green `#1a4731` (Tailwind: use custom `rugby-green`)
- Accent: Warm gold `#c9a227` (Tailwind: use custom `rugby-gold`)
- Dark: Near-black `#0f1a13`
- Light: Off-white `#f5f0e8`
- Muted: `#6b7280` (Tailwind gray-500)

## Typography
- Headings: Bold, uppercase tracking — `font-bold tracking-wide uppercase`
- Body: Regular weight, comfortable line-height — `font-normal leading-relaxed`
- Hero title: Extra-large, impactful — `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-extrabold`
- Section title: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- Card title: `text-xl font-semibold`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl shadow-lg`
- Buttons: `rounded-full`
- Dividers: `border-rugby-gold`

## Visual Style
- Dark hero section with background image overlay
- Green and gold color scheme throughout
- Bold, energetic typography
- Cards with subtle shadows on light background
- Full-width sections alternating dark/light

## Do's
- Use `text-white` on dark/green backgrounds
- Use `text-rugby-green` or `text-gray-800` on light backgrounds
- Use `bg-rugby-green` for primary buttons
- Use `bg-rugby-gold` for accent highlights

## Don'ts
- Never use light text on light backgrounds
- Never use dark text on dark backgrounds
- Avoid low-contrast color combinations
