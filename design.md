# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity

### Mood
- Quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Charcoal: `#1A1A1A` (main background, text)
- Warm Cream: `#FAF7F2` (light background, cards)
- Rich Black: `#0D0D0D` (footer, dark sections)

**Accent Colors:**
- Warm Gold: `#C9A962` (primary accent, CTAs, highlights)
- Soft Gold: `#E8DCC4` (secondary accent, borders)
- Muted Bronze: `#8B7355` (tertiary accent)

**Neutrals:**
- Warm Gray: `#6B6560` (secondary text)
- Light Taupe: `#E5E0D8` (dividers, subtle backgrounds)

### Typography
**Headings & Product Names:**
- Font: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- Weight: 500-600
- Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.15em

**Body & UI:**
- Font: Manrope (sans-serif)
- Weight: 400-500
- Clean, readable, modern

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace (80px+ vertical spacing between sections)
- Large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers (1px, light taupe)
- Maximum content width: 1400px
- Container padding: 24px (mobile), 48px (desktop)

### Components

#### Buttons
- Primary: Solid warm gold background, dark text
  - Hover: Slightly darker gold, subtle lift shadow
- Secondary: Outlined with gold border
  - Hover: Gold background fill
- Border radius: 0 (sharp, editorial) or 2px
- Padding: 16px 32px
- Letter-spacing: 0.1em uppercase

#### Cards
- Background: Warm cream (#FAF7F2)
- Subtle shadow on hover: `0 8px 32px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- Sharp corners or minimal radius (2px)
- Image aspect ratio: 4:5 (portrait, editorial)

#### Dividers
- Hairline style: 1px solid #E5E0D8
- Used between sections and within content

### Animations
- Subtle hover transitions: 300ms ease
- Fade-in on scroll for sections
- Smooth image hover zoom (scale 1.05)
- Cart drawer slide from right

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 768px (primary focus)
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px
