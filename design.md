# SSourcing China — Design System

## Brand Identity
Professional, trustworthy, international B2B sourcing company based in China.

## Color Palette

### Primary — Deep Navy Blue
- Primary: `#1B3A6B` (navy-800) — main brand color, headers, CTAs
- Primary Light: `#2A5298` (navy-600) — hover states
- Primary Dark: `#0F2347` (navy-900) — dark backgrounds

### Accent — China Red (used sparingly)
- Accent: `#C0392B` (red-700) — highlights, badges, key numbers
- Accent Light: `#E74C3C` (red-500) — hover

### Neutral
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Gray 50: `#F8FAFC` — page backgrounds
- Gray 100: `#F1F5F9` — section alternates
- Gray 200: `#E2E8F0` — borders
- Gray 500: `#64748B` — secondary text
- Gray 700: `#334155` — body text
- Gray 900: `#0F172A` — headings

### Status
- Success: `#16A34A`
- Warning: `#D97706`

## Typography

### Font Family
- Headings: Inter (700, 800)
- Body: Inter (400, 500)
- Accent labels: Inter (600)

### Scale
- Display: `text-5xl font-extrabold` (hero headlines)
- H1: `text-4xl font-bold`
- H2: `text-3xl font-bold`
- H3: `text-xl font-semibold`
- Body Large: `text-lg`
- Body: `text-base`
- Small: `text-sm`
- Caption: `text-xs`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-4`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- Card padding: `p-6` or `p-8`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-gray-200`
- Card radius: `rounded-xl`
- Card shadow: `shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow`
- Button radius: `rounded-lg`

## Components

### Primary Button
`bg-[#1B3A6B] hover:bg-[#2A5298] text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-lg transition-colors`

### Accent Button (CTA)
`bg-[#C0392B] hover:bg-[#E74C3C] text-white font-semibold px-8 py-4 rounded-lg transition-colors text-lg`

### Outline Button
`border-2 border-[#1B3A6B] text-[#1B3A6B] hover:bg-[#1B3A6B] hover:text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-lg transition-colors`

### Section Badge
`inline-block bg-[#1B3A6B]/10 text-[#1B3A6B] text-sm font-semibold px-3 py-1 rounded-full uppercase tracking-wide`

### Card
`bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow p-6`

## Layout Patterns
- Alternating section backgrounds: white / gray-50
- Hero: full-width with dark overlay on background image
- Grid: 3-column on desktop, 2-column on tablet, 1-column on mobile
- Navbar: sticky, white background, shadow on scroll

## Do's
- Use navy as the dominant brand color
- Keep layouts clean with generous whitespace
- Use real-world language: "factory audit", "QC inspection", "FOB price"
- Show trust signals prominently (years experience, countries served, inspections done)

## Don'ts
- No gradients except subtle ones in hero sections
- No bright neon colors
- No exaggerated claims ("best in the world")
- No cluttered layouts
