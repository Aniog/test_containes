# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Tone**: Industrial precision meets modern elegance
- **Audience**: Manufacturing professionals, sheet metal fabricators, industrial buyers
- **Keywords**: Precision, Reliability, Innovation, Engineering Excellence

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep navy/slate (#0f172a, #1e293b) - conveys trust and industrial strength
- **Accent**: Warm amber/gold (#f59e0b, #d97706) - highlights CTAs and key elements
- **Neutral**: Cool grays (#64748b, #94a3b8, #e2e8f0) - for text and backgrounds
- **Surface**: White (#ffffff) and very light gray (#f8fafc) for cards and sections
- **Success**: Emerald green (#10b981) for status indicators

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, weights 600-800, tight tracking
- **Body**: Inter, weights 400-500, comfortable line height (1.6-1.75)
- **Monospace/Technical**: For specs and measurements

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace to convey premium quality
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: py-20 to py-32
- Card padding: p-6 to p-8

## Visual Style
- Clean, minimal design with strong hierarchy
- Subtle shadows and borders for depth
- Rounded corners (rounded-lg to rounded-xl) for modern feel
- Gradient accents sparingly used
- High-quality imagery for machinery/products

## Components
- Buttons: Rounded, with subtle hover effects and transitions
- Cards: White background, subtle shadow, hover lift effect
- Navigation: Sticky, clean with blur backdrop
- Forms: Clean inputs with focus states

## Do's
- Use ample whitespace
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use icons from Lucide React
- Implement smooth transitions (transition-all duration-200)
- Ensure mobile responsiveness

## Don'ts
- Avoid cluttered layouts
- Don't use low-contrast text
- Avoid overly decorative elements
- Don't use pure black (#000) - use slate-900 instead
