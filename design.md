# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry.
NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: cream (#FAF7F2) — warm off-white
- Surface: sand (#F5F0E8) — slightly warm beige
- Divider: stone (#E8E2D7) — hairline separator
- Body text: espresso (#3C3228) — deep warm brown-black
- Secondary text: cocoa (#5C4F42) — medium warm brown
- Muted text: taupe (#8B7E6E) — soft greige
- Accent: warmgold (#C5963E) — refined gold
- Accent hover: goldlight (#D4AF5C) — lighter gold
- Accent pale: goldpale (#F0E4CC) — subtle gold wash
- Dark surface: velvet (#2D2420) — deep espresso for contrast sections

## Typography
- Headings / Product Names: Cormorant Garamond (serif), uppercase, wide letter-spacing
- Body / UI: Inter (sans-serif), clean weights
- Product names: font-serif, text-sm, tracking-widest, uppercase
- Section headings: font-serif, text-3xl to text-5xl, tracking-wide
- Buttons: font-sans, text-sm, tracking-wider, uppercase

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Generous whitespace throughout

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold (btn-primary), outlined gold (btn-outline)
- Dividers: hairline border-stone
- Cards: white/cream bg, subtle shadow on hover
- Images: rounded-sm, editorial scale
- Hover transitions: 200-300ms ease-out

## Do's
- Use warm, inviting whitespace
- Keep type hierarchy clear with serif/sans contrast
- Let gold imagery breathe
- Thin, elegant borders and dividers

## Don'ts
- No harsh pure black (#000)
- No loud/bright colors
- No boxy/dense layouts
- No heavy drop shadows
- No discount badges or sale stickers
