# Visual Design System - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Background/Base):** `#1A1A1A` (Ebony) - Used for deep editorial sections.
- **Secondary (Surface):** `#FDFCFB` (Soft Linen) - Primary background color for light sections.
- **Accent:** `#D4AF37` (Champagne Gold) - Used for buttons, highlights, and icons.
- **Text:**
  - Heading: `#1A1A1A` (Ebony) or `#FDFCFB` (Soft Linen)
  - Body: `#4A4A4A` (Muted Charcoal)
  - Muted: `#7A7A7A`
- **Border:** `#E5E5E5` (Light Grey) - Hairline dividers.

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** 'Cormorant Garamond', serif.
  - Product names: `uppercase`, `tracking-[0.2em]`.
- **Body & UI:** 'Inter', sans-serif.

## Standard Styles
- **Buttons (Solid Accent):** `bg-[#D4AF37] text-white hover:bg-[#B8962E] transition-all duration-300 px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm font-medium`.
- **Buttons (Outline):** `border border-[#D4AF37] text-[#D4AF37] hover:bg-[#D4AF37] hover:text-white transition-all duration-300 px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm font-medium`.
- **Dividers:** `h-[1px] bg-[#E5E5E5] w-full`.
- **Cards:** `bg-transparent border-none shadow-none`.

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace.
- Section padding: `py-20` on desktop, `py-12` on mobile.

## Animations
- Subtle fades and hover transforms.
- Smooth scroll.
