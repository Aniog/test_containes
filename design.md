# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury with a warm editorial point of view. The store should feel like a premium demi-fine jewelry brand: refined, intimate, soft-lit, and never loud or discount-driven.

## Palette
Use one consistent palette that flatters gold jewelry:
- Obsidian: `#171310` for deep surfaces and high-contrast text on light areas.
- Espresso: `#2A211B` for elevated dark cards, footer, and drawer surfaces.
- Porcelain: `#F8F2EA` for the main page background.
- Warm Sand: `#E9D9C7` for secondary surfaces, category blocks, and trust areas.
- Champagne Gold: `#B8894A` for premium accents, links, stars, borders, and primary buttons.
- Soft Gold: `#D8B77B` for hover states and subtle highlights.
- Mist: `#FBF8F3` for light cards and form fields.
- Taupe: `#7A6A5D` for readable muted copy.

Define these as Tailwind v4 theme colors in `src/index.css`; do not hardcode random colors in components.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Playfair Display, elegant serif, refined contrast.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names should be uppercase with wide letter spacing.
- Use generous line-height and avoid heavy weights except for small UI labels.

## Layout & Spacing
- Mobile-first, with spacious sections and clear vertical rhythm.
- Desktop layouts should use editorial two-column or multi-card compositions; avoid making desktop feel like a stretched mobile stack.
- Use thin hairline dividers and restrained borders.
- Keep plenty of whitespace around product cards, story sections, and CTAs.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, crisp, pill or softly squared; primary uses Champagne Gold with Obsidian text; outline uses hairline Champagne Gold border.
- Cards: soft shadows, subtle borders, no heavy frames.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid Porcelain/Espresso treatment after scroll.
- Cart drawer: Espresso surface with clear Porcelain text and Champagne Gold actions.

## Imagery
Use warm-lit editorial jewelry imagery, close-ups of gold jewelry on ear/neck/hands, and neutral or dark backgrounds. Use the Strikingly stock image attributes for placeholders.

## Do
- Use serif display headlines with restrained copy.
- Keep text contrast strong on every surface.
- Use subtle transitions and gentle motion.
- Make mobile shopping actions easy to reach and read.

## Don't
- Do not use sale badges, loud red discount styling, neon colors, or generic marketplace UI.
- Do not use low-contrast text on warm neutral surfaces.
- Do not overcrowd product grids or footer columns on mobile.
