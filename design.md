# Design System

## Visual Style
Nature-inspired, calm, and earthy. Clean typography with generous whitespace.

## Colors
- Primary green: `#2d6a4f` (deep forest green)
- Light green: `#52b788` (fresh leaf green)
- Accent: `#b7e4c7` (soft mint)
- Dark background: `#1b2e22` (deep forest night)
- Text on dark: `#f0faf4` (near white with green tint)
- Text muted: `#a8d5b5`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Hero heading: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold tracking-tight`
- Subheading: `text-xl md:text-2xl font-light`
- Body: `text-base font-normal`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-24 px-6`
- Container max width: `max-w-5xl mx-auto`

## Borders & Shadows
- Subtle card shadow: `shadow-lg`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-2xl`

## Do's
- Use earthy greens and natural tones
- Use large, bold hero headings
- Use full-viewport hero sections with background imagery
- Keep text clearly readable against all backgrounds

## Don'ts
- Don't use harsh neon or overly saturated colors
- Don't use dark text on dark backgrounds
- Don't crowd elements — use generous padding
