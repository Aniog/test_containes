# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial machinery - metal folding machines
- **Tone**: Elegant, professional, trustworthy, yet approachable and user-friendly

## Typography
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif) - conveys elegance and craftsmanship
  - H1: text-5xl font-bold (hero), text-4xl font-bold (sections)
  - H2: text-3xl font-semibold
  - H3: text-xl font-semibold
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, modern, highly readable
  - Body: text-base leading-relaxed
  - Small: text-sm

## Color Palette
- **Primary / Brand**: Deep navy blue (#102a43 to #627d98) - trust, professionalism, precision
  - brand-900: #102a43 (darkest, hero backgrounds, headings)
  - brand-800: #243b53 (dark, nav, footer)
  - brand-700: #334e68 (medium-dark, cards on dark)
  - brand-600: #486581 (medium, borders, secondary text)
  - brand-500: #627d98 (medium-light, icons, subtle elements)
  - brand-100: #d9e2ec (light backgrounds)
  - brand-50: #f0f4f8 (lightest, section backgrounds)
- **Accent**: Warm gold/amber (#ffc107 to #ff6f00) - premium feel, CTAs, highlights
  - accent-500: #ffc107 (primary accent, buttons, highlights)
  - accent-600: #ffb300 (hover state for accent)
  - accent-700: #ffa000 (active state)
- **Neutral**: White, grays for backgrounds and text
  - Background: white (#ffffff) and brand-50 (#f0f4f8)
  - Text: brand-900 (#102a43) on light, white on dark

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between elements: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows and rounded corners (rounded-lg, rounded-xl)
- Smooth transitions (transition-all duration-300)
- Cards with white backgrounds on light gray sections
- Dark navy sections for contrast and emphasis
- Gold accent for CTAs and key highlights
- Professional product imagery with clean presentation

## Do's
- Use serif headings for elegance
- Use generous whitespace
- Use gold accents sparingly for premium feel
- Use navy backgrounds for hero and key sections
- Keep layouts clean and uncluttered
- Ensure high contrast for readability
- Use subtle hover effects on interactive elements

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't clutter layouts with too many elements
- Don't use more than 2 font families
- Don't use harsh shadows or heavy borders
- Don't use light text on light backgrounds
- Don't sacrifice readability for style
