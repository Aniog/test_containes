# ARTITECT MACHINERY Website Design Guidelines

## Brand Overview
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Products**: Double folding machine, double folder, sheet metal folder, sheet metal folding machine, metal folder, metal folder machine, metal folding machine
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly

## Visual Style

### Color Palette
- **Primary Dark**: `#1a1a2e` - Deep navy for header/hero backgrounds
- **Primary Accent**: `#c9a227` - Gold/bronze metallic accent for CTAs and highlights
- **Secondary**: `#2d3748` - Charcoal gray for text and secondary elements
- **Light Background**: `#f8f9fa` - Clean off-white for content sections
- **White**: `#ffffff` - Pure white for cards and contrast
- **Text Primary**: `#1a1a2e` - Dark navy for body text
- **Text Light**: `#6b7280` - Gray for secondary text

### Typography
- **Headings**: Montserrat (700, 600) - Strong, modern, industrial feel
- **Body**: Inter (400, 500) - Clean, readable, professional
- **Logo**: Montserrat Bold with letter-spacing

### Design Principles
1. **Elegant Industrial** - Clean lines, sophisticated typography, professional imagery
2. **User-Friendly** - Clear navigation, easy-to-find products, intuitive layout
3. **Trust-Building** - Professional presentation, clear CTAs, contact information visible
4. **Responsive** - Mobile-first approach, proper breakpoints

## Layout Structure

### Header/Navigation
- Sticky header with logo left, navigation center, CTA right
- Transparent on hero, solid on scroll
- Mobile hamburger menu

### Sections
1. **Hero** - Full-width with product focus, strong headline, CTA
2. **Products** - Card grid showcasing main product categories
3. **Features** - Key benefits with icons
4. **About** - Company introduction with image
5. **Contact** - Contact form and information
6. **Footer** - Links, social, copyright

## Component Styling

### Buttons
- Primary: Gold background (#c9a227), dark text, hover darken
- Secondary: Transparent with gold border
- Border-radius: 4px for elegance
- Padding: 12px 24px

### Cards
- White background
- Subtle shadow (0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.1))
- Border-radius: 8px
- Hover: lift effect with larger shadow

### Section Spacing
- Section padding: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Container max-width: 1200px
- Gap between cards: 24px

## Imagery
- Use industrial/machinery imagery
- Professional product photography style
- Clean backgrounds, well-lit equipment

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use high-quality industrial imagery
- Keep navigation simple and clear
- Include clear contact information
- Use consistent spacing and alignment
- Make CTAs prominent but not aggressive

### Don't
- Use too many colors (stick to palette)
- Overcrowd sections with too much content
- Use low-quality or generic stock photos
- Make text too small or hard to read
- Use flashy animations that distract from products
