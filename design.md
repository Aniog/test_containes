# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Generous whitespace, thin hairline dividers.

## Colors
- **Background**: `#FFFFFF` (Pure white for editorial feel)
- **Foreground**: `#1A1A1A` (Near black for crisp typography)
- **Accent**: `#B59972` (Warm gold/bronze for highlights)
- **Muted**: `#F9F8F6` (Very light stone/cream for sections)
- **Border**: `#E5E5E5` (Subtle hairline dividers)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - `font-serif tracking-wide uppercase`
- **Body/UI**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - `font-sans`

## Transitions
- Subtle hover transforms: `hover:scale-[1.02] transition-transform duration-500`
- Soft fades: `transition-opacity duration-300`

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: `bg-[#B59972] text-white px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm hover:bg-[#a38a67] transition-colors`
  - Secondary: `border border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A] px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm hover:bg-[#1A1A1A] hover:text-white transition-all`
