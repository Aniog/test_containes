# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- Industrial precision meets elegant simplicity
- Professional, trustworthy, and approachable
- Clean lines inspired by sheet metal fabrication

## Colors
- **Primary (Navy):** `navy-900` (#0f172a), `navy-800` (#1e293b), `navy-700` (#334155)
- **Accent (Amber/Gold):** `amber-500` (#f59e0b), `amber-400` (#fbbf24), `amber-600` (#d97706)
- **Neutral:** `slate-50` (#f8fafc), `slate-100` (#f1f5f9), `slate-200` (#e2e8f0), `slate-600` (#475569), `slate-800` (#1e293b)
- **White:** `#ffffff` for cards and content areas
- **Success:** `emerald-500` (#10b981)

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (headings + body)
- **Headings:** font-bold, tracking-tight
  - H1: text-5xl lg:text-6xl
  - H2: text-3xl lg:text-4xl
  - H3: text-xl lg:text-2xl
- **Body:** text-base, text-slate-600 on light backgrounds, text-slate-300 on dark backgrounds
- **Small/Caption:** text-sm, text-slate-500

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 lg:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 lg:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 lg:gap-8

## Components
- **Buttons Primary:** bg-amber-500 hover:bg-amber-600 text-navy-900 font-semibold rounded-lg px-6 py-3
- **Buttons Secondary:** border-2 border-amber-500 text-amber-500 hover:bg-amber-500 hover:text-navy-900 rounded-lg px-6 py-3
- **Cards:** bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow border border-slate-100
- **Navigation:** bg-navy-900/95 backdrop-blur-sm text-white sticky top-0

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Use subtle shadows for depth (shadow-sm, shadow-md)
- Use rounded-lg or rounded-xl for containers
- Use transition effects for hover states
- Keep text highly readable with proper contrast

## Don'ts
- Don't use harsh borders; prefer subtle shadows or light borders
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors
- Don't crowd content; maintain breathing room
- Don't use pure black (#000); use navy-900 instead
- Don't use small text on dark backgrounds without sufficient contrast
