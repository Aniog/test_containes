# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand Direction
Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial aesthetic. The experience should feel refined, warm, and premium-but-accessible. Avoid discount-store UI, bright promotional colors, heavy gradients, oversized badges, or cluttered layouts.

## Color Palette
Use one consistent palette sitewide.

- Background: soft ivory `velmora-ivory` (`bg-velmora-ivory`)
- Elevated surface: warm pearl `velmora-pearl` (`bg-velmora-pearl`)
- Deep base / navigation / footer: espresso `velmora-ink` (`bg-velmora-ink`)
- Primary text on light surfaces: espresso `velmora-ink` (`text-velmora-ink`)
- Secondary text: cocoa `velmora-mocha` (`text-velmora-mocha`)
- Hairlines / borders: champagne line `velmora-line` (`border-velmora-line`)
- Accent / premium action color: antique gold `velmora-gold` (`bg-velmora-gold`, `text-velmora-gold`)
- Accent foreground on gold: ivory `text-velmora-ivory`

## Typography
- Headings and editorial statements: Cormorant Garamond
- Body copy, labels, UI, buttons: Inter
- Product names: uppercase with wide tracking, serif, medium weight
- Prefer large editorial headings with calm line-height and generous spacing

## Layout & Spacing
- Use generous whitespace and breathable vertical rhythm
- Prefer max widths around `max-w-7xl` with `px-5 md:px-8`
- Use thin dividers with `border-velmora-line`
- Product cards and content blocks should feel airy, not cramped

## Components
- Buttons should feel premium: either solid gold with dark text or dark filled with light text
- Inputs should have subtle borders and clear readable text
- Cards should use soft shadows and warm surfaces
- Hover transitions should be subtle: opacity, translate-y, and color transitions only

## Imagery
- Warm-lit jewelry photography
- Editorial close-ups of earrings, huggies, necklaces on skin or neutral surfaces
- Dark or neutral backgrounds that flatter gold tones
- Do not overuse images; let whitespace do work

## Do
- Keep text contrast strong and explicit on every surface
- Use serif typography for hero headlines, product names, and section titles
- Make mobile layouts elegant and easy to scan
- Use restrained gold accents

## Don’t
- No loud sale styling
- No neon colors or generic ecommerce blue CTAs
- No dense card borders or heavy box shadows
- No low-contrast beige-on-beige text
