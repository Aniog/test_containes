# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury: warm, editorial, intimate, and premium without looking flashy or promotional. The experience should flatter gold jewelry and feel polished on both mobile and desktop.

## Color system
Use a single rich editorial palette based on deep espresso, warm ivory, and muted gold.

- Background base: warm ivory (`bg-stone-50`)
- Elevated surfaces: soft cream (`bg-stone-100` / `bg-white`)
- Deep anchor sections: espresso (`bg-stone-900`)
- Primary text on light surfaces: `text-stone-900`
- Secondary text on light surfaces: `text-stone-600`
- Primary text on dark surfaces: `text-stone-50`
- Accent metallic tone: muted gold (`text-amber-700`, `bg-amber-700`, `border-amber-700`)
- Divider tone: `border-stone-200` on light surfaces and `border-stone-700/60` on dark surfaces

Do not introduce additional bright brand colors.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body and UI: Inter
- Product names should be uppercase with wide tracking, such as `tracking-[0.24em]`
- Large editorial headings should use generous line-height and spacing, e.g. `text-5xl md:text-7xl leading-none`

## Layout and spacing
- Use wide section padding: `px-5 sm:px-6 lg:px-10 xl:px-16`
- Use generous vertical rhythm: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Keep content width constrained with `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- Use thin separators and subtle shadows rather than heavy borders

## Components
- Buttons should feel refined: rounded-full, compact height, letter-spaced text, subtle hover transitions
- Cards should use clean surfaces with soft shadow, no heavy gradients
- Navigation should be airy and understated
- Trust bars and metadata rows should use small uppercase UI text

## Imagery
- Prefer warm-lit closeups, dark neutral studio backdrops, and intimate styling
- Use large editorial image blocks with soft overlays
- Avoid generic flat-lay discount-shop energy

## Do
- Maintain explicit readable foreground colors on every surface
- Keep hover transitions subtle and fast
- Use whitespace as a luxury cue
- Keep product presentation minimal and elevated

## Don’t
- Don’t use loud sale banners or saturated accent colors
- Don’t crowd cards with too much metadata
- Don’t use low-contrast beige-on-beige text
- Don’t make desktop layouts collapse into a narrow mobile look
