# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand Direction
Velmora is a quiet-luxury demi-fine jewelry brand. The storefront should feel editorial, warm, elevated, and premium-but-accessible. Every section should breathe with generous spacing and restrained detail.

## Visual Tone
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial, intimate
- Impression: premium, feminine, polished, calm
- Avoid: discount energy, loud gradients, cluttered layouts, harsh contrast combinations, over-decoration

## Color Palette
Use these named brand colors consistently across the site.

- `obsidian` — #171311
  - Primary dark surface for navigation, footer, and high-contrast editorial blocks
- `ink` — #2F2926
  - Primary body text on light surfaces
- `parchment` — #F5F0E8
  - Warm premium light surface
- `mist` — #FBF8F4
  - Main page background
- `sand` — #E8DDD0
  - Secondary warm surface and subtle fills
- `taupe` — #9E8B78
  - Muted text and supportive labels
- `gold` — #B68A52
  - Main accent for premium CTAs and key highlights
- `bronze` — #7C5A3C
  - Deeper metallic accent for borders and hover states
- `line` — #DCCFC0
  - Hairline dividers and subtle borders

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `Cormorant Garamond`
- Body copy and interface text: `Inter`
- Product names: uppercase with wide tracking using `tracking-product`
- Use serif display type for hero headlines, section titles, and premium storytelling moments
- Keep body size calm and readable, not oversized

## Components
- Buttons: refined, medium height, soft edges, premium weight
- Cards: light surface, subtle shadow, thin border
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid obsidian after scroll
- Inputs: warm light fill, clear border, dark readable text
- Dividers: thin and understated using `line`

## Spacing
- Use generous vertical padding (`py-16`, `py-20`, `py-24`)
- Keep content constrained with centered containers (`max-w-7xl`)
- Maintain clear breathing room between image and text modules

## Motion
- Use subtle transitions only
- Favor `duration-300` or `duration-500`
- Hover effects should feel soft, not bouncy or flashy

## Do
- Keep layouts elegant and editorial
- Use high-contrast readable text on every surface
- Let imagery lead where appropriate
- Use warm metallic accents sparingly

## Don't
- Do not use bright sale colors
- Do not use heavy borders or dense shadows
- Do not create crowded mobile layouts
- Do not use generic marketplace styling
