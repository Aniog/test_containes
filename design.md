# MicroCosmos Design System

## Theme
A dark, immersive, science-inspired design that evokes the mystery and beauty of the microscopic world.

## Colors
- Background: Deep dark navy/black (`bg-slate-950`, `bg-slate-900`)
- Primary accent: Emerald/teal (`text-emerald-400`, `bg-emerald-500`)
- Secondary accent: Violet/purple (`text-violet-400`, `bg-violet-500`)
- Text primary: White (`text-white`)
- Text secondary: Slate light (`text-slate-300`, `text-slate-400`)
- Card backgrounds: Semi-transparent dark (`bg-slate-900/80`, `bg-slate-800/50`)
- Borders: Subtle glow (`border-emerald-500/20`, `border-violet-500/20`)

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: `font-bold` or `font-extrabold`, large sizes
- Body: `font-normal`, `text-base` or `text-lg`
- Section titles: `text-4xl md:text-5xl font-extrabold`
- Subtitles: `text-lg md:text-xl text-slate-300`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28 px-6 md:px-12`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl border border-slate-700/50`
- Images: `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`
- Glow effects: `shadow-lg shadow-emerald-500/10`

## Visual Style
- Dark immersive backgrounds
- Gradient overlays on hero sections
- Subtle glow/neon accents
- Large, prominent imagery
- Smooth transitions and hover effects

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Use gradient text for emphasis (`bg-gradient-to-r from-emerald-400 to-violet-400 bg-clip-text text-transparent`)
- Use backdrop blur for overlays
- Keep images large and prominent

## Don'ts
- No light backgrounds
- No small cramped layouts
- No harsh borders without transparency
- No plain unstyled text
