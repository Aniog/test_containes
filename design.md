# Bread & Bakery — Design System

## Brand Identity
Warm, artisanal, inviting. Evokes the smell of fresh bread, rustic charm, and handcrafted quality.

## Color Palette
- **Cream / Background**: `#FDF6EC` — warm off-white, like fresh dough
- **Warm Brown / Primary**: `#7C4A1E` — deep toasty brown (Tailwind custom: `brown-700`)
- **Golden / Accent**: `#D4882A` — golden crust color (Tailwind custom: `golden`)
- **Soft Tan**: `#E8C99A` — light wheat tone (Tailwind custom: `tan`)
- **Dark Espresso**: `#2C1A0E` — near-black for headings (Tailwind custom: `espresso`)
- **Muted Text**: `#6B4C2A` — warm mid-brown for body text (Tailwind custom: `brown-500`)
- **White**: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- **Display / Headings**: `Playfair Display` — serif, elegant, artisanal feel
- **Body / UI**: `Lato` — clean, readable sans-serif
- **Heading sizes**: `text-5xl` (hero), `text-4xl` (section), `text-2xl` (card), `text-xl` (sub)
- **Body**: `text-base` or `text-lg`, `leading-relaxed`

## Spacing & Layout
- Section padding: `py-20 px-6` or `py-24 px-8`
- Max content width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto`
- Card gap: `gap-8`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-2xl` for cards, `rounded-full` for buttons/badges

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `shadow-md` or `shadow-lg`, `rounded-2xl`
- Dividers: `border-t border-tan`
- Subtle borders: `border border-tan/50`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-golden text-white rounded-full px-8 py-3 font-semibold hover:bg-brown-700 transition`
- Secondary/Outline: `border-2 border-golden text-golden rounded-full px-8 py-3 hover:bg-golden hover:text-white transition`

## Do's
- Use warm, earthy tones throughout
- Use Playfair Display for all headings
- Use generous whitespace to feel premium
- Use stock images of bread, pastries, bakery interiors
- Subtle warm gradients on hero sections

## Don'ts
- No cold blues or grays as primary colors
- No harsh black backgrounds
- No tight, cramped layouts
- No generic sans-serif headings
