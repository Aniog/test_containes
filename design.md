# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Typography
- Primary font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-weight 700-800, tight tracking
- Body: font-weight 400-500, line-height 1.6
- Small text: font-weight 400, text-brand-300 for muted content

## Colors
- Primary brand: brand-800 (#1f2a40) - deep navy for headers and strong elements
- Secondary brand: brand-600 (#3a4a6b) - for cards and sections
- Accent: accent-500 (#c9a227) - gold/amber for CTAs and highlights
- Backgrounds: white, brand-50 (#f5f7fb), brand-900 (#141c2b)
- Text: brand-900 for headings, brand-700 for body, brand-300 for muted

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 to py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl
- Card padding: p-6 to p-8
- Gap between elements: gap-6 to gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl, border border-brand-100, shadow-sm
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Images: rounded-lg or rounded-xl
- Hover states: shadow-md transition

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-brand-800 text-white hover:bg-brand-700
- Secondary: bg-white text-brand-800 border border-brand-200 hover:border-brand-300
- Accent CTA: bg-accent-500 text-white hover:bg-accent-600

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text clearly readable against backgrounds
- Use brand-50 for alternating section backgrounds
- Use accent color sparingly for CTAs and key highlights
- Maintain consistent padding and alignment

## Don'ts
- Don't use low-contrast text
- Don't overcrowd sections
- Don't use more than 2-3 accent elements per section
- Don't use pure black or pure white for text
- Don't mix too many font weights
