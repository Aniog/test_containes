# Design Guidelines: ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- **Headings:** Bold, clean, minimal, uppercase for top-level headers to look industrial and elegant.
- **Body Text:** Legible, balanced line-height, using primarily neutral grays for reduced eye strain and elegant contrast.

## Colors
- **Brand Colors:**
  - Primary: Deep industrial blue (`#1e3a8a` / `blue-900` or slate-900) to convey trust, machinery, and professionalism.
  - Secondary/Accent: Metallic silver/gray or a striking safety orange/amber (`#f97316` / `orange-500`) for call-to-actions to represent construction and machinery.
- **Backgrounds:**
  - App Background: Clean white (`#ffffff`) or very light gray (`#f8fafc` / `slate-50`)
  - Sections: Alternating between white and dark slate/blue for contrast.

## Spacing & Layout
- **Containers:** Wide max-width for clear, large product showcasing.
- **Padding:** Generous padding (e.g. `py-16` or `py-24`) to give elements room to breathe ("elegant").
- **Grid:** Use CSS grids to organize products neatly.

## Components & Visual Style
- **Cards (Products & Features):** Subtle drop-shadow (`shadow-md`), slight border radius (`rounded-lg`), clean borders. Hover effects (slight lift `hover:-translate-y-1`) but keeping it classy.
- **Buttons:** Solid colors, slight rounding. Text uppercase or semi-bold.
- **Images:** Crisp edges, maintaining aspect ratios `4x3` or `16x9`. Use high-quality stock images representing machinery, factories, and clear paths.
- **Icons:** Use Lucide-react for crisp, elegant line icons.

## Tone
- Professional, robust, but very user-friendly. "The elegant way to shape metal."
