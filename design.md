# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium-but-accessible demi-fine jewelry. Think restrained elegance, not loud or discount.

## Color Palette
- Base: warm off-white `#F7F5F2` and deep charcoal `#1C1917`
- Surface: soft cream `#FAF8F5`
- Accent: warm metallic gold `#C5A059`
- Accent hover: deeper gold `#A8863F`
- Text primary: `#1C1917`
- Text secondary: `#78716C`
- Border: `#E7E5E4`
- Success: `#4A7C59`
- Error: `#B91C1C`

## Typography
- Headings / product names: Cormorant Garamond or Playfair Display, uppercase, wide letter-spacing
- Body / UI: Inter or Manrope
- Base size: 16px
- Line height: 1.6 for body, 1.2 for headings

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace, large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers (`border border-stone-200`)
- Restrained accent color usage
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl`
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold or outlined gold, rounded-full or rounded-lg, subtle hover transitions
- Cards: soft shadow, rounded-xl, hover lift
- Inputs: minimal border, focus ring in gold
- Dividers: thin, low-contrast

## Imagery
- Warm-lit gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Editorial, close-up, model-worn shots
- 9:16 vertical cards for UGC/reel strip

## Motion
- Subtle hover transitions (`transition-all duration-300`)
- Soft shadows on hover
- Smooth scroll behavior
