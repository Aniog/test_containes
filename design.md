# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Brand Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount, not generic.

## Color Palette
- Background: warm ivory/cream (#FAF7F2) — hsl(40, 30%, 96%)
- Surface (cards, dropdowns): warm white (#FFFDF9) — hsl(40, 25%, 99%)
- Dark backgrounds / hero overlays: deep espresso (#1C1917) — hsl(30, 20%, 10%)
- Text: rich brown-black (#2D2A24) — hsl(30, 20%, 16%)
- Muted text: warm gray (#8C857C) — hsl(30, 10%, 52%)
- Borders / dividers: light warm beige (#E3DDD4) — hsl(38, 15%, 86%)
- Accent / gold: warm gold (#C9974B) — hsl(38, 52%, 55%)
- Accent hover: deeper gold (#B8860B) — hsl(38, 100%, 38%)
- Trust / success: muted sage green
- Error: muted rose

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (Google Font), serif, elegant, light-to-regular weight
- Body / UI: Inter (Google Font), clean sans-serif
- Product names: ALL CAPS, tracking (letter-spacing) 0.08em–0.12em
- Price: Inter, medium weight, gold accent color

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Product grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Card padding: p-6

## Borders & Lines
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t (1px) in border color
- No border-radius on most containers (sharp = luxury)
- Subtle border on cards: border border-border

## Interactions
- Hover transitions: 0.3s ease-out on transforms, opacities
- Product card hover: gentle lift (translateY -2px) + shadow
- Button hover: subtle background darken or gold fill
- Nav scroll transition: 0.3s background change

## Buttons
- Primary: gold background (#C9974B), white text, full-width on mobile
- Outline: transparent, 1px gold border, gold text
- Both: uppercase, letter-spacing 0.08em, py-3 px-8, no border-radius
- Transition: background / border-color 0.3s

## Images
- Editorial style — warm lighting, gold jewelry on neutral/dark backgrounds
- Full-bleed hero images
- Product images: consistent aspect ratio (1:1 square or 3:4 portrait)
- UGC reels: vertical 9:16 cards

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable against backgrounds (high contrast)
- Use uppercase + tracking for product names
- Thin hairline separators between sections
- Warm, refined feel throughout

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy borders or thick lines
- No large border-radii (keep it sharp/clean)
- No flashy animations
- No generic stock-style imagery
