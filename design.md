# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Mood
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background (Espresso):** #1A1514 — deep warm brown-black, used for site background
- **Foreground (Cream):** #FDF8F4 — warm ivory, used for text and key elements
- **Accent (Gold):** #C8A052 — warm gold, used for buttons, links, highlights
- **Accent Light:** #E8D5B0 — light gold, used for hover states
- **Muted (Taupe):** #8B7E74 — warm gray-brown, used for secondary text
- **Card:** #2C1F12 — lighter espresso for cards and surfaces
- **Card Hover:** #3A2A18 — hover state for cards
- **Borders:** rgba(232, 221, 209, 0.12) — cream at 12% opacity

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** Playfair Display (serif), 500-600 weight, UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing for product names
- **Body & UI:** Inter (sans-serif), 300-500 weight
- **Size scale:** text-xs (0.75rem) through text-5xl (3rem)
- Product names: uppercase, tracking-widest (0.12em)
- Section headings: serif, text-3xl md:text-4xl, font-semibold

## Buttons
- **Primary (btn-accent):** Solid gold bg, espresso text, uppercase, tracking-wider, hover to light gold
- **Secondary (btn-outline):** Transparent, gold border + text, hover fills gold

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace, section padding: py-20 md:py-28 lg:py-32
- Hairline dividers: 1px solid border-t at 12% opacity

## Cards & Surfaces
- Product cards: bg-espresso-800 (#2C1F12), subtle hover lift + shadow
- Image swap on hover for product cards

## Animations
- Subtle fade-in on scroll
- Smooth 0.3-0.4s transitions on interactive elements
- Cart drawer slides in from right

## Do's
- Use warm gold accents sparingly for impact
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use hairline dividers for separation
- Keep text contrast high (cream on espresso)

## Don'ts
- No bright whites, no cool grays
- No harsh shadows or borders
- No discount badges or sale-red
- No crowded layouts
