# SSourcing China - Design Guidelines

## Visual Style
- **Tone**: Professional, trustworthy, international B2B
- **Feel**: Clean, authoritative, no-nonsense — like a reliable business partner
- **Audience**: Overseas buyers, procurement managers, import businesses

## Colors
- **Primary**: Deep navy blue `#1e40af` (trust, professionalism, international business)
  - Light variants: `#eff6ff`, `#dbeafe`, `#bfdbfe`
  - Dark variants: `#1e3a8a`, `#152354`
- **Accent**: Burnt orange `#c2410c` (CTA buttons, highlights, energy)
  - Light variants: `#fff7ed`, `#ffedd5`
  - Dark variants: `#9a3412`, `#7c2d12`
- **Neutral**: Slate grays for text and backgrounds
  - Background: `#ffffff`, `#f8fafc`, `#f1f5f9`
  - Text: `#0f172a` (headings), `#334155` (body), `#64748b` (muted)
  - Borders: `#e2e8f0`, `#cbd5e1`

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: font-weight 700-800, tracking tight
  - H1: text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl
  - H2: text-3xl md:text-4xl
  - H3: text-xl md:text-2xl
- **Body**: font-weight 400, text-base md:text-lg
- **Small/Label**: font-weight 500, text-sm

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between elements: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Components
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary CTA: bg-accent-500 text-white hover:bg-accent-600, rounded-lg, font-semibold, px-6 py-3
  - Secondary: bg-primary-500 text-white hover:bg-primary-600, rounded-lg, font-medium, px-6 py-3
  - Ghost: border-2 border-primary-500 text-primary-500 hover:bg-primary-50
- **Cards**: bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-neutral-200, hover:shadow-md transition
- **Sections**: Alternating white and neutral-50 backgrounds
- **Icons**: Lucide React, stroke-width 1.5-2, size 20-24

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep copy concise and action-oriented
- Use data points and specifics over vague claims
- Show real process steps, not abstract concepts
- Use professional stock imagery of factories, shipping, QC inspections
- Ensure high contrast for readability

## Don'ts
- Don't use exaggerated claims ("best in the world", "#1 sourcing agent")
- Don't use cartoon illustrations or playful icons
- Don't use gradient backgrounds or flashy animations
- Don't use light text on light backgrounds
- Don't clutter sections with too much information
- Don't use generic placeholder text
