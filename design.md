# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Not**: Loud, discount-looking, generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Base**: Cream (#FAF8F5) — soft warm off-white background
- **Deep neutrals**: #1A1817 (deepest), #242120, #36312D, #4A4540, #6B655C
- **Warm accents**: #E3D9C8, #D4C4A8, #F0EBE3
- **Gold accent**: #C8A45C (primary gold), #B8924A (hover)
- **Text**: deep-700 (#36312D) for body, deep-800 (#242120) for headings
- **Muted text**: deep-400 (#8F8980)

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Playfair Display (serif), weights 400-700
  - Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.12em
  - Hero headlines: 4xl-6xl, medium weight
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weights 300-600
  - Body text: 400, 15-16px
  - UI labels: 500-600, uppercase, letter-spacing: 0.12em

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: sections padded py-20 to py-32
- Cards: p-6, gap-6
- Container max-w-7xl, centered

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid dark (#242120), outlined, or gold accent (#C8A45C). Uppercase, tracked-wide, subtle hover transitions.
- **Dividers**: Hairline border-t border-deep-100
- **Cards**: Soft shadow, hover lift effect
- **Inputs**: Clean bordered style, focus ring gold

## Do's
- Use deep-800/900 for text on cream backgrounds
- Use cream or white text on dark backgrounds
- Keep product imagery warm and editorial
- Use hairline dividers, never thick borders

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy box shadows
- No generic blue links
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No crowded layouts
