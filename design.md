# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Style**: Restrained, elegant, generous whitespace, thin hairline dividers.

## Color Palette
- **Base**: `#1a1a1a` (deep charcoal for dark sections, text)
- **Cream**: `#faf8f5` (page background)
- **Gold**: `#c9a96e` (accent color, CTAs, highlights)
- **Gold Dark**: `#b8944f` (gold hover state)
- **Taupe**: `#d4c5b2` (secondary accent, image placeholders)
- **Taupe Light**: `#e8e0d6` (light backgrounds, borders)
- **Text**: `#2c2c2c` (primary text)
- **Text Light**: `#6b6b6b` (secondary text, captions)
- **Border**: `#e5e0da` (subtle dividers)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable
- **Product Names**: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-wider`)
- **Section Labels**: UPPERCASE, `tracking-widest`, gold accent color

## Spacing
- Generous padding: `py-16 md:py-24` for sections
- Card gaps: `gap-4 md:gap-6`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid dark (`bg-velmora-base`) or outlined (`border border-velmora-base`), uppercase, `tracking-widest`
- **Cards**: Image-first, hover reveals second image + quick add
- **Accordions**: Thin borders, chevron rotation animation
- **Cart Drawer**: Slide-in from right, backdrop overlay

## Animations
- Fade in: `animate-fade-in`
- Slide up: `animate-slide-up`
- Slide in right: `animate-slide-in-right`
- Hover transitions: `duration-300` or `duration-700` for images

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Keep text uppercase with wide tracking for labels
- Use gold accent sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use thin borders (`border-velmora-border`) for subtle separation

## Don'ts
- No bright or neon colors
- No rounded corners on buttons (keep sharp/premium)
- No heavy shadows
- No discount-style badges or urgency messaging
- No cluttered layouts
