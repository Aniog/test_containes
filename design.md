# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial direction: warm ivory surfaces, deep espresso typography, champagne metallic accents, thin hairline borders, generous whitespace, and softened imagery that flatters gold jewelry.

## Palette
- `velmora-ink` `#211816`: primary text and premium dark surfaces.
- `velmora-espresso` `#33241f`: dark panels, footer, hover states.
- `velmora-ivory` `#fbf7ef`: primary page background.
- `velmora-pearl` `#f2e8dc`: secondary soft neutral sections.
- `velmora-mist` `#e8ddd0`: hairline borders and subdued fills.
- `velmora-champagne` `#c59d5f`: primary accent, CTAs, stars, highlights.
- `velmora-gold` `#d9b36c`: warm metallic accent for small details.
- `velmora-sable` `#7a6558`: muted text on light surfaces.
- `velmora-blush` `#ead8cc`: newsletter and editorial accent blocks.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif. Use refined tracking and generous line-height.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif. Use medium weight for labels and buttons.
- Product names: uppercase with wide letter spacing, serif, never cramped.

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first with comfortable vertical rhythm, then expand to multi-column editorial layouts from `md` and `lg` breakpoints.
- Use large white space around premium content. Avoid crowded grids.
- Use thin `border-velmora-mist` dividers and restrained shadows.

## Components
- Primary buttons: solid `velmora-champagne` with `velmora-ink` text, subtle hover to `velmora-gold`.
- Secondary buttons: transparent/outlined with hairline border and dark text.
- Cards: soft ivory or pearl surfaces, clear foreground text, image-led layouts.
- Dark sections: use `velmora-ink` or `velmora-espresso` backgrounds with explicit `velmora-ivory` text.

## Do's
- Keep imagery warm, close-up, editorial, and jewelry-focused.
- Use subtle transitions: opacity, translate, shadow.
- Maintain strong contrast for every label, price, CTA, and product detail.

## Don'ts
- Do not use loud discount colors, generic bright ecommerce blues, heavy borders, or cluttered sale badges.
- Do not place muted text on low-contrast neutral backgrounds.
- Do not use hardcoded image URLs; use the Strikingly stock image tagging system.
