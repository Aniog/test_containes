# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora Fine Jewelry uses a quiet-luxury editorial aesthetic. The storefront should feel premium, calm, warm, and elevated. It should flatter gold jewelry without looking flashy, discount-driven, or overly minimal to the point of feeling empty.

## Color system
Commit to one refined palette sitewide:
- Background: deep espresso ink for hero and footer surfaces. Example Tailwind classes: `bg-stone-950`, `bg-neutral-950`
- Main page surface: warm ivory. Example: `bg-stone-50`
- Secondary surface: soft champagne beige. Example: `bg-stone-100`, `bg-amber-50`
- Text on light surfaces: rich charcoal. Example: `text-stone-900`
- Text on dark surfaces: warm ivory. Example: `text-stone-50`
- Accent metallic tone: muted antique gold. Example: `bg-amber-200`, `text-amber-200`, `border-amber-200`
- Subtle muted text: `text-stone-500` or `text-stone-600`
- Hairline dividers: `border-stone-200/70` on light surfaces, `border-white/10` on dark surfaces

## Typography
- Headings, logo, product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body, UI text, labels, buttons: Inter
- Product names should appear in uppercase with generous tracking. Example: `uppercase tracking-[0.28em]`
- Large editorial headings should use elegant serif proportions. Example: `font-serif text-5xl md:text-7xl`

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous whitespace and breathing room.
- Preferred section rhythm: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl`
- Use consistent horizontal padding: `px-5 sm:px-6 lg:px-10`
- Cards should have soft corners and restrained shadows, never heavy neumorphic effects.

## Components
- Buttons should feel premium and calm. Prefer either a dark solid button with warm gold text or a thin outlined button with subtle hover fills.
- Product cards should have image-first presentation, slim typography, and understated hover transitions.
- Navigation should be clean and spacious with a transparent-to-solid scroll transition over the hero.
- Trust bars, dividers, and accordions should use thin borders and subtle opacity.
- Inputs should have clear borders, warm backgrounds, and dark readable text.

## Imagery
- Use warm-lit editorial jewelry photography with dark or neutral backgrounds.
- Avoid busy lifestyle imagery, bright discount-banner colors, or generic white-background marketplace styling.
- Vertical imagery in the UGC/Reels row should feel social but still premium.

## Motion
- Keep animations subtle: opacity, translate, shadow, and color transitions around 200–400ms.
- No bouncing, scaling, or flashy entrance animations.

## Do
- Maintain strong contrast and readability on every surface.
- Use thin dividers and elegant serif hierarchy.
- Keep the experience premium-but-accessible.
- Preserve a consistent color and spacing system across every page.

## Don't
- Do not use neon colors, oversized badges, or sale-banner styling.
- Do not use low-contrast gold text on beige backgrounds.
- Do not crowd mobile layouts.
- Do not mix multiple aesthetic directions on different pages.
