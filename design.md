# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Velmora uses a quiet luxury editorial direction: deep espresso surfaces, warm ivory content areas, brushed gold accents, soft champagne highlights, generous whitespace, thin hairline dividers, and restrained motion. The site should feel premium, calm, intimate, and giftable, never loud or discount-driven.

## Colors
Use Tailwind theme tokens only, not inline hex values.
- `velmora-ink` for primary text and deep page sections.
- `velmora-espresso` for hero/nav overlays and premium dark panels.
- `velmora-ivory` for main page backgrounds.
- `velmora-porcelain` for elevated cards and forms.
- `velmora-champagne` for soft warm panels.
- `velmora-gold` for primary CTA backgrounds, icons, stars, and refined accents.
- `velmora-brass` for hover states and subtle borders.
- `velmora-muted` for supporting copy.

Do: pair dark surfaces with ivory text, light surfaces with ink text, and gold accents sparingly.
Don't: use bright sale colors, neon accents, generic blue links, or low-contrast text.

## Typography
- Headings and logo: `font-serifDisplay` using Cormorant Garamond.
- Body and UI: `font-sans` using Manrope.
- Product names: uppercase, wide letter spacing, serif, controlled line height.

## Layout & Spacing
- Mobile-first with spacious vertical rhythm.
- Desktop sections use max-width containers, large editorial imagery, and balanced two-column layouts.
- Use thin dividers (`border-velmora-hairline`) and rounded image corners for a polished editorial feel.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid gold or outlined dark/gold, pill-shaped, subtle transitions.
- Cards: soft porcelain surfaces, fine borders, gentle shadows, clear readable foreground text.
- Product imagery: warm gold jewelry on dark or neutral backgrounds via Strikingly stock image tags.
- Cart drawer: dark overlay, ivory drawer, clear quantity controls and totals.

## Motion
Use subtle transitions only: opacity, translate, scale, and color changes. Avoid bouncy or playful effects.
