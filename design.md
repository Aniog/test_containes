# MicroCosmos Design System

## Brand Identity
MicroCosmos is a visually immersive website exploring the hidden world of microscopic life — bacteria, cells, fungi, crystals, and more. The aesthetic is dark, scientific, and awe-inspiring, with vivid accent colors evoking bioluminescence and microscope imagery.

## Color Palette
- Background (deep dark): `bg-gray-950` (#030712)
- Surface / Card: `bg-gray-900` (#111827)
- Surface elevated: `bg-gray-800` (#1f2937)
- Primary accent (cyan/teal): `text-cyan-400` (#22d3ee), `bg-cyan-500` (#06b6d4)
- Secondary accent (violet): `text-violet-400` (#a78bfa)
- Highlight (emerald): `text-emerald-400` (#34d399)
- Text primary: `text-white`
- Text secondary: `text-gray-300`
- Text muted: `text-gray-500`
- Border: `border-gray-700` or `border-cyan-500/30`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Hero heading: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-extrabold tracking-tight`
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- Subheading: `text-xl font-semibold`
- Body: `text-base text-gray-300 leading-relaxed`
- Caption / label: `text-sm text-gray-500 uppercase tracking-widest`

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- Section padding: `py-20 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Components

### Cards
- Dark background with subtle border: `bg-gray-900 border border-gray-700 rounded-2xl overflow-hidden`
- Hover effect: `hover:border-cyan-500/50 hover:shadow-lg hover:shadow-cyan-500/10 transition-all duration-300`

### Buttons
- Primary: `bg-cyan-500 hover:bg-cyan-400 text-gray-950 font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-full transition`
- Ghost: `border border-cyan-500 text-cyan-400 hover:bg-cyan-500/10 px-6 py-3 rounded-full transition`

### Images
- All images use rounded corners: `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`
- Aspect ratios: hero `16x9`, gallery `4x3` or `1x1`, feature `3x2`
- Overlay gradient on hero: `bg-gradient-to-b from-transparent to-gray-950`

### Navigation
- Sticky top nav: `sticky top-0 z-50 bg-gray-950/80 backdrop-blur-md border-b border-gray-800`
- Nav links: `text-gray-300 hover:text-cyan-400 transition`

## Visual Style
- Use dark backgrounds throughout
- Accent glows: `shadow-cyan-500/20`, `shadow-violet-500/20`
- Gradient text for headings: `bg-gradient-to-r from-cyan-400 to-violet-400 bg-clip-text text-transparent`
- Section dividers: subtle `border-t border-gray-800`
- Bioluminescent glow effects on key elements

## Do's
- Use rich, full-bleed images in every section
- Maintain high contrast between text and backgrounds
- Use cyan/violet gradient accents for headings and highlights
- Keep cards dark with subtle borders

## Don'ts
- No light backgrounds
- No low-contrast text (e.g., gray on gray)
- No flat, colorless layouts
- No small or sparse image usage — images should dominate the layout
