# Design System — French Food & Dishes

## Visual Identity
Elegant, warm, and inviting. Inspired by classic French bistro aesthetics — rich creams, deep burgundy, warm gold accents, and dark charcoal text. The feel should be sophisticated yet approachable.

## Color Palette
- **Background (page):** Warm off-white `#FAF7F2` — `bg-[#FAF7F2]`
- **Surface (cards):** Pure white `#FFFFFF` — `bg-white`
- **Primary (burgundy):** `#8B1A2F` — `bg-[#8B1A2F]` / `text-[#8B1A2F]`
- **Accent (gold):** `#C9A84C` — `text-[#C9A84C]` / `border-[#C9A84C]`
- **Dark text:** Charcoal `#1C1C1C` — `text-[#1C1C1C]`
- **Muted text:** Warm gray `#6B6560` — `text-[#6B6560]`
- **Divider:** Light warm gray `#E8E0D5` — `border-[#E8E0D5]`

## Typography
- **Display / Hero headings:** Playfair Display, serif — large, elegant, italic for emphasis
- **Body / UI text:** Inter, sans-serif — clean and readable
- **Heading sizes:**
  - H1 (hero): `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold`
  - H2 (section): `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
  - H3 (card title): `text-xl font-semibold`
  - Body: `text-base` or `text-lg`
  - Caption / label: `text-sm text-[#6B6560]`

## Spacing & Layout
- Page max width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto`
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6` or `gap-8`
- Border radius: `rounded-2xl` for cards, `rounded-full` for pills/badges

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-[#E8E0D5]`
- Card shadow: `shadow-md hover:shadow-xl transition-shadow`
- Accent divider: thin gold line `border-t-2 border-[#C9A84C]`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-[#8B1A2F] text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold hover:bg-[#6e1525] transition-colors`
- Outline: `border-2 border-[#8B1A2F] text-[#8B1A2F] px-6 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold hover:bg-[#8B1A2F] hover:text-white transition-colors`

## Imagery
- Use stock images for hero, dish cards, and regional sections
- Hero: full-width, 16x9 or 3x2 ratio, overlaid with dark gradient for text legibility
- Dish cards: 4x3 ratio, object-cover, rounded top corners

## Do's
- Use Playfair Display for all headings and section titles
- Use gold accents sparingly for decorative elements (dividers, icons, highlights)
- Maintain generous whitespace between sections
- Use warm, food-photography-style imagery

## Don'ts
- Don't use cold blues or grays as primary colors
- Don't use flat, unstyled buttons
- Don't crowd sections — keep breathing room
- Don't use low-contrast text on colored backgrounds
