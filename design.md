# Design System — Spring Water Site

## Brand Identity
Clean, natural, refreshing. Inspired by mountain springs and pure water.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Blue**: `#1a6fa8` — deep spring water blue (`text-blue-700`, `bg-blue-700`)
- **Light Blue**: `#e0f2fe` — soft sky/water tint (`bg-sky-100`, `text-sky-100`)
- **Accent Teal**: `#0891b2` — fresh teal highlight (`text-cyan-600`, `bg-cyan-600`)
- **White**: `#ffffff` — clean backgrounds (`bg-white`, `text-white`)
- **Off-white**: `#f0f9ff` — subtle section backgrounds (`bg-sky-50`)
- **Dark Text**: `#0f172a` — headings (`text-slate-900`)
- **Body Text**: `#334155` — paragraphs (`text-slate-700`)
- **Muted Text**: `#64748b` — captions, labels (`text-slate-500`)

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: `font-bold` or `font-extrabold`, `tracking-tight`
  - H1: `text-5xl md:text-7xl`
  - H2: `text-3xl md:text-4xl`
  - H3: `text-xl md:text-2xl`
- **Body**: `text-base md:text-lg`, `leading-relaxed`
- **Labels/Caps**: `text-sm uppercase tracking-widest font-semibold`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28 px-6`
- Card padding: `p-8`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-8 md:gap-12`
- Max content width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl shadow-md`
- Buttons: `rounded-full`
- Dividers: `border-sky-200`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-blue-700 hover:bg-blue-800 text-white font-semibold rounded-full px-8 py-3 transition`
- Secondary/Outline: `border-2 border-blue-700 text-blue-700 hover:bg-blue-50 rounded-full px-8 py-3 transition`

## Do's
- Use sky/blue tones consistently for water theme
- Keep layouts spacious and airy
- Use large, impactful hero images
- Maintain high contrast between text and backgrounds

## Don'ts
- Don't use dark backgrounds except for footer
- Don't use more than 2 font weights in a single section
- Don't crowd elements — whitespace is key
- Don't use low-contrast text on colored backgrounds
