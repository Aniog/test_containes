# Microcosm — Design System

## Theme
Dark, scientific, immersive. Inspired by microscopy and the hidden worlds within nature.

## Colors
- Background (deep space): `bg-[#050a14]`
- Surface (card/section): `bg-[#0d1a2e]`
- Surface elevated: `bg-[#0f2040]`
- Accent primary (cyan/teal): `text-cyan-400`, `border-cyan-400`, `bg-cyan-400`
- Accent secondary (violet): `text-violet-400`
- Text primary: `text-white`
- Text secondary: `text-slate-300`
- Text muted: `text-slate-500`
- Divider: `border-slate-700`

## Typography
- Font: "Space Grotesk" (headings), "Inter" (body) — loaded via Google Fonts in index.html
- Hero title: `text-6xl md:text-8xl font-bold tracking-tight text-white`
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-white`
- Subheading: `text-xl font-semibold text-cyan-400`
- Body: `text-base text-slate-300 leading-relaxed`
- Caption: `text-sm text-slate-500`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-6 md:px-12 lg:px-24`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-4` or `gap-6`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-slate-700/50`
- Glow effect: `shadow-[0_0_30px_rgba(34,211,238,0.15)]`
- Rounded: `rounded-2xl`

## Images
- Use data-strk-img / data-strk-bg system for all stock images
- Aspect ratios: hero 16x9, gallery 1x1 or 4x3, feature 3x2

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds throughout
- Use cyan/teal as the primary accent color
- Use large, full-bleed images in hero and feature sections
- Use grid layouts for galleries (3-4 columns on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 on mobile)
- Add subtle gradient overlays on images for text legibility

## Don'ts
- No white or light backgrounds
- No bright/saturated colors other than cyan and violet accents
- No small or cramped image layouts
- No light text on light backgrounds
