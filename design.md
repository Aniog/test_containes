# Design Guidelines - SSourcing China

## Visual Style
- **Tone**: Professional, trustworthy, international, and practical.
- **Color Palette**:
  - **Primary**: Deep Navy (#0F172A) - represents stability and professionalism.
  - **Secondary**: Industrial Blue (#2563EB) - for actions and highlights.
  - **Accent**: Safety Orange (#EA580C) - used sparingly for high-visibility CTAs (industrial feel).
  - **Background**: Soft Gray (#F8FAFC) and White (#FFFFFF).
- **Typography**:
  - **Headings**: Inter, Sans-serif, Bold (700), Tight tracking.
  - **Body**: Inter, Sans-serif, Regular (400), Optimized for readability.
- **Borders & Spacing**:
  - Border Radius: `rounded-lg` (8px) for cards and buttons.
  - Spacing: Clean, generous white space to reflect international standards.
- **Components**:
  - Cards with subtle shadows (`shadow-sm`).
  - Clear, high-contrast buttons.
  - Interactive hover states.

## Do's
- Use high-quality imagery of factories, cargo, and quality inspections.
- Maintain a clean, minimalist layout.
- Use explicit labels and clear hierarchy.

## Don'ts
- No flashy animations or distracting colors.
- Avoid vague or exaggerated claims.
- No cluttered sections.
