# SSourcing China Design System

## Brand direction
- Professional, practical, and international.
- Build trust through clarity, whitespace, restrained color, and operational detail.
- Visual tone should feel like a capable sourcing partner for overseas buyers, not a consumer brand.

## Typography
- Primary font: Inter.
- Headings should feel confident and compact: `text-3xl md:text-5xl font-semibold tracking-tight`.
- Section titles: `text-2xl md:text-4xl font-semibold tracking-tight`.
- Body text: `text-base md:text-lg leading-7 text-slate-600`.

## Color system
- Page background: `bg-white` or `bg-slate-50`.
- Main text: `text-slate-900`.
- Secondary text: `text-slate-600`.
- Borders: `border-slate-200`.
- Primary action color: `bg-blue-700 text-white hover:bg-blue-800`.
- Secondary action color: `bg-white text-slate-900 border border-slate-300 hover:bg-slate-50`.
- Trust highlights: `bg-emerald-50 text-emerald-700`.
- Operational accents and warning/problem areas: `bg-amber-50 text-amber-700`.

## Surfaces
- Main cards: `rounded-3xl border border-slate-200 bg-white shadow-sm`.
- Featured sections: `rounded-[32px] bg-slate-900 text-white`.
- Inputs: `rounded-2xl border border-slate-300 bg-white text-slate-900 placeholder:text-slate-400`.

## Spacing
- Standard page section spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`.
- Content container: `mx-auto max-w-7xl px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`.
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`.
- Grid gaps: `gap-6 md:gap-8`.

## Imagery
- Use realistic factory, supplier verification, QC inspection, packaging, and shipping visuals.
- Images should support trust and process clarity.
- Prefer wide industrial/editorial visuals over glossy lifestyle imagery.

## Do
- Keep layouts airy and structured.
- Use clear section intros and practical bullet points.
- Maintain strong contrast for every text surface.
- Use blue for primary actions and emerald for credibility cues.

## Don't
- Do not use flashy gradients or exaggerated startup visuals.
- Do not use oversized claims, countdowns, or aggressive sales language.
- Do not place light text on light backgrounds or muted text on busy images.
- Do not overcrowd desktop layouts into narrow single-column sections.
