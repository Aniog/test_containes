# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry for women 25–45. No loud discount cues, no generic e-commerce chrome.

## Color palette
- Base: `#1a1816` — deep espresso/charcoal for text and strong UI
- Base light: `#2a2623` — hover/secondary dark surfaces
- Background: `#f7f5f2` — warm ivory page background
- Card: `#ffffff` — clean product cards and panels
- Accent: `#c8a46e` — muted warm gold for CTAs and highlights
- Accent hover: `#b08d57` — deeper gold on interaction
- Muted: `#9a9590` — warm stone for secondary text
- Border: `#e3dfd8` — hairline dividers
- Cream: `#efeae3` — soft blocks/newsletter

## Typography
- Headings / product names: **Cormorant Garamond** (serif), light to normal weight
- Body / UI: **Inter** (sans-serif), 300–600 weights
- Product names are UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`0.18em`)
- Headings use tight line-height (`1.0–1.1`); body uses `1.6`

## Spacing & layout
- Mobile-first; generous whitespace on desktop
- Container max-width `1280px` with `px-5 md:px-8`
- Section vertical spacing `py-16 md:py-24`
- Hairline dividers (`1px` border color) between sections

## Components
- Primary button: solid gold background, white text, uppercase tracking, no radius
- Secondary button: 1px dark border, transparent background, uppercase tracking
- Cards: white background, subtle soft shadow, no border radius
- Inputs: 1px border, transparent background, focus ring on accent

## Imagery
- Warm gold jewelry on dark or neutral backgrounds
- Large editorial hero; product grid uses consistent 4:3 or 3:4 ratios
- UGC strip uses 9:16 vertical cards

## Animation
- Subtle hover transitions `300–400ms`
- Product card hover reveals second image and quick-add overlay
- Navigation background transitions on scroll
