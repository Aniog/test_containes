# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** ivory-50 (#fefcf8) — warm off-white editorial base
- **Dark text / accents:** midnight-950 (#1a1510) — deep espresso, not pure black
- **Warm accent:** brand-600 (#c06e24) — burnished gold, used for CTAs, highlights
- **Light accent:** warm-50 (#faf7f2) — used for subtle section backgrounds, cards
- **Muted:** midnight-200 (#d2ccb4) / midnight-400 (#a19369) / midnight-600 (#786547) — for borders, secondary text
- **White overlays on dark:** white with varying opacity, ivory-100/200/300 tints

## Typography
- **Headings / product names:** Cormorant Garamond (serif), light/semibold weights, uppercase with wide letter-spacing for product names
- **Body / UI:** Inter (sans), 300/400/500 weights, clean and minimal
- **Product names:** uppercase, tracking-widest (0.12em+)
- **Section labels:** uppercase, tracking-wider, 11-12px, sans-serif

## Layout
- Max width: 1536px (screen-2xl) centered
- Generous whitespace throughout
- 4px/8px/16px/24px/32px spacing scale
- Section padding: 64px/96px (py-16/py-24) vertically
- Thin hairline dividers (border-t border-midnight-200/60)

## Components
- **Buttons:** btn-primary (brand-600 bg, white text, uppercase, tracking-wider) / btn-outline (border, midnight-950 text, hover fills dark)
- **Cards:** no borders, soft shadow, hover scale effect on images
- **Product cards:** 4:5 aspect ratio images, hover reveals second image + quick-add button
- **Navigation:** transparent over hero, solid on scroll, subtle backdrop-blur
- **Cart drawer:** slides in from right, full-height, max-w-md

## Do's
- Use warm-toned imagery (gold jewelry, dark/neutral backgrounds)
- Keep text clearly readable — high contrast at all times
- Use serif for elegance, sans for utility
- Thin, refined borders and dividers
- Subtle hover transitions (300-500ms)

## Don'ts
- No bright or cold colors (no blues, neons, harsh whites)
- No generic e-commerce patterns (no heavy borders, no flashy badges)
- No pure black text on white — use midnight-950 on ivory-50
- No loud animations or gimmicks
- No emoji usage in UI text
