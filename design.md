# MicroCosmos Design System

## Theme Concept
"MicroCosmos" - A journey into the invisible, microscopic world. The theme should be dark, mysterious, and awe-inspiring, letting the vibrant colors of microscopic and macro photography pop.

## Colors
- **Background**: Dark, almost black. (e.g., `bg-slate-950`)
- **Text**: Light gray/white for readability. (e.g., `text-slate-100`, `text-slate-300`)
- **Accent**: A vibrant bio-luminescent green or blue (e.g., `text-emerald-400`, `bg-emerald-500`)

## Typography
- Main font: 'Inter', system font.
- Headings: Bold, slightly glowing or high contrast.

## Layout & Spacing
- Use extensive image grids to fulfill the "much more pictures" requirement.
- Smooth paddings (`py-16`, `py-24`) between sections.
- Rounded corners for image cards (`rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`).

## Image Guidelines
- High usage of `data-strk-img` and `data-strk-bg`.
- Interpolated descriptions should target words like: "microscopic", "macro photography", "tiny insect", "cellular structure", "water drop macro", "leaf texture", "microcosm".
