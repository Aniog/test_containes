# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount.
- Audience: Women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase, $30–$120 price point.

## Color Palette
- **velmora-cream**: #FAF7F2 — Primary background, warm off-white
- **velmora-ivory**: #F5F0E8 — Secondary background, cards
- **velmora-charcoal**: #2C2825 — Primary text, headings
- **velmora-graphite**: #4A4541 — Secondary text, body copy
- **velmora-stone**: #8A8580 — Muted text, captions
- **velmora-gold**: #C5A572 — Primary accent, CTAs, highlights
- **velmora-gold-dark**: #A88B5A — Hover state for gold
- **velmora-gold-light**: #D4BC94 — Light accent, subtle fills
- **velmora-rose**: #E8D5C8 — Secondary accent, soft blush
- **velmora-border**: #E5E0D8 — Hairline dividers, borders

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Google Font) — elegant serif
- **Body/UI**: Inter (Google Font) — clean sans-serif
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.2em]` letter-spacing
- **Heading weights**: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold)

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Generous section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card border radius: rounded-lg (8px)
- Button border radius: rounded-none (sharp, luxury feel)

## Components
- Buttons: Solid gold fill or outlined with gold border. Hover: darken gold or fill. Sharp corners.
- Cards: Ivory background, subtle shadow, hairline border. Hover: slight lift.
- Dividers: 1px border-velmora-border, thin and elegant
- Nav: Transparent over hero, solid velmora-cream on scroll

## Animations
- Transitions: 300ms ease for hovers, 500ms for page sections
- Hover on cards: scale(1.02) with shadow lift
- Button hover: color shift, no transform
- Subtle fade-in on scroll for sections

## Don'ts
- No bright or neon colors
- No rounded pill buttons (except variant selectors)
- No heavy shadows or gradients
- No busy patterns or textures
- No emoji in UI
