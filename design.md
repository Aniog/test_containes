# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. No loud discount vibes. Generous whitespace, thin hairlines, restrained accent color.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF7F2` (warm cream)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (white cards)
- Primary text: `#1A1714` (warm charcoal)
- Secondary text: `#6B6560` (warm gray)
- Muted text: `#A39E99` (light warm gray)
- Accent: `#C9A96E` (muted gold)
- Accent hover: `#B8975A` (darker gold)
- Dark section: `#1A1714` (warm charcoal)
- Border: `#E8E2DA` (warm hairline)
- Error: `#C45B4A`
- Success: `#5A8F6E`

## Typography
- Headings / product names: Cormorant Garamond, serif. Uppercase for product names with wide letter-spacing.
- Body / UI: Inter, sans-serif.
- Hero headline: 48–64px serif, weight 400, line-height 1.1.
- Section titles: 32–40px serif, weight 400.
- Product names: 14px serif, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.15em.
- Body: 16px sans, weight 400, line-height 1.65.
- Small UI: 12–13px sans, weight 500, letter-spacing 0.05em.

## Spacing
- Section padding: 80–120px vertical on desktop, 48–64px on mobile.
- Container max-width: 1280px, centered.
- Grid gap: 24–32px.
- Card border-radius: 0 (sharp editorial edges) or 2px.
- Button border-radius: 0.

## Components
- Buttons: solid accent fill with white text, or 1px dark outline. Hover: slight background darken or scale(1.02). Transition 300ms ease.
- Inputs: 1px border (#E8E2DA), no border-radius, focus ring in accent.
- Cards: no shadow by default, subtle hover shadow.
- Dividers: 1px #E8E2DA hairlines.

## Animation
- Subtle hover transitions (300ms ease) on all interactive elements.
- Navbar: transparent → solid on scroll.
- Image hover: slight scale(1.03) with overflow hidden.
