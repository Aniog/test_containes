# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - `font-serif`
  - Uppercase with wide letter spacing for titles: `tracking-[0.2em]`
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - `font-sans`

## Palette
- **Primary (Deep Refined Base)**: `bg-[#1A1A1A]` (Deep Charcoal) / `text-[#F5F5F0]` (Off-white)
- **Accent (Warm Metallic)**: `text-[#C5A059]` (Gold)
- **Backgrounds**: 
  - Main: `bg-[#FDFDFB]` (Very light cream/white)
  - Dark: `bg-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Dividers**: `border-[#E5E5E0]` (Thin hairline)

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: `bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white hover:bg-[#333333] transition-all`
  - Outline: `border border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A] hover:bg-[#1A1A1A] hover:text-white transition-all`
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, editorial feel.
- **Components**: Subtle shadows, fine lines, elegant hover states.
