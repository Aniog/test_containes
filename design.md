# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design Guidelines

## Visual Identity
*   **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium.
*   **Palette**:
    *   Cream (Background): `#F9F6F1` (Tailwind: `bg-brand-cream`)
    *   Gold (Accent): `#D4AF37` (Tailwind: `text-brand-gold`, `bg-brand-gold`)
    *   Charcoal (Text/Primary): `#1A1A1A` (Tailwind: `text-brand-charcoal`)
    *   Stone (Muted): `#7A7A7A` (Tailwind: `text-brand-stone`)
*   **Typography**:
    *   Headings/Product Names: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif). Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`).
    *   Body/UI: `Inter` (Sans-serif).
*   **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers (`border-brand-stone/20`).
*   **Spacing**: Generous whitespace.
*   **Buttons**: Solid Charcoal or Outlined Gold/Charcoal.
*   **Images**: Large editorial imagery, warm-lit, close-ups.

## Do's and Don'ts
*   **Do**: Use uppercase for product names and section titles.
*   **Do**: Use hairline dividers for sections.
*   **Do**: Incorporate subtle hover transitions on cards.
*   **Don't**: Use loud colors or heavy shadows.
*   **Don't**: Use rounded corners on buttons (keep them sharp or very slightly rounded).
