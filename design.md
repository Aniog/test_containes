# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Quiet luxury with a warm editorial mood. The site should feel refined, calm, and premium-but-accessible. Avoid loud promotional styling, crowded layouts, bright discount colors, or heavy gradients.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body, navigation, buttons, labels, inputs: Inter
- Product names should appear uppercase with generous tracking.

## Color system
Use one consistent palette throughout the site:
- Background base: deep espresso-plum
- Elevated dark surfaces: softened charcoal-plum
- Light editorial surface: warm ivory
- Accent metallic: antique gold
- Accent soft gold: champagne gold
- Primary text on dark: warm ivory
- Secondary text on dark: muted beige-lilac
- Primary text on light: near-black brown
- Hairline borders: translucent warm gold or soft ivory depending on surface

Suggested Tailwind token mapping:
- bg-velvet
- bg-velvet-soft
- bg-ivory
- text-ivory
- text-ink
- text-mist
- text-gold
- border-line-dark
- border-line-light
- bg-gold
- bg-gold-soft

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous vertical spacing: sections should generally use py-16 to py-24 on mobile/desktop scaling.
- Max content width should feel editorial, typically max-w-7xl.
- Use thin dividers and soft shadows, never heavy borders.
- Cards should have rounded corners, but keep them refined and not overly playful.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, slim, slightly tall, with subtle hover transitions. Prefer solid antique gold on dark backgrounds and refined outline buttons elsewhere.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid dark surface on scroll.
- Product cards: strong imagery, uppercase product name, restrained metadata, elegant hover motion.
- Inputs: light surface with dark readable text.
- Cart drawer: dark elevated panel with clearly readable totals and controls.

## Imagery
- Favor close-up warm-lit jewelry imagery on model or soft neutral props.
- Use dark or neutral backdrops that flatter gold tones.
- UGC strip should mimic polished vertical social content, not casual low-quality thumbnails.

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text always readable.
- Use subtle transitions, soft shadow, and generous whitespace.
- Maintain a premium editorial rhythm.

## Don't
- Do not use neon, harsh gradients, sale-badge styling, or coupon aesthetics.
- Do not rely on inherited text colors on custom surfaces.
- Do not stack desktop layouts into overly narrow mobile-style columns on large screens.
