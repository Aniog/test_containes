# MicroCosmos Design System

## Theme
A dark, immersive design inspired by the microscopic world. Deep navy/black backgrounds with vibrant accent colors that evoke bioluminescence and cellular structures.

## Colors
- **Background**: `bg-slate-950` (deep dark navy)
- **Surface**: `bg-slate-900` (card backgrounds)
- **Surface Hover**: `bg-slate-800`
- **Primary Accent**: `text-emerald-400` / `bg-emerald-500` (bioluminescent green)
- **Secondary Accent**: `text-cyan-400` / `bg-cyan-500` (aquatic blue)
- **Tertiary Accent**: `text-purple-400` / `bg-purple-500` (cellular purple)
- **Text Primary**: `text-white`
- **Text Secondary**: `text-slate-300`
- **Text Muted**: `text-slate-400`
- **Border**: `border-slate-700`

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (via system)
- **Hero Title**: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold tracking-tight`
- **Section Title**: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- **Subtitle**: `text-lg md:text-xl text-slate-300`
- **Body**: `text-base text-slate-300`
- **Caption**: `text-sm text-slate-400`

## Spacing
- **Section Padding**: `py-20 md:py-28`
- **Container**: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- **Card Padding**: `p-4 md:p-6`
- **Gap between cards**: `gap-4 md:gap-6`

## Borders & Shadows
- **Card Border**: `border border-slate-700/50 rounded-xl`
- **Image Border**: `rounded-lg overflow-hidden`
- **Glow Effect**: `shadow-lg shadow-emerald-500/10`

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds with bright accent text for contrast
- Use rounded corners on all cards and images
- Add subtle glow effects on hover states
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Overlay text on images with dark gradients

## Don'ts
- Don't use light backgrounds
- Don't use small text without sufficient contrast
- Don't crowd elements together
- Don't use sharp corners
