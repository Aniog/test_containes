# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial direction: warm ivory pages, deep espresso text, antique gold accents, restrained shadows, thin hairline dividers, and generous whitespace. The brand should feel premium and calm, never loud or discount-driven.

## Palette
- `velmora-ivory` `#F7F1E8`: primary page background and soft sections.
- `velmora-porcelain` `#FFFCF7`: elevated cards, drawers, and form surfaces.
- `velmora-espresso` `#201812`: primary foreground, nav text, headings.
- `velmora-walnut` `#4E3728`: secondary text and soft UI copy.
- `velmora-truffle` `#7A6758`: muted details, captions, icons.
- `velmora-gold` `#B8863B`: primary accent for buttons, prices, rules, and selected states.
- `velmora-champagne` `#E7CFA7`: soft accent backgrounds and hover states.
- `velmora-onyx` `#0F0B08`: deep editorial overlays and dark hero surfaces.

## Typography
- Headings and product names use Cormorant Garamond, elegant and high contrast.
- Body and interface labels use Manrope, clean and modern.
- Product names are uppercase with wide letter spacing.

## Layout and spacing
- Use mobile-first layouts that expand to multi-column grids at tablet and desktop widths.
- Prefer generous vertical padding, max-width containers, and consistent page gutters.
- Use thin `border-velmora-champagne` or `border-velmora-espresso/10` dividers.

## Components
- Primary buttons: solid antique gold with dark text, rounded-full, subtle hover darkening.
- Secondary buttons: transparent/porcelain surface with thin gold or espresso border.
- Product cards: porcelain surfaces, editorial imagery, soft shadow, hairline border, quiet hover lift.
- Forms: porcelain or ivory background, espresso text, clear focus rings in gold.

## Do's
- Use warm editorial imagery and restrained overlay gradients.
- Keep important text high contrast: espresso on ivory/porcelain, ivory on onyx overlays.
- Use slow, subtle transitions for hover states.

## Don'ts
- Do not use bright sale colors, loud badges, heavy drop shadows, or generic ecommerce styling.
- Do not place low-contrast tan text over light backgrounds for essential information.
- Do not mix multiple accent palettes.
