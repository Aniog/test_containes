# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium.
- **Concept**: Minimalist but warm, focusing on the craftsmanship and timelessness of gold jewelry.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF9F6` (Alabaster) - `bg-[#FAF9F6]`
- **Foreground**: `#1A1A1A` (Onyx) - `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Accent**: `#C5A059` (Soft Gold) - `text-[#C5A059]`, `bg-[#C5A059]`
- **Muted**: `#717171` (Stone) - `text-[#717171]`
- **Border**: `#E5E5E5` (Platinum) - `border-[#E5E5E5]`
- **Surface**: `#F4F1EA` (Sand) - `bg-[#F4F1EA]`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif). Used for brand name, page titles, and section headers.
  - Class: `font-serif`
  - Style: Elegant, sometimes italicized for emphasis.
- **Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond, UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing.
  - Class: `font-serif uppercase tracking-[0.2em]`
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif). Used for descriptions, navigation, and interface elements.
  - Class: `font-sans`

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Onyx with Alabaster text. `bg-[#1A1A1A] text-[#FAF9F6] px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm`
  - Secondary: Outlined Onyx. `border border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A] px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm hover:bg-[#1A1A1A] hover:text-[#FAF9F6] transition-colors`
- **Dividers**: Thin hairlines. `h-[1px] bg-[#E5E5E5]`
- **Transitions**: Soft fade and subtle scale on hover. `transition-all duration-500 ease-out`
- **Whitespace**: Generous margins and padding to create an editorial feel.
