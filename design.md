# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- Industrial precision meets elegant simplicity
- Professional, trustworthy, modern

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Navy):** `navy-900` (#0f172a), `navy-800` (#1e293b), `navy-700` (#334155)
- **Accent (Amber/Gold):** `accent-500` (#f59e0b), `accent-400` (#fbbf24), `accent-600` (#d97706)
- **Neutral:** `slate-50` (#f8fafc), `slate-100` (#f1f5f9), `slate-200` (#e2e8f0), `slate-600` (#475569), `slate-800` (#1e293b)
- **White:** #ffffff for cards and content areas
- **Background:** Light pages use `slate-50`, dark sections use `navy-900`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-tight
  - H1: text-5xl lg:text-6xl
  - H2: text-3xl lg:text-4xl
  - H3: text-xl lg:text-2xl
- Body: text-base, text-slate-600 on light, text-slate-300 on dark
- Small/Caption: text-sm, text-slate-500

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 lg:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 lg:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 lg:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl, shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Borders: border border-slate-200

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-accent-500 hover:bg-accent-600 text-navy-900 font-semibold rounded-lg px-6 py-3
- Secondary: border-2 border-navy-900 text-navy-900 hover:bg-navy-900 hover:text-white rounded-lg px-6 py-3
- On dark: border-2 border-white text-white hover:bg-white hover:text-navy-900

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable with high contrast
- Use subtle transitions on hover
- Maintain consistent spacing rhythm
- Use amber/gold sparingly as accent

## Don'ts
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors
- Don't use low-contrast text
- Don't crowd elements together
- Don't use arbitrary hex codes outside the palette
