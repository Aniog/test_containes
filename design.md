# SSourcing China - B2B Website Design System

## Visual Style

**Overall Aesthetic:** Clean, trustworthy, international B2B professional
- White and light gray backgrounds (#FFFFFF, #F8FAFC)
- Navy blue primary (#1E3A5F) for trust and professionalism
- Steel blue secondary (#3B6B9A) for accents
- Charcoal text (#1F2937) for readability
- Muted gray (#6B7280) for secondary text
- Success green (#059669) for trust indicators

## Typography

- Font: Inter (system-ui fallback)
- Headings: 600-700 weight, tight tracking
- Body: 400-500 weight, 1.6 line-height
- Sizes: 14px base, 16px body, 20-24px subheadings, 32-48px headlines

## Colors

```css
--primary: #1E3A5F;
--primary-light: #3B6B9A;
--secondary: #F1F5F9;
--text: #1F2937;
--text-muted: #6B7280;
--success: #059669;
--border: #E5E7EB;
--white: #FFFFFF;
```

## Components

- Buttons: Solid navy primary, outline secondary, rounded-lg, 44px min height
- Cards: White bg, subtle shadow, rounded-xl, 24px padding
- Forms: Clean inputs with 8px border radius, focus ring on navy
- Navigation: Fixed top bar, clean links, logo left, CTA right
- Sections: Generous 80-120px vertical padding

## Layout

- Max width: 1280px centered
- Responsive: Mobile-first, breakpoints at 768px, 1024px
- Grid: 12-column on desktop, stacked on mobile
- Spacing: Consistent 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px increments

## Do's

- Use ample white space
- Keep copy concise and practical
- Show realistic factory/QC/shipping imagery
- Use icons sparingly for clarity
- Maintain professional tone throughout

## Don'ts

- No exaggerated claims or hype language
- No bright or playful colors
- No decorative elements that distract
- No stock photo clichés
- No popups or intrusive elements
