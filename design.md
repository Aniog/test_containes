# Velmora Design System

## Direction
Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial look: warm neutrals, refined dark text, soft metallic accents, thin dividers, and generous spacing. The storefront should feel premium, feminine, and calm rather than trend-heavy or discount-led.

## Tailwind tokens
- Background: `bg-velmora-pearl`
- Soft surfaces: `bg-velmora-mist`
- Primary text: `text-velmora-ink`
- Secondary text: `text-velmora-truffle`
- Accent: `text-velmora-gold`, `border-velmora-gold`, `bg-velmora-gold`
- Dark editorial surface: `bg-velmora-ink`
- Soft rose accent surface: `bg-velmora-rose`

## Typography
- Display headings and product names: `font-display`
- Body copy and UI: `font-sans`
- Product names should use uppercase with airy tracking such as `uppercase tracking-[0.3em]` only if converted into a token or component style; prefer existing utility combinations like `uppercase tracking-[0.28em]` sparingly.
- Keep body text readable and understated.

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous vertical rhythm: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Keep content centered with `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Use thin borders such as `border border-velmora-ink/10`
- Prefer rounded editorial corners like `rounded-3xl`

## Components
- Buttons should feel premium: solid dark with light text, or pearl/transparent with dark borders.
- Cards should use soft shadows and elegant hover lift.
- Image areas should feel spacious and never cramped.
- Use restrained motion: opacity, translate, shadow, and color transitions.

## Do
- Keep contrast high and text legible on every surface.
- Use gold accents sparingly for emphasis.
- Let imagery, spacing, and typography create the luxury feel.
- Keep product UI clean and confidence-building.

## Don't
- Do not use loud sale styling, bright gradients, or flashy badges.
- Do not crowd the layout with dense copy.
- Do not mix multiple color directions.
- Do not use low-contrast text on warm neutral backgrounds.
