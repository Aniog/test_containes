# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual direction
Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial look with a deep espresso base, warm champagne gold accents, soft ivory surfaces, and restrained blush-taupe secondary tones. The feeling should be intimate, premium, and modern rather than flashy.

## Core palette
- Background base: deep espresso-brown / near-black surfaces for hero, footer, and premium panels.
- Main light surface: warm ivory / soft cream for page backgrounds and cards.
- Accent metallic: muted champagne gold for buttons, borders, ratings, and premium highlights.
- Secondary neutral: taupe / mushroom for muted blocks and dividers.
- Text on dark: soft ivory, never pure white glare.
- Text on light: deep espresso for strong readability.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: elegant serif, preferably Cormorant Garamond.
- Body, navigation, filters, UI controls: Inter.
- Product names should be uppercase with generous tracking.
- Headings should feel airy with large line-height and moderate weight.

## Spacing and layout
- Use large vertical spacing between sections.
- Keep content width refined and centered.
- Use thin hairline borders and gentle rounded corners.
- Avoid cramped layouts, especially on mobile.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, slightly rounded, subtle hover lift, no harsh gradients.
- Cards: soft shadow, clean borders, generous padding.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, turns solid with blur on scroll.
- Inputs: understated borders, clear focus states, no loud colors.

## Imagery
- Editorial, warm-lit, intimate close-ups of jewelry on skin, fabric, or minimal props.
- Favor dark neutral or warm cream scenes that flatter gold.
- Avoid loud props, discount-sale energy, saturated backgrounds, or busy compositions.

## Do
- Use consistent gold accent styling across buttons, badges, and stars.
- Keep transitions subtle and smooth.
- Make all text readable with explicit foreground colors.
- Preserve a premium feel on mobile with stacked but spacious layouts.

## Do not
- Do not use bright promotional reds or neon accents.
- Do not use pure black on pure white with no warmth.
- Do not make UI look like a generic marketplace.
- Do not overcrowd grids or collapse spacing too tightly.
