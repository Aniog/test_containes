# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette

### Primary
- **Deep Charcoal** `#1C1917` — main text, nav solid bg, footer bg (stone-900)
- **Warm Cream** `#FAF7F2` — page background, card backgrounds (custom)
- **Gold Accent** `#B8860B` — CTA buttons, accent highlights, links (custom gold)
- **Gold Light** `#D4A843` — hover states, secondary gold (custom)

### Neutrals
- **Stone-800** `#292524` — secondary dark text
- **Stone-600** `#57534E` — muted text, captions
- **Stone-400** `#A8A29E` — dividers, borders, disabled
- **Stone-200** `#E7E5E4` — light borders, subtle dividers
- **Stone-100** `#F5F5F4` — alternate section bg
- **White** `#FFFFFF` — card surfaces, modals

### Semantic
- **Success** `#166534` — confirmation messages
- **Error** `#991B1B` — error states

## Typography

### Headings & Product Names
- Font: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- Weight: 400–600
- Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em–0.25em
- Hero headline: text-5xl to text-7xl, tracking-wide
- Section headings: text-2xl to text-4xl

### Body & UI
- Font: Inter (sans-serif)
- Weight: 300–500
- Body text: text-sm to text-base, tracking-normal
- Buttons: uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em, text-xs to text-sm
- Navigation: uppercase, letter-spacing 0.15em, text-xs

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace — sections py-20 to py-28
- Container max-width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Product grid gap: 6–8 (24–32px)
- Hairline dividers: border-stone-200, border-t

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-gold text-white, uppercase, tracking-widest, px-8 py-3, hover:bg-gold-light
- Secondary: border-gold text-gold, uppercase, tracking-widest, px-8 py-3, hover:bg-gold hover:text-white
- Transition: duration-300

## Cards & Surfaces
- Product cards: bg-white, subtle shadow on hover, rounded-none (sharp corners for editorial feel)
- Image aspect ratios: 3x4 for products, 16x9 for hero, 9x16 for UGC reels
- Hover: second image reveal, subtle scale(1.02)

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth transitions (duration-300, ease-in-out)
- No bouncy or playful animations
- Fade-in on scroll where appropriate
- Cart drawer slides from right

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Maintain warm, editorial tone
- Ensure strong contrast (dark text on light bg, light text on dark bg)

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/saturated colors
- Don't use rounded corners on cards (editorial = sharp)
- Don't use playful or bouncy animations
- Don't mix too many font weights
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (badges, countdown timers, etc.)
