# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.
- Target: Women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase, $30–$120 price point.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF7F2` (warm off-white / cream)
- **Surface / Card**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white)
- **Text Primary**: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6B6460` (warm gray-brown)
- **Text Muted**: `#9C9490` (light warm gray)
- **Accent**: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod — warm gold)
- **Accent Hover**: `#9A7209` (deeper gold)
- **Accent Light**: `#F5ECD7` (pale gold wash)
- **Border**: `#E8E2DA` (warm light border)
- **Divider**: `#D4CCC2` (warm mid border)
- **Dark Section BG**: `#1A1714` (deep warm black for contrast sections)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weights 400/500/600
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weights 300/400/500/600
- **Product Names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond 500
- **Buttons**: Inter 500, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em

### Font Sizes
- Hero headline: text-5xl md:text-7xl
- Section heading: text-3xl md:text-4xl
- Product name: text-lg tracking-widest uppercase
- Body: text-base
- Small / caption: text-sm

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: py-20 md:py-28 for sections
- Max content width: max-w-7xl mx-auto
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Card padding: p-6

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E8E2DA]
- Card borders: border border-[#E8E2DA] or shadow-sm
- No heavy borders or thick outlines

## Buttons
- **Primary (accent)**: bg-[#B8860B] text-white hover:bg-[#9A7209], uppercase, tracking-wider, px-8 py-3, transition-all duration-300
- **Secondary (outlined)**: border border-[#B8860B] text-[#B8860B] hover:bg-[#B8860B] hover:text-white, uppercase, tracking-wider
- **Subtle**: text-[#6B6460] hover:text-[#1A1A1A], underline on hover

## Shadows
- Soft only: shadow-sm, shadow-md on hover
- No hard shadows

## Hover Transitions
- All interactive: transition-all duration-300
- Product cards: subtle scale on hover (hover:scale-[1.02])
- Images: opacity transition for second image reveal

## Icons
- Lucide React icons, stroke-width 1.5 for elegance

## Do's
- Use warm, golden tones throughout
- Keep whitespace generous
- Use serif for emotional/editorial moments
- Use thin hairline dividers between sections
- Ensure strong text contrast on all backgrounds

## Don'ts
- No bright/saturated colors
- No heavy shadows or thick borders
- No discount/sale aesthetics
- No generic e-commerce look
- No low-contrast text (especially on dark sections)
