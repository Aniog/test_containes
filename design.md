# Design System: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - Desktop: `text-4xl`, `font-serif`
  - Mobile: `text-3xl`, `font-serif`
  - Notes: Product names in UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.2em]`
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - Desktop: `text-base`, `font-sans`
  - Notes: Clean, readable, light/regular weights.

## Colors
- **Primary (Background)**: `#fcfbf7` (Warm off-white/editorial cream) -> `bg-[#fcfbf7]`
- **Secondary (Text)**: `#1a1a1a` (Off-black) -> `text-[#1a1a1a]`
- **Accent (Gold/Metallic)**: `#b49b6b` (Muted gold) -> `text-[#b49b6b]`, `bg-[#b49b6b]`
- **Muted (Dividers/Borders)**: `#e5e5e5` (Thin hairlines) -> `border-[#e5e5e5]`

## Layout & Spacing
- Generous whitespace: `py-16` or `py-24` for sections.
- Thin borders: `border-t-[1px] border-[#e5e5e5]`
- Buttons: Solid accent or outlined accent. `px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-wider text-sm`.

## Components
- **Navbar**: Sticky, transparent to solid transition.
- **Card**: Large image, serif text, minimal metadata.
- **Drawer**: Cart drawer with slide-in animation.
