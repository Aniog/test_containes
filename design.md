# Flower Shop Design System

## Visual Style
A warm, elegant, and natural design that reflects the beauty and freshness of flowers.

## Colors
- Primary: `bg-rose-500` (warm rose pink)
- Primary hover: `hover:bg-rose-600`
- Secondary: `bg-green-600` (natural leaf green)
- Secondary hover: `hover:bg-green-700`
- Accent: `bg-amber-400` (sunny yellow)
- Background: `bg-rose-50` (very light rose)
- Card backgrounds: `bg-white`
- Text primary: `text-gray-900`
- Text secondary: `text-gray-600`
- Text muted: `text-gray-500`

## Typography
- Headings: `font-serif` with `font-bold` or `font-semibold`
- Body text: `font-sans` (default Inter font)
- Large headings: `text-4xl lg:text-6xl`
- Section headings: `text-3xl lg:text-4xl`
- Subheadings: `text-xl lg:text-2xl`
- Body text: `text-base lg:text-lg`

## Spacing & Layout
- Section padding: `py-16 lg:py-24`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 lg:p-8`
- Button padding: `px-6 py-3` or `px-8 py-4` for larger buttons

## Borders & Shadows
- Card shadows: `shadow-lg hover:shadow-xl`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-lg` for cards, `rounded-full` for buttons
- Subtle borders: `border border-rose-200`

## Interactive Elements
- Buttons: Primary uses `bg-rose-500 hover:bg-rose-600 text-white`, Secondary uses `bg-green-600 hover:bg-green-700 text-white`
- Hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300`
- Focus states: `focus:ring-2 focus:ring-rose-500 focus:ring-offset-2`

## Do's
- Use warm, natural colors that evoke flowers and nature
- Maintain generous white space for elegance
- Use high-quality imagery with proper aspect ratios
- Ensure text has sufficient contrast against backgrounds
- Use consistent spacing throughout

## Don'ts
- Avoid harsh, cold colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much content
- Avoid using too many different fonts
- Don't use low-contrast text combinations
- Avoid sharp, angular designs - prefer soft, rounded elements
