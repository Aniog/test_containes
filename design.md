# Design Guidelines - ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style
Elegant, professional, industrial, and user-friendly. The site should convey reliability and high-end engineering.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Global default)
- **Heading Font**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Bold, 600-800 weight)
- **Body Font**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Normal, 400 weight)
- **Example Class**: `font-sans`

## Colors
- **Primary**: Navy/Deep Blue (#1e293b) - `bg-slate-800`, `text-slate-800`
- **Secondary**: Industrial Gray (#64748b) - `bg-slate-500`, `text-slate-500`
- **Accent**: Precision Orange or Gold (#f59e0b) - `bg-amber-500`, `text-amber-500`
- **Background**: Light Gray/White (#f8fafc) - `bg-slate-50`
- **Surface**: White (#ffffff) - `bg-white`

## Borders & Spacing
- **Border Radius**: 4px (Sharp, professional) - `rounded-sm`
- **Spacing**: Generous padding for a clean look - `p-8`, `m-4`
- **Borders**: Thin, subtle borders - `border border-slate-200`

## Components Style
- **Buttons**: Rounded-sm, high contrast, elegant hover effects.
- **Cards**: Minimalist with subtle shadows - `shadow-sm`, `hover:shadow-md`.
- **Navigation**: Clean, fixed top-bar, clear hierarchy.

## Do's
- Use high-quality stock images of machinery and industrial settings.
- Maintain plenty of whitespace.
- Use clear, legible typography.
- Consistent alignment.

## Don'ts
- Use overly bright or neon colors.
- Overcrowd the layout.
- Use rounded/bubbly corners (stick to `rounded-sm`).
- Low contrast text.
