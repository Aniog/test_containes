# Fantasy Kingdoms — Design System

## Theme
Dark fantasy aesthetic. Rich, immersive, regal. Inspired by medieval strategy games.

## Colors
- Background primary: `#0d0f1a` (deep navy-black) — `bg-[#0d0f1a]`
- Background secondary: `#131629` (dark navy) — `bg-[#131629]`
- Card surface: `#1a1e35` (dark indigo) — `bg-[#1a1e35]`
- Card border: `#2a2f52` (muted indigo) — `border-[#2a2f52]`
- Accent gold: `#c9a84c` (royal gold) — `text-[#c9a84c]`, `border-[#c9a84c]`
- Accent gold light: `#e8c96a` — `text-[#e8c96a]`
- Accent purple: `#7c5cbf` — `text-[#7c5cbf]`
- Accent purple light: `#a07de0` — `text-[#a07de0]`
- Text primary: `#e8e4d9` (parchment white) — `text-[#e8e4d9]`
- Text secondary: `#9a95a8` (muted lavender) — `text-[#9a95a8]`
- Text muted: `#5c5870` — `text-[#5c5870]`
- Success green: `#4caf7d` — `text-[#4caf7d]`
- Danger red: `#c94c4c` — `text-[#c94c4c]`
- Warning amber: `#c9844c` — `text-[#c9844c]`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (system fallback)
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-wide, text-[#e8e4d9]
- Section titles: text-[#c9a84c] font-semibold uppercase tracking-widest text-xs
- Body: text-[#e8e4d9] text-sm
- Muted: text-[#9a95a8] text-sm

## Borders & Radius
- Cards: `rounded-xl border border-[#2a2f52]`
- Buttons: `rounded-lg`
- Inputs: `rounded-lg border border-[#2a2f52] bg-[#0d0f1a]`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `p-6`
- Card padding: `p-5`
- Gap between cards: `gap-4` or `gap-6`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-[#c9a84c] text-[#0d0f1a] font-semibold hover:bg-[#e8c96a]`
- Secondary: `bg-[#1a1e35] border border-[#2a2f52] text-[#e8e4d9] hover:border-[#c9a84c]`
- Danger: `bg-[#c94c4c] text-white hover:bg-red-600`
- Ghost: `text-[#9a95a8] hover:text-[#e8e4d9]`

## Navigation
- Sidebar: `bg-[#0d0f1a] border-r border-[#2a2f52]`
- Active nav item: `bg-[#1a1e35] text-[#c9a84c] border-l-2 border-[#c9a84c]`
- Inactive nav item: `text-[#9a95a8] hover:text-[#e8e4d9] hover:bg-[#131629]`

## Do's
- Use gold accents for important values, titles, and CTAs
- Use dark card surfaces with subtle borders
- Use icons alongside labels for navigation
- Show stat cards with colored icons
- Use progress bars for resource/power levels

## Don'ts
- No white backgrounds
- No light mode styles
- No flat/minimal design — use depth and layering
- No bright neon colors
