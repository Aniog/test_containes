# SSourcing China Visual Style Guide

## Brand direction
A clean, trustworthy, international B2B presentation for overseas buyers evaluating a China sourcing partner. The site should feel practical, structured, and credible rather than salesy.

## Typography
- Primary font: Inter
- Headings: `font-semibold` to `font-bold`
- Body text: `text-sm md:text-base leading-7`
- Long-form article text: `text-base leading-8`

## Color system
- Page background: `bg-slate-950` only for deep footer/nav accents, otherwise use `bg-white` and `bg-slate-50`
- Primary text: `text-slate-900`
- Secondary text: `text-slate-600`
- Muted text: `text-slate-500`
- Primary brand accents: `bg-blue-700`, `text-blue-700`, `border-blue-700`
- Supporting accents: `bg-teal-600`, `text-teal-600`, `border-teal-600`
- Trust surfaces: `bg-white`, `bg-slate-50`, `border-slate-200`
- Dark footer/nav surface: `bg-slate-950`, `text-slate-100`

## Layout
- Use wide but controlled containers: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Section rhythm: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Card spacing: `rounded-2xl p-6 md:p-8`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Components
- Buttons: primary `bg-blue-700 text-white hover:bg-blue-800`, secondary `border border-slate-300 bg-white text-slate-900 hover:bg-slate-50`
- Cards: `rounded-2xl border border-slate-200 bg-white shadow-sm`
- Small labels/kickers: `text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-[0.2em] text-blue-700`
- Section headings: `text-3xl md:text-5xl font-semibold tracking-tight text-slate-900`
- Data/trust metrics: `text-3xl font-semibold text-slate-900`

## Imagery
- Use realistic sourcing-related visuals: factory floor, supplier meetings, inspection process, packaging checks, container loading, shipping coordination
- Prefer image panels with rounded corners and subtle overlays
- Images should support credibility, not feel overly glossy or lifestyle-focused

## Do
- Keep content practical and specific
- Use explicit readable foreground colors on every surface
- Prioritize whitespace and structured information hierarchy
- Use subtle borders and restrained shadows

## Don’t
- Do not use neon colors or startup-style gradients everywhere
- Do not use exaggerated claims or cluttered icon walls
- Do not place light text on light cards or dark text on dark backgrounds
- Do not make the desktop layout feel like a mobile single-column stack
