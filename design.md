# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium.
- **Palette**:
  - `background`: #FDFBF7 (Cream Light)
  - `foreground`: #1A1A1A (Deep Stone)
  - `primary`: #1A1A1A
  - `secondary`: #F5F1EA (Cream Dark)
  - `accent`: #C5A059 (Gold Accord)
  - `muted`: #71717A
  - `border`: #E2E2E2
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: Serif (Cormorant Garamond)
  - UI/Body: Sans-serif (Manrope)
  - Product Names: UPPERCASE, `tracking-widest` or `tracking-extra`
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, thin hairline dividers.
- **Animations**: Soft fades, subtle hover scales (1.02), smooth transitions.

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid #1A1A1A, white text, uppercase, tracked.
  - Secondary: Outlined #1A1A1A or Solid #C5A059.
- **Cards**: Minimalist, high-quality imagery, subtle hover reveals.
- **Dividers**: 0.5px or 1px stroke with low opacity.

## Do's
- Use large editorial imagery.
- Keep UI elements thin and refined.
- Focus on warm lighting in images.

## Don'ts
- No loud or bright neon colors.
- No heavy shadows or bulky borders.
- No cluttered layouts.
