# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand direction
A refined industrial style that feels premium, precise, and approachable. The site should communicate engineering confidence without looking cold or overly technical.

## Typography
- Primary font: Inter
- Headings: bold, clean, confident
- Body text: calm, readable, spacious
- Example Tailwind classes: `text-4xl md:text-6xl font-semibold tracking-tight`, `text-base md:text-lg leading-7`

## Color palette
Use named brand colors from Tailwind config.
- `graphite`: #0F1720
- `steel`: #334155
- `brass`: #B98A2F
- `mist`: #E8EDF2
- `cloud`: #F8FAFC
- `slateLine`: #CBD5E1

## Surfaces
- Main page background: `bg-slate-50`
- Primary dark panels: `bg-slate-950 text-white`
- Secondary panels: `bg-white text-slate-950`
- Soft technical panels: `bg-slate-100 text-slate-950`

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous whitespace and strong section rhythm
- Example classes: `px-6 md:px-10 lg:px-16`, `py-16 md:py-24`, `max-w-7xl mx-auto`, `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders and shadows
- Rounded corners should feel modern but restrained
- Example classes: `rounded-3xl`, `border border-slate-300`, `shadow-[...]` should be avoided in favor of standard shadows like `shadow-sm`, `shadow-lg`

## Buttons
- Primary: dark background with light text and subtle hover lift
- Secondary: white or transparent with visible border
- Example classes: `bg-slate-950 text-white hover:bg-slate-700`, `border border-slate-300 text-slate-950 hover:bg-slate-100`

## Imagery and graphic language
- Prefer clean product presentation, technical confidence, and structured layouts
- Decorative elements should feel mechanical and architectural, not playful

## Do
- Keep contrast high and text clearly readable
- Use elegant industrial language
- Keep desktop layouts multi-column where appropriate
- Make mobile layouts simple and breathable

## Don't
- Do not use neon colors
- Do not use cramped cards or tiny text
- Do not rely on low-contrast gray text for important content
- Do not make the experience feel harsh or overly heavy
