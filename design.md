# Design System

## Brand
Clean, modern SaaS landing page aesthetic. Professional, trustworthy, approachable.

## Colors
- Primary: Indigo (`indigo-600`, `#4F46E5`) — CTAs, links, accents
- Primary hover: `indigo-700`
- Primary light: `indigo-50` — backgrounds, badges
- Background: `white` / `gray-50` for alternating sections
- Surface: `white` with `shadow-sm` or `shadow-md`
- Text primary: `gray-900`
- Text secondary: `gray-600`
- Text muted: `gray-400`
- Border: `gray-200`
- Success: `green-600` / `green-50`
- Error: `red-600` / `red-50`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Hero heading: `text-5xl font-bold text-gray-900` (desktop), `text-3xl` (mobile)
- Section heading: `text-3xl font-bold text-gray-900`
- Subheading: `text-xl font-semibold text-gray-800`
- Body: `text-base text-gray-600`
- Label: `text-sm font-medium text-gray-700`
- Caption/muted: `text-sm text-gray-500`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-4` (desktop), `py-12 px-4` (mobile)
- Card padding: `p-6` or `p-8`
- Form field gap: `space-y-4`
- Section gap: `space-y-12`

## Borders & Radius
- Cards: `rounded-2xl`
- Buttons: `rounded-lg`
- Inputs: `rounded-lg border border-gray-300`
- Badges: `rounded-full`

## Shadows
- Cards: `shadow-sm` default, `shadow-md` on hover
- Navbar: `shadow-sm`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-indigo-600 hover:bg-indigo-700 text-white font-semibold rounded-lg px-6 py-3 transition-colors`
- Secondary/outline: `border border-gray-300 text-gray-700 hover:bg-gray-50 rounded-lg px-6 py-3`
- Destructive: `bg-red-600 hover:bg-red-700 text-white`

## Inputs
- `w-full border border-gray-300 rounded-lg px-4 py-2.5 text-gray-900 placeholder-gray-400 focus:outline-none focus:ring-2 focus:ring-indigo-500 focus:border-transparent`

## Do's
- Use consistent indigo as the single accent color
- Use white cards on gray-50 backgrounds
- Keep generous whitespace between sections
- Use subtle shadows, not heavy drop shadows

## Don'ts
- Don't use dark backgrounds for main content areas
- Don't mix multiple accent colors
- Don't use very small text (below text-sm) for important content
