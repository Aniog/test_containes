# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: warm ivory/cream (#FAF8F5) for main, deep charcoal (#1A1A1A) for hero/footer
- Primary text: near-black (#1A1A1A)
- Secondary text: warm gray (#6B6560)
- Accent: warm gold (#C9A96E) - used for CTAs, highlights, stars
- Accent hover: deeper gold (#B8944F)
- Borders: soft warm gray (#E8E4DF)
- Card backgrounds: white (#FFFFFF)
- Footer bg: deep charcoal (#1A1A1A) with light text (#E8E4DF)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- Hero headlines: large serif, italic optional for emphasis

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8

## Dividers
- Thin hairline: border-t border-[#E8E4DF]

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold bg, white text, rounded-none (sharp edges for luxury feel)
- Secondary: outlined gold border, gold text, transparent bg
- Hover: subtle opacity/brightness shift
- Full-width on mobile, auto on desktop for CTAs

## Shadows
- Soft, warm shadows: shadow-[0_2px_20px_rgba(0,0,0,0.06)]
- Hover: slightly deeper shadow

## Transitions
- Smooth, subtle: transition-all duration-300 ease-out
- Image hover: scale(1.03) with overflow-hidden container

## Images
- Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Editorial style, soft lighting
- 9:16 for UGC/reels cards
- 4:5 or 3:4 for product cards
