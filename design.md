# Serengeti Pulse — Visual Design Guide

## Typography
- Primary: "Playfair Display" — a tall, elegant serif font for headings
- Fallback: Georgia, serif
- Body: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif for readability
- Headings should feel majestic, with generous letter-spacing and line-height

## Color Palette — "Warm Earth"
- Burnt Orange: #C25A1E (primary accent, buttons, highlights)
- Deep Ochre: #B8860B (secondary accent, borders, icons)
- Acacia Green: #5C7A2D (tertiary accent, nature elements, hover states)
- Parchment: #F5F0E8 (page background)
- Warm Sand: #EDE4D3 (card backgrounds, subtle surfaces)
- Dark Earth: #3B2A1A (text on light backgrounds)
- Clay: #8B5E3C (muted text, secondary text)
- Sunset Glow: #D4783B (gradient accents, special highlights)
- Dust: #C4B5A5 (subtle borders, dividers)

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace to evoke the vastness of the savanna
- Section padding: py-24 md:py-32
- Card gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Max content width: max-w-7xl

## Do's
- Use warm, earthy tones throughout
- Keep typography elegant and spacious
- Use subtle animations that evoke nature (dissolve, parallax, slow fades)
- Full-bleed images where appropriate for immersion

## Don'ts
- No cold blues or stark whites
- No harsh shadows — use soft, warm shadows
- No jarring fast animations
- No small cramped typography
