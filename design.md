# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
**Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Colors
- **Background**: `#FCFAF9` (Off-white, warm neutral) - Tailwind: `bg-background`
- **Foreground/Text**: `#1A1614` (Deep charcoal/brown) - Tailwind: `text-foreground`
- **Accent/Gold**: `#D4AF37` (Soft metallic gold) - Tailwind: `text-accent`
- **Muted**: `#9C958E` (Soft pebble grey) - Tailwind: `text-muted-foreground`
- **Base Border**: `#E9E3DF` (Very soft warm grey) - Tailwind: `border-border`

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif)
  - Style: Elegant, uppercase for product names with wide letter spacing.
  - Tailwind Classes: `font-serif tracking-[0.2em] uppercase`
- **Body & UI Elements**: `Inter` (Sans-serif)
  - Style: Clean, readable, professional.
  - Tailwind Classes: `font-sans`

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid `#1A1614` with white text, no border radius (minimalist), subtle hover transform.
  - Secondary: Outlined or soft accent wash.
- **Dividers**: 1px hairline, warm grey.
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, editorial feel.

## Visual Effects
- **Transitions**: Ease-in-out, 300ms.
- **Shadows**: Very soft, diffuse shadows for cards.
- **Images**: Large, editorial-style imagery.
