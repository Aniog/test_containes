# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
**Mood:** Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Base (deep warm charcoal):** `#1C1917` (stone-900) — primary background for hero/dark sections
- **Cream (warm off-white):** `#FAF7F2` — primary light background
- **Gold Accent:** `#C9A96E` — primary accent, buttons, highlights
- **Gold Hover:** `#B8944F` — darker gold for hover states
- **Text Dark:** `#1C1917` — primary text on light backgrounds
- **Text Light:** `#FAF7F2` — primary text on dark backgrounds
- **Muted Text:** `#78716C` (stone-500) — secondary/muted text
- **Divider:** `#E7E5E4` (stone-200) — hairline dividers
- **Card BG:** `#FFFFFF` — product cards on light background
- **Warm Gray:** `#F5F0EB` — subtle warm gray for alternating sections

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body/UI:** Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond
- **Section headings:** Cormorant Garamond, regular weight, large scale

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace (py-20 to py-28 for sections)
- Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Product grid: 5 columns on desktop, 2 on mobile
- Hairline dividers (1px, stone-200)

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold (#C9A96E) bg, cream text, hover darker gold
- Secondary: outlined, gold border, gold text, hover fill gold
- Pill buttons for variant selectors
- All buttons: smooth transition (duration-300), subtle hover lift

## Images
- Editorial, warm-lit photography
- Dark/neutral backgrounds for jewelry close-ups
- Large hero imagery, full-bleed where possible
- Hover reveals second product image

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth transitions (300ms ease)
- No bouncy or playful animations
- Soft fade-ins on scroll
- Gentle hover lifts on cards

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Keep UI minimal and restrained
- Use gold accent sparingly
- Ensure strong contrast for accessibility

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/saturated colors
- Don't use playful or bouncy animations
- Don't crowd sections with too much content
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (badges, aggressive CTAs)
- Don't use thin/light fonts on dark backgrounds without contrast check
