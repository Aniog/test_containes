# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: Cream/Off-white (`#FCFBF7`)
- **Primary Text**: Charcoal/Deep Slate (`#1A1A1A`)
- **Accent/Heading**: Rich Gold (`#C5A059`)
- **Secondary/Muted**: Soft Greige (`#A8A29A`)
- **Divider**: Hairline Slate (`#E5E5E5`)

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: "Cormorant Garamond", serif.
- **Body & UI**: "Inter", sans-serif.
- **Styling**: Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).

## Design Elements
- **Whitespace**: Generous, editorial spacing.
- **Dividers**: Thin `border-b-[0.5px]` borders.
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal or Gold.
  - Secondary: Outlined with thin borders.
- **Transitions**: Subtle hover effects (opacity, slight scale).
- **Shadows**: Very soft, minimal (`shadow-sm`).
