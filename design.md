# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial Machinery / Sheet Metal Folding Equipment
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly — industrial precision meets refined aesthetics

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Deep Navy** (#0F172A): Primary background for hero, footer, and dark sections. Conveys authority and precision.
- **Steel Blue** (#1E3A5F): Accent for headings, borders, and interactive elements. Industrial yet refined.
- **Warm Gold** (#C8973E): Primary accent for CTAs, highlights, and premium feel. Elegant metallic touch.

### Neutral Colors
- **Slate-50** (#F8FAFC): Light backgrounds for content sections
- **Slate-100** (#F1F5F9): Card backgrounds, subtle surfaces
- **Slate-200** (#E2E8F0): Borders, dividers
- **Slate-400** (#94A3B8): Muted text, placeholders
- **Slate-600** (#475569): Body text
- **Slate-800** (#1E293B): Headings on light backgrounds
- **White** (#FFFFFF): Card surfaces, text on dark backgrounds

### Semantic Colors
- **Success**: #16A34A (green-600)
- **Error**: #DC2626 (red-600)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) — clean, modern, excellent readability
- **Headings**: font-weight 700-800, tracking tight (-0.02em)
- **Body**: font-weight 400, line-height 1.6-1.75
- **Accent/Label**: font-weight 500, uppercase, tracking wide (0.08em)

### Scale
- Hero heading: text-5xl md:text-6xl lg:text-7xl
- Section heading: text-3xl md:text-4xl lg:text-5xl
- Subheading: text-xl md:text-2xl
- Body: text-base md:text-lg
- Small/Label: text-sm

## Spacing & Layout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28 lg:py-32
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Consistent vertical rhythm with multiples of 4px

## Visual Elements

### Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl, shadow-sm hover:shadow-lg transition-shadow
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Subtle borders: border border-slate-200
- Gold accent lines: h-1 w-16 bg-gold

### Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: bg-gold text-white hover:bg-amber-600 font-semibold px-8 py-3 rounded-lg
- **Secondary**: border-2 border-navy text-navy hover:bg-navy hover:text-white
- **Ghost**: text-white border border-white/30 hover:bg-white/10

### Cards
- White background, subtle shadow, rounded corners
- Hover: slight lift with shadow increase
- Image top, content bottom with padding

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace for elegant feel
- Keep navigation simple and intuitive
- Use gold accents sparingly for premium feel
- Ensure high contrast for readability
- Use smooth transitions (duration-300)
- Make CTAs prominent and clear

## Don'ts
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors at once
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much text
- Don't use thin or light fonts on dark backgrounds
- Don't use harsh shadows or excessive borders
- Don't use playful or casual design elements
- Don't sacrifice readability for style
