# Design System — Paws & Whiskers Pet Site

## Brand Identity
Warm, friendly, and playful. The site celebrates the joy of dogs and cats with a clean, modern layout that feels approachable and fun.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Amber/Warm Orange):** `amber-500` (#F59E0B) — used for CTAs, highlights, accents
- **Secondary (Teal):** `teal-600` (#0D9488) — used for cat section accents, secondary buttons
- **Background:** `stone-50` (#FAFAF9) — warm off-white page background
- **Surface/Card:** `white` with `shadow-md` and `rounded-2xl`
- **Text Primary:** `stone-800` (#292524) — headings and body
- **Text Muted:** `stone-500` (#78716C) — captions, subtitles
- **Dog Section Accent:** `amber-50` background with `amber-500` accents
- **Cat Section Accent:** `teal-50` background with `teal-600` accents

## Typography
- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Hero Heading:** `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-extrabold text-stone-800`
- **Section Heading:** `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-stone-800`
- **Card Title:** `text-xl font-semibold text-stone-800`
- **Body Text:** `text-base text-stone-600 leading-relaxed`
- **Caption/Label:** `text-sm text-stone-500`

## Spacing & Layout
- **Page max width:** `max-w-6xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- **Section padding:** `py-16 md:py-24`
- **Card padding:** `p-6`
- **Grid gaps:** `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- **Cards:** `rounded-2xl shadow-md hover:shadow-lg transition-shadow`
- **Images:** `rounded-xl object-cover`
- **Dividers:** `border-stone-200`

## Buttons
- **Primary CTA:** `bg-amber-500 hover:bg-amber-600 text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-full transition-colors`
- **Secondary CTA:** `bg-teal-600 hover:bg-teal-700 text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-full transition-colors`
- **Outline:** `border-2 border-amber-500 text-amber-600 hover:bg-amber-50 px-6 py-3 rounded-full`

## Do's
- Use warm, inviting imagery of dogs and cats
- Keep sections clearly separated with alternating backgrounds
- Use emoji sparingly for personality (🐾, 🐶, 🐱)
- Maintain generous whitespace between sections
- Use rounded corners on all cards and images

## Don'ts
- Don't use harsh dark backgrounds for main content
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors per section
- Don't use small font sizes below `text-sm`
- Don't use pure black (#000) — use `stone-800` instead
