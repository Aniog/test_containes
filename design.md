# SSourcing China — Design System

## Brand Identity
Professional B2B sourcing agency. Clean, trustworthy, international.

## Colors
- **Primary Navy**: `#1B2B4B` — main brand, nav, headings (`bg-navy`, `text-navy`)
- **Accent Red**: `#C0392B` — CTAs, highlights (`bg-red-600`, `text-red-600`)
- **Steel Blue**: `#2E6DA4` — links, secondary actions (`text-blue-700`)
- **Light Gray**: `#F4F6F9` — section backgrounds (`bg-gray-50`)
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` — cards, content areas
- **Body Text**: `#374151` — `text-gray-700`
- **Heading Text**: `#1B2B4B` — `text-[#1B2B4B]`
- **Muted Text**: `#6B7280` — `text-gray-500`
- **Border**: `#E5E7EB` — `border-gray-200`

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **H1**: `text-4xl md:text-5xl font-bold text-[#1B2B4B]`
- **H2**: `text-3xl font-bold text-[#1B2B4B]`
- **H3**: `text-xl font-semibold text-[#1B2B4B]`
- **Body**: `text-base text-gray-700 leading-relaxed`
- **Small/Muted**: `text-sm text-gray-500`
- **Label/Badge**: `text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-wide`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: `bg-red-600 hover:bg-red-700 text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-lg transition-colors`
- **Secondary**: `border-2 border-[#1B2B4B] text-[#1B2B4B] hover:bg-[#1B2B4B] hover:text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-lg transition-colors`
- **Ghost/Link**: `text-blue-700 hover:underline font-medium`

### Cards
- `bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-200 p-6 hover:shadow-md transition-shadow`

### Badges
- `inline-block bg-blue-100 text-blue-800 text-xs font-semibold px-3 py-1 rounded-full`
- Industry badge: `bg-gray-100 text-gray-700 text-xs font-medium px-3 py-1 rounded-full`

### Section Headers
- Centered: `text-center mb-12`
- Eyebrow label: `text-red-600 text-sm font-semibold uppercase tracking-widest mb-2`
- Title: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-[#1B2B4B] mb-4`
- Subtitle: `text-lg text-gray-600 max-w-2xl mx-auto`

## Do's
- Use navy for authority, red only for CTAs and key highlights
- Maintain generous whitespace between sections
- Use real-world language: "We verify factories" not "We leverage synergies"
- Cards should have subtle shadows, not heavy borders
- Icons should be consistent size (w-6 h-6 or w-8 h-8)

## Don'ts
- No dark text on dark backgrounds
- No light gray text on white (use gray-600 minimum for body)
- No centered text for long paragraphs
- No more than 2 font weights per section
- No exaggerated claims or superlatives
