# Intertwined - Design System

## Concept
A "Humanistic Minimalist" aesthetic for environmental photography. The design explores the unity between humanity and nature.

## Typography
- **Body / UI**: Inter (Sans-Serif) — clean, modern, highly readable
- **Quotes / Accents**: Caveat (Handwriting) — organic, personal, human touch

## Color Palette
- **Skin Tones**: #F5EBE0 (light), #E8D5C4 (mid), #D4A88C (warm), #B8846A (deep)
- **Forest Greens**: #A8C8A0 (light), #5A7D4A (mid), #2D4A24 (deep), #1A2E14 (dark)
- **Sky Blues**: #D6E6F2 (pale), #A4C8E8 (light), #6B9BC4 (mid), #3D6F94 (deep)
- **Warm Stone**: #F5F0EB (light), #E8E2D9 (mid)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace, editorial breathing room
- Section padding: py-24 md:py-32
- Content max-width: max-w-6xl or max-w-7xl

## Visual Style
- Large, immersive photography
- Split-screen juxtapositions
- Subtle borders and dividers
- Smooth transitions and morphing effects via Framer Motion
- Pull quotes in handwriting font overlapping images
- Do: Use ample negative space, let photos breathe
- Don't: Clutter with too many elements, use harsh colors, small text on photos
