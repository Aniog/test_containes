# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `ivory` (#FDFBF7) — warm off-white
- **Surface**: `cream` (#F7F3ED) — slightly darker warm neutral for cards
- **Foreground / Text**: `charcoal` (#1C1917) — rich warm black
- **Muted text**: `stone` (#78716C) — warm gray for secondary text
- **Accent**: `gold` (#B8860B) — dark goldenrod, the primary brand accent
- **Accent light**: `gold-light` (#D4A843) — lighter gold for hover states
- **Accent dark**: `gold-dark` (#8B6508) — deeper gold for active states
- **Border**: `sand` (#E7E0D6) — warm hairline dividers

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names**: `Cormorant Garamond` (serif), weights 300–600
- **Body / UI**: `Inter` (sans-serif), weights 300–500
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.15em
- **Heading sizes**: Hero 4xl–6xl, Section 3xl, Card lg–xl

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections py-16 to py-24
- Container max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Cards: rounded-none or rounded-sm (sharp, editorial)
- Hairline dividers: border-sand (1px)

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-gold text-white hover:bg-gold-light, px-8 py-3, uppercase tracking-widest text-sm font-medium
- Outlined: border border-gold text-gold hover:bg-gold hover:text-white
- No border-radius or very subtle (rounded-sm)

## Shadows & Effects
- Minimal shadows: shadow-sm on hover only
- Hover transitions: duration-300 ease-in-out
- Image hover: scale-105 with overflow-hidden container
- Opacity transitions for overlays

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep imagery large and editorial
- Use thin hairline dividers (border-sand)
- Subtle hover animations (scale, opacity)
- Uppercase + tracking-widest for product names and CTAs

## Don'ts
- No rounded-lg or rounded-full on cards
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/sale badges or loud promotional elements
