# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual direction
Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial look: warm ivory surfaces, deep espresso contrast, champagne gold accents, large jewelry imagery, restrained motion, generous whitespace, and thin hairline borders. The storefront should feel premium and intimate, never loud, discount-driven, or generic.

## Palette
Use named Tailwind colors from `tailwind.config.js` only.

- `velmora-ivory` (#F7F1E8): primary page background.
- `velmora-cream` (#FFF9F0): elevated card and input surface.
- `velmora-linen` (#E8D9C2): soft section background and dividers.
- `velmora-sand` (#C9AA7D): muted metallic accent.
- `velmora-gold` (#B98B45): primary accent for CTAs, icons, ratings, and focused states.
- `velmora-espresso` (#231A16): primary text and dark editorial surfaces.
- `velmora-cocoa` (#4A342C): secondary text.
- `velmora-rose` (#A76758): warm editorial highlight used sparingly.
- `velmora-mist` (#F1E7D8): hover and soft panel tint.

## Typography
- Headings and logo: elegant serif, `Cormorant Garamond`, via `font-serif`.
- Body and UI: clean sans, `Manrope`, via `font-sans`.
- Product names: uppercase, wide letter-spacing, serif, medium weight.
- Use large serif headlines with relaxed line-height and concise copy.

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first with comfortable vertical rhythm.
- Desktop sections use max width `max-w-7xl`, generous `px-6 lg:px-10`, and large `py-16 lg:py-28`.
- Use thin borders (`border-velmora-linen`) and whitespace rather than heavy boxes.
- Product grids are 2 columns on mobile where possible, 3–5 columns on desktop depending on context.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, pill-shaped, either solid `bg-velmora-gold text-velmora-cream` or outlined with `border-velmora-gold text-velmora-espresso`.
- Cards: soft cream/ivory surfaces, subtle shadow, hairline border, refined hover lift.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid ivory with subtle border after scroll.
- Cart drawer: cream panel, espresso text, clear quantity controls, readable contrast.
- Forms: cream inputs, espresso text, gold focus ring, readable placeholder.

## Imagery
- Use warm gold jewelry imagery on neutral or dark editorial backgrounds through the Strikingly image tagging system.
- Use vertical 9:16 imagery for UGC/Reels cards.
- Avoid hardcoded external image URLs.

## Do / Don't
- Do use restrained gold accents, serif type, editorial spacing, and high contrast.
- Do keep all text explicitly readable on dark or light surfaces.
- Don't use sale badges, loud reds, neon colors, chunky shadows, or crowded layouts.
- Don't make the design feel like a marketplace or discount store.
