# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm editorial aesthetic. Demi-fine gold jewelry at accessible luxury pricing ($30–$120). Target: women 25–45, self-purchase and gifting.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** #FAF7F2 (warm cream)
- **Foreground:** #1C1C1C (deep charcoal, almost black)
- **Accent / Gold:** #C9A84C (refined warm gold), hover: #B8943B
- **Gold Light:** #E8D48B (for subtle highlights)
- **Gold Dark:** #A8882E (for depth)
- **Muted bg:** #F0EBE4 (warm beige)
- **Muted fg:** #8B8580 (warm gray for secondary text)
- **Border:** #E5DDD4 (thin hairline)
- **Card bg:** #FFFFFF
- **Stone:** #8B8580 (body text for low emphasis)
- **Stone Light:** #B8B0A8 (placeholder/disabled)

## Typography
- **Headings:** 'Cormorant Garamond', serif — elegant serif with contrast
- **Body / UI:** 'Inter', sans-serif — clean, legible
- **Product Names:** UPPERCASE, tracking .12em (tracking-wider), serif
- **Price:** sans-serif, medium weight

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 88rem (1400px) on large screens
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Thin hairline dividers (border-b border-[#E5DDD4])

## Interactive States
- Gold accent for primary buttons, hover darkens slightly
- Subtle opacity hovers on product cards (scale 1.02, shadow transition)
- Underline animation on nav links
- Smooth 0.3s ease transitions

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C9A84C] text-white, hover:bg-[#B8943B], uppercase, tracking-wider, px-8 py-3
- Outline: border border-[#1C1C1C] text-[#1C1C1C], hover:bg-[#1C1C1C] hover:text-white
- Ghost: transparent, hover:bg-muted

## Product Cards
- White card background, subtle shadow on hover
- Image with aspect-ratio 1:1
- Product name in uppercase serif with wide tracking
- Price in sans-serif medium
- Quick add button appears on hover (desktop)

## Navigation
- Sticky, transparent over hero, solid white bg on scroll
- Logo in serif, UPPERCASE, tracking-widest
- Links in uppercase, text-sm, tracking-wide
- Search + Cart icons on right
