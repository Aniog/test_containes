# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design Guidelines

## Visual Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Color Palette
- **Base**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Off-Black)
- **Background**: `#F9F7F2` (Warm Bone/Cream)
- **Accent/Metallic**: `#D4AF37` (Antique Gold)
- **Text Primary**: `#2D2D2D` (Soft Charcoal)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6B6B6B` (Muted Grey)
- **Border**: `#E5E5E5` (Thin Hairline)

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond`, Serif
  - Example: `font-serif text-3xl font-medium tracking-tight`
- **Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond`, Serif, UPPERCASE, Wide Spacing
  - Example: `font-serif uppercase tracking-[0.2em] text-sm`
- **Body & UI**: `Inter`, Sans-serif
  - Example: `font-sans text-base leading-relaxed`

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid `#1A1A1A`, white text, wide tracking, no-rounding or very slight (2px).
  - Secondary: Outlined `#1A1A1A`, thin border.
- **Dividers**: Hairline `border-t-[0.5px] border-black/10`.
- **Shadows**: Very soft, subtle elevation.
- **Transitions**: Smooth 300ms ease-in-out.

## Layout
- Generous whitespace.
- Large editorial imagery.
- Sticky navigation with transparency.
- Full-bleed hero sections.
