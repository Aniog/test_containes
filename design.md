# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Design Guide

## Brand Personality
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine — NOT loud, NOT discount, NOT generic.

## Color Palette

### Primary palette
- **Background**: `#0A0A0A` (near-black, deep refined base) — used sitewide as page bg
- **Surface**: `#111111` (one step lighter) — cards, nav solid state, drawers
- **Surface raised**: `#1A1A1A` — hover states, elevated elements
- **Text primary**: `#F5F0EB` (warm ivory) — main body text
- **Text secondary**: `#A0988E` (warm taupe) — secondary text, labels
- **Accent / gold**: `#C9A96E` (warm metallic gold) — CTAs, accent lines, star ratings, price emphasis
- **Accent hover**: `#D4B878` (lighter gold) — button hover
- **Border**: `#2A2A2A` (subtle dark) — dividers, hairline rules
- **Border accent**: `#C9A96E` — accent borders, active states

### Utility
- **Error**: `#E45757`
- **Success**: `#5CA87C`
- **Overlay**: `rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.6)` — modals, drawers

## Typography

### Headings & product names
- **Font**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif
- **Weight**: 300 (light) for large hero, 500 (medium) for headings, 600 (semibold) for emphasis
- **Letter-spacing**: Product names in UPPERCASE + `tracking-[0.15em]`
- **Style**: Elegant serif, italic available for accent

### Body & UI
- **Font**: `Inter`, sans-serif
- **Weight**: 300 (light body), 400 (regular), 500 (medium)
- **Size**: 14px base for UI, 15px for body

## Spacing & Layout
- **Generous whitespace** — `py-16 md:py-24` for major sections
- **Max content width**: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- **Product grid gaps**: `gap-6 md:gap-8`
- **Hairline dividers**: `border-t border-[#2A2A2A]` — thin, restrained

## Borders & Dividers
- **Hairline**: `border-t border-[#2A2A2A]`
- **Card border**: `border border-[#2A2A2A] rounded-sm`
- **Gold accent line**: `border-t border-[#C9A96E]` (thin, 60px wide)

## Shadows
- **Soft shadow**: `shadow-lg shadow-black/20`
- **Card hover**: `shadow-xl shadow-black/30`
- **Drawer**: `shadow-2xl shadow-black/40`

## Buttons
- **Primary (solid gold)**: `bg-[#C9A96E] text-black font-medium px-8 py-3 hover:bg-[#D4B878] transition-colors`
- **Outline**: `border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E] hover:bg-[#C9A96E]/10`
- **Text button**: `text-[#C9A96E] hover:text-[#D4B878]`

## Animations
- **Transitions**: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`
- **Hover lift**: `hover:-translate-y-0.5`
- **Fade in**: `opacity-0 translate-y-4 animate-fadeIn`

## Images
- **Style**: Editorial, warm lighting, gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- **Aspect ratios**: Product 1:1, Hero 16:9 (widescreen), Category tiles 3:4
- **Hover**: Reveal second image on product cards

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use serif for headings, sans-serif for body
- Use gold accent sparingly (CTAs, dividers, highlights)
- Keep it dark, warm, editorial
- Uppercase + wide tracking for product names

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright colors
- Don't use heavy borders or thick dividers
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (flashy badges, price slashes)
- Don't overuse the gold accent — it's a highlight, not a background
