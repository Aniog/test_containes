# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount, not generic.

## Color Palette
- Background: brand-cream (#F5F0EB) — warm off-white base
- Surface: brand-white (#FEFCF9) — cards, modals, drawers
- Text Primary: brand-dark (#1A1614) — rich near-black
- Text Secondary: brand-stone (#6B6560) — muted body text
- Accent / Gold: brand-gold (#C9A96E) — CTAs, accents, highlights
- Gold Hover: brand-goldDark (#A9844A)
- Border/Divider: brand-warm (#E8DDD0) — thin hairline dividers
- Light background sections: brand-light (#F9F6F3)

## Typography
- Headings (h1-h3, product names): Cormorant Garamond, uppercase with wide letter spacing (tracking-widest or tracking-[0.15em])
- Body / UI / Navigation: Inter (300-500 weight)
- Product names: uppercase, tracking-[0.15em], font-serif
- Price: font-sans, font-medium

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace — py-16 to py-24 on sections
- Max content width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-brand-warm

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-brand-gold text-white, hover:bg-brand-goldDark, uppercase tracking-widest, text-sm, px-8 py-3
- Outline: border-2 border-brand-dark text-brand-dark, hover:bg-brand-dark hover:text-brand-white, uppercase tracking-widest

## Transitions & Hover
- Subtle hover transitions: duration-300 ease-out
- Product card: image scale on hover, shadow lift
- Links: opacity or underline fade

## Image Treatments
- Editorial / moody lighting
- Warm gold jewelry on dark or neutral backgrounds
- No harsh white studio lighting
