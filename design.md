# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity

### Mood
- Quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Charcoal: `#1A1A1A` (main background, text)
- Warm Cream: `#FAF7F2` (light background sections)
- Rich Black: `#0D0D0D` (footer, dark sections)

**Accent Colors:**
- Antique Gold: `#C9A962` (primary accent, CTAs, highlights)
- Soft Gold: `#E8DCC4` (secondary accent, borders)
- Champagne: `#F5E6C8` (hover states, subtle highlights)

**Neutral Colors:**
- Warm Gray: `#8A8580` (secondary text)
- Light Stone: `#E5E0D8` (dividers, borders)
- Off White: `#FDFCFA` (cards, elevated surfaces)

### Typography
**Headings & Product Names:**
- Font: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- Weight: 500-600
- Letter-spacing: 0.08em (wide for uppercase product names)
- Product names: UPPERCASE

**Body & UI:**
- Font: Manrope (sans-serif)
- Weight: 400-500
- Clean, readable

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace (80px+ vertical spacing between sections)
- Large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers (1px, light stone color)
- Maximum content width: 1400px
- Grid: 12-column system

### Components
- Buttons: Solid antique gold or outlined with gold border
- Cards: Minimal, subtle shadow on hover
- Transitions: 300ms ease-out
- Shadows: Soft, diffused (0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08))

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px
