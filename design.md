# Design System for ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style
The visual style is elegant, industrial yet modern, and user-friendly. We focus on clarity, trust, and showcasing the precision of our machinery.

## Colors
- **Primary:** `slate-900` (Deep industrial gray for headers and strong elements)
- **Secondary:** `amber-600` (A highlight color suggesting sparks/metalwork without being aggressive)
- **Accent:** `blue-600` (For trustworthy links or secondary actions)
- **Background:** `slate-50` (Very light gray to keep it clean but less harsh than pure white)
- **Surface/Card:** `white` (For cards and elevated surfaces)
- **Text:** `slate-800` (Main text for readability), `slate-500` (Muted/secondary text)

## Typography
- **Headings & Body:** Google Font "Inter" (Clean, mechanical, highly readable)
- Keep headings strong: `font-bold` or `font-extrabold`
- Use ample line weight: `leading-relaxed` for paragraphs

## Spacing & Layout
- Use generous padding (e.g., `py-20`, `px-6`) to create a spacious, elegant feel.
- Elements should never feel cramped.
- Borders should be subtle (`border-slate-200`) or non-existent in favor of soft shadows (`shadow-md`).

## Do's
- DO ensure high contrast between text and background.
- DO use high-quality stock images of machinery and industrial environments.
- DO use Lucide icons with sharp, clear strokes.
- DO use responsive grid layouts (`grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3`).

## Don'ts
- DON'T use overly playful or juvenile fonts.
- DON'T clutter the interface with too much text; let the images and key features breathe.
- DON'T use neon or overly bright colors as backgrounds.
