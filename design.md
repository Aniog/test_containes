# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry brand.

## Color Palette
- **Background (cream):** `#FAF7F2` — warm off-white, flatters gold
- **Foreground (charcoal):** `#1A1A1A` — near-black for text
- **Accent (warm gold):** `#B8860B` — dark goldenrod, premium metallic feel
- **Accent light:** `#D4A853` — lighter gold for hover states
- **Muted:** `#6B6B6B` — secondary text
- **Muted light:** `#F5F0E8` — subtle section backgrounds
- **Border:** `#E8E0D4` — warm hairline dividers

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names:** `Cormorant Garamond`, serif — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI:** `Inter`, sans-serif — clean, modern
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.15em
- **Heading sizes:** Hero: text-5xl/6xl, Section: text-3xl/4xl, Card: text-lg/xl

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Cards: rounded-none or rounded-sm (sharp, editorial)
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-border (1px)

## Components
- **Buttons:** Solid accent bg with cream text, or outlined with accent border. Uppercase tracking-wider text-sm. Hover: slight opacity or shade shift.
- **Cards:** No border-radius, subtle shadow-sm on hover, transition-all duration-300
- **Images:** object-cover, aspect ratios maintained
- **Hover effects:** scale-[1.02], opacity transitions, translateY(-2px)

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography hierarchy clear
- Use thin hairline dividers between sections
- Maintain warm, editorial feel
- Use accent color sparingly for CTAs and highlights

## Don'ts
- No rounded-lg or rounded-xl (too casual)
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy shadows
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/sale-style badges
