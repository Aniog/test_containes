# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| background | #FAF8F5 | Page base, warm off-white |
| foreground | #1C1917 | Primary text, deep charcoal |
| card | #FFFFFF | Cards, product surfaces |
| card-foreground | #1C1917 | Card text |
| muted | #F5F0EB | Subtle section backgrounds |
| muted-foreground | #78716C | Secondary text, labels |
| border | #E7E0D8 | Hairline dividers, borders |
| accent | #C49A5C | Gold accent — CTAs, highlights |
| accent-foreground | #1C1917 | Text on gold accent |
| destructive | #B91C1C | Errors, removal actions |

## Typography
- Headings / Product names: **Cormorant Garamond** (serif), UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing
- Body / UI: **Inter** (sans-serif)
- Letter-spacing: `tracking-[0.2em]` for product names, `tracking-wide` for nav links

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections use `py-20 md:py-28`
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6 md:gap-8`
- Hairline dividers: `border-b border-border`

## Components
- Buttons: Solid gold with dark text, or outlined with gold border. `rounded-sm` (subtle radius).
- Cards: White background, soft shadow on hover, image zoom effect.
- Inputs: Clean bottom-border style or subtle rounded with border.
- Hover: Subtle scale (1.02), shadow elevation, 300ms ease transition.

## Don'ts
- No bright colors, neon, or discount badges
- No harsh shadows or heavy borders
- No busy patterns or gradients
- No emoji or decorative icons in content
