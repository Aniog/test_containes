# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Brand Personality
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium yet accessible.

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - Style: Semi-bold to Light, high contrast.
  - Product Names: Uppercase with `tracking-widest-xl` (letter-spacing: 0.25em).
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - Style: Regular (400) for body, Medium (500) for buttons and UI labels.

## Color Palette
- **Warm White (`#FDFBF7`)**: Primary background for a clean, editorial feel.
- **Charcoal/Stone (`#1A1A1A` / `#2C2C2C`)**: Primary text and deep accents.
- **Velmora Gold (`#D4AF37`)**: Brand accent color for buttons, active states, and small details.
- **Taupe/Cream (`#8C7E74` / `#F5F2ED`)**: Secondary backgrounds and dividers.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Velmora Gold with White text, minimal padding, sharp or slightly rounded corners (4px).
  - Secondary: Outlined Charcoal or Velmora Gold.
- **Dividers**: Thin hairlines (0.5px or 1px) in Taupe or Light Gray.
- **Imagery**: Large editorial focus, warm lighting, generous whitespace around images.
- **Transitions**: Soft fade-ins, subtle hover lifts, smooth scroll behavior.

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace to evoke a "luxury" feel.
- Sticky navigation with transparency.
- Responsive grids with 2 columns on mobile, 4-5 on desktop.
