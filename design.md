# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry DTC brand.
Target: women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase, $30–$120 price point.

## Color Palette
- Base: cream (#FAF8F5) — page backgrounds
- Sand: (#F3EFE9) — card backgrounds, subtle surfaces
- Gold accent: (#C8A96E) — CTAs, highlights, links
- Gold hover: (#B8954F)
- Deep: (#1C1A18) — primary text
- Surface: (#262422) — dark backgrounds (hero overlay, footer)
- Muted: (#8C8984) — secondary text, captions
- Divider: (#E5E0D9) — hairlines on light bg
- Dark divider: (#3A3734) — hairlines on dark bg

## Typography
- Headings/Product names: Playfair Display (serif), UPPERCASE with 0.15em letter-spacing
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif), clean weights 300–600

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace — sections have at least py-20 (80px)
- Cards have p-4 or p-6
- Buttons: px-8 py-3.5

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold (#C8A96E) or outlined gold, uppercase, letter-spacing
- Dividers: hairline thin (h-px), color depends on bg
- Cards: subtle shadow, sand bg, rounded-none (editorial feel)
- Hover: gentle opacity or color transitions, 300ms

## Do
- Use warm, gold-tinted neutrals
- Use serif headings in uppercase with letter-spacing
- Keep generous whitespace
- Subtle, elegant transitions

## Don't
- No bright/loud colors
- No rounded pill buttons (keep minimal radius)
- No generic e-commerce tropes (badges, strikethrough prices, urgency timers)
- No harsh shadows or heavy borders
