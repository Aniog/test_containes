# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background (primary):** `#FAF8F5` — warm ivory/cream
- **Background (secondary):** `#F3F0EB` — soft warm gray
- **Surface / Card:** `#FFFFFF` — pure white
- **Text (primary):** `#1A1A1A` — near-black charcoal
- **Text (secondary):** `#6B6560` — warm taupe
- **Text (muted):** `#9B9590` — soft warm gray
- **Accent / Gold:** `#B8952E` — refined warm gold
- **Accent (hover):** `#9A7B24` — deeper gold
- **Accent (light):** `#F5EDDA` — pale gold wash
- **Border / Divider:** `#E8E4DF` — warm hairline
- **Error:** `#C45C4A` — muted terracotta
- **Success:** `#5A8A6A` — muted sage

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names:** Cormorant Garamond (serif), UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (0.15em–0.25em)
- **Body / UI:** Inter (sans-serif), normal weight, clean and readable
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.15em
- **Section titles:** UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.2em

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 to py-28 on desktop, py-12 to py-16 on mobile
- Card gaps: gap-6 to gap-8
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl with px-6 md:px-8 lg:px-12

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E8E4DF]
- Subtle rounded corners: rounded-sm (2px) or rounded (4px)
- No heavy borders, no thick outlines

## Shadows
- Soft, subtle shadows only: shadow-sm or custom shadow
- Card hover: shadow-md with smooth transition

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold background, white text, uppercase, tracking-wide
- Secondary: outlined gold border, gold text, transparent background
- Full-width on product pages, auto on nav
- Hover: slight darken, smooth transition

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions: duration-300 ease-out
- Image zoom on hover: scale-105 with overflow-hidden
- Fade-in on scroll: opacity transition
- Cart drawer: slide-in from right

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use warm, editorial imagery
- Maintain consistent gold accent usage
- Ensure strong contrast for accessibility

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No heavy shadows or thick borders
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No cluttered layouts
