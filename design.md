# MicroCosmos — Design System

## Concept
A visually immersive single-page website exploring the microscopic world — bacteria, cells, tardigrades, diatoms, and other tiny organisms invisible to the naked eye. The design should evoke a sense of wonder, scientific curiosity, and the beauty of the unseen.

## Color Palette
- **Deep Space Background**: `#0a0e17` (near-black with a cool blue tint)
- **Card / Surface Background**: `#111827` (gray-900)
- **Accent Teal**: `#2dd4bf` (teal-400) — primary accent for buttons, highlights
- **Accent Cyan**: `#06b6d4` (cyan-500) — secondary accent
- **Bioluminescent Green**: `#a3e635` (lime-400) — for subtle glowing elements
- **Text Primary**: `#f1f5f9` (slate-100)
- **Text Secondary**: `#94a3b8` (slate-400)
- **Text Muted**: `#64748b` (slate-500)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, weight 700-800, tight tracking
- **Body**: Inter, weight 400, relaxed line-height
- **Display/Hero**: Large sizes with gradient text effects

## Visual Style
- Dark theme throughout with a sense of depth
- Gradient overlays and subtle glow effects
- Smooth rounded corners on cards (rounded-2xl)
- Glass-morphism effects on cards (subtle backdrop blur)
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Subtle grid/dot patterns in backgrounds

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds with vibrant accent colors
- Apply teal/cyan gradients for hero text
- Use card-based layouts with hover effects
- Keep sections well-spaced with clear visual hierarchy
- Use Lucide icons for visual elements

## Don'ts
- Avoid light/white backgrounds
- Avoid harsh borders — use subtle shadows instead
- Don't overcrowd sections
- Avoid flat, uninteresting typography
