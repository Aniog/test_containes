# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

Velmora uses a quiet luxury editorial identity: warm ivory surfaces, refined espresso ink, burnished gold accents, and generous whitespace. The site should feel premium, calm, feminine, and giftable without discount-style urgency.

## Palette
- Base: warm ivory (`velmora-ivory`) for main backgrounds.
- Deep contrast: espresso (`velmora-espresso`) for text, headers, overlays, and premium dark sections.
- Secondary surface: parchment (`velmora-parchment`) and sand (`velmora-sand`) for soft editorial panels.
- Accent: burnished gold (`velmora-gold`) and soft gold (`velmora-softGold`) for CTAs, rules, icons, and jewelry-forward details.
- Muted copy: taupe (`velmora-taupe`) for supporting text.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond via `font-serifDisplay`, elegant and high contrast.
- Body and UI: Manrope via `font-sans`, clean and modern.
- Product names: uppercase, wide letter spacing, restrained sizes.

## Layout and spacing
- Use mobile-first responsive layouts.
- Prefer large section padding (`py-16`, `md:py-24`) and centered max-width containers (`max-w-7xl`).
- Use hairline dividers with `border-velmora-line`.
- Cards should feel soft, editorial, and airy, never boxed-in or crowded.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid espresso or gold-accent styles, subtle hover transitions, no loud gradients.
- Product cards: warm neutral image wells, fine typography, quick-add reveal on hover.
- Navigation: transparent over homepage hero, solid ivory on scroll or internal pages.
- Cart drawer: clean ivory panel with clear contrast and obvious quantity controls.

## Do
- Keep text readable with explicit `text-velmora-espresso`, `text-velmora-ivory`, or `text-velmora-taupe`.
- Use warm editorial images and dark/neutral backgrounds that flatter gold jewelry.
- Keep animation subtle: opacity, transform, soft shadow.

## Don't
- Do not use bright sale colors, heavy badges, neon accents, or generic marketplace styling.
- Do not overcrowd mobile screens.
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-ivory text for important values.
