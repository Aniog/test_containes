# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `cream` (#FDFBF7) — warm off-white base
- **Foreground/Text**: `charcoal` (#1A1A1A) — near-black for body text
- **Accent**: `gold` (#B8860B) — warm dark gold for CTAs, highlights
- **Accent Light**: `gold-light` (#D4A843) — lighter gold for hover states
- **Muted**: `taupe` (#8B7D6B) — warm gray-brown for secondary text
- **Surface**: `linen` (#F5F0E8) — slightly darker warm surface for cards
- **Border**: `sand` (#E8E0D4) — subtle warm borders/dividers
- **Dark**: `espresso` (#2C2420) — dark warm brown for dark sections

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond` (serif), weights 300–700
- **Body/UI**: `Inter` (sans-serif), weights 300–600
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond 500

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: sections use py-16 md:py-24
- Cards: p-0 (image bleeds) or p-6 for text areas
- Grid gaps: gap-4 md:gap-6 lg:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-sand (1px)
- No heavy borders; prefer subtle shadows or no border

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-gold text-cream px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm font-medium hover:bg-gold-light transition-colors
- Secondary/Outlined: border border-charcoal text-charcoal px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm hover:bg-charcoal hover:text-cream transition-colors

## Shadows
- Soft: shadow-sm or shadow-md with warm tones
- Cards on hover: shadow-lg transition-shadow

## Animations
- Transitions: duration-300 ease-in-out
- Hover scale on product images: hover:scale-105
- Fade-in on scroll (optional)

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep imagery large and editorial
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Restrained accent color usage
- Subtle hover transitions

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded corners on product cards (use rounded-none or rounded-sm)
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/sale badges styling
