# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury with a warm editorial mood. The site should feel like a premium demi-fine jewelry atelier: intimate, polished, calm, and accessible. Avoid loud sales styling, heavy gradients, generic marketplace layouts, or overly bright retail colors.

## Palette
Use a deep refined base with warm neutrals and restrained metallic accents.

- Ink: deep espresso-black for primary text and immersive dark sections. Example Tailwind: `bg-velmora-ink`, `text-velmora-ink`.
- Cocoa: softened dark brown for secondary dark surfaces. Example: `bg-velmora-cocoa`.
- Porcelain: warm cream page background. Example: `bg-velmora-porcelain`.
- Champagne: pale neutral card surface. Example: `bg-velmora-champagne`.
- Sand: subtle borders and muted fills. Example: `border-velmora-sand`.
- Gold: elegant metallic accent for buttons, rules, highlights. Example: `bg-velmora-gold`, `text-velmora-gold`.
- Gold-dark: accessible hover/accent text on light backgrounds. Example: `text-velmora-goldDark`.
- Blush: barely warm editorial accent blocks. Example: `bg-velmora-blush`.

Do: keep contrast high; use ink text on light surfaces and porcelain text on dark surfaces.
Don't: place pale beige text on cream backgrounds or dark brown text over dark image overlays.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, high contrast, refined tracking.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names must be uppercase with wide letter-spacing.
- Use generous line height for editorial copy.

Example classes: `font-serif`, `font-sans`, `tracking-[0.18em]`, `uppercase`.

## Layout and Spacing
- Mobile-first with generous but practical spacing.
- Desktop pages should use multi-column editorial layouts, not mobile stacking.
- Use large imagery, airy sections, and thin hairline dividers.
- Containers should generally use `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 sm:px-8 lg:px-10`.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid gold or thin outlined dark/gold styles; slight hover lift, no loud shadows.
- Cards: soft cream/champagne surfaces, hairline borders, restrained shadows on hover only.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid warm cream after scroll with clear contrast.
- Cart drawer: calm light surface with explicit ink text and clear action buttons.

## Imagery
Use warm-lit gold jewelry, model close-ups, ear/neck detail shots, editorial still life, and neutral/dark backgrounds. Use the Strikingly image tagging system with contextual text references. Avoid unrelated fashion, busy product collages, or discount-style banners.
