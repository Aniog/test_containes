# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- Target: Women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase, $30–$120 price range.

## Color Palette
- **Base/Background**: `#1A1714` (deep warm charcoal) — primary background
- **Surface**: `#2A2622` (warm dark gray) — cards, elevated surfaces
- **Surface Light**: `#F8F5F0` (warm ivory) — light mode backgrounds
- **Text Primary**: `#F5F0EB` (warm cream) — main text on dark
- **Text Dark**: `#1A1714` (deep charcoal) — text on light backgrounds
- **Text Muted**: `#8B7E74` (warm taupe) — secondary text
- **Accent Gold**: `#C4A265` (rich gold) — primary accent, CTAs
- **Accent Gold Light**: `#D4B87A` — hover states
- **Accent Gold Dark**: `#A8884F` — pressed states
- **Border**: `#3A352F` (subtle warm dark) — dividers, borders on dark
- **Border Light**: `#E8E2DA` — borders on light backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings / Logo**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- **Product Names**: UPPERCASE, `letter-spacing: 0.15em`
- **Logo**: UPPERCASE, `letter-spacing: 0.3em`

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace — sections padded 80px+ vertically on desktop
- Max content width: 1280px centered
- Card border-radius: 2px (minimal, jewelry-forward)
- Button border-radius: 2px

## Visual Style
- Thin hairline dividers (`border-[#3A352F]`)
- Soft shadows: `shadow-lg shadow-black/10`
- Hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`
- Buttons: solid accent fill or outlined with accent border
- Images: warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Overlay gradients on image cards

## Component Patterns
- Navbar: transparent → solid on scroll, backdrop blur
- Cards: subtle scale on hover, image zoom effect
- Buttons: uppercase, wide letter-spacing, solid accent or outlined
- Inputs: clean bottom-border style or minimal outlined

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or discount-looking elements
- No heavy borders or boxy layouts
- No generic e-commerce feel
- No bright/bold accent colors
- No decorative flourishes — keep it minimal and refined
