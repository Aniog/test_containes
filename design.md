# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF7F2` (warm cream) — primary page background
- **Warm accent**: `#F5EDE3` (warm beige) — section backgrounds, cards
- **Sand**: `#E8DDD0` — borders, dividers, subtle backgrounds
- **Gold (primary accent)**: `#C9A96E` — buttons, links, highlights, CTAs
- **Gold light**: `#D4BA8A` — hover states
- **Gold dark**: `#A88B52` — active/pressed states
- **Charcoal**: `#2C2C2C` — hero text, dark sections
- **Dark**: `#1A1A1A` — footer, overlays
- **Text**: `#3D3D3D` — body text
- **Muted**: `#8A8A8A` — secondary text, placeholders
- **Border**: `#E5DDD3` — hairline dividers
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` — card backgrounds, contrast elements

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weights 400-600
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weights 300-500
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em-0.2em, serif
- **Section headings**: Serif, large, generous tracking
- **Body text**: 14-16px, line-height 1.6-1.8

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: 80-120px vertical (desktop), 48-64px (mobile)
- Card padding: 24-32px
- Gap between grid items: 24-32px

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid gold (#C9A96E) with white text, or outlined with gold border. Rounded-none or rounded-sm. Min height 44px. Hover: darken gold. Transition 200ms.
- **Cards**: White or warm background, subtle shadow on hover, no border radius (or minimal 2px)
- **Dividers**: Hairline (1px), color #E5DDD3
- **Inputs**: Clean, minimal border, gold focus ring
- **Navigation**: Transparent over hero, solid cream on scroll

## Do's
- Use warm, editorial photography
- Keep layouts spacious and breathable
- Use serif for emotional/editorial moments
- Use thin hairline dividers between sections
- Subtle hover transitions (200-300ms)
- Soft shadows (shadow-sm to shadow-md)

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/saturated colors
- Don't use heavy borders or thick dividers
- Don't crowd elements — always leave breathing room
- Don't use rounded corners larger than 4px
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (badges, aggressive sales text)
- Don't use dark text on gold backgrounds (use white instead)
