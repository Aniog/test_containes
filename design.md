# ARTITECT MACHINERY Visual Style

## Brand mood
Elegant, industrial, trustworthy, and user-friendly. The site should feel premium and precise, suitable for sheet metal folding equipment and manufacturing buyers.

## Colors
- Use a deep charcoal / slate foundation for navigation and hero surfaces, with classes such as `bg-slate-950`, `bg-slate-900`, and `text-white`.
- Use warm amber accents to suggest precision engineering and premium machinery, with classes such as `bg-amber-500`, `text-amber-400`, and `border-amber-300`.
- Use clean light sections for readability, with classes such as `bg-slate-50`, `bg-white`, `text-slate-950`, and `text-slate-600`.
- Avoid low-contrast gray-on-gray text. Always pair dark surfaces with white or slate-100 text.

## Typography
- Use Inter as the primary font.
- Headlines should be bold, spacious, and confident: `text-4xl`, `md:text-6xl`, `font-semibold`, `tracking-tight`.
- Body copy should be clear and readable: `text-base`, `md:text-lg`, `leading-7`.

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous vertical spacing: `py-16`, `md:py-24`.
- Use centered max-width containers: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 lg:px-8`.
- Use responsive grids, keeping desktop layouts multi-column and mobile layouts stacked.

## Components
- Cards should use `rounded-3xl`, soft borders, and subtle shadows.
- Buttons should be large and easy to tap: `rounded-full px-6 py-3 font-semibold`.
- Product cards should include explicit foreground colors and readable labels.

## Do
- Make the machinery products feel durable, precise, and easy to evaluate.
- Use concise benefit-led copy.
- Keep calls to action prominent.

## Don't
- Do not use playful colors or cluttered layouts.
- Do not use invisible or low-contrast text.
- Do not hardcode arbitrary hex colors in JSX classes.
