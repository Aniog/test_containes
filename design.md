# Bike Website Design Guide

## Visual Style
A modern, clean design celebrating the freedom and adventure of cycling. The design should feel energetic yet professional, appealing to both casual riders and cycling enthusiasts.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `bg-blue-600` (vibrant cycling blue)
- **Primary Hover**: `bg-blue-700`
- **Secondary**: `bg-green-500` (nature/eco-friendly green)
- **Secondary Hover**: `bg-green-600`
- **Accent**: `bg-orange-500` (energy/adventure orange)
- **Neutral Dark**: `bg-gray-900` (headers, text)
- **Neutral Light**: `bg-gray-100` (backgrounds)
- **White**: `bg-white` (cards, sections)

## Typography
- **Headings**: `font-bold` with `text-4xl`, `text-3xl`, `text-2xl`, `text-xl`
- **Body Text**: `text-gray-700` for readability
- **Accent Text**: `text-blue-600` for links and highlights
- **Large Text**: `text-lg` for important descriptions

## Spacing & Layout
- **Section Padding**: `py-16` for major sections, `py-8` for subsections
- **Container**: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- **Grid Layouts**: `grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3 gap-8`
- **Card Spacing**: `p-6` for card content

## Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners `rounded-lg`, padding `px-6 py-3`
- **Cards**: White background with subtle shadow `bg-white shadow-lg rounded-lg`
- **Images**: Rounded corners `rounded-lg` with proper aspect ratios
- **Icons**: Use Lucide React icons, size `w-6 h-6` or `w-8 h-8`

## Visual Elements
- **Gradients**: Use subtle gradients for hero sections
- **Shadows**: `shadow-lg` for cards, `shadow-xl` for elevated elements
- **Borders**: Minimal use, `border-gray-200` when needed
- **Hover Effects**: Smooth transitions with `transition-all duration-300`

## Do's
- Use high-quality bike imagery with proper aspect ratios
- Maintain consistent spacing throughout
- Use the color palette consistently
- Ensure good contrast for accessibility
- Keep text readable with proper line heights

## Don'ts
- Don't use too many colors outside the defined palette
- Avoid cluttered layouts
- Don't use small, hard-to-read text
- Avoid overly complex animations
- Don't mix different border radius styles
