# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand
Velmora is a premium demi-fine DTC jewelry brand. The visual identity communicates quiet luxury, warmth, and editorial sophistication. Gold jewelry is the hero.

## Color Palette
- `brand-bg`: #F5F0EB (warm cream / editorial background)
- `brand-white`: #FAF7F2 (warm white for cards, overlays)
- `brand-dark`: #1A1614 (warm near-black for primary text on light)
- `brand-gold`: #C9A96E (warm gold accent — buttons, highlights, links)
- `brand-gold-dark`: #B8944A (darker gold for hover states)
- `brand-muted`: #8C8279 (muted text, secondary info)
- `brand-border`: #E0D8CF (thin dividers, borders)
- `brand-light`: #EAE3DA (subtle hover backgrounds, light accents)
- `brand-charcoal`: #2C2826 (secondary text on light bg)

## Typography
- **Headings (h1-h3, product names, logo):** Cormorant Garamond, serif. Product names UPPERCASE with tracking-widest.
- **Body / UI:** Inter, sans-serif. Clean, readable.
- **Letter-spacing for product names:** `tracking-[0.15em]` uppercase
- **Hairline dividers:** `border-t border-[#E0D8CF]` with thin weight

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: `py-16 md:py-24` for sections
- `px-4 md:px-8 lg:px-16` for container padding
- Max width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto`

## Visual Style
- Buttons: solid gold (`bg-brand-gold text-white hover:bg-brand-gold-dark`) or outlined (`border-brand-gold text-brand-gold`)
- Shadows: `shadow-sm` for cards, `shadow-md` for hover states
- Borders: `border border-brand-border` for cards
- Hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300`
- Images: large editorial style, warm lighting
- Dividers: thin `border-t border-brand-border` hairline
- Focus rings: `ring-brand-gold` for accessibility

## Do's
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use warm, editorial imagery
- Keep gold as the single accent color
- Use serif for elegance, sans-serif for clarity
- Hairline dividers between sections

## Don'ts
- No loud, discount, or sale aesthetics
- No bright or neon colors
- No heavy borders or thick lines
- No generic e-commerce stock photos
- No cluttered layouts
