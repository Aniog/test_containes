# SSourcing China - Design Document

## Visual Style
Clean, professional, international B2B appearance. Trust-oriented with clear typography and structured layouts.

## Typography
- Main Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Heading Weight: 700 (bold)
- Body Weight: 400 (regular)

## Colors (Tailwind v4 theme)
- Primary (Navy Blue): `#0F172A` (slate-900) - For trust and professionalism.
- Secondary (Gold/Yellow): `#EAB308` (yellow-500) - For highlighting CTAs and key value points.
- Accent (Silver/Gray): `#64748B` (slate-500) - For secondary text and borders.
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (white) and `#F8FAFC` (slate-50) for sectioning.

## Layout & Spacing
- Vertical Content Spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Max Width Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`

## Components
- Navigation: Fixed header with clean links and a prominent CTA button.
- Buttons: Rounded-lg with transition effects.
- Cards: Subtle shadows, white backgrounds, and clear icons.
- Images: High-quality visuals with `hover:scale-105` transitions.

## Do's
- Use realistic sourcing/factory visuals.
- Keep text clear and focused on buyer benefits.
- Ensure all CTA's lead to the inquiry form.

## Don'ts
- Avoid cluttered designs or flashy animations.
- No exaggerated promises.
- Never use unreadable text contrast.
