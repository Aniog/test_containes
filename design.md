# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry.

## Color Palette
- **Cream** (#FDFAF6): Primary background — warm off-white
- **Warm White** (#F5F0EB): Secondary background — subtle contrast
- **Espresso** (#3C2415): Dark accent — deep warm brown for text and emphasis
- **Gold** (#C8A45C): Metallic accent — buttons, links, highlights
- **Warm Black** (#1A1410): Primary text — softer than pure black
- **Taupe** (#E8E0D5): Hairline borders, dividers, subtle elements
- **Muted Gold** (#F7F2E7): Lightest accent — hover states, subtle backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — 400, 500, 600, 700 weights. Product names in UPPERCASE with letter-spacing 0.15em.
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) — 300, 400, 500, 600 weights. Clean and legible for UI elements.

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections use py-20 to py-32
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl (1280px) centered
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-taupe

## Components
- Buttons: Solid gold (#C8A45C) with white text, or outlined gold border. Rounded-sm, uppercase tracking-wider text.
- Cards: Cream background, subtle hover shadow-lg transition
- Product grids: gap-8, 2 cols mobile, 4-5 cols desktop

## Do's
- Use warm, refined language
- Keep ample breathing room
- Maintain 45-75 character line lengths for readability
- Use gold sparingly as accent only

## Don'ts
- No bright/saturated colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No discount badges or sale language
- No pure black (#000) — always use warm black
