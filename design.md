# MicroCosmos Website Design Guide

## Visual Style
A modern, scientific, and visually stunning design that captures the wonder of microscopic worlds.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep space blue (#0B1426) - for backgrounds and headers
- **Secondary**: Cosmic purple (#6366F1) - for accents and highlights  
- **Accent**: Electric cyan (#06B6D4) - for interactive elements
- **Text**: Pure white (#FFFFFF) and light gray (#F1F5F9) for readability
- **Background**: Dark gradient from deep blue to black

## Typography
- **Headings**: Large, bold, modern sans-serif (text-4xl to text-6xl)
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif (text-base to text-lg)
- **Captions**: Smaller, elegant text for image descriptions (text-sm)

## Layout Principles
- **Hero Section**: Full-screen with stunning background image
- **Grid Layouts**: Masonry-style grids for image galleries
- **Cards**: Elevated cards with subtle shadows and rounded corners
- **Spacing**: Generous padding and margins for breathing room

## Visual Elements
- **Borders**: Subtle rounded corners (rounded-lg, rounded-xl)
- **Shadows**: Soft, layered shadows for depth
- **Gradients**: Subtle gradients for backgrounds and overlays
- **Animations**: Smooth hover effects and transitions

## Image Style
- **High contrast**: Vibrant microscopic imagery
- **Aspect ratios**: Mix of 16:9, 4:3, and 1:1 for visual variety
- **Overlays**: Dark overlays on background images for text readability

## Interactive Elements
- **Buttons**: Rounded, with hover effects (bg-cyan-500 hover:bg-cyan-600)
- **Links**: Underlined on hover with color transitions
- **Cards**: Subtle lift effect on hover (hover:shadow-xl)

## Do's
- Use high-quality microscopic imagery throughout
- Maintain consistent spacing with Tailwind classes (p-6, m-4, etc.)
- Create visual hierarchy with typography scales
- Use the cosmic color palette consistently

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright, jarring colors that compete with images
- Don't overcrowd sections - maintain white space
- Don't use small images - showcase the microscopic beauty
- Don't use generic stock photos - focus on scientific imagery
