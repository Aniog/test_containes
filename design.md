# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Base**: Warm cream/ivory (`velvet-50`: #faf8f6) — soft, editorial background
- **Deep base**: Rich espresso (`velvet-900`: #1a1512, `velvet-950`: #0f0c0a) — footer, dark accents, text on light
- **Mid tones**: Warm taupe/greige (`velvet-200`: #e6ddd6, `velvet-300`: #d4c5b8, `velvet-500`: #a88d7b)
- **Accent**: Burnished gold (`gold-500`: #d4982e, `gold-600`: #c17d24) — CTAs, badges, highlights
- **Text**: velvet-900 (#1a1512) for body, velvet-500/600 for secondary, velvet-50 for dark backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), light weight (300–400), wide tracking
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), regular weight (400), clean
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, tracking-widest-plus (0.25em), font-medium
- **Section headings**: serif, text-3xl to text-4xl, font-light, tracking-wide

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: sections have py-20 md:py-28
- Content max-width: max-w-screen-2xl
- Side padding: px-6 md:px-12

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-velvet-200
- Card borders: none, use subtle shadows or background contrast

## Buttons
- **Primary**: btn-accent — gold-500 bg, white text, hover gold-600, tracking-widest uppercase
- **Outline**: btn-outline — velvet-300 border, velvet-800 text, hover bg-velvet-50
- **Dark**: btn-primary — velvet-900 bg, velvet-50 text, hover velvet-800

## Cards
- Product cards: aspect-[3/4], bg-velvet-100, rounded-sm, subtle hover transitions
- Image hover: crossfade to second image, quick-add button slides up

## Shadows
- Soft, refined: shadow-sm, shadow-lg only on hover
- Accent shadow: shadow-gold-500/20 on gold buttons

## Animation
- fade-in: 0.6s ease-out (hero content)
- slide-up: 0.6s ease-out (CTAs)
- slide-in-right: 0.4s ease-out (cart drawer)
- All transitions: duration-300 to duration-500

## DO's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use uppercase + wide letter-spacing for product names
- Neutral warm backgrounds to let gold jewelry stand out
- Subtle hover states, never jarring

## DON'Ts
- No bright/saturated colors that compete with gold
- No bold font weights on headings (use light)
- No heavy borders or box shadows
- No discount-looking badges or sale tags
- No generic e-commerce patterns (no pure white + blue)
