# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, demi-fine jewelry. Premium but accessible ($30-$120).
Target: women 25-45, self-purchase and gifting.

## Color Palette
- Background: `hsl(40 20% 97%)` — warm off-white, editorial feel
- Foreground: `hsl(0 0% 12%)` — near-black with subtle warmth
- Primary/Gold: `hsl(38 60% 55%)` — warm gold accent (buttons, links, highlights)
- Border: `hsl(40 10% 84%)` — subtle warm gray
- Muted: `hsl(40 10% 92%)` — light warm gray for surfaces
- Card: `hsl(40 20% 99%)` — barely-there warmth
- No dark mode — always light with warm undertones

## Typography
- Headings (h1-h3, product names): Cormorant Garamond serif, uppercase with wide letter-spacing (0.12em) for product names
- Body & UI: Inter sans-serif, clean and minimal
- Scale: h1 ~3.5rem, h2 ~2rem, h3 ~1.25rem, body 0.938rem

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: px-6 (mobile), md:px-12, lg:px-16
- Max content width: max-w-7xl (80rem)
- Full-bleed hero, full-bleed UGC reel

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: `border-t border-border/50`
- Border color: hsl(40 10% 84%)
- Border radius: 0.25rem (minuscule, mostly squared-off)

## Shadows
- Soft, subtle shadows only on cards: `shadow-sm`
- Hover: `shadow-md` with smooth transition
- Buttons: hover `shadow-lg`

## Interactions
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- Scale on product cards (hover:scale-[1.02])
- Opacity fades on image swaps
- Smooth scroll behavior

## Buttons
- Primary: gold background, white text, uppercase, tracking-wider
- Outline: bordered, transparent bg, dark text, inverts on hover
- Both: 12px x- 12px y padding, 14px font, uppercase

## DO's
- Use warm off-white backgrounds
- Gold as accent ONLY — not overwhelming
- Serif for elegance, sans for readability
- Product names in UPPERCASE with wide tracking
- Large editorial images
- Generous whitespace
- Thin, subtle dividers
- Soft shadows on hover

## DON'Ts
- No loud or neon colors
- No discount/voucher visual language
- No generic stock photography
- No dark mode
- No cluttered layouts
- No bold/black typography
- No heavy borders
