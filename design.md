# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#faf8f5` (warm ivory) — main page bg
- **Surface**: `#ffffff` (white) — cards, nav solid, modals
- **Foreground**: `#1a1412` (deep warm brown-black) — primary text
- **Muted Foreground**: `#6b5e54` (warm taupe) — secondary text
- **Muted**: `#f0ece6` (light warm beige) — subtle bg, hover states
- **Border**: `#e5ded5` (warm hairline)
- **Accent**: `#b8960c` (antique gold) — CTAs, links, highlights
- **Accent Hover**: `#9a7d0a` (deeper gold)
- **Accent Foreground**: `#ffffff` (white on gold buttons)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), 400/500/600/700
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), 300/400/500/600
- **Product names**: Uppercase, `tracking-[0.2em]`, serif
- **Logo**: Serif, uppercase, `tracking-[0.3em]`, font-weight 500

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace, max-width container: `max-w-7xl` (1280px)
- Section vertical padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Card padding: `p-4 md:p-6`
- Border radius: `rounded-sm` for cards, `rounded-none` for buttons

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid accent bg or outlined accent border. No rounded corners (sharp rectangular). Uppercase tracking-wide sans-serif.
- **Cards**: White bg, subtle shadow on hover, thin warm border
- **Dividers**: `border-[#e5ded5]` hairline
- **Inputs**: Clean, minimal, bottom-border style or thin border

## Do's
- Use warm neutrals throughout
- Generous whitespace
- Subtle hover transitions (200-300ms ease)
- Sharp geometric buttons (no rounded corners)
- High contrast text

## Don'ts
- No bright colors, no neon
- No heavy shadows or gradients
- No rounded pill buttons (except variant selectors)
- No discount-looking badges
- No Comic Sans or playful fonts
