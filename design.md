# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Base (deep warm charcoal):** `#1C1917` (stone-900) — primary background for hero, footer, dark sections
- **Cream (warm off-white):** `#FAF7F2` — page background, card backgrounds
- **Gold Accent:** `#C9A96E` — primary accent, CTAs, highlights, hover states
- **Gold Light:** `#D4B87A` — lighter gold for hover/secondary accents
- **Text Primary:** `#1C1917` — headings, body text on light backgrounds
- **Text Secondary:** `#78716C` (stone-500) — muted text, descriptions
- **Text on Dark:** `#FAF7F2` — text on dark backgrounds
- **Divider:** `#E7E5E4` (stone-200) — hairline dividers
- **Card Background:** `#FFFFFF` — product cards on cream

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body/UI:** Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond
- **Nav links:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.1em, Inter, text-sm

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Hairline dividers (1px, stone-200)

## Buttons
- **Primary (accent):** bg-gold, text-white, uppercase, letter-spacing, hover:bg-gold-light
- **Secondary (outlined):** border-gold, text-gold, uppercase, hover:bg-gold hover:text-white
- Rounded: none or minimal (2px), premium feel
- Padding: generous (14px 32px)

## Images
- Large editorial imagery
- Warm-lit, gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Hover: subtle scale or second image reveal
- Aspect ratios: 3x4 for product cards, 16x9 for hero, 9x16 for UGC reels

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth transitions (300ms ease)
- Hover: image swap, gentle scale (1.02)
- Nav: transparent to solid on scroll
- No bouncy or flashy animations

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Keep product names uppercase with wide letter-spacing
- Use gold accent sparingly and consistently
- Ensure strong contrast on all text

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or saturated colors
- Don't use rounded/bubbly UI elements
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (badges, loud sales banners)
- Don't mix too many fonts
- Don't use low-contrast text
