# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial metal folding machinery
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly — precision engineering meets refined aesthetics

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep charcoal `#1C1C1C` — authority, industrial strength
- **Secondary**: Steel blue `#2E5077` — precision, trust, engineering
- **Accent**: Warm gold `#C8A951` — premium quality, craftsmanship
- **Background**: Off-white `#F5F5F0` — clean, professional
- **Surface**: White `#FFFFFF` — cards, content areas
- **Muted**: Cool gray `#6B7280` — secondary text
- **Border**: Light gray `#E5E7EB` — subtle dividers

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, font-weight 700-800, tracking tight
- **Body**: Inter, font-weight 400, line-height 1.6
- **Accent text**: Uppercase, letter-spacing wide, small size for labels/tags

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Content max-width: `max-w-7xl`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows on cards: `shadow-sm` to `shadow-md`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-lg` to `rounded-xl`
- Gold accent lines/borders for premium feel
- Industrial imagery with dark overlays for hero sections
- Product cards with clean white backgrounds and subtle hover effects

## Do's
- Use gold accents sparingly for emphasis
- Keep layouts clean and uncluttered
- Use high-contrast text on dark backgrounds
- Maintain consistent spacing rhythm
- Use subtle transitions on hover states

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much content
- Don't use decorative fonts
- Don't use heavy drop shadows
- Don't mix too many font sizes
