# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury. Warm, editorial, premium-but-accessible demi-fine gold jewelry.
NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Base / Background**: `#0F0E0E` — deep warm black, used as primary dark background
- **Surface / Card**: `#1A1918` — slightly lifted dark surface
- **Primary Text**: `#F5F0EB` — warm off-white
- **Secondary Text**: `#A89F94` — warm muted taupe
- **Accent**: `#C8A97E` — warm champagne gold (CTAs, highlights, stars)
- **Accent Hover**: `#D4BA8E` — slightly lighter gold
- **Divider**: `#2E2C2A` — hairline dividers
- **Light Background**: `#F5F0EB` — warm off-white for light sections
- **Light Text**: `#1A1918` — on light backgrounds

## Typography
- **Serif**: Cormorant Garamond (Google Fonts)
  - Headlines, product names, section titles, logo
  - Product names: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (0.15em)
- **Sans-serif**: Inter (Google Fonts)
  - Body text, UI labels, buttons, navigation, prices

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace. Sections separated by 80–120px.
- Mobile-first, but desktop has max-width containers at 1280px.

## Components
- **Buttons**: solid accent gold (`#C8A97E`) with dark text, or outlined with gold border
- **Cards**: subtle rounded corners (8px), no heavy shadows. Soft shadow on hover.
- **Dividers**: 1px solid `#2E2C2A`
- **Hover transitions**: 300ms ease for all interactive elements
