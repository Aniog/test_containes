# Design Guidelines - ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style
Elegant, professional, and industrial yet user-friendly. The design should convey precision and reliability.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: 'Inter', sans-serif (for readability and modern feel)
- **Headings**: 'Inter', sans-serif, bold/semibold
- **Body**: 'Inter', sans-serif, normal/light

## Colors
- **Primary (Industrial Blue)**: #1e3a8a (Tailwind `blue-900`)
- **Secondary (Steel Gray)**: #4b5563 (Tailwind `gray-600`)
- **Accent (Precision Orange)**: #f97316 (Tailwind `orange-500`)
- **Background**: #f9fafb (Tailwind `gray-50`)
- **Surface**: #ffffff (Tailwind `white`)
- **Text (Main)**: #111827 (Tailwind `gray-900`)
- **Text (Muted)**: #6b7280 (Tailwind `gray-500`)

## Layout
- Clean grid layouts
- Generous whitespace for an elegant feel
- Responsive containers (`max-w-7xl mx-auto`)

## Components
- **Cards**: Soft shadows, rounded corners (`rounded-lg shadow-sm`)
- **Buttons**: Clear calls to action, rounded (`rounded-md px-4 py-2 font-medium transition-colors`)
- **Navigation**: Sticky header, clear hierarchy

## Spacing
- Consistent padding and margins (`p-4`, `p-8`, `mb-4`, `gap-6`)

## Imagery
- High-quality industrial imagery focusing on metal folding machines and folding results.
- Use `data-strk-img` and `data-strk-bg` for stock images.
