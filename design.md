# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Warm cream `#FAF7F2` (main page bg)
- **Secondary Background**: Deep charcoal `#1A1714` (hero, footer, dark sections)
- **Accent**: Rich gold `#C9A96E` (CTAs, highlights, links)
- **Accent Hover**: Deep gold `#B8944F`
- **Text Primary**: Deep brown-black `#2C2520`
- **Text Secondary**: Warm gray `#8A8279`
- **Text on Dark**: Warm white `#F5F0EA`
- **Card Background**: Soft white `#FFFFFF`
- **Border**: Warm light gray `#E8E2D9`
- **Muted Surface**: `#F0EBE3`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond, serif. Uppercase with wide letter-spacing for product names.
- **Body / UI**: Inter, sans-serif. Clean and legible.
- **Font Sizes**: Use Tailwind scale. Hero h1: text-5xl/6xl, Section h2: text-3xl/4xl, Product names: text-lg, Body: text-sm/text-base.

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace. Sections: py-20 to py-28.
- Max content width: max-w-7xl (1280px).
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E8E2D9].
- Grid gaps: gap-6 to gap-8.

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C9A96E] text-white, hover:bg-[#B8944F], rounded-none or rounded-sm, uppercase, tracking-widest, text-xs/font-semibold.
- Secondary: border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E], hover:bg-[#C9A96E] hover:text-white.
- Premium feel: no rounded-full, no large border-radius.

## Cards
- Clean white bg, subtle shadow on hover (shadow-md).
- Image aspect ratios: 4x3 for products, 3x4 for UGC, 16x9 for category tiles.
- Hover: second image reveal, slight scale on image.

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth transitions. transition-all duration-300.
- No bouncy or playful animations.
- Fade-in on scroll where appropriate.

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Uppercase + tracking-widest for product names and CTAs
- Generous padding and margins
- Warm, gold-accented color scheme throughout
- High contrast text on all backgrounds

## Don'ts
- No bright/saturated colors
- No rounded-full buttons
- No playful/bouncy animations
- No generic blue/purple schemes
- No low-contrast text (muted text must still be readable)
