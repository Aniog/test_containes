# Design System: Strikingly Generators Hub

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Josefin Sans`, sans-serif (Google Fonts Fallback)
  - `font-family: 'Josefin Sans', sans-serif;`
  - Weight: 700 (Bold)
  - Style: Uppercase (`uppercase`)
  - Line-height: 1.2
- **Body**: `Open Sans`, sans-serif (Google Fonts)
  - `font-family: 'Open Sans', sans-serif;`
  - Weight: 400 (Regular)
  - Size: 14px (`text-[14px]`)
  - Line-height: 1.5

## Colors
- **Brand Purple**: `#8159BB`
- **AI Gradient**: `linear-gradient(to right, #7671FF, #CB0C9F)`
- **Body Text**: `#636972`
- **Headings**: `#4B5056`
- **H1 Line 1**: `#2E2E2F`
- **Card Border**: `#C6C9CD`
- **Subtle Divider**: `#E2E4E7`
- **Light Background**: `#F4F6F8`
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` (Primary Background)

## Spacing
- Multiples of 10px (e.g., `p-5`, `m-10`, `gap-5`).
- Small gaps: 5px (`gap-1.25`).
- Block spacing: 20px (`mb-5`).
- Section spacing: 40px (`py-10`).
- Hero vertical padding: 60px-80px (`py-[60px]` to `py-[80px]`).
- Button padding: 9px vertical, 15px horizontal (`py-[9px] px-[15px]`).

## Components

### Buttons
- **Standard**: 36px height, 4px border-radius, 14px uppercase bold.
- **Big**: 44px height.
- **Primary (AI Gradient)**: Fill gradient, white text (`text-white`).
- **Secondary (Ghost)**: Transparent bg, 1px Brand Purple border, Brand Purple text.

### Cards
- White background, 1px `#C6C9CD` border, 3px border-radius, 20px padding.
- Hover: 1px `#8159BB` border, shadow-sm (lift).

### Tags
- 11px uppercase bold, 2px vertical / 6px horizontal padding.
- Ghost: Brand Purple border/text.
