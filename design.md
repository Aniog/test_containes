# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Voice
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: Warm ivory (#FAF7F2) — main site background
- **Surface**: White (#FFFFFF) — cards, modals, drawers
- **Primary text**: Deep warm charcoal (#1C1C1C)
- **Secondary text**: Warm gray (#6B6B6B)
- **Muted/border**: Warm taupe (#E5E0D8)
- **Accent / Gold**: (#C9A94E) — CTAs, accents, hover states
- **Accent hover**: (#B8922E) — darker gold for hover
- **Light accent**: (#F5F0E8) — subtle gold tinted backgrounds
- **Deep base**: (#1A1A1A) — footer, dark sections

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names**: "Cormorant Garamond", serif — elegant, uppercase with wide tracking for product names
- **Body / UI**: "Inter", sans-serif — clean, readable
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, tracking-widest (0.15em+)

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px, centered
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Thin hairline dividers (border-t border-[#E5E0D8])

## Components
- **Buttons**: Full-width or inline, gold accent bg (#C9A94E) with white text, rounded-none, uppercase tracking-widest, py-3 px-8
- **Outlined buttons**: border border-[#C9A94E] text-[#C9A94E] bg-transparent
- **Product cards**: White bg, subtle shadow, image with hover zoom effect, uppercase product name
- **Dividers**: Thin 1px lines in warm taupe (#E5E0D8)

## Effects
- Hover transitions: 300ms ease
- Subtle shadow on cards: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
- Image zoom on hover: scale-105 transition
- Sticky nav: transparent -> solid white on scroll with backdrop-blur

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use uppercase with wide letter-spacing for product names
- Keep gold accents restrained
- Use editorial-style large images
- Thin hairline dividers

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No heavy shadows
- No discount-store feel
- No crowded layouts
- No generic stock photos
