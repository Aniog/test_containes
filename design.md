# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand Direction
ARTITECT MACHINERY should feel premium, engineered, and trustworthy. The visual tone is elegant industrial: refined layouts, confident typography, spacious composition, and practical calls to action.

## Core Personality
- Elegant, not flashy
- Industrial, not cold
- Technical, but easy to understand
- Premium, but approachable

## Color System
Use a restrained, professional palette.

- Primary background: soft stone white
- Secondary background: warm steel gray
- Deep surface: graphite
- Primary accent: brass gold
- Supporting accent: slate blue-gray
- Text: charcoal on light surfaces, white on dark surfaces

Recommended Tailwind-style pairings:
- Page background: `bg-stone-50`
- Section contrast: `bg-slate-100`
- Dark premium areas: `bg-slate-900 text-white`
- Accent actions: `bg-amber-500 text-slate-950`
- Muted labels: `text-slate-500`
- Main body copy: `text-slate-700`
- Headlines: `text-slate-950`
- Borders: `border-slate-200`

## Typography
- Use a clean sans-serif display with strong hierarchy
- Headlines should feel architectural and confident
- Body copy should be concise and readable
- Keep line lengths moderate for easy scanning

Recommended Tailwind-style scale:
- Hero title: `text-4xl md:text-6xl font-semibold tracking-tight`
- Section title: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-semibold tracking-tight`
- Eyebrow labels: `text-xs uppercase tracking-[0.3em]`
- Body copy: `text-base md:text-lg leading-7`

## Layout
- Use wide, breathable sections with clear separation
- Desktop layouts should feel structured and balanced, not stacked like mobile
- Cards should have generous padding and subtle borders
- Prefer 2-column and 3-column grids on desktop

Recommended Tailwind-style spacing:
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Content wrapper: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 md:px-10`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Grid gap: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Surfaces
- Use soft borders and understated shadows
- Round corners should feel modern but not playful
- Large showcase blocks can use gradient overlays and layered panels

Recommended Tailwind-style surfaces:
- Primary card: `rounded-3xl border border-slate-200 bg-white shadow-sm`
- Dark feature panel: `rounded-3xl border border-white/10 bg-slate-900`
- Highlight chip: `rounded-full border border-amber-400/30 bg-amber-400/10`

## Interaction Style
- Buttons should be clear and confident
- Hover states should be subtle and refined
- Important actions should stand out through contrast, not animation overload

Recommended Tailwind-style actions:
- Primary button: `inline-flex items-center rounded-full bg-amber-500 px-6 py-3 text-sm font-medium text-slate-950`
- Secondary button: `inline-flex items-center rounded-full border border-slate-300 px-6 py-3 text-sm font-medium text-slate-900`

## Imagery
- Prefer machinery close-ups, folded metal sheets, manufacturing precision, and clean workshop environments
- If using stock imagery, choose dramatic but clean industrial visuals with controlled lighting
- Avoid generic office, cartoon, or overly busy factory imagery

## Do
- Keep the interface clear and easy to scan
- Use strong contrast for all text and data
- Emphasize product capability, precision, and reliability
- Make contact and inquiry actions obvious

## Don't
- Do not use neon colors or overly saturated accents
- Do not crowd sections with too much copy
- Do not rely on low-contrast gray text for important content
- Do not make the layout feel consumer-tech or playful
