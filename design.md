# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FDFBF7` (warm ivory) — site-wide page background
- Text primary: `#1C1C1C` (deep charcoal, near-black)
- Text muted: `#7A756A` (warm gray)
- Gold accent: `#C9A96E` — CTAs, dividers, accents
- Gold hover: `#B8944F`
- Light warm: `#F5F0E6` — newsletter blocks, hover states
- Divider: `#E5DFD3` (hairline warm beige)
- Card bg: `#FFFFFF`
- Overlay: `rgba(28, 28, 28, 0.6)`

## Typography
- Headings (h1-h4, product names): Cormorant Garamond (Google Font), serif, elegant
- Product names in UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.15em]` wide letter-spacing
- Body / UI text: Inter (Google Font), clean sans-serif
- Navigation: Inter, uppercase, tracking-wider

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout (py-16 to py-24 on sections)
- Max-width containers: `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- Thin hairline dividers: `border-t border-[#E5DFD3]`
- Subtle shadows for cards: `shadow-sm hover:shadow-md`

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: gold background (`#C9A96E`), white text, rounded-none, px-8 py-4
- Hover: darken gold (`#B8944F`), subtle transition
- Outlined: border gold, text gold, hover fills gold

## Animations
- Subtle fade-in on scroll (`opacity-0 translate-y-4` → `opacity-100 translate-y-0`)
- Product card hover: scale image slightly, show second image
- Smooth color transitions (duration-300)

## Images
- Editorial style: warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- High contrast, moody lighting
