# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Visual Style Guide

## Brand Identity
- **Brand**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial machinery (folding machines, sheet metal equipment)
- **Style**: Elegant, sophisticated industrial with premium feel
- **Tone**: Professional, trustworthy, precise, high-quality

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: `#0f172a` (slate-900) - Deep navy/slate for dark sections
- **Secondary Background**: `#ffffff` (white) - Clean white for content sections
- **Accent/Gold**: `#d4a853` - Warm gold/brass for CTAs and highlights
- **Accent Hover**: `#c49a3c` - Darker gold for hover states
- **Text Dark**: `#1e293b` (slate-800) - For body text on light backgrounds
- **Text Light**: `#f8fafc` (slate-50) - For text on dark backgrounds
- **Text Muted**: `#94a3b8` (slate-400) - For secondary text
- **Border Light**: `#e2e8f0` (slate-200) - Subtle borders
- **Card Background**: `#f8fafc` (slate-50) - Light card surfaces

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Inter', sans-serif (weights 600, 700, 800)
- **Body**: 'Inter', sans-serif (weight 300, 400)
- **Sizes**:
  - Hero Title: text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold
  - Section Headings: text-3xl md:text-4xl font-semibold
  - Card Titles: text-xl font-semibold
  - Body: text-base leading-relaxed
  - Small/Caption: text-sm

## Layout
- Max container width: max-w-7xl mx-auto
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Consistent 24px (gap-6) grid gaps
- Responsive grid: 1 col mobile, 2 col tablet, 3-4 col desktop

## Components

### Navigation
- Fixed/sticky header, transparent on hero, white bg on scroll
- Logo: "ARTITECT MACHINERY" in uppercase, tracking-wider, font-bold
- Links in nav: uppercase, text-sm, tracking-wider

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Gold background (`#d4a853`), dark text, px-8 py-3, rounded-none, uppercase, tracking-wider, font-medium
- **Secondary**: Transparent with gold border, gold text, same sizing
- **Ghost**: No background, white/light text, underline on hover

### Cards
- White or transparent bg
- Subtle shadow or border
- Clean, minimal design
- Product cards: image on top, title, brief description

### Hero
- Full-screen dark background with overlay
- Large headline with gold accent
- Subtle background pattern or texture overlay

### Section Transitions
- Alternating backgrounds (dark section, white section, gray section)
- Subtle fade/slide animations on scroll

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography clean and minimal
- Use gold accents sparingly for emphasis
- Maintain high contrast for readability
- Use professional imagery of machinery

## Don'ts
- Don't use overly bright or neon colors
- Don't overcrowd layouts
- Don't use playful or casual fonts
- Don't use low-quality or pixelated images
- Don't overuse gold - it's an accent only
