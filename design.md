# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design Guidelines

## Visual Style
Elegant, professional, and user-friendly machinery brand. Focuses on high-quality sheet metal folding solutions.

### Colors
- **Primary**: #1a1a1a (Deep Charcoal / Black) - Represents strength and precision.
- **Secondary**: #f5f5f5 (Off-white / Light Gray) - Clean background for modern feel.
- **Accent**: #c5a059 (Soft Gold / Brass) - Represents premium quality and elegance.
- **Text**: #333333 (Dark Gray) for readability.

### Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, Bold (700) - Clean, authoritative.
- **Body**: Inter, Regular (400) - Easy to read.

### UI Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded-md, primary background with white text, or accent background for CTAs.
- **Cards**: Minimalist with subtle shadows and border-radius.
- **Sections**: Generous white space to maintain "Elegant" feel.

### Icons
- Use Lucide React for consistent, thin-stroke professional icons.
