# Fruit Farm Design System

## Brand Identity
A simple, warm, and natural feel. Clean layouts with generous whitespace. Earthy greens and warm oranges evoke freshness and nature.

## Colors
- **Primary Green**: `#4a7c59` — main brand color, used for nav, headings, buttons (`text-green-700`, custom: `farm-green`)
- **Accent Orange**: `#e07b39` — highlights, CTAs, accents (`text-orange-500`, custom: `farm-orange`)
- **Background**: `#faf7f2` — warm off-white page background (`bg-stone-50`)
- **Surface**: `#ffffff` — cards and sections (`bg-white`)
- **Text Primary**: `#1c1c1c` — headings (`text-gray-900`)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6b7280` — body copy, captions (`text-gray-500`)
- **Border**: `#e5e7eb` — dividers, card borders (`border-gray-200`)

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: `font-semibold` or `font-bold`, `text-gray-900`
- **Body**: `font-normal`, `text-gray-600`
- **Nav links**: `text-sm font-medium`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 px-4` or `py-20 px-6`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Components
- **Buttons (primary)**: `bg-green-700 text-white px-6 py-2.5 rounded-full hover:bg-green-800 transition`
- **Buttons (outline)**: `border border-green-700 text-green-700 px-6 py-2.5 rounded-full hover:bg-green-50 transition`
- **Cards**: `bg-white rounded-2xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6`
- **Nav**: `bg-white border-b border-gray-100 sticky top-0 z-50`
- **Section headings**: `text-3xl font-bold text-gray-900`
- **Section subheadings**: `text-gray-500 mt-2 text-base`

## Do's
- Use rounded corners (`rounded-xl`, `rounded-2xl`, `rounded-full`)
- Keep layouts clean with lots of whitespace
- Use green as the dominant brand color
- Use orange sparingly for accents and highlights
- Use simple, readable sans-serif typography

## Don'ts
- Don't use dark backgrounds for main content areas
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors
- Don't use tight spacing — always breathe
- Don't use complex gradients or heavy shadows
