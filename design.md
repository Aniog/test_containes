# Digital Design System: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- **Background**: `#F9F8F6` (Warm Sand) - `bg-[#F9F8F6]`
- **Foreground (Text)**: `#1A1A1A` (Charcoal) - `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#C5A059` (Refined Gold) - `text-[#C5A059]` / `bg-[#C5A059]`
- **Muted**: `#717171` (Stone Grey) - `text-[#717171]`
- **Border**: `#E5E5E5` (Soft Grey) - `border-[#E5E5E5]`
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) - `bg-white`

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. Use for editorial headlines, product names (UPPERCASE).
  - Tailwind: `font-serif tracking-widest uppercase`
- **Body/UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif. Use for product descriptions, navigation, buttons.
  - Tailwind: `font-sans`

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace.
- Thin hairline dividers.
- Max-width containers for editorial feel.

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Solid: `bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white py-3 px-8 transition-all hover:bg-[#333333]`
  - Outline: `border border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A] py-3 px-8 transition-all hover:bg-[#1A1A1A] hover:text-white`
- **Cards**:
  - Minimalist, no shadows or soft subtle ones. `hover:opacity-90 transition-opacity`

## Do's & Don'ts
- **Do**: Use large, high-quality images.
- **Do**: Keep letter spacing wide for product titles.
- **Don't**: Use loud shadows or bright primary colors.
- **Don't**: Overcrowd the interface.
