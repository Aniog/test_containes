# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity

### Mood
- **Quiet luxury, warm, editorial**
- Premium demi-fine jewelry
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- `velmora-cream` - #FAF7F2 (warm cream background)
- `velmora-charcoal` - #1A1A1A (rich dark for text)
- `velmora-gold` - #C9A962 (warm metallic gold accent)
- `velmora-gold-light` - #E8D5A8 (lighter gold for hover states)

**Secondary Colors:**
- `velmora-sand` - #F0EBE3 (soft neutral)
- `velmora-warm-gray` - #6B6560 (muted text)
- `velmora-white` - #FFFFFF (pure white for cards)

**Accent:**
- `velmora-gold` - #C9A962 (buttons, links, highlights)

### Typography

**Headings & Product Names:**
- Font: **Cormorant Garamond** (elegant serif)
- Weight: 500-600
- Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.15em

**Body & UI:**
- Font: **Inter** (clean sans-serif)
- Weight: 400-500
- Line height: 1.6 for body text

### Spacing System
- Base unit: 4px
- Section padding: 80px (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Card padding: 24px
- Generous whitespace throughout

### Visual Elements
- Thin hairline dividers: 1px solid #E8E5E0
- Subtle shadows: 0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)
- Border radius: 0px (sharp, editorial) or 2px (subtle)
- Buttons: solid or outlined, no rounded corners

### Animations
- Hover transitions: 300ms ease
- Fade-in on scroll: subtle opacity transitions
- Image hover: smooth scale (1.02) and crossfade

## Component Guidelines

### Buttons
- Primary: solid gold background, dark text
- Secondary: outlined with gold border
- Hover: slight darken or fill change

### Cards
- White background
- Subtle shadow on hover
- Sharp or minimal border radius

### Dividers
- Thin, light color
- Used between sections and content blocks

### Images
- Editorial style
- Warm lighting
- Gold jewelry on neutral/dark backgrounds
