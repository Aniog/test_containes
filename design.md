# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Visual Style
Elegant industrial aesthetic - professional, trustworthy, and modern. Clean lines with subtle metallic accents.

## Typography
- Primary font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-weight 700-800, tight letter-spacing
- Body: font-weight 400-500, relaxed line-height
- Example: `font-heading: font-bold tracking-tight`, `font-body: font-normal leading-relaxed`

## Colors
- Primary: Deep navy blue `#0f172a` (slate-900) - headers, navigation, footer
- Secondary: Steel blue `#1e40af` (blue-800) - accents, links, CTAs
- Accent: Warm amber `#d97706` (amber-600) - highlights, badges, special elements
- Background: Clean white `#ffffff` and light gray `#f8fafc` (slate-50)
- Text: Dark slate `#1e293b` (slate-800) for body, `#0f172a` for headings
- Muted: `#64748b` (slate-500) for secondary text
- Border: `#e2e8f0` (slate-200) for subtle dividers

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card gap: `gap-6 md:gap-8`
- Element spacing: `space-y-4` to `space-y-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-xl border border-slate-200 shadow-sm`
- Hover: `hover:shadow-md transition-shadow duration-300`
- Buttons: `rounded-lg` with subtle hover states
- No heavy shadows - keep it clean and modern

## Layout
- Desktop: Multi-column grids, side-by-side layouts
- Mobile: Single column stacking with `md:` breakpoints
- Hero: Full-width with centered content
- Products: 3-column grid on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 on mobile

## Do's
- Use plenty of white space
- Keep text readable with good contrast
- Use subtle gradients for depth
- Include clear CTAs
- Maintain consistent spacing rhythm

## Don'ts
- No cluttered layouts
- No harsh color combinations
- No tiny text on mobile
- No overwhelming animations
- No dark backgrounds for body text
