# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- **Primary/Base:** Deep Ebony (`#1A1A1A`) - Used for footer, deep backgrounds, and high-contrast text.
- **Background/Surface:** Soft Alabaster (`#F9F7F2`) - Main background color for a warm, editorial feel.
- **Accent/Action:** Radiant Gold (`#D4AF37`) - Used for primary buttons, highlights, and secondary accents.
- **Neutral/Muted:** Oyster Gray (`#E5E1D9`) - Used for dividers, muted backgrounds, and secondary UI elements.
- **Text:** 
  - Main: `#2D2D2D` (off-black)
  - Light: `#FFFFFF`
  - Muted: `#707070`

## Typography
- **Headings (Logo, Product Names, Section Titles):** Serif - `Cormorant Garamond`, `Playfair Display`.
  - Product Names: UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.2em]`.
- **Body & UI (Descriptions, Buttons, Labels):** Sans-serif - `Inter`, `Manrope`.

## Components & Styles
- **Buttons:** 
  - Primary: Radiant Gold background, White text, no border.
  - Secondary: Transparent background, Radiant Gold border and text.
  - Text: Uppercase, tracking-widest, medium weight.
- **Cards:** Clean whitespace, thin hairline dividers (`border-[#E5E1D9]`), subtle hover shadows.
- **Imagery:** Large, editorial, warm lighting. Close-ups of gold jewelry.
- **Dividers:** 1px hairline in Oyster Gray.
- **Transitions:** Soft fade-in, subtle hover scale/opacity.

## Do's & Don'ts
- **DO:** Use generous whitespace.
- **DO:** Keep layout airy and focused.
- **DO:** Use serif for emotional headings.
- **DON'T:** Use bright, saturated colors (other than the gold accent).
- **DON'T:** Use rounded corners everywhere; keep it modern and sharp but softened by materials.
- **DON'T:** Add complex shadows or gradients.
