# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Gold: #D4AF37 (`velmora-gold`)
  - Gold Light: #F4E1A6 (`velmora-gold-light`)
  - Brandy (Warm Accent): #8E5A31 (`velmora-brandy`)
  - Sand (Neutral Base): #F9F7F2 (`velmora-sand`)
  - Charcoal (Deep Text/UI): #1A1A1A (`velmora-charcoal`)
  - Muted Gold: #C5A059 (`velmora-muted-gold`)
- **Typography**:
  - Headings & Product Names: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif)
  - Body & UI: `Inter` (Sans-serif)
  - Style: Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest-extra`).

## UI Elements
- **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers (`hairline-divider`).
- **Dividers**: Low opacity charcoal or subtle gold.
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal or Brandy with white text.
  - Secondary: Outlined Charcoal or Brandy.
  - Accent: Velmora Gold.
- **Transitions**: Smooth, subtle fades and transforms.
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace for editorial feel.

## Component Specifics
- **Product Cards**: Minimalist, hover reveals alternate view.
- **Imagery**: Large, high-quality editorial shots with warm lighting.
- **Navigation**: Sticky, transparent-to-solid on scroll.
