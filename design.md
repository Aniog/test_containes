# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry:** Industrial sheet metal folding machines
- **Tone:** Elegant, professional, trustworthy, user-friendly

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Deep Navy):** `#1a2332` — used for headers, nav, hero backgrounds
- **Primary Light:** `#2a3a4e` — used for hover states, secondary backgrounds
- **Accent (Copper/Gold):** `#c8956c` — used for CTAs, highlights, accents
- **Accent Hover:** `#b07d56` — darker accent for hover
- **Surface White:** `#ffffff` — card backgrounds, content areas
- **Surface Light:** `#f7f8fa` — alternating section backgrounds
- **Text Dark:** `#1a2332` — primary text on light backgrounds
- **Text Medium:** `#4a5568` — secondary/body text
- **Text Light:** `#6b7280` — muted text, captions
- **Border:** `#e2e8f0` — subtle borders and dividers

## Typography
- **Headings:** `font-family: 'Playfair Display', serif;` — elegant, refined
- **Body:** `font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif;` — clean, readable
- **Heading sizes:** Use Tailwind `text-5xl` (hero), `text-4xl` (section), `text-2xl` (card), `text-xl` (sub)
- **Body sizes:** `text-base` (default), `text-lg` (lead), `text-sm` (caption)
- **Font weights:** Headings `font-bold` or `font-semibold`, body `font-normal` or `font-medium`

## Spacing
- **Section padding:** `py-20 md:py-28` (vertical), `px-6 md:px-8` (horizontal)
- **Container max-width:** `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- **Card padding:** `p-6 md:p-8`
- **Gap between elements:** `gap-6` (cards), `gap-4` (inline), `gap-2` (tight)

## Borders & Shadows
- **Cards:** `rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-[#e2e8f0]` or `rounded-2xl shadow-lg`
- **Buttons:** `rounded-lg` or `rounded-full`
- **Images:** `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`
- **Hover shadows:** `hover:shadow-xl transition-shadow duration-300`

## Buttons
- **Primary:** `bg-[#c8956c] text-white hover:bg-[#b07d56] rounded-lg px-8 py-3 font-medium`
- **Secondary:** `border-2 border-[#c8956c] text-[#c8956c] hover:bg-[#c8956c] hover:text-white rounded-lg px-8 py-3 font-medium`
- **Ghost:** `text-[#c8956c] hover:underline font-medium`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use copper/gold accent sparingly for emphasis
- Maintain consistent card heights in grids
- Use smooth transitions on hover states

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or neon colors
- Don't crowd content — maintain breathing room
- Don't use more than 2 font families
- Don't use harsh borders — keep them subtle
- Don't use pure black (#000) for text — use deep navy instead
