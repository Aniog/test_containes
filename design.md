# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Tagline:** Precision Engineered. Perfectly Formed.
- **Industry:** Sheet metal folding & forming machinery

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- `brand-navy`: `#0D1B2A` — Deep navy, primary background for dark sections
- `brand-steel`: `#1C3A5E` — Steel blue, secondary dark surface
- `brand-gold`: `#C9A84C` — Warm gold, primary accent / CTA
- `brand-gold-light`: `#E8C96A` — Light gold, hover states

### Neutral Colors
- `brand-white`: `#FFFFFF`
- `brand-off-white`: `#F5F6F8` — Light page background
- `brand-light-gray`: `#E8EAED` — Borders, dividers
- `brand-mid-gray`: `#8A9BB0` — Muted text, captions
- `brand-dark-gray`: `#2E3A4A` — Body text on light backgrounds

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Headings:** `Playfair Display` — elegant serif for brand authority
- **Body / UI:** `Inter` — clean, readable sans-serif

### Scale
- `text-xs`: 0.75rem — captions, labels
- `text-sm`: 0.875rem — secondary text
- `text-base`: 1rem — body
- `text-lg`: 1.125rem — lead text
- `text-xl`: 1.25rem — card titles
- `text-2xl`: 1.5rem — section subtitles
- `text-3xl`: 1.875rem — section titles (mobile)
- `text-4xl`: 2.25rem — section titles (desktop)
- `text-5xl`: 3rem — hero subtitle
- `text-6xl`: 3.75rem — hero headline

## Spacing
- Section vertical padding: `py-20` (desktop), `py-14` (mobile)
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6`
- Card padding: `p-8`
- Gap between cards: `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-brand-light-gray`
- Card shadow: `shadow-lg`
- Hover shadow: `shadow-xl`
- Border radius: `rounded-2xl` for cards, `rounded-full` for pills/badges

## Buttons
- **Primary CTA:** `bg-brand-gold text-brand-navy font-semibold px-8 py-3 rounded-full hover:bg-brand-gold-light transition-all`
- **Secondary / Outline:** `border-2 border-brand-gold text-brand-gold px-8 py-3 rounded-full hover:bg-brand-gold hover:text-brand-navy transition-all`
- **Ghost (dark bg):** `text-white border border-white/30 px-6 py-2 rounded-full hover:border-brand-gold hover:text-brand-gold transition-all`

## Navigation
- Sticky top nav with `bg-brand-navy/95 backdrop-blur`
- Logo: Playfair Display, gold accent on "ARTITECT"
- Nav links: Inter, text-sm, uppercase tracking-widest
- Active/hover: gold underline

## Cards
- White background on light sections
- Dark (`bg-brand-steel`) on dark sections
- Gold top-border accent on feature cards: `border-t-4 border-brand-gold`

## Do's
- Use Playfair Display for all headings
- Use gold accents sparingly for maximum impact
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use dark navy sections to break up the page rhythm
- Keep CTAs prominent with gold color

## Don'ts
- Don't use pure black backgrounds
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors per section
- Don't use light text on light backgrounds
- Don't crowd elements — spacing is part of the elegance
