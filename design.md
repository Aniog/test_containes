# Design Preferences - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial.
- **Colors**:
  - `background`: `#ffffff` (White - Editorial Clean)
  - `foreground`: `#0a0a0a` (Deep Charcoal)
  - `primary`: `#1a1a1a` (Rich Black)
  - `accent`: `#8b7355` (Warm Metallic Gold tint - `hsl(38 45% 45%)`)
  - `muted`: `#f5f5f0` (Soft Bone/Parchment)
- **Typography**:
  - **Serif**: `Cormorant Garamond` (Hero, Headings, Product Names)
  - **Sans-serif**: `Inter` (Body, UI, Metadata)
- **Buttons**:
  - `solid`: Black background, white text, no radius (Sharp edges).
  - `outline`: Thin 1px black border, white/transparent background.
- **Components**:
  - `Hairline Dividers`: `border-border` (Minimal, 1px thin).
  - `Spacing`: Generous whitespace for luxury airy feel.
  - `Shadows`: Very soft, almost invisible.

## UI Elements
- **Navigation**: Transparent backdrop-blur, transitioning to solid on scroll.
- **Cards**: Borderless or hairline split, hover reveals secondary image.
- **Inputs**: Minimal, underlined or thin border, Inter typography.
