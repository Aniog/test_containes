# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Playfair Display', serif. Elegant, authoritative, and professional.
- **Body**: 'Inter', sans-serif. Clean, modern, and highly readable.

## Colors
- **Primary**: Slate 900 (`#0f172a`). Represents strength, reliability, and machinery.
- **Secondary**: Amber 600 (`#d97706`). Used for accents, representing heat, sparks, and precision.
- **Background**: Slate 50 (`#f8fafc`). Clean and professional.
- **Surface**: White (`#ffffff`). For cards and modals.
- **Text**: Slate 900 for headings, Slate 600 for body text.

## UI Style
- **Borders**: Subtle thin borders (border-slate-200).
- **Shadows**: Soft, professional shadows (shadow-sm, shadow-md).
- **Spacing**: Generous padding and margins for a clean, user-friendly feel.
- **Components**: Rounded corners (rounded-lg) for a modern yet approachable look.

## Do's
- Use high-contrast headers.
- Maintain plenty of whitespace.
- Use elegant transitions for hover states.

## Don'ts
- Don't use neon or overly bright colors (except for specific amber accents).
- Avoid cluttered layouts.
- Avoid low-contrast text.
