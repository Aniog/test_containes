# ARTiTECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand Identity
- Brand name: ARTITECT MACHINERY - written in all caps, with a refined industrial feel
- Tone: Elegant, professional, trustworthy, user-friendly

## Color Palette
- Primary: Deep navy blue (#1B2A4A) - conveys trust, professionalism, industrial strength
- Primary light: (#2C4A7C)
- Accent: Warm gold/brass (#C9A84C) - premium quality, elegance
- Accent light: (#E8D5A3)
- Background: Off-white (#F7F8FA)
- Surface: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text: Dark charcoal (#1A1A2E)
- Text muted: (#6B7280)
- Border: Light grey (#E5E7EB)

## Typography
- Headings: Georgia/serif font family for elegance
- Body: Inter, system-ui sans-serif
- Heading sizes: text-4xl (hero), text-3xl (section titles), text-2xl (subsections)
- Body: text-base, leading-relaxed

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-20 lg:py-24
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl with subtle shadow (shadow-sm hover:shadow-md)
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Inputs: rounded-lg border

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#1B2A4A] text-white hover:bg-[#2C4A7C] transition
- Secondary: border border-[#1B2A4A] text-[#1B2A4A] hover:bg-[#1B2A4A] hover:text-white
- Accent: bg-[#C9A84C] text-white hover:bg-[#B8983B]

## Navigation
- Fixed top header with transparent to solid background on scroll
- Logo on left, nav links on right
- Smooth scroll to sections

## Section Styles
- Hero: Full width, elegant dark background with subtle overlay, centered content
- Products: Grid layout with card-style product displays
- About: Clean, text-focused with subtle decorative elements
- Contact: Clean form with elegant styling

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable with good contrast
- Use the gold accent sparingly for emphasis
- Maintain consistent spacing throughout
- Use smooth transitions and animations

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections
- Don't use overly technical jargon in headings
- Don't use aggressive marketing language
