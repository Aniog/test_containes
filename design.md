# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand Direction
Velmora Fine Jewelry should feel like quiet luxury: editorial, warm, refined, and premium without looking inaccessible. The store should flatter gold jewelry with rich contrast, soft neutrals, and restrained metallic accents.

## Palette
Use Tailwind's built-in stone, neutral, and amber families for a confident single-direction palette.

- Base canvas: `bg-stone-100`, `bg-stone-50`
- Deep luxury surface: `bg-stone-950`, `bg-neutral-900`
- Premium card surface: `bg-stone-50`, `bg-white/80`
- Primary text on light: `text-stone-900`
- Secondary text on light: `text-stone-600`, `text-stone-500`
- Primary text on dark: `text-stone-100`
- Secondary text on dark: `text-stone-300`
- Accent / metallic cue: `bg-amber-400`, `text-amber-400`, `border-amber-400/60`
- Hairline dividers: `border-stone-200`, `border-stone-800`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Google font `Cormorant Garamond`
- Body, navigation, labels, UI: Google font `Inter`
- Product names should be uppercase with wide built-in tracking such as `tracking-widest`
- Headings should be elegant, roomy, and lightly weighted

## Spacing & Layout
- Favor generous whitespace: `py-16`, `py-20`, `lg:py-24`
- Keep content centered with `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Use air between content blocks with `gap-6`, `gap-8`, `gap-10`

## Components
- Buttons should feel premium: rounded-full, restrained, and tactile
- Use subtle hover motion such as `hover:-translate-y-0.5`, `transition-all`, `duration-300`
- Product cards should use soft shadows like `shadow-xl shadow-stone-900/5`
- Use thin dividers instead of heavy borders

## Imagery
- Use warm-lit editorial jewelry photography
- Favor close-up details, ear styling, neck styling, velvet trays, and neutral stone surfaces
- Avoid discount-store imagery, neon tones, or cluttered scenes

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text always readable
- Let the hero image and whitespace create the luxury feeling
- Use accent color sparingly
- Keep product cards minimal and typography polished

## Don't
- Do not use loud sale colors or generic marketplace styling
- Do not use dense layouts or cramped mobile spacing
- Do not rely on low-contrast muted text for key content
