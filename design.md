# Design System - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial.
- **Vibe**: Premium demi-fine jewelry, high-end, sophisticated.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Base)**: `#1C1C1C` (Deep Onyx) - Used for text and dark backgrounds.
- **Secondary (Neutral)**: `#F9F7F2` (Warm Parchment) - Used for page backgrounds.
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#C5A059` (Soft Gold) - Used for buttons, hover states, and call-to-actions.
- **Contrast**: High contrast for readability, subtle dividers (`#E5E5E5`).

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif)
  - Style: Elegant, high-contrast serif.
  - Usage: H1-H4, Product Names (UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.2em]`).
- **Body & UI**: `Inter` / `Manrope` (Sans-serif)
  - Style: Clean, readable.
  - Usage: Paragraphs, UI labels, small metadata.

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - `primary`: Solid `#1C1C1C`, white text.
  - `outline`: Border `#1C1C1C`, transparent background.
  - `ghost`: No background, underlined on hover.
- **Cards**: Minimal, generous whitespace, soft shadows on hover.
- **Dividers**: Thin hairlines (`h-[1px] bg-neutral-200`).

## Icons
- **Library**: `Lucide React`
- **Style**: Thin weights, minimal.

## Mobile First
- All layouts are mobile-responsive with appropriate padding and font scaling.
- Navigation becomes compact on mobile.
