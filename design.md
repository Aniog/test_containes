# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: #FAF8F5 (warm ivory/cream)
- Foreground/text: #1A1A1A (near-black)
- Muted background: #F0EDE8 (warm stone)
- Muted foreground: #6B6560 (warm gray)
- Primary/accent: #B8860B (dark goldenrod — warm gold)
- Primary foreground: #FFFFFF
- Secondary/accent2: #8B7355 (warm bronze)
- Border: #E3DDD5 (warm taupe)
- Card background: #FFFFFF
- Card foreground: #1A1A1A
- Ring/focus: #B8860B
- Destructive: #9B2C2C
- Success: #2D6A4F

## Typography
- Headings & product names: "Cormorant Garamond", serif (Google Fonts)
- Body & UI: "Inter", sans-serif
- Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.08em
- Nav links: Inter, uppercase, tracking-wider, text-sm

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px, centered
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E3DDD5] (1px)

## Visual Style
- Buttons: solid accent (#B8860B) with white text, rounded-none, px-8 py-3, uppercase tracking-wider text-sm font-medium
- Outline buttons: border border-[#B8860B] text-[#B8860B] bg-transparent
- Hover transitions: 300ms ease, subtle scale or opacity
- Soft shadows for cards: shadow-sm hover:shadow-md
- Images: full-bleed where possible, editorial quality
- Dividers: thin 1px hairline in border color
- Links: underline on hover, transition-all

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use warm gold accent sparingly
- Keep text readable with high contrast
- Use serif for elegance, sans-serif for clarity
- Thin, refined borders and dividers

## Don'ts
- No bright reds, blues, or neon
- No heavy shadows or gradients
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount-store aesthetics
- No overuse of accent color
