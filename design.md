# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand
- **Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Tagline**: Precision Metal Folding Solutions
- **Industry**: Industrial Machinery / Sheet Metal Equipment

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a3a5c` (Deep Navy) - Trust, professionalism, industrial strength
- **Secondary**: `#c9a84c` (Antique Gold) - Elegance, premium quality
- **Accent**: `#e63946` (Crimson Red) - Call-to-action highlights
- **Background Light**: `#f8f9fa` (Soft Gray-White) - Clean, modern
- **Background Dark**: `#0f1f2e` (Dark Navy) - Sections that need contrast
- **Text Primary**: `#1a1a1a` (Near Black)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6c757d` (Medium Gray)
- **Text on Dark**: `#ffffff` (White)

## Typography
- **Headings**: `font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif; font-weight: 700-800;`
- **Body**: `font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif; font-weight: 400;`
- **H1**: `text-5xl md:text-6xl font-extrabold tracking-tight`
- **H2**: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- **H3**: `text-xl md:text-2xl font-semibold`
- **Body**: `text-base leading-relaxed`

## Spacing
- **Section Padding**: `py-20 md:py-28 px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- **Container**: `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- **Card Padding**: `p-6 md:p-8`
- **Gap between elements**: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**: `bg-[#c9a84c] text-white font-semibold px-8 py-3 rounded hover:bg-[#b8983e] transition-colors`
- **Secondary/Outline**: `border-2 border-[#1a3a5c] text-[#1a3a5c] font-semibold px-8 py-3 rounded hover:bg-[#1a3a5c] hover:text-white transition-colors`
- **Ghost on Dark**: `border border-white/30 text-white px-6 py-3 rounded hover:bg-white/10 transition-colors`

### Cards
- **Product Card**: `bg-white rounded-lg shadow-md hover:shadow-xl transition-shadow border border-gray-100 overflow-hidden`
- **Feature Card**: `bg-white/5 backdrop-blur-sm rounded-lg border border-white/10 p-6`

### Navigation
- **Navbar**: `fixed top-0 w-full bg-white/95 backdrop-blur-md shadow-sm z-50`
- **Nav Links**: `text-[#1a1a1a] hover:text-[#c9a84c] transition-colors font-medium`
- **Logo**: Bold uppercase with gold accent

## Layout Principles
- Full-width sections with centered content containers
- Alternating light/dark sections for visual rhythm
- Generous whitespace for elegance
- Responsive grid layouts (1 col mobile, 2-3 col desktop)

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO use the gold accent sparingly for highlights and CTAs
- DO maintain high contrast text on all backgrounds
- DON'T use more than 3 colors in any component
- DON'T crowd elements - keep breathing room
- DO use subtle shadows instead of heavy borders
- DON'T use pure black (`#000000`) - use `#1a1a1a` instead
