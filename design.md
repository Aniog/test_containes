# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Vibe
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry for women 25-45.
Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Typography
- Headings & product names: `Playfair Display` (serif) — elegant, classic, refined
- Body & UI: `Inter` (sans-serif) — clean, legible, modern
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-[0.15em])
- Section labels: 10px uppercase, wide tracking, sans-serif

## Color Palette
- Background: #FAF7F2 (warm cream/off-white) — the main page bg
- Text: warm-900 (#3D352D) for body, warm-950 (#1A1614) for headings
- Accent / CTAs: gold-600 (#A8833A) — warm gold, not brassy
- Light gold accents: gold-400 (#D4AB4E), gold-500 (#C9A84C)
- Borders/separators: warm-200 (#E0D9CD) — thin hairline dividers
- Cards/surfaces: white (#FFFFFF) or cream-50 (#FDFCF9)
- Dark footer: warm-950 (#1A1614) with cream-200 text
- Hero overlay: warm-950/80 gradient

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 (mobile), py-24 (desktop)
- Container: max-w-7xl with px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Grid gaps: gap-4 md:gap-6

## Visual Style
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Large editorial imagery (aspect-[4/5] for products, 16x9 for hero)
- Thin hairline dividers (border-warm-200, h-px bg-warm-200)
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300, duration-500, duration-700)
- Soft shadows only on sticky nav (shadow-sm)
- Buttons: uppercase, tracking-wider, solid gold-600 or outlined with warm border
- Product cards: clean, no border, no shadow until hover
- Quick-add overlay slides up from bottom on hover

## Do's
- Use warm cream backgrounds (#FAF7F2), white for cards
- Use gold-600 (#A8833A) for primary CTAs and accent elements
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Uppercase with wide tracking for product names
- Thin hairline borders for separation
- Large, editorial product images
- Clean, minimal layouts with plenty of whitespace

## Don'ts
- No harsh colors (no pure black, no bright red)
- No heavy shadows or deep borders
- No discount-looking elements (no sale tags, no % off badges)
- No generic stock photography — use warm-lit editorial jewelry images
- No crowded layouts
- No rounded corners (radius: 0rem)
