# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Generous whitespace, large editorial imagery, thin hairline dividers.

## Colors
- **Background**: `#FDFCFB` (Off-white/Ecru) - `bg-[#FDFCFB]`
- **Foreground (Text)**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep charcoal) - `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Accent (Primary)**: `#8C7351` (Warm Bronze/Antique Gold) - `bg-[#8C7351]`, `text-[#8C7351]`
- **Muted**: `#71717A` (Stone grey) - `text-[#71717A]`
- **Border**: `#E2E2E2` (Light stone) - `border-[#E2E2E2]`

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - Product names: `uppercase tracking-[0.2em] font-medium`
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - Body: `leading-relaxed font-light`

## Icons
- Lucide React (Thin stroke-width: 1.5)

## Spacing & Borders
- Generous padding: `p-8`, `p-12`, `p-24`
- Thin borders: `border-[0.5px]`
- Soft shadows: `shadow-sm` (barely visible)

## Buttons
- Solid Accent: `bg-[#8C7351] text-white hover:bg-[#7A6446] transition-colors duration-300 py-4 px-8 uppercase tracking-widest text-xs`
- Outlined: `border border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A] hover:bg-[#1A1A1A] hover:text-white transition-all duration-300 py-4 px-8 uppercase tracking-widest text-xs`
