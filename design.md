# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#faf8f5` (warm cream)
- Surface: `#ffffff` (pure white)
- Primary text: `#1a1a1a` (rich charcoal)
- Secondary text: `#6b6560` (warm taupe)
- Accent gold: `#b8956a` (muted warm gold)
- Accent gold hover: `#a07d54` (deeper gold)
- Border/hairline: `#e8e2db` (soft warm gray)
- Dark section bg: `#1a1a1a` (charcoal)
- Dark text on dark bg: `#faf8f5` (cream)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6

## Components
- Buttons: Solid gold or outlined gold, rounded-none or rounded-sm
- Cards: Minimal borders, soft shadows on hover
- Dividers: Thin hairline (border-t border-[#e8e2db])
- Accordions: Clean, minimal with subtle borders

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300 ease-out)
- Soft fade-ins on scroll
- Smooth image zoom on hover

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use warm, muted tones
- Ensure strong contrast for accessibility
- Product names in UPPERCASE with tracking-widest

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No discount badges or loud promotional elements
- No heavy shadows or gradients
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No cluttered layouts
