# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand direction
A premium industrial website with a calm, elegant presence and a user-friendly structure. The tone should feel precise, confident, modern, and easy to scan for busy buyers.

## Visual language
- Use refined neutral backgrounds with deep charcoal surfaces and warm metallic accents.
- Pair strong typography with generous whitespace.
- Keep layouts clean, balanced, and professional rather than flashy.
- Prioritize readability and trust.

## Color system
- Page background: soft stone / cool white tones such as `bg-stone-50` and `bg-white`
- Primary dark surface: `bg-slate-950`
- Secondary dark surface: `bg-slate-900`
- Accent metallic tone: `amber-600`, `amber-500`, `amber-100`
- Supporting border tones: `border-stone-200`, `border-white/10`, `border-amber-200`
- Primary text on light surfaces: `text-slate-950`
- Secondary text on light surfaces: `text-slate-600`
- Primary text on dark surfaces: `text-white`
- Secondary text on dark surfaces: `text-slate-300`

## Typography
- Use Inter for all text.
- Hero heading: bold, compact, premium feeling, e.g. `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-semibold tracking-tight`
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-semibold tracking-tight`
- Eyebrow text: uppercase with letter spacing, e.g. `text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-[0.3em]`
- Body text: `text-base md:text-lg leading-7`

## Layout and spacing
- Use centered containers such as `mx-auto max-w-7xl px-6 lg:px-8`.
- Major sections should use spacious vertical rhythm such as `py-20 md:py-28`.
- Cards should use rounded corners `rounded-3xl` with subtle borders and soft shadows.
- Prefer multi-column layouts on desktop and stacked layouts on mobile.

## Components
- Buttons should feel substantial and easy to click. Prefer rounded full buttons with clear contrast.
- Product cards should include a title, short explanation, and bullet-style highlights.
- Statistic or capability blocks should use dark surfaces with high-contrast text.
- CTA sections should feel premium and simple, with one primary action and one secondary action.

## Imagery
- Use industrial machinery and sheet metal fabrication visuals sparingly and purposefully.
- Hero and product visuals should suggest precision manufacturing, sheet metal folding, and factory-grade engineering.
- Use the Strikingly image tagging system instead of hardcoded URLs.

## Do
- Use explicit text colors on every major surface.
- Maintain a strong hierarchy and lots of breathing room.
- Keep copy straightforward and buyer-friendly.
- Use elegant accent color moments instead of large bright color fields.

## Don’t
- Don’t use neon colors or overly playful shapes.
- Don’t overcrowd sections with too much text.
- Don’t rely on low-contrast muted text for important information.
- Don’t collapse desktop layouts into overly narrow single-column compositions.
