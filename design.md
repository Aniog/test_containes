# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Base (background): `cream` #FBF7F4 — warm off-white
- Secondary BG: `sand` #F0EAE4 — warm light beige
- Accent (gold): `gold` #C8A96E — warm muted gold
- Accent hover: `gold-dark` #B8954A
- Primary text: `espresso` #2C2420 — deep warm brown-black
- Secondary text: `charcoal` #3D3632
- Muted/accent text: `blush` #D4A99A — dusty rose
- Light accent BG: `blush-light` #F5EBE8
- Subdued text: `stone` #8A817C
- Light border: `stone-light` #B8B0AA

## Typography
- Headings, product names, logo: Playfair Display (serif)
- Body, UI, buttons, nav: Inter (sans-serif)
- Product names: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest 0.25em)
- Headings: font-medium, leading-tight

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace (py-24, py-32 sections)
- Thin hairline dividers (h-px bg-velmora-sand)
- Max container width: max-w-7xl, centered

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold (btn-primary) or outlined gold (btn-outline)
- Product cards: subtle hover shadow, second image on hover, quick add-to-cart
- Nav: transparent over hero, solid cream bg on scroll
- Cart: slide-out drawer from right

## Do's
- Use velmora color tokens consistently
- Keep text high contrast against backgrounds
- Use serif for brand moments, sans for functional UI

## Don'ts
- No pure black (#000) or harsh whites
- No bright/neon colors
- No rounded-xl or overly playful border radii (use rounded-sm or none)
- No boxy hard shadows (use subtle shadow-md at most)
