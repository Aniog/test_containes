# SSourcing China Design System

## Brand direction
- Clean, trustworthy, international B2B presentation
- Practical tone focused on reliability, process clarity, and risk reduction
- Use restrained visual hierarchy rather than flashy marketing patterns

## Color system
- Primary surfaces: `bg-white`, `bg-slate-50`, `bg-slate-100`
- Primary text: `text-slate-950`, `text-slate-800`, `text-slate-600`
- Brand accents and CTAs: `bg-blue-700`, `bg-blue-800`, `text-blue-700`, `border-blue-200`
- Trust / success accents: `bg-emerald-50`, `text-emerald-700`, `border-emerald-200`
- Warning / problem accents: `bg-amber-50`, `text-amber-700`, `border-amber-200`

## Typography
- Font family: Inter
- Hero title: `text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl font-semibold tracking-tight`
- Section title: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-semibold tracking-tight`
- Card title: `text-xl font-semibold`
- Body copy: `text-base md:text-lg leading-7 text-slate-600`
- Labels and eyebrow text: `text-sm font-semibold uppercase tracking-[0.2em]`

## Layout and spacing
- Use centered containers: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Section spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Card spacing: `rounded-2xl p-6 md:p-8`
- Keep generous whitespace and clear column separation on desktop

## Components
- Buttons should be high-contrast with clear hover states
- Cards should use `rounded-2xl border border-slate-200 bg-white shadow-sm`
- Dark sections should explicitly set foreground colors, for example `bg-slate-950 text-white`
- Use badges for trust signals and process labels
- Forms should feel operational and practical, not decorative

## Imagery
- Use realistic visuals related to supplier meetings, factory production, quality checks, packaging, and container shipping
- Prefer images that feel documentary and operational rather than generic lifestyle imagery
- Mix wide logistics/factory scenes with close-up inspection details

## Do
- Keep all text highly readable against every surface
- Emphasize process transparency, verification, and execution support
- Use structured content blocks for B2B scanning
- Support both desktop multi-column layouts and mobile clarity

## Don't
- Do not use exaggerated claims or hype-heavy language
- Do not use neon colors, glassmorphism, or overly playful styling
- Do not use low-contrast text on cards, forms, or image overlays
- Do not overcrowd sections with too many decorative elements
