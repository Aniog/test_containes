# Design System

## Visual Style
Warm, artisanal, and creative. Inspired by the craft of writing and drawing.

## Colors
- Background: warm cream/off-white `bg-amber-50`
- Primary accent: deep graphite/charcoal `text-gray-900`
- Secondary accent: golden yellow `text-yellow-500`, `bg-yellow-400`
- Muted text: `text-gray-500`
- Highlight: `bg-yellow-100`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (sans-serif)
- Hero heading: `text-6xl lg:text-8xl font-black tracking-tight`
- Subheading: `text-xl lg:text-2xl font-medium`
- Body: `text-base font-normal`
- Labels/caps: `text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-widest`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-6 lg:px-16`
- Card padding: `p-6 lg:p-8`
- Gap between elements: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Subtle card border: `border border-amber-200`
- Soft shadow: `shadow-md`
- Rounded corners: `rounded-2xl` for cards, `rounded-full` for pills/badges

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-gray-900 text-white px-8 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold hover:bg-gray-700 transition`
- Secondary: `border-2 border-gray-900 text-gray-900 px-8 py-3 rounded-full font-semibold hover:bg-gray-900 hover:text-white transition`

## Do's
- Use warm, earthy tones to evoke creativity and craftsmanship
- Use large, bold typography for impact
- Use generous whitespace
- Use yellow accents sparingly for emphasis

## Don'ts
- Don't use cold blue/gray palettes
- Don't use small, cramped layouts
- Don't use low-contrast text on light backgrounds
