# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Mood & Tonality
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- **Background**: Ivory/Cream (`hsl(30, 20%, 98%)`)
- **Foreground/Text**: Charcoal (`hsl(20, 14%, 18%)`)
- **Primary/Accent**: Warm Gold (`hsl(40, 45%, 56%)`)
- **Secondary**: Soft Beige (`hsl(30, 15%, 94%)`)
- **Muted**: Soft Grey-Beige (`hsl(30, 15%, 92%)`)
- **Border**: Pale Beige (`hsl(30, 15%, 85%)`)

## Typography
- **Serif (Headings & Names)**: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif.
- **Sans-serif (UI & Body)**: 'Inter', sans-serif.
- **Product Names**: Uppercase, wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.25em]`).

## Design Elements
- **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers (`h-[1px]`), `border-border`.
- **Radii**: Sharp corners (`rounded-none`).
- **Transitions**: Smooth, editorial flow (`duration-500 ease-[cubic-bezier(0.23,1,0.32,1)]`).
- **Buttons**:
  - Solid: `bg-primary text-primary-foreground hover:bg-primary/90 rounded-none h-12 px-8 uppercase tracking-widest text-xs transition-colors`
  - Outline: `border border-foreground bg-transparent hover:bg-foreground hover:text-background rounded-none h-12 px-8 uppercase tracking-widest text-xs transition-colors`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace.
- Restrain the accent color.
- Use large, warm-lit editorial imagery.
- Keep UI elements clean and minimal.

## Don'ts
- Use rounded corners (keep it sharp/upscale).
- Use loud, vibrant colors.
- Crowded layouts.
- Heavy shadows (use soft, subtle ones).
