# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Color Palette**:
  - `background`: Ivory / Alabaster (#FAFAF9) - Tailwind: `bg-[#FAFAF9]`
  - `foreground`: Deep Charcoal (#1C1917) - Tailwind: `text-[#1C1917]`
  - `accent`: Soft Gold (#D4AF37) - Tailwind: `bg-[#D4AF37]` or `text-[#D4AF37]`
  - `muted`: Stone 400 (#A8A29E)
  - `border`: Stone 200 (#E7E5E4)
- **Typography**:
  - **Headings & Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif.
    - Product names: UPPERCASE, `tracking-[0.2em]`.
  - **Body & UI**: `Manrope`, sans-serif.
- **Spacing & Layout**:
  - Generous whitespace.
  - Large editorial imagery.
  - Thin hairline dividers (1px).
  - Max-width containers for editorial feel.

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal or Solid Gold, uppercase, tracking widest.
  - Secondary: Outlined Charcoal, thin border.
- **Cards**: Minimal, hover transitions (opacity/scale), quick-add functionality.
- **Animations**: Soft fades, smooth transitions.

## Do's & Don'ts
- **Do**: Use high-quality imagery with warm lighting.
- **Do**: Maintain strict alignment and hierarchy.
- **Don't**: Use loud colors or heavy shadows.
- **Don't**: Crowd the layout with too much text.
