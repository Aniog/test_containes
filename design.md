# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Quiet luxury with warm editorial softness. The experience should feel refined, calm, intimate, and premium-but-accessible.

## Palette
Use one consistent neutral direction sitewide.

- `velmora-ink` — `#201815` — main text, dark surfaces, high-contrast navigation
- `velmora-cocoa` — `#4b3b34` — supporting dark text, subtle overlays
- `velmora-truffle` — `#7b675b` — muted copy, secondary UI text
- `velmora-parchment` — `#f7f1ea` — primary page background
- `velmora-sand` — `#eadfce` — cards, dividers, warm surfaces
- `velmora-blush` — `#efe6dc` — newsletter block, soft highlight surfaces
- `velmora-champagne` — `#cba06d` — restrained metallic accent for buttons, borders, badges

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `Cormorant Garamond`
- Body and UI: `Inter`
- Product names should be uppercase with wide tracking.
- Keep line lengths airy and editorial.

## Layout and spacing
- Generous whitespace, especially between homepage sections.
- Use wide desktop containers with comfortable padding: `px-5 md:px-8 lg:px-12`.
- Use thin warm dividers instead of heavy borders.
- Cards should feel soft and elevated, never harsh.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, restrained, slightly tall, subtle hover lift.
- Navigation: transparent over the homepage hero, solid parchment on scroll.
- Product cards: image-first, minimal text, fast quick-add action.
- Use soft shadows such as `shadow-card` and `shadow-float`.

## Imagery
- Favor warm-lit closeups of gold jewelry on neutral or deep editorial backdrops.
- Keep imagery elegant and intimate, not busy.
- Use vertical lifestyle imagery for UGC/Reels-style modules.

## Do
- Use explicit readable text colors on every surface.
- Keep accent usage restrained.
- Make mobile feel polished and spacious.
- Use subtle transitions only.

## Don’t
- Do not use loud sale colors or discount-store styling.
- Do not stack everything into a cramped single column on desktop.
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-beige text.
- Do not introduce extra accent colors outside this palette.
