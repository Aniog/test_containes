# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- **Primary (Ebony)**: `#121212` - Deep, sophisticated base for high contrast.
- **Secondary (Champagne)**: `#FAF9F6` - Soft, warm neutral for backgrounds.
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#C5A059` - Refined metallic accent.
- **Muted (Slate)**: `#6B7280` - For secondary text and icons.
- **Surface (Cream)**: `#F7F3F0` - Soft surfaces for cards and sections.

## Typography
- **Headings/Serif**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. `letter-spacing: 0.05em;`.
- **UI/Sans**: `Inter`, sans-serif. `font-weight: 400, 500, 600`.
- **Uppercase Labels**: `text-transform: uppercase; letter-spacing: 0.15em; font-size: 0.75rem;`.

## Spacing & Layout
- **Whitespace**: Generous, `p-8` to `p-24`.
- **Dividers**: Hairline, `border-black/5` or `border-white/10`.
- **Transitions**: `transition-all duration-500 ease-in-out`.
- **Borders**: Minimal, no heavy shadows. Subtle `shadow-sm` on hover.

## Buttons
- **Primary**: Solid Ebony with White text.
- **Outline**: Outlined Ebony with transparent background.
- **Minimal**: Underlined Serif text.
