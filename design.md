# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory/cream)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (pure white for cards)
- Text Primary: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)
- Text Secondary: `#6B6560` (warm taupe)
- Text Muted: `#9B9590` (soft warm gray)
- Accent: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod - gold metallic)
- Accent Hover: `#996F0A` (darker gold)
- Accent Light: `#F5E6C8` (pale gold wash)
- Border: `#E8E2DA` (warm light border)
- Border Thin: `#D4CFC8` (hairline divider)
- Dark Section: `#1A1A1A` (near-black for contrast sections)
- Dark Text: `#FAF8F5` (cream on dark)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, modern
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- Heading sizes: text-4xl to text-6xl for hero, text-2xl to text-3xl for section titles
- Body: text-sm to text-base

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 to py-24
- Card gaps: gap-6 to gap-8
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl

## Components
- Buttons: solid accent or outlined, rounded-none or rounded-sm (sharp/premium feel)
- Cards: minimal, thin borders, soft hover shadows
- Dividers: hairline thin (border-t border-border-thin)
- Inputs: minimal borders, clean focus states

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300, ease-out)
- Soft shadows on hover
- Smooth opacity/scale transitions for image reveals

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep it editorial and refined
- Use serif for headings, sans-serif for body
- Gold accents sparingly
- Thin hairline dividers

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No rounded-full buttons (too casual)
- No heavy shadows
- No bright/neon colors
