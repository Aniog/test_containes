# Velmora Fine Jewelry Visual Identity

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- Base: #F9F7F2 (Soft Silk - Creamy neutral)
- Primary/Text: #1A1A1A (Deep Charcoal)
- Accent: #BFA16F (Warm Gold)
- Muted: #EAE5D8 (Stone)
- Border: #D1CEC5 (Hairline Silver-Gold)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
- Body/UI: Inter (Sans-serif)
- Product Names: Cormorant Garamond, UPPERCASE, tracking-widest

## Components
- Buttons: Solid Primary (#1A1A1A) with light text or Outlined Primary.
- Transitions: Subtle 300ms ease.
- Spacing: Generous whitespace, 16px/24px/64px scale.
- Dividers: 0.5px hairline.

## Do's
- Large editorial images.
- Centered headers for a boutique feel.
- High contrast for accessibility.

## Don'ts
- Loud colors or heavy gradients.
- Cluttered grids.
- Generic "Sale" badges (use elegant typography instead).
