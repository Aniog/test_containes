# Design Guidelines - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Palette**: 
  - **Base**: Refined Deep Charcoal (#1A1A1A) / Soft Warm Stone (#F5F2ED)
  - **Accent**: Burnished Gold (#C5A059)
  - **Surface**: Cream Ivory (#FFFDF9)
  - **Text**: Deep Onyx (#121212)
- **Typography**:
  - **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - **Body/UI**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - **Product Names**: All caps, wide letter-spacing (uppercase tracking-widest)
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, elegant hairlines.

## Component Styles
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Burnished Gold, white text, no border-radius or very small.
  - Secondary: Outlined, thin stroke, elegant hover fade.
- **Cards**: Minimalist, image-focused, hover effect reveals secondary image.
- **Dividers**: hairline-thin, muted colors.

## Do's & Don'ts
- **Do**: Use high-quality editorial imagery.
- **Do**: Maintain consistent tracking on all uppercase text.
- **Do**: Use soft animations (framer-motion).
- **Don't**: Use rounded corners on premium buttons.
- **Don't**: Use loud, bright colors.
- **Don't**: Overcrowd the layout.
