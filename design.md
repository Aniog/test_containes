# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
Elegant, refined industrial machinery brand. Projects precision, reliability, and craftsmanship.
Clean lines, generous whitespace, sophisticated color palette inspired by steel and metalwork.

## Typography
- Primary font: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, modern, highly legible
- Headings: font-semibold to font-bold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, leading-relaxed
- Sizes: responsive scale using Tailwind text classes

## Color Palette
- Primary: slate-900 (#0f172a) - deep steel, authority
- Secondary: slate-700 (#334155) - medium steel
- Accent: amber-600 (#d97706) - warm metallic gold for CTAs and highlights
- Accent hover: amber-700 (#b45309)
- Background: white + slate-50 (#f8fafc) - clean, bright
- Surface cards: white with subtle border slate-200
- Text primary: slate-900
- Text secondary: slate-600
- Text muted: slate-400
- Footer: slate-900

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-lg, clean, amber accent for primary, slate outline for secondary
- Cards: rounded-xl, border border-slate-200, shadow-sm, hover:shadow-md transition
- Navigation: fixed top, white bg, border-b border-slate-100, backdrop-blur

## Do
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text high contrast against backgrounds
- Use amber sparingly for emphasis
- Professional product imagery
- Clean typography hierarchy

## Don't
- No cluttered layouts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy shadows or excessive decoration
- No text on low-contrast backgrounds
