# MicroCosmos Design System

## Theme
A dark, immersive, science-inspired design that evokes the beauty of the microscopic world. Deep navy/dark backgrounds with vibrant accent colors reminiscent of bioluminescence and cellular structures.

## Colors
- Background: `#0a0e1a` (deep navy-black)
- Surface: `#111827` (dark card surface)
- Surface elevated: `#1e293b` (slightly lighter surface)
- Primary accent: `#06b6d4` (cyan - bioluminescent glow)
- Secondary accent: `#8b5cf6` (violet - cellular structures)
- Tertiary accent: `#10b981` (emerald - organic life)
- Text primary: `#f1f5f9` (near-white)
- Text secondary: `#94a3b8` (muted slate)
- Text muted: `#64748b` (dimmer slate)
- Border: `#1e293b` (subtle dark border)
- Glow: `rgba(6, 182, 212, 0.15)` (cyan glow effect)

## Typography
- Font: "Inter" for body, "Space Grotesk" for headings
- Headings: font-weight 700-800, tracking tight
- Body: font-weight 400, leading relaxed
- Hero title: text-5xl md:text-7xl font-extrabold
- Section title: text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold
- Subheading: text-xl font-medium
- Body text: text-base leading-relaxed

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-32
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: border border-[#1e293b] rounded-2xl
- Glow shadow: shadow-[0_0_30px_rgba(6,182,212,0.1)]
- Hover glow: hover:shadow-[0_0_40px_rgba(6,182,212,0.2)]

## Visual Style
- Dark immersive backgrounds
- Subtle gradient overlays
- Glowing accent elements
- Rounded corners (rounded-2xl for cards, rounded-full for badges)
- Smooth transitions (transition-all duration-300)

## Do's
- Use gradient text for hero headings (bg-gradient-to-r from-cyan-400 via-violet-400 to-emerald-400)
- Add subtle glow effects on interactive elements
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Keep text highly readable against dark backgrounds

## Don'ts
- Don't use pure black (#000) as background
- Don't use low-contrast text combinations
- Don't overcrowd sections with too many elements
- Don't use sharp corners on cards or containers
