# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial sheet metal folding equipment
- **Tone**: Professional, reliable, precision-focused, elegant yet approachable

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, modern, highly legible
- **Headings**: font-weight 700-800, tight letter-spacing
- **Body**: font-weight 400-500, comfortable line-height (1.6-1.7)
- **Labels/Tags**: font-weight 600, uppercase with tracking-wide

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep navy blue `#0f172a` (slate-900) - conveys trust and professionalism
- **Secondary**: Steel blue `#1e40af` (blue-800) - industrial feel
- **Accent**: Warm amber `#d97706` (amber-600) - draws attention to CTAs
- **Accent Hover**: `#b45309` (amber-700)
- **Background**: Clean white `#ffffff` and light gray `#f8fafc` (slate-50)
- **Surface**: `#f1f5f9` (slate-100) for cards and sections
- **Text Primary**: `#0f172a` (slate-900)
- **Text Secondary**: `#475569` (slate-600)
- **Text Muted**: `#64748b` (slate-500)
- **Border**: `#e2e8f0` (slate-200)

## Layout
- **Max Content Width**: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- **Section Padding**: py-20 md:py-28
- **Container Padding**: px-6 md:px-8 lg:px-12
- **Grid Gaps**: gap-6 to gap-8
- **Card Border Radius**: rounded-xl or rounded-2xl

## Visual Style
- **Cards**: White background, subtle shadow (shadow-sm), hover:shadow-md, border border-slate-200
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary: bg-amber-600 text-white, hover:bg-amber-700, rounded-lg, font-semibold
  - Secondary: border-2 border-slate-300, hover:border-slate-400, rounded-lg
- **Section Dividers**: Subtle gradient backgrounds or clean white/gray alternation
- **Icons**: Lucide React, stroke-width 1.5 for elegant feel
- **Images**: Stock images of industrial machinery, metal fabrication, precision engineering

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace for breathing room
- Keep navigation clean and minimal
- Use amber accent sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Maintain consistent card styles across product grid
- Use subtle animations (hover effects, smooth transitions)
- Ensure high contrast for readability

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/neon colors
- Don't overcrowd sections with too much text
- Don't use heavy shadows or 3D effects
- Don't use decorative fonts that reduce readability
- Don't use more than 2-3 colors per section
