# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury: warm, editorial, and refined. The visual language should feel elevated and intimate rather than loud, promotional, or trend-heavy.

## Color direction
Commit to a dark editorial base with warm gold accents.

- Background base: soft cream for most surfaces (`bg-velmora-cream`)
- Deep editorial panels: espresso-black for hero, footer, and rich contrast moments (`bg-velmora-ink`, `bg-velmora-panel`)
- Metallic accent: muted gold for buttons, highlights, dividers, and focus states (`bg-velmora-gold`, `text-velmora-gold`)
- Secondary neutral: warm sand and stone for cards and supporting surfaces (`bg-velmora-sand`, `text-velmora-stone`)
- Divider color: thin hairline warm neutral (`border-velmora-line`)

### Do
- Use strong contrast between text and background.
- Keep dark sections rich and elegant, with cream text.
- Use gold sparingly as an accent, not as a large background everywhere.
- Let whitespace and typography carry the premium feeling.

### Don't
- Do not use neon gold, bright yellow, or discount-red sales treatments.
- Do not mix cool grays with warm gold tones.
- Do not use heavy borders or oversized shadows.
- Do not let body text become too light on cream or too dark on dark surfaces.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond (`font-display`)
- Body copy and interface text: Inter (`font-sans`)
- Product names must be uppercase with wide tracking (`tracking-product`)
- Eyebrow labels and navigation use small uppercase letter spacing (`tracking-eyebrow`)

## Layout and spacing
- Use roomy section spacing with generous padding (`py-20`, `py-24`, `lg:py-28`)
- Keep content inside a centered shell (`max-w-7xl`, `px-4`, `sm:px-6`, `lg:px-8`)
- Favor two-column editorial layouts on desktop and stacked layouts on mobile
- Keep cards rounded and soft (`rounded-3xl`, `rounded-2xl`)

## Components
- Buttons: premium, restrained, either solid gold or fine outlined cream/ink
- Inputs: soft cream or white surfaces with clear readable text and subtle borders
- Product cards: minimal copy, refined shadows, image-first layout
- Navigation: sticky, transparent over hero, solid and blurred after scroll
- Drawers and overlays: warm cream surfaces with dark readable text

## Motion
- Use subtle transitions only (`duration-300`, `duration-500`, `ease-velvet`)
- Hover effects should feel soft: fade, lift, reveal, and shimmer
- Avoid bouncy or playful animation
