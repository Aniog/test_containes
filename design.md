# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity

### Mood
- Quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Charcoal: `#1A1A1A` (main background, text)
- Warm Cream: `#FAF7F2` (light background, cards)
- Rich Black: `#0D0D0D` (footer, dark sections)

**Accent Colors:**
- Warm Gold: `#C9A962` (primary accent, CTAs, highlights)
- Soft Gold: `#E8DCC4` (secondary accent, borders)
- Muted Bronze: `#8B7355` (subtle accents)

**Neutral Colors:**
- Warm Gray: `#6B6560` (secondary text)
- Light Gray: `#E5E0DB` (borders, dividers)
- Off White: `#FDFCFA` (pure white alternative)

### Typography
**Headings & Product Names:**
- Font: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- Weight: 500-600
- Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.15em

**Body & UI:**
- Font: Manrope (sans-serif)
- Weight: 400-500
- Clean, readable, modern

### Design Elements
- Generous whitespace
- Large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers (1px, light gray)
- Restrained accent color usage
- Subtle hover transitions (200-300ms ease)
- Soft shadows (subtle, warm-tinted)
- Premium buttons (solid gold or outlined)

### Spacing System
- Base unit: 4px
- Section padding: 80px (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Card padding: 24px
- Grid gap: 24px (desktop), 16px (mobile)

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px
