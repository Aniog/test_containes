# 老虎公园 Design System

## Theme
Dark, dramatic nature/wildlife aesthetic. Deep blacks and dark greens with amber/orange tiger-stripe accents. Evokes the power and mystery of tigers in the wild.

## Colors
- Background: `#0a0a0a` (near black)
- Surface: `#111111` (dark card)
- Surface elevated: `#1a1a1a`
- Primary accent: `#d97706` (amber-600, tiger orange)
- Secondary accent: `#92400e` (amber-800, deep orange)
- Text primary: `#f5f5f5` (near white)
- Text secondary: `#a3a3a3` (neutral-400)
- Text muted: `#525252` (neutral-600)
- Border: `#262626` (neutral-800)
- Green accent: `#166534` (green-800, jungle)

## Typography
- Font: Noto Serif SC (Chinese serif for headings), Inter (body)
- Heading large: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold`
- Heading medium: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- Heading small: `text-xl md:text-2xl font-semibold`
- Body: `text-base text-neutral-300`
- Caption: `text-sm text-neutral-500`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container: `max-w-6xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-neutral-800`
- Card radius: `rounded-2xl`
- Accent border: `border-amber-600/30`
- Shadow: `shadow-2xl shadow-black/60`
- Glow effect: `shadow-amber-600/20`

## Do's
- Use amber/orange for highlights, CTAs, and decorative stripes
- Use large, full-bleed hero images with dark overlays
- Use serif Chinese fonts for main headings
- Keep backgrounds very dark for dramatic contrast
- Use subtle tiger-stripe decorative elements (diagonal lines)

## Don'ts
- Don't use light backgrounds
- Don't use bright/neon colors
- Don't use rounded pill buttons — prefer sharp or slightly rounded
- Don't crowd sections — use generous whitespace
