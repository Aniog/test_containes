# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
Elegant industrial machinery brand. Combines precision engineering with refined aesthetics.

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) - elegant, sophisticated
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable, friendly
- Weights: Inter 300/400/500/600/700, Playfair 400/500/600/700

## Color Palette
- Primary Dark: #14161a (deep charcoal, backgrounds, headers)
- Primary Surface: #1e2127 (card backgrounds, sections)
- Accent Gold: #c8a45c (buttons, highlights, decorative elements)
- Accent Gold Light: #d4b87a (hover states)
- Light Background: #f7f5f0 (warm off-white, page background)
- Text Primary: #14161a (on light backgrounds)
- Text Light: #e8e4dc (on dark backgrounds)
- Text Muted: #7a7d85 (secondary text)
- Border: #e0dcd5 (light borders)
- Border Dark: #2a2d35 (dark borders)

## Spacing & Layout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Content max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8

## Components Style
- Buttons: rounded-sm (sharp, industrial), font-medium
- Cards: bg-white, subtle border, no heavy shadow
- Dividers: thin gold line accent
- Icons: Lucide React, stroke-width 1.5

## Do's
- Use sharp corners (rounded-sm) for industrial feel
- Gold accent sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- High contrast text on any background
- Generous whitespace for breathing room

## Don'ts
- No rounded-full or pill shapes
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No gradients
