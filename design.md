# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury with an editorial demi-fine jewelry feel. The site should feel warm, refined, generous, and premium-accessible rather than loud, trendy, or discount-driven.

## Palette
Use a deep refined base with warm neutral surfaces and restrained metallic accents.

- Obsidian: deep green-black for luxury surfaces and primary text. Example Tailwind: `velmora-obsidian`.
- Ink: softened dark text for body copy. Example Tailwind: `velmora-ink`.
- Ivory: warm page background. Example Tailwind: `velmora-ivory`.
- Pearl: elevated card surface. Example Tailwind: `velmora-pearl`.
- Champagne: metallic accent for primary buttons, rules, and small highlights. Example Tailwind: `velmora-champagne`.
- Champagne Dark: hover and active accent. Example Tailwind: `velmora-champagneDark`.
- Taupe: subdued supporting text and borders. Example Tailwind: `velmora-taupe`.
- Rose: soft newsletter and gift accents. Example Tailwind: `velmora-rose`.

Do: pair dark surfaces with ivory or champagne text. Pair light surfaces with obsidian or ink text.
Do not: use bright discount colors, saturated pinks, generic blue links, or low-contrast beige-on-white text.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: elegant serif, Cormorant Garamond. Use graceful tracking and large line-height.
- Product names: uppercase with wide letter-spacing.
- Body and UI: Manrope or Inter-style sans serif.
- Navigation and buttons: small uppercase sans serif with tracking.

## Layout and Spacing
- Mobile-first with airy stacked sections; desktop should use balanced multi-column layouts.
- Use generous section padding, thin hairline dividers, and soft card shadows.
- Product grids should breathe; avoid dense marketplace styling.
- Full-bleed hero imagery and large editorial image moments are encouraged.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid champagne on dark text, or thin outlined buttons on transparent surfaces.
- Cards: warm pearl surface, subtle border, soft shadow, refined hover lift.
- Navigation: sticky, transparent over hero, turns ivory/solid with a subtle border when scrolled.
- Cart drawer: dark overlay, pearl drawer, explicit readable text colors.
- Form fields: warm ivory/pearl surfaces with clear dark text and champagne focus ring.

## Motion
Use subtle transitions: opacity, translate, and shadow. No bouncy or flashy effects. Keep animations fast and graceful.
