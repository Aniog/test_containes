# Visual Style Guide: ARTITECT MACHINERY

## General Vibe
- Elegant, professional, trustworthy, industrial but refined.
- Focuses on user-friendliness while maintaining a strong machinery brand identity.

## Colors
- **Primary**: Slate/Charcoal Gray (`bg-slate-900`, `text-slate-900`) representing machinery and strength.
- **Secondary**: A rich metallic gold or bronze for accents (we will use a warm amber/yellow tone `text-amber-600`, `bg-amber-600` from utility classes to give a premium feel).
- **Background**: Soft off-white/light gray (`bg-slate-50`) to keep it clean and elegant, differentiating from stark white while reducing harsh contrast.
- **Text**: `text-slate-700` and `text-slate-900` for clear readability on light backgrounds.
- **Accents**: White for cards (`bg-white`). 

## Typography
- **Headings**: Clean sans-serif, bold but not overwhelming (`font-bold`, `tracking-tight`). We'll stick to Inter (default).
- **Body Text**: Readable, clean (`text-slate-600`, `leading-relaxed`).

## Layout & Components
- **Buttons**: Premium feel. Solid primary buttons (`bg-slate-900 text-white hover:bg-slate-800`), secondary buttons for elegance (`border border-slate-300 bg-transparent text-slate-800 hover:bg-slate-50`).
- **Cards**: Minimalist, slight shadow (`shadow-md`, `rounded-xl`, `bg-white`, `border border-slate-100`).
- **Spacing**: Generous padding (`py-16`, `px-6`, `gap-8`) to feel airy and uncrowded.
- **Borders**: Subtle, light (`border-slate-200`).
