# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Base**: warm-cream (#faf7f2) — main background
- **Deep**: charcoal (#1a1a1a) — primary text, dark surfaces
- **Accent**: gold (#c9a84c) — buttons, highlights, links
- **Accent Light**: gold-light (#dfc278) — hover states
- **Accent Dark**: gold-dark (#a88b3a) — active states
- **Neutral**: sand (#d4c9b8) — dividers, borders, subtle elements
- **Rose**: rose (#b8756e) — secondary accent, sale tags
- **Stone**: stone (#8b8580) — secondary text, muted elements

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Playfair Display (serif), uppercase, wide letter-spacing (0.15em)
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weights 300–600
- **Hero heading**: Playfair Display, 600 weight, 3.5–5rem
- **Section titles**: Playfair Display, 500 weight, 2.25–2.75rem
- **Product names**: Playfair Display, uppercase, tracking-[0.15em], text-sm or text-base
- **Body**: Inter, 400 weight, 0.875–1rem, leading-relaxed

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: sections use py-20 to py-32
- Cards: p-6 to p-8
- Grid gaps: gap-6 to gap-8
- Max content width: max-w-7xl (1280px) centered

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold bg with charcoal text, or outlined gold border. Rounded-none (sharp) or rounded-sm. Tracking-wide uppercase.
- Dividers: hairline 1px gradient (transparent → sand → transparent)
- Cards: clean, no heavy borders, subtle shadow on hover
- Inputs: clean border-bottom style, not full box borders
- Hover: subtle scale (1.02), soft shadow, color transitions 0.3s ease

## Imagery
- Large, editorial, warm-lit
- Gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- 4:5 or 3:4 aspect ratios for product cards
- 16:9 for hero, 9:16 for UGC/reels strip

## Do's
- Use warm-cream and charcoal as primary surface/text pair
- Use gold sparingly as accent — buttons, links, icons
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use hairline dividers
- Uppercase product names with wide tracking
- Subtle hover transitions

## Don'ts
- No bright/saturated colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded-full buttons
- No discount/badge styling
- No generic blue links
- No overcrowded layouts
