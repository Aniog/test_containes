# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry.
- Target: Women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase. Premium-but-accessible ($30–$120).

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Cream `#FAF7F2` — warm, soft, editorial base
- **Secondary Background**: Sand `#F0EBE3` — for cards, sections, dividers
- **Accent Gold**: `#C9A84C` — primary CTA, links, highlights
- **Accent Gold Light**: `#DFC878` — hover states, subtle accents
- **Accent Gold Dark**: `#A88A3A` — pressed states, deeper gold
- **Text Primary**: Charcoal `#1E1B18` — main body text, headings
- **Text Secondary**: Espresso `#2C2420` — softer dark
- **Text Muted**: Stone `#8C8279` — captions, secondary info
- **Rose**: `#D4A59A` — subtle accent for sale badges, highlights
- **Pearl**: `#F5F0E8` — card backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, refined
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- **Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond, UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.12em
- **CTAs**: Inter, UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.12em, font-weight: 500

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: section padding `px-6 md:px-12 lg:px-20`
- Section vertical spacing: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Hairline dividers between sections: gradient from transparent → gold-tinted → transparent
- Cards: subtle shadow, rounded-none (sharp, editorial), hover lift effect

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid gold (`btn-primary`) or outlined gold (`btn-outline`). No rounded corners. Uppercase, wide tracking.
- **Cards**: Sharp corners, subtle shadow on hover, smooth transitions
- **Inputs**: Clean, minimal, thin bottom border, no background fill
- **Navigation**: Transparent on hero, solid cream on scroll, hairline bottom border

## Do's
- Use warm cream/sand backgrounds
- Use serif for headings and product names
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use hairline dividers for section separation
- Keep gold as the ONLY accent color
- Uppercase + wide tracking for product names and CTAs

## Don'ts
- No rounded buttons or cards
- No bright/neon colors
- No bold shadows or heavy borders
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No generic blue links
- No crowded layouts
- No pure black (`#000`) — use charcoal instead
