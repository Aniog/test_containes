# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Visual Style

**Overall Aesthetic**: Elegant industrial sophistication with user-friendly accessibility. Clean, professional, and trustworthy.

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: Deep Navy (#0A1628) - Authority and precision
- Secondary: Warm Gold (#C5A46E) - Premium quality and craftsmanship
- Accent: Soft Cream (#F8F6F1) - Clean, approachable backgrounds
- Text: Charcoal (#2C3E50) - Readable, professional text
- Light Gray (#E8E6E1) - Subtle backgrounds and dividers
- White (#FFFFFF) - Clean surfaces and contrast

**Typography**:
- Headings: Inter, weight 600-700, elegant and modern
- Body: Inter, weight 400-500, highly readable
- Scale: 14px base, with generous line-height (1.7)

**Spacing**:
- Generous whitespace for breathing room
- Consistent padding: 2rem, 3rem, 4rem, 6rem
- Section gaps: 5rem minimum

**Borders & Shadows**:
- Subtle shadows for depth: 0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- Clean borders: 1px solid #E8E6E1
- Rounded corners: 8px for cards, 4px for buttons

**Buttons**:
- Primary: Navy background, white text, gold hover
- Secondary: Transparent with navy border, navy text
- Padding: 14px 32px
- Smooth transitions: 200ms ease

**Cards**:
- White background
- Subtle shadow
- Gold accent line at top
- Clean typography hierarchy

**Navigation**:
- Fixed top bar
- Clean logo left, menu right
- Subtle bottom border
- Mobile hamburger menu

**Hero Section**:
- Full viewport height
- Centered content
- Elegant typography
- Subtle background pattern or gradient

**Products Grid**:
- 3-column desktop, 2-column tablet, 1-column mobile
- Card-based layout
- Hover lift effect
- Clear product names

**Contact Form**:
- Clean inputs with subtle borders
- Focus states with gold accent
- Clear labels
- Success/error messaging

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Maintain consistent alignment
- Ensure high contrast for readability
- Use gold accents sparingly for emphasis
- Keep navigation simple and intuitive

## Don'ts
- Avoid cluttered layouts
- Don't use bright or garish colors
- Avoid small touch targets on mobile
- Don't use excessive animations
- Avoid low-contrast text
