# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif). Product names should be UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing.
- **Body & UI Elements:** Inter (clean sans-serif).

## Color Palette
- **Background (Light):** `#FAF9F6` - A soft, warm editorial cream.
- **Foreground (Text):** `#1A1A1A` - Deep charcoal for high contrast without the harshness of pure black.
- **Primary / Accent (Metallic Gold):** `#C5A059` - Used for buttons, hover states, and accents.
- **Primary Foreground (Text on Accent):** `#FFFFFF`
- **Muted Background:** `#F2EFE9` - For subtle section backgrounds (e.g., newsletter, footer).
- **Muted Foreground:** `#767169` - For secondary text and subdued information.
- **Border / Divider:** `#E5E0D8` - Hairline thin dividers.

## Visual Style
- **Mood:** Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.
- **Spacing:** Generous whitespace.
- **Borders & Dividers:** Thin hairline dividers for a refined look.
- **Shadows:** Soft, subtle shadows, mostly reserved for floating elements like the sticky nav or cart drawer.
- **Transitions:** Subtle and smooth hover transitions.
- **Buttons:** Premium feel, solid or outlined accent color.
