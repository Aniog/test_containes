# SSourcing China — Design System

## Brand Identity
Professional, trustworthy, international B2B sourcing agent. Clean lines, confident navy, and a practical layout that supports long-form business reading.

## Color Palette
- Primary (brand): `#0f172a` — deep navy/slate-900 (trust, stability)
- Primary accent: `#2563eb` — blue-600 (action, links, buttons)
- Primary accent hover: `#1d4ed8` — blue-700
- Background: `#ffffff` — white
- Surface muted: `#f8fafc` — slate-50
- Border: `#e2e8f0` — slate-200
- Text primary: `#0f172a` — slate-900
- Text secondary: `#475569` — slate-600
- Text muted: `#64748b` — slate-500
- Success: `#16a34a` — green-600
- Warning: `#d97706` — amber-600

## Typography
- Font: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- Headings: semibold to bold, tight line-height (`leading-tight`), tracking-tight for large headlines
- Body: 16px base, `leading-relaxed` for readability
- Small text: 14px, slate-600

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: `1280px` (`max-w-7xl`)
- Section vertical padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6` to `gap-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Consistent container padding: `px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-md, semibold, primary navy or blue accent; hover lift with shadow-md
- Cards: rounded-xl, white background, subtle border, shadow-sm
- Forms: rounded-md inputs, clear labels, focus ring in blue
- Section labels: uppercase, small, tracking-wider, blue-600

## Imagery
- Realistic factory, QC inspection, shipping/logistics, and product-category visuals
- Use the stock image system with `data-strk-img` and `data-strk-bg` attributes
- Prefer 16:9 for hero backgrounds and 4:3/3:2 for card thumbnails

## Do's
- Keep content scannable with short headings and bullet lists
- Use plenty of white space
- Ensure text contrast is strong on all backgrounds
- Make CTAs prominent and repeated at section ends

## Don'ts
- No exaggerated claims (e.g., "lowest prices guaranteed")
- No dense walls of text
- No low-contrast text on images without overlays
