# SSourcing China - Design System

## Visual Style
Clean, trustworthy, international B2B aesthetic. Professional and practical with no exaggerated claims.

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-weight 700-800
- Body: font-weight 400-500
- Small text: font-weight 400

## Colors
- Primary: Blue tones (#1e40af, #2563eb, #3b82f6) - trust, professionalism
- Primary dark: #1e3a5f - navy for headers/footer
- Accent: Orange (#f97316) - CTAs, highlights
- Background: White (#ffffff), Light gray (#f8fafc, #f1f5f9)
- Text: Dark slate (#0f172a, #1e293b, #334155, #64748b)
- Success: Green (#16a34a)
- Border: Light gray (#e2e8f0)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card gap: gap-6 lg:gap-8
- Element spacing: space-y-4, space-y-6

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl, shadow-sm, border border-gray-100
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Inputs: rounded-lg, border border-gray-300

## Do's
- Use plenty of white space
- Keep sections clearly separated
- Use icons from Lucide React consistently
- Show real factory/QC/shipping imagery
- Use clear hierarchy with heading sizes
- Include trust signals (certifications, years of experience)

## Don'ts
- No flashy animations or gradients
- No exaggerated claims or hype language
- No cluttered layouts
- No bright/neon colors
- No cartoon-style illustrations
