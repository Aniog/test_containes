# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury. Warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry for women 25–45.

## Color Palette
- Background: cream (#FAF8F5) — warm, soft, never stark white
- Text primary: espresso (#2C2420) — deep warm brown-black
- Accent: gold (#B8860B) — refined, not brassy
- Accent light: gold-pale (#F5ECD7) — for subtle backgrounds
- Muted: taupe (#8B7D72), taupe-light (#C4B8AD)
- Surface: warm-white (#FDFCFA), sand (#E8E0D5)

## Typography
- Headings / Product names: Playfair Display, serif, UPPERCASE with 0.15em letter-spacing
- Body / UI: Inter, 300–500 weight
- Product names: font-serif, tracking-wider, uppercase
- Hero headline: text-5xl md:text-7xl font-serif text-balance

## Spacing
- Generous: section padding py-20 md:py-28
- Grid gaps: gap-6 to gap-8
- Content max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold bg or outlined gold border, font-sans, uppercase tracking-wider text-sm
- Cards: warm-white bg, subtle shadows on hover, rounded-none or subtle 2px
- Dividers: hairline border-t border-taupe-light/30
- Badges: gold-pale bg, gold text, subtle

## Imagery
- Large editorial hero image, warm-lit gold jewelry on models
- Product images on sand/warm-neutral backgrounds
- No loud colors, no discount badges, no sale stickers

## Do
- Use cream as primary page background
- Keep text espresso on light backgrounds
- Use gold sparingly as accent color
- Generous whitespace
- Subtle transitions (0.35s ease)

## Don't
- No pure black (#000) or pure white (#fff)
- No bright/neon colors
- No large bold sans-serif for headings
- No discount-style badges or stickers
- No border-radius > 4px on product cards
