# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FBF7F1` (warm cream/off-white) — page background
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` — cards, modals, drawers
- **Text Primary**: `#1A1614` (deep warm charcoal) — body and headings
- **Text Secondary**: `#8B7D72` (warm gray) — muted text, captions
- **Accent Gold**: `#C9A96E` — buttons, links, accent elements, hover states
- **Accent Gold Dark**: `#B8954F` — button hover
- **Deep Warm**: `#3D3028` — footer backgrounds, dark sections
- **Border**: `#E8E0D6` — thin hairline dividers
- **Success/Warm**: `#D4A373` — secondary accents

## Typography
- **Headings (h1-h3, product names)**: `Playfair Display`, serif. Product names in UPPERCASE with tracking-wide (letter-spacing).
- **Body / UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif.
- **Font weights**: Headings 400-600, Body 300-400, Buttons 500.

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Thin hairline dividers (border-b border-[#E8E0D6])

## Buttons
- Primary: Solid gold (`#C9A96E`) bg, white text, rounded-none, px-8 py-3
- Hover: Darker gold (`#B8954F`)
- Outline: Border gold, text gold, transparent bg
- Subtle transition on all interactive elements

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin borders: 1px solid `#E8E0D6`
- Soft shadows on hover: shadow-md

## Interactive States
- Subtle opacity and transform transitions on hover (duration-200)
- Nav link hover: underline or color shift to gold
- Product card hover: slight lift (translateY(-2px)), shadow

## Misc
- Product prices: gold accent color, serif font
- Star ratings: gold color
- Section titles: centered, serif, uppercase with wide tracking
