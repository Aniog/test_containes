# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Brand Identity
Velmora Fine Jewelry is a premium demi-fine jewelry brand. The design language speaks "quiet luxury" — warm, editorial, refined. NOT loud, NOT discount.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: `#FAF7F2` (warm ivory) — main page background
- **Secondary Background**: `#F3EDE4` (light warm beige) — section alternation
- **Deep Base**: `#1A1410` (rich warm black) — primary text, nav, footer
- **Warm Gold Accent**: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod) — CTAs, accents, highlights
- **Gold Light**: `#D4A843` (soft gold) — hover states, secondary accents
- **Gold Muted**: `#C5A55A` (muted gold) — borders, dividers, subtle elements
- **Text Primary**: `#2C2420` (warm dark brown) — body text
- **Text Secondary**: `#6B5B4E` (warm medium brown) — descriptions, meta
- **Text Muted**: `#9C8B7A` (warm taupe) — placeholders, subtle labels
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` — cards, overlays, buttons
- **Cream**: `#FDF9F3` (soft cream) — card backgrounds
- **Border**: `#E8DFD4` (warm light border) — dividers, card borders

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), normal case
- **Logo**: Cormorant Garamond, light weight, wide tracking

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-32
- Container max-w-7xl with px-4 md:px-6 lg:px-8

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid gold accent for primary CTAs, outlined gold for secondary. Rounded-sm, uppercase tracking-wide, font-medium, py-3 px-8
- **Cards**: Cream background (#FDF9F3), subtle border (#E8DFD4), rounded-lg, soft shadow on hover, smooth transition
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline (1px) in border color, max-w-xs mx-auto
- **Inputs**: Clean, minimal, warm border, focus:ring gold accent
- **Nav**: Transparent over hero, solid (#1A1410) on scroll. White text. Thin underline on hover links.

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions: transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out
- Cards: gentle lift on hover (transform -translate-y-1) with shadow increase
- Buttons: opacity/transform shift on hover
- Smooth scroll for anchors

## Don'ts
- No bright neon colors
- No harsh black/white contrast without warm tones
- No generic e-commerce templates feel
- No cluttered layouts
- No low-contrast text (ensure WCAG AA minimum)
