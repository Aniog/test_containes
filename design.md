# SSourcing China - Design System

## Brand Identity
Professional B2B sourcing agent website. Clean, trustworthy, international.

## Colors
- Primary: Navy blue `#1e3a5f` (trust, professionalism)
- Primary Light: `#2d5a8e`
- Accent: Orange `#e86c2e` (CTA, energy)
- Accent Hover: `#d45a1c`
- Background: White `#ffffff`
- Surface: Light gray `#f8fafc`
- Surface Alt: `#f1f5f9`
- Text Primary: `#1e293b` (slate-800)
- Text Secondary: `#475569` (slate-600)
- Text Muted: `#94a3b8` (slate-400)
- Border: `#e2e8f0` (slate-200)
- Success: `#16a34a`
- Warning: `#eab308`

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-tight
- H1: text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl
- H2: text-3xl md:text-4xl
- H3: text-xl md:text-2xl
- Body: text-base, text-slate-600
- Small: text-sm, text-slate-500

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Components
- Buttons primary: bg-accent text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-lg font-semibold hover:bg-accent-hover transition
- Buttons secondary: border-2 border-primary text-primary px-6 py-3 rounded-lg font-semibold hover:bg-primary hover:text-white transition
- Cards: bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-200 p-6
- Sections alternate: white and surface backgrounds

## Do's
- Use plenty of whitespace
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use icons to support text
- Maintain consistent spacing
- Use subtle shadows on cards

## Don'ts
- No dark backgrounds for main content areas
- No overly saturated colors
- No more than 2 font weights per element
- No text smaller than 14px for body content
- No exaggerated marketing claims
