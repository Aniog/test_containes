# Design System — Paws & Tails Dog Store

## Brand Identity
Warm, trustworthy, and friendly. The site should feel like a welcoming family breeder, not a cold e-commerce store.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Amber/Warm Brown):** `amber-700` (#b45309) — CTAs, highlights, accents
- **Primary Light:** `amber-100` (#fef3c7) — backgrounds, badges
- **Primary Dark:** `amber-900` (#78350f) — hover states
- **Neutral Dark:** `stone-900` (#1c1917) — headings, body text on light bg
- **Neutral Mid:** `stone-600` (#57534e) — secondary text
- **Neutral Light:** `stone-100` (#f5f5f4) — page background
- **White:** `white` — cards, nav
- **Accent Green:** `emerald-600` (#059669) — "Available" badges
- **Accent Red:** `rose-500` (#f43f5e) — "Sold" badges

## Typography
- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings:** `font-bold`, `text-stone-900`, sizes: `text-4xl` / `text-3xl` / `text-2xl` / `text-xl`
- **Body:** `text-stone-600`, `text-base` or `text-sm`
- **Labels/Badges:** `text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-wide`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-16 px-4` or `py-20 px-6`
- Card padding: `p-5` or `p-6`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl shadow-md border border-stone-200`
- Buttons: `rounded-full` for primary CTAs, `rounded-lg` for secondary
- Nav: `shadow-sm border-b border-stone-200`

## Buttons
- **Primary:** `bg-amber-700 hover:bg-amber-800 text-white font-semibold rounded-full px-6 py-3`
- **Secondary/Outline:** `border-2 border-amber-700 text-amber-700 hover:bg-amber-50 rounded-full px-6 py-3`
- **Ghost:** `text-stone-600 hover:text-amber-700`

## Components
- **Dog Card:** white bg, rounded-2xl, image top (aspect-square object-cover), info below, badge overlay on image
- **Hero:** full-width, warm gradient or image bg, large heading, subtext, CTA button
- **Nav:** white bg, logo left, links right, sticky top
- **Footer:** stone-900 bg, white text

## Do's
- Use warm amber tones for interactive elements
- Keep cards clean with generous whitespace
- Use rounded corners throughout
- Show dog availability with colored badges

## Don'ts
- Don't use cold blue/gray as primary color
- Don't use dark backgrounds for main content areas
- Don't use small text for dog names or prices
- Don't use sharp corners on cards
