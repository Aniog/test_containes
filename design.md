# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry aesthetic
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

## Color Palette
- Background primary: `#faf8f5` (warm cream)
- Background secondary: `#f5f0eb` (soft taupe cream)
- Background dark: `#1a1a1a` (deep charcoal)
- Text primary: `#1a1a1a` (near black)
- Text secondary: `#6b6560` (warm gray)
- Text on dark: `#faf8f5` (cream)
- Accent gold: `#c9a96e` (warm metallic gold)
- Accent gold hover: `#b8944f` (deeper gold)
- Accent gold light: `#e8dcc8` (pale gold)
- Border: `#e8e2db` (soft warm border)
- Border dark: `#3d3d3d` (dark border)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) - elegant, editorial
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, modern
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-32
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold or outlined gold, rounded-none (sharp edges for luxury feel)
- Cards: minimal borders, soft shadows on hover
- Accordions: thin hairline dividers
- Inputs: minimal borders, focus ring in gold

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- Soft fade-ins
- Smooth scroll behavior
- Cart drawer slide-in from right

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use gold accents sparingly
- Maintain strong contrast for accessibility
- Use serif fonts for headings and product names

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No rounded corners on buttons (keep sharp for luxury)
- No heavy shadows or borders
