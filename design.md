# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

Velmora uses a quiet luxury editorial direction: warm ivory surfaces, deep espresso contrast, soft champagne metallic accents, and refined typography. The style should feel premium and calm, never loud or discount-driven.

## Palette
- Ink espresso: `#201913` (`velmora-ink`) for primary text, nav text on light surfaces, and high-contrast UI.
- Deep umber: `#2F241B` (`velmora-umber`) for dark panels and footer.
- Warm ivory: `#F8F3EC` (`velmora-ivory`) for page backgrounds.
- Soft parchment: `#EFE4D6` (`velmora-parchment`) for cards and section contrast.
- Champagne gold: `#B88A44` (`velmora-gold`) for primary accents and premium CTAs.
- Soft gold: `#D8BB7D` (`velmora-soft-gold`) for fine lines and subtle highlights.
- Rose clay: `#A46E5C` (`velmora-clay`) for restrained editorial accents.
- Muted taupe: `#7D7167` (`velmora-taupe`) for secondary copy.
- Porcelain: `#FFFDF8` (`velmora-porcelain`) for elevated cards and inputs.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, high contrast, generous line height.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names: uppercase with wide tracking (`tracking-[0.22em]` only where a specific editorial label treatment is needed).

## Components
- Buttons: solid champagne-gold or fine outlined espresso/gold. Rounded-full, generous horizontal padding, subtle hover lift.
- Cards: porcelain or transparent editorial cards with hairline borders and soft shadows.
- Dividers: thin soft-gold or ink with low opacity.
- Imagery: warm jewelry editorial images. Use large crops, vertical UGC cards, and full-bleed hero styling.

## Do
- Use generous whitespace and restrained motion.
- Keep all text high-contrast and explicitly readable on dark or image backgrounds.
- Use hairline borders and subtle shadows for premium detail.

## Don't
- Do not use bright sale colors, loud badges, or dense discount-style grids.
- Do not use generic saturated e-commerce blue or red CTAs.
- Do not place low-contrast taupe text over image or dark backgrounds.
