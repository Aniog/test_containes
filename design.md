# SSourcing China - Visual Design Guide

## Brand Identity
- **Industry:** B2B sourcing, manufacturing, supply chain
- **Tone:** Professional, trustworthy, international, practical
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: #0C4A6E (deep navy blue - trust, professionalism)
  - Primary Dark: #083344
  - Primary Light: #1E6B94
  - Accent: #F59E0B (amber/gold - CTA buttons, highlights)
  - Accent Dark: #D97706
  - Surface: #FFFFFF
  - Surface Alt: #F8FAFC (light gray sections)
  - Surface Dark: #1E293B (footer, dark sections)
  - Text: #1E293B
  - Text Muted: #64748B
  - Text Light: #94A3B8
  - Border: #E2E8F0
  - Success: #059669
  - Error: #DC2626

## Typography
- **Headings:** Plus Jakarta Sans, bold (700-800), tight tracking
- **Body:** Inter, regular (400-500), comfortable line height (1.6)
- **h1:** 3rem (2rem mobile), letter-spacing: -0.025em
- **h2:** 2rem (1.5rem mobile), letter-spacing: -0.02em
- **h3:** 1.5rem (1.25rem mobile)
- **Body:** 1rem (16px)
- **Small:** 0.875rem (14px)

## Layout
- Max content width: 1280px (mx-auto max-w-7xl)
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Grid gaps: gap-6 to gap-8

## Components
- **Primary Button:** bg-accent text-white, rounded-lg, px-6 py-3, font-semibold, hover:bg-accent-dark
- **Secondary Button:** bg-white text-primary border border-primary, rounded-lg, px-6 py-3
- **Cards:** bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-border, hover:shadow-md transition
- **Section Headers:** centered, with small uppercase label above heading
- **Icons:** Lucide React, w-6 h-6 or w-8 h-8 with primary color

## Do's
- Use navy blue for headings and primary elements
- Use amber/gold for CTA buttons and important highlights
- Keep backgrounds light (white or light gray) for readability
- Use plenty of whitespace between sections
- Use real-looking stock images for factory, QC, shipping visuals
- Keep text high-contrast (#1E293B on white)

## Don'ts
- Don't use decorative or playful fonts
- Don't use neon or overly bright colors
- Don't crowd elements together
- Don't use exaggerated claims or overly promotional language
- Don't use low-contrast text combinations
