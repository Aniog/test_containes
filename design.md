# Strikingly Generators Hub Design Guide

## Visual direction
A polished Strikingly marketing page with a clean white canvas, restrained borders, compact cards, and selective AI-gradient accents.

## Typography
- Headings: Brandon Grotesque 700 when available, otherwise Josefin Sans 700, then Poppins 700.
- Headings are uppercase with tight line-height.
- Body: Open Sans 400, 14px base, 1.5 line-height.
- Buttons use the heading font in uppercase.

## Color tokens
- Heading dark: `#4B5056`
- Hero heading dark: `#2E2E2F`
- Body text: `#636972`
- Brand purple: `#8159BB`
- AI gradient start: `#7671FF`
- AI gradient end: `#CB0C9F`
- Card border: `#C6C9CD`
- Divider: `#E2E4E7`
- Light background: `#F4F6F8`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`

## Components
- Buttons: 4px radius, 36px standard height, 44px large height.
- Primary buttons use the AI gradient with white text only.
- Ghost buttons use white background, 1px purple border, purple text.
- Cards use white background, 1px border, 3px radius, 20px padding.
- Hover state uses a subtle lift shadow and purple border without scaling.
- Tags use uppercase heading font, 11px size, 3px radius, transparent fill.

## Layout and spacing
- Max content width: 1200px, centered.
- Section spacing: 40px.
- Standard internal spacing: 20px.
- Tight spacing may use 10px or 5px.
- Hero vertical padding: 60px to 80px.
- Desktop layouts keep multi-column structure; mobile stacks cleanly.

## Imagery
- Use inline SVG illustrations only.
- Hero may use a very soft purple/pink wash.
- Category and feature illustrations are simple, light line-art.

## Do
- Keep text contrast high and explicit on every surface.
- Use semantic sections with clear headings.
- Keep the page feeling like a useful directory, not an SEO trap.
- Preserve generous whitespace and simple borders.

## Don't
- Do not use tinted section backgrounds outside the hero wash.
- Do not use fake testimonials, ratings, or counters.
- Do not use dark text on gradient buttons.
- Do not add hover scale, rotation, or flashy animations.
