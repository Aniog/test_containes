# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** `#0a0a0a` (near-black) — deep, refined base that makes gold jewelry glow
- **Surface:** `#111111` — card backgrounds, footer
- **Surface secondary:** `#1a1a1a` — hover states, subtle containers
- **Border:** `#2a2a2a` — hairline dividers, subtle separators
- **Text primary:** `#f5f0eb` (warm ivory) — body copy
- **Text secondary:** `#a09890` (warm taupe) — secondary text, labels
- **Accent / Gold:** `#c9a96e` (warm gold) — CTAs, hover states, accent elements
- **Accent hover:** `#d4b87a` — lighter gold hover
- **White:** `#ffffff` — on dark backgrounds for high contrast text

## Typography
- **Headings & product names:** 'Cormorant Garamond', serif — elegant, editorial
- **Body & UI:** 'Inter', sans-serif — clean, readable
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE with tracking (letter-spacing) of 0.08em–0.12em
- **Font weights:** Heading 300–600, Body 300–500

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace (py-16 to py-24 for sections)
- Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Thin hairline borders (border border-[#2a2a2a])
- Subtle dividers (border-b border-[#2a2a2a])

## UI Elements
- **Buttons:** Solid gold background (#c9a96e) with dark text, or outlined in gold. Rounded-none for editorial feel. Padding: px-8 py-3.
- **Cards:** Dark surface (#111111), subtle border, smooth hover transitions
- **Shadows:** Soft, warm-tinted shadows on hover
- **Transitions:** All interactive elements use duration-300 ease-in-out
- **Dividers:** Thin 1px lines in #2a2a2a

## Product Images
- Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Editorial, moody lighting
- 1:1 aspect ratio for product grid
- 16:9 for hero imagery

## DO's
- Use uppercase with wide tracking for product names
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Keep the gold accent restrained — a little goes a long way
- Smooth, subtle animations (opacity, translateY)
- Mobile-first responsive design

## DON'Ts
- Don't use bright colors or loud gradients
- Don't use heavy borders or thick dividers
- Don't overuse the gold accent — it's a highlight, not a background
- Don't use generic sans-serif for headings
- Don't crowd elements together
- Don't use flashy animations
