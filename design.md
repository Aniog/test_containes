# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity

### Mood
- Quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Background: `#FAF8F6` (warm off-white/cream)
- Text Primary: `#1A1814` (warm charcoal)
- Text Secondary: `#6B635A` (warm gray-brown)
- Accent Gold: `#C9A962` (muted gold)
- Accent Gold Hover: `#B8954F` (deeper gold)

**Secondary Colors:**
- Card Background: `#FFFFFF`
- Border: `#E8E4DF` (warm light gray)
- Overlay: `rgba(26, 24, 20, 0.6)`

### Typography
- **Headings/Product Names:** Cormorant Garamond (serif)
  - Font weights: 400, 500, 600
  - Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.15em
  
- **Body/UI:** Manrope (sans-serif)
  - Font weights: 400, 500, 600
  - Clean, modern, highly readable

### Spacing System
- Base unit: 4px
- Section padding: 80px (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Card padding: 24px
- Component gaps: 16px, 24px, 32px

### Visual Effects
- Subtle shadows: `0 4px 20px rgba(26, 24, 20, 0.08)`
- Hover transitions: 300ms ease
- Hairline dividers: 1px solid #E8E4DF
- Border radius: 0px (sharp, editorial) or 4px (buttons)

### Component Guidelines

#### Buttons
- Primary: Solid gold background, white text
- Secondary: Outlined with gold border
- Hover: Slight darken/shift
- Padding: 16px 32px
- Letter-spacing: 0.1em uppercase

#### Cards
- White background
- Subtle shadow on hover
- Sharp corners (no border-radius)
- Image aspect ratio: 4:5 (portrait)

#### Dividers
- Hairline style: 1px solid #E8E4DF
- Used between sections and content blocks

## Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 640px
- Tablet: 640px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use generous whitespace
- DO: Use large editorial imagery
- DO: Keep animations subtle and smooth
- DON'T: Use bright/discount colors
- DON'T: Use rounded corners on cards
- DON'T: Use loud promotional messaging
- DON'T: Clutter the interface
