# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Palette**:
  - Primary: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Carbon - for strong contrast)
  - Accent: `#D4AF37` (Classic Gold - for metallic feel)
  - Background: `#FDFBF7` (Alabaster White - warm editorial feel)
  - Secondary: `#A39682` (Taupe Stone - for subtle dividers and secondary text)
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif (Elegant, premium)
  - Body/UI: 'Inter', sans-serif (Clean, readable)
  - Product Names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.15em

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid `#1A1A1A`, white text, minimal padding, sharp corners.
  - Secondary: Outlined `#1A1A1A`, minimal padding.
- **Dividers**: Thin hairlines (`border-stone-200`).
- **Cards**: Minimal, soft shadows on hover, generous whitespace.

## Brand Voice
Editorial, sophisticated, understated.
