# Velmora Fine Jewelry Visual Direction

## Brand mood
Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium-but-accessible demi-fine jewelry. The store should feel intimate and considered rather than promotional or loud.

## Palette
Use one consistent deep editorial palette that flatters gold:
- `velmora-ink` (`#17120E`) for primary dark backgrounds and highest-contrast text.
- `velmora-espresso` (`#2A2019`) for secondary dark surfaces.
- `velmora-cream` (`#F7F1E8`) for warm page background.
- `velmora-parchment` (`#EFE2D0`) for elevated neutral panels.
- `velmora-champagne` (`#C8A76A`) for restrained metallic accents and primary CTAs.
- `velmora-bronze` (`#8B6A3F`) for accent borders, muted links, and hover states.
- `velmora-blush` (`#D9B8A8`) for soft editorial details only.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, light to semibold weights.
- UI and body: Inter, clean sans-serif.
- Product names are uppercase with wide tracking (`uppercase tracking-[0.22em]`) and a serif face.

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first, generous whitespace, centered content with `max-w-7xl` on desktop.
- Use large editorial image blocks, thin hairline dividers, and calm grid rhythm.
- Avoid crowded sections; use `py-16 md:py-24` for major sections.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid champagne on dark or outlined hairline on light, no bright discount colors.
- Cards: warm cream/parchment surfaces, thin bronze/ink borders, subtle shadow on hover.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid cream after scroll; sticky and refined.
- Cart drawer and filters: explicit dark readable text on warm surfaces.

## Do
- Keep contrast high and text readable.
- Use warm gold/neutral imagery through the stock image helper.
- Use subtle transitions (`duration-300`, gentle opacity/translate changes).

## Don’t
- No loud sale colors, badges, neon, heavy gradients, or generic marketplace styling.
- No low-contrast beige-on-beige text for important content.
- No hardcoded external image URLs.
