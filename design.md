# SSourcing China - Design System

## Brand Overview
SSourcing China is a professional China-based sourcing agent helping overseas B2B buyers find reliable suppliers, verify factories, manage quality control, oversee production, and coordinate shipping. The brand must convey trust, professionalism, clarity, and international competence.

## Visual Style
- Clean, modern, international B2B aesthetic
- Trustworthy and practical — no hype or exaggerated claims
- Generous whitespace, clear hierarchy, readable typography
- Professional photography of real factories, QC inspections, shipping, and meetings

## Color Palette (Tailwind)
Primary:
- Navy: #0F172A (slate-900) — headers, primary buttons, trust
- Deep Blue: #1E3A5F — accents, links

Neutral:
- Slate-900: #0F172A
- Slate-700: #334155
- Slate-600: #475569
- Slate-500: #64748B
- Slate-400: #94A3B8
- Slate-200: #E2E8F0
- Slate-100: #F1F5F9
- White: #FFFFFF

Accent (subtle, professional):
- Teal-600: #0D9488 — for success/trust indicators
- Amber-600: #D97706 — for highlights (used sparingly)

Backgrounds:
- Page: #FFFFFF or #F8FAFC
- Cards/Sections: #FFFFFF with subtle borders
- Dark footer: #0F172A

## Typography
- Font: Inter (system-ui fallback)
- Headings: 600-700 weight, tight tracking
- Body: 400-500 weight, 1.6 line-height for readability
- Sizes:
  - H1: 2.75rem - 3.5rem (hero)
  - H2: 1.875rem - 2.25rem
  - H3: 1.25rem - 1.5rem
  - Body: 1rem (16px)
  - Small: 0.875rem

## Spacing & Layout
- Consistent 8px grid
- Max content width: 1280px (container)
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-20
- Card padding: p-6 or p-8
- Responsive: mobile-first, then md/lg breakpoints

## Buttons
Primary (CTA):
- bg-slate-900 text-white hover:bg-slate-800
- px-6 py-3 rounded-md font-medium
- Focus ring: focus:ring-2 focus:ring-slate-400

Secondary:
- border border-slate-300 text-slate-700 hover:bg-slate-50
- px-6 py-3 rounded-md font-medium

## Cards
- White background
- Subtle border: border-slate-200
- Rounded-lg
- Shadow: shadow-sm (subtle)
- Hover: subtle lift or border color change

## Navigation
- Top nav: clean, logo left, links center/right
- Mobile: hamburger menu
- Active link: underline or bold navy
- Sticky on scroll for desktop

## Forms
- Clean inputs with slate borders
- Focus: ring-2 ring-slate-400
- Labels above fields
- Error messages in red-600, small text
- Submit button full width on mobile, auto on desktop

## Imagery
- Realistic factory, warehouse, QC inspection, container shipping, supplier meetings
- Use high-quality stock photos (Unsplash or equivalent) with professional B2B feel
- Avoid overly polished stock; prefer authentic industrial scenes
- Consistent aspect ratios: 16:9 for heroes, 4:3 or 3:2 for cards

## Tone & Copy
- Professional, clear, practical
- No superlatives or "world's best" claims
- Focus on process, reliability, and risk reduction
- Use "we help you..." language

## Pages Structure
1. Home - Hero, Services, Process, Products, Problems, Trust, Case Studies, FAQ, CTA
2. Services - Detailed service descriptions
3. How It Works - Step-by-step sourcing process
4. Products We Source - Categories with examples
5. Case Studies - Real client stories (anonymized)
6. Blog - Educational articles on sourcing
7. Contact - Inquiry form + contact info

## Accessibility
- Semantic HTML
- Proper heading hierarchy
- Alt text on all images
- Keyboard navigable
- Sufficient color contrast

## SEO
- Page title: "China Sourcing Agent | Supplier Verification, QC & Shipping | SSourcing China"
- Clear meta descriptions
- Structured content with headings
