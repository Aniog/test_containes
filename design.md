# Design System: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium.
- **Color Palette**:
  - **Background**: `#FDFCFB` (Warm White)
  - **Foreground/Text**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Off-Black)
  - **Muted/Secondary**: `#71717A` (Stone Gray)
  - **Accent/Gold**: `#C5A059` (Soft Gold Metallic - used sparingly)
  - **Surface**: `#F9F8F6` (Sand/Light Cream)
  - **Border**: `#E5E5E5` (Light Stone hairline)
- **Typography**:
  - **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif). Product names in UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.2em]`.
  - **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans).
- **Styling**:
  - Generous whitespace.
  - Thin hairline dividers (0.5px or border-stone-200).
  - Subtle transitions (duration-300).
  - Soft shadows (shadow-sm).
  - Buttons: Solid Deep Off-Black for primary, Outlined for secondary.

## Tailwind Classes Mapping
- **Primary Text**: `text-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Secondary Text**: `text-[#71717A]`
- **Accent Text**: `text-[#C5A059]`
- **Background**: `bg-[#FDFCFB]`
- **Surface**: `bg-[#F9F8F6]`
- **Heading Fonts**: `font-serif` (mapped to Cormorant Garamond)
- **Body Fonts**: `font-sans` (mapped to Inter)
- **Dividers**: `border-stone-200/50`
