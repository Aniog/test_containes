# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif). Elegant, editorial feel. 
  - Product names in UPPERCASE with `tracking-widest` (0.2em).
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif). Clean, readable, professional.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: #26211E (Deep warm charcoal/brown) - `bg-primary`, `text-primary`.
- **Accent**: #A68966 (Warm metallic gold/bronze) - `text-accent`, `bg-accent`.
- **Background**: #F9F7F5 (Soft creamy neutral) - `bg-background`.
- **Border**: #E5E1DD (Thin subtle dividers).

## UI Elements
- **Transitions**: Smooth 500-700ms transitions for hovers and page entries.
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid #26211E with #F9F7F5 text.
  - Secondary/Outline: Hairline border #26211E.
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace to convey luxury.

## Imagery
- Large full-bleed hero sections.
- 3:4 aspect ratio for products (editorial portrait style).
- Warm lighting, minimalist backgrounds.
