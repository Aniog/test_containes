# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: White (#ffffff) and cream-50 (#fefcf5) for warm, editorial feel
- **Text**: ink-900 (#1a1817) for headings, ink-700 (#4d4947) for body
- **Accent**: gold-500 (#d48d1f) for buttons, hover states, accent lines
- **Subtle accents**: cream-100 (#fcf7e6) for soft backgrounds, ink-50 (#f6f5f4) for subtle sections
- **Borders**: ink-200 (#d0cdcb) for thin hairline dividers
- **Stars/ratings**: gold-400 (#e5a62e)

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names / Logo**: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- **Body / UI / Navigation**: Inter (sans-serif)
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)
- **Price**: serif, gold-500 accent

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout (py-16 to py-24 on sections)
- Max-width containers (max-w-7xl mx-auto)
- Thin hairline dividers (border-t border-ink-200)
- Large editorial imagery, full-bleed hero

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold-500 background, white text, hover gold-600
- Outline: border gold-500, text gold-500, hover bg gold-50
- Smooth transitions on all interactive elements

## Effects
- Subtle hover transitions (transition-all duration-300)
- Soft shadows on cards (shadow-sm hover:shadow-md)
- Images with slight scale on hover
- Smooth scroll behavior

## Do's
- Use warm cream/ivory backgrounds
- Generous padding and whitespace
- Serif for elegance, sans for readability
- Gold accents sparingly for impact
- Editorial, lifestyle imagery

## Don'ts
- No bright or loud colors
- No heavy borders or thick lines
- No discount/coupon-cluttered feel
- No generic stock photography
- No crowded layouts
