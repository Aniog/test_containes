# Design System

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Modern, elegant, with subtle depth and smooth motion
- **Mood**: Professional yet welcoming, creative yet polished
- **Motion**: Gentle, purposeful animations (fade-in, slide-up, subtle hover states)

## Colors
- **Background**: Deep charcoal (`#0f0f0f`) with soft gradients
- **Surface**: Elevated dark gray (`#1a1a1a` to `#2a2a2a`)
- **Text Primary**: Crisp white (`#ffffff`)
- **Text Secondary**: Soft gray (`#a0a0a0`)
- **Accent**: Warm gradient from amber to coral (`#f59e0b` to `#f97316`)
- **Accent Secondary**: Cool slate (`#64748b`)

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (via Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: Bold, tracking-tight, gradient text for emphasis
- **Body**: Regular weight, relaxed line-height (1.6-1.7)
- **Scale**: Large hero (4xl-6xl), section titles (2xl-3xl), body (base-lg)

## Spacing
- **Sections**: py-16 to py-24
- **Cards**: p-6 to p-8
- **Grid gaps**: gap-6 to gap-8
- **Max width**: max-w-6xl for content containment

## Components
- **Cards**: Rounded corners (rounded-2xl), subtle border, hover lift effect
- **Buttons**: Pill-shaped or rounded-lg, gradient backgrounds, shadow on hover
- **Icons**: Lucide React, consistent sizing (w-5 h-5 or w-6 h-6)

## Do's
- Use gradient text for headings
- Add subtle backdrop blur for glassmorphism effects
- Include generous whitespace
- Use smooth transitions (transition-all duration-300)
- Add depth with layered shadows

## Don'ts
- No harsh borders or sharp corners
- Avoid pure black backgrounds
- No cluttered layouts
- Don't use low-contrast text
- Avoid default browser styles
