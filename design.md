# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury for modern gifting and self-purchase: warm, editorial, elevated, and intentionally restrained. The site should flatter gold jewelry with rich contrast, soft light, and generous whitespace.

## Color palette
Add these colors to Tailwind as named tokens and reuse them consistently.
- `velvet` — deep espresso base for headings, overlays, and primary text. Hex: `#241d1a`
- `truffle` — secondary dark brown for layered surfaces. Hex: `#40312b`
- `ivory` — warm editorial background. Hex: `#f7f2eb`
- `pearl` — soft panel background and dividers. Hex: `#efe6db`
- `champagne` — metallic accent for buttons, ratings, and emphasis. Hex: `#d8b36b`
- `rose` — muted warm accent for subtle highlights. Hex: `#c79a81`
- `smoke` — muted body copy and support text. Hex: `#8a7a72`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, serif
- Body copy and UI: Inter, sans-serif
- Product names should be uppercase with wide tracking
- Large serif headlines should feel editorial, not decorative

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous vertical spacing between sections
- Keep content in a centered container with roomy gutters
- Use thin hairline dividers in `pearl`
- Favor asymmetry in hero and story sections, but keep product grids orderly

## Components
- Buttons should feel premium: solid `champagne` with `velvet` text or transparent `ivory`/`pearl` outlines
- Cards should use soft shadows and clean borders, never heavy outlines
- The cart drawer, filters, and accordions should feel minimal and refined
- Navigation should be transparent over the hero and turn into a blurred ivory surface on scroll

## Imagery
- Prefer warm-lit editorial close-ups of gold jewelry on skin, dark stone, velvet, or neutral surfaces
- Use vertical social-style imagery for the UGC strip
- Avoid anything that feels discount, flashy, or mass-market

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text explicitly readable on every surface
- Use restrained animation, subtle hover transitions, and elegant motion
- Maintain a consistent premium tone across all sections

## Don't
- Do not use loud gradients, bright sale colors, or oversized badges
- Do not crowd the mobile layout
- Do not mix multiple visual directions or accent colors
