# MicroCosmos Design System

## Brand Identity
MicroCosmos is a science-meets-wonder brand exploring the hidden microscopic world. The visual style is dark, immersive, and awe-inspiring — like peering through a microscope into another universe.

## Color Palette
- Background (deep space): `bg-slate-950` (#020617)
- Surface cards: `bg-slate-900` (#0f172a)
- Surface elevated: `bg-slate-800` (#1e293b)
- Primary accent (bioluminescent teal): `text-teal-400` / `bg-teal-400` (#2dd4bf)
- Secondary accent (violet glow): `text-violet-400` / `bg-violet-400` (#a78bfa)
- Highlight (amber/gold): `text-amber-400` (#fbbf24)
- Body text: `text-slate-200` (#e2e8f0)
- Muted text: `text-slate-400` (#94a3b8)
- Borders: `border-slate-700` (#334155)

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Hero heading: `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-black tracking-tight`
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- Card title: `text-xl font-semibold`
- Body: `text-base font-normal leading-relaxed`
- Caption/label: `text-sm text-slate-400`

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- Section padding: `py-20 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6` or `gap-8`
- Border radius: `rounded-2xl` for cards, `rounded-full` for badges/pills

## Visual Style
- Dark immersive backgrounds with subtle gradient overlays
- Glowing accent borders: `border border-teal-500/30`
- Glassmorphism cards: `bg-slate-900/80 backdrop-blur-sm`
- Gradient text for headings: `bg-gradient-to-r from-teal-400 to-violet-400 bg-clip-text text-transparent`
- Subtle glow effects using `shadow-lg shadow-teal-500/20`
- Image overlays with gradient fades at bottom

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds throughout — never white or light gray
- Use teal/violet gradients for hero text and key headings
- Use many high-quality images in grid and mosaic layouts
- Add subtle hover effects on cards: `hover:scale-105 transition-transform`
- Use rounded corners consistently

## Don'ts
- No white or very light backgrounds
- No flat, colorless designs
- No small or sparse image sections — images should dominate
- No low-contrast text on dark backgrounds
