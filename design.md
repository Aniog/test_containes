# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand Personality
- Elegant but user-friendly
- Industrial precision meets approachable design
- Trustworthy, professional, and modern

## Typography
- Primary font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold, tight tracking
- Body: font-normal, relaxed line-height

## Color Palette
- Primary: slate-900 (deep charcoal for authority)
- Secondary: slate-700 (supporting text)
- Accent: blue-600 (trust, precision)
- Background: white and slate-50
- Text: slate-900 and slate-600

## Spacing
- Generous padding and margins
- Section spacing: py-20 to py-32
- Card spacing: p-6 to p-8

## Components
- Rounded corners: rounded-lg to rounded-xl
- Subtle shadows: shadow-sm to shadow-md
- Clean borders: border-slate-200
- Smooth transitions: transition-all duration-300

## Layout
- Max width containers: max-w-7xl
- Centered content with mx-auto
- Responsive grid layouts
- Mobile-first approach

## Visual Style
- Clean, minimal design
- High-quality imagery
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Ample white space
- Professional iconography
