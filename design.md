# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — not loud, not discount, not generic.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory) — primary page background
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white) — cards, overlays
- **Foreground**: `#1A1A1A` (near-black) — primary text
- **Muted**: `#6B6560` (warm taupe) — secondary text
- **Muted Background**: `#F0EDE8` (soft warm gray) — subtle backgrounds
- **Accent**: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod) — primary accent, CTAs, links
- **Accent Hover**: `#996F0A` — darker gold for hover states
- **Accent Light**: `#F5E6C8` (pale gold) — subtle gold backgrounds
- **Border**: `#E5E0D8` (warm light gray) — dividers, borders
- **Star**: `#D4A843` (warm gold) — ratings

## Typography
- **Headings / Brand**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern
- **Product Names**: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E5E0D8]
- Subtle rounded corners: rounded-sm (2px) for cards, rounded-full for pills

## Shadows
- Soft, warm shadows: shadow-[0_2px_20px_rgba(0,0,0,0.06)]
- Hover elevation: shadow-[0_8px_30px_rgba(0,0,0,0.1)]

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold background, white text, uppercase, tracking-wide
- Secondary: outlined gold border, gold text, transparent bg
- Full-width on product pages

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions: transition-all duration-300 ease-out
- Image zoom on hover: scale-105
- Fade-in on scroll: opacity-0 to opacity-100
- Cart drawer slide-in from right

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded-xl or rounded-2xl (too casual)
- No bold/heavy font weights for body text
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No loud gradients
