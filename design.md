# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Positioning**: Premium-but-accessible ($30–$120), target women 25–45.
- **Key descriptor**: Warm minimalism with gold accents.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: warm neutral `#FDFBFA` (off-white), cream sections `#F8F5F0`
- **Text**: `#1C1C1C` (ink black) for body; `#FDFBFA` on dark surfaces
- **Gold accent**: `#D4B87A` (main gold), `#B8944E` (darker gold for hover)
- **Warm neutrals**: `#F0EBE4` (borders/dividers), `#A3998D` (muted text)
- **Cards**: white `#FFFFFF` with subtle shadow
- **Dark sections**: `#2C2826` deep warm brown (not pure black)

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names**: Playfair Display (serif), uppercase, wide letter-spacing
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), clean, readable
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE with `tracking-wide-lg` (0.08em)
- **Size scale**: h1: 5xl–6xl, h2: 4xl, h3: 2xl–3xl, body: base

## Spacing & Layout
- **Generous whitespace**: sections have py-16 to py-24
- **Max width**: 1280px for content, full-bleed for hero
- **Grid gaps**: gap-6 to gap-8
- **Padding**: px-4 on mobile, px-6 md, px-8 lg

## Borders & Dividers
- **Thin hairline borders**: `border border-warm-200` or `border-b border-warm-200`
- **Dividers**: horizontal `border-t border-warm-200` at 1px

## Shadows & Transitions
- **Subtle shadows**: `shadow-sm` on cards, `shadow-md` on hover
- **Transitions**: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out` for hover effects
- **Button hover**: slight darken (bg-gold-500 -> bg-gold-600)

## Buttons
- **Primary (accent)**: bg-gold-400 text-ink, hover bg-gold-500
- **Outlined**: border border-gold-400 text-gold-400 hover bg-gold-50
- **Pill shape**: rounded-full for variant selectors
- **Standard**: rounded-none for editorial feel on CTAs

## Imagery
- **Style**: Warm-lit, editorial, gold jewelry on neutral/dark backgrounds
- **Placeholder**: Dark warm tone (#3D3832) backgrounds for product cards
- **Aspect ratios**: 1:1 for product grids, 3:4 for hero, 9:16 for UGC reel
