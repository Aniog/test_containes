# 星闪医疗器械有限公司 - Design System

## Brand Identity
- Company: 星闪医疗器械有限公司 (Xingshan Medical Devices Co., Ltd.)
- Industry: Medical Devices / Healthcare
- Tone: Professional, trustworthy, precise, innovative

## Color Palette
- Primary Blue: `#0A4FA6` (deep medical blue) → Tailwind: `blue-800` or custom `primary`
- Accent Cyan: `#00B4D8` → Tailwind: `cyan-500`
- Dark Navy: `#0D1B2A` → Tailwind: `slate-950`
- Light Background: `#F4F8FF` → Tailwind: `slate-50`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Text Primary: `#0D1B2A` (dark navy)
- Text Secondary: `#4A5568` → Tailwind: `slate-600`
- Border: `#E2E8F0` → Tailwind: `slate-200`
- Success Green: `#10B981` → Tailwind: `emerald-500`

## Typography
- Font Family: "Noto Sans SC" (Chinese), "Inter" (Latin) — loaded via Google Fonts
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-tight
  - H1: text-4xl md:text-6xl
  - H2: text-3xl md:text-4xl
  - H3: text-xl md:text-2xl
- Body: text-base, leading-relaxed, text-slate-600
- Small/Caption: text-sm, text-slate-500

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 px-4 md:px-8 lg:px-16
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between grid items: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: border border-slate-200
- Card shadow: shadow-md hover:shadow-xl transition-shadow
- Border radius: rounded-xl (cards), rounded-full (badges/pills)

## Components Style
- Buttons Primary: bg-blue-700 hover:bg-blue-800 text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-lg font-semibold
- Buttons Secondary: border-2 border-blue-700 text-blue-700 hover:bg-blue-50 px-6 py-3 rounded-lg font-semibold
- Section headings: centered, with a short colored underline accent
- Cards: white bg, border, shadow, rounded-xl, hover lift effect
- Nav: sticky top, white bg with shadow, logo left, links right

## Do's
- Use clean white backgrounds for content sections
- Use deep blue (#0A4FA6) for primary CTAs and headings
- Use cyan accents for highlights and icons
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Use professional medical imagery

## Don'ts
- No dark backgrounds for main content (only hero/footer)
- No low-contrast text combinations
- No overly decorative fonts
- No cluttered layouts
