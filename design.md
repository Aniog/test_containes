# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Velmora Fine Jewelry should feel like quiet luxury: editorial, warm, refined, and premium without looking flashy or discount-led.

## Color direction
Use one consistent warm-editorial palette that flatters gold jewelry.

- `velmora-ivory` — soft parchment background for page sections. Example: `bg-velmora-ivory`
- `velmora-cream` — lighter panel background. Example: `bg-velmora-cream`
- `velmora-rose` — blush-neutral accent surface. Example: `bg-velmora-rose`
- `velmora-gold` — restrained metallic accent for buttons, dividers, badges. Example: `text-velmora-gold`, `border-velmora-gold`
- `velmora-mocha` — muted editorial secondary text. Example: `text-velmora-mocha`
- `velmora-ink` — deep espresso for main text and dark sections. Example: `text-velmora-ink`, `bg-velmora-ink`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `Cormorant Garamond`, elegant, airy, slightly dramatic.
- Body and UI: `Inter`, crisp and minimal.
- Product names should be uppercase with generous tracking. Example: `tracking-[0.28em] uppercase`

## Layout and spacing
- Prefer generous vertical spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Constrain content using `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 md:px-8`
- Use thin dividers: `border border-velmora-gold/20`
- Cards should breathe with internal padding and soft shadows

## Visual style
- Editorial hero image with warm gradient overlays
- Hairline borders instead of heavy boxes
- Subtle transitions only: `duration-300 ease-out`
- Buttons should feel premium: solid deep ink or outlined gold
- Product surfaces should use clean backgrounds with clearly readable foreground text

## Do
- Keep the site calm and luxurious
- Use warm neutrals with deep contrast
- Let product imagery and typography lead the page
- Make mobile layouts comfortable and uncluttered

## Don't
- Do not use loud sale colors or discount styling
- Do not use saturated neon accents
- Do not overcrowd sections or stack too tightly on desktop
- Do not use low-contrast text on warm surfaces
