# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — not loud, not discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background (cream)**: `#faf8f5` — primary page background
- **Surface (warm white)**: `#ffffff` — cards, modals, elevated surfaces
- **Foreground (deep warm)**: `#1a1714` — primary text, near-black with warm undertone
- **Muted (warm gray)**: `#8a8178` — secondary text, subtle elements
- **Border (soft)**: `#e8e4df` — dividers, borders
- **Accent (gold)**: `#c9a96e` — CTAs, highlights, brand accent
- **Accent hover**: `#b89555` — darker gold for hover states
- **Accent light**: `#f5ebe0` — soft blush for subtle backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings / Brand**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Card gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid gold accent or outlined gold, rounded-none or rounded-sm, uppercase tracking-wider
- **Cards**: Clean, minimal borders, soft hover shadows
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline (border-t border-border)
- **Inputs**: Minimal, bottom-border or subtle outline style

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300, ease-out)
- Soft fade-ins on scroll
- Smooth cart drawer slide-in

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep layouts editorial and spacious
- Use serif for headings, sans-serif for body
- Maintain warm, muted tones
- Use thin dividers and subtle borders

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy shadows or gradients
- No rounded-full buttons (keep sharp or slightly rounded)
- No loud discount badges or sale stickers
- No cluttered layouts
