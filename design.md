# SSourcing China - Design System

## Brand Identity
Professional B2B sourcing agent website. Clean, trustworthy, international.

## Colors
- **Primary (Navy):** `navy-900` (#0f1b3d), `navy-800` (#1a2d5a), `navy-700` (#243f77)
- **Accent (Blue):** `accent-500` (#2563eb), `accent-600` (#1d4ed8), `accent-400` (#60a5fa)
- **Success (Green):** `success-500` (#10b981)
- **Warning (Orange):** `orange-500` (#f97316)
- **Surface:** `surface-50` (#f8fafc), `surface-100` (#f1f5f9), `surface-200` (#e2e8f0)
- **Text:** `text-900` (#0f172a), `text-700` (#334155), `text-500` (#64748b), `text-300` (#cbd5e1)
- **White:** #ffffff

## Typography
- Font: Inter
- Headings: font-bold, tracking-tight
  - H1: text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl
  - H2: text-3xl md:text-4xl
  - H3: text-xl md:text-2xl
  - H4: text-lg font-semibold
- Body: text-base text-text-700
- Small: text-sm text-text-500

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Components
- Buttons primary: bg-accent-500 hover:bg-accent-600 text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-lg
- Buttons secondary: border-2 border-accent-500 text-accent-500 hover:bg-accent-500 hover:text-white px-6 py-3 rounded-lg
- Cards: bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-surface-200 p-6
- Badges: bg-accent-500/10 text-accent-500 text-sm font-medium px-3 py-1 rounded-full

## Do's
- Use navy backgrounds for hero and CTA sections
- Use white/surface-50 for content sections, alternating with surface-100
- Keep text highly readable with strong contrast
- Use subtle shadows and borders for depth
- Use Lucide icons consistently

## Don'ts
- No dark text on dark backgrounds
- No exaggerated claims or flashy animations
- No cluttered layouts
- No low-contrast text combinations
