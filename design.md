# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: #F5F2ED (warm putty/cream)
- Text Primary: #1C1917 (warm near-black)
- Text Secondary: #6B6358 (warm taupe)
- Gold Accent: #B8943C (antique gold)
- Gold Light: #D4B96A (lighter gold for hover states)
- Border/Divider: #E5DED5 (warm light)
- Card Background: #FFFFFF
- Dark Section/Footer: #1C1917
- Error: #DC2626
- Success: #059669

## Typography
- Headings / Product Names: "Cormorant Garamond", serif
- Product names: UPPERCASE with tracking-wide (letter-spacing)
- Body / UI text: "Inter", sans-serif
- Font weights: Heading 500-600, Body 300-400, Bold 600

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace (py-16 to py-24 on sections)
- Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t with border-[#E5DED5]
- Page padding: px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold bg (#B8943C) with white text, or outlined with gold border
- Button hover: bg-[#D4B96A] or darker gold
- Transitions: duration-300 ease-out
- Shadows: subtle, soft (shadow-sm, shadow-md)
- Cards: white bg, soft shadow on hover

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use warm gold as accent only (not as primary bg)
- Product names in uppercase with wide tracking
- Thin dividers between sections
- Editorial imagery style
- Subtle hover transitions

## Don'ts
- No loud colors
- No heavy borders
- No discount/fire-sale aesthetics
- No generic stock photo feel
- No crowded layouts
