# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand direction
ARTITECT MACHINERY should feel precise, premium, and approachable. The design should combine industrial confidence with elegant restraint.

## Visual style
- Use a dark graphite foundation with soft slate surfaces.
- Introduce a warm metallic accent for highlights, buttons, and focus elements.
- Keep layouts spacious and structured with clear section rhythm.
- Prefer subtle gradients, soft borders, and restrained shadows over flashy effects.
- Use rounded corners that feel modern but not playful.

## Typography
- Use Inter as the primary font.
- Headings should feel engineered and confident: bold weights, tight tracking, balanced line lengths.
- Body text should remain simple, readable, and calm.
- Example Tailwind direction: `text-slate-950`, `text-slate-700`, `tracking-tight`, `font-semibold`, `font-bold`.

## Color direction
- Page background: deep graphite / charcoal surfaces.
- Content panels: slate, zinc, and white overlays with strong contrast.
- Accent color: muted brass / champagne gold.
- Supporting neutrals: slate-100 through slate-900.
- Example Tailwind direction: `bg-slate-950`, `bg-slate-900`, `bg-white`, `text-white`, `text-slate-300`, `border-white/10`, `bg-amber-400`, `text-amber-300`.

## Spacing and layout
- Use generous horizontal padding and vertical breathing room.
- Desktop sections should feel wide and composed, not stacked like mobile.
- Cards should align cleanly in grids with consistent spacing.
- Example Tailwind direction: `max-w-7xl`, `px-6`, `md:px-10`, `py-20`, `gap-6`, `rounded-3xl`.

## Components
- Buttons should be solid, high contrast, and easy to click.
- Cards should use visible foreground colors and lightly separated surfaces.
- Statistic blocks should be simple and bold.
- Labels and chips should have explicit foreground/background pairings.

## Do
- Keep all important text highly readable against its surface.
- Use elegant contrast and hierarchy.
- Make CTAs clear and user friendly.
- Use consistent border, radius, and spacing patterns.

## Don't
- Do not use neon colors or overly saturated gradients.
- Do not use low-contrast gray text on dark cards.
- Do not overcrowd sections.
- Do not make the site feel cold, technical, or intimidating.
