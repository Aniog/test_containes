# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora Fine Jewelry uses a quiet-luxury editorial look: warm, refined, intimate, and premium without feeling flashy or discount-driven.

## Color system
Commit to one confident direction sitewide: a deep espresso-plum base with warm ivory surfaces and muted gold accents.

- Background base: `bg-stone-950`
- Surface base: `bg-stone-900`
- Warm light surface: `bg-stone-100`
- Card surface: `bg-stone-50`
- Primary text on dark: `text-stone-50`
- Primary text on light: `text-stone-900`
- Secondary text: `text-stone-500` or `text-stone-600`
- Hairline dividers: `border-stone-200/70` on light, `border-stone-700/60` on dark
- Accent: `bg-amber-200`, `text-amber-200`, `border-amber-200/70`
- Premium button hover: `hover:bg-amber-100`

Do:
- Keep contrast strong and text always explicit on each surface.
- Use gold accents sparingly.
- Favor deep backgrounds with warm ivory content blocks.
- Use subtle translucent overlays and soft shadows.

Don't:
- Do not use bright yellow metallic colors.
- Do not mix multiple accent colors.
- Do not use heavy gradients or discount-style badges.
- Do not use low-contrast beige text on ivory backgrounds.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif
- Body and UI: `Inter`, sans-serif
- Product names: uppercase with wide tracking like `tracking-[0.35em]`
- Editorial headings: use large serif sizes with generous leading

## Spacing and layout
- Prefer roomy section spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Use contained widths like `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 md:px-8`
- Use thin borders and negative space instead of heavy boxes
- Mobile first, but preserve elegant multi-column desktop layouts

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-full, refined, medium height, subtle transitions
- Cards: light surfaces, soft shadow, thin border
- Inputs: ivory or white background, explicit dark text, rounded-full or rounded-2xl
- Navigation: transparent over hero, then solid dark on scroll
- Footer: dark, editorial, structured with understated separators
