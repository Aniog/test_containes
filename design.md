# Visual Style: SSourcing China

## Typography
- **Heading Font**: Inter, Sans-serif
- **Body Font**: Inter, Sans-serif
- **Scale**:
  - h1: `text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl font-extrabold tracking-tight`
  - h2: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold tracking-tight`
  - h3: `text-2xl md:text-3xl font-semibold`
  - body: `text-base md:text-lg text-muted-foreground`

## Colors
- **Primary**: Navy Blue (#0f172a / slate-900) - Professional, stable, trustworthy.
- **Secondary**: Golden Yellow (#eab308 / yellow-500) - Energy, quality, attention to detail.
- **Background**: White (#ffffff) / Light Gray (#f8fafc / slate-50)
- **Foreground**: Slate 900 (#0f172a)
- **Muted**: Slate 500 (#64748b)

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: `bg-slate-900 text-white hover:bg-slate-800 transition-colors px-6 py-3 rounded-md font-semibold`
  - Secondary: `bg-yellow-500 text-slate-900 hover:bg-yellow-600 transition-colors px-6 py-3 rounded-md font-semibold`
- **Cards**: `bg-white border border-slate-200 rounded-xl shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow p-6`
- **Sections**: `py-16 md:py-24`

## Guidelines
- Use realistic stock images for factories, QC inspections, and cargo shipping.
- Maintain a clean, international B2B look.
- Use iconography to represent services.
- Ensure high contrast for readability.
- Clear CTA placement in hero and footer.
