# Design Guidelines - ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style
The ARTITECT MACHINERY brand embodies precision, strength, and elegance in industrial machinery. The design should feel high-end, reliable, and user-friendly.

### Typography
- **Heading**: Inter, sans-serif, font-weight 700 (bold) or 800 (extrabold). Example: `text-4xl font-extrabold tracking-tight`
- **Body**: Inter, sans-serif, font-weight 400. Example: `text-base font-normal leading-relaxed`
- **Monospace (for technical specs)**: SFMono-Regular, Consolas, 'Liberation Mono', Menlo, monospace

### Colors
- **Primary**: Deep Slate (#0F172A) - `bg-slate-900` / `text-slate-900`
- **Secondary**: Industrial Silver (#94A3B8) - `bg-slate-400` / `text-slate-400`
- **Accent**: Precision Navy (#1E40AF) - `bg-blue-800` / `text-blue-800`
- **Background**: Soft White (#F8FAFC) - `bg-slate-50`
- **Surface**: Pure White (#FFFFFF) - `bg-white`

### Spacing & Borders
- **Border Radius**: Small, precise rounded corners. Example: `rounded-sm` or `rounded-md`
- **Shadows**: Subtle elevation to suggest quality. Example: `shadow-sm` or `shadow-md`
- **Padding/Margin**: Generous whitespace to enhance "elegant" feel. Example: `p-6`, `my-12`

## Components
- **Buttons**: Clean, with solid backgrounds for primary actions and subtle borders for secondary.
- **Cards**: Minimalist cards with thin borders for products.
- **Navigation**: Sticky, transparent-to-solid on scroll for a modern look.

## Tone & Voice
- Professional, technical yet accessible, solution-oriented.
- Keywords: Precision, Durability, Innovation, Mastery.
