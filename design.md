# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like a premium editorial jewelry house: quiet luxury, warm, refined, feminine, and modern. The storefront must feel elevated and intentional, never loud, crowded, or discount-led.

## Palette
Use one consistent warm editorial palette across the full site.

- `velmora-ink` — deep espresso text and dark surfaces — `#1F1A18`
- `velmora-cocoa` — softened dark brown for overlays and footer depth — `#3B322D`
- `velmora-parchment` — primary page background — `#F6F0E8`
- `velmora-linen` — cards, borders, soft panels — `#E7DDD1`
- `velmora-champagne` — premium metallic accent — `#D6B48A`
- `velmora-bronze` — deeper accent for hover and buttons — `#8B6A4B`
- `velmora-mist` — muted supporting text — `#8F7B69`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `Cormorant Garamond`
- Body, navigation, UI, labels: `Inter`
- Product names should be uppercase with generous letter-spacing.
- Headings should feel editorial, with large scale and airy line-height.

## Visual principles
- Large image-led sections with generous breathing room.
- Hairline borders using `velmora-linen`.
- Soft shadows only, never harsh card shadows.
- Buttons should feel premium: solid champagne/bronze or outlined ink.
- Use restrained motion: fade, lift, gentle scale.
- Keep readable contrast on every surface.

## Tailwind guidance
Preferred utility combinations:
- Page shell: `bg-velmora-parchment text-velmora-ink`
- Soft surface: `bg-velmora-linen/55 border border-velmora-linen`
- Accent button: `bg-velmora-champagne text-velmora-ink hover:bg-velmora-bronze hover:text-white`
- Secondary button: `border border-velmora-ink text-velmora-ink hover:bg-velmora-ink hover:text-velmora-parchment`
- Muted text: `text-velmora-mist`
- Editorial heading: `font-display`
- Product tracking: `tracking-product`

## Do
- Use calm spacing and clear hierarchy.
- Let imagery and typography carry the premium feel.
- Keep product cards sophisticated and uncluttered.
- Favor warm neutrals and subtle metallic accents.

## Don't
- Do not use bright sale colors or discount banners.
- Do not use pure black backgrounds or stark white cards.
- Do not crowd sections with too many UI controls.
- Do not use low-contrast text on warm surfaces.
