# ARTITECT MACHINERY — Design System

## Brand
Industrial sheet metal folding machinery. Elegant, precise, trustworthy.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif) — conveys heritage, quality, craftsmanship
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, modern, highly readable
- **Weights**: Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500), Semibold (600), Bold (700)

## Colors
- **Primary Navy** (#1a1f2e): Backgrounds, headers, footer, primary buttons
- **Brass/Gold** (#c8a45c): Accents, highlights, CTAs, decorative lines
- **Warm White** (#f8f7f4): Page background, light sections
- **Warm Gray** (#f0ede7): Alternating section backgrounds
- **Text Dark** (#1a1f2e): Body text on light backgrounds
- **Text Light** (#ffffff): Text on dark backgrounds

## Spacing
- Section padding: px-6 md:px-12 lg:px-24, py-16 md:py-24
- Max content width: max-w-7xl (1280px)
- Card gaps: 6-8 (24-32px)

## Borders & Shadows
- Subtle borders: border-warm-200 or border-navy-100
- No heavy shadows — clean, modern aesthetic
- Thin brass accent lines for section dividers

## Do's
- Use ample white space
- Keep layouts clean and uncluttered
- Use brass sparingly as accent only
- Large, impactful headings with Playfair Display
- Professional product photography where applicable

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No cluttered layouts
- No dark backgrounds behind long body text
