# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Direction
Velmora uses a warm editorial palette inspired by candlelit interiors, brushed gold, and creamy paper packaging. The storefront should feel quiet, elevated, and intentionally restrained.

## Color palette
Add these as named Tailwind colors and use them consistently:
- `obsidian` — deep espresso base for headers, footer, overlays, and premium grounding surfaces
- `truffle` — rich brown for secondary text and icons
- `taupe` — muted warm neutral for soft dividers and quiet backgrounds
- `sand` — light warm beige for borders and subtle fills
- `champagne` — restrained metallic accent for buttons, highlights, and hover states
- `porcelain` — soft ivory page background
- `pearl` — brighter light surface for cards and panels

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, generous line height
- Body and interface: Inter, clean sans-serif
- Product names: uppercase with wide letter spacing using a named tracking utility

## Layout and spacing
- Favor large editorial sections with generous vertical spacing: `py-16`, `md:py-24`
- Use centered containers such as `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 md:px-8`
- Use thin borders like `border-sand/50`
- Cards should feel airy with roomy padding and soft shadows

## Components
- Buttons: premium and restrained; use solid `champagne` or outlined `obsidian`
- Nav: transparent over hero, solid porcelain with blur after scroll
- Product cards: image-first, uppercased name, small refined metadata, subtle hover lift
- Inputs: rounded full, thin border, clean focus ring in champagne

## Imagery
- Prefer warm, close-up editorial jewelry imagery with neutral or dark backdrops
- UGC should feel candid but polished, like Reels stills from a luxury creator
- Avoid overly saturated backgrounds, discount-style badges, or loud overlays

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and readable on every surface
- Use whitespace as a luxury cue
- Keep hover transitions soft and understated

## Don’t
- Do not use bright sales colors or discount-banner styling
- Do not mix multiple accent colors
- Do not overcrowd sections or collapse desktop into a narrow mobile stack
