- Cards: rounded-2xl, border border-slate-700/50
- Images: rounded-xl or rounded-2xl, overflow-hidden

## Do's
- Use gradient text for hero headings (bg-gradient-to-r from-cyan to-violet)
- Use subtle glow effects on accent elements
- Keep images large and prominent
- Use grid layouts for galleries (2-col, 3-col responsive)
- Add smooth hover transitions on cards and images

## Don'ts
- No bright white backgrounds
- No small, cramped image thumbnails
- No harsh borders or box shadows
- No inline styles
