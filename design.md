# Ski Website Design System

## Theme
A premium, adventurous ski/snowboard themed website. Dark, icy, and bold — evoking the feeling of fresh powder, mountain peaks, and high-speed descents.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Blue** (`sky-blue`): `#1E90FF` — electric blue for CTAs and highlights
- **Deep Navy** (`deep-navy`): `#0A1628` — main dark background
- **Ice White** (`ice-white`): `#E8F4FD` — primary text on dark backgrounds
- **Snow White** (`snow-white`): `#FFFFFF` — pure white for headings
- **Glacier** (`glacier`): `#A8D8EA` — soft blue for secondary text and accents
- **Frost** (`frost`): `#1A2E4A` — card/section backgrounds
- **Powder** (`powder`): `#D6EAF8` — light tint for subtle backgrounds
- **Accent Orange** (`slope-orange`): `#FF6B35` — warm accent for badges and tags

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Hero Heading**: `text-6xl md:text-8xl font-black tracking-tight text-snow-white`
- **Section Heading**: `text-3xl md:text-5xl font-bold text-snow-white`
- **Card Title**: `text-xl font-bold text-snow-white`
- **Body Text**: `text-base text-glacier`
- **Caption/Label**: `text-sm font-medium text-glacier`
- **Nav Links**: `text-sm font-semibold uppercase tracking-widest text-ice-white`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Card gap: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-frost` or `border border-sky-blue/20`
- Card shadow: `shadow-xl shadow-black/40`
- Rounded: `rounded-2xl` for cards, `rounded-full` for badges/buttons
- Glassmorphism: `bg-white/5 backdrop-blur-md border border-white/10`

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: `bg-sky-blue text-snow-white font-bold px-8 py-3 rounded-full hover:bg-blue-500 transition`
- Secondary: `border border-sky-blue text-sky-blue font-bold px-8 py-3 rounded-full hover:bg-sky-blue/10 transition`

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds with bright blue/white text for high contrast
- Use large, full-bleed hero images with overlay gradients
- Use bold, heavy typography for headings
- Use card grids with subtle borders and hover effects
- Use badges with `slope-orange` for tags like "Beginner", "Expert"

## Don'ts
- Don't use light backgrounds for main sections
- Don't use thin or light font weights for headings
- Don't use low-contrast text combinations
- Don't use more than 3 accent colors per section
