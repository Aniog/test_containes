# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like a quiet-luxury editorial jewelry house: warm, restrained, elevated, and highly readable. The site should never feel promotional, loud, neon, or discount-oriented.

## Palette
Use one consistent, warm-dark direction that flatters gold jewelry.

- Background base: deep espresso-plum for hero and footer surfaces. Example Tailwind token: `bg-velmora-ink`
- Secondary surface: soft ivory stone for content sections. Example token: `bg-velmora-ivory`
- Warm card surface: pale champagne. Example token: `bg-velmora-champagne`
- Accent metallic: antique gold. Example token: `bg-velmora-gold`, `text-velmora-gold`
- Primary text on light surfaces: deep cocoa. Example token: `text-velmora-espresso`
- Primary text on dark surfaces: warm ivory. Example token: `text-velmora-cream`
- Dividers: sheer gold-brown hairline. Example token: `border-velmora-line`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body, navigation, and controls: Inter
- Product names should be uppercase with wide tracking, using refined serif styling.
- Large editorial headlines should use relaxed line-height and strong contrast.

## Spacing and layout
- Generous vertical spacing between sections.
- Desktop layouts should feel editorial, not cramped.
- Mobile layouts should remain breathable with stacked content and full-width calls to action.
- Prefer max widths around 6xl/7xl with strong outer padding.

## Components
- Buttons: refined pill or slightly rounded rectangles, high contrast, subtle hover lift, no heavy gradients.
- Cards: soft shadows, thin borders, warm backgrounds, visible readable text.
- Navigation: transparent over hero initially, solid dark glass surface after scroll.
- Product tiles: large imagery, clear price, minimal clutter, quick add only on hover/tap affordance.
- Newsletter block: dark, luxurious, strong contrast with gold accent button.
- Cart drawer: clean editorial panel with compact order summary.

## Imagery
- Use warm-lit editorial jewelry photography.
- Mix close-up model shots with clean product still-life imagery.
- Backgrounds should stay neutral, warm, or dark so gold jewelry stands out.
- UGC row should mimic vertical social reels without becoming playful or loud.

## Motion
- Use subtle transitions only: opacity, slight translate, soft shadow changes, drawer slide.
- Avoid bouncy or gimmicky animation.

## Do
- Keep text contrast explicit on every surface.
- Use thin dividers and restrained metallic highlights.
- Let imagery and whitespace carry the premium feel.

## Don’t
- Do not use flashy sale styling, badges everywhere, saturated pinks, or discount-store patterns.
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-beige text.
- Do not overcrowd cards or collapse desktop into overly narrow mobile-like stacks.
