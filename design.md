# Visual Identity - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry that feels curated and timeless.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (Primary), #F9F6F0 (Cream/Soft Neutral for sections)
- **Foreground**: #1C1C1C (Charcoal for deep contrast)
- **Accents**: #D4AF37 (Velmora Gold - metallic/primary accent), #8B5E3C (Bronze for depth)
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline borders in #E5E7EB (Slate-200) with low opacity.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif). Elegant, high-contrast serif.
- **Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond, UPPERCASE, tracking-widest (0.25em).
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif). Clean, legible, modern.
- **Micro-copy**: Inter, uppercase, 10px-12px, tracking-widest.

## Layout & Components
- **Whitespace**: Generous gutters and section padding (py-24 or py-32).
- **Imagery**: Large editorial shots, 3:2 or 4:3 ratios for products, 9:16 for UGC reels.
- **Transitions**: Subtle opacity fades and 300ms ease-in-out transforms.
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal background with Primary-Gold text/border on hover.
  - Secondary: Outlined (1px) Charcoal or Gold with subtle background fill on hover.

## Implementation Details (Tailwind)
- Use \`font-serif\` for luxury elements.
- Use \`tracking-widest\` for uppercase headers.
- Use \`hairline-divider\` class for subtle separations.
- Responsive focus: \`md:grid-cols-2\`, \`lg:grid-cols-4\`.
