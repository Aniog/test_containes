# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Brand Identity
- Industrial machinery brand
- Elegant yet approachable
- Colors: Slate blue-gray (#1e293b), Steel blue (#3b82f6), Light steel (#94a3b8), Off-white (#f8fafc)
- Accent: Warm amber (#f59e0b) for CTAs

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: 700-800 weight, tight letter-spacing
- Body: 400 weight, relaxed line-height (1.6)

## Colors
- Primary: #1e293b (slate-900) - headers, nav, dark sections
- Primary foreground: #f8fafc (slate-50) - text on dark
- Accent: #f59e0b (amber-500) - buttons, highlights
- Accent hover: #d97706 (amber-600)
- Muted: #64748b (slate-500) - secondary text
- Background: #f8fafc (slate-50) - light sections
- Card: #ffffff - card backgrounds
- Border: #e2e8f0 (slate-200)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 (80px) to py-24 (96px)
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl
- Card gap: gap-8

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-lg, px-6 py-3, uppercase tracking-wide, font-semibold
- Cards: rounded-xl, shadow-lg, hover:shadow-xl transition
- Navigation: fixed top, bg-white/90 backdrop-blur, border-b
