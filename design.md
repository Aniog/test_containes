# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand Direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury: editorial, warm, refined, and premium without looking flashy or discount-driven. The interface should flatter gold jewelry with a dark romantic base and soft ivory content surfaces.

## Color Palette
Use these named colors consistently across the app.

- obsidian-plum: deep background for hero/nav/footer, example `bg-[#221c1f]`
- velvet-ink: primary text on light surfaces, example `text-[#241d1f]`
- soft-ivory: main page background, example `bg-[#f6f0ea]`
- champagne: warm card and block surface, example `bg-[#efe3d6]`
- antique-gold: accent color for buttons, highlights, dividers, example `bg-[#b78b54]` / `text-[#b78b54]`
- rose-mist: subtle secondary accent for tags and muted panels, example `bg-[#ddc6bb]`
- parchment-line: thin borders/dividers, example `border-[#d9c7b7]`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif
- Body and UI: Inter, clean sans-serif
- Product names: uppercase with generous tracking, example `tracking-[0.28em]`
- Hero heading: large serif with tight leading, example `text-5xl md:text-7xl leading-none`
- Small labels/nav: uppercase sans-serif with tracking, example `text-xs tracking-[0.3em]`

## Layout and Spacing
- Use spacious section padding, example `px-5 md:px-8 lg:px-12` with `py-16 md:py-24`
- Constrain content with `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- Use thin hairline dividers and restrained shadows
- Avoid crowded mobile layouts; stack gracefully on small screens, expand into editorial grids on desktop

## Components
- Buttons: premium, slightly rounded, never bulky; use solid antique-gold primary and transparent outlined secondary
- Cards: soft ivory or champagne surfaces with explicit dark text and subtle shadow
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid obsidian-plum with blur on scroll
- Product imagery: large, warm, editorial, with soft zoom on hover
- Ratings, trust points, and small metadata should use muted but readable colors

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text readable on every surface
- Use restrained motion such as fade, slide, and soft scale hover
- Maintain elegant whitespace and alignment
- Keep the visual system consistent sitewide

## Don’t
- Don’t use bright sale-like reds or neon accents
- Don’t use heavy borders, loud gradients, or crowded product cards
- Don’t make the site look like fast-fashion ecommerce
