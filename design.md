# Visual Identity - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- **Primary (Base):** `#1a1a1a` (Deep Charcoal/Black)
- **Secondary (Background):** `#f9f7f2` (Soft Stone/Cream)
- **Accent (Metallic):** `#d4af37` (Antique Gold)
- **Text:** `#2c2c2c` (Soft Black)
- **Muted:** `#717171` (Ash Gray)
- **Hairline:** `#e5e5e5` (Light Gray)

## Typography
- **Headings/Product Names:** 'Cormorant Garamond', Serif
- **Body/UI:** 'Inter', Sans-serif
- **Product Titles:** Uppercase, letter-spacing: 0.2em

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace
- Thin hairline dividers (0.5px or 1px)
- Large editorial imagery
- Mobile-first responsive design

## Components
- **Buttons:** Solid Gold/Black or Outlined. No rounded corners (sharp or slightly rounded).
- **Navigation:** Transparent hero nav, solid on scroll.
- **Transitions:** Subtle fades and soft hover shadows.
