# Design Guidelines: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
- Quiet luxury, warm, editorial.
- Premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- Base: `#FDFBF7` (Soft Pearl/Off-white)
- Text/Primary: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Anthracite)
- Accent: `#BC9C6C` (Warm Gold Metallic)
- Muted: `#757575` (Editorial Gray)
- Surface: `#F5F2ED` (Warm Sand)

## Typography
- Headings: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif.
- UI/Body: `Inter`, sans-serif.
- Product Names: UPPERCASE, `tracking-[0.2em]`.

## UI Elements
- Borders: `1px solid #E5E5E5` (thin hairline).
- Buttons: Solid Primary (`bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white`) or Outlined (`border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A]`).
- Hover: Soft opacity transitions and subtle scale.
- Shadows: `shadow-sm` for cards, `shadow-2xl` for modals/drawers.

## Components
- Cards: Minimalist, generous padding, hover image reveal.
- Navbar: Sticky, transparent to solid transition.
- Section Spacing: `py-16` to `py-24` (generous whitespace).
