# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm editorial, premium demi-fine
- **NOT**: Loud, discount-looking, generic e-commerce

## Color Palette
- **Cream** (background): `#FAF8F5` — warm off-white page background
- **Espresso** (text/accents): `#1A1512` — rich dark brown for text and dark surfaces
- **White** (surface): `#FFFFFF` — cards, elevated surfaces
- **Gold** (accent): `#C4A45A` — warm bronze-gold for buttons, links, highlights
- **Taupe** (muted): `#9B8E7E` — secondary text, subtle elements
- **Border** (hairline): `#E8E2D8` — thin dividers, borders

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — uppercase with wide letter-spacing
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, tracking-widest

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl`

## Components
- Buttons: solid gold (`bg-gold text-white`) or outlined (`border-gold text-gold`)
- Hairline dividers: `border-t border-border`
- Cards: white surface, subtle shadow, hover shadow elevation
- Transitions: smooth 300ms ease on hover

## Do's
- Use warm cream background
- Large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers
- Subtle hover transitions
- Serif headings, sans-serif body

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy borders
- No discount badges
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No dark text on dark backgrounds
