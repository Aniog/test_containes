# Tea Website Design System

## Brand Identity
Warm, natural, and serene. The design evokes the calm ritual of tea — earthy tones, soft greens, and warm creams.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Forest Green** `#2D5016` — primary brand color, used for headings and CTAs
- **Sage Green** `#6B8F47` — secondary accent, hover states
- **Matcha** `#8FAF5A` — light accent, badges, highlights

### Neutral Colors
- **Cream** `#FAF6EE` — page background
- **Warm White** `#FDF9F3` — card backgrounds
- **Parchment** `#EDE8DC` — section dividers, borders
- **Warm Gray** `#8C7B6B` — body text, secondary text
- **Dark Brown** `#2C1810` — headings, strong text

### Accent Colors
- **Amber** `#C8860A` — warm highlights, star ratings
- **Terracotta** `#C4622D` — call-to-action buttons, tags

## Typography

### Font Stack
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif) — elegant, classic
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable

### Scale
- `text-5xl` / `text-6xl` — hero headings
- `text-3xl` / `text-4xl` — section headings
- `text-xl` / `text-2xl` — sub-headings
- `text-base` / `text-lg` — body text
- `text-sm` — captions, labels

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20` or `py-24`
- Container max-width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto px-6`
- Card padding: `p-6` or `p-8`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Subtle borders: `border border-[#EDE8DC]`
- Card shadow: `shadow-md` with warm tint
- Rounded corners: `rounded-2xl` for cards, `rounded-full` for pills/badges

## Buttons
- **Primary**: `bg-[#2D5016] text-[#FAF6EE] hover:bg-[#6B8F47] rounded-full px-8 py-3`
- **Secondary**: `border border-[#2D5016] text-[#2D5016] hover:bg-[#2D5016] hover:text-[#FAF6EE] rounded-full px-8 py-3`
- **Accent**: `bg-[#C4622D] text-white hover:bg-[#a8521f] rounded-full px-8 py-3`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace to create a calm, airy feel
- Layer soft background colors between sections (cream → warm white → parchment)
- Use serif headings for elegance
- Keep imagery natural and warm-toned
- Use subtle hover transitions (`transition-all duration-300`)

## Don'ts
- Don't use harsh blacks or pure whites
- Don't use cold blues or grays
- Don't crowd elements — tea is about calm and space
- Don't use more than 3 font weights per section
