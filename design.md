# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury: editorial, warm, and refined. The storefront should flatter gold jewelry and never feel loud, salesy, or mass-market.

## Color palette
Use one consistent, elegant direction sitewide:
- Canvas: warm ivory for the main page background
- Surface: soft cream cards and panels
- Surface Alt: deep espresso for hero and dramatic sections
- Ink: rich brown-black for primary text
- Ink Muted: taupe-brown for secondary copy
- Line: pale champagne divider color
- Accent: brushed gold for buttons, highlights, and premium emphasis
- Accent Soft: muted gold-beige for soft fills

Suggested named values implemented in theme:
- `canvas` `#f6f0e8`
- `surface` `#fffaf4`
- `surfaceAlt` `#241915`
- `ink` `#1e1713`
- `inkMuted` `#6b5b50`
- `line` `#dfd1c2`
- `accent` `#b78d52`
- `accentSoft` `#ead9c0`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `Cormorant Garamond`
- Body, navigation, UI, pricing, inputs: `Inter`
- Product names must be uppercase with wide tracking.

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous whitespace.
- Large editorial image blocks.
- Thin dividers and soft shadows.
- Desktop layouts should feel spacious, never cramped.
- Mobile layouts should stay premium with breathable spacing.

## Components
- Buttons: rounded full pills, premium weight, subtle hover darkening or border emphasis
- Cards: soft cream background, faint border, soft shadow
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid cream after scroll
- Inputs: soft cream fill, subtle border, dark readable text
- Testimonials and trust elements: restrained, never loud

## Motion
- Use subtle opacity, transform, and shadow transitions only.
- Avoid bouncy or flashy animation.

## Do
- Keep all text clearly readable against every background.
- Use warm editorial image treatments.
- Use thin borders and restrained gold accents.
- Maintain consistent spacing rhythm.

## Don't
- Do not use discount banners, bright promo colors, or harsh gradients.
- Do not mix multiple visual directions.
- Do not rely on low-contrast muted text for important information.
- Do not use arbitrary hex values in JSX class strings.
