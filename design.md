# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry
- Base: Soft creamy neutrals with deep earthy contrast and warm gold accents

## Colors
- Background: cream (#faf7f4) — warm off-white
- Text: velvet-950 (#302621) — deep warm espresso
- Accent: gold-500 (#d69936) — warm metallic gold for CTAs and highlights
- Subtle: velvet-200 (#e6ddd4) — hairline dividers, subtle borders
- Surface: velvet-50 (#faf8f6), velvet-100 (#f3eeea) — cards and elevated sections

## Typography
- Headings & Product Names: Cormorant Garamond (serif), uppercase with wide letter-spacing (tracking-wider/widest)
- Body & UI: Inter (sans-serif), clean and readable
- Product name style: font-serif, uppercase, tracking-wider, font-medium

## Components
- Buttons: Solid dark (btn-primary) or gold accent (btn-accent), or outlined (btn-outline). Uppercase, tracking-wider, generous padding.
- Dividers: Thin hairline border (velvet-200)
- Cards: Soft shadow, subtle hover lift, cream backgrounds

## Layout
- Generous whitespace, editorial spacing
- Large imagery, thin dividers
- Max width 1400px for content containers

## Do's
- Use velvet-950 for text on cream/light backgrounds
- Use gold-500 sparingly as accent
- Keep ample padding and whitespace
- Use uppercase + wide tracking for brand moments

## Don'ts
- No pure black text
- No loud colors or neon accents
- No heavy/dark UI chrome
- No discount-style badges (no red sale tags)
