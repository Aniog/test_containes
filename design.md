# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial machinery manufacturing (sheet metal folding machines)
- **Tone**: Elegant, professional, trustworthy, user-friendly

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a365d` (Deep Navy Blue) - conveys trust, professionalism, industrial strength
- **Secondary**: `#2d3748` (Charcoal Gray) - modern, industrial feel
- **Accent**: `#c9a227` (Brass Gold) - premium, elegant touch
- **Light Background**: `#f8f9fa` (Off-white)
- **Dark Background**: `#0f172a` (Dark Slate)
- **Text Primary**: `#1a202c` (Near Black)
- **Text Secondary**: `#4a5568` (Medium Gray)
- **Text Light**: `#f7fafc` (Light Gray)
- **Success**: `#38a169` (Green)
- **Border**: `#e2e8f0` (Light Gray)

## Typography
- **Headings**: "Playfair Display" - elegant, sophisticated serif
- **Body**: "Inter" - clean, readable sans-serif
- **Font Sizes**:
  - Hero Title: 4rem (64px)
  - Section Title: 2.5rem (40px)
  - Card Title: 1.5rem (24px)
  - Body: 1rem (16px)
  - Small: 0.875rem (14px)

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows for depth: `0 4px 20px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.08)`
- Rounded corners: 8px for cards, 4px for buttons
- Smooth transitions: 0.3s ease for hover effects
- Professional imagery with industrial aesthetic

## Layout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Grid gap: 32px
- Responsive breakpoints:
  - Mobile: < 640px
  - Tablet: 640px - 1024px
  - Desktop: > 1024px

## Components

### Header
- Fixed position, white background with subtle shadow
- Logo on left, navigation centered, CTA button on right
- Sticky on scroll with slight shadow

### Hero Section
- Full-width with dark background
- Large headline with gold accent on key word
- Subheadline in lighter color
- Two CTA buttons: primary (gold) and secondary (outline)
- Background: subtle industrial pattern or gradient

### Product Cards
- White background with subtle shadow
- Image placeholder area (industrial gray)
- Product name in navy
- Brief description in gray
- "Learn More" link with arrow
- Hover: lift effect with enhanced shadow

### Features Section
- Alternating layout (icon + text)
- 3-4 key benefits
- Gold accent icons

### Contact Section
- Dark background for contrast
- Simple contact form
- Company information sidebar

### Footer
- Dark background matching header
- Logo, navigation links, contact info
- Copyright notice

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use generous whitespace, keep layouts clean
- DO: Use gold accents sparingly for elegance
- DO: Include clear CTAs and navigation
- DON'T: Use too many colors (keep to palette)
- DON'T: Make it feel cluttered or overwhelming
- DON'T: Use casual language - keep professional
