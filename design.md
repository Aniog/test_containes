# SSourcing China Design Guide

## Brand Direction
A clean, trustworthy, international B2B website for overseas buyers sourcing from China. The design should feel practical, organized, and credible rather than flashy.

## Visual Style
- Use a light neutral background with white content cards.
- Use deep navy for primary surfaces and headings: Tailwind `slate-900`, `slate-800`.
- Use blue accents for CTAs and highlights: Tailwind `blue-600`, `blue-700`.
- Use subtle supporting tones for sections and borders: Tailwind `slate-50`, `slate-100`, `slate-200`, `slate-300`.
- Use green only for reassurance or quality-related details: Tailwind `emerald-600`, `emerald-700`.
- Use strong readable foreground colors on every surface: `text-slate-900`, `text-slate-700`, `text-white`, `text-slate-500`.

## Typography
- Font: Inter.
- Headlines should feel clear and businesslike, using `font-semibold` or `font-bold`.
- Body text should remain concise, readable, and practical.
- Prefer sizes such as `text-4xl md:text-6xl` for hero headings, `text-2xl md:text-4xl` for section titles, and `text-base md:text-lg` for supporting text.

## Layout
- Use wide desktop layouts with centered containers such as `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`.
- Use generous spacing: `py-16 md:py-24` for major sections.
- Desktop should keep multi-column layouts where appropriate; mobile should stack cleanly.
- Cards should use rounded corners, subtle borders, and soft shadows.

## Components
- Primary buttons: dark or blue solid backgrounds with white text.
- Secondary buttons: white background with slate border and slate text.
- Navigation should be simple, clear, and sticky on desktop.
- Use badges for trust points and service categories.
- Use icon-supported cards for process and services.

## Imagery
- Use realistic visuals related to factories, product sourcing, quality inspection, packaging, containers, and shipping.
- Avoid overly staged corporate visuals.
- Hero and feature visuals should support the trust-building tone.

## Do
- Keep interfaces readable and uncluttered.
- Emphasize inquiry generation with repeated but tasteful CTAs.
- Show process clarity and operational detail.
- Make all text highly legible against each background.

## Don't
- Do not use neon colors or startup-style gradients.
- Do not make exaggerated claims.
- Do not overcrowd sections with too many decorative elements.
- Do not use low-contrast text.
