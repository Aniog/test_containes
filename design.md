# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif). Elegant, editorial feel.
  - Product Names: Uppercase, tracking-widest (`tracking-[0.2em]`).
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif). Clean, legible, modern.

## Color Palette
- **Background (Velmora Cream)**: `#F9F6F1` - Soft editorial base.
- **Text (Velmora Charcoal)**: `#1A1A1A` - High contrast, premium feel.
- **Accent (Velmora Gold)**: `#D4AF37` - Warm metallic for subtle highlights.
- **Muted (Velmora Sand)**: `#E5E0D8` - For dividers, trust bars, and subtle backgrounds.

## Layout & Spacing
- **Whitespace**: Generous margins and padding to evoke "Quiet Luxury".
- **Dividers**: Thin `1px` hairlines (`bg-velmora-charcoal/10`).
- **Borders**: Minimal, focusing on content and imagery.

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid charcoal with cream text.
  - Outline: Thin charcoal border, transitions to solid on hover.
- **Product Cards**: Minimalist grid, hover reveals secondary lifestyle image.
- **Hero**: Full-bleed with high-fashion editorial imagery.

## Visual Effects
- **Transitions**: Soft transitions (`300ms-700ms`) for hovers and page loads.
- **Shadows**: Very soft or non-existent to keep the design clean.
- **Grayscale**: Subtle grayscale filters on UGC to maintain tonal consistency.
