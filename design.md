# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design Guidelines

## Visual Identity

### Brand Archetype
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

### Color Palette
- **Base (Backgrounds/Surfaces):** Warm soft neutrals. `#FAFAFA` (Main bg), `#F3F2EE` (Secondary bg).
- **Text & Accents (Foreground):** Deep refined espresso brown for text to give a softer contrast than harsh black, yet highly legible. `#2B2521`.
- **Primary Color (Buttons/Highlights):** Warm metallic-inspired soft brass/gold. `#B89E78`.
- **Secondary Accent:** A subtle grayish green or taupe for muted elements. `#97948F`.

### Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** Elegant Serif. Recommended: `Cormorant Garamond` (requires adding to index.html). Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`).
- **Body & UI:** Clean Sans Serif. `Inter` (already present, standard, or `Manrope`).

### UI Elements
- **Whitespace:** Generous padding and margins. Use `p-8`, `gap-8`, `py-16`, etc.
- **Imagery:** Large editorial imagery.
- **Borders:** Thin hairline dividers (`border-gray-200` or custom color). Use border 1px instead of stark outlines.
- **Hover States:** Subtle transitions (`transition-all duration-300`). Soft opacities, very subtle scaling.
- **Shadows:** Soft, diffused shadows (`shadow-sm`, `shadow-md` with low opacity), avoiding harsh boxes.
- **Buttons:** Premium feel. Solid primary color with white text, or outlined accent with primary text. No overly rounded corners (use `rounded-none` or `rounded-sm`).

### Tailwind Conventions to Keep in Mind
- Font families: We will set `font-serif` to Cormorant Garamond and `font-sans` to Inter.
- Heading sizes should be large but refined, `font-light` or `font-normal`.
