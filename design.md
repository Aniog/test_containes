# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial direction: warm ivory surfaces, deep espresso text, refined oxblood depth, and muted champagne-gold accents. The brand should feel premium and calm, never loud, discounted, or generic.

## Palette
- `velmora-ivory` (`#F7F1E8`): primary page background and soft sections.
- `velmora-cream` (`#FFFBF5`): elevated cards, forms, drawer surfaces.
- `velmora-espresso` (`#241915`): main text and dark premium surfaces.
- `velmora-umber` (`#5C3B2E`): secondary warm text and borders.
- `velmora-oxblood` (`#4A171C`): deep accent backgrounds and solid premium buttons.
- `velmora-champagne` (`#C8A96A`): restrained metallic accent, hairlines, icons, focus rings.
- `velmora-mist` (`#E8DED2`): subtle dividers and muted UI.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif.
- Product names are uppercase with wide letter spacing.

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first layouts with generous whitespace.
- Desktop sections use editorial split layouts and spacious grids.
- Use thin hairline borders in `velmora-mist` or low-opacity `velmora-champagne`.
- Cards should feel light: soft shadows, subtle transitions, no heavy borders.

## Components
- Primary buttons: oxblood background, cream text, champagne hover/focus detail.
- Secondary buttons: transparent or cream background with espresso/umber border.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid cream after scroll, sticky at top.
- Cart drawer and modals: cream background with explicit espresso text.

## Imagery
- Warm gold jewelry on model or neutral/dark editorial backgrounds.
- Images should be large, cropped with intention, and paired with nearby text references for stock-image generation.
- Do not use loud sale banners, generic discount visuals, or saturated neon colors.

## Accessibility
- All text must have explicit readable color against its surface.
- Muted text remains legible using `velmora-umber`, not low-contrast gray.
- Interactive elements must include visible focus states and clear labels.
