# Visual Style Guide

## Typography
- Font Family: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif (from index.html)
- Headings: Bold, tight letter spacing, slate-900 `text-slate-900 tracking-tight`
- Body: Normal weight, slate-600 `text-slate-600`

## Colors
- Primary: Blue-600 `bg-blue-600` / `text-blue-600`
- Background: White `bg-white`
- Surface: Slate-50 `bg-slate-50`
- Border: Slate-200 `border-slate-200`

## Spacing
- Standard Padding: 1rem (4) to 2rem (8)
- Section Spacing: 4rem (16) to 6rem (24)

## Components
- Cards: White background, subtle shadow, rounded-lg `bg-white shadow-sm border border-slate-200 rounded-lg`
- Buttons: Rounded-md, font-medium, transition `rounded-md font-medium transition-colors`
