# SSourcing China Design Guidelines

## Visual Style
Clean, trustworthy, international B2B sourcing agent. Professional and practical.

## Colors
- **Primary**: Deep Blue (Tailwind: `blue-900`, #1e3a8a) - Represents stability and trust.
- **Secondary**: Slate Gray (Tailwind: `slate-600`, #475569) - Professional and neutral.
- **Accent**: Amber/Orange (Tailwind: `amber-600`, #d97706) - High visibility for CTAs.
- **Background**: Neutral Gray (Tailwind: `slate-50`, #f8fafc) or White.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, 600 or 700 weight. Clear and bold.
- **Body**: Inter, 400 weight. Highly readable.

## Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners (`rounded-md`), clear hover states.
- **Cards**: Subtle shadows (`shadow-sm` to `shadow-md`), white backgrounds.
- **Icons**: Lucide React - Thin or medium stroke.
- **Images**: Realistic imagery of factories, quality control, warehouses, and shipping. Use stock image system.

## Spacing & Layout
- Large sections with ample padding (`py-16` to `py-24`).
- Centered containers (`container mx-auto px-4`).
- Grid-based layouts for features and services.

## Tone & Voice
- Professional, clear, practical.
- Focused on reliability and problem-solving.
- Avoid exaggerated claims.
