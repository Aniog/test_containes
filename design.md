# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury with editorial restraint. The visual world is warm, softly dramatic, and premium without feeling intimidating or discount-driven. The interface should flatter gold jewelry through contrast, texture, whitespace, and polished typography.

## Palette
Use this palette consistently across the whole site. These colors must be represented as named Tailwind tokens.

- `obsidian` — `#171413` — primary deep base for hero areas, dark surfaces, text on light gold moments
- `espresso` — `#241D1A` — secondary dark tone for overlays and rich grounding surfaces
- `parchment` — `#F5EFE7` — main page background
- `ivory` — `#FCF8F3` — elevated cards and lighter content surfaces
- `taupe` — `#8C7C70` — muted body copy, separators, secondary UI
- `gilded` — `#BC9562` — refined metallic accent for buttons, links, and emphasis
- `rose` — `#D7B89A` — restrained warm accent for hover states and soft highlights

## Typography
- Headings and editorial statements: `Cormorant Garamond`, elegant and airy
- Body copy, UI labels, navigation, and forms: `Inter`
- Product names: serif, uppercase, generous letter spacing using `tracking-velmora`
- Small UI labels: uppercase with `tracking-luxe`

## Layout and spacing
- Favor generous whitespace and calm section rhythm
- Use clean content containers with wide breathing room on desktop and tidy padding on mobile
- Prefer thin dividers instead of heavy borders
- Product cards, tiles, and panels should feel sculpted and light, never crowded

## Components
- Buttons: rounded full, premium, tactile, never overly bold
- Cards: warm ivory surfaces or image-led layouts with subtle shadow
- Navigation: transparent over the hero, solid parchment once scrolled
- Inputs: refined, understated borders with strong contrast
- Testimonials: minimal editorial cards with clear readable text

## Motion
- Use subtle opacity, translate, and shadow transitions
- Hover effects should feel soft and expensive, not playful or bouncy
- Drawers and menus should glide smoothly with restrained timing

## Do
- Keep contrast high and text explicitly readable on every surface
- Use gold accents sparingly for premium emphasis
- Let imagery and typography carry the luxury feel
- Maintain consistent editorial spacing from hero to footer

## Don’t
- Do not use loud gradients, bright discount colors, or crowded badge-heavy layouts
- Do not make the site feel like fast fashion or marketplace UI
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-beige text
- Do not stack too many competing accents in one section
