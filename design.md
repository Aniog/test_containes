# Visual Identity - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Refined Base):** `#1A1A1A` (Deep Charcoal/Black)
- **Accent (Warm Metallic):** `#D4AF37` (Gold Accent)
- **Secondary (Editorial Neutral):** `#F9F7F2` (Warm Bone/Off-white)
- **Muted (Text/Dividers):** `#71717A` (Zinc/Muted Gray)

Tailwind Classes:
- bg-primary, text-primary-foreground
- bg-accent, text-accent-foreground
- bg-secondary, text-secondary-foreground
- border-border

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** Serif (Cormorant Garamond)
  - `font-serif`
  - `tracking-widest uppercase` (for product names)
- **Body & UI:** Sans-serif (Inter)
  - `font-sans`

## UI Elements
- **Dividers:** Thin hairline (`border-t-[0.5px] border-zinc-200`)
- **Buttons:**
  - Premium Solid: `bg-accent text-white px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm hover:opacity-90 transition-all`
  - Premium Outlined: `border border-primary text-primary px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm hover:bg-primary hover:text-white transition-all`
- **Transitions:** Soft fades and subtle transforms.
- **Shadows:** Very subtle `shadow-sm` or none.

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace.
- Use large editorial imagery.
- Keep text aligned and intentional.
- Use Uppercase for headers where specified.

## Don'ts
- Use bright, neon, or "sale" red/yellow colors.
- Use heavy shadows or rounded corners (keep it sharp/elegant).
- Overcrowd the layout.
