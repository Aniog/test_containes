# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Personality
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Direct-to-consumer premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Colors
- **Background (Primary)**: `#F9F7F2` (Soft Cream) - Tailwind: `bg-[#F9F7F2]`
- **Background (Deep)**: `#1A1A1A` (Deep Charcoal) - Tailwind: `bg-[#1A1A1A]`
- **Text (Primary)**: `#2C2C2C` (Deep Gray/Black) - Tailwind: `text-[#2C2C2C]`
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#B89F70` (Muted Gold) - Tailwind: `text-[#B89F70]` / `bg-[#B89F70]`
- **Divider**: `#E5E5E5` (Light Gray) - Tailwind: `border-[#E5E5E5]`

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. Elegant and high-contrast.
- **UI & Body**: `Inter`, sans-serif. Clean and modern.
- **Product Titles**: `Cormorant Garamond`, All Caps, letter-spacing: 0.1em.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Solid: `bg-[#2C2C2C] text-white hover:bg-[#B89F70] transition-colors`
  - Outlined: `border border-[#2C2C2C] text-[#2C2C2C] hover:bg-[#2C2C2C] hover:text-white transition-all`
- **Dividers**: Thin hairlines (`border-t border-[#E5E5E5]`).
- **Cards**: Large images, minimal text, soft hover transitions.
