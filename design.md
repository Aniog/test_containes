# Serengeti Pulse — Design System

## Visual Identity: "Warm Earth"
A palette inspired by the African savanna at golden hour.

## Colors
- **Burnt Orange** (`#C85A17`): Primary accent — buttons, highlights, active states
- **Deep Ochre** (`#B8860B`): Secondary accent — borders, subtle elements
- **Acacia Green** (`#5C7A3E`): Tertiary accent — nature elements, badges
- **Savanna Cream** (`#F5F0E8`): Page background
- **Warm Sand** (`#E8DCC8`): Card backgrounds, surfaces
- **Dark Earth** (`#3B2F1E`): Primary text
- **Dust Brown** (`#6B5B3E`): Secondary/muted text
- **Sunset Gold** (`#D4952A`): Subtle highlights

## Typography
- **Primary**: Playfair Display (Google Fonts) — tall, elegant serif
  - Headings use weight 700-800
  - Body uses weight 400-500
- **Fallback**: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace — sections use `py-24` to `py-32`
- Full-bleed imagery where appropriate
- Cards use `rounded-lg` with subtle shadows

## Do's
- Use warm, earthy tones throughout
- Keep typography elegant with proper letter-spacing
- Use generous padding and breathing room
- Layer warm gradients for depth

## Don'ts
- No cold blues or neon colors
- No harsh pure-white backgrounds
- No sans-serif for headings
- No cramped layouts
