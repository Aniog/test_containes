# Design System: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Identity
**Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.
**Whitespace**: Generous, airy, editorial-inspired.

## Color Palette
- **Background (Cream)**: `#FAF9F6` (Tw: `bg-[#FAF9F6]`)
- **Foreground (Charcoal)**: `#1A150E` (Tw: `text-[#1A150E]`)
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#C5A059` (Tw: `text-[#C5A059]` or `bg-[#C5A059]`)
- **Muted (Taupe)**: `#A69B8F` (Tw: `text-[#A69B8F]`)
- **Border**: `#E5E2DD` (Tw: `border-[#E5E2DD]`)

## Typography
- **Headings (Serif)**: `'Cormorant Garamond', serif`
  - Used for: Brand logo, page titles, section headers, product names.
  - Characteristics: Wide letter spacing for uppercase product names.
- **Body (Sans-serif)**: `'Manrope', sans-serif`
  - Used for: Body text, UI elements, buttons, navigation.
  - Characteristics: Clean, legible, modern.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal with White text, minimal padding, elegant.
  - Secondary: Outlined Charcoal or Gold, thin border.
- **Dividers**: Hairline thin (`0.5px` if possible or standard `1px`), subtle.
- **Cards**: Minimalist, no borders or shadows unless hovering. Soft hover transitions.
- **Inputs**: Clean, underlined or subtle border, focus effect.

## Imagery
- **Style**: Warm-lit, close-ups, editorial high-fashion photography.
- **Ratios**: 3x2, 4x3, 9x16 for UGC.
