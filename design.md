# MicroCosmos Design System

## Theme
A dark, immersive, science-inspired design that evokes the mystery of the microscopic world. Deep dark backgrounds with vibrant accent colors reminiscent of fluorescence microscopy.

## Colors
- **Background**: `deep-dark` (#0a0a0f) - near-black with a slight blue tint
- **Surface**: `surface` (#12121a) - slightly lighter dark for cards
- **Surface Hover**: `surface-hover` (#1a1a2e) - card hover state
- **Primary**: `primary` (#6366f1) - indigo/violet for primary actions
- **Accent**: `accent` (#06b6d4) - cyan for highlights
- **Accent Secondary**: `accent-warm` (#f59e0b) - amber for warm accents
- **Text Primary**: `text-primary` (#f1f5f9) - near-white
- **Text Secondary**: `text-secondary` (#94a3b8) - muted slate
- **Border**: `border-subtle` (#1e293b) - subtle borders

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts), weights 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800
- Headings: font-bold or font-extrabold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, text-base or text-lg
- Hero title: text-5xl md:text-7xl font-extrabold
- Section titles: text-3xl md:text-5xl font-bold
- Subtitles: text-lg md:text-xl font-light text-secondary

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-32
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-2xl border border-border-subtle
- Images: rounded-xl or rounded-2xl overflow-hidden
- Subtle glow: shadow-lg shadow-primary/10

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace between sections
- Use gradient overlays on hero backgrounds
- Use rounded corners on all cards and images
- Keep text highly readable with good contrast
- Use subtle animations/transitions on hover

## Don'ts
- Don't use pure white backgrounds
- Don't use small text without sufficient contrast
- Don't crowd elements together
- Don't use sharp corners
- Don't use inline styles
