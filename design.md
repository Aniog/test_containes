# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry.

## Color Palette
- Background: cream (#FAF8F5) — soft warm ivory
- Primary Accent: warm-500 (#B8945A) — muted gold
- Deep Base: midnight (#1A1A1C) — for hero backgrounds, dark cards
- Body Text: charcoal (#2D2D30) — soft near-black
- Muted Text: stone (#8A8780) — for secondary info
- Hairlines: warm-200 (#EBE1D0) — thin dividers

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) — elegant, refined
  - Product names: UPPERCASE with wider tracking
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- Font sizes: generous scale, plenty of whitespace

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28 lg:py-32
- Cards: generous internal padding
- Hairline dividers (1px) between sections

## Buttons
- Primary: solid warm-500 bg, white text, uppercase, wider tracking
- Outline: warm-500 border + text, transparent bg, fills on hover
- No rounded corners (sharp, architectural)

## Cards
- White/cream bg, subtle shadow on hover
- Thin hairline borders
- Hover: subtle translateY + shadow

## Animations
- Fade-up on scroll for sections
- Smooth 300ms transitions on interactive elements
- Subtle scale on button press (active:scale-[0.98])

## Do
- Generous whitespace
- Hairline dividers between sections
- UPPERCASE product names with wide letter-spacing
- Soft shadows on hover only
- Restrained accent color usage

## Don't
- No bright/bold colors beyond the gold accent
- No rounded corners on buttons/cards
- No discount/badge aesthetics
- No generic e-commerce look
- No dark text on dark backgrounds
