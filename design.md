# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand
Velmora is a demi-fine DTC jewelry brand. Quiet luxury, warm editorial feel. Target: women 25–45, self-purchase and gifting.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#f8f6f2` (warm ivory/cream) — main page bg
- Surface: `#ffffff` (white) — cards, modals, drawers
- Text Primary: `#1a1a1a` (near-black) — body text
- Text Secondary: `#6b6258` (warm taupe) — muted text
- Accent / Gold: `#c9a96e` (warm gold) — CTAs, accent lines, hover states
- Accent Hover: `#b8934f` (darker gold)
- Border: `#e5ddd3` (warm beige) — dividers, borders
- Overlay: `rgba(26, 26, 26, 0.4)` — image overlays
- Trust/Utility: `#4a7c59` (deep sage) — only for success/shipping indicators

## Typography
- Headings: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif — all h1/h2/h3, product names, quotes
- Body: 'Inter', sans-serif — all UI text, descriptions, nav links
- Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.08em (tracking-widest in Tailwind)
- Sizes: h1: 3.5rem (56px), h2: 2.5rem (40px), h3: 1.5rem (24px), body: 0.938rem (15px), small: 0.813rem (13px)

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Page padding: px-6 md:px-12 lg:px-16
- Generous whitespace between sections

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#e5ddd3]
- Card hover: subtle shadow-lg transition, scale-[1.02] on image
- Buttons: rounded-none (sharp/clean), px-10 py-4

## Visual Style DOs
- Use warm gold (#c9a96e) sparingly — just CTAs, accent lines, star ratings
- All buttons: uppercase, tracking-widest, text-sm
- Hover transitions: duration-300 ease-out
- Images: full-bleed editorial style, warm tones
- Product cards: clean white bg, generous padding

## Visual Style DON'Ts
- No loud colors, no bright reds/blues
- No rounded corners on buttons (use rounded-none)
- No heavy borders or thick lines
- No generic stock photography
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/voucher styling
