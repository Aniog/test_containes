# MicroCosmos Design System

## Brand Identity
MicroCosmos is a visually immersive website exploring the hidden world of microscopic life — bacteria, cells, fungi, plankton, and more. The aesthetic is dark, scientific, and awe-inspiring, with vivid accent colors inspired by bioluminescence and microscopy imagery.

## Color Palette
- Background (deep dark): `bg-gray-950` (#030712)
- Surface / Card: `bg-gray-900` (#111827)
- Surface elevated: `bg-gray-800` (#1f2937)
- Primary accent (cyan/teal): `text-cyan-400` (#22d3ee), `bg-cyan-500` (#06b6d4)
- Secondary accent (violet): `text-violet-400` (#a78bfa)
- Highlight (emerald): `text-emerald-400` (#34d399)
- Body text: `text-gray-100` (#f3f4f6)
- Muted text: `text-gray-400` (#9ca3af)
- Borders: `border-gray-700` (#374151)

## Typography
- Font family: "Inter" (Google Fonts) for body; system-ui fallback
- Hero heading: `text-6xl md:text-8xl font-black tracking-tight`
- Section heading: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- Subheading: `text-xl font-semibold`
- Body: `text-base font-normal leading-relaxed`
- Caption / label: `text-sm text-gray-400`

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto`
- Section padding: `py-20 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6` or `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card border: `border border-gray-700 rounded-2xl`
- Glow effect (cyan): `shadow-[0_0_30px_rgba(34,211,238,0.15)]`
- Glow effect (violet): `shadow-[0_0_30px_rgba(167,139,250,0.15)]`

## Image Style
- Images are full-bleed, rounded, and use `object-cover`
- Hero uses a large background image with dark overlay
- Gallery uses a masonry-style or grid layout with many images
- Images should feel scientific: microscopy, cells, organisms, nature close-ups

## Do's
- Use dark backgrounds throughout
- Use vivid cyan, violet, and emerald accents for headings and highlights
- Use large, full-width images in hero and gallery sections
- Use subtle glows and gradients to add depth
- Keep text clearly readable (light text on dark backgrounds)

## Don'ts
- No light/white backgrounds
- No low-contrast text (e.g., gray on gray)
- No cluttered layouts — let images breathe
- No sans-serif fonts other than Inter
