# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- Base (cream): `#FBF7F0` → `bg-velmora-cream`
- Sand: `#F0EBE3` → `bg-velmora-sand`
- Surface: `#F6F2EC` → `bg-velmora-surface`
- Charcoal (text): `#1C1C1C` → `text-velmora-charcoal`
- Smoke (body text): `#3D3D3D` → `text-velmora-smoke`
- Stone (muted): `#6B6B6B` → `text-velmora-stone`
- Mist (subtle): `#A8A8A8` → `text-velmora-mist`
- Gold accent: `#C8A96E` → `bg-velmora-gold`
- Gold light: `#E8D5A3` → `bg-velmora-goldLight`
- Gold dark: `#9B7D4A` → `bg-velmora-goldDark`
- Border: `#E5E0D8` → `border-velmora-border`

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — `font-serif`
- Body/UI: Inter (sans-serif) — `font-sans`
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`)
- Headings use `tracking-tight` for elegance

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: sections use `py-20 md:py-28 lg:py-32`
- Cards use `p-6 md:p-8`
- Hairline dividers: `border-t border-velmora-border`

## Components
- Buttons: uppercase, medium weight, wide tracking
- Primary: dark bg, cream text
- Accent: gold bg, white text
- Outline: transparent, charcoal border
- Cards: subtle hover shadow, smooth transitions
- No rounded corners on product cards (minimalist)

## Do
- Use velmora-* color tokens
- UPPERCASE for product names
- Serif for all headings
- Hairline borders
- Subtle hover transitions (300ms)
- Keep backgrounds warm and neutral

## Don't
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy shadows or glossy effects
- No rounded luxury-style badges
- No black backgrounds
- No discount-looking elements
