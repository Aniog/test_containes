# ARTITECT MACHINERY Design System

## Visual Direction
Create a premium industrial website that feels elegant, calm, and easy to navigate. The brand should feel precise, dependable, and refined rather than aggressive or overly technical.

## Typography
- Primary font: Inter
- Headings: `font-semibold tracking-tight text-brand-ink`
- Body text: `text-base md:text-lg leading-7 text-brand-slate`
- Eyebrows and labels: `text-xs md:text-sm font-semibold uppercase tracking-widest text-brand-bronze`

## Color Palette
Add these named colors to Tailwind and use the names instead of raw hex values in class strings.
- `brand-ink`: `#16202E`
- `brand-slate`: `#556274`
- `brand-steel`: `#A8B3C1`
- `brand-mist`: `#E8EDF3`
- `brand-ivory`: `#F7F4EE`
- `brand-bronze`: `#B88A52`
- `brand-white`: `#FFFFFF`

## Surfaces
- Main page background: `bg-brand-ivory`
- Cards: `bg-white border border-brand-ink/10 shadow-lg shadow-brand-ink/5`
- Dark premium sections: `bg-brand-ink text-brand-white`
- Soft highlight surfaces: `bg-brand-mist`

## Layout
- Keep content wide and spacious: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 md:px-8`
- Use generous section spacing: `py-20 md:py-28`
- Prefer clean 2-column layouts on desktop and stacked layouts on mobile
- Use rounded surfaces: `rounded-3xl`

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-brand-ink text-brand-white hover:bg-brand-slate`
- Secondary: `border border-brand-ink/15 bg-white text-brand-ink hover:bg-brand-mist`
- Accent links and small highlights: `text-brand-bronze`

## Iconography
- Use simple line icons from Lucide
- Keep icons medium sized and paired with concise text

## Do
- Keep every section readable with strong contrast
- Use premium spacing and restrained accents
- Emphasize product clarity, reliability, and operator-friendly design
- Make calls to action visible without looking pushy

## Don't
- Do not use neon colors or heavy gradients
- Do not crowd the page with dense technical tables
- Do not use low-contrast gray text on light cards
- Do not mix many accent colors in one section
