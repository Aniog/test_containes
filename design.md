# Design System: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Color Palette**:
  - **Base**: Deep Charcoal / Cocoa (#1A1A1A / #2C2421)
  - **Primary**: Cream / Soft Sand (#F9F7F2) - Main background for editorial feel.
  - **Accent**: Muted Gold (#C5A059) - For CTAs and highlights.
  - **Secondary**: Stone Gray (#E5E5E5) - For dividers and subtle backgrounds.
  - **Contrast**: High contrast for readability, ensuring accessibility.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - Usage: Product names, Page titles, Section headers.
  - Weights: 400, 500, 600.
  - Style: UPPERCASE for product titles with letter-spacing `tracking-[0.2em]`.
- **Body & UI**: Manrope (Sans-serif)
  - Usage: Descriptions, Navigation, Buttons, Form elements.
  - Weights: 400, 500, 600.

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Muted Gold with Cream text, or Solid Deep Charcoal.
  - Secondary: Outlined Deep Charcoal.
  - Hover: Subtle opacity change or slight shift.
- **Dividers**: 1px Hairline dividers (`bg-stone-200` or `opacity-20`).
- **Shadows**: Soft, diffused shadows for cards and drawers.
- **Imagery**: Large editorial shots, 3:4 or 1:1 ratios, warm lighting.

## Layout
- **Containers**: Generous padding and whitespace.
- **Mobile-first**: Responsive grids (1 col mobile, 2 col tablet, 4-5 col desktop).
- **Transitions**: Smooth 300ms ease-in-out for all hover states.
