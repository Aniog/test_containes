# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Visual Identity Guidelines

## Brand Overview
- **Brand Name**: Velmora Fine Jewelry
- **Type**: Direct-to-consumer (B2C) demi-fine jewelry store
- **Target Audience**: Women 25-45, gifting and self-purchase
- **Price Range**: $30–$120 (premium-but-accessible)

## Mood & Aesthetic
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- **Style**: Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: `#0D0D0D` (rich black)
- **Secondary Background**: `#1A1A1A` (soft black)
- **Card Background**: `#242424` (charcoal)
- **Primary Text**: `#F5F5F0` (warm white)
- **Secondary Text**: `#A8A8A0` (muted warm gray)
- **Accent Color**: `#C9A962` (warm gold)
- **Accent Hover**: `#D4B978` (lighter gold)
- **Border Color**: `#333333` (subtle dark gray)
- **Success**: `#4A7C59` (muted green)
- **Error**: `#B85450` (muted red)

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: 
  - Font: "Cormorant Garamond", serif
  - Weight: 500-600
  - Letter-spacing: 0.05em (wide)
  - Transform: UPPERCASE for product names
  
- **Body & UI**:
  - Font: "Inter", sans-serif
  - Weight: 400-500
  - Letter-spacing: normal

- **Font Sizes**:
  - Hero headline: 3.5rem (56px) desktop, 2.5rem mobile
  - Section titles: 2rem (32px)
  - Product names: 0.875rem (14px)
  - Body: 1rem (16px)
  - Small/Caption: 0.875rem (14px)

## Spacing System
- **Section Padding**: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Container Max Width**: 1400px
- **Grid Gap**: 24px (desktop), 16px (mobile)
- **Component Padding**: 24px

## Visual Elements
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline (1px) in border color
- **Shadows**: Soft, subtle — `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.3)`
- **Border Radius**: 0px for buttons (sharp), 4px for cards
- **Hover Transitions**: 300ms ease, subtle transforms

## Component Styles

### Buttons
- **Primary (Accent)**:
  - Background: `#C9A962`
  - Text: `#0D0D0D`
  - Padding: 16px 32px
  - Border-radius: 0
  - Hover: `#D4B978`, slight scale
  
- **Secondary (Outline)**:
  - Background: transparent
  - Border: 1px solid `#C9A962`
  - Text: `#C9A962`
  - Hover: fill with accent

### Cards
- Background: `#1A1A1A`
- Border: 1px solid `#333333`
- Hover: subtle lift, reveal second image

### Form Inputs
- Background: `#1A1A1A`
- Border: 1px solid `#333333`
- Focus: border-color `#C9A962`
- Text: `#F5F5F0`

## Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: < 640px
- **Tablet**: 640px - 1024px
- **Desktop**: > 1024px

## Animations
- **Page Load**: Fade in (opacity 0 to 1, 500ms)
- **Hover Effects**: 300ms ease transitions
- **Scroll Reveals**: Subtle fade-up for sections
- **Cart Drawer**: Slide in from right (300ms)

## Do's and Don'ts
- ✅ Use generous whitespace
- ✅ Large editorial imagery
- ✅ Thin hairline dividers
- ✅ Restrained accent color
- ✅ Subtle hover transitions
- ✅ Soft shadows
- ✅ Premium button feel
- ❌ Don't use loud colors
- ❌ Don't create discount-looking layouts
- ❌ Avoid generic e-commerce patterns
- ❌ Don't overcrowd elements
