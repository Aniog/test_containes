# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background (cream):** `#FAF7F2` — warm off-white page background
- **Foreground (charcoal):** `#1A1A1A` — primary text
- **Accent (gold):** `#B8860B` — warm dark gold for CTAs, highlights, accents
- **Accent light:** `#D4A853` — lighter gold for hover states
- **Muted:** `#6B6B6B` — secondary text, captions
- **Muted light:** `#F5F0E8` — subtle section backgrounds
- **Border:** `#E8E2D9` — hairline dividers
- **Card:** `#FFFFFF` — card surfaces

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names:** `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. Product names in UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.2em]`.
- **Body / UI:** `Inter`, sans-serif.
- **Heading sizes:** Hero: `text-5xl md:text-7xl`, Section: `text-3xl md:text-4xl`, Card: `text-lg`
- **Body:** `text-sm md:text-base`, line-height relaxed

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections use `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8`
- Cards: `rounded-none` or very subtle `rounded-sm`
- Thin hairline dividers: `border-t border-border`

## Components
- **Buttons:** Solid accent background with cream text, or outlined with accent border. `px-8 py-3 text-sm tracking-widest uppercase font-sans`. Hover: slightly lighter gold.
- **Cards:** No border-radius, subtle shadow on hover `shadow-sm hover:shadow-md transition-shadow`
- **Hover transitions:** `transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out`
- **Images:** Object-cover, aspect ratios maintained

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography hierarchy clear (serif headings, sans body)
- Use gold accent sparingly — CTAs, stars, highlights
- Maintain warm, editorial feel
- Use thin 1px borders for separation

## Don'ts
- No rounded corners on cards/images (keep editorial/sharp)
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy shadows
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/sale-style badges
- No generic e-commerce feel
