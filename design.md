# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry:** Industrial sheet metal folding machines
- **Tone:** Elegant, professional, trustworthy, user-friendly

## Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep navy `#1a2744` — authority, trust
- **Primary Light:** `#2a3f66` — hover states, accents
- **Accent:** Warm copper/gold `#c8873e` — elegance, premium quality
- **Accent Light:** `#d9a05e` — hover states
- **Background:** Clean white `#ffffff`
- **Surface:** Light gray `#f7f8fa`
- **Text Primary:** Dark charcoal `#1a1a2e`
- **Text Secondary:** Medium gray `#5a6275`
- **Text Muted:** `#8a92a6`
- **Border:** `#e2e5eb`

## Typography
- **Headings:** Inter, weight 700-800, tracking tight
- **Body:** Inter, weight 400-500
- **Accent text:** Inter, weight 600

## Tailwind Classes Reference
- Headings: `font-bold tracking-tight text-[#1a1a2e]`
- Body: `text-[#5a6275] leading-relaxed`
- Primary button: `bg-[#1a2744] text-white hover:bg-[#2a3f66]`
- Accent button: `bg-[#c8873e] text-white hover:bg-[#d9a05e]`
- Cards: `bg-white rounded-2xl shadow-sm border border-[#e2e5eb]`
- Section backgrounds: alternate between `bg-white` and `bg-[#f7f8fa]`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 lg:py-28`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-8`
- Gap between grid items: `gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: `rounded-2xl shadow-sm border border-[#e2e5eb]`
- Buttons: `rounded-lg`
- Hover shadow: `hover:shadow-md transition-shadow`

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep text readable with high contrast
- Use copper/gold accent sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Maintain consistent section rhythm
- Use subtle transitions on interactive elements

## Don'ts
- Don't use dark backgrounds for large sections (keep it light and open)
- Don't use more than 2 accent colors
- Don't crowd elements — maintain breathing room
- Don't use thin/light font weights for important text
