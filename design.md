# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Overall direction
Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial aesthetic with a deep espresso base, warm ivory surfaces, and restrained champagne-gold accents. The mood should feel warm, refined, intimate, and premium-but-accessible.

## Color system
- Background base: deep espresso / near-black brown for hero, navigation overlays, footer, and rich sections.
- Surface: warm ivory / porcelain for cards, forms, and product panels.
- Accent: muted champagne gold for buttons, borders, ratings, icons, and emphasis.
- Text primary on light surfaces: deep cocoa.
- Text primary on dark surfaces: soft ivory.
- Text secondary: warm taupe / muted stone with strong enough contrast.

Use named colors in Tailwind config instead of arbitrary hex values in class strings.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant and editorial.
- Body, navigation, controls, and metadata: Inter.
- Product names should be uppercase with wide tracking.
- Serif headings should use generous line-height and visual breathing room.

## Spacing and layout
- Use large vertical spacing between homepage sections.
- Keep content width refined and not overly wide.
- Use thin hairline dividers and subtle borders.
- Cards should have generous padding and soft shadows.
- Mobile-first layouts should stack cleanly, but desktop should expand into editorial multi-column compositions.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, slightly rounded, either solid champagne-gold with dark text or transparent with a fine ivory/gold border.
- Inputs: warm ivory surfaces with clear dark text and refined borders.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, then solid deep espresso on scroll.
- Product cards: minimal chrome, image-first, quick actions on hover, strong readable labels.
- Cart drawer: dark shell with ivory panels or text for clear contrast.

## Motion
- Use subtle transitions only: opacity, translate, shadow, border-color.
- Avoid bouncy or playful animation.

## Do
- Keep text contrast explicit on every surface.
- Maintain one cohesive palette across all pages.
- Favor whitespace and restraint over decoration.

## Don’t
- Do not use loud sale styling, bright discount colors, or generic marketplace visuals.
- Do not use low-contrast beige-on-beige text.
- Do not mix multiple accent colors.
