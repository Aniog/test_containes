# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Audience**: Women 25-45, gifting and self-purchase, $30-$120 price range.

## Color Palette

### Primary Backgrounds
- **Cream 50** (#FFFDF9) - Main page background
- **Cream 100** (#FEFCF5) - Card backgrounds
- **Cream 200** (#FDF8EE) - Hover states on light

### Charcoal (Text & UI)
- **Charcoal 800** (#1F1D1A) - Primary text
- **Charcoal 700** (#2D2926) - Headings
- **Charcoal 500** (#5A5550) - Secondary text
- **Charcoal 400** (#7A7670) - Muted text
- **Charcoal 200** (#D1CECC) - Borders/dividers

### Gold Accent
- **Gold 500** (#C4A265) - Primary accent, buttons, links
- **Gold 600** (#B8960B) - Hover accent
- **Gold 300** (#F0C87A) - Highlights, badges

### Stone (Subtle backgrounds)
- **Stone 100** (#F5F3F0) - Section alternation
- **Stone 200** (#EBE8E3) - Borders on dark

## Typography

### Headings (Serif)
- Font: Cormorant Garamond
- Weights: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semi-bold)
- Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.2em
- Section headings: Title case, letter-spacing: 0.05em

### Body (Sans-serif)
- Font: Inter
- Weights: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semi-bold)
- Body text: 16px, line-height 1.6
- Small text/UI: 14px
- Tracking: Normal for body, wider for buttons

## Spacing
- Section padding: 64px vertical on desktop, 48px on mobile
- Card gap: 24px
- Content max-width: 1280px

## Components

### Buttons
- Primary: Solid charcoal background, cream text, hover to gold
- Outline: Charcoal border, charcoal text, hover solid fill
- Gold: Solid gold background, white text

### Cards
- Clean white/cream background
- Subtle shadow on hover
- Thin hairline borders
- Generous internal padding

### Dividers
- Thin hairline (1px) in charcoal-100
- Used between sections and content blocks

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep imagery warm and editorial
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Maintain consistent gold accents
- Use smooth transitions (300ms)
- Keep mobile-first responsive design

## Don'ts
- Don't use harsh colors or gradients
- Don't use heavy shadows
- Don't use rounded corners larger than 4px
- Don't mix accent colors
- Don't use decorative fonts for body text
