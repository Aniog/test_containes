# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: cream (#FAF7F2) — warm off-white, used sitewide.
- **Text**: ink (#1A1510) — deep almost-black brown for body text.
- **Accent / Gold**: gold-500 (#C9941A) — warm muted gold for CTAs, accents, hover states.
- **Secondary accent**: velvet-700 (#6F5A45) — warm brown for secondary text, subtle borders.
- **Borders / Dividers**: velvet-200 (#E8DDD0) — thin hairline dividers.
- **Hover / Light fills**: velvet-100 (#F5EFE6) — subtle warm hover backgrounds.
- **White**: for cards, overlays, nav backgrounds on scroll.

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif). Product names in UPPERCASE with tracking-wider (0.12em).
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), 400 weight, 15px base.
- **Nav links**: Inter, 11px uppercase, tracking-widest.
- **Price**: Cormorant Garamond, italic, 500 weight.

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout. Section padding: py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-32.
- Max content width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6.
- Card gap: gap-6 md:gap-8.

## Borders
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-velvet-200.
- Card borders: none (borderless editorial look), subtle shadow on hover: hover:shadow-lg.
- Buttons: 1px border, rounded-none (sharp edges for luxury feel).

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-gold-500 text-cream, hover:bg-gold-600.
- Outline: border border-ink text-ink, hover:bg-ink hover:text-cream.
- Both: uppercase, tracking-wider, text-sm, px-8 py-3, rounded-none.

## Transitions
- All interactive: transition-all duration-300 ease-out.
- Hover: subtle scale or opacity shift on images.

## Image Style
- Editorial, warm-lit, dark/neutral backgrounds.
- Product images: 1:1 square, with subtle shadow.
- Hero: full-bleed, warm lighting.
