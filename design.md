# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry for women 25–45. No loud discount cues, no generic marketplace styling.

## Color palette
- Background primary: warm off-white `#F9F7F2`
- Background secondary: soft cream `#F2EFE9`
- Surface/hero dark: deep espresso `#2A211C`
- Text primary: near-black `#1A1614`
- Text secondary: warm gray `#6B625B`
- Accent: antique gold `#B9975B`
- Accent hover: muted gold `#A8864D`
- Divider: hairline warm gray `#E2DDD5`
- Error: muted terracotta `#B85C4E`
- Success: sage `#6B8E6E`

## Typography
- Headings & product names: Cormorant Garamond (serif), 300–600.
- Product names are UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.18em]` letter-spacing.
- Body & UI: Inter, weights 300–500.

## Spacing
- Section vertical padding: `py-20 md:py-28`.
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`.
- Generous whitespace; avoid cramped layouts.

## Components
- Buttons: solid accent with cream text, or outlined accent on cream; pill shape `rounded-none` with `tracking-widest` uppercase labels.
- Cards: subtle warm shadow, hover lift `-translate-y-1` and shadow deepen.
- Dividers: 1px `border-[#E2DDD5]` hairlines.
- Inputs: bottom border only or full bordered; focus ring accent.
- Icons: thin-stroke Lucide at 1.5px, sizes 18–24.

## Imagery
- Warm gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds.
- Use stock-image tags for all photos; placeholder SVGs until loaded.
- Hero full-bleed; product grid 4:3 or 3:4; UGC 9:16.

## Motion
- Subtle transitions: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`.
- Nav background transition on scroll.
- Image crossfade on product hover.
- Cart drawer slides from right.

## Accessibility
- Minimum 4.5:1 contrast for body text.
- Focus-visible rings on interactive elements.
- Alt text on all images.
