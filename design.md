# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Cream** (#FBF8F4): Page background, card backgrounds, negative space
- **Truffle** (#2C2420): Body text, headings, primary buttons, nav solid background
- **Gold** (#C8A96E): Accent buttons, decorative lines, icon highlights, hover states
- **Blush** (#D4A99C): Secondary accent, testimonial backgrounds, newsletter block

Do's:
- Use cream as the dominant background
- Gold only as accent, never as background fill for large areas
- Truffle for text and primary UI
- Blush sparingly for warmth

Don'ts:
- NO pure black (#000)
- NO pure white (#FFF) — use cream instead
- NO neons, NO discount reds, NO sale badges
- NO heavy borders or box shadows

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em–0.25em
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), 14px–16px, line-height 1.6
- **Hero headlines**: 3rem–5rem, font-light or font-normal
- **Section titles**: 2rem–2.5rem, font-normal, uppercase tracking-wider

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 md:px-10
- Card gaps: 6–8 on mobile, 8–10 on desktop

## Imagery
- Warm, editorial photography of gold jewelry on models
- Dark/neutral backgrounds to flatter gold tones
- 4:5 or 3:4 aspect ratios for product cards

## UI Elements
- Buttons: subtle hover transitions, rounded-none (sharp edges feel premium)
- Dividers: 1px hairline, truffle at 20% opacity
- Cards: no border, subtle shadow on hover only
- Inputs: underline style, minimal
