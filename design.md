# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Colors
- Base:      #FCFAF7 (warm cream)
- Surface:   #FFFFFF (white)
- Muted:     #F5F2ED (warm stone)
- Border:    #E8E3DC (hairline)
- Text:      #1C1917 (warm deep brown-black)
- Secondary: #78716C (warm gray)
- Accent:    #A68A56 (bronze gold)
- AccentHov: #8B6F3F (darker gold)

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif), uppercase for product names, wide tracking
- Body: Inter (sans-serif)
- Product names: uppercase, tracking-[0.15em]

## Spacing
Generous whitespace. Sections: py-20 to py-28.
Hairline dividers: border-t border-border.
Card gaps: 6-8 on desktop, 4 on mobile.

## Components
- Buttons: solid accent bg, white text, rounded-full or rounded, smooth hover
- Cards: subtle shadow on hover, transition-all duration-300
- Nav: transparent -> white bg on scroll, backdrop-blur
