# SSourcing China Design System

## Visual Identity
- Clean, professional, trustworthy B2B aesthetic
- Color palette: Deep navy (#1e3a8a) as primary, white/light gray backgrounds, orange accent (#f97316) for CTAs
- Inter font family throughout
- Generous whitespace, clear hierarchy

## Typography
- Headings: font-bold to font-extrabost, text-3xl to text-5xl
- Body: font-normal, text-base (16px), text-gray-600
- Small text: text-sm, text-gray-500

## Colors
- Primary: brand-500 (#1e40af) - navy blue
- Primary Dark: brand-700 (#172554) - dark navy
- Accent: accent-500 (#f97316) - orange for CTAs
- Background: white, gray-50 for alternating sections
- Text: gray-900 for headings, gray-600 for body, gray-400 for muted

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl with px-4 md:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 to p-8

## Components
- Cards: white bg, subtle shadow, rounded-xl, hover lift effect
- Buttons: rounded-lg, font-semibold, transition colors
- Primary CTA: bg-accent-500 hover:bg-accent-600 text-white
- Secondary CTA: bg-brand-500 hover:bg-brand-600 text-white

## Layout
- Max width container: max-w-7xl mx-auto
- Grid layouts: grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3
- Navigation: sticky, white bg with shadow on scroll

## Do's
- Use consistent spacing across sections
- Maintain clear visual hierarchy
- Keep text readable with sufficient contrast
- Use stock images for factory/QC/shipping visuals

## Don'ts
- Don't use excessive colors or gradients
- Don't overcrowd sections
- Don't use low-contrast text
- Don't make exaggerated claims
