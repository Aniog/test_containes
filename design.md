# Design System: MicroCosmos

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Playfair Display', serif (Sophisticated, elegant)
- **Body**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Clean, readable)

## Colors
- **Primary**: Emerald 400 (#34d399) - Symbolizes growth and life at a micro level.
- **Secondary**: Slate 400 (#94a3b8) - For descriptions and less important text.
- **Background**: Slate 900 (#0f172a) - Dark, mysterious background to make vibrant micro-photographs pop.
- **Accent**: Violet 500 (#8b5cf6) - For highlights and interesting details.

## Spacing
- Standard Tailwind spacing (p-4, m-4, gap-4).
- Generous padding for sections (py-20).

## Visual Style
- **Cards**: Dark Slate background with subtle borders and shadows.
- **Images**: High-quality photography with rounded corners and hover effects.
- **Micro-interactions**: Gentle transitions and scale effects on hover.

## Best Practices
- High contrast for readability.
- Clear hierarchy with font sizes and weights.
- Responsive layouts using Tailwind grid and flex.
