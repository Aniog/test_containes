# Velmora Fine Jewelry Visual System

Velmora uses a quiet luxury editorial direction: warm ivory surfaces, deep espresso text, muted clay accents, and soft champagne metallic details. The design should feel premium and intimate rather than promotional.

## Palette
- Foundation: warm ivory (`velmora-ivory`, example `bg-velmora-ivory`) for most pages and cards.
- Deep contrast: espresso (`velmora-espresso`, example `text-velmora-espresso`) for primary text, nav, buttons, and footer surfaces.
- Soft surfaces: pearl (`velmora-pearl`) and porcelain (`velmora-porcelain`) for subtle panels and secondary blocks.
- Metallic accent: champagne (`velmora-champagne`) for premium CTAs, borders, icons, and small highlights.
- Earth accent: clay (`velmora-clay`) for warm callouts and newsletter areas.
- Muted copy: taupe (`velmora-taupe`) for readable secondary text.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond, elegant serif, generous line height, refined tracking.
- Body and UI: Manrope, clean sans-serif, readable and restrained.
- Product names are uppercase with wide letter-spacing (`uppercase tracking-[0.22em]` or `tracking-widest`).

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first with generous vertical breathing room (`py-16 md:py-24`).
- Desktop sections use centered max-width containers (`max-w-7xl mx-auto px-5 sm:px-8`).
- Use hairline dividers (`border-velmora-espresso/10`) and subtle rounded cards.
- Desktop should use multi-column editorial layouts; mobile stacks cleanly.

## Components
- Buttons: premium, restrained, either espresso filled with ivory text or champagne filled with espresso text. Use pill shapes or slightly rounded rectangles, never loud discount styling.
- Cards: soft ivory/pearl surfaces, thin borders, understated shadows (`shadow-soft`).
- Imagery: warm gold jewelry on model, dark neutral or soft editorial backgrounds. Use large full-bleed hero images and refined product crops.
- Animations: subtle hover lift, opacity fades, image scale no more than 105%.

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and all text readable.
- Use gold accents sparingly.
- Keep the experience elegant, warm, premium, and editorial.

## Do not
- Do not use bright sale colors, discount badges, neon tones, or generic marketplace layouts.
- Do not overcrowd product grids or use heavy shadows.
- Do not use low-contrast taupe text on dark backgrounds.
