# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Base (deep warm charcoal):** #1C1917 (stone-950) — primary background for hero/dark sections
- **Cream (warm off-white):** #FAF7F2 — primary light background
- **Ivory:** #F5F0EB — card backgrounds, subtle surfaces
- **Gold Accent:** #C9A96E — primary accent, CTAs, highlights
- **Gold Hover:** #B8944F — darker gold for hover states
- **Text Primary:** #1C1917 — on light backgrounds
- **Text Secondary:** #78716C (stone-500) — muted text, descriptions
- **Text on Dark:** #FAF7F2 — on dark backgrounds
- **Divider:** #E7E0D8 — hairline dividers

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond (serif), weights 400/500/600
- **Body/UI:** Inter (sans-serif), weights 400/500/600
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond
- **Section headings:** Cormorant Garamond, tracking wide

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section padding: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Card padding: 24px
- Grid gaps: 24px (product cards), 32px (sections)

## Components
- **Buttons:** Solid gold (#C9A96E) with cream text, or outlined with gold border. Rounded-none or rounded-sm. Min height 48px. Hover: darken gold. Transition 200ms.
- **Cards:** Ivory background, subtle shadow on hover. No border radius (or 2px max).
- **Dividers:** 1px, #E7E0D8, full width
- **Inputs:** Cream background, stone border, gold focus ring

## Interactions
- Subtle hover transitions (200-300ms ease)
- Image hover: slight scale (1.02) or reveal second image
- Soft shadows: shadow-sm default, shadow-md on hover
- No aggressive animations

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Use uppercase + wide tracking for product names
- Keep accent color (gold) restrained — for CTAs and highlights only
- Ensure strong contrast on all text

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/saturated colors
- Don't use rounded corners > 4px
- Don't use heavy shadows
- Don't mix too many font weights
- Don't use gold as a background for large areas
