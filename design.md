# Design System: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Palette
- **Primary (Background)**: `#FAF9F6` (White Linen - soft, editorial neutral)
- **Secondary (Base)**: `#1A1A1A` (Rich Black - for high contrast text and buttons)
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#C4A484` (Warm Gold/Bronze - for subtle accents and highlights)
- **Muted**: `#E5E4E2` (Platinum Gray - for borders and hairlines)
- **Overlay**: `rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05)` (Soft shadows and subtle hovers)

## Typography
- **Headings/Serif**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. Used for elegance and brand name.
  - Class: `font-serif`
- **Body/Sans-serif**: `Inter`, sans-serif. Used for readability and UI.
  - Class: `font-sans`
- **Product Names**: Uppercase, wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).

## Components & UI
- **Buttons**:
  - Solid: `bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white hover:bg-[#333333] transition-all`
  - Outline: `border border-[#1A1A1A] text-[#1A1A1A] hover:bg-[#1A1A1A] hover:text-white transition-all`
- **Dividers**: Hairline (`border-t-[0.5px] border-[#E5E4E2]`)
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace. Vertical sections: `py-20` to `py-32`.
- **Transitions**: Ease-in-out duration 300ms.

## Do's
- Use high-quality, warm-lit editorial imagery.
- Keep the layout spacious and balanced.
- Use hairline dividers for a refined look.
- Use wide letter-spacing for uppercase titles.

## Don'ts
- No loud or bright colors.
- No heavy shadows or rounded corners (prefer sharp or very slightly rounded).
- No discount-looking badges.
- No cluttered grids.
