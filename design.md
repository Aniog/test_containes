# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design Guidelines

## Brand Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry. Premium but accessible.

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - Style: Elegant, sophisticated.
  - Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).
- **Body & UI**: Manrope (Sans-serif)
  - Style: Clean, modern, highly legible.

## Color Palette
- **Primary/Background**: `#FAF9F6` (Alabaster - soft cream editorial look)
- **Foreground/Text**: `#121212` (Onyx - rich black for high contrast)
- **Accent**: `#BFA169` (Velmora Gold - warm metallic accent for jewelry reference)
- **Secondary Text**: `#555555` (Slate Gray - for descriptions and secondary UI)
- **Muted/Borders**: `#E2E2E2` (Light Gray - for hairline dividers)
- **Success**: `#4A6741` (Deep Sage)
- **Error**: `#A34A4A` (Soft Terracotta)

## Layout & Spacing
- **Whitespace**: Generous, editorial feel.
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline borders (`1px`).
- **Cards**: Soft shadows (`shadow-sm` or none) with clean edges.
- **Transitions**: Subtle hover effects (e.g., image zoom, opacity changes).

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Accent (`#BFA169`) or Solid Onyx (`#121212`) with white text.
  - Secondary: Outlined/Ghost with hairline borders.
- **Navigation**: Sticky, transparent behavior, transitioning to solid on scroll.
- **Components**: Utilize shadcn UI for consistency.

## Images
- Large, editorial close-ups.
- Warm-lit jewelry on models or clean neutral backgrounds.
- 9:16 aspect ratio for UGC reels.
- 1:1 or 3:2 for product listings.
