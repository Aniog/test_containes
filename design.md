# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.
- Target: women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase, $30–$120 price point.

## Color Palette
- **Cream (primary bg)**: #F5F0EB — warm off-white, main page background
- **Charcoal (dark bg/text)**: #1C1917 — warm deep brown-black, hero/footer backgrounds, primary text
- **Gold Accent**: #C9A96E — warm metallic gold, buttons, highlights, links
- **Gold Hover**: #B8944F — darker gold for hover states
- **Gold Light**: #E8D5B0 — soft gold for subtle backgrounds
- **Warm Gray**: #6B5E52 — secondary text on light backgrounds
- **Muted**: #9C8E82 — muted text, placeholders
- **Border**: #E5DDD3 — warm hairline borders
- **Card White**: #FAF7F3 — slightly warm white for cards
- **Pure White**: #FFFFFF — for high-contrast elements

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond, serif. Elegant, refined.
- **Body/UI**: Inter, sans-serif. Clean, modern.
- **Product Names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond.
- **Logo**: "VELMORA" in Cormorant Garamond, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.25em.

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace. Sections separated by 80–120px vertical padding.
- Hairline dividers (1px, color: border #E5DDD3).
- Max content width: 1280px, centered.
- Mobile-first responsive design.

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold (#C9A96E) bg, white text, rounded-none or slight rounded, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em.
- Secondary: outlined (1px gold border), gold text, transparent bg.
- Hover: gold darkens, subtle transition 0.3s.

## Cards & Shadows
- Product cards: minimal shadow, warm border on hover.
- Soft shadows: shadow-sm with warm tones.
- Hover: second image reveal, slight scale or shadow increase.

## Images
- Large editorial imagery. Warm-lit, gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds.
- Hero: full-bleed, warm close-up.
- Product: clean, centered on neutral bg.

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth. Transition durations: 0.3s ease.
- No jarring animations. Hover reveals, soft fades.
- Nav: transparent → solid on scroll.

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Use uppercase + wide letter-spacing for product names
- Keep gold accent restrained — buttons, links, small highlights only
- Use hairline dividers between sections

## Don'ts
- Don't use loud/bright colors
- Don't use generic e-commerce styling
- Don't crowd elements — give everything room
- Don't use heavy shadows or thick borders
- Don't mix too many font styles
