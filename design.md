# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#faf8f5` (warm off-white / ivory)
- **Surface / Card**: `#ffffff` (white)
- **Primary (accent)**: `#c9a84c` (warm gold / brushed brass)
- **Primary hover**: `#b8943a` (deeper gold)
- **Text primary**: `#1a1a1a` (near-black)
- **Text secondary**: `#6b6258` (warm taupe)
- **Text muted**: `#9a9085` (light taupe)
- **Border / Divider**: `#e5ded6` (warm sand)
- **Footer bg**: `#1a1a1a` (dark)
- **Footer text**: `#d4cfc9` (warm light)
- **Hero overlay**: `#1a1a1a` at 40% opacity

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif
  - Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: `tracking-[0.12em]`
  - Hero heading: `text-5xl` to `text-7xl`, font-light
- **Body / UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif, `font-light` or `font-normal`

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card gap: `gap-6 md:gap-8`
- Element spacing: `space-y-3` or `space-y-4`

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: `border-t border-[#e5ded6]`
- Soft shadows: `shadow-sm` on cards, `shadow-md` on hover
- Rounded: `rounded-sm` on cards, `rounded-full` on pills

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold (`bg-[#c9a84c]`), white text, `px-8 py-3`, uppercase, `tracking-[0.08em]`, `text-sm`, `hover:bg-[#b8943a]` transition
- Outline: `border border-[#c9a84c] text-[#c9a84c]` hover fill
- Subtle hover transition: `transition-all duration-300`

## Interactive States
- Hover: `opacity-90` scale effect on images, `transition-transform duration-500`
- Nav: transparent on hero, solid white with shadow on scroll
- Links: underline on hover with `transition-all`

## Layout
- Sticky nav, full-bleed hero, sections with generous padding
- Product grid: 5 columns on desktop, 3 on tablet, 2 on mobile
- Mobile-first responsive design

## Do's
- Use warm off-white backgrounds
- Gold accents sparingly
- Thin, elegant dividers
- Uppercase wide-spaced product names
- Generous whitespace
- Soft shadows and transitions
- Large editorial imagery

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright colors or loud accents
- Don't use heavy borders
- Don't crowd elements
- Don't use generic e-commerce fonts
- Don't use discount/vibe-y messaging
