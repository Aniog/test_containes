# SSourcing China - Design System

## Brand
- Primary: Navy (#1a365d / navy-800) — trust, professionalism
- Accent: Gold (#d4951f / gold-500) — quality, premium
- Background: White (#ffffff) with warm gray (#f8fafc) alternate sections
- Text: Slate gray (#1e293b) for body, navy for headings

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: font-bold, navy-900
- Body: font-normal, text-slate-700
- Sizes: h1=text-5xl, h2=text-3xl/4xl, h3=text-2xl, body=text-base/leading-relaxed

## Components
- Buttons: rounded-lg, navy-800 bg with white text, gold-500 bg for CTA
- Cards: bg-white, rounded-xl, shadow-sm, border border-slate-100, p-8
- Section spacing: py-16 md:py-24
- Container: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8

## Visual Style
- Clean, modern, international B2B
- Use stock images for factory/QC/shipping scenes via data-strk-img
- Icons from Lucide React
- No exaggerated gradients or animations
- Professional color palette with strong contrast
