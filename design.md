# MicroCosmos Design System — Flat Fresh Edition

## Brand Identity
MicroCosmos uses a flat, light, and fresh visual style. Clean white surfaces, soft pastel accents, no shadows, no gradients, no blur. Inspired by nature's gentle palette — mint, sky, coral, and sage.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** `#F5FAF8` — very light mint-white (bg-[#F5FAF8])
- **White surface:** `#FFFFFF` — pure white cards (bg-white)
- **Text primary:** `#2C3E50` — dark blue-gray (text-[#2C3E50])
- **Text secondary:** `#7F8C8D` — medium gray (text-[#7F8C8D])
- **Mint (primary):** `#3DBFA8` — fresh teal-mint (bg-[#3DBFA8], text-[#3DBFA8])
- **Sky blue:** `#5BA4CF` — soft sky blue (bg-[#5BA4CF], text-[#5BA4CF])
- **Coral:** `#FF8B64` — soft coral/orange (bg-[#FF8B64], text-[#FF8B64])
- **Yellow:** `#FFD166` — soft warm yellow (bg-[#FFD166])
- **Lavender:** `#9B8EC4` — soft purple (bg-[#9B8EC4], text-[#9B8EC4])
- **Green:** `#6BCB77` — fresh green (bg-[#6BCB77], text-[#6BCB77])
- **Border:** `#D9EDE8` — light mint border (border-[#D9EDE8])
- **Border dark:** `#B2CFC8` — slightly darker mint border

## Typography
- **Font family:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Display headings (h1):** `text-5xl md:text-6xl font-black tracking-tight text-[#2C3E50]`
- **Section headings (h2):** `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold text-[#2C3E50]`
- **Card headings (h3):** `text-lg font-bold text-[#2C3E50]`
- **Body text:** `text-base leading-relaxed text-[#7F8C8D]`
- **Label / tag:** `text-xs font-bold uppercase tracking-widest`

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Section vertical padding: `py-20 md:py-24`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Grid gaps: `gap-5 md:gap-6`

## Components

### Navigation
- Fixed top bar, white background, light bottom border: `fixed top-0 w-full bg-white border-b border-[#D9EDE8] z-50`
- Logo: bold mint + dark text
- Nav links: `text-[#7F8C8D] hover:text-[#2C3E50] font-medium transition-colors`
- Active link: `text-[#3DBFA8] font-semibold`

### Buttons
- **Primary:** `bg-[#3DBFA8] text-white font-semibold px-6 py-2.5 rounded-lg hover:bg-[#2CA898] transition-colors`
- **Secondary:** `bg-white text-[#2C3E50] font-semibold px-6 py-2.5 rounded-lg border border-[#D9EDE8] hover:bg-[#F5FAF8] transition-colors`
- **Accent yellow:** `bg-[#FFD166] text-[#2C3E50] font-semibold px-6 py-2.5 rounded-lg hover:bg-[#FFC233] transition-colors`

### Cards
- Background: `bg-white border border-[#D9EDE8] rounded-xl`
- Hover: `hover:border-[#3DBFA8] transition-colors`
- No shadows — flat appearance
- Colored top accent strip for category

### Badges / Tags
- Mint: `bg-[#E8F7F4] text-[#3DBFA8] text-xs font-bold px-3 py-1 rounded-md`
- Blue: `bg-[#EAF4FB] text-[#5BA4CF] text-xs font-bold px-3 py-1 rounded-md`
- Coral: `bg-[#FFF0EB] text-[#FF8B64] text-xs font-bold px-3 py-1 rounded-md`

### Dividers
- `border-t border-[#D9EDE8]` — light mint dividers

## Do's
- Use soft pastel fills for accent elements
- Keep backgrounds white or very light mint
- Use rounded-lg / rounded-xl for cards and buttons
- Use light borders (not bold black)
- Keep plenty of whitespace

## Don'ts
- No box-shadow of any kind
- No backdrop-blur or filter effects
- No gradients
- No dark backgrounds
- No glow effects
