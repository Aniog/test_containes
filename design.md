# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Direction
Quiet luxury. Warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- `velvet` (#1A1410) — primary text, near-black warm base
- `espresso` (#2C2118) — dark backgrounds, nav solid
- `bark` (#4A3728) — secondary dark surfaces
- `gold` (#B8924A) — primary accent, CTAs, borders
- `gold-light` (#D4AF6E) — hover states, highlights
- `gold-pale` (#F0E0C0) — subtle tints
- `sand` (#C8A97E) — warm mid-tone, secondary accents
- `champagne` (#E8D5B0) — light warm surfaces
- `cream` (#FAF6F0) — page background
- `ivory` (#F5EFE6) — card backgrounds
- `blush` (#E8C9A8) — warm blush tint
- `stone` (#8B7355) — muted text, captions
- `mist` (#D4C4B0) — hairlines, borders, dividers

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names**: `font-serif` (Cormorant Garamond) — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI**: `font-sans` (Manrope) — clean, modern
- Product names: UPPERCASE, `tracking-widest2` (0.25em letter-spacing)
- Section labels: uppercase, `tracking-widest` (0.1em), small size, `font-sans`
- Hero headline: `font-serif text-5xl md:text-7xl font-light`

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace: sections use `py-20 md:py-28`
- Container: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 md:px-12`
- Cards: `p-6` internal padding

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairlines: `border-mist` (1px solid #D4C4B0)
- Card borders: `border border-mist`
- Accent borders: `border-gold`

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: `bg-gold text-cream hover:bg-gold-light` — solid gold
- Secondary: `border border-gold text-gold hover:bg-gold hover:text-cream` — outlined gold
- All buttons: `tracking-widest font-sans text-xs uppercase px-8 py-3 transition-all duration-300`

## Shadows
- Cards: `shadow-sm` with warm tint
- Hover: `shadow-md` lift effect
- Nav solid: `shadow-sm`

## Animations
- Hover transitions: `transition-all duration-300`
- Image hover: scale 1.05, `transition-transform duration-500`
- Page elements: fade-up on scroll (staggered)

## Do's
- Use serif for all product names, headlines, pull quotes
- Use generous whitespace — let the jewelry breathe
- Warm gold accents only — no cool blues or greens
- Thin hairline dividers between sections
- Large editorial imagery

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded corners on images (keep square/rectangular)
- No generic sans-serif headings
- No cluttered layouts
