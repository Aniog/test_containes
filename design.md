# Strikingly Generators Hub Visual Style

Use the Strikingly component-kit look for the `/generators` hub page.

## Typography
- Headings and buttons: Brandon Grotesque when available, fallback to Josefin Sans, then Poppins. Use weight 700, uppercase, line-height 1.2.
- Body: Open Sans, weight 400, 14px base, line-height 1.5.
- H1: 40-48px desktop, 28-32px mobile. H2: 26-32px desktop.

## Colors
- Brand purple: `#8159BB` for accents, outlines, badges, and simple icon strokes.
- AI gradient: `linear-gradient(90deg, #7671FF, #CB0C9F)` only for primary CTAs and the second H1 line.
- Body text: `#636972`.
- Section headings: `#4B5056`.
- Hero H1 first line: `#2E2E2F`.
- Card border: `#C6C9CD`.
- Divider: `#E2E4E7`.
- Light background: `#F4F6F8`.
- Default background: `#FFFFFF`.

## Components
- Buttons: 36px standard height, 44px large height, 4px radius, uppercase heading font. Filled buttons use the AI gradient with white text. Ghost buttons are transparent with a 1px brand-purple border and brand-purple text.
- Cards: white background, 1px `#C6C9CD` border, 3px radius, 20px padding. Hover changes border to brand purple with a subtle shadow and slight upward lift.
- Tags: 11px uppercase heading font, 2px by 6px padding, 3px radius, brand-purple text and border, no fill.

## Layout
- Maximum content width: 1200px centered.
- Use 20px gaps between cards and blocks.
- Use 40px vertical spacing between major sections.
- Hero uses 60-80px vertical padding and may include a very faint purple/pink wash.
- Responsive grids: 3 columns on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 on mobile.

## Do
- Keep the page clean, directory-like, and useful.
- Make all text readable against its background.
- Use semantic HTML and visible focus states.
- Use inline SVG for decorative illustrations and icons.

## Don't
- Do not use fake ratings, testimonials, counts, or social proof.
- Do not use dark text on gradient or filled buttons.
- Do not use tinted backgrounds outside the hero except the neutral light-gray directory/footer surfaces.
- Do not create bare link lists for generator entries.
