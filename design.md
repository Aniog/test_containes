# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
Industrial machinery brand that balances manufacturing precision with architectural elegance. Clean, confident, and approachable.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Deep Navy):** `#1a1f36` — headers, footer, primary buttons
- **Accent (Warm Gold):** `#c8963e` — CTAs, highlights, links
- **Surface (Warm White):** `#faf9f6` — page backgrounds
- **Card (White):** `#ffffff` — cards, content panels
- **Muted (Stone Gray):** `#e8e5df` — subtle borders, dividers
- **Text Primary:** `#1a1f36` — body text, headings
- **Text Secondary:** `#6b7280` — subtitles, muted text
- **Text On Dark:** `#f1f5f9` — text on navy/dark backgrounds

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts, already loaded)
- Headings: font-semibold or font-bold, tracking-tight
- Body: font-normal, leading-relaxed
- Size scale: text-sm (0.875rem), text-base (1rem), text-lg (1.125rem), text-xl (1.25rem), text-2xl (1.5rem), text-3xl (1.875rem), text-4xl (2.25rem), text-5xl (3rem)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-stone-200
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- Inputs: rounded-lg border border-stone-300 focus:ring-2 focus:ring-amber-500/30

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO use warm white backgrounds with navy text for clean readability
- DO use gold accents sparingly for CTAs and key highlights
- DO maintain generous whitespace for an elegant feel
- DON'T use harsh pure black or pure white
- DON'T use overly bright or neon colors
- DON'T crowd elements — let the design breathe
