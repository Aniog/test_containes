# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora Fine Jewelry should feel like quiet luxury: warm, editorial, intimate, and refined. The experience should feel premium-but-accessible for women shopping for themselves or gifting. Avoid loud promotions, discount-store energy, harsh contrast gimmicks, or cluttered ecommerce patterns.

## Palette
Commit to one dark editorial direction that flatters gold jewelry.

- Background: deep espresso plum (`bg-stone-950` with warm undertones)
- Surface: softened charcoal (`bg-stone-900` / `bg-stone-800`)
- Elevated cards: warm ink (`bg-neutral-900`)
- Primary text: soft ivory (`text-stone-100`)
- Secondary text: warm taupe (`text-stone-300` / `text-stone-400`)
- Hairlines/borders: translucent champagne (`border-stone-700/70`)
- Accent: muted antique gold (`text-amber-200`, `bg-amber-200`, `border-amber-200`)
- Accent contrast text: deep espresso (`text-stone-950`)

Always pair dark surfaces with explicit light foreground classes. Never leave important text color implicit.

## Typography
- Headings, logo, editorial pull quotes, product names: Cormorant Garamond
- Body, UI, labels, navigation, pricing, filters: Inter
- Product names should be uppercase with generous tracking (`tracking-[0.28em]` or close semantic utility)

## Layout and spacing
- Use generous vertical spacing: `py-16`, `py-20`, `py-24` on major sections
- Keep content width refined with `max-w-7xl` and comfortable padding like `px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-10`
- Use thin dividers, soft shadows, and rounded corners sparingly
- Desktop layouts should feel editorial and spacious; mobile should stay breathable, never cramped

## Component styling
- Buttons: premium, minimal, with either antique gold fills and dark text or translucent outlined styles with subtle hover lift
- Product cards: large imagery, understated metadata, light hover transforms, quick-add overlay only on hover/focus where space allows
- Navigation: transparent over hero, then solid dark glass surface on scroll
- Inputs: dark surface, clear borders, ivory text, obvious focus ring with muted gold accent
- Accordions and drawers: elevated dark surfaces with explicit text color and thin borders

## Imagery
- Use warm-lit jewelry photography on models or elegant close-ups
- Prefer dark neutral or creamy editorial backgrounds that highlight gold tones
- UGC/reels imagery can feel slightly softer and more candid, but still premium

## Do
- Keep the visual language calm and cohesive
- Use serif headlines at generous scale
- Preserve strong readability and contrast in every section
- Let whitespace and imagery carry the luxury feel

## Don’t
- Don’t use bright discount colors, sale badges, or noisy banners
- Don’t overcrowd cards with too much metadata
- Don’t mix multiple accent colors
- Don’t use generic light-theme ecommerce defaults
