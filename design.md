# MicroCosmos Design System

## Typography
- Headings: Inter, font-bold, tracking-tight
- Body: Inter, font-normal
- Hero title: text-5xl md:text-7xl font-extrabold
- Section titles: text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold
- Card titles: text-xl font-semibold

## Colors
- Primary background: slate-950 (#020617)
- Section card background: slate-900 (#0f172a)
- Accent: cyan-400 (#22d3ee)
- Accent secondary: emerald-400 (#34d399)
- Text primary: white
- Text secondary: slate-300
- Text muted: slate-400

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl
- Grid gap: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Visual Style
- Dark, scientific theme evoking microscopy
- Subtle glow effects on cards
- Rounded corners on cards (rounded-2xl)
- Smooth hover transitions
- Gradient overlays on hero images
- Do: use dark backgrounds with vibrant accent colors
- Don't: use light backgrounds or harsh white
