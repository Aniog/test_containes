# Design Guidelines: ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style
Elegant, professional, and industrial-modern. The design should convey precision engineering and reliability while remaining accessible and user-friendly.

## Colors
- **Primary (Industrial Black)**: `#1A1A1A` - Used for headers, footers, and primary text.
- **Secondary (Metallic Steel)**: `#707070` - Used for secondary text and borders.
- **Accent (Precision Blue)**: `#0047AB` - Used for buttons, links, and highlights to suggest trust and technology.
- **Background (Clean White)**: `#FFFFFF` - Primary background for readability.
- **Muted (Soft Gray)**: `#F5F5F5` - Used for section backgrounds and card surfaces.

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Inter', sans-serif. Semi-bold or Bold (`font-semibold`, `font-bold`).
- **Body**: 'Inter', sans-serif. Regular (`font-normal`).
- **Accent**: 'Inter', sans-serif. Medium (`font-medium`).

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous padding and margins (`p-8`, `m-4`, `gap-8`) to maintain an "elegant" feel.
- Clean, grid-based layouts (`grid`, `grid-cols-1`, `md:grid-cols-2`, `lg:grid-cols-3`).
- Cards with subtle shadows (`shadow-sm`) and rounded corners (`rounded-lg`).

## Components Style
- **Buttons**: Rounded-md corners, clear hover states, precision blue backgrounds.
- **Cards**: Minimalist, high-quality images, clean typography.
- **Navigation**: Sticky header, simple mobile menu.

## Do's
- Use high-quality stock images of machinery and metalwork.
- Keep text concise and professional.
- Use whitespace to separate content.
- Ensure all text is high-contrast and readable.

## Don'ts
- Don't use neon or overly bright colors (except as small accents).
- Don't clutter the interface with too many elements.
- Don't use low-resolution images.
