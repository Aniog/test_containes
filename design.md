# Design System - ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style
The brand "ARTITECT MACHINERY" combines industrial precision with architectural elegance. The design should feel robust, high-end, and extremely professional while remains accessible and easy to navigate for industrial clients.

## Colors
- **Primary (Slate-950)**: `#020617` - Used for main backgrounds and primary text. Represents strength and reliability.
- **Secondary (Slate-500)**: `#64748b` - Used for secondary text and borders.
- **Accent (Amber-600)**: `#d97706` - Used for CTA buttons, highlights, and icons. Adds a touch of refined elegance (metallic feel).
- **Background (Slate-50)**: `#f8fafc` - Used for main body background to keep it clean and user-friendly.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: 'Inter', sans-serif.
- **Headings**: Semi-bold to Bold, tight letter spacing (`tracking-tight`).
- **Body**: Regular, legible line height (`leading-relaxed`).

## UI Elements
- **Cards**: Minimalist with subtle shadows (`shadow-sm`) and rounded corners (`rounded-lg`).
- **Buttons**: Rounded-md, primary uses Accent color, hover state with slight darkening.
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace to avoid clutter and maintain an "architectural" feel.

## Components
- Navbar: Sticky, clean, with a clear logo and navigation.
- Hero: Large, high-quality images of machinery with bold typography.
- Product Grid: Clean alignment showing categories of folding machines.
- Contact Form: Simple, modern, and accessible.
