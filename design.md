# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. Target: women 25-45.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `deep-50` (#f7f5f0) — warm ivory
- **Text**: `deep-950` (#2c211c) — rich dark brown
- **Accent**: `gold-600` (#cc8125) — warm gold, used sparingly on CTAs and highlights
- **Accent hover**: `gold-700` (#a96322)
- **Cream surfaces**: `cream-100` (#fdf9f3)
- **Muted text**: `deep-500` (#a48a65) or `deep-600` (#8d7357)
- **Dividers**: `deep-200` (#dbd1bc) — hairline thin

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weight 500
  - Product names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing 0.12em
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif)
  - Labels/captions: tracking-wide (0.08-0.10em)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace. Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Max content width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8

## Components
- **Primary Button**: gold-600 bg, white text, no border-radius (sharp but not aggressive)
  - Or: 2px border-radius for a subtle refined feel
- **Outline Button**: deep-700 border, deep-800 text, fills on hover
- **Cards**: subtle shadow-sm, cream-100 bg, hairline border
- **Dividers**: thin 1px deep-200 horizontal rules
- **Hover**: subtle 300ms ease transitions, slight scale or shadow lift

## Imagery
- Warm-lit gold jewelry on model, dark/neutral backgrounds
- Large editorial hero images, vertical 9:16 for UGC reel

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Maintain generous whitespace
- Keep accents restrained (gold used only on CTAs and key highlights)
- Strong contrast: dark text on light backgrounds
- Thin hairline dividers

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No discount badges or sale tags
- No loud gradients
- No heavy drop shadows
- No generic blue links
