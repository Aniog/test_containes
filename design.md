# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Base (deep warm charcoal):** #1C1917 (stone-900) — primary background for hero/dark sections
- **Cream (warm off-white):** #FAF7F2 — primary light background
- **Ivory:** #F5F0EB — secondary light background, card backgrounds
- **Gold accent:** #C9A96E — primary accent, CTAs, highlights
- **Gold hover:** #B8944F — darker gold for hover states
- **Text dark:** #1C1917 — headings on light backgrounds
- **Text medium:** #57534E (stone-600) — body text on light backgrounds
- **Text light:** #FAF7F2 — text on dark backgrounds
- **Text muted:** #A8A29E (stone-400) — subtle labels, captions
- **Divider:** #E7E5E4 (stone-200) — hairline dividers
- **Border:** #D6D3D1 (stone-300) — subtle borders

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond, serif. Elegant, refined.
  - H1: 48-64px, font-weight 400-500, tracking wide
  - H2: 36-48px, font-weight 400-500
  - H3: 24-30px, font-weight 500
- **Body:** Inter, sans-serif. Clean, modern.
  - Body: 14-16px, font-weight 400, line-height 1.6
  - Small: 12-13px, font-weight 400
  - Button: 13-14px, font-weight 500-600, uppercase, tracking-widest
- **Product names:** Cormorant Garamond, UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace. Sections: 80-120px vertical padding (desktop), 48-64px (mobile)
- Max content width: 1280px
- Grid gaps: 16-24px for product cards
- Section padding: px-6 md:px-8 lg:px-12

## Components
- **Buttons:** Solid gold (#C9A96E) with cream text, or outlined with gold border. Rounded-none or rounded-sm. Subtle hover transitions (0.3s). Min height 44px.
- **Cards:** Minimal, no visible border on light bg. Soft shadow on hover. Image-first.
- **Dividers:** 1px hairline, stone-200 color
- **Accordions:** Clean, minimal. Plus/minus icon. Stone-200 border bottom.
- **Pill selectors:** Rounded-full, stone-200 border, gold bg when active

## Interactions
- Hover transitions: 0.3s ease
- Image hover: subtle scale (1.02) or second image reveal
- Nav: transparent → solid cream on scroll (backdrop-blur)
- Cart drawer: slide from right

## Do's
- Use warm, muted tones. Gold as the ONLY accent color.
- Keep typography elegant with generous line-height
- Use thin hairline dividers between sections
- Large editorial imagery with warm tones
- Subtle, refined animations

## Don'ts
- No bright/saturated colors
- No rounded corners on buttons (max rounded-sm)
- No heavy shadows or bold borders
- No generic e-commerce look
- No discount/sale aesthetic
