# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: #faf8f5 (warm cream)
- Surface: #ffffff (pure white)
- Primary text: #1a1a1a (deep charcoal)
- Secondary text: #6b6560 (warm taupe)
- Muted text: #9a9490 (soft warm gray)
- Accent/Gold: #c9a96e (warm metallic gold)
- Accent hover: #b8944f (darker gold)
- Border: #e8e4df (warm light gray)
- Dark sections: #1a1a1a (deep charcoal)
- Dark text on dark: #faf8f5 (warm cream)

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif)
- Body/UI: Inter (clean sans-serif)
- Product names: UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest)

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: border-t border-[#e8e4df]
- Subtle borders on cards

## Shadows
- Soft, warm shadows: shadow-sm, shadow-md
- Hover: shadow-lg with transition

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold background, dark text
- Secondary: outlined with gold border
- Full-width on product pages
- Premium feel with proper padding and transitions

## Animations
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300, ease-in-out)
- Smooth opacity and transform changes
- Fade-in on scroll (optional)

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep typography elegant and restrained
- Use gold accent sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- Large editorial imagery
- Warm, inviting tone

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No discount badges or sale stickers
- No cluttered layouts
- No heavy shadows or gradients
- No generic e-commerce patterns
