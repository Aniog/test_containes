# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial machinery - metal folding equipment
- **Style**: Elegant but user-friendly - professional industrial aesthetic with modern, clean design

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, modern, highly readable
- **Headings**: font-weight 700-800, tight letter-spacing for impact
- **Body**: font-weight 400-500, comfortable line-height (1.6-1.7)
- **Hierarchy**: 
  - H1: 3rem-4rem (hero sections)
  - H2: 2rem-2.5rem (section titles)
  - H3: 1.25rem-1.5rem (card titles)
  - Body: 1rem (16px base)

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep navy blue `#0f172a` (slate-900) - conveys trust, professionalism
- **Secondary**: Steel blue `#1e40af` (blue-800) - industrial feel
- **Accent**: Warm amber `#d97706` (amber-600) - draws attention to CTAs
- **Background**: Clean white `#ffffff` and light gray `#f8fafc` (slate-50)
- **Text**: Dark slate `#1e293b` (slate-800) for body, near-black for headings
- **Borders**: Light gray `#e2e8f0` (slate-200)

## Layout & Spacing
- **Container**: max-w-7xl with responsive padding
- **Section spacing**: py-16 to py-24 between major sections
- **Card spacing**: gap-6 to gap-8 in grids
- **Grid**: 1 column mobile, 2 columns tablet, 3 columns desktop

## Visual Style
- **Cards**: White background, subtle shadow (shadow-lg), rounded corners (rounded-xl)
- **Buttons**: Rounded-lg, bold text, hover effects with smooth transitions
- **Images**: Full-bleed hero images, product cards with consistent aspect ratios
- **Icons**: Lucide React icons, consistent sizing (w-6 h-6 to w-12 h-12)
- **Transitions**: Smooth hover effects (transition-all duration-300)

## Do's
- Use high-contrast text on all backgrounds
- Maintain generous whitespace for readability
- Use consistent iconography throughout
- Show product images prominently
- Include clear CTAs on every section

## Don'ts
- Don't use overly decorative fonts
- Don't clutter pages with too many elements
- Don't use bright/neon colors that clash with industrial theme
- Don't hide important information behind complex navigation
