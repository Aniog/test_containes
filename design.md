# Design System: ARTITECT MACHINERY

## Visual Style
The brand identity for Artitect Machinery is **Industrial Elegance**. It combines the raw, powerful nature of sheet metal folding machines with a sophisticated, premium aesthetic.

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Playfair Display`, serif. Used for titles to convey elegance and authority. (Tailwind: `font-serif`)
- **Body**: `Inter`, sans-serif. Used for readability and a modern, professional feel. (Tailwind: `font-sans`)

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Dark)**: `#1A1A1A`. Deep charcoal/black for a solid, machinery-like foundation. (`brand-dark`)
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#C5A059`. A sophisticated metallic gold to represent high quality and precision. (`brand-gold`)
- **Secondary (Gray)**: `#333333`. Medium gray for contrast and UI elements. (`brand-gray`)
- **Background (Light)**: `#F5F5F5`. Off-white/light gray background for a clean, user-friendly workspace. (`brand-light`)

## UI Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded (not fully circular), with bold typography. Primary buttons use the accent color.
- **Cards**: Minimalistic with subtle shadows and clean borders.
- **Imagery**: High-contrast, professional photography of industrial machinery and folded metal parts.

## Do's and Don'ts
### Do
- Use plenty of white space to maintain elegance.
- Use high-quality imagery of metal textures and machines.
- Keep the navigation simple and direct.

### Don't
- Use overly bright or neon colors.
- Use cluttered layouts.
- Over-animate elements; keep transitions smooth and subtle.
