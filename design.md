# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FDFBF9` (warm ivory)
- **Surface/Cards**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Text Primary**: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6B6360` (warm gray)
- **Text Muted**: `#9C9490` (light warm gray)
- **Accent**: `#8B6F47` (warm gold/bronze)
- **Accent Hover**: `#7A5F3A`
- **Accent Light**: `#F5EDE4` (cream)
- **Border**: `#E8E2DC` (warm border)
- **Border Light**: `#F0EBE6`
- **Gold Metallic**: `#C9A96E` (for decorative accents)
- **Star Rating**: `#C9A96E`
- **Error/Sale**: `#B85C4A`

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif, weight 300–600
  - Product names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em
  - Section headings: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.1em
- **Body / UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif, weight 300–500
  - Base size: 14px, line-height 1.6
- **Buttons**: Inter, weight 400, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.12em, 12px

## Spacing
- Section padding: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- Content max-width: 1200px
- Card gap: 24px (desktop), 16px (mobile)
- Component internal padding: 24px

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary (solid)**: bg accent, text white, h-12, px-8, rounded-none (sharp edges), uppercase, tracking-widest
- **Outlined**: border accent, text accent, bg transparent, same sizing
- **Hover**: darken fill or fill outlined

### Cards (Product)
- No border, no shadow at rest
- Subtle shadow on hover: `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`
- Image aspect ratio 3:4
- Product name: serif, uppercase, tracking-wide
- Price: sans-serif, weight 400

### Dividers
- Hairline: 1px solid border-light
- Section dividers: 1px solid border

### Badges / Tags
- Uppercase, 10px, tracking-widest, accent color text

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers
- Subtle hover transitions (200ms ease)
- Restrained accent color usage
- Let the product imagery breathe

## Don'ts
- No heavy shadows or borders
- No bright/saturated colors
- No rounded corners on buttons
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount-style badges or flash sale aesthetics
- No generic stock photo feel
