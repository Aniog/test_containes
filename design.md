# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Velmora is a direct-to-consumer demi-fine gold jewelry brand. The visual identity is quiet luxury, warm, editorial, and premium. Not loud, not discount-looking, not generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: cream (#faf7f0) — warm off-white base
- Text: dark-900 (#1c1916) — near-black with warmth
- Accent / Gold: gold (#c4a87a) — warm metallic gold
- Gold Light: goldLight (#ede3bc) — soft champagne
- Gold Dark: goldDark (#8a6e46) — deeper bronze-gold
- Neutrals: warm gray scale (dark-50 through dark-900)
- Surfaces: white, cream, dark-50 for cards
- Dividers: dark-200/60 — thin hairline separators

## Typography
- Headings (h1-h6, product names, logo): Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
- Body & UI: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- Product names: uppercase with wide tracking (tracking-widest)
- Buttons: uppercase, small, medium weight, wide tracking
- Section titles: serif, large, centered

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Max-width containers: 1280px with padding
- Large editorial imagery — full-bleed hero
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Product grid: 2 cols mobile, 3 cols tablet, 4-5 cols desktop

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers: 1px solid dark-200/60
- No heavy borders
- Subtle borders on cards

## Buttons
- Primary: dark-900 bg, cream text, uppercase, tracking-wider, hover to dark-800
- Accent: gold bg, cream text, uppercase, tracking-wider, hover to goldDark
- Outline: dark-900 border, dark-900 text, hover fills dark-900
- All buttons: px-8 py-4, text-sm, font-medium, uppercase, tracking-wider

## Hover & Transition Effects
- Subtle opacity transitions on images (hover: scale-[1.02] or opacity-90)
- Smooth color transitions on buttons and links (duration-300)
- Soft shadows on cards (shadow-sm hover:shadow-md)
- Cart drawer slides in from right

## Product Cards
- Clean white/cream card with subtle shadow
- Square or 4:3 image ratio
- Product name in uppercase serif, tracking-widest
- Price in sans-serif, regular weight
- Hover: reveal second image, quick "Add to Cart" overlay

## Do's
- Use warm, editorial imagery
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Product names in uppercase serif with wide tracking
- Subtle animations (fade-in, slide-up on scroll)
- Gold accents on CTAs and key elements

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or heavy borders
- No discount-looking badges or sale tags
- No generic stock photography
- No cramped layouts
- No heavy shadows
- No bright red or blue accents
