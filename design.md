# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand mood
Quiet luxury, editorial calm, warm femininity, premium without feeling inaccessible.

## Direction
Commit to a deep espresso and aubergine base with warm champagne-gold accents and soft ivory surfaces.

## Typography
- Display / headings: Cormorant Garamond
- Body / UI: Manrope
- Product names: uppercase with wide tracking

## Color tokens
Add these as named colors in Tailwind config and reuse them consistently.
- `velmora-ink`: `#1e1716`
- `velmora-espresso`: `#2c221f`
- `velmora-aubergine`: `#4a3940`
- `velmora-champagne`: `#b79a64`
- `velmora-sand`: `#d8c7b3`
- `velmora-mist`: `#ede4db`
- `velmora-ivory`: `#fbf8f3`

## Surfaces
- Main page background: ivory
- Hero / footer / premium dark sections: ink or espresso
- Cards: ivory with subtle border and very soft shadow
- Accent surfaces: champagne or sand only in restrained amounts

## Spacing
- Use generous whitespace
- Desktop sections should feel editorial and open
- Mobile spacing should stay breathable, never cramped
- Preferred section rhythm: `py-16 md:py-20 lg:py-24`

## Borders and dividers
- Use thin warm dividers like `border-velmora-sand/40`
- Rounded corners should feel refined, not playful
- Preferred radii: `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl`

## Buttons
- Primary: dark filled button with ivory text
- Secondary: transparent or ivory button with sand border and dark text
- Hover should be subtle, never flashy

## Imagery
- Warm-lit closeups of gold jewelry
- Editorial portrait crops
- Neutral dark or soft stone backgrounds
- Avoid bright discount-store styling or generic marketplace imagery

## Do
- Use strong readable contrast on every surface
- Keep text elegant and restrained
- Let imagery and whitespace carry the luxury feel
- Use soft transitions, light shadows, thin borders

## Don't
- Do not use bright sale colors
- Do not use harsh gradients or neon accents
- Do not use crowded grids with tight spacing
- Do not allow text to inherit low-contrast colors on tinted surfaces
