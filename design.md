# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `cream` (#FDFBF7) — warm off-white base
- **Foreground/Text**: `charcoal` (#1A1A1A) — near-black for body text
- **Accent**: `gold` (#B8860B) — warm dark gold for CTAs, highlights
- **Accent Light**: `gold-light` (#D4A843) — lighter gold for hover states
- **Muted**: `taupe` (#8B7D6B) — warm gray-brown for secondary text
- **Surface**: `linen` (#F5F0E8) — slightly darker warm surface for cards/sections
- **Border**: `sand` (#E8E0D4) — subtle warm border color

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weights 300–600
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weights 300–500
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, Cormorant Garamond
- **Heading sizes**: Hero 4xl–6xl, Section 3xl–4xl, Card lg–xl

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections py-16 to py-24
- Container max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-8
- Cards: rounded-none or rounded-sm (sharp, editorial)
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-sand

## Components
- **Buttons**: Solid accent bg-gold text-cream px-8 py-3 uppercase tracking-widest text-sm font-medium. Hover: bg-gold-light. Outlined variant: border border-gold text-gold.
- **Cards**: No border-radius, subtle shadow-sm on hover, transition-all duration-300
- **Navigation**: Transparent over hero, bg-cream on scroll. Serif logo left, sans-serif links center.

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep imagery large and editorial
- Use thin hairline dividers (border-sand)
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- Uppercase + tracking for product names and CTAs

## Don'ts
- No rounded corners on cards (keep editorial/sharp)
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/sale-style badges
