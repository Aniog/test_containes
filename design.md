# SSourcing China Design System

## Brand Direction
A clean, international B2B website for overseas buyers who need a dependable sourcing partner in China. The visual language should feel credible, organized, practical, and professional rather than flashy or salesy.

## Typography
- Use Inter for all text.
- Headings: strong, clear, compact line-height. Example Tailwind: `text-slate-950 font-semibold tracking-tight`
- Body text: readable and neutral. Example Tailwind: `text-slate-600 leading-7`
- Eyebrow labels and metadata: uppercase, small, spaced out. Example Tailwind: `text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-[0.2em] text-blue-700`

## Color System
- Primary text: `text-slate-950`
- Secondary text: `text-slate-600`
- Muted text: `text-slate-500`
- Main page background: `bg-slate-50`
- Section surface: `bg-white`
- Alternate surface: `bg-slate-100`
- Primary action color: `bg-blue-700 text-white`
- Primary action hover: `hover:bg-blue-800`
- Borders: `border-slate-200`
- Trust highlight backgrounds: `bg-blue-50`, `bg-emerald-50`, `bg-amber-50`

## Layout
- Use a centered content container: `mx-auto w-full max-w-7xl px-6 lg:px-8`
- Desktop sections should breathe with generous vertical spacing: `py-16 lg:py-24`
- Cards should use rounded corners, soft borders, and light shadow: `rounded-3xl border border-slate-200 bg-white shadow-sm`
- Avoid cramped mobile layouts. Use single column on small screens and 2-4 columns on larger screens when appropriate.

## Components
- Primary button: `inline-flex items-center justify-center rounded-full bg-blue-700 px-6 py-3 text-sm font-semibold text-white transition hover:bg-blue-800`
- Secondary button: `inline-flex items-center justify-center rounded-full border border-slate-300 bg-white px-6 py-3 text-sm font-semibold text-slate-900 transition hover:bg-slate-100`
- Section heading block: eyebrow + heading + supporting paragraph with left-aligned layout
- Stat card: concise metric with supporting label, no decorative clutter
- Form inputs: `rounded-2xl border border-slate-300 bg-white px-4 py-3 text-sm text-slate-900 placeholder:text-slate-400 focus:border-blue-700 focus:outline-none`

## Imagery
- Use realistic visuals related to factories, supplier visits, product inspections, packaging, freight coordination, and international trade.
- Prefer clean industrial and logistics imagery that matches the surrounding text.
- Hero and featured case study visuals should feel credible and documentary-like.

## Do
- Keep trust and clarity above visual drama.
- Use explicit readable foreground colors on every surface.
- Present information in clear sections and practical lists.
- Use badges and highlights sparingly for trust points and process steps.

## Don't
- Do not use neon colors, glassmorphism, or startup-style gradients.
- Do not make claims that sound unrealistic or exaggerated.
- Do not center everything; this is a content-heavy B2B site and should read like a professional service website.
- Do not rely on low-contrast text for secondary information.
