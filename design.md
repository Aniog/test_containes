# Design System — Forno Bakery & Pizza

## Brand Identity
Warm, artisanal, inviting. Evokes the smell of fresh bread and wood-fired pizza.

## Colors
- **Primary (Crimson Red):** `#C0392B` → Tailwind: `primary` → `bg-primary`, `text-primary`
- **Primary Dark:** `#922B21` → `bg-primary-dark`
- **Accent (Golden Amber):** `#E67E22` → `bg-accent`, `text-accent`
- **Accent Light:** `#F5CBA7` → `bg-accent-light`
- **Background (Cream):** `#FFF8F0` → `bg-cream`
- **Surface (Warm White):** `#FFFFFF` → `bg-white`
- **Dark (Rich Brown):** `#2C1810` → `bg-dark`, `text-dark`
- **Muted (Warm Gray):** `#7D6B5E` → `text-muted`
- **Border:** `#E8D5C4` → `border-warm`

## Typography
- **Font:** Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body) via Google Fonts
- **H1:** `text-5xl md:text-7xl font-bold font-serif text-dark`
- **H2:** `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold font-serif text-dark`
- **H3:** `text-xl font-semibold font-serif text-dark`
- **Body:** `text-base text-muted leading-relaxed`
- **Caption:** `text-sm text-muted`

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-20 px-4 md:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Borders & Shadows
- Card: `rounded-2xl shadow-md border border-warm`
- Button: `rounded-full`
- Image: `rounded-xl overflow-hidden`

## Buttons
- **Primary:** `bg-primary text-white rounded-full px-8 py-3 font-semibold hover:bg-primary-dark transition`
- **Outline:** `border-2 border-primary text-primary rounded-full px-8 py-3 font-semibold hover:bg-primary hover:text-white transition`
- **Accent:** `bg-accent text-white rounded-full px-8 py-3 font-semibold hover:opacity-90 transition`

## Do's
- Use warm, earthy tones throughout
- Use serif fonts for headings to feel artisanal
- Use generous whitespace
- Use rounded corners on cards and images
- Use stock images for food photography

## Don'ts
- Don't use cold blues or grays as primary colors
- Don't use sharp corners on cards
- Don't use small font sizes for headings
- Don't use low-contrast text on colored backgrounds
