# Velmora Fine Jewelry Visual System

Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial direction: warm ivory surfaces, refined espresso ink, and restrained antique-gold accents that flatter demi-fine gold jewelry without looking loud or promotional.

## Palette
- Base page: warm ivory (`velmora-ivory`, example Tailwind `bg-velmora-ivory`).
- Deep editorial surfaces: espresso brown (`velmora-espresso`, example `bg-velmora-espresso text-velmora-ivory`).
- Secondary neutral: pearl and sand (`velmora-pearl`, `velmora-sand`) for cards and content blocks.
- Accent: antique gold (`velmora-gold`) for buttons, fine rules, ratings, and focus states.
- Muted text: taupe (`velmora-taupe`) only on light backgrounds, never as primary text.

## Typography
- Headings and product names: Cormorant Garamond serif, elegant and airy.
- Body and UI: Inter sans-serif for clarity.
- Product names: uppercase with wide letter spacing (`uppercase tracking-[0.22em]` or `tracking-[0.18em]`).

## Layout and spacing
- Mobile-first with generous vertical spacing; desktop uses editorial split sections and multi-column grids.
- Use thin hairline dividers (`border-velmora-line`) and restrained shadows.
- Keep product cards clean with soft neutral image wells, minimal labels, and clear pricing.

## Components
- Buttons: premium solid espresso or antique-gold fills; secondary buttons are thin outlined pills.
- Navigation: transparent over hero, solid ivory on scroll, readable in both states.
- Cart drawer and filters: light pearl/ivory surfaces with explicit espresso text.

## Do
- Use large warm jewelry imagery, editorial whitespace, gentle hover transitions, and accessible contrast.
- Keep copy refined and non-discount-looking.

## Don't
- Do not use bright sale colors, heavy gradients, loud badges, cluttered product cards, or low-contrast muted text.
