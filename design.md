# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora should feel like quiet luxury: warm, editorial, polished, and premium without becoming flashy. The storefront should flatter gold jewelry and feel giftable, feminine, and elevated.

## Palette
Use one consistent direction across the site:
- `ink` — deep plum-charcoal for hero overlays, footer, and high-contrast text. Example Tailwind token: `bg-ink`, `text-ink`.
- `porcelain` — soft warm ivory for page backgrounds. Example: `bg-porcelain`.
- `sand` — subtle warm neutral for cards and separators. Example: `bg-sand`, `border-sand-deep`.
- `champagne` — restrained metallic accent for buttons, icons, emphasis. Example: `bg-champagne`, `text-champagne-ink`.
- `rosewood` — muted plum accent for small labels and hovers. Example: `text-rosewood`, `border-rosewood`.

## Typography
- Headings, logo, and product names: Cormorant Garamond with elegant spacing.
- Body, navigation, labels, form controls: Inter.
- Product names should be uppercase with generous letter-spacing. Example: `tracking-[0.32em] uppercase`.
- Editorial headings should be large, airy, and refined. Example: `font-display text-4xl md:text-6xl leading-none`.

## Layout and spacing
- Prioritize generous whitespace and breathing room.
- Use thin dividers and subtle borders instead of heavy card chrome.
- Max content width should feel editorial, not cramped. Example: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`.
- On desktop, avoid stacking everything into one narrow column; use asymmetric editorial grids where suitable.

## Components
- Buttons should feel premium with soft corners, thin borders, and calm hover transitions.
- Use soft shadows sparingly. Example: `shadow-[0_20px_60px_rgba(39,24,31,0.08)]` only for key surfaces.
- Product cards should be clean and image-led with restrained metadata.
- Trust bars, accordions, and footer columns should use clear readable foreground colors.

## Imagery
- Use warm-lit gold jewelry imagery on neutral or dark editorial backgrounds.
- Avoid loud lifestyle imagery, discount banners, neon colors, and busy collage layouts.
- Large hero and category imagery should feel magazine-like and softly luxurious.

## Motion
- Keep motion subtle and smooth.
- Prefer opacity, translate, and scale transitions around 200–400ms.
- Hover states should feel refined, never bouncy or playful.

## Do
- Keep contrast strong and text readable on every surface.
- Use champagne accents selectively.
- Preserve a premium, giftable mood.

## Don’t
- Do not use bright sale colors, loud gradients, or crowded UI.
- Do not use generic marketplace styling.
- Do not let gold accents overpower readability.
