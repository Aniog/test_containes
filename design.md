# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Color Palette
- **Background**: Rich warm charcoal `#1a1612` (main), `#241f19` (elevated surfaces)
- **Surface**: Warm off-white `#faf6f0` (cards, light sections)
- **Text Primary**: `#faf6f0` on dark, `#1a1612` on light
- **Text Muted**: `#a69882` on dark, `#7a6e60` on light
- **Accent/Gold**: `#c9a96e` — primary CTA, highlights, star ratings
- **Accent Hover**: `#dfc28a` — lighter gold for hover states
- **Border**: `#3a3229` thin hairlines on dark, `#e8ddd0` on light

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond, serif — elegant, editorial weight 300-600
- **Body/UI**: Inter, sans-serif — clean, readable weight 300-500
- **Product Names**: UPPERCASE, tracking-[0.2em] in Cormorant Garamond
- **Logo**: VELMORA — Cormorant Garamond weight 500, tracking-[0.3em]

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections use py-16 md:py-24
- Max content width: max-w-7xl mx-auto
- Cards: rounded-lg, subtle shadow
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Visual Style
- Hairline dividers: border-b border-[#3a3229]/30
- Buttons: accent solid (bg-[#c9a96e] text-[#1a1612]) or outlined (border-[#c9a96e] text-[#c9a96e])
- Transitions: transition-all duration-300 ease-out
- Hover effects: subtle scale, shadow lift, color shift
- Images: warm-lit, gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- No loud colors, no discount aesthetics
