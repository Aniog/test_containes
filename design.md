# Strikingly Generators Hub Design

## Visual direction
- Use a clean Strikingly marketing style: airy white layout, thin dividers, compact cards, and restrained purple accents.
- Keep the page looking like a useful organized directory, not a keyword wall.
- Default page background: white. Reserve tinted treatment for the hero wash only.

## Typography
- Headings and buttons: Josefin Sans 700 as the fallback heading face for Brandon Grotesque. Always uppercase.
- Body: Open Sans 400, 14px base, 1.5 line-height.
- Large heading scale: H1 about 40 to 48px desktop, 28 to 32px mobile.
- Section headings: about 26 to 32px desktop.

## Colors
- Brand purple: #8159BB
- AI gradient: linear-gradient(90deg, #7671FF 0%, #CB0C9F 100%)
- Body text: #636972
- Section headings: #4B5056
- Hero heading line 1: #2E2E2F
- Card border: #C6C9CD
- Divider: #E2E4E7
- Light background surface: #F4F6F8
- Page background: #FFFFFF

## Components
### Buttons
- Height 36px default, 44px large.
- Radius 4px.
- Filled buttons use the AI gradient with white text only.
- Ghost buttons use transparent background, 1px purple border, purple text.

### Cards
- White background, 1px #C6C9CD border, 3px radius, 20px padding.
- Hover: small shadow and purple border. No scaling, bouncing, or tilt.

### Tags
- Josefin Sans 700, uppercase, 11px.
- Ghost style: purple text, 1px purple border, no fill.

## Layout and spacing
- Max content width: 1200px and centered.
- Prefer 20px between blocks and cards.
- Prefer 40px between major sections.
- Hero vertical spacing: 60 to 80px.
- Use responsive 3/2/1 card grids where the brief calls for them.

## Do
- Keep all content highly readable with explicit foreground colors.
- Preserve semantic structure with one H1 and clear section headings.
- Make interactive states visible with obvious focus rings.

## Don't
- Do not use dark text on gradient buttons.
- Do not turn directory sections into bare link lists.
- Do not add fake ratings, testimonials, or inflated proof points.
- Do not add tinted section backgrounds outside the hero.
