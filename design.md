# SSourcing China - Design System

## Visual Style
- Clean, professional B2B look with international trust feel
- Color palette: Navy blue (#0F2B5B) primary, Orange (#E8763A) accent/CTA, White (#FFFFFF) background, Light gray (#F7F8FA) sections, Dark gray (#1A1A2E) text
- Fonts: Inter (headings & body), clean sans-serif
- Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Card style: white bg, rounded-xl, subtle shadow, hover elevation
- Buttons: rounded-lg, medium font weight, transition hover effects
- Section spacing: py-20 lg:py-28
- Consistent use of navy-to-blue gradients for hero sections
- Icons: Lucide React for consistency
- Typography: headings 3xl-5xl, body base-lg, clear hierarchy
