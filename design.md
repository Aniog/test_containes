# Visual Style Guide - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Palette
- **Primary/Background**: Off-white/Bone (`#FAF9F6`). Provides a soft, editorial backdrop for gold.
- **Text/Foreground**: Deep Charcoal (`#1A1A1A`). High contrast and premium feel.
- **Accent/Gold**: Subdued Gold (`#C5A059`). Used for CTAs, highlights, and subtle accents.
- **Muted/Border**: Soft Grey (`#E5E5E5`). Used for thin hairline dividers.

## Typography
- **Headings (Serif)**: `Cormorant Garamond`. Sophisticated, quiet luxury.
  - Class: `font-serif`
- **Body & UI (Sans)**: `Inter`. Clean, accessible, modern.
  - Class: `font-sans`
- **Product Names**: Uppercase with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).
  - Class: `uppercase-spaced`

## Layout & Components
- **Whitespace**: Generous. Minimum section spacing of `py-20`.
- **Dividers**: Thin `1px` lines (`border-t border-[#E5E5E5]`).
- **Cards**: Minimalist, no shadows or soft shadows (`shadow-sm` on hover).
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal (`bg-[#1A1A1A] text-white`) or Solid Gold (`bg-[#C5A059] text-white`).
  - Secondary: Outlined (`border border-[#1A1A1A]`).
- **Transitions**: Smooth `300ms` eases for hovers and reveals.

## Do's and Don'ts
### Do:
- Use large, high-quality editorial imagery.
- Keep text center-aligned for hero sections and headings.
- Use uppercase for navigational labels and product titles.
- Maintain consistent spacing between sections.

### Don't:
- Use bright, neon, or "loud" colors.
- Use drop shadows that are too heavy or dark.
- Crowd the UI with too many elements.
- Use rounded corners; prefer sharp edges (`rounded-none`) or minimal rounding.
