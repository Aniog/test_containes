# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Cream** (#FDFAF7) — Page background
- **Surface** (#FFFFFF) — Cards, elevated surfaces
- **Espresso** (#1C1917) — Primary text
- **Taupe** (#8B7E74) — Secondary text, muted elements
- **Gold** (#B8965E) — Primary accent, buttons, links
- **Gold Deep** (#9C7E4A) — Hover states, richer accents
- **Gold Light** (#D4B87A) — Subtle highlights, borders
- **Border** (#E8E2D9) — Hairline dividers, subtle borders
- **Charcoal** (#1A1817) — Footer, dark sections
- **Charcoal Soft** (#2D2A28) — Dark section text accents

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Playfair Display, serif. UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (0.15em–0.25em).
- **Body / UI**: Inter, sans-serif. Clean, readable.
- **Hero**: Large serif headlines, generous line-height.

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace throughout. Section padding: py-20 to py-28.
- Max content width: 1440px container.
- Thin hairline dividers (border-Border, 1px).

## Buttons
- **Primary**: bg-gold text-white hover:bg-gold-deep. Rounded, smooth transition.
- **Outline**: border border-gold text-gold hover:bg-gold hover:text-white.
- **Size**: px-8 py-3 for main CTAs. Letter-spacing: wider.

## Cards
- Clean white surfaces with subtle hover shadows.
- Image-dominant. Hover: reveal second image + quick add to cart.
- Thin borders, 8px radius.

## Animations
- Subtle fade-in on scroll (CSS animation).
- Smooth hover transitions (300ms ease).
- Cart drawer slides in from right.

## Do's
- Do use generous whitespace
- Do use uppercase serif for product names
- Do maintain strong contrast
- Do use gold sparingly as accent

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/loud colors
- Don't use bold shadows or heavy borders
- Don't use discount-style badges (no "SALE" in red)
- Don't use generic sans-serif for product names
