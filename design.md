# SSourcing China Design System

## Brand personality
Clean, trustworthy, international B2B, and practical. The site should feel like a reliable operations partner for overseas buyers, not a flashy marketplace.

## Colors
- Primary navy: use `brand-navy` for headers, deep backgrounds, and primary text.
- Slate text: use `brand-slate` for body copy.
- Blue accent: use `brand-blue` for CTAs, links, and small highlights.
- Amber accent: use `brand-amber` for practical trust markers and quote highlights.
- Light surfaces: use `brand-cloud`, `brand-mist`, and white for page sections and cards.

## Typography
Use Inter from Google Fonts. Headings should be confident and compact with `font-semibold` or `font-bold`. Body copy should stay readable with `text-base` to `text-lg` and clear line height.

## Layout
- Desktop: wide B2B layout with strong hero, two-column content blocks, and card grids.
- Mobile: stack sections into one column with generous spacing.
- Use rounded cards, subtle borders, and soft shadows. Avoid heavy gradients or decorative clutter.

## Components
- Primary CTA: solid blue button with white text, rounded full, clear hover state.
- Secondary CTA: white or transparent button with navy/blue text and border.
- Cards: white background, slate border, navy headings, readable slate body text.
- Badges: light blue or amber tinted backgrounds with explicit dark text.

## Image style
Use realistic factory, quality inspection, supplier meeting, warehouse, container shipping, and product sourcing visuals. Images should support trust and operational competence.

## Do
- Keep copy specific and practical.
- Make inquiry CTAs easy to find.
- Use strong contrast for all text.
- Make forms simple and professional.

## Don't
- Do not use exaggerated claims like guaranteed lowest price or risk-free sourcing.
- Do not use low-contrast text or decorative text over busy images without an overlay.
- Do not overcrowd mobile layouts.
