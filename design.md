# Design System: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.
- **Color Palette**:
  - `bg-velmora-stone`: #F9F7F2 (Sand/Off-white for warmth)
  - `bg-velmora-dark`: #1A1A1A (Deep Charcoal for contrast)
  - `text-velmora-primary`: #1A1A1A (Rich Black/Charcoal)
  - `text-velmora-muted`: #6B6B6B (Soft Grey)
  - `accent-velmora-gold`: #C5A059 (Champagne Gold)
  - `accent-velmora-gold-light`: #D4B886 (Soft Gold)
- **Border**: 
  - `border-velmora-hairline`: Thin hairline dividers (0.5px or 1px with low opacity).

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: `font-serif` (Cormorant Garamond).
- **Body & UI**: `font-sans` (Inter).
- **Product Names Styling**: UPPERCASE with `tracking-[0.2em]` letter-spacing.

## Components Style
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid `bg-velmora-dark` with `text-velmora-stone`, transition to `bg-velmora-gold`.
  - Secondary: Outlined `border-velmora-dark` or `border-velmora-stone`.
- **Cards**: Minimalist, soft shadows on hover, generous padding.
- **Gallery**: Large editorial imagery, thumbnails.

## Do's and Don'ts
- **DO**: Use generous whitespace.
- **DO**: Use large editorial imagery.
- **DON'T**: Use loud colors or generic e-commerce gradients.
- **DON'T**: Use small, crowded layouts.
