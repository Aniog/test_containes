# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Direction
Quiet luxury, warm editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. No loud discount aesthetics. Soft shadows, generous whitespace, thin hairline dividers.

## Color Palette
- Base: `#0F0F0F` (near-black, used for primary text and dark sections)
- Background: `#FAF8F5` (warm off-white)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (pure white cards)
- Accent: `#B8924F` (warm antique gold)
- Accent hover: `#A37E3E`
- Muted text: `#6B6560`
- Hairline: `#E7E2DB`
- Subtle surface: `#F3EFEA`

## Typography
- Headings / product names: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif, uppercase with `letter-spacing: 0.18em`
- Body / UI: `Inter`, sans-serif
- Display hero: `Cormorant Garamond` 48–72px, weight 300–400
- Section titles: `Cormorant Garamond` 28–36px, uppercase, wide tracking
- Body: `Inter` 14–16px, weight 400, line-height 1.65
- Small / captions: `Inter` 12px, weight 400

## Spacing
- Section vertical padding: 80–120px desktop, 56–72px mobile
- Container max-width: 1280px, centered with `mx-auto px-6 lg:px-10`
- Hairline dividers: 1px solid `#E7E2DB`
- Card border-radius: 0px (editorial sharp edges) or 2px for subtle softness
- Button border-radius: 0px (sharp, premium)

## Components
- Buttons: solid accent `#B8924F` with white text, or outlined accent on light. No radius. Uppercase, wide tracking, 14px. Padding 14px 28px.
- Inputs: 1px border `#E7E2DB`, no radius, focus ring accent.
- Cards: no border, no radius, subtle shadow `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.04)`. Hover lift `translateY(-4px)`.
- Icons: Lucide React, stroke-width 1.2–1.5, thin aesthetic.

## Motion
- Transitions: `all 0.35s cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.1, 0.25, 1)`
- Image hover: scale 1.04 over 0.6s
- Cart drawer: slide from right 0.35s ease
- Stagger children: 0.08s

## Imagery
Warm gold jewelry on dark or neutral backgrounds. Soft editorial lighting. No harsh whites.
