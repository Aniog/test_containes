# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Overview
Elegant industrial aesthetic for a precision sheet metal machinery brand. Clean, trustworthy, and professional.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: Bold, tight tracking, slate-900
- **Body**: Regular weight, slate-600/700, relaxed leading
- **Scale**: text-4xl to text-7xl for hero, text-xl to text-3xl for sections

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Blue-600 (#2563eb) - CTAs, accents, icons
- **Primary Hover**: Blue-700 (#1d4ed8)
- **Background**: White (#ffffff) and Slate-50 (#f8fafc)
- **Text Primary**: Slate-900 (#0f172a)
- **Text Secondary**: Slate-600 (#475569)
- **Text Muted**: Slate-500 (#64748b)
- **Dark Sections**: Slate-900 (#0f172a) with white text
- **Borders**: Slate-200 (#e2e8f0)

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 (5rem)
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl
- Grid gaps: gap-8 (2rem)
- Card padding: p-6 to p-8

## Components
- **Cards**: White background, rounded-2xl, subtle shadow, hover:shadow-xl
- **Buttons**: Rounded-lg, px-6 to px-8, py-3 to py-4, font-semibold
- **Inputs**: Rounded-lg, border-slate-300, focus:ring-blue-500

## Visual Style
- Rounded corners: rounded-lg, rounded-xl, rounded-2xl
- Shadows: shadow-lg, shadow-xl on hover
- Transitions: duration-200 to duration-300
- Icons: Lucide React, blue-600 on light backgrounds

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Maintain consistent spacing rhythm
- Use blue-600 as the primary accent
- Keep text highly readable with proper contrast

## Don'ts
- Don't use pure black (#000000)
- Don't overcrowd sections
- Don't use more than 2-3 colors per section
- Don't use small font sizes below text-sm
