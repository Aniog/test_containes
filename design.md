# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design System

## Brand Identity
ARTITECT MACHINERY is a premium manufacturer of metal folding machines. The brand conveys precision engineering, industrial elegance, and reliability.

## Colors
- **Primary (Steel Blue)**: `#2C3E6B` - Deep navy/steel tone, conveys trust and precision
- **Primary Dark**: `#1A2748` - Darker navy for hover states and deep accents
- **Primary Light**: `#E8ECF4` - Light steel background tint
- **Accent (Amber/Gold)**: `#C8922A` - Warm metallic gold for highlights, CTAs, and key accents
- **Accent Light**: `#F5E6C8` - Light gold for subtle backgrounds
- **Neutral White**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Neutral Light**: `#F5F6F8` - Page background
- **Neutral Medium**: `#D1D5DB` - Borders and dividers
- **Text Primary**: `#1A1F2E` - Near-black for main text
- **Text Secondary**: `#5A6278` - Muted slate for secondary text

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, modern, highly readable
- **Headings**: Weight 700-800, letter-spacing -0.02em
- **Body**: Weight 400, line-height 1.6
- **Size Scale**:
  - Hero title: text-5xl (3rem) md:text-6xl (3.75rem)
  - Section title: text-3xl md:text-4xl
  - Card titles: text-xl
  - Body: text-base

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl
- Card padding: p-6 md:p-8
- Gap between grid items: gap-8

## Borders & Shadows
- Cards: rounded-xl, shadow-sm border border-[#D1D5DB]
- Buttons: rounded-lg
- No harsh shadows; use subtle shadow-sm or shadow-md

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#2C3E6B] text-white hover:bg-[#1A2748]
- Accent/Gold: bg-[#C8922A] text-white hover:brightness-110
- Outline: border-2 border-[#2C3E6B] text-[#2C3E6B] hover:bg-[#2C3E6B] hover:text-white

## Design Principles
- Elegant but not cold - use warm gold accents to humanize the industrial feel
- Clean layouts with generous whitespace
- Professional product imagery
- Clear information hierarchy
- Subtle hover effects and transitions
