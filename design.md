# SSourcing China Visual Design Guide

## Brand direction
- Professional B2B sourcing consultancy for overseas buyers working with Chinese factories.
- Clean, trustworthy, international, practical, and calm. Avoid exaggerated claims or overly playful elements.

## Color system
- Primary: deep navy for headers, navigation, and trust-focused surfaces. Example Tailwind classes: `bg-slate-950`, `text-slate-950`, `border-slate-200`.
- Accent: restrained blue for CTAs and links. Example Tailwind classes: `bg-blue-700`, `hover:bg-blue-800`, `text-blue-700`.
- Secondary accent: amber for operational highlights and quality checkpoints. Example classes: `bg-amber-100`, `text-amber-700`.
- Backgrounds: white and very light slate. Example classes: `bg-white`, `bg-slate-50`, `bg-slate-100`.
- Text: use explicit readable foregrounds. Main copy: `text-slate-700`; headings: `text-slate-950`; muted text: `text-slate-500`.

## Typography
- Use Inter from Google Fonts for a modern international B2B look.
- Large page headlines should be confident and compact: `text-4xl md:text-6xl font-semibold tracking-tight`.
- Section headings: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-semibold tracking-tight`.
- Body copy: `text-base md:text-lg leading-7`.

## Layout and spacing
- Desktop sections use centered max-width containers: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-6 lg:px-8`.
- Use generous vertical rhythm: `py-16 md:py-24`.
- Cards use subtle borders and shadows: `rounded-2xl border border-slate-200 bg-white shadow-sm`.
- Responsive grids should stack on mobile and use 2-4 columns on desktop.

## Visual style
- Use realistic factory, quality control, packaging, container, and logistics imagery through the Strikingly stock image attributes.
- Combine image panels with process cards and practical copy.
- Use simple line icons from Lucide React for services and trust points.

## Do
- Keep CTAs direct and practical.
- Make form fields easy to scan and complete.
- Use explicit foreground colors on all custom backgrounds.
- Keep claims measured and credible.

## Don't
- Do not use neon colors, exaggerated gradients, or consumer-style hype.
- Do not use low-contrast text on dark or image backgrounds.
- Do not imply guaranteed supplier outcomes or unrealistic savings.
