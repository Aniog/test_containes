# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Visual Identity

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Background/Core):** `bg-[#F9F7F2]` (Soft Parchment)
- **Secondary (Dark/Text):** `text-[#1A1A1A]` (Deep Charcoal)
- **Accent (Jewelry/CTA):** `text-[#C5A059]` / `bg-[#C5A059]` (Muted Gold)
- **Neutral:** `border-[#E5E5E5]` (Soft Grey)

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names:** Serif - Cormorant Garamond / Playfair Display
  - Classes: `font-serif tracking-widest uppercase`
- **Body & UI Elements:** Sans-serif - Inter / Manrope
  - Classes: `font-sans font-light`

## UI Elements
- **Borders:** Thin hairline dividers (`border-b border-[#E5E5E5]`)
- **Spacing:** Generous whitespace, `py-20` for sections.
- **Buttons:** Solid Gold (`bg-[#C5A059] text-[#F9F7F2]`) or Outlined (`border border-[#C5A059] text-[#C5A059]`)
- **Transitions:** `transition-all duration-300 ease-out`
- **Shadows:** Very soft, barely there `shadow-sm`

## Imagery
- Close-up, warm-lit shots of jewelry on models.
- Editorial style, 3x2 or 4x3 ratios.
- Backgrounds to images should be neutral or dark charcoal for high contrast.

## Do's & Don'ts
- **Do:** Use uppercase for product titles with wide letter spacing.
- **Do:** Keep the layout breathable.
- **Don't:** Use bright, saturated colors (other than the gold accent).
- **Don't:** Use thick, heavy borders.
- **Don't:** Use rounded corners everywhere; keep them sharp or slightly rounded for a premium feel.
