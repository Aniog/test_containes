# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Base / Background**: `#0F0F0F` — deep warm black (primary background)
- **Surface**: `#1A1A1A` — slightly lighter black for cards/sections
- **Surface Elevated**: `#242424` — elevated surfaces, hover states
- **Text Primary**: `#F5F0E8` — warm off-white
- **Text Secondary**: `#A8A095` — warm muted gray
- **Text Muted**: `#6B655C` — subtle captions
- **Accent / CTA**: `#C8A97E` — warm champagne gold
- **Accent Hover**: `#D4B88F` — lighter gold
- **Hairline**: `rgba(245,240,232,0.08)` — subtle dividers
- **Success**: `#7A9E7E` — muted sage

## Typography
- **Headings / Display**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif. Weight 400–600.
- **Body / UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif. Weight 300–500.
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, `letter-spacing: 0.15em`, `font-size: 0.75rem`.
- **H1 (Hero)**: `clamp(2.5rem, 5vw, 4.5rem)`, weight 400, line-height 1.1
- **H2 (Section)**: `clamp(1.75rem, 3vw, 2.5rem)`, weight 400, line-height 1.2
- **Body**: `0.875rem`, weight 400, line-height 1.7
- **Caption / Label**: `0.75rem`, weight 400, letter-spacing 0.12em, uppercase

## Spacing
- Section padding: `80px` desktop, `48px` mobile
- Container max-width: `1280px`
- Horizontal padding: `24px` mobile, `48px` tablet, `80px` desktop
- Grid gap: `24px` mobile, `32px` desktop
- Card border-radius: `0px` (sharp editorial edges)
- Button border-radius: `0px`

## Shadows & Effects
- Card hover: `0 8px 32px rgba(0,0,0,0.4)`
- Image hover scale: `1.04` over 600ms ease
- Button transitions: `all 0.3s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)`
- Page transitions: `opacity 0.4s ease, transform 0.4s ease`

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO use generous whitespace
- DO use thin hairline dividers (`border-t border-white/10`)
- DO keep product cards minimal with white/off-white text on dark
- DO use uppercase tracking-wide for product names and nav
- DON'T use bright saturated colors
- DON'T use rounded corners on cards or buttons
- DON'T use heavy drop shadows
- DON'T use gradients as backgrounds
