# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Website Design Document

## Brand Identity

**Brand Name:** ARTITECT MACHINERY  
**Tagline:** Precision Engineering for Professional Metalworking

## Visual Style

### Color Palette
- **Primary Dark:** #1a1a2e (Deep Navy)
- **Secondary:** #16213e (Dark Blue)
- **Accent:** #e94560 (Industrial Red)
- **Steel Gray:** #4a5568 (Metal Gray)
- **Light Gray:** #f7fafc (Background Light)
- **White:** #ffffff (Pure White)
- **Text Dark:** #2d3748 (Dark Text)
- **Text Light:** #718096 (Muted Text)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter (700, 800 weights) - Bold, modern, industrial feel
- **Body:** Inter (400, 500 weights) - Clean readability
- **Accent Text:** Inter (600) - For CTAs and highlights

### Design Principles
1. **Elegant Industrial** - Professional machinery aesthetic with refined touches
2. **Clean & Spacious** - Generous whitespace, clear visual hierarchy
3. **User-Friendly** - Intuitive navigation, clear CTAs, accessible content
4. **Trust-Building** - Showcase expertise and quality craftsmanship

## Layout Structure

### Header
- Fixed navigation bar with logo and main menu
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Mobile-responsive hamburger menu

### Hero Section
- Full-width hero with bold headline
- Subtle animated gradient or industrial imagery
- Primary CTA button
- Trust indicators (years of experience, clients served)

### Products Section
- Grid layout showcasing 4 main product categories
- Clean product cards with hover effects
- Clear product names and brief descriptions
- "Learn More" buttons

### Features Section
- Three-column layout highlighting key advantages
- Icon-driven design
- Concise feature descriptions

### About Section
- Company story and mission
- Statistics/metrics section
- Professional team or facility imagery

### Contact Section
- Clean contact form
- Company contact information
- Location/business hours

### Footer
- Company logo and tagline
- Quick links
- Social media links
- Copyright

## Component Guidelines

### Buttons
- Primary: Red accent (#e94560) with white text
- Secondary: Outlined with border, transparent background
- Hover: Slight scale and shadow effect

### Cards
- White background with subtle shadow
- Rounded corners (8px)
- Hover: Lift effect with enhanced shadow

### Navigation
- Desktop: Horizontal menu items
- Mobile: Slide-out menu
- Active state: Accent color underline

### Form Inputs
- Clean border style
- Focus state: Accent color border
- Error state: Red border with message

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use high-quality imagery of machinery
- Maintain consistent spacing throughout
- Use clear, professional language
- Include trust signals (certifications, testimonials)
- Ensure mobile responsiveness
- Use accent color sparingly for emphasis

### Don'ts
- Don't overcrowd sections
- Don't use low-quality or generic imagery
- Don't use too many font sizes/styles
- Don't make CTAs hard to find
- Don't neglect mobile experience
