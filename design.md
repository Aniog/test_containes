# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Brand direction
Velmora uses a quiet-luxury editorial look: warm, refined, intimate, and premium without feeling flashy.

## Color palette
Use these named colors only.

- `velmora-ink` — deep editorial text and dark surfaces — example: `text-velmora-ink`, `bg-velmora-ink`
- `velmora-espresso` — richer dark accent for overlays and buttons — example: `bg-velmora-espresso`
- `velmora-cream` — main site background — example: `bg-velmora-cream`
- `velmora-ivory` — elevated cards and soft sections — example: `bg-velmora-ivory`
- `velmora-sand` — borders and quiet dividers — example: `border-velmora-sand`
- `velmora-taupe` — secondary UI text — example: `text-velmora-taupe`
- `velmora-gold` — restrained metallic accent — example: `bg-velmora-gold`, `text-velmora-gold`
- `velmora-goldSoft` — subtle accent tint and hover surfaces — example: `bg-velmora-goldSoft/20`
- `velmora-mist` — soft section backdrop — example: `bg-velmora-mist`

## Typography
- Headings and product names: `font-display` using Cormorant Garamond
- Body and UI: `font-sans` using Inter
- Product names should be uppercase with wide spacing: `uppercase tracking-product`

## Borders and dividers
- Prefer thin, quiet dividers: `border border-velmora-sand/70`
- Use rounded corners sparingly; prefer `rounded-3xl` for large editorial cards and `rounded-full` for pills only

## Shadows
- Use only named shadows: `shadow-velmora` and `shadow-velmora-soft`
- Keep shadows soft and atmospheric, never harsh

## Spacing
- Favor airy section spacing: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Keep content width elegant with `max-w-7xl` and generous horizontal padding

## Buttons
- Primary: `bg-velmora-espresso text-velmora-cream hover:bg-velmora-gold`
- Secondary: `border border-velmora-sand text-velmora-ink hover:border-velmora-gold hover:text-velmora-gold`

## Do
- Keep layouts calm and breathable
- Let imagery lead
- Use warm dark overlays with clear readable text
- Keep UI labels small, refined, and uppercase where appropriate

## Don't
- Do not use loud sale styling, bright promo colors, or discount-banner energy
- Do not use low-contrast text on editorial imagery
- Do not use heavy borders, aggressive shadows, or cramped layouts
