# Design Guidelines - SSourcing China

## Visual Style
SSourcing China is a professional, international B2B sourcing agency. The design should emphasize trust, reliability, and expertise.

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Standard Google Font)
- **Headings**: Semi-bold to Bold, clean and authoritative.
- **Body**: Regular, high legibility for business information.

### Color Palette
- **Primary (Blue)**: `#1e3a8a` (Deep Navy) - Represents trust and stability.
- **Secondary (Gold/Orange)**: `#f59e0b` (Amber) - Used for accents and CTAs.
- **Background**: `#ffffff` (White) / `#f8fafc` (Light Gray) - Clean and professional.
- **Text**: `#1e293b` (Slate-800) for body, `#0f172a` (Slate-900) for headings.

### Spacing & Layout
- **Container**: Max-width `1280px`.
- **Vertical Spacing**: Generous `section-padding` (`py-16` to `py-24`).
- **Layout**: Clear grid-based layouts, plenty of white space.

### Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners (`rounded-md`), consistent padding, clear color contrast.
- **Cards**: Subtle shadows (`shadow-sm` or `shadow-md`), clean borders.
- **Icons**: Lucide icons, consistent size and color.

### Do's
- Use high-quality, realistic imagery (sourcing, QC, shipping).
- Maintain a clean, professional B2B aesthetic.
- Ensure easy navigation and clear CTAs.

### Don'ts
- Use overly bright or neon colors.
- Overcrowd pages with too much text.
- Use low-contrast text.
