# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial machinery manufacturing (sheet metal folding machines)
- **Tone**: Elegant but user-friendly, professional yet approachable

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: #1a1a2e (Deep Navy) - conveys trust and professionalism
- **Secondary**: #16213e (Dark Blue) - for depth and contrast
- **Accent**: #e94560 (Coral Red) - industrial accent for CTAs and highlights
- **Neutral Light**: #f8f9fa (Off-white) - backgrounds
- **Neutral Dark**: #2d3436 (Charcoal) - text
- **Metallic Gray**: #636e72 (Steel gray) - secondary text and borders

## Typography
- **Headings**: "Playfair Display" - elegant serif for brand prestige
- **Body**: "Inter" - clean, readable sans-serif for user-friendliness
- **Sizes**:
  - H1: 3.5rem (hero), H2: 2.5rem, H3: 1.75rem
  - Body: 1rem, Small: 0.875rem

## Visual Style
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows for depth (box-shadow: 0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08))
- Rounded corners (8px) for approachability
- Smooth transitions (0.3s ease)
- Professional imagery with industrial aesthetic

## Layout
- Max-width container: 1200px
- Responsive breakpoints: 768px (mobile), 1024px (tablet)
- Section padding: 80px vertical
- Grid gap: 32px

## Components
- **Header**: Fixed, transparent to solid on scroll
- **Hero**: Full-width with background image, headline, CTA
- **Product Cards**: Image, title, description, specs, CTA
- **Features**: Icon-based grid with descriptions
- **Contact Form**: Clean form with validation
- **Footer**: Multi-column with links and contact info

## Do's and Don'ts
- DO: Use high-quality industrial imagery
- DO: Keep text concise and scannable
- DO: Use accent color sparingly for CTAs
- DON'T: Use too many colors (stick to palette)
- DON'T: Overcrowd content - let it breathe
- DON'T: Use flashy animations - keep it professional
