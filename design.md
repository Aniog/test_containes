# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand
Quiet luxury demi-fine gold jewelry. Premium-but-accessible ($30–$120). Editorial, warm, refined. Never loud, never discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- Base (deep brown-black): #1A1514 — primary text, dark backgrounds
- Surface (warm cream): #F5F0EB — page background
- Card (white): #FFFFFF — cards, elevated surfaces  
- Accent (burnished gold): #C8A15E — buttons, links, highlights, CTAs
- Accent Hover: #B08A4A — hover states
- Muted: #8B8580 — secondary text, placeholders
- Border: #E5E0D9 — hairline dividers
- Success: #5B8469 — trust bar, positive indicators

## Typography
- Headings / Product names: Playfair Display (serif) — elegant, uppercase product names with wide tracking
- Body / UI: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
- Hero headlines: Playfair Display, 4xl–6xl, light weight
- Product name on PDP: uppercase, tracking-[.15em]

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections padded py-20–py-28
- Max width: max-w-7xl, centered
- Hairline dividers: border-b border-[#E5E0D9]
- Cards: subtle shadow, rounded-none or rounded-sm (not rounded-xl)

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C8A15E] text-white hover:bg-[#B08A4A], uppercase tracking-wider
- Outline: border-[#C8A15E] text-[#C8A15E] hover:bg-[#C8A15E] hover:text-white
- Ghost: text-[#1A1514] hover:text-[#C8A15E]

## Do
- Clean serif headings with wide letter-spacing
- Thin hairline borders
- Subtle hover transitions (300ms ease)
- Editorial, spacious layouts
- Gold accent sparingly for CTAs and highlights

## Don't
- No bright/neon colors
- No large rounded corners (max rounded-sm)
- No heavy shadows (max shadow-sm)
- No discount badges, sale stickers, loud CTAs
- No generic blue links
