## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use gold accent sparingly
- Ensure strong contrast (charcoal on cream, ivory on onyx)
- Use thin hairline dividers

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/saturated colors
- Don't use rounded buttons (keep sharp/editorial)
- Don't crowd sections with too much content
- Don't use playful/bouncy animations
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (no starburst badges, no aggressive sale tags)
