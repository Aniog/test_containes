# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Identity

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#0F0F0F` (near-black) — deep refined base that makes gold jewelry glow
- **Surface/Cards**: `#1A1A1A` (dark charcoal) — for cards, dropdowns, secondary surfaces
- **Surface Elevated**: `#242424` — for hover states, active surfaces
- **Primary Accent**: `#C9A96E` (warm gold) — CTAs, links, accent highlights
- **Primary Accent Hover**: `#D4B87A` (lighter gold) — button hover
- **Text Primary**: `#F5F0EB` (warm white) — main body text
- **Text Secondary**: `#A0988E` (warm gray) — secondary text, labels
- **Text Muted**: `#6B6560` (dark warm gray) — placeholders, disabled
- **Border/Divider**: `#2A2A2A` — hairline dividers, borders
- **Border Accent**: `#C9A96E` — gold borders
- **Error**: `#D94A4A`
- **Success**: `#4CAF50`

## Typography
- **Headings (h1-h3, product names)**: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif — 400-600 weight
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE with tracking (letter-spacing) of 0.08em–0.12em
- **Body / UI**: 'Manrope', sans-serif — 300-500 weight
- **Logo**: 'Cormorant Garamond', serif, uppercase, tracked at 0.15em

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24 lg:py-32
- Content max-width: 1280px (mx-auto px-4 md:px-8)
- Card gap: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#2A2A2A] (0.5px or 1px)
- Thin borders on cards: border border-[#2A2A2A]
- Rounded corners: none or very subtle (rounded-sm)

## Shadows
- Soft shadows: shadow-[0_4px_20px_rgba(0,0,0,0.3)]
- Card hover: shadow-[0_8px_30px_rgba(201,169,110,0.1)] (gold tinted)

## Transitions
- Subtle hover transitions: transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out
- Opacity fades: transition-opacity duration-300
- Transform scales: hover:scale-[1.02]

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-[#C9A96E] text-[#0F0F0F] font-medium uppercase tracking-widest text-sm
- Hover: bg-[#D4B87A]
- Primary size: py-3 px-8
- Outline: border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E] hover:bg-[#C9A96E] hover:text-[#0F0F0F]

## DO's
- Use the warm gold accent sparingly — it should feel special
- Large editorial imagery with generous whitespace
- Thin, elegant dividers between sections
- Subtle scroll-triggered animations (fade-up)

## DON'Ts
- No bright colors, no discount banners, no sale flags
- No loud patterns or gradients
- No heavy borders or thick lines
- No generic e-commerce elements (no "Best Seller" badges, no countdown timers)
