# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry brand targeting women 25-45.

## Color Palette
- **Cream (#FAF7F2)**: Primary background, warm off-white
- **Sand (#EDE4D8)**: Subtle dividers, secondary backgrounds
- **Warm Gray (#6B5E53)**: Body text, secondary UI
- **Charcoal (#1E1C1A)**: Primary text, dark elements
- **Gold (#C8A96E)**: Primary accent, CTAs, metallic feel
- **Gold Light (#E0CFA8)**: Subtle gold highlights, hover states
- **Gold Dark (#A8894A)**: Active states, deeper gold
- **Rose (#D4A999)**: Soft accent for warmth
- **Taupe (#8B7D71)**: Muted text, secondary labels

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Playfair Display (serif), UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (tracking-widest or superwide)
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif), clean weights 300–600
- **Hierarchy**: Large serif headlines for hero/sections, medium serif for product names, small sans-serif for everything else

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: px-6 md:px-12 lg:px-20
- Max content width: 1440px
- Product cards: comfortable gaps (gap-8 or gap-10)

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: 1px, color sand (#EDE4D8)
- No heavy borders — airy and refined

## Buttons
- Primary: solid charcoal bg, white text, hover -> gold-dark bg
- Outline: charcoal border, charcoal text, hover -> charcoal bg + white text
- Accent: gold bg, white text, hover -> gold-dark bg
- All: uppercase, tracking-wider, font-medium, smooth 300ms transitions

## Images
- Warm-lit gold jewelry on dark/neutral backgrounds
- Editorial, model-worn when possible
- Large hero images, 4:3 or 3:4 product shots

## Interactions
- Subtle hover transitions (300ms ease)
- Soft box-shadows on cards
- Smooth scroll
- Cart drawer slides in from right
- Quick-add on product hover
