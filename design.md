# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual Style
Quiet luxury, warm, and editorial. 

## Colors
- Velmora Gold: `#C5A059` (Tailwind: `gold`)
- Velmora Cream: `#F9F7F2` (Tailwind: `velmora-cream`)
- Velmora Charcoal: `#1A1A1A` (Tailwind: `velmora-charcoal`)
- Velmora Sand: `#E0D8CC` (Tailwind: `velmora-sand`)

## Typography
- Headings: Serif (`Cormorant Garamond`, `Playfair Display`)
- Product Names: Serif, UPPERCASE, wide letter-spacing (`tracking-ultra-wide`)
- Body/UI: Sans-serif (`Inter`, `Manrope`)

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace
- Hairline dividers (1px with low opacity)
- Large editorial imagery

## Components
- Buttons: Solid or outlined with brand colors, 0.5s transitions.
- Cards: Minimalist, hover image reveals, quick add.
- Nav: Transparent to solid scroll transition.

## Do's
- Use SERIF for all emotional/brand headers.
- Use wide tracking for uppercase product names.
- Keep background colors light or dark (cream/charcoal), avoiding mid-tones unless for accents.

## Dont's
- Don't use bright neon colors.
- Don't use heavy borders.
- Don't crowd elements; favor whitespace.
