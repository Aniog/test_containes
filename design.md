# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background (cream):** `#FAF7F2` — warm off-white for page backgrounds
- **Foreground (charcoal):** `#1A1A1A` — near-black for body text
- **Accent (warm gold):** `#B8860B` — dark goldenrod for CTAs, links, highlights
- **Accent light:** `#D4A853` — lighter gold for hover states
- **Muted:** `#6B6B6B` — for secondary text, captions
- **Muted light:** `#F5F0E8` — warm beige for section backgrounds
- **Border:** `#E8E2D9` — subtle warm border/divider color

## Typography
- **Headings / Product names:** `Cormorant Garamond`, serif — elegant, editorial
- **Body / UI:** `Inter`, sans-serif — clean, modern, readable
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.15em
- **Heading sizes:** Hero: text-5xl/6xl, Section: text-3xl/4xl, Card: text-lg/xl

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections py-16 md:py-24
- Max content width: max-w-7xl mx-auto
- Card padding: p-4 md:p-6
- Grid gaps: gap-4 md:gap-6 lg:gap-8

## Components
- **Buttons:** Solid accent bg with cream text, or outlined with accent border. Rounded-none (sharp edges). px-8 py-3. Uppercase tracking-widest text-sm.
- **Cards:** No border-radius, subtle shadow-sm on hover, transition-all duration-300
- **Dividers:** Thin hairline (border-t border-border), not heavy
- **Hover:** opacity transitions, subtle scale(1.02), underline on text links

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep imagery large and editorial
- Use serif for emotional/brand text
- Use sans-serif for functional UI
- Maintain warm, golden tone throughout

## Don'ts
- No rounded corners on buttons or cards (use rounded-none or rounded-sm max)
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No cluttered layouts
- No discount/sale-style badges
