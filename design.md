# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
- **Mood**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine.
- **Palette**:
  - Primary (Background): `#FDFCFB` (Soft Warm White)
  - Secondary (Dark): `#1A1A1A` (Deep Charcoal for text and high contrast)
  - Accent (Metallic/Warm): `#C5A059` (Soft Gold / Brass)
  - Muted: `#F5F2F0` (Light Beige for cards/sections)
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: `Cormorant Garamond` (Serif)
  - UI/Body: `Inter` (Sans-serif)
  - Product Names: `Cormorant Garamond`, Uppercase, Letter-spacing: 0.1em.
- **Layout**: Generous whitespace, large editorial imagery, 1px hairline dividers.
- **Components**:
  - Buttons: Solid charcoal or outlined gold for premium feel.
  - Cards: Soft shadows or subtle borders.
  - Hover: Subtle transitions (opacity, slight scale).
