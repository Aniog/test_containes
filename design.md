# Visual Identity: Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Mood & Tone
- Quiet Luxury, Warm, Editorial, Premium.
- Avoid loud discounts or generic flashy elements.
- Focus on craftsmanship and timeless elegance.

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif.
- **Body & UI**: `Inter`, sans-serif.
- **Styling**: Product names in UPPERCASE with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`).

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1A1A1A` (Charcoal / Deep Base)
- **Secondary**: `#F9F7F2` (Ivory / Soft Neutral)
- **Accent**: `#C5A059` (Warm Gold Metallic)
- **Muted**: `#707070` (Refined Gray for secondary text)

## UI Elements
- **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers (`border-[0.5px]`, `border-stone-200`).
- **Whitespace**: Generous padding and margins for editorial feel.
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal with Ivory text.
  - Secondary: Outlined Charcoal or Gold.
- **Transitions**: Subtle hover transitions (soft opacity or slight color shifts).

## Components
- large editorial imagery.
- Sticky navigation with transparency.
- Full-width hero sections.
- Reels-style UGC row.
