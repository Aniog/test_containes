# SSourcing China — Design System

## Brand Identity
SSourcing China is a professional B2B China sourcing agency. The visual identity must convey trust, reliability, international professionalism, and operational expertise.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Brand Blue**: `#1a4f8a` — primary actions, headings, nav background
- **Brand Blue Dark**: `#0f3060` — hover states, footer
- **Brand Blue Light**: `#2563eb` — links, accents

### Secondary Colors
- **Accent Orange**: `#e8600a` — CTAs, highlights, badges
- **Accent Orange Hover**: `#c4500a` — CTA hover

### Neutral Colors
- **White**: `#ffffff`
- **Off-White / Background**: `#f8fafc`
- **Light Gray**: `#f1f5f9`
- **Border Gray**: `#e2e8f0`
- **Text Muted**: `#64748b`
- **Text Body**: `#334155`
- **Text Dark**: `#0f172a`

### Status Colors
- **Success Green**: `#16a34a`
- **Warning Yellow**: `#ca8a04`

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary**: Inter (Google Fonts) — all body and UI text
- **Headings**: Inter, weight 700–800

### Scale
- **Hero H1**: `text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl font-extrabold`
- **Page H1**: `text-3xl md:text-4xl font-bold`
- **Section H2**: `text-2xl md:text-3xl font-bold`
- **Card H3**: `text-xl font-semibold`
- **Body**: `text-base text-slate-700`
- **Small / Caption**: `text-sm text-slate-500`

## Spacing
- Section vertical padding: `py-16 md:py-24`
- Container max-width: `max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`
- Card padding: `p-6 md:p-8`
- Gap between cards: `gap-6 md:gap-8`

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: `bg-orange-600 hover:bg-orange-700 text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-lg`
- **Secondary**: `border-2 border-blue-800 text-blue-800 hover:bg-blue-800 hover:text-white font-semibold px-6 py-3 rounded-lg`
- **Ghost/Link**: `text-blue-700 hover:underline`

### Cards
- `bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-slate-200 p-6`
- Hover: `hover:shadow-md transition-shadow`

### Navigation
- Background: `bg-blue-900` (brand blue dark)
- Text: `text-white`
- Active link: `text-orange-400`

### Badges
- Service badge: `bg-blue-100 text-blue-800 text-xs font-medium px-2.5 py-0.5 rounded-full`
- Trust badge: `bg-green-100 text-green-800`

### Section Dividers
- Light gray background alternating: `bg-white` / `bg-slate-50`

## Do's
- Use Inter font throughout
- Keep generous whitespace — sections breathe
- Use real-looking factory/QC/shipping imagery via stock image system
- Maintain high contrast: dark text on light backgrounds
- Use orange CTAs sparingly for maximum impact
- Trust signals (years, clients, countries) in large bold numbers

## Don'ts
- No dark mode — this is a professional B2B site, light only
- No neon colors or gradients that look cheap
- No exaggerated claims ("best in the world", "guaranteed")
- No small unreadable text on colored backgrounds
- No crowded layouts — max 3 columns on desktop for cards
