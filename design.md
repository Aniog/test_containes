# Velmora Fine Jewelry Design System

## Visual Style
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine jewelry.

## Colors
- **Primary**: #121212 (Deep refined base) - `bg-primary`, `text-primary`
- **Accent**: #C5A059 (Soft Gold) - `bg-accent`, `text-accent`
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (Clean White) / #FAF9F6 (Warm Off-white)
- **Muted**: #717171 - `text-muted-foreground`
- **Border**: #E5E5E5 - `border-border`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif) - `font-serif`
- **Body/UI**: Inter / Manrope (Sans-serif) - `font-sans`
- **Product Names**: Uppercase, wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`)

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Premium Solid: `premium-button` (Solid black, white text)
  - Premium Outline: `outline-button` (Black border, white bg, black text)
- **Dividers**: Thin hairline dividers (`hairline-divider`)
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, editorial layout.

## Transitions
- Subtle hover transitions, soft shadows, smooth animations.
