# Design System: VELMORA Fine Jewelry

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: VELMORA Fine Jewelry
- **Vibe**: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, premium demi-fine.
- **Tone**: Sophisticated, timeless, accessible.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Serif)
  - Usage: H1, H2, H3, Product Names.
  - Styles: Medium (500), Semi-bold (600).
- **Body & UI**: Inter (Sans-serif)
  - Usage: Body text, UI elements, button labels.
  - Styles: Regular (400), Medium (500).
- **Product Names**: Uppercase with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-[0.2em]`).

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Base)**: `#1A1A1A` (Stone Black/Deep Charcoal)
- **Secondary (Background)**: `#F9F7F2` (Warm Parchment/Soft Cream)
- **Accent (Metallic)**: `#AF9463` (Muted Gold)
- **Muted Accent**: `#E5E0D5` (Soft Beige)
- **Text**:
  - Main: `#1A1A1A`
  - Muted: `#666666`
- **Surface**:
  - Card Bg: `#FFFFFF`
  - Border: `#E5E5E5`

## Visual Elements
- **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers (`border-stone-200`).
- **Whitespace**: Generous, high-end editorial feel.
- **Imagery**: Large, high-quality jewelry photography on models or clean neutral backgrounds.
- **Transitions**: Soft fade-in, subtle hover scales, smooth scroll.
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal with Gold or White text.
  - Secondary: Outlined Gold or Charcoal.

## Tailwind Classes (Standardized)
- `font-serif`: Cormorant Garamond
- `font-sans`: Inter
- `bg-parchment`: `#F9F7F2`
- `text-charcoal`: `#1A1A1A`
- `text-gold`: `#AF9463`
- `border-hairline`: `border-[0.5px] border-stone-200`
- `tracking-widest-serif`: `font-serif uppercase tracking-[0.2em]`
