# ARTITECT MACHINERY - Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: ARTITECT MACHINERY
- **Industry**: Industrial sheet metal folding machines
- **Tone**: Elegant, professional, trustworthy, yet user-friendly

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#1a1a2e` (Deep navy/charcoal - sophistication)
- **Secondary**: `#16213e` (Dark blue - industrial reliability)
- **Accent**: `#e94560` (Coral red - action/CTA buttons)
- **Accent Secondary**: `#0f3460` (Medium blue - depth)
- **Light**: `#f8f9fa` (Off-white - backgrounds)
- **Text Primary**: `#1a1a2e` (Dark for readability)
- **Text Secondary**: `#6c757d` (Muted gray)
- **Text Light**: `#ffffff` (White for dark backgrounds)
- **Border**: `#e9ecef` (Light gray)

## Typography
- **Headings**: "Playfair Display" - elegant serif for brand sophistication
- **Body**: "Inter" - clean, readable sans-serif
- **Font Sizes**:
  - Hero title: 56px (desktop), 36px (mobile)
  - Section titles: 42px (desktop), 28px (mobile)
  - Subheadings: 24px
  - Body: 16px
  - Small: 14px

## Layout & Spacing
- **Max content width**: 1200px
- **Section padding**: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Component spacing**: 24px between elements
- **Card padding**: 32px
- **Border radius**: 8px (cards), 4px (buttons)

## Visual Effects
- **Shadows**: Subtle box-shadow for cards: `0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- **Hover transitions**: 0.3s ease for all interactive elements
- **Button hover**: Slight lift with enhanced shadow
- **Image hover**: Subtle scale (1.02) with smooth transition

## Components

### Header/Navigation
- Fixed position, white background with subtle shadow on scroll
- Logo on left, navigation links center, CTA button right
- Mobile: Hamburger menu

### Hero Section
- Full-width with industrial background image
- Overlay gradient for text readability
- Large headline, subheadline, and dual CTA buttons

### Product Cards
- White background with subtle shadow
- Product image (aspect ratio 4:3)
- Product name, brief description
- "Learn More" link with arrow
- Hover: slight lift effect

### Features Section
- Icon-based feature cards in grid
- 3-column layout (desktop), 1-column (mobile)
- Subtle icons in accent color

### About Section
- Split layout: image left, text right
- Company story and statistics

### Contact/CTA Section
- Dark background with accent color elements
- Contact form with elegant styling
- Company contact information

### Footer
- Dark background matching brand
- Multi-column layout with links
- Copyright and social links

## Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: < 640px
- **Tablet**: 640px - 1024px
- **Desktop**: > 1024px

## Do's
- Use generous white space for elegance
- Maintain consistent spacing throughout
- Use high-quality industrial imagery
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use clear visual hierarchy

## Don'ts
- Don't use too many colors (keep it cohesive)
- Don't clutter the design with too many elements
- Don't use generic stock photos - prefer industrial/machinery context
- Don't make it feel cold or impersonal - keep user-friendly
