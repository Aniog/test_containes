# Visual Identity - Velmora Fine Jewelry

## Brand Essence
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial, and sophisticated. A direct-to-consumer demi-fine jewelry brand that feels premium yet accessible.

## Typography
- **Headings & Product Names**: `Cormorant Garamond`, Serif.
  - Product names use `uppercase` with `tracking-widest` (letter-spacing).
- **Body & UI**: `Inter` / `Manrope`, Sans-serif.
  - Clean, legible, and modern.

## Color Palette
- **Background (Cream/Off-white)**: `#FDFBF7` (Tailwind: `bg-[#FDFBF7]`)
- **Primary Text (Charcoal)**: `#1A1A1A` (Tailwind: `text-[#1A1A1A]`)
- **Accent (Gold)**: `#D4AF37` (Tailwind: `text-[#D4AF37]`, `bg-[#D4AF37]`)
- **Muted (Grey)**: `#8A8A8A` (Tailwind: `text-[#8A8A8A]`)
- **Border/Divider (Hairline)**: `#E5E5E5` (Tailwind: `border-[#E5E5E5]`)

## UI Elements
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace.
- **Borders**: Thin hairline dividers (`border-[0.5px]`).
- **Cards**: Minimalist, high-quality editorial imagery.
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Charcoal with White text.
  - Secondary: Outlined Charcoal or Gold.
- **Transitions**: Subtle hover effects, soft fades.

## Do's
- Use large editorial imagery.
- Maintain wide letter spacing for uppercase text.
- Use thin dividers to separate editorial sections.

## Don'ts
- No loud or vibrant "sale" banners.
- No rounded corners on main containers (keep it sharp/elegant).
- Avoid overcrowding layouts.
