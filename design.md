# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine gold jewelry.
NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory cream) — site base
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` — cards, elevated surfaces
- Deep Base: `#1A1512` (warm espresso black) — primary text, footer, deep accents
- Accent Gold: `#C8A45C` (warm champagne gold) — buttons, links, highlights
- Accent Gold Hover: `#B8953F` — hover states
- Accent Light: `#F5EDE0` (soft champagne) — subtle backgrounds, badges
- Muted Taupe: `#8C7E72` — secondary text, dividers
- Border: `#E8E2D9` (warm bone) — hairline borders
- Muted Text: `#6B625A` — tertiary text, labels
- Star Gold: `#D4A853` — rating stars

## Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif) — hero, product names, section titles
- Product Names: UPPERCASE with letter-spacing: 0.15em
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) — descriptions, UI, navigation
- Accent Serif: Playfair Display italic for subheadings/captions

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 md:px-6
- Card gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Components
- Buttons: Solid gold accent (`bg-[#C8A45C] hover:bg-[#B8953F] text-white`) or outlined (`border border-[#C8A45C] text-[#C8A45C]`)
- Hairline dividers: `border-t border-[#E8E2D9]`
- Card hover: subtle scale 1.02 + shadow
- Transitions: duration-300 ease-out

## Do's
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Large editorial imagery
- Subtle hover transitions
- Thin hairline borders and dividers
- Serif headings, uppercase product names with wide tracking

## Don'ts
- No harsh black (#000) text
- No bright/distracting colors
- No heavy box shadows
- No discount badges or sale banners
- No overly rounded corners (use rounded-sm or rounded-md)
