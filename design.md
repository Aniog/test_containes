# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry.
- NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background (Light):** #FAF7F2 (warm cream)
- **Secondary Background:** #F3EDE4 (warm sand)
- **Dark Base:** #1A1714 (deep warm charcoal)
- **Text Primary:** #1A1714 (deep warm charcoal)
- **Text Secondary:** #6B6156 (warm muted brown)
- **Text Muted:** #9C9488 (warm stone)
- **Accent (Gold):** #C9A96E (warm metallic gold)
- **Accent Hover:** #B8954F (deeper gold)
- **Accent Light:** #E8D5B0 (light gold wash)
- **Border:** #E5DDD2 (warm hairline)
- **White:** #FFFFFF

## Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond, serif. Uppercase with wide letter-spacing for product names.
- **Body/UI:** Inter, sans-serif. Clean and legible.
- **Font Sizes:**
  - Hero headline: text-5xl md:text-7xl, tracking-wide
  - Section headings: text-3xl md:text-4xl, tracking-wide
  - Product names: text-lg, uppercase, tracking-[0.15em]
  - Body: text-base, leading-relaxed
  - Small/caption: text-sm, text-secondary

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace. Sections: py-20 md:py-28
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl, centered
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-[#E5DDD2]

## Buttons
- **Primary (Accent):** bg-[#C9A96E] text-white hover:bg-[#B8954F], px-8 py-3, tracking-wider, uppercase, text-sm, font-medium
- **Secondary (Outlined):** border border-[#C9A96E] text-[#C9A96E] hover:bg-[#C9A96E] hover:text-white, px-8 py-3, tracking-wider, uppercase, text-sm
- **Subtle:** text-[#1A1714] underline underline-offset-4 hover:text-[#C9A96E]

## Cards
- Product cards: bg-white, subtle shadow on hover, rounded-none (sharp corners for editorial feel)
- Image aspect ratio: 4x5 for product shots
- Hover: second image fade-in, slight scale

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth transitions: transition-all duration-300
- Hover lifts: hover:-translate-y-1
- No bouncy or playful animations

## Do's
- Use warm, muted tones throughout
- Keep generous whitespace
- Use serif for emotional/editorial moments
- Use thin hairline dividers between sections
- Ensure strong text contrast on all backgrounds

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright or saturated colors
- Don't use rounded corners (keep editorial/sharp)
- Don't use playful or bouncy animations
- Don't use generic e-commerce patterns (badges, countdown timers)
- Don't use light text on light backgrounds
