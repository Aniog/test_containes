# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: quiet luxury, warm, editorial
- Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `ivory` (#FDFBF7) — warm off-white
- **Surface**: `cream` (#F7F3ED) — slightly darker warm neutral for cards
- **Foreground/Text**: `charcoal` (#1C1917) — rich warm black
- **Muted text**: `stone` (#78716C) — warm gray for secondary text
- **Accent**: `gold` (#B8860B) — dark goldenrod, the primary brand accent
- **Accent light**: `gold-light` (#D4A843) — lighter gold for hover states
- **Accent dark**: `gold-dark` (#8B6508) — deeper gold for active states
- **Border**: `sand` (#E7E0D6) — warm hairline dividers

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weights 300–600
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weights 300–500
- **Product names**: Cormorant Garamond, UPPERCASE, letter-spacing: 0.15em
- **Nav links**: Inter, uppercase, letter-spacing: 0.08em, font-weight 400

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: sections use py-16 md:py-24
- Max content width: max-w-7xl (1280px)
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8
- Card padding: p-4 md:p-6

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-sand (1px)
- No heavy borders or box shadows on cards
- Subtle shadow on hover: shadow-sm

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-gold text-white hover:bg-gold-light, rounded-none (sharp edges), px-8 py-3
- Secondary/Outlined: border border-gold text-gold hover:bg-gold hover:text-white
- All buttons: uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em, text-sm, font-medium

## Hover & Transitions
- All interactive elements: transition-all duration-300
- Product cards: image scale on hover (scale-105)
- Links: opacity transition

## Do's
- Use generous whitespace
- Keep imagery large and editorial
- Use serif for emotional/brand text
- Use thin hairline dividers
- Keep UI minimal and restrained

## Don'ts
- No bright/neon colors
- No heavy drop shadows
- No rounded buttons (use sharp/square)
- No cluttered layouts
- No generic e-commerce feel
- No discount badges or sale stickers
