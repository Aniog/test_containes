# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — not loud, not discount-looking.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF8F5` (warm ivory) — main page background
- **Surface**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white) — cards, overlays
- **Primary Text**: `#1A1A1A` (near black) — headings, body
- **Secondary Text**: `#6B6560` (warm taupe) — captions, muted text
- **Accent**: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod) — CTAs, links, hover states
- **Accent Hover**: `#996F0A` (darker gold)
- **Border**: `#E8E4DF` (warm light gray) — dividers, card borders
- **Star Rating**: `#C9A96E` (soft gold)
- **Success**: `#4A7C59` (muted sage)
- **Overlay**: `rgba(26, 26, 26, 0.4)` — modal backdrops

## Typography
- **Headings / Product Names**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) — elegant, editorial
  - Hero: 48–64px, weight 300 (light)
  - Section titles: 32–40px, weight 400
  - Product names: 16–18px, weight 400, UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em
- **Body / UI**: Inter (sans-serif) — clean, readable
  - Body: 15–16px, weight 400
  - Small/captions: 13px, weight 400
  - Buttons: 13px, weight 500, UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.1em

## Spacing
- Section padding: 80–120px vertical (desktop), 48–64px (mobile)
- Card gap: 24px
- Component padding: 16–24px

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: 1px solid `#E8E4DF`
- Card borders: 1px solid `#E8E4DF`
- Border radius: 2px (minimal, refined)

## Shadows
- Card hover: `0 4px 20px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.06)`
- Dropdown/drawer: `0 8px 40px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.12)`

## Buttons
- **Primary (accent)**: solid gold background, white text, uppercase, 13px, letter-spacing 0.1em
- **Secondary (outline)**: transparent background, gold border, gold text
- Hover: slight opacity change or darker gold

## Animations
- Hover transitions: 300ms ease
- Image zoom on hover: scale(1.03)
- Fade in: 400ms ease
- Cart drawer slide: 300ms ease-out

## Do's
- Generous whitespace
- Large editorial imagery
- Thin hairline dividers
- Restrained accent color
- Subtle hover transitions
- Soft shadows
- Uppercase product names with wide letter-spacing

## Don'ts
- No loud colors or gradients
- No discount-looking badges or banners
- No generic e-commerce patterns
- No heavy borders or thick dividers
- No bright/neon accent colors
- No cluttered layouts
