# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Visual Identity
- Mood: Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry — NOT loud, NOT discount-looking.
- Target: Women 25–45, gifting and self-purchase, $30–$120 price point.

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#FAF7F2` (warm cream) — primary page background
- **Warm accent**: `#F5EDE3` — section backgrounds, cards
- **Sand**: `#E8DDD0` — borders, dividers, subtle backgrounds
- **Gold**: `#C9A96E` — primary accent, CTAs, interactive elements
- **Gold dark**: `#A8863F` — hover states on gold
- **Charcoal**: `#2C2C2C` — primary text
- **Espresso**: `#1A1612` — hero overlays, dark sections
- **Muted**: `#8A8279` — secondary text, captions
- **Muted light**: `#B5AFA8` — placeholder text, disabled states

## Typography
- **Headings**: Cormorant Garamond (serif), weights 400–600
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif), weights 300–500
- **Product names**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.25em, serif
- **Buttons/Labels**: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing 0.15em, sans-serif, text-xs

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: px-5 md:px-8 lg:px-12 xl:px-20
- Vertical rhythm: py-16 md:py-24 for sections
- Card gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Hairline dividers: border-t border-brand-sand
- No heavy borders or shadows
- Soft shadows only on hover: shadow-sm

## Buttons
- Primary: bg-brand-gold text-white, hover:bg-brand-gold-dark
- Outline: border-brand-gold text-brand-gold, hover fills
- All uppercase, tracking-wide, text-xs, py-3.5 px-8
- Transition: duration-300 ease-out

## Images
- Editorial, warm-lit, gold jewelry on neutral/dark backgrounds
- Product cards: 3x4 ratio
- Hero: 16x9 or full-bleed
- UGC: 9x16 vertical

## Animations
- Subtle, smooth transitions (300ms ease-out)
- Fade-in on scroll
- Hover: image swap on product cards, gentle scale on category tiles
- Cart drawer slides from right

## Do's
- Use serif for all headings and product names
- Keep whitespace generous
- Use gold sparingly as accent
- Ensure strong text contrast on all backgrounds

## Don'ts
- Don't use bright/saturated colors
- Don't use heavy shadows or thick borders
- Don't use sans-serif for headings
- Don't crowd elements — let them breathe
