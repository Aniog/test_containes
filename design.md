# SSourcing China - Visual Style Guide

## Brand Identity
- **Tone**: Professional, Reliable, Clear, Practical, Trustworthy.
- **Goal**: Qualified B2B Sourcing Inquiries.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: #0F172A (Slate-900) - Main text, headers, professionalism.
- **Secondary**: #2563EB (Blue-600) - CTAs, highlights, trustworthiness.
- **Accent**: #F59E0B (Amber-500) - Trust points, important notices.
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (White) / #F8FAFC (Slate-50).
- **Surface**: #FFFFFF (White).
- **Text**: 
  - Main: #1E293B (Slate-800)
  - Muted: #64748B (Slate-500)

## Typography
- **Font Family**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Google Fonts).
- **Headings**: Slate-900, Bold/Semi-bold.
- **Body**: Slate-800, weight 400.

## Spacing & Layout
- **Containers**: Max-width 1280px (xl).
- **Padding**: 
  - Sections: py-16 / py-24.
  - Components: p-4 to p-8.
- **Grid Range**: 1 to 4 columns depending on device and content.

## Components Style
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: bg-blue-600 text-white rounded-md px-6 py-3 font-medium hover:bg-blue-700 transition.
  - Secondary: border border-slate-200 text-slate-900 hover:bg-slate-50 transition.
- **Cards**: bg-white border border-slate-100 shadow-sm rounded-lg p-6.
- **Forms**: Input fields with slate-200 borders, blue-600 focus ring.

## Visuals
- Realistic factory, QC, and shipping photography. 
- Use of `data-strk-img` and `data-strk-bg` for stock system.
