An AI site generator is a tool that builds a complete, ready-to-publish website from a short description of what you need. Instead of starting with a blank canvas or hunting through templates, you tell the generator about your business, project, or goal—and the AI designs and assembles the site for you in seconds. The result is a real, live site that you can customize further or publish as-is.

A template is a fixed layout that you populate with your own text and images. A generator, by contrast, writes the content, picks the structure, and composes the design based on your description. Templates require manual assembly; generators produce a finished starting point from a single prompt. You still have full control to edit and tweak everything afterward.
