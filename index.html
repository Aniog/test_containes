Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator is a tool that turns a short text description of your business or project into a complete, responsive website. Instead of dragging boxes around or picking a blank template, you tell it what your site is for and it builds the structure, copy, and layout for you in seconds.

Strikingly's generators are specialized versions of this idea, tuned for specific goals like a portfolio, a restaurant website, or a one-page launch.

A template gives you the same starting layout every time, with placeholder text you have to replace yourself. A generator looks at what you describe and produces a site already written for your specific business, with sections and copy that fit your situation, not a generic one.

Yes. You can generate, edit, and publish a site without a credit card. Paid plans add features like custom domains, more storage, and advanced commerce, but the core build-and-launch flow is free to try.

You can build small business sites, landing pages, portfolios, blogs, online stores, and link-in-bio pages. The generators on this page each target a specific use case, but every one of them produces a real, editable Strikingly site you fully own.

Fully. After the generator builds the first version, you can edit any text, swap images, rearrange sections, change colors, and add new pages. The output is a starting point you finish, not a fixed result.

Yes. Every site produced by a Strikingly generator is responsive by default. The layout, image sizes, and navigation adapt automatically so visitors get a clean experience on phones, tablets, and desktops without any extra work on your part.
