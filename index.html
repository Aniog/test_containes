Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI site generator?

An AI site generator is a tool that takes a short description of what you're building and produces a complete, ready-to-edit website. Instead of starting from a blank template and arranging every section yourself, you describe the goal and the AI assembles a first draft for you.

You can then edit any text, image, or layout to make the site your own.

How is a generator different from a template?

A template is a fixed layout that you fill with your own content. A generator looks at what you described and produces a site that already includes relevant sections, sample copy, and structure tailored to that goal.

You still get full editing control afterwards, you just start much closer to the finish line.

Are these generators free to use?

Yes. You can generate, customize, and publish a site on Strikingly's free plan without entering payment details. Paid plans unlock features like a custom domain and advanced ecommerce, but they are optional.

What kinds of sites can I build?

Business sites, personal sites, portfolios, landing pages, blogs, online stores, and link-in-bio hubs are all supported. The categories above cover the most common starting points; if your goal isn't listed, the general AI Website Generator can handle most cases.

Can I customize what the generator produces?

Completely. After the generator drafts your site, every section, color, image, and word is editable in the Strikingly editor. You can rearrange sections, swap themes, add new pages, and change anything that doesn't fit your brand.

Do generated sites work on mobile?

Yes. Every site Strikingly produces is responsive by default, so it adapts to phones and tablets without extra work. You can preview the mobile layout in the editor before publishing.
