FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is an AI site generator?

An AI site generator is a tool that builds a complete, customized website based on a simple text description. Instead of starting from scratch or dragging components around, you tell the AI what your business is, and it generates the layout, images, and starter copy for you instantly.

How is a generator different from a template?

A template is a pre-designed layout where you manually replace placeholder text and images with your own content. A generator creates a unique site specifically for your input, meaning the layout, images, text, and structure are tailored to your business from the moment it's generated.

Are these generators free to use?

Yes, you can use our AI generators to create and preview sites for free. We offer free publishing options as well as premium plans if you need advanced features like a custom domain name or e-commerce tools.

What kinds of sites can I build?

You can build almost anything. Our system covers everything from simple one-page personal portfolios and link-in-bio pages to full multi-page business websites, online stores, and professional blogs. If you can describe it, our AI can generate a starting point for it.

Can I customize what the generator produces?

Absolutely. The generated site is just the starting point. Once it's built, you have full control in our editor to change colors, fonts, images, text, add new sections, or rearrange the layout to perfectly match your vision.

Do generated sites work on mobile?

Yes, every site generated by our AI is fully responsive. It will automatically adapt and look great on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers without you needing to design separate mobile versions.
