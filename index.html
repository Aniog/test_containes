FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about AI site generators.

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to build a complete website from a short description. Instead of picking a template and customizing it manually, you tell the AI what your business or project is about, and it creates a site with relevant content, layout, and design in seconds. Strikingly's generators are tailored to specific industries and site types so the result is more focused and useful from the start.

A template is a static starting point. You pick a design and then spend time replacing placeholder text, swapping images, and rearranging sections to fit your needs. A generator is dynamic. You describe what you want, and the AI builds a site with content and structure that matches your description. You still get full editing control after generation, but the starting point is much closer to a finished site.
