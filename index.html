Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to create a complete website based on a short description of your business, project, or goal. Instead of starting from a blank canvas or manually customizing a template, you tell the AI what you need, and it assembles a fully designed site with layout, content, images, and navigation in seconds. Think of it as having a designer and developer rolled into one, available instantly.
A template is a fixed design that you fill with your own content. It looks the same for everyone who uses it. A generator, on the other hand, creates a unique site tailored to your specific inputs. It considers your industry, your goals, and your preferences to produce a site that fits you, not a generic layout. This means less manual customization and a result that feels more personal from the start.
