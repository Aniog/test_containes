Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator is a tool that creates a complete website based on a short text description. Instead of choosing a template and customizing it manually, you describe your business, project, or goal in a sentence or two, and the AI builds a site tailored to your needs. It handles layout, content structure, and design automatically.

A template is a fixed design that you fill with your own content. A generator uses AI to create a unique layout, copy, and structure based on your specific inputs. Templates can save time, but generators produce a site that is uniquely suited to your purpose, with less manual editing required.

Yes, you can generate and customize a site for free. There is no credit card required to start. Some advanced features, such as custom domains, ecommerce checkout, and premium analytics, are available on paid plans, but you can build and preview your site without any cost.

Our generators cover a wide range of use cases: business websites, online stores, portfolios, landing pages, blogs, wedding sites, restaurant sites, and link-in-bio pages. If you have a specific need not listed here, the AI site builder can create custom sites for almost any purpose.

Absolutely. After the generator creates your site, you can edit every part of it: text, images, colors, fonts, layout, and more. Our site editor gives you full control, so you can start with an AI-generated foundation and fine-tune it until it matches your vision.

Yes, all sites generated by Strikingly are fully responsive. They adapt automatically to desktops, tablets, and phones. Our generators build mobile-first layouts by default, so your site looks great and is easy to navigate on any screen size.
