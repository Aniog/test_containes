Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about AI site generators.

An AI site generator is a tool that takes a short description of your business, goal, or industry and turns it into a working website. You write a sentence or two, the generator builds a layout, writes the copy, picks images, and gives you a live, editable site in seconds.

Each generator on this page is tuned to a specific kind of site — a portfolio, an online store, a wedding page — so the starting point is closer to what you actually need than a blank canvas would be.

A template is a fixed design you fill in yourself. Every template-based site looks the same until you spend time customizing it.

A generator is the opposite starting point. It asks for a sentence about your business and uses that to choose the layout, write the copy, and assemble the pages. You get a site that is already shaped around you, and you can still customize anything you want from there.

Yes. You can generate, customize, and publish a site from any of these generators without a credit card.

Strikingly also has paid plans that unlock a custom domain, more storage, advanced ecommerce features, and priority support. You only pay if you choose to upgrade later.

Whatever you need a website for. The generators on this page are organized by category — websites, landing pages, portfolios, blogs, online stores, and link-in-bio pages — and each one is tuned to the conventions of that kind of site.

You can also mix and match: a small business owner might start with the AI Website Generator and add a blog or a store as they grow. Each generator builds on the same underlying editor, so all your pages live in one place.

Yes. The generator gives you a complete first draft, and from there you can edit any text, swap any image, change colors and fonts, add or remove sections, and rearrange pages.

Nothing is locked. If you decide to start a different generator's site from scratch, you can keep the editor and just describe a new direction.

Yes. Every generator produces a responsive site that automatically adjusts to phone, tablet, and desktop screens. The editor lets you preview each breakpoint and make adjustments if you want a different look on small screens.

Mobile responsiveness is part of the underlying builder, so you don't have to remember to enable it or pick a mobile-friendly template. It is the default.
