FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is an AI site generator?

An AI site generator uses artificial intelligence to create a complete website based on a short description of your business, project, or goal. Instead of starting from a blank page or a rigid template, you describe what you need and the generator assembles a ready-to-edit site with pages, layout, and content.

How is a generator different from a template?

A template is a pre-designed layout you fill in manually. A generator creates a unique starting point tailored to your description, including relevant sections and sample content. You still get full control to edit, but the initial build is much faster and more relevant.

Are these generators free to use?

Yes. You can generate, customize, and publish a site without a credit card. Paid plans are available for advanced features, custom domains, and removing Strikingly branding, but the core generator experience is free to start.

What kinds of sites can I build?

You can build business sites, personal sites, landing pages, portfolios, blogs, online stores, link-in-bio pages, event sites, and more. Each generator is tuned for a specific type of site so the structure and content suggestions match your goal.

Can I customize what the generator produces?

Absolutely. After generation you can edit text, swap images, reorder sections, add pages, change colors, and connect your own domain. The generator gives you a strong starting point; you decide the final result.

Do generated sites work on mobile?

Yes. Every site produced by a Strikingly generator is responsive by default and will look great on phones, tablets, and desktops without extra work.
