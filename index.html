An AI site generator is a tool that creates a complete website from a short description of what you need. You can then customize anything before publishing.

A template is a pre-designed layout that you fill in yourself. A generator uses AI to create a unique site based on your specific description. With a template, every user starts from the same design. With a generator, the structure, content, and style are personalized to match what you describe.

Yes, you can generate, customize, and publish a site for free. Strikingly offers a free plan that includes AI-generated sites, mobile-responsive design, and basic features. You can build websites, landing pages, portfolios, blogs, online stores, and link-in-bio pages. Each generator is tuned for a specific use case, but you are not locked in. After generating a site, you can change the layout, add sections, switch colors, and adjust everything to fit your brand.

Absolutely. The AI gives you a starting point, not a finished product. After generation, you have full control over every element. Change text, swap images, adjust colors, add or remove sections, and tweak the layout. The generator saves time on the first draft; you make it yours.

Yes, every site generated by Strikingly is responsive by default. That means it automatically adapts to look great on phones, tablets, and desktops. You do not need to build a separate mobile version. The AI handles the responsive layout as part of the generation process.
