Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator is a tool that turns a short description of your business or project into a complete, ready-to-publish website. Instead of starting from a blank template and picking sections one by one, you tell the generator what you're building and it assembles a sensible first draft for you.

You can then edit any text, swap any image, and rearrange anything. The generator is a starting point that saves you the slowest hour of website building, not a black box you have to live with.

A template is the same starting layout for everyone. A generator looks at what you wrote about your site and produces a layout, copy, and section choices tailored to that goal. A wedding site and a restaurant site that both start from the same generator will end up with very different first drafts.
