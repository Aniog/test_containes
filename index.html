Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about how the generators work.

An AI site generator is a tool that builds a working website from a short description of what you want. You tell it about your business, audience, or goal, and it produces a full draft, including layout, copy, and stock imagery, in seconds.

Each generator on this page is tuned for a different kind of site, so the result feels appropriate for that use case from the start.

A template is a fixed layout with placeholder text and images that you fill in. A generator skips that step. You describe what your site is for, and the AI returns a draft with your subject already in place. You can still pick a different look, swap sections, and edit anything by hand.

Yes. You can generate, customize, and publish a site without paying or entering a credit card. Paid plans unlock features like custom domains, advanced ecommerce, and removal of the Strikingly subdomain.

Most kinds. Business sites, personal sites, portfolios, blogs, online stores, landing pages, link-in-bio pages, and more. The directory above is the easiest way to see if there's a generator tuned for your use case, but you can always start from the general AI site builder if your goal doesn't match a specific generator.

Anything in the draft is editable. Rewrite the copy, swap images, reorder sections, change colors, add new pages, or hand off to a designer. The generator is the starting point, not the end of the road.

Yes. Every generator produces a responsive site that adapts to phone, tablet, and desktop screen sizes by default. You can still tweak the mobile layout in the editor if you want a specific arrangement on small screens.
