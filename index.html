Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator is a tool that creates a fully functional website based on a short description of your business, project, or goal. Instead of designing pages by hand or picking through templates, you tell the AI what you need and it builds a complete site for you in seconds. The result is a real website with layout, content, and design that you can edit further.

A generator creates a site from scratch using artificial intelligence, tailored to your specific needs. A template is a pre-made design that you fill with your own content. Generators adapt to your description, industry, and goals, while templates require you to fit your content into a fixed structure. Generators also handle common setup tasks like SEO, mobile responsiveness, and site structure automatically.

Yes, every generator on this page is free to use. You can create a site, customize it, and preview it without entering a credit card. If you choose to publish your site to a custom domain, Strikingly offers affordable paid plans that unlock additional features like custom domains, analytics, and e-commerce functionality.

You can build almost any type of site: business websites, landing pages, portfolios, blogs, online stores, link-in-bio pages, wedding sites, restaurant sites, and more. Each generator is tuned for a specific use case, so you get a site that fits your needs from the start. The AI adjusts the design, layout, and content structure based on the generator you choose.

Absolutely. After the AI generates your site, you can edit text, change colors, swap images, rearrange sections, and modify any element using Strikingly's drag-and-drop editor. The generator gives you a head start, but you have full control over the final result. No coding required.

Yes. Every site produced by Strikingly's generators is fully responsive and looks great on phones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile optimization is built into every generator, so you don't need to adjust anything for mobile viewing.
