FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

An AI site generator is a tool that builds a complete website for you based on a short description. Instead of picking a template and dragging blocks around, you describe what you need in plain language — for example, “I’m a yoga instructor in Austin who needs a site for class schedules and booking” — and the AI generates a full, styled site in seconds.

Every generator on this page is powered by Strikingly’s AI builder. Each one is tuned for a specific goal (portfolio, store, landing page, etc.) so the AI knows what sections, features, and layouts to include.

A template is a static layout you manually fill in. You pick a design, then spend time replacing placeholder text, uploading images, and rearranging sections to match your needs.

A generator creates a custom site from scratch based on your input. The AI writes the copy, picks the images, structures the sections, and applies a cohesive design — all tailored to what you described. You can still customize everything afterward, but the heavy lifting is done in seconds.
