Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI site generators.

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to create a complete website based on a short description of your business or project. Instead of choosing a template and editing every page manually, you tell the AI what you need and it builds a fully designed, ready-to-publish site in seconds. Strikingly's AI generators are tailored for specific use cases—portfolios, stores, landing pages, blogs, and more—so the output is relevant from the start.

A template is a fixed layout that you fill with your own content. You still need to replace placeholder text, swap images, and reconfigure sections to match your brand. An AI generator builds a site from scratch based on your specific input, so the layout, structure, and content are generated for your use case. This saves time and produces a site that feels custom rather than assembled from pre-built pieces.

Yes, all of Strikingly's AI generators are free to use. You can generate a complete website, customize it with our drag-and-drop editor, and publish it on a Strikingly subdomain without paying anything. Premium plans are available if you need a custom domain, additional storage, e-commerce features, or advanced analytics, but there is no charge to generate and preview your site.

Strikingly's AI generators cover a wide range of site types, including business websites, one-page landing pages, professional portfolios, blogs, online stores, and link-in-bio pages. Each generator is optimized for its category, so a portfolio generator produces different layouts and sections than a store generator. If your project doesn't fit one of these categories, you can always start with the general AI Site Builder, which adapts to any description.

Absolutely. After the AI generates your site, you can edit everything using Strikingly's visual editor. Change the layout, swap colors and fonts, add or remove sections, upload your own images, and edit any text. The AI gives you a strong starting point, but every part of the site remains fully customizable so the final result reflects your brand and personality.

Yes. All sites generated by Strikingly's AI are fully responsive and work on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. The layouts adapt automatically to different screen sizes, and you can preview the mobile version of your site at any time in the editor. Mobile responsiveness is built into every generator by default, so you don't need to worry about separate mobile settings or custom breakpoints.
