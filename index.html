FAQ

Frequently asked questions

An AI site generator is a tool that takes a short description of what you want to build and produces a complete website in seconds. You give it the basics about your business or project, and it picks a layout, writes the copy, and arranges photos and sections for you.

Every generator on this page is tuned for a specific job, like a restaurant site or a portfolio for designers, so the result lands closer to what you actually need.

A template is a static starting point. You drag in your own copy and images and rearrange the sections yourself. A generator goes one step further: it reads the description you write and decides which sections, headlines, and layout fit your goal, then assembles the site automatically.

You can still customize anything afterward. The generator just gets you to a working draft faster.

Yes. You can generate, edit, and publish a site without a credit card. Paid plans add things like custom domains, advanced analytics, and store features, but the generation itself and a published Strikingly subdomain are free to start.

Everything is editable. After the generator builds your draft, you can rewrite copy, swap photos, change the color theme, add or remove sections, and connect a custom domain. The generator gets you to a strong starting point; from there it's a regular Strikingly site.
