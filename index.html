FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

An AI site generator is a tool that creates a complete website for you based on a short description. Instead of starting with a blank page or choosing from hundreds of templates, you simply describe what you need, and our AI builds a custom site in seconds. Each generator is optimized for a specific goal—like portfolios, online stores, or landing pages—so the result is tailored to what you're building.

A template gives you a static layout that you manually fill in. A generator creates a unique site structure based on your specific input. With a template, you're locked into a fixed design. With a generator, the AI adapts the layout, sections, and content suggestions to match what you describe, giving you a more custom result from the start.

Yes, you can generate, customize, and publish a site for free. Our free plan includes hosting, a Strikingly subdomain, and all the core features you need to get online. If you want a custom domain, advanced ecommerce, or more storage, you can upgrade to a paid plan later. But there's no credit card required to start.

You can build almost any kind of site: business websites, portfolios, blogs, online stores, landing pages, event pages, and more. Each generator is tuned for its purpose, so a portfolio generator will suggest galleries and about sections, while a store generator will set up product pages and a checkout flow.

Absolutely. The generator gives you a complete starting point, but you have full control from there. You can edit text, swap images, change colors, add or remove sections, and rearrange the layout. Our editor is drag-and-drop simple, so you don't need any technical skills to make the site truly yours.

Yes, every site generated with Strikingly is fully responsive and mobile-ready from the first second. Your site will automatically adapt to look great on phones, tablets, and desktops. You can preview how it looks on different screen sizes right in the editor and make adjustments if needed.
