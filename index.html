An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to build a complete website from a short text description. You can then customize everything before publishing.
A template is a fixed starting design that you must fill in and adapt yourself. A generator creates a unique site from scratch based on your description, so the result is personalized to your needs. Generators also handle content creation, layout decisions, and image selection, whereas templates leave all of that work to you.
