FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to build a complete website for you based on a short description of what you need. Instead of dragging and dropping elements or choosing from a template gallery, you simply describe your business, project, or goal in plain language, and the AI designs a fully functional, responsive site tailored to what you described.

Strikingly's AI site generator goes further by producing complete, publish-ready sites with real content, layout, and styling in seconds. You can then customize every detail — text, images, colors, sections — before going live.

A template is a static, pre-designed layout that you manually fill in with your own content. You pick a template, then spend time replacing placeholder text, swapping images, and adjusting the design to fit your needs. A generator, by contrast, builds the entire site for you from scratch based on your description. It creates the copy, selects the layout, and assembles the pages automatically.

Think of a template as a blank canvas with a suggested composition, and a generator as a skilled designer who listens to your brief and delivers a finished piece. Generators are faster and produce more personalized results, especially when you're starting from zero.

Yes. All of Strikingly's generators are free to start. You can generate a complete site, preview it, customize it, and publish it without ever entering a credit card. Strikingly offers a generous free tier that lets you go live with a fully functional site at no cost.

If you later need premium features like a custom domain, additional pages, or advanced ecommerce tools, paid plans are available. But the core generator experience — from first prompt to published site — is entirely free.

You can build virtually any kind of site with Strikingly's generators. The most popular categories include business websites, portfolios, online stores, blogs, landing pages, and link-in-bio pages. Within each category, we offer specialized generators for specific industries and use cases — wedding websites, restaurant sites, photography portfolios, and more.

If you don't see a generator that exactly matches your need, you can jump straight into the AI site builder at /s/ai_site_builder and describe any kind of site you want. The AI handles the rest.

Absolutely. After the generator produces your site, you have full control over every element. You can edit text, swap images, change colors and fonts, add or remove sections, and rearrange the layout. The generator gives you a strong starting point, but the final result is entirely yours to shape.

Strikingly's editor is designed to be simple enough for beginners while offering enough depth for experienced users. If you can edit a document, you can customize a Strikingly site.

Yes, every site generated by Strikingly is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops right out of the box. Mobile responsiveness is built into the generator's output by default — you don't need to configure anything or check a separate mobile preview unless you want to make device-specific adjustments.

Strikingly has been a leader in mobile-first website design since day one, and our generators inherit that philosophy. Your site will look great on any screen size, from the moment it's generated.
