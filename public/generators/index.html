Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to build a complete website for you based on a short description of your business, project, or goal. Instead of dragging and dropping elements or writing code, you describe what you need and the AI assembles a ready-to-publish site in seconds.

Strikingly's generators are pre-configured for specific use cases — portfolios, stores, blogs, and more — so the AI already understands the structure and content your site type needs before you type a single word.

A template gives you a blank visual starting point — you still need to replace every piece of placeholder text and swap in your own images. A generator goes further: it uses AI to write your actual content, choose a layout that fits your industry, and produce a site that already feels like yours from the first preview.

Think of a template as a blank canvas and a generator as a skilled assistant who paints the first draft for you. You still have full control to edit anything afterward.

Yes. You can generate, preview, and customize your site without entering a credit card. Strikingly's free plan lets you publish a site on a Strikingly subdomain at no cost.

Paid plans unlock custom domains, additional storage, e-commerce features, and priority support. You can upgrade at any time, but you never have to in order to get a live site.

The directory on this page covers six main categories: business and personal websites, landing pages, portfolios, blogs, online stores, and link-in-bio pages. Within each category there are generators tuned for specific industries and audiences — photographers, restaurants, yoga instructors, musicians, and many more.

If you don't see a generator that matches your exact need, the AI Site Builder at the top of this page can handle any site type. Just describe what you're building and it will figure out the right structure.

Absolutely. The generated site is a starting point, not a locked-down product. Once the AI builds your first draft, you can edit any text, swap images, change colors, reorder sections, add new pages, and adjust the layout — all without writing code.

Strikingly's editor is designed to be fast and visual, so most customizations take seconds rather than minutes.

Yes. Every site generated by Strikingly is fully responsive by default. The layout, typography, and images automatically adapt to any screen size — phone, tablet, or desktop — without any extra work on your part.

Mobile performance is built into the platform, not bolted on afterward. You can preview the mobile view at any time inside the editor before you publish.
