Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator builds a complete website for you from a short description. Instead of picking a template and filling it in section by section, you describe what your business or project does, and the generator produces the pages, copy structure, and layout in one pass.

You can keep what the AI made, edit any part of it, or generate a fresh version. The goal is to get past the blank page and land on something that already looks like a real site.

A template gives you the same fixed layout as everyone else who picked it, with placeholder text you have to replace. A generator starts from your description and builds a site that's already shaped around what you do, with real-looking copy and sections that match your goal.

If you don't like what the generator produces, you can regenerate or edit it. Templates are a starting layout; generators are a starting site.

Business sites, personal sites, portfolios, blogs, online stores, landing pages, and link-in-bio pages are all supported. The directory above is organized around those categories so you can land on the closest starting point.

If your idea doesn't fit a category exactly, you can still start from the general AI Site Builder and describe what you want in your own words.

Yes. After generation, the site opens in the Strikingly editor, where every section, color, photo, and word can be changed. You can also rearrange sections, add new ones, or remove pieces you don't need.
