Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to create a complete website based on a short description of your business or project. Instead of starting from a blank page or manually customizing a template, you tell the AI what you need in a sentence or two, and it generates a full site including layout, copy, images, and styling. Strikingly's generators are specialized for different industries and use cases, so the result is tailored to your specific needs from the start.
A template is a fixed design layout that you fill with your own content. It looks the same for everyone who uses it until you manually edit every section. A generator, by contrast, creates a unique site from scratch based on your specific input. It writes original copy, selects relevant imagery, and structures pages around your particular business or goal. Think of a template as a pre-built house you need to redecorate, while a generator is an architect who builds a house tailored to you.
