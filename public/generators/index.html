Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to build a complete website from a short description of your business or goal. Instead of choosing a template and manually editing sections, you describe what you need and the generator creates pages, layouts, copy, and images tailored to your input.

Strikingly's AI generators go a step further by optimizing every output for mobile devices, SEO best practices, and fast load times, so the site you get is ready to publish from day one.

A template gives you a pre-designed layout and expects you to fill in content, rearrange sections, and handle design decisions yourself. A generator does that work for you. It takes your description and builds a finished site with relevant content, structure, and styling already in place.

You can still customize everything the generator produces, but you start from something much closer to a finished product rather than an empty shell.

Yes, every Strikingly AI generator is free to try. You can generate a site, preview it, and customize it without entering a credit card. Publishing on a free Strikingly subdomain is also included at no cost.

If you want a custom domain, advanced analytics, or premium features, paid plans are available, but the core generation and publishing experience is free.

You can build business sites, portfolios, landing pages, blogs, online stores, event pages, link-in-bio pages, and more. Each generator is optimized for a specific type of site, which means the structure, sections, and content suggestions are tailored to your use case rather than generic.

Whether you are a freelancer, a restaurant owner, a wedding couple, or a startup founder, there is a generator designed for what you are building.

Absolutely. The AI-generated site is a starting point, not a final product. You can change text, swap images, adjust colors, rearrange sections, add new pages, and modify the layout using Strikingly's visual editor. No coding knowledge is required.

Most users spend a few minutes making tweaks before publishing. The generator gets you about 90% of the way there, and the editor handles the rest.

Yes, every site produced by a Strikingly AI generator is fully responsive by default. The layouts, images, text sizing, and navigation automatically adapt to phones, tablets, and desktops without any extra configuration on your part.

Mobile responsiveness is built into the generation process, not added as an afterthought, so you do not need to create a separate mobile version of your site.
