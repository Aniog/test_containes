FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

An AI site generator is a tool that builds a complete, ready-to-publish website from a simple description of your business or project. Instead of dragging elements around a canvas or writing code, you tell the generator what you need in plain language, and the AI handles the layout, content structure, and design decisions. Strikingly's generators are built on our AI Site Builder, so every site comes out responsive, SEO-friendly, and hosted from the moment it's created.

A template is a static starting point with placeholder text and stock images that you replace piece by piece. A generator is an active tool that builds a tailored site for you based on the description you provide. Instead of starting from a generic layout and filling in the blanks, the generator makes design and content decisions that match your specific use case, industry, or goal. Templates give you a frame; generators give you a finished draft.

Yes, every generator on this page is free to use. You can generate a site, preview it, customize it, and publish it on a strikingly.com subdomain without entering a credit card. If you want to connect a custom domain, remove Strikingly branding, or access advanced features, we offer affordable paid plans. But the core experience costs nothing.

You can build almost any type of site with our generators. The most common categories are business and personal websites, single-page landing pages, creative portfolios, blogs with built-in SEO, online stores with payment processing, and link-in-bio pages for creators. Within each category, we have specialized generators for specific audiences like photographers, restaurants, wedding couples, and more. If you do not see your exact use case, the general AI Site Builder can handle it.

Absolutely. Think of the generator as your first draft. Once the AI builds your site, you can change every element: swap fonts and colors, rearrange sections, add your own images, edit all the text, and connect third-party tools. The Strikingly editor gives you full drag-and-drop control over the generated site, so nothing is locked in place.

Yes, every site created through a Strikingly generator is fully responsive and mobile-optimized by default. Our platform has always been mobile-first, and the AI builder inherits that approach. Your generated site will adapt its layout, images, and navigation for phones, tablets, and desktops without any extra work on your part.
