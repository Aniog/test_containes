Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI site generators.

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to build a complete website based on a short description you provide. Instead of starting from a blank page or manually customizing a template, you simply tell the AI what kind of site you need — for example, a restaurant website or a photographer portfolio — and the AI generates a fully designed site with relevant pages, content, and layout in seconds.

A template gives you a fixed design that you still have to fill in and customize manually. A generator, on the other hand, creates a site from scratch based on your specific input. The AI adapts the structure, text, images, and layout to your industry and goals. This means you get a site that is uniquely suited to your needs rather than modifying a one-size-fits-all design.

Yes, all generators on this page are free to use. You can create a site, customize it, and preview it without entering any payment information. If you choose to publish your site under a custom domain or upgrade to access advanced features, Strikingly offers paid plans, but generating a site costs nothing.

You can build almost any type of site: business websites, landing pages, portfolios, blogs, online stores, and link-in-bio pages. There are specialized generators for specific industries such as restaurants, weddings, photography, real estate, fashion, and more. If you don’t see a specific generator for your needs, you can always start with the general AI Site Builder and describe what you want.

Absolutely. The AI generates a starting point, but every site is fully editable. You can change the layout, colors, fonts, images, text, and add or remove sections using Strikingly’s drag-and-drop editor. The generator gives you a head start, and then you have complete creative control to make the site your own.

Yes. Every site generated by Strikingly is responsive by default, meaning it automatically adapts to look great on phones, tablets, and desktop computers. Mobile optimization is built into every generator, so you don’t need to design separate mobile layouts.
