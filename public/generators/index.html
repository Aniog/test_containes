Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI site generators.

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to create a complete website from a short text description. Instead of starting from a blank page or manually arranging templates, you type what your site is about and the AI builds a layout, picks colors, and writes initial copy. It removes the design and technical barriers so anyone can launch a professional-looking site in minutes, regardless of experience level.

A template is a fixed pre-designed layout that you fill with your own content. A generator, on the other hand, creates a unique layout tailored to your specific input. While templates require you to adapt your content to a pre-existing design, a generator adapts the design to your content. Many generators also produce starter copy, suggest images, and set up site structure automatically, saving hours of manual configuration.
