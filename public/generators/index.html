Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator is a tool that creates a complete website based on a short text description. Instead of choosing a template and editing it manually, you tell the AI what kind of site you need — a portfolio, a store, a blog — and it generates a custom site with appropriate layouts, pages, and content. Strikingly's AI generators are designed to get you from idea to published site in under a minute.
A template is a fixed starting layout that you fill with your own content. A generator uses AI to assemble pages, sections, and copy tailored to what you describe. With a template, you choose a design and adapt it. With a generator, you describe your goal and the design adapts to you. The result is less time spent rearranging and more time building.
Yes. You can use any of our generators to create a site for free. There's no credit card required to generate a site, customize it, and publish it on a Strikingly subdomain. Premium features like custom domains, advanced analytics, and additional storage are available on paid plans.
Our generators cover a wide range of use cases: business websites, personal portfolios, online stores, blogs, landing pages, event sites, wedding websites, restaurant sites, and more. Each generator is tailored to its specific purpose, so a restaurant site comes with menu and reservation sections, while a store site comes with product listings and checkout.
Absolutely. After the AI generates your site, you can edit every part of it using Strikingly's drag-and-drop editor. Change colors, fonts, images, layout, and content. Add or remove sections. The generator gives you a strong starting point, and the editor gives you full control over the final result.
Yes. Every site generated by our AI is responsive by default, meaning it automatically adjusts to look great on phones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile optimization is built into every generator, so you don't need to tweak anything for smaller screens. You can preview and fine-tune the mobile view in the editor if needed.
