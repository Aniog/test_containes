Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI site generators.

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to build a complete website from a short text description. Instead of choosing a pre-made template and customizing it manually, you simply tell the AI what your business or project is about, and it generates a fully designed site including layout, content, and images in seconds. Think of it as having a professional web designer who works instantly and never sleeps.

A template is a fixed design that you fill with your own content. You still need to rearrange sections, swap images, and adjust colors to make it yours. A generator is proactive: it creates a unique layout and draft content tailored to your description. You start much closer to a finished site and spend less time on setup. Generators also learn from trends and best practices, while templates remain static until manually updated.

Yes, you can generate and preview your site completely free with no credit card required. Premium plans unlock custom domains, advanced analytics, and ecommerce features. There is no time limit on the free plan, so you can take as long as you need to build and refine your site before upgrading.

Our generators cover a wide range of site types including business websites, landing pages, portfolios, blogs, online stores, event pages, and link-in-bio pages. Each generator is tuned to its category, so a restaurant website generator knows to include menus and reservation links, while a photographer generator emphasizes galleries and booking. If your project does not fit neatly into one category, the AI Site Builder can handle any type of site from a general description.

Absolutely. The generated site is fully editable with Strikingly's drag-and-drop editor. You can change any text, swap images, rearrange sections, modify colors and fonts, and add custom code.
