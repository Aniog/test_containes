An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to build a complete website from a short text description. You describe your business, project, or goal in a sentence or two, and the generator produces a fully designed site with layout, copy, images, and structure already in place.

Unlike traditional website builders where you drag and drop elements manually, an AI generator does the heavy lifting for you. You can then customize anything it produces — swap images, rewrite text, rearrange sections — before publishing.

A template is a pre-designed layout that you fill in yourself. You pick a design, then manually replace the placeholder text, images, and sections with your own content. Templates look great, but they require significant effort to customize.

A generator, on the other hand, creates a unique site tailored to your specific description. The AI writes the copy, selects relevant imagery, and structures the pages based on what you tell it about your business. The result is a site that already feels personal to you, not a generic starting point.
