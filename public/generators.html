FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is an AI site generator?

An AI site generator creates a complete website based on a short description you provide. It chooses layout, content, and styling automatically so you can launch quickly without starting from a blank page.

How is a generator different from a template?

A template is a pre-designed layout you fill in manually. A generator builds the site for you from a description, including text and structure, then you can edit anything afterward.

Are these generators free to use?

Yes. You can generate, customize, and publish without a credit card. Paid plans are available if you want extra features or a custom domain.

What kinds of sites can I build?

You can build business sites, landing pages, portfolios, blogs, online stores, and link-in-bio pages. Each generator is tuned for a specific goal or industry.

Can I customize what the generator produces?

Absolutely. After generation you can edit text, swap images, reorder sections, and change the design using the normal Strikingly editor.

Do generated sites work on mobile?

Yes. Every site produced by a generator is responsive and tested on phones, tablets, and desktops by default.
