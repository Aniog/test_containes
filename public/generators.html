Frequently Asked Questions

An AI site generator is a tool that builds a complete, publish-ready website from a short description you provide. Instead of dragging blocks or picking templates, you describe what your site is about — like "a bakery in Brooklyn that does custom cakes" — and our AI generates a tailored site with text, imagery, layout, and structure. Think of it as the difference between building furniture from a flat-pack kit and having it assembled for you based on a photo reference.

A template gives you a pre-designed skeleton with placeholder text and stock photos that you must fill in yourself — swapping images, writing paragraphs, adjusting sections. A generator does that work for you. When you describe your business or project, the AI produces a complete site with relevant copy, imagery, and structure already in place. Templates are a great starting point for designers; generators are the finish line, faster.
