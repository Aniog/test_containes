Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI site generators.

An AI site generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to build a complete website based on a short description of your business or project. Instead of choosing a template and manually dragging elements into place, you simply tell the AI what you need, and it generates a full site layout with relevant content, images, and styling in seconds. Think of it as having a designer and developer on demand, but in software form.

A template is a fixed layout that you fill with your own content. Every user starting from the same template sees the same structure. By contrast, an AI generator creates a unique layout for each user based on their description. Two people describing the same type of business may get different but equally appropriate designs. The generator also writes initial copy, selects images, and sets up pages that match your goal, saving hours of setup work.
