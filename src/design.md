# SSourcing China - Design System

## Visual Style

**Overall Aesthetic:** Clean, trustworthy, international B2B. Professional and practical with no exaggerated claims. Focus on clarity and credibility.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: #0F172A (Slate 900) - Deep navy for headers and primary text
- Secondary: #1E40AF (Blue 800) - Trustworthy blue for CTAs and accents
- Accent: #0EA5E9 (Sky 500) - Light blue for highlights
- Neutral: #64748B (Slate 500) - Muted text and borders
- Light: #F8FAFC (Slate 50) - Backgrounds and cards
- White: #FFFFFF - Cards and content areas
- Success: #059669 (Emerald 600) - Trust indicators

**Typography:**
- Font: Inter (system-ui fallback)
- Headings: 600-700 weight, tight tracking
- Body: 400-500 weight, 1.6 line-height
- Scale: H1 48px, H2 36px, H3 24px, Body 16px

**Spacing:**
- Consistent 8px grid system
- Section padding: 80px vertical on desktop, 48px on mobile
- Card padding: 32px
- Gap between elements: 24px

**Components:**
- Buttons: Rounded corners (6px), 48px height, clear hover states
- Cards: White background, subtle shadow, clean borders
- Forms: Clean inputs with focus states, proper labels
- Navigation: Fixed header, clear active states

**Imagery:**
- Realistic factory, QC inspection, and shipping visuals
- Professional photography style
- Consistent aspect ratios (16:9 for hero, 4:3 for cards)

**Do's:**
- Use ample white space
- Maintain consistent alignment
- Use semantic HTML
- Ensure all text has proper contrast
- Keep CTAs prominent but not overwhelming

**Don'ts:**
- Avoid overly bright or saturated colors
- No decorative flourishes or gradients
- No stock photo clichés or generic business imagery
- Avoid superlatives or exaggerated claims
- No cluttered layouts or excessive icons
