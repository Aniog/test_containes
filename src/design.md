# Velmora Fine Jewelry - Design System

## Visual Identity
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry aesthetic.

## Color Palette
- **Base**: #0F0F0F (deep refined black)
- **Surface**: #1A1A1A (elevated surfaces)
- **Accent**: #C5A46E (warm gold metallic)
- **Text Primary**: #F5F5F5 (warm off-white)
- **Text Secondary**: #A3A3A3 (muted gray)
- **Divider**: #2A2A2A (hairline)
- **Success**: #4A7043 (subtle green for trust elements)

## Typography
- **Headings/Product Names**: Playfair Display (serif), uppercase, wide letter-spacing
- **Body/UI**: Inter (sans-serif), clean and readable

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: 80px vertical (mobile: 48px)
- Card gaps: 24px
- Thin hairline dividers (1px)

## Interactive States
- Subtle hover transitions (200ms ease)
- Soft shadows on cards
- Premium button styling (solid gold or outlined)
