# SSourcing China - Design Guidelines

## Brand Colors
- Primary Blue: `#1B4A7A` (trustworthy, corporate navy)
- Primary Blue Hover: `#153E68`
- Accent Red: `#C0392B` (CTA, urgency)
- Accent Red Hover: `#A93226`
- Light Background: `#F8F9FA` (clean, light gray)
- Card Background: `#FFFFFF`
- Dark Text: `#1E293B`
- Medium Text: `#475569`
- Light Text: `#94A3B8`
- Border: `#E2E8F0`
- Success Green: `#059669` (for trust badges)

## Typography
- Headings: Inter, bold, dark (#1E293B)
- Body: Inter, regular, medium (#475569)
- Font sizes: h1: 3rem, h2: 2.25rem, h3: 1.5rem, body: 1rem, small: 0.875rem

## Spacing
- Section padding: py-20 (5rem)
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl mx-auto px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8
- Card padding: p-6 or p-8
- Gap between cards: gap-8

## Visual Style
- Cards: white bg, subtle shadow (shadow-sm), rounded-lg (0.5rem), border
- Buttons: rounded-md, font-semibold, px-6 py-3
- Primary CTA: bg-accent-red text-white, hover:bg-accent-red-hover
- Secondary CTA: bg-primary-blue text-white, hover:bg-primary-blue-hover
- Dividers: thin gray border-t
- Icons: Lucide React, 5-6 size, primary-blue or accent-red depending on context

## Layout
- Sticky header with white background
- Footer with dark navy background
- Sections alternate between white and light gray backgrounds
- Mobile: single column, hamburger menu
- Desktop: multi-column grids

## Imagery
- Use realistic factory/QC/shipping/trade visuals via stock image system
- B2B professional style - no cartoonish elements
- Images in 16:9 or 4:3 ratios for hero/section backgrounds
- Images in 4:3 or 1:1 for card thumbnails

## Do's
- Clean, minimalist layouts
- Trust signals (badges, certifications) prominently placed
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Professional B2B tone throughout
- Generous whitespace

## Don'ts
- No exaggerated claims or superlatives
- No cartoonish illustrations
- No overly bright or playful colors
- No cluttered layouts
- No hardcoded image URLs - use the stock image system
