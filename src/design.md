# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Design System

## Brand Personality
Quiet luxury. Warm, editorial, premium-but-accessible. Demi-fine gold jewelry for women 25–45.

## Color Palette
- **Base BG:** Cream-50 (#fdfbf7) — warm off-white paper texture
- **Text Primary:** Brand-900 (#1a1512) — deep espresso black
- **Text Secondary:** Brand-500 (#7a6654) — warm taupe
- **Accent:** Gold-500 (#d4af37) — warm gold, used sparingly
- **Border/Divider:** Brand-200 (#d9cec2) — fine hairline
- **Hover/Active BG:** Brand-100 (#ede6de)
- **Sale/Price:** Gold-700 (#b8963f)

## Typography
- **Headings / Logo / Product Names:** Cormorant Garamond (serif), uppercase, wide letter-spacing for product names
- **Display / Hero Title:** Playfair Display SC (small caps)
- **Body / UI:** Inter (sans-serif), clean, light weight
- **Product names:** UPPERCASE, tracking-widest, text-sm
- **Prices:** Inter medium, gold-700

## Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace: section padding y-20 md:y-28
- Max content width: 1280px (mx-auto px-6)
- Thin hairline dividers (border-t border-brand-200)

## Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid gold-500 on brand-900 text. Hover: gold-400. Dark on hover.
- **Outline:** Border brand-800, text brand-800. Hover: fill brand-800, text cream-50.
- **Ghost:** No border, subtle hover bg.

## Effects
- Subtle hover transitions (duration-300)
- Soft shadows on cards (shadow-sm hover:shadow-md)
- Fade-in / slide-up animations on scroll
- Thin, refined borders (border border-brand-200/50)

## DOs
- Use warm gold as the only accent color, sparingly
- Let whitespace breathe
- Use large editorial-style imagery
- Fine hairline dividers between sections
- Product names in uppercase with wide letter-spacing
- Serif for all headings and product names

## DON'Ts
- No loud colors, no bright reds/blues
- No heavy shadows
- No discount/coupon styling (no "SALE" badges)
- No cluttered layouts
- No bright white — use warm off-white
- No generic e-commerce patterns
