# Velmora Fine Jewelry — Visual Design System

## Mood
Quiet luxury, warm, editorial. Premium demi-fine jewelry. NOT loud, NOT discount-looking, NOT generic e-commerce.

## Color Palette
- Background: #FAF7F2 (warm cream/ivory)
- Card/Surface: #FFFFFF
- Text Primary: #1C1C1C (near-black, high contrast)
- Text Secondary: #6B6B6B (medium gray)
- Text Muted: #9C9C9C (lighter gray)
- Gold Accent: #C9A96E (warm gold)
- Gold Hover: #B8944C (deeper gold)
- Deep Charcoal: #1C1C1C
- Border/Divider: #E5E0D6 (warm light gray/taupe)
- Trust Bar BG: #F0EBE3 (warm beige)
- News/Brand Accent BG: #2D2D2D (dark charcoal)
- Star/Review: #C9A96E (gold)
- Error: #D94A4A
- Success: #4A7C59

## Typography
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (Google Font), serif, elegant
- Body/UI: Inter (Google Font), clean sans-serif
- Product Names: UPPERCASE, letter-spacing wide (tracking-widest), serif font
- Nav links: uppercase, tracking-wider, small size

## Spacing
- Generous whitespace throughout
- Section padding: py-16 md:py-24
- Card padding: p-6
- Grid gaps: gap-6 md:gap-8

## Borders & Dividers
- Thin hairline dividers (border-t border-[#E5E0D6])
- Subtle borders on cards (border border-[#E5E0D6]/50)
- Soft shadows on hover: shadow-lg shadow-black/5

## Buttons
- Primary: solid gold bg ([#C9A96E]), white text, hover: darker gold ([#B8944C])
- Outline: border gold, text gold, hover: bg gold, white text
- Pill shape or slightly rounded (rounded-full or rounded-md)
- Smooth transition 300ms

## Hover Effects
- Subtle scale (scale-[1.02]) on product cards
- Smooth opacity transitions (duration-300)
- Image zoom on hover (scale-105)
- Underline animation on links

## Accessibility
- High contrast text on all backgrounds
- Gold accent never used for critical text — only decorative/accent
- Focus rings on interactive elements
